
    
      Subjects that have met eligibility criteria will undergo treatment with the hybrid closed
      loop system under various experimental conditions.There is no statistically powered study
      hypothesis testing.

      The investigational centers will be encouraged to include subjects of different ethnicities
      including Hispanic, Native American, and African-American.

      The study is anticipated to last no more than 12 months from investigational center
      initiation to finalization of all data entry and monitoring procedures. Subjects are expected
      to participate for approximately 1- 2 months.
