
    
      This is a retrospective comparative case series comprised of 70 patients being treated for
      polypoidal choroidal vasculopathy with intravitreal ranibizumab injections and 112 control
      subjects. Patients were genotyped for the LOC387715 (rs10490924), HTRA1 gene (rs11200638),
      and VEGF (rs3025039 and rs833069)polymorphism using Real-Time polymerase chain reaction.
