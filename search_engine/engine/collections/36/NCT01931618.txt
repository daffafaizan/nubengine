
    
      Setting: Online study among Norwegian employees.

      Participants: At-risk drinkers among employees in a select group of norwegian workplaces is
      recruited through an e-mail to their job e-mail account, and advertisements on the intranet
      of their organization.

      Design: Randomized controlled trial (RCT). Subjects in both conditions receives a single
      session screening procedure including individualized normative feedback. The control group
      receives an online booklet about the effects of alcohol. The treatment group receives the
      multi session follow up program "Balance".
