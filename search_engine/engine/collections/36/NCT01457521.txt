
    
      This trial will compare two oral analgesic regimens in women undergoing first trimester
      medical abortion with mifepristone and misoprostol. The primary study outcome is pain.
      Secondary objectives include comparing the regimens for the total amount of ibuprofen taken,
      use of other analgesics, vaginal bleeding, adverse events, successful completion of abortion,
      overall satisfaction with the abortion procedure.
