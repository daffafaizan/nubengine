
    
      This is a prospective observational study to the aim of investigating the effectiveness and
      safety of integrative Korean medicine treatment (generally consisting of herbal medicine,
      Chuna manual medicine, bee venom pharmacopuncture and pharmacopuncture, acupuncture,
      electroacupuncture, and cupping) in cervical disc herniation patients with neck pain or
      cervical radiculitis diagnosed by cervical MRI and clinical symptoms including cervical
      radiculopathy at 4 locations of Jaseng Hospital of Korean Medicine (Gangnam, Bucheon,
      Daejeon, Haeundae) as assessed through of pain, functional disability, work loss, and quality
      of life patient-reported outcomes with a 5-year follow-up period
