
    
      This is a 2-year prospective interrupted time series (ITS) study of usual asthma care
      (baseline period) (year 1) compared to care with the eAAPS in place (intervention period)
      (year 2). The setting is two academic family health teams (primary health care teams
      including family physicians, nurses, and allied health members) in Hamilton, Ontario and one
      community-based family health team in Brampton, Ontario.
