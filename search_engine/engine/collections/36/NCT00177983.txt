
    
      The objective of this study is to assess the feasibility of using the World Wide Web (Web)
      and in-home computers to provide patients suffering from schizophrenia and their families
      advice from health care professionals, social support, and information.

      The purposes of this intervention are to improve: 1) the availability, convenience and use of
      needed mental health services; 2) family support for their ill family members; and 3) patient
      and family outcomes.
