
    
      Objective of the study is to confirm the safety and performance of Straumann BLX Implants
      used for tooth replacement in cleared indications up to 12 months after implant placement.
      Implants used are Straumann® BLX Implant Roxolid® SLActive®. Regular Base (3.5 mm): implant
      diameters 3.75 and 4.5mm, in lengths 6, 8, 10, 12,14, 16 and 18 mm Wide Base (4.5 mm):
      diameter 5.5 mm in lengths 6, 8, 10 and 12 mm
