
    
      Patients who will give their informed non-opposition will be included. Participants will
      undergo 6 metacognitive tasks in which they will be tested on newly learnt information (i.e.,
      episodic memory) or prior knowledge (i.e., semantic memory) and asked to evaluate their
      performance either prior or after the test. Correlation between their evaluation and their
      actual performance will be measured. Performance and correlations will be compared across
      groups.
