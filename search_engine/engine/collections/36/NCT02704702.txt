
    
      This is an open-label, randomized, 6-sequence, 3-period, 3-way crossover study. Within each
      period, randomized subjects will be 2 dosing regimens with the same drug(fimasartan or
      rosuvastatin) or study drug combination(fimasartan+rosuvastatin) on day 1 and quaque die(qd)
      from day 4 to day 10. After day 1 and day 10 dosing will be followed by pharmacokinetic
      sampling for 48 hours or 72 hours.
