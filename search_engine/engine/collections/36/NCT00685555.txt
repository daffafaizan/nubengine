
    
      The Switch™ Program is a unique community and family-based behavior change initiative that
      helps motivate children to change three critical health behaviors that are risk factors for
      childhood overweight and obesity. Through community education, family-based strategies and
      partnerships with schools, 3rd, 4th and 5th graders are encouraged to: Switch what you Do™
      Switch what you View™ Switch what you Chew™.
