
    
      Asymptomatic subjects will be recruited to this study. Pressure pain threshold, heart rate,
      heart rate variability and skin conductance will be measured before, immediately after and
      fifteen minutes after the intervention.

      Subjects will be randomized into three groups: Passive accessory intervertebral mobilization
      one, passive accessory intervertebral mobilization two and placebo group.

      The researcher that will do the intervention will be blinded to the outcomes measures, and
      the researcher that will do the measurements will be blind to the intervention.
