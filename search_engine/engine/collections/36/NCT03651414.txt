
    
      Phase 1 - Patients enrollment (October 2018 - May 2019) Each Investigator will have to record
      data concerning 70 patients. In particular 40 patients "outliers" (max 10 per month) and 30
      patients "control" (max 6 per month). It will be collected clinical and organisational
      information. The Investigators of the wards that will not have patients "outliers" will have
      to enroll 70 patients "control" (max 15 per month).

      Phase 2 - Database review and validation (June 2019 - October 2019) The data collected will
      be analysed to process the endpoints.
