
    
      Second-year residents from different programs of residency in obstetrics and gynecology in
      Porto Alegre, RS, Brazil, will be invited to participate in this study. they will be
      randomized to receive a comprehensive training in laparoscopy using modern virtual reality
      program or the traditional method of training according to their residency program. After 12
      weeks of training, they will finish their training and they will perform advanced
      laparoscopic surgery in the simulator according to their specific needs, for example,
      salpingectomy, oophorectomy advanced laparoscopic suture or hysterectomy. Data generated from
      the simulator program will be compared between groups.
