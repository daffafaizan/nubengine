
    
      40 healthy individuals between the ages of 60-80 who agreed to participate in the study will
      be included in the study. Participants will be divided into 2 groups randomly. Both groups
      will be given balance exercises with aerobic exercise. The second group will be given second
      additional cognitive task besides doing usual exercises that the first group is performing.
      Exercises will be given in the form of a home exercise program and the treatment will last
      for 6 weeks. The continuity of the exercises will be controlled by telerehabilitation method.
      The participants will be evaluated before and after the treatment.
