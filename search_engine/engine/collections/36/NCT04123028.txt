
    
      The prospective one year study in Thai COPD patients for the clinical outcomes of COPD
      compare between eosinophilic and non-eosinophilic which determine by blood eosinophil counts
      equal or more than 300 cells/microliters. The primary outcomes was COPD exacerbation, the
      secondary outcomes were lung function, dyspnea and quality of life.
