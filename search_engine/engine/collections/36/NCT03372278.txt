
    
      The objectives of this study are to confirm the safety and performance of the Zimmer Maxera
      Cup mated with either a BIOLOX® delta or BIOLOX® OPTION femoral head when used in primary
      total hip arthroplasty.

      In total 250 patients will be enrolled into the study at up to 10 sites.All potential study
      subjects will be required to participate in the Informed Consent Process.
