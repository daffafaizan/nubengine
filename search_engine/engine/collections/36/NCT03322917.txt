
    
      Prospective interventional consecutive case series including 156 eyes of 80 patients affected
      by chronic post-LASIK OSS who were treated with autologous E-PRP 6 times a day as monotherapy
      for 6 weeks. Assessment after treatment with E-PRP included: dry eye symptoms, change in
      corrected distance visual acuity (CDVA), corneal fluorescein staining (CFS) and conjunctival
      hyperemia.
