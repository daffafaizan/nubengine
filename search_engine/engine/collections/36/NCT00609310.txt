
    
      The preparation contains a mixture of 200mg chamomile and tea (green tea) extract which
      contains naturally occurring bioflavonoids (2%) together with vitamins and folic acid. This
      flavonoids mixture will be applied for 3 years and the compliance and effects of this
      treatment will be determined by measuring the serum concentrations of apigenin and EGCG of
      the patients.
