
    
      A total of 350 cases of proximal humeral fracture are planned to be recruited and receive
      semi-shoulder replacement in Department of Orthopedics and Trauma，Peking University People's
      Hospital．The blood pressure data are collected from patients' anesthesia records. Both the
      systolic and diastolic blood pressure are recorded at four time spots during operation: the
      time before using of bone cement (time period A), 0-5 min after using cement (time period B),
      5-10 min after using cement (time period C) and 10-15min after using cement (time period D).
      The data of blood pressure are collected according to anesthesia records．
