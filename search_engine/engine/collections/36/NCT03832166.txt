
    
      "Fruits and Veggies for Health" is a prospective, randomized two group study to evaluate a
      community-engaged screening, identification, and F&V intervention to improve CKD outcomes in
      140 AA adults (greater than 18 years). Participants will be randomized to a 1) F&V only (F&V
      Only), or 2) F&V plus a nutrition/cooking program (F&V + Cook).
