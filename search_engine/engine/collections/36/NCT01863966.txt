
    
      1. Achalasia patients will participate hot water drinking therapy: to drink 200ml of hot
           water before and after a meal, as well as 2 hours before sleep and to chew food
           carefully and swallow with hot soup.

        2. Patients are followed by achalasia associated symptom scores, barium esophagram and
           endoscopy.

        3. If patients are unsatisfied with hot water drinking therapy, they receive pneumatic
           dilation
