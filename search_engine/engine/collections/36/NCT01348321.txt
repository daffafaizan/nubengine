
    
      An investigator blind clinical randomized prospective two months study was designed. One
      hundred sixty nine acne patients presenting in our outpatients clinic were randomly allocated
      to two treatment groups: azithromycin and levamisole ( group 1 ) and azithromycin alone (
      group 2 ) .One hundred forty eight patients completed the duration of the study and their
      clinical responses were evaluated and compared by using monthly photographic records .
