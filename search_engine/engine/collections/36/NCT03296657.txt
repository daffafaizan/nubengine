
    
      This is pilot study involving approximately 20 subjects with at least 20 dental pockets
      between 2- 4mm with bleeding for sampling. Subjects will have up to 10 sites identified as
      "sampling sites" that will have supragingival plaque, subgingival plaque, and gingival
      crevicular fluid (GCF) collected at Baseline, 30 minutes, 24 hours, and Week 2.
