
    
      The purpose of this study is to analyze stability, safety and efficacy of the Metaphyseal Hip
      Prosthesis (MHP) compared to the Stanmore hip replacement at short term (1 year) and long
      term (10 years).

      Bone remodeling will be analyzed at 10 years using DEXA (Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry
      (bone scan test)) measurements.

      This is a single-center prospective study with 25 patients in the study and 25 in the control
      group in open Randomized Clinical Trial.
