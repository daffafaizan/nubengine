
    
      Transcranial direct current stimulation is a non-invasive brain neuromodulation technique.
      tDCS can be a new therapy in depression.

      This study focuses on tDCS cost-utility-analysis. Each patient is following during 12 months.
      Patients are randomized in 2 arms. Arm A usual care with tDCS cure or arm B usual care
      without tDCS.

      Patient in arm A get a initial tDCS cure one week after randomization. If these patients are
      answering to the treatment, they can have a new cure in case of relapse. This new cure can
      start from the second month following initial treatment.
