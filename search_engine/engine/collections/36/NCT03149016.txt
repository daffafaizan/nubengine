
    
      In the last three decades, the number of adult patients seeking short orthodontic treatment
      time has apparently increased as the conventional orthodontic treatment usually lasts for
      20-24 months. Furthermore, orthodontic treatment is often associated with root resorption,
      bone degradation and caries development. Patients nowadays desire to end the orthodontic
      treatment as soon as possible due to phsycosocial reasons and to get the goals of the
      treatment without affecting their social life .

      So many tooth movement accelerating methods have been proposed. However, the surgical
      approached are considered the most effective and promising methods in accelerating tooth
      movement. The current research aims to evaluate the efficacy of the a new proposed surgical
      method compared to the conventional method in upper incisors' retraction.
