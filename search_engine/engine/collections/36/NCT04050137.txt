
    
      There take part in this study women or men aged between 18 and 65 years, with a diagnosys by
      a physician specializing in Fabry disease with FabryScan. Patients must present a stable
      evolution, with a scoring system of disease severity (DS3) that must be less than 8 points
      per year in the last 2-3 years and they have to presence of neuropathic pain with a score
      greater than or equal to 4 in the questionnaire "Douleur neuropathique (DN4)".
