
    
      In the IEDL group,2 portals 0.5cm were used one for the endoscope and the other for
      instruments. For every additional level one portal is added. The endoscope and instruments
      are directly placed over the surface of lamina without any dissection and saline under pump
      pressure is used to open a potential working space. Unilateral laminotomy and bilateral
      decompression under endoscopic vision is performed. In the microsurgical group, bilateral
      fenestration is performed in the usual manner
