
    
      Technical evaluation of magnetic resonance imaging and spectroscopy will be performed on
      normal volunteers. These studies will be conducted in the NIH MRI systems located in Suburban
      Hospital in Bethesda, Maryland. These studies may involve the intravenous administration of
      commercially available MR contrast media and exercise. The results will be used to evaluate
      the performance of various pulse sequences, gradient coils, and RF coils on human subjects
      and will provide essential ground work for specific patient protocols.
