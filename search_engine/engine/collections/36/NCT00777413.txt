
    
      The study was conducted as an open label, balanced, randomised, two-treatment, two-period,
      two-sequence, single-dose, crossover , bioequivalence study on minocycline comparing
      minocycline 100mg tablets of Ranbaxy with Minocin 100mg capsules of in healthy, adult, human,
      subjects under fed conditions.

      A total of 24 non-smoking subjects (17 men and 7 women) were included in this study, of which
      24 finished the study according to the protocol.
