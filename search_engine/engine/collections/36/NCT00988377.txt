
    
      A randomized, cross-over design study was conducted. Whey protein preloads (10-40 g) were
      provided in 300 ml water. At 30 min following consumption, subjects were fed an ad libitum
      pizza meal. Satiety, and blood glucose were measured at baseline and selected intervals pre-
      (0, 15 and 30 min) and post-meal (50, 65, 80 and 95 min).
