
    
      This study will be a randomized control trial, used to compare the effectiveness of virtual
      reality and cyclic training on balance in sub-acute stroke patients. Subjects with sub-acute
      stroke meeting the predetermined inclusion and exclusion criteria will be divided into two
      groups using simple random sampling technique. Assessment will be done by using berg balance
      scale (BBS) and time up and go test. Subjects in one group will be treated with Virtual
      reality and Conventional therapy and in other group Cyclic training and Conventional therapy.
