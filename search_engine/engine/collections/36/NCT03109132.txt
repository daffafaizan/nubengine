
    
      This test compares 4 models for the duration of up to 3 days each and 2 models with duration
      of up to 24 hours. Each subject will tested the models and will provide feedback at
      predefined times during the test. During the test and according to initial result the team
      may decide to shorten the test duration.

      The testing staff will include a dermatologist and a technical representative that will
      supervise the test in order to monitor and control the required performance of the subjects
      according to test protocol, and relevant outcomes. During the test the dermatologist will
      monitor any unexpected skin reaction or patient discomfort.
