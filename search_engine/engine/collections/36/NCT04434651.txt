
    
      This study was performed in the Neurosurgical operating room and included 52 patients
      allocated into two groups: the treatment group (block group) and the control group (non-block
      group). 26 patients enrolled in block group and 26 patients in non-block group.

      Intraoperative ICP monitoring by subdural ICP monitors will be done every 20 minutes in both
      block and non- block groups until craniotomy has been occurred, then at the time of closure
      of the dura . Arterio- jugular venous oxygen difference and Jugular venous bulb oxygen
      saturation changes will be recorded every 20 minutes in block and non- block groups
      throughout time of the surgery till closure of the dura.
