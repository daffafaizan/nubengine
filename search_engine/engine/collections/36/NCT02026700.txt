
    
      The aim of the study is the evaluation of the effectiveness, tolerance and cosmetic
      acceptability of Bariéderm cream among patients presenting with chronic allergic or irritant
      contact dermatitis of the hands. We shall enrol adults presenting with CONTACT DERMATITIS
      EXCLUDING FLARE-UPS of the hands: whether allergic or irritant, able to understand the
      protocol and agrees to sign the information and informed consent form. Bariéderm cream will
      be applied minimum twice daily over 21 days. Response will be evaluated by self and physician
      filled questionnaires. effectiveness, tolerance and cosmetic acceptability will be evaluated
      by a scale of 1-10, 1 meaning "not at all" and 10 meaning "very much". Statistics will be
      performed by SPSS software with suitable statistical tests. In case of an adverse event
      caused by the product, patch-test with ingredients of Bariéderm cream will be performed.
