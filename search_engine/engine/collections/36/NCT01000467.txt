
    
      To evaluate the safety and pharmacokinetics of probucol by multiple oral administration of
      one 250-mg probucol tablet once daily after breakfast (250 mg/day), two 250-mg probucol
      tablets once daily after breakfast (500 mg/day), and one 250-mg probucol tablet twice daily
      after breakfast and dinner (500 mg/day) for 14 days in healthy male subjects.
