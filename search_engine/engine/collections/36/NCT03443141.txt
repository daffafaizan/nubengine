
    
      A prospective, randomized study will be performed. Patients are randomized using a random
      number table into 1:1 allocation into 2 groups: patients receiving a vitamin E-containing
      dressing (group 1) and a conventional dressing (group 2). In group 1 the wound will be
      covered with a Vitamin E embebbed gauze and plastic adhesive tape. In Group 2, , the wound
      will be covered with standard gauze and plastic adhesive tape.

      The primary outcomes variable will be occurrence of incisional SSI, defined by CDC criteria.
      Follow-up will be 30 days postoperatively.
