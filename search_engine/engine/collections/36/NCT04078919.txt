
    
      A randomized placebo controlled clinical trial will be conducted in modares hospital in
      Tehran, Iran. After review of the inclusion and exclusion criteria and explanation of the
      design of the study, written consent form will be completed. The participants are 66 eligible
      patients, aged 35-70 years. Intervention group will be received low calories diets as well as
      20 percent of daily calories from mixed nuts (peanuts, pistachio and almond) and control
      group will be received low calories diets for 8 weeks. Fasting blood sample will be taken to
      measure inflammatory markers (Interleukine-6 [IL-6] and Interleukine-10 [IL-10] and
      C-Reactive Protein [CRP]).
