
    
      The researchers have the animal model that the extract of the Agaricus blazei Murill mushroom
      is effective in the treatment of type II DM mice conducted on 2002. The dose effects were
      also found. The researchers conducted the pilot study also showing the same effects in 2003.
      So the researchers designed this trial. The elixir of the Agaricus blazei Murill mushroom is
      popular in Taiwan for chronic diseases, such as type II DM.
