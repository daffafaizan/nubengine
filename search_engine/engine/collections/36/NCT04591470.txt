
    
      All infertile male patients, visited between February 2000 and September 2020 and which were
      tested with MAR (Mixed Autoglobulin Reaction) test, were included in this retrospective
      study. Sperm quality (number of spermatozoa, motility, vitality and normal forms) were also
      studied in this patients. The aim of the study is to evaluate if there is a higher incidence
      of positive MAR test among patients undergoing inguinal hernioplasty compared to the general
      population
