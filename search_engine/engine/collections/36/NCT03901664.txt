
    
      The goal of this study is to determine if artificial intelligence allow to detect lymphocyte
      subpopulations specific of diseases (such as primary Sjogren' syndrome and systemic lupus
      erythematosus) among patients with suspicion of autoimmune diseases.

      All patients who had an evaluation of their lymphocyte subpopulations between january 2010
      and december 2016 will be included in a retrospective analysis.
