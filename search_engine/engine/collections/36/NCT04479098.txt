
    
      The present study aimed to verify the effects of 12 weeks of resistance exercise training and
      successive 12 weeks of detraining on body composition (Body Mass, Body Mass Index, Fat Mass,
      and Fat-Free Mass), lipid profile (Triglycerides, Total Cholesterol, LDL-Cholesterol,
      HDL-Cholesterol), muscle strength (1RM test), oxidative stress (Thiobarbituric acid reactive
      substances,Non-Protein Thiols, Catalase activity, and Superoxide Dismutase), and inflammatory
      markers (Interferon γ, Tumor Necrosis Factor α, interleukin 6 , Adiponectin, and Interleukin
      4) of postmenopausal breast cancer survivors undergoing tamoxifen treatment.
