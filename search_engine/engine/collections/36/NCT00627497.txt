
    
      This pivotal clinical trial is being conducted to evaluate the DIAM Spinal Stabilization
      System for the treatment of single-level degenerative lumbar spinal stenosis from L2 to L5.
      The implant under investigation in this clinical trial is the DIAM Spinal Stabilization
      System. And following a decompression procedure, the device will be implanted using a
      posterior surgical approach. Patients in the control group will receive a lumbar
      decompression.
