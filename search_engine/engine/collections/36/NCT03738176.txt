
    
      -  The enrolled patients will be divided randomly into two groups.

        -  Test group will receive topical sesame oil (3 times /day after eating) for a month.

        -  Control group will receive topical corticosteroid (3 times /day after eating) for a
           month.

        -  Assessment of the appearance score and severity of pain as well as the clinical
           parameter outcome will be done at baseline and at the end of two and four weeks and will
           be recorded in the patients' questionnaires & by clinical sign score . MDA will be
           measured at the baseline & at the end of four weeks after treatment.
