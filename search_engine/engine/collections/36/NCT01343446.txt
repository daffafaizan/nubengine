
    
      Abstract Objective: To examine the effect of sleep impairment on coping style of patients
      with diabetes Research Design and Methods: A total of 105 patients (40 male, 65 female) with
      diabetes mellitus were recruited from outpatient diabetic clinics in 2011 February. All
      patients were asked to fill the sociodemographic inquiry, Epworth Sleepiness Scale and Coping
      Style Inventory.
