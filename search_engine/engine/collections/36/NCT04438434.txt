
    
      57 subjects, with an indication for a third molar extraction, will be enrolled in the study.
      Participants selection will take place at the IRCCS "Ca" Granda Ospedale Maggiore Policlinico
      of Milan - UOC Surgery Maxillo Facial and Dentistry where the internship activity of the
      Degree Course in Dentistry and Dental Prosthetics, Dental Hygiene and the School of
      Specialization in Orthodontics of the University of Milan is held.

      Subsequently, subjects will be equally divided and randomly assigned into the test or control
      groups. Assessment of outcomes will be carried out at a distance of 7 days.
