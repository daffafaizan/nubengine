
    
      -  Subject: The patient of gastritis, gastric ulcer, and duodenal ulcer infected with
           Helicobacter pylori

        -  170 subjects per group

        -  Eradication regimens are as follows: AOC group, treated with amoxicillin, rabeprazole,
           clarithromycin for 7 days; AOM group, treated with amoxicillin, rabeprazole,
           metronidazole for 7 days; sequential group, treated with amoxicillin, rabeprazole for 5
           day, followed by clarithromycin, metronidazole, rabeprazole for 5 days; concomitant
           group, treated with amoxicillin, clarithromycin, metronidazole, rabeprazole for 7 days
