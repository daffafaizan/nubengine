
    
      Participants will be recruited in three different age intervals: 18-45 years, 55-80 years and
      80+ years. These participants will all undergo the same stimulation test with 0.1 mg LHRH,
      0.2 mg TRH, and 0.25 mg ACTH (Synachten). The tests will be perfomed at the same time of day
      for all particpants (between 08:15 and 08:45) and participants will be fasting for the test.
      Blood samples will be taken just before and at 10, 20, 30, 45, 60, 90 and 120 minutes
      following the stimulation test. Serum from blood samples will be stored at -20 degrees until
      analysis.
