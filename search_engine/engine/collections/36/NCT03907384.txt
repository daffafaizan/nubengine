
    
      Using a parallel experimental design, participants were randomly assigned to either MPT
      (n=14) or non-exercising control group (n=14) for 12 weeks. Participant in the MPT group
      trained 3 days/week . Participants' brachial to ankle PWV (baPWV), systolic and diastolic BP,
      AIx, plasma NO levels were measured at baseline and after 20 weeks of their assigned
      intervention.
