
    
      The study is a randomised controlled trial. Overall aim is to evaluate if a orthopaedic
      manual therapy assessment (physiotherapy) assessment of patients referred to orthopaedic
      surgeon bring any changes to referral routines, patient satisfaction and health-related
      variables when compared to standard practice. Patients were randomised to either
      physiotherapy assessment or standard practice (orthopaedic surgeon assessment).
