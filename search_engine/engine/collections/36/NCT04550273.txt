
    
      The study group (n=20) received three sessions of aerobic walking exercise per week for 3
      months. Every session was done on an electronic treadmill with no inclination started with 5
      minutes warm-up then 30 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic training with 60-75% of target
      heart rate then followed or ended by 5 minutes cool down. The control group (n=20) will
      receive no training.
