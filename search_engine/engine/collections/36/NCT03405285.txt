
    
      The Connected Catheter (C2P) is a replaceable urinary prosthesis that is intended for use in
      male patients 18 years of age or older who have impaired bladder emptying due to neurogenic
      lower urinary tract dysfunction, and who are capable of operating the device in accordance
      with the provided instructions for use, or who have trained caregivers capable of doing the
      same. The device must be replaced every 29 days
