
    
      This study examines the effect of neuropsychological rehabilitation on the disorder inhibtion
      in the child with ADHD.

      Sample includes 60 subjects aged 9-13 years with disorder of inhibition evalued with
      neuropsychological test. Neuropsychological rehabilitation protocol was developed and
      evaluated.

      This study use baseline and includes a control group and an experimental group. The type of
      cognitive procedure (up / down, or rehabilitative / metacognitive) is examined.
