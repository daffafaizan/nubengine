
    
      SNIPPV is used to prevent Intubation in Infants in which nCPAP has already failed (Infants
      that met intubation criteria) and also is used electively for extubation when nCPAP
      extubation has previously failed or infants with Prolonged mechanical ventilation (more than
      15 days) with high respiratory parameters (PMAP > 10 cmH2O and FiO2>35%).
