
    
      Each child should fulfill the requirements of having a dental condition which needs treatment
      in two dental sessions, where each session should not exceed thirty minutes. At the first
      visit ,either sublingual or intranasal Dexmedetomidine will be used, while at the second
      visit the alternate route will be implemented in a cross-over design. At least one week
      interval between the two visits will be secured.
