
    
      Methodology: 66 amateur runners, after simple size calculation, will be randomized into two
      groups. Experimental group will carry out the proprioceptive exercise program during 8 weeks
      at the begining of the season and control group will continue with their usual training
      routine. The main variable running related injury will be collected weekly during the
      follow-up.

      Results: When all the data needed for the study has been collected, researchers gathers
      results and exports them for statistical interpretation.
