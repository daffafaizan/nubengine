
    
      This study is a genetic association study. It included 50 cases of TPP patients and 80 cases
      of male, hyperthyroid patients who didn't have hypokalemia as a well characterized controls.
      After informed consent were obtained, genomic DNA from leukocyte were extracted. Pooled DNA
      were constructed and whole genome scan using 10K GeneChip microarray were genotyped on pooled
      genomic DNA.
