
    
      A prospective single-arm study designed to further evaluate the of of laparoscopic total
      gastrectomy for remnant gastric cancer will be performed, to evaluate the safety,
      feasibility, long-term and oncological outcomes. The evaluation parameters are perioperative
      mobility and mortality, perioperative clinical efficacy, postoperative life quality, immune
      function and 3-year survival and recurrence rates.
