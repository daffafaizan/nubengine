
    
      Acupuncture may alleviate abdominal or back pain of patients with advanced pancreatic cancer.
      Acupuncture participants would receive electroacupuncture on Jiaji (Ex-B2) points for 3 times
      in a week. Placebo acupuncture participants would receive placebo acupuncture on non-point
      points for the same period. Pain scores on numeric rating scale (NRS) and blood levels of
      Î²-endorphin and nerve growth factor are compared.
