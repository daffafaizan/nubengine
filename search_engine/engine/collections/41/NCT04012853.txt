
    
      The aim of this study is to evaluate the feasibility and efficacy of transcranial Direct
      Current Stimulation (tDCS) administered at home with real-time monitoring via VA Tele-health
      for persistent post traumatic headache (PTH) associated with mTBI, and the impact of this
      treatment on persistent PTH associated functional recovery. Total of 20 participants (10
      treatment group, 10 sham control) will be included in this study. The investigators will also
      evaluate recruitment strategies and participants clinical characteristics.
