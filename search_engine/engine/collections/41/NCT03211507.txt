
    
      Idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF) is a scarring lung disease. It damages the air sacs that
      allow oxygen to be transferred into the blood and transported to vital organs. These changes
      make people with IPF cough and feel short of breath. It not known what causes the damage.
      People who get IPF are usually older than 40Í¾ it's a very serious illness that cannot be
      cured and gets worse over time. Statistics show that IPF is becoming more common in the UK
      but it's not known why. It can be difficult for doctors to tell if someone has IPF or another
      disease called asbestosis.
