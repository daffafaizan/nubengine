
    
      The study is a prospective, consecutive, multi-center, 1:1 randomized study (UroLift
      System:TURP). The study is intended to be conducted at up to 20 different centers in Europe
      and Canada to enroll up to 100 subjects.

      Subject follow-up visits are at 2 weeks, 1, 3, 6, 12 and 24 months for all subjects.
