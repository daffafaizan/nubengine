
    
      This randomized controlled trial explores the efficacy of an 8-week standing exergaming
      program in people with major neurocognitive disorder (MNCD) residing in long-term care
      facilities. Fifty inpatients with mild to moderate MNCD will be randomly assigned to three
      times weekly for eight weeks 15 min of exergaming versus watching preferred music videos. The
      Montr√©al Cognitive Assessment (MoCA), Short Physical Performance Battery (SPPB),
      Neuropsychiatric Inventory (NPI), Cornell Scale for Depression in Dementia (CSDD), Dementia
      Quality of Life (DQoL), Katz activities of daily living (ADL) and Instrumental ADL (IADL) are
      measured at baseline and post intervention.
