
    
      Double blind Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT) : the clinicians and the patents were blinded
      of the allocation group.

      A total of 137 embryo transfers were randomly allocated into either arm I (67 ) starting
      luteal support as daily IM injections of 100 mg prontogest on day of egg retrieval , or arm
      II (66) starting the same P4 dose on day of embryo transfer
