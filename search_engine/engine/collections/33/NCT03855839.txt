
    
      Poor posture have historically been blamed for pain and disability in the general population.
      A treatment-strategy that have gain popularity in recent years is the use of a posture shirt
      which is a compressive shirt designed and marketed to the general public and health service
      providers as a way to improve posture and consequently alter muscle activity, reduce
      musculoskeletal pain and discomfort. This study will investigate the effect of posture shirts
      on posture.
