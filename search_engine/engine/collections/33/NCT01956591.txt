
    
      This retrospective study aims to evaluate the medical charts of patients who were treated
      with oxybutynin for at least six months for primary hyperhidrosis. The investigators
      variables of interest are: (1) quality of life before pharmacological therapy and after six
      weeks, (2)self-perception of improvement in hyperhidrosis after six weeks of treatment and on
      last visit (whenever this occur, as long as this happens after six months), (3) side effects
      related to drug use.
