
    
      Participants will be randomized allocated to four groups: rTMS and AR group, sham rTMS and AR
      group, AR group ,and conventional physiotherapy group in current study. All participants
      underwent four weeks, 3 sessions per week, and 60 minutes per session training program. Motor
      evoked potential (MEP), Time up go test (TUG), Berg Balance Scale (BBS), Fugl-Meyer
      Assessment (FMA), Multi-directional Reach Test (MDRT),The Postural Assessment Scale for
      Stroke Patients (PASS), and Taiwanese Depression Questionnaires (TDQ) will be assessed before
      and after intervention in all participants.
