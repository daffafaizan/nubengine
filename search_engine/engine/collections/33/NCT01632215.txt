
    
      There was studied 40 patients. Group 1 patients received 600 mg of gabapentin 1 hour before
      surgery; G2 received placebo 1 hour before surgery.

      Anesthesia was regional intravenous block. Postoperative analgesia when needed was with
      acetaminophen. If needed was administered codeine. The evaluation of neuropathic pain was
      made by DN4 questionnaire. Pain intensity was assessed by numeric scale at block and after
      30min, 1h, 2h, 2wk, 4wk, 6m. It was noted the duration of analgesia, and the amount of
      acetaminophen and codeine.

      The statistical program used for analysis of the results was the Instat Graph
