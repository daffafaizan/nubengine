
    
      The study evaluates the potential of MC2-01(CAL/BDP 0.005/0.064 w/w%) Cream to induce a
      photoallergic skin reaction. Because MC2-01 Cream is formulated for topical use and has shown
      to absorb light within the range of natural sunlight, it is necessary to determine the
      potential of this product to cause a photoallergic reaction after application to the skin.
