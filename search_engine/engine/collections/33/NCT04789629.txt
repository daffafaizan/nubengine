
    
      This is a randomized controlled study, in which the 30 children with diaplegic randomly
      divide into control and study groups by excel sheat on computer, each group include 15child.
      Each child will receive laser at a frequency of three sessions per week for 12 session, in
      addition to conventional physiotherapy program, while the control group have 15 child will
      receive conventional physiotherapy. Four variables will choose to detect the effect of laser
      therapy: (1) muscle tone, (2) range of motion (ROM) , (3) gross movement,(4) poplital angle
      assessment . These values will recorde in both groups before starting treatment and after the
      treatment period and after one month of stop treatment .
