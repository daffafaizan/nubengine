
    
      A single application of an investigational patch test panel containing 11 hydrogel metal
      allergens and a corresponding patch test panel containing 11 petrolatum metal allergens will
      be applied to the upper back of human subjects to test diagnostic efficacy and safety. Patch
      test panels will be removed after being worn for approximately 48 hours. Study will require 6
      visits over the course of 21 days; patch application, patch removal and 4 patch test site
      evaluation visits at days 4, 7, 14 and 21.
