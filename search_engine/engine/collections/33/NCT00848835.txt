
    
      There are few prospective long term follow-up studies on implanted cardiac device
      (pacemaker/implantable cardioverter defibrillator (ICD)) survivability. This study will
      evaluate the long term performance (5 years) of the transvenous defibrillation leads (ENDOTAK
      ENDURANCE EZ and teh ENDOTAK RELIANCE. Data will be collected at implant, 1 and 6 months, 1,3
      and 5 years.
