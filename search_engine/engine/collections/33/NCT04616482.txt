
    
      The study is a non-experimental feasibility study to explore the acceptability, practicality
      and demand for the use of a 12-week, personalized, online program to teach and coach patients
      about therapeutic carbohydrate restriction for individuals with obesity and/or at risk for
      type 2 diabetes/with type 2 diabetes. The program is intended to enhance the usual clinical
      care provided for patients with these conditions and will allow for physicians to monitor
      patient progress through the program. Both physician and patient perspectives will be
      explored.
