
    
      The primary outcome measures were the number of oocytes retrieved, the number of oocytes
      injected, number of oocytes fertilized, fertilization rate, number of embryos and their
      quality (G1: good embryos, G2: fair embryos and G3: bad embryos), number of embryos
      transferred (ET), number of embryo sacs, embryo implantation rate, pregnancy rate and
      abortion rate for all 3 groups and pregnancy rate and abortion rate for both group (I) and
      group (II).
