
    
      Adult patients who had unilateral cochlear implantation will be included. Postural stability
      measurements will be done by the Biodex Balance System. Three preprogrammed main tests and
      their 9 subtests will be applied. Postural stability, fall index, sensory integration will be
      calculated. All tests will be done in four conditions; implant off(1), implant on(2), implant
      on music on(3), and implant off again(4).
