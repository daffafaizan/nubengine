
    
      This study is a multicenter, double-blind, randomized, parallel-group study designed to
      assess the safety and efficacy of IDP-122 Lotion in comparison with its vehicle. To be
      eligible for the study, participants must be at least 18 years of age and have a clinical
      diagnosis of moderate to severe psoriasis (defined as an IGA score of 3 or 4).
