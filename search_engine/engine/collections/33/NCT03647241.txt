
    
      The sample of this study will be chosen according the following steps:

        -  The examination of patients referred to the Department of Orthodontics at the University
           of Damascus Dental School (clinical examination, diagnostic plaster models, radiographic
           photos , external and internal oral photographs) then subjects who meet the inclusion
           criteria will be included in this trial.

        -  A random sample of 100 patients from the sampling frame will be chosen.

        -  An information sheet ( including the research objective and treatment plan in detail )
           will be given to each candidate. Then consent forms will be collected from those who
           agree to participate.

        -  A random selection of 60 patients will be performed. Then a random distribution of these
           patients to 3 groups will be done.
