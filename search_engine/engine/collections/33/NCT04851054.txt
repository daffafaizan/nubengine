
    
      DESIGN: Prospective multicentric study.

      PARTICIPATION: Colorectal Surgery Units of Hospital del Mar, Barcelona; Hospital Universitari
      Joan XXIII, Tarragona; Hospital Universitari La Fe, Valencia; Hospital Clínico San Carlos,
      Madrid; Hospital General de Granollers, Granollers; Hospital Germans Trias i Pujol, Badalona;
      Athalaia, Xarxa Assistencial Universitària de Manresa, Manresa

      STUDY SUBJECTS: 280 consecutive patients with non-metastatic colon cancer in whom a curative
      intent resection is performed.

      DETERMINATIONS: Determination of the proangiogenic cytokine VEGF in serum on the
      postoperative day four to assess its relationship with oncological results on long-term
      follow-up.
