
    
      Design:

        -  Prospective Randomized Controlled trial

        -  Population analysis based on Intention To Treat

        -  Patient and data analyzer blinded (double-blind trial)

      Outcomes observed:

        -  Primary: pain

        -  Secondaries: overall operative time, complication rate, cost-effectiveness analysis,
           cosmetic evaluation, quality of life, CO2 consumption

      Nbr of patients included: 260

      Port used: Triport, Olympus
