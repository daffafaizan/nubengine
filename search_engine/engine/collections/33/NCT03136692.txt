
    
      The study team analyzed de-identified user data collected by Lose It! for the purposes of
      investigating behavioral aspects of weight loss and maintenance, including engagement with
      the platform; user-reported data on diet, exercise, and other behaviors; data generated by
      Lose It! describing macronutrient or total calorie intake; periodicity of weight-related
      actions; user-reported demographic information; and any data from linked applications within
      Lose It!. All data was anonymized and exchanged as limited data sets.
