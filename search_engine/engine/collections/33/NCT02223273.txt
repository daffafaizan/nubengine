
    
      Study Objective: The purpose of this study is to evaluate a multifaceted strategy to increase
      evidence based therapies for patients with acute ischemic stroke.

      Study Population; Patients with suspected stroke or transient ischemic attack (TIA) with
      symptoms lasting up to 24h hours.

      Quality Improvement Multifaceted Intervention: The strategy includes a simulation based team
      training, case manager, check lists, reminders and educational material.
