
    
      This prospective interventional study included 93 patients with JIA and TMJ disorders at the
      start of functional orthodontic therapy. Children were prospectively followed in the 24
      months of treatment and completed the Oral Health Impact Profile (OHIP-14) before treatment
      (T0), and at 6 months (T1), 12 months (T2), and 24 months (T3) after the start of therapy.
