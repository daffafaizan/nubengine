
    
      The project is a prospective study designed to characterize behaviour and its
      psycho-physiological correlates of participants with ASD (children and adults) in comparison
      with subjects without ASD matched in age during blood test in common practice.

      The aim is to analyse in an automated and standardised way video recording during a blood
      test performed as part of routine follow-up care for people presenting ASD (children and
      adults), qualitative data (type of emotion) and quantitative (intensity)

        1. Emotional facial reactions

        2. Behavioural reactions (video recording of participants' movements)

        3. Physiological reactions (wireless smart watch worn on the wrist)

        4. Psychological reactions (self and hetero questionaires) to characterize the signs of
           pain and anxiety associated with this situation)
