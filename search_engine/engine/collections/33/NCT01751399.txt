
    
      The study is conducted in 4 groups, based on the Child-Pugh classification of hepatic
      impairment as follows:

      Group 1: Participants with normal hepatic function (Control); Group 2: Participants with mild
      hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh class A); Group 3: Participants with moderate hepatic
      impairment (Child-Pugh class B); and Group 4: Participants with severe hepatic impairment
      (Child-Pugh class C).
