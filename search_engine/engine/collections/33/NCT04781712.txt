
    
      This is prospective, feasible, parallel study in post-operative patients with breast cancer
      and during treatment. Disability of Arm, Shoulder, Hands (DASH), Physical activity data
      (IPAQ-SF), Physical measurement such as hand grip strength, body mass index (BMI), muscle
      mass, and differences in arm circumference, PRO-CTCAE, Distress Thermometer, needs and
      satisfaction in app, personal health record of app (exercise, daily step count, etc.) Measure
      will be evaluated on 1-month, 2-month, 4-month, 6-month, 9-month, and 12-month after POD.
