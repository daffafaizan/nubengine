
    
      This will be a retrospective cohort study comparing women obtaining an abortion in New Mexico
      and living in Texas and women obtaining an abortion in New Mexico and living in New Mexico in
      two different time periods (time period #1 is from January 1 2012-December 31 2012 and time
      period #2 is from May 1 2014-April 30 2015. Our exposure is a state in which a woman lives in
      (New Mexico or Texas). Our intervention is the change in Texan laws occurring from July
      2013-January 2014.
