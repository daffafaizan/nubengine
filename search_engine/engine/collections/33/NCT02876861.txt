
    
      The purpose of this study is to see if getting chemotherapy with Gemcitabine and Cisplatin
      (Gemcitabine, 1250mg/m2, d1 d8, Cisplatin, 75mg/m2, d1-d3, 21d, 2-4 cycles) for 2-4 cycles
      can help shrink the tumor before undergoing surgery and improves the overall survival for
      high-grade upper tract urothelial carcinoma.
