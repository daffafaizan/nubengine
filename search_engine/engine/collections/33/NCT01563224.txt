
    
      After informed consent and screening participants will attend for 3 study days. On each of
      these there will be a set of baseline measurements including EEG, saccadic eye movements,
      vital signs, motor co-ordination task, subjective visual analogue ratings and questionnaires
      and blood sample. After dosing these measurements will be repeated at intervals during the
      day up to 6 hours after dosing. Participants will be allowed home after a health check.
