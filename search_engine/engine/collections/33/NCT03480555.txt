
    
      Patients will be recruited on day 1-2 of ICU admission. On day 5, patients will be randomized
      into one of the two intervention arms;

      Replenish Protein group:

      Patients randomized to this group will receive 2 g of protein/kg/day (acceptable range 1.8 -
      2.2 g of protein/kg/day) for day 6-14.

      Standard Protein group:

      Patients randomized to this group will receive protein at 0.8 - 1.0 g of protein/kg/day for
      day 6-14
