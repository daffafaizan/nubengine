
    
      This study intends to establish a system for screening of children with congenital heart
      disease,with the combination of cardiac auscultation , the test of oxygen saturation, the use
      of cloud computing and data base. The investigators decide to do this project from three main
      points.1:How to establish a data-base system? 2:How to get heart sound signal? 3.How to
      analyze so many signals?
