
    
      Patients will be randomized into the following groups:

        -  Group 1: Art therapy as supportive care in 6 consecutive sessions of cancer treatments
           with or without additional supportive care

        -  Group 2: 6 consecutive sessions of cancer treatments with or without additional
           supportive care.

      Symptom assessment with the Edmonton Symptom Assessment System (ESAS) is done before and
      after each treatment session for each patient.

      The FACT-G questionnaire on the quality of life at the beginning and the end of the care for
      the two groups (group benefiting from the art-therapy and group not benefiting from the
      art-therapy) and a questionnaire on the contribution of art therapy, at the end of care, for
      the participants of the art-therapy group.
