
    
      This is an observational post-authorization study on a group of patients undergoing
      microsurgery on the Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery and Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery
      Service at Ramon y Cajal Hospital, Madrid for processes that require reconstruction with a
      flap microvascular. A group of 67 patients treated according protocol postoperative
      prevention of deep vein thrombosis with Bemiparina will be compared with data collected
      retrospectively from a group of 134 patients treated previously with an earlier protocol with
      enoxaparin as main antithrombotic agent.
