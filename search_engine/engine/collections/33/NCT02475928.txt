
    
      Taste disorders are symptomatologies in patients with cirrhosis, these have a direct effect
      in modification of food consumption, increasing weight loss, anorexia and malnutrition, which
      impacts in decompensation rates and mortality.

      It has been proposed that zinc deficiency is one of the causes for development of taste
      disorders. The aim of this study is to describe the presence of taste disorders in patients
      with cirrhosis and evaluate the effect of zinc supplementation in treatment of taste
      disorders, and in progression of chronic liver disease.
