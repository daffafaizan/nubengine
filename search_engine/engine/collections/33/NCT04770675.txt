      divided into an 80%/20% split (training and testing). It is a wish to obtain a spread of
      videos coming from across Europe, preferable from Germany, France and Denmark, with up to 800
      videos coming from Denmark

      The patient population indicated are patients indicated for full airway bronchoscopy. Of this
      group it is the aim to enrol the following:

        1. Patients with obstructing tumors (when tumor blocks the view of the camera) - no more
           than 5% (120 patients) of studies

        2. Bronchoscopies on indication of haemoptysis (active bleeding) - no more than 5% (120
           patients) of studies

        3. Patients with stents or valves - no more than 5% (120 patients) of studies
           bronchoscopies.

        4. Patients with former lung operations, partial or full resections - no more than 2% (48
           patients).
