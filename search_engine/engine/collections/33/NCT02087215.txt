
    
      Local treatment of diabetic foot ulcers:

        1. Classification of International Working Group of the Diabetic Foot (IWGDF)

        2. pre-treatment measurements including diameter and area

        3. pre-treatment laboratory values including fasting glucose, hemoglobin, hemoglobin A1c,
           leucocyte count and c-reactive protein

        4. pre-treatment wound culture

        5. treatment either by placebo gel containing polymer of carbopol ultrex (1%) or by
           formulation gel: addition of borate as sodium penta boric acid pentahydrate 3% (w/v) and
           two different copolymer as pluronic block namely F68 2% (w/v) and f127 2% (w/v).

        6. post-treatment measurements and values
