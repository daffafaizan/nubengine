
    
      From 166 first time mother-infant recruited, 128 completed the trail. Intervention group,
      mother-infant dyads got a month long weekly based training in small workshop groups about
      nutrition, feeding and parenting when infants were 4-6 months old. training was given by a
      highly experience pediatric dietitian and social worker. Thereafter, internet-based support
      continued until infants reached 12 months. The control group received customary support via
      municipal mother-child health clinics. Mealtime interactions were videotaped at home setting
      at 12 months and were evaluated using the Chatoor feeding scale by blinded (to group)
      viewers.
