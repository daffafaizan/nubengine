
    
      A phase IIIB randomized, open-label, multicenter, parallel-arm study to evaluate the
      short-term safety and tolerability of the abacavir/lamivudine fixed-dose combination tablet
      administered once-daily or the separate abacavir and lamivudine tablets administered
      twice-daily, as part of a three or four-drug regimen, in antiretroviral naive HIV-1 infected
      subjects.
