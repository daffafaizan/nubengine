
    
      Patients in fasting arm will practice partial fasting during 3 days (D-1, D1 (D day of
      chemotherapy) and D2) following a food guide during 4 chemotherapy sessions. Patients in
      usual alimentation arm will continue to take food as usual practice.

      In each arm, patients will be interviewed at each chemotherapy sessions about nausea and
      vomiting (number and intensity of nausea and vomiting, treatments taken). Denutrition,
      quality of life improvement, compliance to partial fasting, treatment response will be
      evaluate too.
