
    
      The investigators aim to report ICU mortality for patients according to BMI category and to
      describe the detailed ICU clinical course of illness :

        -  Risk factors for ICU mortality : age, sex, baseline comorbidities

        -  Causes of death

        -  Risk factors for use of non invasive mechanical ventilation

        -  Non-fatal outcomes

        -  Ventilator-free days

        -  ICU length of stay.
