
    
      Endoscopic submucosal dissection (ESD) is a technique for endoscopic tissue resection that
      enables endoscopists to control the depth and direction of diathermic knife
      incisions.Recently, the number of reports in which ESD has been successfully used to treat
      colorectal tumors has increased. But the complatios with colorectal ESD were higher than
      esophagus and stomach ESD.We design the clincal trial to investigate the efficacy of
      transanal tube drainage in colorectal ESD.
