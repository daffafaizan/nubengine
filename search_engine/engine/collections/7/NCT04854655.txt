
    
      3 groups of 45 patients will receive class II restorations by using calcium and phosphate
      releasing hybrid restorative material (Activa Presto), fluoride releasing hybrid restorative
      material (Giomer), and conventional nanofilled resin composite. Then these restorations
      evaluated at 24 hours after placement, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months and 12 months according
