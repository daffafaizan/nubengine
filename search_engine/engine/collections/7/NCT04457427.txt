
    
      15 patients undergoing tracheostomy for failure to wean will be randomized into one of 3
      cohorts (5 in each cohort): Cohort A: tracheostomy with no intervention; Cohort B:
      tracheostomy with DPS implantation and immediate stimulation and monitoring and Cohort C:
      tracheostomy with DPS implantation with monitoring followed with stimulation on day 5.
