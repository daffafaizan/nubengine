
    
      Patients with MS between 0-5,5 score according to the Extended Disability Status Scale (EDSS)
      and healthy individuals of similar age and sex to patients will be included in the study. The
      muscle strength, joint position sensation, gait, and fatigue will be evaluated once.

      The investigators will use descriptive statistics and t-tests to compare demographic
      characteristics between groups and for the categorical variables chi-square. Effect of the
      group (MS patients or healthy controls), condition (Single task and dual-task conditions),
      and group Ã— condition interaction will be compared using two-way repeated-measures ANOVA.
