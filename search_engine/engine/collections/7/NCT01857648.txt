
    
      This is an intervention study conducted with 176 people attended by two Primary Health Units
      in the city of SÃ£o Paulo, Brazil. The intervention group received home visits of Community
      Health Workers trained on counseling on physical activity promotion. These workers received a
      course of 12 hours aimed to improve their knowledge and attitude about physical activity
