
    
      The study is a 2 x 2 design. The first factor thought to be of relevance for the efficacy is
      the whether the content is selected by the participants themselves or by a therapist. The
      second factor consists of two levels: regular, continuous weekly support from a therapist or
      guidance on demand by a health care team consisting of a physician, a nurse, a clinical
      psychologist, an IT-technician and the principal investigator of the study.
