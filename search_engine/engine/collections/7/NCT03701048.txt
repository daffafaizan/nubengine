
    
      Pfannenstiel incision will be done under general or spinal anesthesia. The abdomen will be
      opened and fetus and placenta will be delivered. Then the abdomen will be closed. In Group 1
      rectus muscle will be closed with interrupted sutures. In group 2 rectus muscle will not be
      closed. Postoperative pain scores, distance between the rectus muscles and bowel functions
      will be evaluated.
