
    
      This is a randomized controlled trial in patients with globus. All participants will be
      allocated into 3 groups (16 subjects/group) : Proton pump inhibitors (miracid®) , anxiolytics
      drug (Deanxit®) and psycho-education. The study duration is 4 weeks long.

      Data regarding clinical severity and quality of life of all participants will be collected at
      the beginning and at the end of treatment.
