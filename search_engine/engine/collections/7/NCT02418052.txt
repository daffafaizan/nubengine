
    
      After the cervical esophagogastric anastomoses is completed and the skin incision is closed,
      the patient's occiput will be lifted, and then the neck will be maintained in flexing
      position by an assistant. The underside of the chin will be fixed to the anterior chest wall
      with two stout nylon sutures by the surgeon. The neck will be fixed in the neutral flexing
      position for 7 to 10 days after surgery.
