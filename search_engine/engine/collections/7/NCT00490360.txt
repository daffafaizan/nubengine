
    
      Neodajuvant chemotherapy with gemcitabine / cisplatin is applied to patients with resectable
      cancer of pancreatic head. Two cycles of chemotherapy are given on day 1 and 15 each. After
      restaging excludes disease progression, a standard Whipple procedure is performed.

      Staging and restaging procedures include abdominal CT, diagnostic laparoscopy, PET/CT, tumor
      markers (CEA, CA 19-9) and assessment of the quality of life by the QLQ-30.
