
    
      The study sample comprised 70 Caucasian Brazilian individuals with normal occlusion and at
      least four of Andrew's six keys. Maxillary and mandibular dental casts were digitized (3D)
      and the images were analyzed by Delcam Power SHAPET 2010 software. Landmarks on the lingual
      surface of teeth were selected to determine the shape and size of dental arches.
