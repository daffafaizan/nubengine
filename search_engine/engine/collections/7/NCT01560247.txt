
    
      To determine the revascularization rate, clinical efficacy and safety in a "real-world
      setting" of the CE-marked MindFrame System in ischemic stroke patients with thrombotic
      neurovascular occlusions caused by an embolus deemed appropriate for endovascular treatment.
      Target vessels include the basilar, internal carotid and middle cerebral (M1 and M2 segments)
      arteries.
