
    
      The aim of this study is to evaluate the effects of magnesium sulfate as an adjuvant to
      bupivacaine in suprascapular nerve block on the duration and quality of postoperative
      analgesia following shoulder arthroscopy, postoperative pain VAS scores, intraoperative
      fentanyl requirements, sedation scores, respiratory depression, postoperative 24 hours
      cumulative morphine consumption.
