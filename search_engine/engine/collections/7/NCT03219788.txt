
    
      The investigators designed this study to examine the effect of different small doses of
      Remifentanil on the incidence of cough reflex during emergence from anesthesia in obese
      patients undergoing bariatric surgery. The investigators will study if these small doses will
      delay the recovery of the patient or not. The other expected side effects will be observed
      eg. Respiratory depression. Time to the first painkiller required will be registered
