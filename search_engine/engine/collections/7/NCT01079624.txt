
    
      Effects of GIP and GLP-1 on gastric emptying, appetite control and insulin release in
      relation to glucose levels in the bloodstream. Infusions of GIP and GLP-1 are given under
      simultaneous gastric emptying study and appetite questionnaires and blood sampling for
      analysis of insulin, glucose, and other gut hormones such as glucagon, ghrelin, and PYY, as
      well as those administered, GIP and GLP-1.
