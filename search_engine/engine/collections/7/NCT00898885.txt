
    
      Methods: For this study we analyzed histological samples of oral mucosa of 10 patients with
      clinical diagnosis of GVHD, which have areas of epithelium, and salivary gland. The
      histopathological aspects were observed by the author of this work and his advisor, so
      blinded by applying the criteria for Horn and the Consensus. We consistency between the two
      classifications obtained and histological correlation with the clinical classification of
      GVHD (Akpek, 2001) and the curve of survival.
