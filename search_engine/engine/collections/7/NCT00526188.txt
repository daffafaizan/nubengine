
    
      Adult Chinese patients with known focal or suspected liver lesions, referred for magnetic
      resonance imaging (MRI) for further diagnostic work-up, who have undergone or are scheduled
      to undergo a defined SOR procedure, within one month before or after the study MRI.

      The data for the Secondary Outcome Measure "Lesion size and location" has been documented but
      not analyzed. The data for the Secondary Outcome Measure "Safety" are reflected in the
      Adverse Event section.
