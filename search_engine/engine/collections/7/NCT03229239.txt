
    
      Keratoconus is a corneal disease with the progressive thinning of the central corneal stroma.
      The main treatment methods are corneal transplantation, corneal collagen cross-linking, etc.
      The effect of corneal collagen cross-linking is limited, and the lack of donor cornea is
      really a very big problem. In this pilot project, investigators would like to study whether
      corneal stroma implantation can increase the biomechanics of the cornea, which can, to some
      extent, delay the progress of keratoconus. This will allowed us to determine if useful data
      can be obtained, and if so, the donor cornea can be greatly saved.
