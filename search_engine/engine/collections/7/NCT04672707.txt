
    
      The purpose is to study the pharmacokinetic characteristics and safety of bromine hexane
      hydrochloride in healthy adults after oral administration of bromine hexane hydrochloride
      tablets. Then to explore the mechanism related to the role of transmembrane serine proteases
      (TMPRSSs) through the study, and to provide the basis for the clinical rational
      administration of bromine hexane hydrochloride tablets.
