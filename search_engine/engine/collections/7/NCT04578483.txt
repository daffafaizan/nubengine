
    
      This is a prospective, observational, cohort study. Patients undergoing elective surgery will
      be invited to the study. The written informed consent will be obtained prior to
      participation. Demographic data, underlying condition, surgical procedures, consumption of
      anesthetics and analgesics, analgesic methods, postoperative complications, pain intensity
      and life quality will be collected from medical history and by questionnaires.
