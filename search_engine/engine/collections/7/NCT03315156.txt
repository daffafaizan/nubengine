
    
      Methods:

      Taste assessment will be done using Electrogustometer (EGM). Tongue stimulation will be
      performed using low currents of up to 100uA. Using the protocol as specified in the EGM user
      manual.

      Population: 40 Unilateral AOM patients, aged 8-40. Without chronic otitis media, pregnancy or
      impaired judgement.
