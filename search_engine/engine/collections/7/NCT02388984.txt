
    
      Compound danshen dripping pills is a kind of traditional Chinese medicine(TCM), consists of
      Danshen (Radix Salviae Miltiorrhizae), Sanqi (Radix Notoginseng) and borneol. This study is
      being conducted to evaluate the efficacy and safety of compound danshen dripping pills in
      patients with diabetic retinopathy(Syndrome Of Qi-Stagnation and Blood Stasis), when compared
      with placebo.
