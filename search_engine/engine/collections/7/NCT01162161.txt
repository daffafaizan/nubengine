
    
      There will be three groups of patients:

        1. hydrostatic pulmonary edema

        2. toxic pulmonary edema

        3. not ventilated patients without any pulmonary edema

      Samples of a routine bronchoalveolar lavage and EDTA-blood will be analysed in respect of
      sphingolipids, ceramide, sphingomyelin-phosphodiesterase and several cytokines.
