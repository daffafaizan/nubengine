
    
      Our objective is to determine the metabolic availability of Tryptophan in white maize. Seven
      young, healthy, male adults will receive graded levels (13.2, 26.4, 39.7, 52.9) of tryptophan
      requirement of 3.78 mg/kg/d as a crystalline amino acid (AA) mixture and a porridge of white
      cornmeal protein respectively.
