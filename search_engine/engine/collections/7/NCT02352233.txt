
    
      Primary outcome:

      To determine the safety and feasibility of the retro view during colonoscopy using RETROVIEW
      colonoscope. Safety was measured by the number of bleeding or perforation rates as adverse
      event during the procedure. Feasibility was measured by the number of cases that is possible
      to achieve a retroflexion in the colon Secondary outcome: withdrawal phase time and total
      procedure time.
