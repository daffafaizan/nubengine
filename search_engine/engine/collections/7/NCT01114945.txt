
    
      Hypothesis: the use of a video laryngoscope will improve the glottic view compared to direct
      laryngoscopy, and secondarily, use of the video laryngoscope will reduce the time required to
      achieve successful tracheal intubation in patients undergoing bariatric surgery. (Weight loss
      surgery). The three types of video laryngoscopy devices include; the Verethon GlideScope, LMA
      McGrath and Karl-Storz Video-Mac.
