
    
      Children will be given a battery of tests to determine the validity of these measures for
      children with severe hand burns. The tests include:

        -  Pinch and grip strength;

        -  Stereognosis;

        -  Speed of manipulation using the Nine Hole Peg Test, Box & Blocks Test, and the Moberg
           Pickup Test;

        -  Measurement of Range of Motion of the wrist and fingers.
