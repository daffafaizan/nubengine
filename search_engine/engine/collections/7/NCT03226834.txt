
    
      Patients are randomly allocated in two groups:

        -  control group: patients are asked to choose one of the U sequence among the preselected
           music style proposed by the medical device MUSIC CARE

        -  test group: patients are asked to prepare their own play-list containing their favourite
           songs

      Anxiety is assessed before and after a 20 min-musicotherapy session by using the State-Trait
      Anxiety Inventory (STAI) for anxiogenic situation.

      Other parameters are measured such as blood pressure, cardiac frequency, level of pain after
      surgery, care stay duration, potent post-surgery complications.
