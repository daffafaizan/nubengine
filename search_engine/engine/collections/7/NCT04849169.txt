
    
      A prospective, multicenter, single-arm clinical study. The study will be conducted at up to
      eight investigational sites in the US and will enroll up to 30 participants. The population
      for this investigation is adult participants who suffer a perforation of the coronary artery
      during a percutaneous coronary intervention requiring management of hemorrhage while
      providing distal perfusion until definite treatment is determined.
