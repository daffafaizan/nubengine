
    
      The study is consisted of two parts; First, observational study of gastric cancer patients
      admitted to hospital, diagnosing the presence of delirium or subsyndromal delirium and
      collecting patient data, including assessment, self-report surveys, and electronic medical
      records.

      Second, Designing a guideline for treating delirium patients in Korea using the study data
      and reviewing other studies, guidelines.
