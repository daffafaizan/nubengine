
    
      This trial is aiming to answer a clinical question whether in patients with asymptomatic
      necrotic teeth, the use of triple antibiotic paste as intracanal medication with an
      anti-inflammatory drug, compared to a calcium hydroxide, reduce postoperative pain and
      intracanal bacteria or not? trial design will be Randomized, controlled, double blinded,
      unicenter, parallel, two-arm, superiority trial with 1:1 allocation ratio.
