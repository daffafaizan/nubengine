
    
      To evaluate treatment adherence and blood pressure reduction, through the HiperCross App,
      between two groups of hypertensive patients in a Hypertension Public Ambulatory in Porto
      Alegre.

      The investigators hope to identify better adherence to treatment, as well as a significant
      reduction in blood pressure, all of these results will be better evaluated using the
      HiperCross application.
