
    
      Telemedicine system mainly contains a comprehensive eye examination electric-records
      (E-record), which can transfer the fundus pictures from rural hospitals to professional
      Graders (golden standard for a diagnose of Diabetes Retinopathy (DR) and Diabetes Macular
      Edema(DME)). In Zhongshan Ophthalmic Center (ZOC). By this system, the investigators can
      evaluate the capacity of rural eye doctors about the diagnosis of DR/DME, and give proper
      suggestions for the treatment and revisit plan.
