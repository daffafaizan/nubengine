
    
      The "MiCo - Mirena or conventional medical treatment for menorrhagia" study consist of two
      parts, MiCo Asia-Pacific and MiCo MA0901 (Rest of World).

      Data from both parts will be analysed in separate pools as well as in a global pool. The
      trial alias are IMPACT Nos. 14697, 14536 (NCT01085487).
