
    
      Participants were divided into 2 groups of approximately equal size. In the first group,
      participants received a Capsaicin 8% patch applied for 30 minutes to the feet; in the second
      group, participants received a placebo patch applied for 30 minutes to the feet. Participants
      were involved in the study for approximately 12 weeks and have visited the clinic
      approximately 6 times.
