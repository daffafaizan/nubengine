
    
      The investigators conducted this prospective study in women with twins pregnancy planned for
      trial of vaginal delivery and gave birth between January 2014 and December 2019, in the
      Department of obstetrics and Gynaecology of the Sabah Maternity Hospital, Sabah Specialized
      Area, Kuwait. The Sabah Maternity Hospital is a tertiary care hospital for the country of
      Kuwait with approximately 12500 deliveries per year. The study protocol was approved by the
      Ethics Committee.
