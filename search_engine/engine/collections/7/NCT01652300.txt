
    
      In this clinical trial study,140 pregnant women receiving care from Khaje Rabi Regional
      Health Center in Mashhad city who were members of a community based multi-center were chosen
      by systematic clustering methods and randomly allocated into test and control groups. After
      intervention, two questionnaires were completed for demographic and pregnancy details of the
      participants. Knowledge, health belief and health behavior were assessed before intervention.
      The test group had two educational sessions lasting 1 hour, focused on oral and dental health
      and especially on oral and dental health during pregnancy. women in the control group
      received no education. Knowledge, health belief and health behavior were assessed immediately
      after and one month after intervention of all participants. Analysis of data was done using
      SPSS, t-test and chi-square tests.
