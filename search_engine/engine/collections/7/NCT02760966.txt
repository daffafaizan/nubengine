
    
      Randomized clinical trial, which objective is to assess the effectiveness of 70ยบ alcohol
      compared with neutral soap on the cord separation time of a selection of newborns. 34
      newborns admitted to the maternity ward of the hospital have participated. The study is
      divided into two phases, hospital and home. The first stage is located in the hospital, where
      the patients who will become part of the study are chosen. After receiving the informed
      letting of the legal guardian the second part of this phase is started; which aim is to
      explain the steps to be followed to make a correct cleaning cord technique. The home phase
      consists of being in contact with the family through Whatsapp on the 5th day from birth, the
      day of the cord fall and 5 days after this fall. Making some simple questions with simple
      answers such as yes or no and with the possibility, or not, of sending a picture of the cord
      state in each follow-up visit.
