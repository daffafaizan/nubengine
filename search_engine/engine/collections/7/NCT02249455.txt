
    
      This study is carried out to determine the effectiveness of acapella and expiration in
      lateral position with open glottis to clear the secretion in COPD patients. The sessions will
      be carried twice daily for twenty minutes. The outcome measures are values of pre and post
      intervention fev1/fvc and breathlessness cough sputum scale. Duration of the study will be
      for five days.
