
    
      The study was designed as a randomized, controlled, clinical trial. patients who were
      undergoing to receive chemotherapy were divided into three groups: Group I: was given
      conventional treatment. Group II: was given topical oral gel of chamomile. Group III: was
      given topical oral gel of chamomile in combination with the conventional treatment.

      All patients have clinically evaluated at the start of the chemotherapy and three weeks later
      for pain and oral mucositis severity.
