
    
      This is a prospective, non-interventional, multicenter study in patients with acute pulmonary
      embolism admitted to Cardiology, Emergency and Internal Medicine Departments in Italy.

      The aim of the study is to assess contemporary management strategies in patients with acute
      pulmonary embolism in terms of diagnosis, risk stratification, hospitalization and treatment
      in Italy and assess

        1. their association with in-hospital and 30-day mortality and

        2. their adherence to current guidelines of the European Society of Cardiology (ESC).
