
    
      The aim of the study is to develop and validate quantitative interactive attention evaluation
      criteria by using games on touchscreen and or eye tracking in patients with intellectual
      disability. Tests will have to be suitable whatever severity and etiology of intellectual
      disability. Tests have to evaluate attention and concentration troubles in intellectual
      disability.Once validated, tests could be used as outcome measures in clinical trials and as
      cognitive reeducation tools in intellectual disability.
