
    
      The investigators developed an app (called ChemotherApp) for Android that automatically sends
      text messages with homecare orientation to cancer patients in intervention group. The main
      features of this app are: to register information such as name, number of cellphone, gender,
      and chemotherapy schedule of the patient. Other feature is to register the orientation that
      will be sent to patients by text messages. Daily, the app send a registered orientation to
      each patient.
