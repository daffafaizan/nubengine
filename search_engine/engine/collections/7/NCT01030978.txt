
    
      Primary Aim: (1) To determine whether youth randomized to the Bright Bodies (BB) Healthy
      Lifestyle Program have lower 2-hr plasma glucose levels than those randomized to standard
      lifestyle counseling (control) after 6 months.

      Secondary Aims: (2) To determine if greater conversions from IGT to normal glucose tolerance
      (NGT) and less development of T2DM occurs for those in the BB vs Control Group; (3)To
      determine if there is improvement in anthropometric/metabolic parameters in BB vs Control
      Group; and (4) To determine if there is an improvement in psychosocial and family dynamic
      outcomes of the child in BB vs Control Group.
