
    
      The specific aims of this sub-study are:

      Specific Aim #1: Work with game development team to develop a web-based version of
      smokeSCREEN.

      Specific Aim # 2: Build a pre-post survey into the smokeSCREEN game to anonymously collect
      survey information on players' perspectives, beliefs, behaviors around smoking.

      Specific Aim # 3: Collaborate with youth programs to pilot test the implementation of the new
      web-based version of smokeSCREEN into youth programs.
