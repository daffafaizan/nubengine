
    
      Background: Diarrhea is a medical condition characterized by frequent stool, more than 3
      times (and more than 5-7 times in newborns) within the last 24 hours, and/or loose stool. The
      fecal matter is liquid, its daily mass exceeds 200 g per day, and water content reaches 95%.

      Objectives: To demonstrate the antidiarrheal efficacy of colloidal silicon dioxide in tablet
      dosage form (Carbowhite) in patients with acute diarrhea.

      Methods: A randomized clinical trial, double-blind, placebo-controlled, multi-center.
