
    
      In this balanced, randomized, and controlled prospective study, 100 pregnant ladies at full
      term diabetic patients will be admitted to Minia University hospital. The patients will be
      randomly assigned into two groups. The intervention group will receive PRP at surgery,
      whereas the control group will receive the usual care. All patients will be evaluated at
      baseline, one week, two weeks, four weeks and eight week after the cesarean section.
