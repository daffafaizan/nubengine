
    
      Specific Objective

        1. To develop and validate a diabetes mobile-app (MyD-App) for patients with uncontrolled
           Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus.

        2. To measure the effectiveness of a diabetes mobile application for improving knowledge,
           compliance and control using the newly developed and validated MyD-App among patients
           with uncontrolled Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus.

      Hypothesis

        1. There is an increased in patient's knowledge on diabetes after MyD-App usage.

        2. There is an increased in patient's compliance on medication and treatment after MyD-App
           usage.

        3. There is a diabetes control improvement (HbA1C < 6.5% or HbA1C reduction â‰¥ 1%) among
           Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus patients in Kedah after MyD-App usage.
