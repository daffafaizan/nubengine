
    
      Subjects first undergo an 18F-FDG test, followed by 68Ga-NOTA-FAPI04 or 18F-NOTA-FAPI04 test
      in groups. The purpose of the comparison of the maximum of standardized uptake value(SUVmax )
      between 68Ga/18F-NOTA-FAPI04 and 18F-FDG PET/CT for the target lesion of colorectal cancer is
      to investigate the superiority of FAPI in diagnosis of colorectal cancer.
