
    
      The purpose of this study is to determine if stratification of individuals based on responses
      to a Carbohydrate Intolerance Questionnaire (CIQ), genetic screening, and/or DNA methylation
      affects weight loss success and/or health outcomes of women following the Curves moderate
      carbohydrate, high protein, low fat diet (30% carbohydrate, 45% protein, 25% fat) or the
      Curves carbohydrate restricted, high protein, moderate fat diet (20% carbohydrate, 45%
      protein, 35% fat).
