
    
      The purpose of the study is to demonstrate the long term safety and effectiveness of the
      Ovation™/Ovation Prime™ Abdominal Stent Graft System for the endovascular treatment of
      infrarenal abdominal aortic aneurysms in a post-approval environment.

      The clinical objective of the study is to evaluate the long term safety and effectiveness of
      the Ovation™/Ovation Prime™ Abdominal Stent Graft System assessed at 5 years through freedom
      from Aneurysm-Related Mortality.

      Additionally, this study will evaluate the physician training program for modifications based
      on IDE and post-approval experience.
