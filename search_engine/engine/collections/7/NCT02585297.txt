
    
      Prospective non interventional study in the Department of Life Sciences, Bar-Ilan University
      and IVF Unit at Meir Hospital.

      The control group consisting of 50 semen samples from men with no sperm problems and 50
      vaginal samples from their partners.

      The study group consisting of 50 semen samples from men with male fertility problems and 50
      vaginal samples from their partners.

      The semen taken from the remains after fertilization of the IVF laboratory. (Liquid otherwise
      routinely thrown).

      Vaginal samples taken from women undergoing IVF treatment under anesthesia after informed
      consent.

      Enrollment also collect the following data: age, race, chronic illnesses, past surgeries and
      former fertility treatments. Data will be collected through anamnesis of the patient or the
      medical file after consent.
