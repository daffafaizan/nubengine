
    
      The purpose of the study is to investigate the impact of co-morbidities, history, and
      allergen tests on the outcome of allergen provocation tests.

      Subjects are children aged 0-18 years old investigated for allergy to foods or antibiotics.
      Data entered in the database are demographics, co-morbidities, symptoms, SPT, specific IgE
      and details of the allergen provocations.
