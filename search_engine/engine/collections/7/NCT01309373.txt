
    
      This is a retrospective, cross-sectional, non-interventional observational, cohort
      epidemiological study conducted in the outpatient mental health facility setting in patients
      with schizophrenia. The inclusion of two patient cohorts is contemplated, recruited in mental
      health facilities. One cohort will involve patients estimated to have inadequate adherence to
      antipsychotic treatment, while the other will consist of patients with adequate adherence to
      such treatment. N/A
