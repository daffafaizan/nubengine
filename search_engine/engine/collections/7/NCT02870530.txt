
    
      in this study the investigators aim to test a new technique for gastric bypass as a treatment
      for morbid obesity which is division-less gastric bypass in comparison to mini-gastric
      bypass. in this study 50 patients with morbid obesity will included in the study , randomly
      will divided into 2 group each, group will include 25 patients. group 1 will operated with
      division-less gastric bypass, while group 2 will operated by min-gastric by pass.
