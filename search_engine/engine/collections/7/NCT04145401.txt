
    
      The objectives of this study are:

        1. To estimate the individual component survivorship of EVOLUTION® Revision Tibia,
           EVOLUTION® Revision CCK Femur, and EVOLUTION® CCK Tibial Insert.

        2. To find out the cumulative incidence of component revision of each component in this
           combination.

        3. To find out the functional outcome scores at early, midterm, and long-term follow-up.
