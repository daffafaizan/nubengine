
    
      This pilot study is a controlled and randomized clinical trial (simple ER) with two groups in
      parallel, comparing the combination of a multi-component therapeutic exercise protocol (ME)
      versus vestibular exercises (VPH) in over 70 years. The trial was approved by the Ethics
      Committee on Research with code 2410-N-19 and the study was conducted in accordance with the
      Helsinki Declaration.
