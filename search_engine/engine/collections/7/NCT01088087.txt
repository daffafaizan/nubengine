
    
      We randomly sort the participant as placebo group and colostrum group. Each group consists of
      17 people. and the duration of our study for one participant is 3 weeks. (Participant takes
      colostrum for 3 weeks.)

      When a person gets leaky gut syndrome, his or her endotoxin and lactulose/mannitol ratio (L/M
      ratio, in urine) can elevate.

      So We will compare the value of those two parameters and LFT. etc. after the administration
      of colostrum or placebo.
