
    
      Patients randomized to one of the treatment groups: 1) SRT with porcine preparation -
      poractant alfa; 2) SRT with bovine preparation - beractant. Continuous monitoring and
      simultaneous recordeding of 1) saturation (SpO2) and heart rate (HR) measured with pulse
      oximetry, cerebral tissue oxygenation measured with near-infrared spectroscopy,
      amplitude-integrated electroencephalography.
