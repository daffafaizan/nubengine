
    
      120 patients with chronic pure tone tinnitus over 6 months were randomly divided into 6
      groups, one group was given auditory stimulation, one group was given somatosensory
      stimulation, one group was given auditory stimulation + somatosensory stimulation, one group
      was given auditory stimulation + vestibular stimulation, the last group was given auditory
      stimulation + somatosensory stimulation + vestibular stimulation.
