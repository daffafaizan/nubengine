
    
      The purpose of this clinical trial is for assessing the survival benefit of extended
      lymphadenectomy and nerve clearance versus standard lymphadenectomy in
      pancreaticoduodenectomy of pancreatic adenocarcinoma. And we will focus on the R0 resection
      using LEEPP(Leeds Pathology Protocol) methods to check whether the tumor is eradicated or
      not.
