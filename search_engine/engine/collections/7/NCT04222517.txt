
    
      Design of a simple blind randomized controlled trial study. The total number of subjects was
      66. In the main group (A) - 33, in the other group (B) - 33. Operation - sublay retromuscular
      using prolene mesh implant. In group A Hemoblock 15 ml in the retromuscular and 15 ml in the
      subcutaneous spaces were used. Wounds were drained by vacuum-suction drainage. Postoperative
      monitoring of early ultrasound examinations on the 3rd, 7th, 10th, 12th, 15th, 18th, 21st,
      25th, 28th, 30th days after drainage removal.

      In group B were used a standard intervention (sublay retromuscular).
