
    
      Purpose: To report the rare complication of foveal epiretinal membrane (ERM) formation on
      residual internal limiting membrane (ILM) with severe contraction after previous
      fovea-sparing internal limiting membrane (ILM) peeling.

      Study design: Retrospective case series Methods: We report patients who previously received
      fovea-sparing ILM peeling and complicated with foveal ERM formation and contraction.
