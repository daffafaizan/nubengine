
    
      Medical menstrual regulation (MMR) entails the use of uterine evacuation medications by women
      with late menses without confirming pregnancy status. Provision of MMR in the United States
      could expand reproductive choice and service options for women. This study will collect data
      on the acceptability, efficacy, safety and feasibility of MMR among women with missed menses
      of 1-21 days
