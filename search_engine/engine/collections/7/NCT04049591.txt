
    
      The primary objective of HD PCI is to compare the effect of a policy of higher dose heparin
      (100 U/kg) use to a policy of lower dose heparin (70 U/kg) use on death, myocardial
      infarction (MI) or target vessel revascularization (TVR) in patients undergoing elective
      percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI).
