
    
      A single oral dose of the test or reference product was administered to the volunteers on two
      separate occasions under fasting conditions with at least a 7 day washout between doses. Food
      and fluid intake were controlled during each confinement period.

      A total of twenty eight (28) healthy subjects (21 males and 7 females) were enrolled in the
      study of which twenty seven (27) subjects completed the study.
