
    
      This study will be delimited to the following respects:

      Sixty patients with advanced secondary upper lower limb lymphedema will be recruited from the
      Department of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, Cairo University Hospital. The patients
      will be randomly divided into two groups 30 subjects each.

      Group I:

      In this group, thirty patients will receive complete decongestive physiotherapy treatment
      (compression therapy, lymph reducing exercises and skin care). The sessions will be twelve
      sessions (30 min) of Complete decongestive therapy three times per week over four weeks.

      Group II :

      In this group, thirty patients will receive compression bandaging they for twelve sessions
      three times per week over four weeks. (Espinosa-de-Los-Monteros et al.,2009).
