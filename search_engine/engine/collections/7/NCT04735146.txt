
    
      The primary objective is the evaluation of an integrated, co-designed intervention of adapted
      physical activity, included in the birth accompaniment courses, in order to improve the
      quality of life of pregnant women.

        -  The secondary objective is the evaluation of the feasibility, efficacy and safety of the
           intervention in terms of general satisfaction with the proposed intervention, levels of
           physical activity practiced, physical performance, self-efficacy, sleep quality, anxiety
           and depression.

        -  The tertiary objective is to translate, adapt and validate in Italian a specific
           questionnaire to measure physical activity levels in pregnant women, Pregnancy Physical
           Activity Questionnaire (PPAQ), through the use of Actigraph accelerometers (ActiLife6
           wGT3X-BT ).
