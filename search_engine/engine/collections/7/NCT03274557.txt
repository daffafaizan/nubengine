
    
      Achilless tendinosis is a major clinical problem. There is a need for new studies to map the
      effectiveness of treatment. This study will compare treatment effectiveness between
      traditional physical therapy with surgical treatment using (RFM). The tendinosis grade will
      be evaluated using MRI and /or ultrasound before and after intervention. The outcome
      measurment include VAS and function using ( FAOS FOOT & ANKLE SURVEY) at 6,12 weeks and 6, 12
      months after intervention.
