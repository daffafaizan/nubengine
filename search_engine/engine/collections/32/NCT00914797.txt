
    
      To investigate the serum and urine concentrations of terbutaline and evaluate the difference
      between inhaled terbutaline (2 mg) and oral terbutaline (10 mg) in order to distinguish
      doping with terbutaline from anti-asthmatic treatment with terbutaline.

      To investigate the above mentioned in three groups: healthy men, male asthmatic subjects and
      male elite athletes with asthma.
