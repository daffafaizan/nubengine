
    
      This study is being run entirely on the ProofPilot clinical trial platform. Participants
      enroll online, submit selfies, and are shipped dietary various dietary supplements containing
      a one or more of Keratin, Astaxanthin, Collagen Peptides and Pro-berry powder in vegan and
      non-vegan formats. All participants will receive active ingredients but in various
      fomulations.

      Half the participants will receive the formations for free. The other half will pay a
      discounted amount for the supplements.
