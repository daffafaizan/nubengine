
    
      50 patients with Functional Dyspepsia (FD) diagnosed by General Practitioners are randomized
      in two therapy group: 1) drug only group and 2) drug + Visceral Manipulation group. Group 1
      receive drug therapy for 4 weeks, Group 2 receive drug therapy for 4 weeks and 4 Visceral
      Manipulation visits with weekly frequency (visit 1 at day 1 of drug therapy). FD symptoms are
      recorded with VAS at baseline and after 1 and 4 weeks of therapy, and after 8 weeks (4 weeks
      after the finish of all therapies).
