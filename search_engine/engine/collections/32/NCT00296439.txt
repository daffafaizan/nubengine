
    
      Hemodialysis patients will receive weekly nutrition counseling on how to lower their serum
      Phosphorus levels. Motivational interviewing techniques and strategies will be employed such
      as reflective listening, using affirmations, and summarizing. Average serum Phosphorus levels
      pre-dialysis will be compared to s.Ph post counseling. This will be compared to a group
      receiving nutrition counseling via standard treatment on a monthly basis.
