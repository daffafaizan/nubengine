
    
      We recruited 13 individuals who met the DSM-IV criteria for a diagnosis of high functioning
      autistic disorder (n=1) or Asperger's Disorder (n=12),corroborated by the Autism Diagnostic
      Interview-Revised. Participants were free of any medical or comorbid psychiatric disorders.
      Other inclusion criteria included weigh of at least 16 kg, either a verbal or performance
      intelligence quotient (IQ) above 80, and a score greater than 10 on the social withdrawal
      subscale of the Aberrant Behavior Checklist-Community.
