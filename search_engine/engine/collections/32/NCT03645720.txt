
    
      1. Ultrasonography is performed to determine the maturity of vascular access 4 weeks after
           AVG creation.

        2. The first puncture is performed with plastic cannula or metal needle when it is judged
           that the blood vessel has matured.

        3. Four skilled nurses perform vascular access puncture with plastic or metal needle

        4. Ultrasound are used to evaluate and record the complications related to the puncture in
           plastic or metal needle group every month.

        5. Complications and dialysis adequacy between the two groups will be evaluated after 6
           months
