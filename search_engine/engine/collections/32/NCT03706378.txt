
    
      There will be a total of 9 test sessions and each session will last up to 3 hours. At each
      test session, the following procedures will be performed: two finger-prick blood samples,
      five minutes apart to measure baseline blood glucose and insulin concentrations. Subject will
      be served the test food and to consume within15 minutes. Following the test meal, blood
      samples will be collected at the following time points: 15, 30, 45, 60, 90, 120, 150 and
      180min for glucose measurements and insulin measurements will be collected at 30, 60, 90,
      120, 150 and 180min.
