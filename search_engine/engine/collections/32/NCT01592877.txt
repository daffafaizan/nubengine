
    
      In this study, 50 volunteer patients are tested on the UWB non-contact breath rate and heart
      rate monitor. The testing time length for each patient is set to two hours. Subject is not to
      be restrained and nurse is allowed to take care the subject while testing. The breath rate
      and heart rate data will be analyzed for their accuracy against the MP60 (Philips) bedside
      monitor.
