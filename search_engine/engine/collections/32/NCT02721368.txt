
    
      This is a multi-centered, randomized, double-blind, split-face clinical study on
      investigational device. The subjects in this clinical trial receive application of the
      investigational device on the right and left side of the mid-facial region (Neuramis® Volume
      Lidocaine or Juvederm® Voluma® with Lidocaine). After the pivotal study, extension study will
      be conducted to confirm long-term safety and efficacy.
