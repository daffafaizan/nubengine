
    
      This Randomized study was carried out at Pakistan Railway Hospital Rawalpindi. A total of 28
      patients were screened out as per inclusion criteria and randomly placed into two groups A
      and B through lottery method. The Visual Analogue scale, Shoulder Pain and Disability Index,
      and Range of Motion of shoulder joint were used as outcome measures. All the patients were
      assessed at baseline before intervention and at the completion of 3weeks of treatment.
