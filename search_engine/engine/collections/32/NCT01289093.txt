
    
      The investigators include consecutively all in all 140 patients. 100 with inguinal hernias;
      50 who is getting a Lichtenstein operation and 50 who is getting a laparoscopic operation.
      Furthermore minimum 20 patients who is getting a laparoscopic operation for incisional hernia
      and minimum 20 who went through umbilical herniotomy. The investigators monitor their pain,
      sensation of mesh, movement limitations, over-all well-being, fatigue and life-quality, with
      both CCS and VAS questionnaires 5 times. One time preoperative and 4 times after operation,
      on the 1sth, 7th, 30th and 90th day after operation.
