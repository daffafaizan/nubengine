
    
      This study is been conduct in National Taiwan University Hospital , Taiwan. The main
      measurement tool is a self-report questionnaire, which is designed to answer some outcome
      measures for the following aspect:

        1. To find out the knowledge, attitudes, shared decision making level, subjective norm, and
           behavior intentions towards clinical trials in cancer patients.

        2. To find out the main influencing factors of behavior intentions towards participating in
           clinical trials
