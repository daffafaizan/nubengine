
    
      In this randomized controlled study was conducted with 60 patients undergoing elective
      laparoscopic cholecystectomy in a training and research hospital in Turkey between March and
      August 2019. The participants were divided into two equal groups. Verbal structured education
      and written documents were given to the training group before surgery by a multidisciplinary
      team. The control group had the routine education. Data were collected using a Patient
      Information Form, the State Anxiety Inventory, a visual analog scale, and the Barthel Index
      for Activities of Daily Living.
