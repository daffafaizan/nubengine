
    
      The study will include patients meeting the study criteria monitored in the delivery .

      Verbal and written informed consent will be obtained from all patients before starting the
      study. The patients will be randomised to 2 groups. In one group, vaginal lavage (the vagina
      will first be washed with saline in a 20cc injector) will be applied then Propess will be
      used and in the other group Propess will be used without any lavage.

      It is planned to include 70 patients in each group.
