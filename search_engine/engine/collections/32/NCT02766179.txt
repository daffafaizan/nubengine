
    
      The objectives of this study is to compare short-term outcomes of CPAP and Adjustable
      thermoplastic oral appliance in the treatment of obstructive sleep apnea. Study design is a
      randomized cross-over trial during 6-week period of each treatment in 50 patients. Primary
      outcomes are quality of life, degree of sleepiness, and severity of OSA determined by FOSQ
      ESS, and AHI or RDI, respectively. Wash-in period 2 weeks and Wash-out periods 2 weeks are
      required before start each treatment.
