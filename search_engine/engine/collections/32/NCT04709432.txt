
    
      ECMO treatment in children is still in its infancy in China, and it has made considerable
      progress in recent years. In order to further understand the development of ECMO treatment in
      critically ill children, the investigator conduct this cross-sectional research through
      questionnaires. The investigator collect general information of the hospital and ECMO
      equipment, etiology, compilation, and mode of ECMO.
