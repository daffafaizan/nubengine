
    
      Type 2 diabetic patients with nonproliferative diabetic retinopathy (NPDR) (study population)
      and healthy volunteers (control population).

      Inclusion Criteria:

      Age over 18 years; type-2 diabetes; NPDR levels 10, 20 and 35/47 (ETDRS severity scale); and
      an area of retinal leakage of at least 25% of the RLA map (macular area).

      Exclusion Criteria:

      Presence of media opacity; vitreous syneresis or posterior vitreous detachment, and; previous
      photocoagulation treatments or any type of intraocular surgery or intravitreal medications;
      and other retinal pathologies.
