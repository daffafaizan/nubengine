
    
      There are no protocol-specific procedural requirements for this registry. Enrolled subjects
      will undergo a diagnostic angiography procedure including an FFR measurement with the CPM
      System and NAVVUS Catheter according to the center standard of care and the Instructions for
      Use of the CPM System and Navvus Catheter. Participants will be followed for the duration of
      hospital stay, an expected average of 1 day.
