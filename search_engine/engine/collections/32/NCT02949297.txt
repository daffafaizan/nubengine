
    
      A prospective clinical evaluation of the effectiveness of the Ovation Alto Abdominal Stent
      Graft System when used in the treatment of subjects with abdominal aortic aneurysm (AAA). The
      primary objective is to evaluate treatment success at 12 months with the Ovation Alto
      Abdominal Stent Graft system. The study will have up to 16 sites, and up to 75 patients based
      on attrition rates. The follow-up intervals will be at 1, 6, and 12 months following initial
      implant procedure.
