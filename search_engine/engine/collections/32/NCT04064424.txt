
    
      This research includes three phases:

        1. To better understand the motivators and inhibitors of skin cancer risk behaviors in
           various populations including use of indoor tanning and outdoor sun exposure;

        2. To develop content messages and images related to skin cancer risk that are resonant
           with various populations;

        3. To test the relative effectiveness of these messages in reducing likelihood of or actual
           skin cancer risk behaviors in various populations.
