
    
      You will receive a study drug for the 8 week study. No subject will be given a placebo (an
      inactive substance) during treatment.The study drugs (over the counter acne medications) have
      been previously tested and their safe use has been established. However, not all risks or
      side effects are known. In rare cases of allergy, side effects may be life threatening.The
      most common side effects of this study drug are:

        1. Dry skin

        2. Peeling skin

        3. Red skin

        4. Burning or tingling (temporary)
