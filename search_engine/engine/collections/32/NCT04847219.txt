
    
      Flash Glucose Mornitoring will be used in patients with type 2 diabetes who are treated with
      premixed insulin. The frequency of hypogycemia and the blood glucose control will be analyzed
      by flash glucose mornitoring once a month for 3 months and doctors will adjust the
      hypoglycemia treatment according to the results every month. HbA1c, glycemic variation,
      beta-cell function and androgen levels will be measured.
