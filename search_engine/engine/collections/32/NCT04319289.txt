
    
      Forty five women complaining from myalgia with vitamin D deficiency participating in this
      study. Their ages are ranged from 30 to 40 years.

      They are assigned randomly into three groups:

        -  Group (A): included 15 patients who are participating in an aerobic interval training
           exercise program with vitamin D supplementation (cholecalciferol 400 IU/day).

        -  Group (B): included 15 patients who are receiving vitamin D supplementation only
           (cholecalciferol 400 IU/day).

        -  Group (C): included 15 patients who are participating in an interval training exercise
           program only.

      The intervention will last for 12 weeks
