
    
      This is a risk reduction intervention for adults who are living with HIV, are experiencing
      trauma-related stress symptoms, and are at risk for HIV transmission. By first treating
      trauma symptoms, the effects of a skills-building HIV risk reduction intervention for adults
      experiencing trauma-related symptoms such as hyperarousal, dissociation, and avoidance will
      be enhanced. This is based on a model the proposes trauma-related symptoms have direct
      effects on HIV risk behavior. Therefore, the successful treatment of trauma-related symptoms
      will facilitate HIV risk behavior change.
