
    
      After screened for the Inclusion criteria and the exclusion criteria,254patients undergoing
      unilateral thoracoscopic lobectomy are randomly assigned to two groups,disconnecting
      pulmonary vague nerve branches(D)and preserve pulmonary vague nerve branches(P).The surgeons
      find the vagus nerve pulmonary branch leading to the hilum and count the number of branches
      on all patients included.The incidence of chronic cough after surgery are compared between
      groups.
