
    
      This retrospective study aimed to develop a new algorithm for automatic localization of
      epilepsy foci in children with intractable epilepsy.

      Pediatric patients with intractable epilepsy would be retrospectively included in this study.
      The inclusion criteria included (1) age between 10 and 18 years; (2) detailed presurgical
      evaluation including seizure semiology evaluation, MRI, EEG, and interictal 18F-FDG PET/CT
      scanning; (3) postsurgical MRI examinations. The exclusion criteria included any history of
      central nervous system (CNS) disease such as trauma and CNS tumor.
