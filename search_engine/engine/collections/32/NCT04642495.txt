
    
      In this prospective single center study subjects aged at least 4 years underwent testing of
      visual acuity with SW, Landolt-C and Tumbling-E symbols (Freiburg acuity test: FrACT-C,
      FrACT-E). Prediction intervals are to be established for SW compared to FrACT-C or -E and for
      FrACT- E compared to FrACT-C.
