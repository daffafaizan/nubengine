
    
      Compares the patients' scores of UPDRS-III before and six-month after receiving deep brain
      stimulation for Parkinson's disease under MAC or AAA anesthesia. Besides, the research also
      compares the adverse events, LED, dosage of anesthetic drugs, wake-up time, surgical time,
      Hoehn&Yahr, PQD-39, MMSE, MoCA, HAMA, HAMD and patients' satisfaction between the two groups
      mentioned above.
