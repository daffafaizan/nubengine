
    
      Neqstent is designed as an adjunctive therapy for patients with unruptured and failed
      bifurcation aneurysm embolizations. Neqstent can be positioned at the neck of the aneurysm
      with or without existing coils and other embolization products. Embolization coils should be
      added to fill the dome of the aneurysm after placement of Neqstent.
