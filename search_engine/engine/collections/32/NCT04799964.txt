
    
      The Flow Diverter is an intracranial implant designed to be placed in a parent artery so as
      to divert blood flow away from an aneurysm. This clinical trial is carried out in 14 research
      centers. The subjects are randomly divided into the experimental group using flow
      diverter(Ton-bridgeMT) and the control group using Tubridge at a ratio of 1:1.
