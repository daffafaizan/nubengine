
    
      Treatment regimen:

      Bendamustine (B) 90 mg/m2 iv day 1, 2 Gemcytabine (G) 800 mg/m2 iv day 1, 4 Dexamethasone (D)
      40 mg iv/po day 1,2,3,4

      Course of treatment every 21-28 days, 4 courses of treatment max; next round of treatment may
      be given if ANC>1000/μl and PLT>75000/μl. Up to 7-day delay is permitted.
