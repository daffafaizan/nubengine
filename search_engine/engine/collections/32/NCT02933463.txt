
    
      The aim of this study is designed to clinically evaluate the retention and development of
      caries when using self-priming (Embrace Wetbond) and conventional flowable composite
      (Clinpro) as pit and fissure sealant in young first permanent molars.

      PICO:

      P: children aged 6-9 years old with high caries risk.

      I: Embrace Wetbond.

      C: Clinpro seal.

      O:

      Primary outcome: Retention of sealant; Device of measurement: questionnaire; unit: binary.

      Secondary outcome: Retention and development of caries; Device of measurement: Modified
      simonsen's criteria; Unit: scoring system (0-4) tactile and visual examination.
