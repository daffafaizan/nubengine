
    
      OUTLINE:

      Participants are randomized to 1 of 2 groups. Both groups receive access to a 42-day quit
      smoking program.

      GROUP I: Quitbot program, which involves prompted and focused conversations for 42 days.

      GROUP II: Texting program, which involves quit smoking messages for 42 days. After the
      completion of study, patients are followed up at 3, 6, and 12 months.
