
    
      The purpose of this study will to evaluate the effects of a ritual training program on
      disfigurement, self-esteem, social interaction, and body image in head and neck cancer (HNC)
      patients.

      A prospective, repeated measures, randomized controlled therapeutic intervention design was
      employed. The experimental group will receive a ritual training program, and the control
      group will receive routine care. Patients will be assessed at three time points: baseline
      assessment (T0), and then at 1, 2, and 3-months (T1, T2, and T3) after participating in the
      ritual training program or routine care.
