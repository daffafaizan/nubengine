
    
      Children with autism spectrum disorder registered in Xinhua Hospital are measured in aspects
      of behavioural accessment, genetic test, blood lab test, gut microbiome and neuroimaging
      including structural and functional magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), functional near -
      infrared spectroscopy(f-NIRS), electroencephalography and eye movement. The investigators
      conduct the real world study to explore mechanisms of autism based on the multi-modal data.
