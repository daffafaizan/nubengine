
    
      A total of 96 second primary second molars from 32 children ,who met inclusion criteria and
      were 5-9 years old were selected for the study. Teeth were randomly divided into three groups
      according to the planned treatment: O-MTA (n=32 teeth), R-MTA (n=32 teeth) and FS (n=32
      teeth). Clinical and radiographical follow-up examinations were conducted at 3, 6 and 9
      months postoperatively. The data were evaluated with Chi-square test, Cochran Q test,
      Post-Huc Dunn test and Kaplan-Meier analysis.
