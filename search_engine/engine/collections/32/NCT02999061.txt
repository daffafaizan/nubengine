
    
      The purpose of the study is to determine the prevalence of dental caries in preterm birth
      children aged 2-5 years in primary dentition.

      PICO format:- Population (P) = preterm children aged 2-5 years attending Cairo University
      Children's Hospital "Abo El-Rish" at El-Monira, Cairo, Egypt.

      Outcome (O) =prevalence of dental caries
