
    
      Ixabepilone, 16 mg/m2 or 20mg/m2, weekly x 3, in 4 week cycles, x 4 cycles. Prostatectomy 2-8
      weeks after completion of chemotherapy (this was standard of care).

      This protocol evaluated weekly ixabepilone prior to robotic prostatectomy for patients with
      high risk localized prostate cancer. PSA response rate, tumor margin status and pathologic
      responses were assessed.
