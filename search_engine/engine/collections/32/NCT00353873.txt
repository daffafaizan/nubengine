
    
      A multicentre, randomised, double-blind, double dummy, parallel group study to compare the
      salmeterol/fluticasone propionate combination (SERETIDE™) at a dose of 50/100mcg twice daily
      and fluticasone propionate (FLIXOTIDE™) at a dose of 200mcg twice daily, both delivered via a
      dry powder inhaler (DISKUS™) for 12 weeks in asthma in children aged 4-11 years not
      controlled by inhaled corticosteroids alone at medium dose
