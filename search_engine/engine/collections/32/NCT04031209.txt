
    
      The objectives of the study are to document the performance and safety of the G7 Dual
      Mobility System when used in primary total hip arthroplasty for the treatment of femoral neck
      fracture by analysis of standard scoring systems, radiographs, revision and adverse event
      records.The study will include a maximum of 15 centers and up to 300 implanted hips. Each
      center may enroll up to a maximum of 40 hips to permit the consistency of outcomes across a
      variety of investigators and clinical setting
