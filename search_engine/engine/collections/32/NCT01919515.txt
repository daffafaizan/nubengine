
    
      The aim of this study is to clinically evaluate the performance of a new NuSmile ZR esthetic
      primary molar crown, compared with 3M ESPE Primary Stainless Steel crowns (currently the most
      common full coverage crown for the restoration of primary molars when a crown is necessary)in
      the restoration of 50 paired, prepared primary first and second molar teeth in the deciduous
      dentition of child patients.
