
    
      Observational study of 20 adult postoperative cardiac surgery patients. The observation will
      last from 0 to 2. postoperative day. On admission to the ICU the patients will receive a
      noninvasive monitor recording arterial pulse waveform and ECG. The monitoring will continue
      to the ward for two days. The data will be compared with the routine monitoring methods in
      the ICU and the ward.
