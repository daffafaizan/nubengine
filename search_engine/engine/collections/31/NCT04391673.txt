
    
      The aim of this longitudinal study is to investigate whether illness perceptions predict
      dizziness handicap following a treatment as usual 8-week vestibular rehabilitation programme
      in patients with chronic vertigo and dizziness.

      Participants will be recruited consecutively from patients attending the dizziness and
      balance exercise programme at Guys & St Thomas' NHS Foundation Trust. Consenting patients
      will complete the following questionnaires before and after rehabilitation:

        1. The Dizziness Handicap Inventory (DHI)

        2. The Illness Perceptions Questionnaire - Revised (IPQ-R)

        3. The Patient Health Questionnaire Anxiety and Depression Scale (PHQ-ADS)

      The Dizziness Handicap Inventory (DHI) at discharge will be the dependent variable. We will
      also demographic data including age, gender, and illness type.
