
    
      The specific aims of the study are to test the effectiveness of acupuncture and laser
      acupoint on hypertension. The hypothesis is: the stimulation of a pattern of acupoints with
      acupuncture and laser will result in significant reductions in blood pressure. The points
      that are going to be used for the study are: Zusanli (St36), Tianshu (St25), Quchi (LI11).
