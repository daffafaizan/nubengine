
    
      Volunteers who have been diagnosed with venous insufficiency according to the criteria of
      inclusion, who applied to Istanbul University Istanbul Medical Faculty Cardiovascular Surgery
      Department will be included this study.

      Participants will be randomly allocated 2 groups using the 'Research Randomizer' website. In
      both groups, a common evaluation protocol will be applied to the patients.

      Exercise training in addition to compression bandages will be applied to first group,
      compression bandages will be applied to second group.
