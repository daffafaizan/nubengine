
    
      With the further development of endoscopic technique, thyroid surgery of the robot has
      emerged, which may become a trend of endoscopic technology due to its convenience and
      accuracy.In this study, the advantages and disadvantages of endoscopic thyroidectomy and
      robotic thyroid surgery were compared, and the experience in the transition stage of thyroid
      surgery in robotic and endoscopic thyroidectomy was summarized.
