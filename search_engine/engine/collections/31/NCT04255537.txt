
    
      This is a Quality Improvement Program which enroll all the consecutive patients hospitalized
      with ACS. The data elements that are central to the study include the primary and secondary
      quality indicators (QIs) that are used to define the performance and to promote improved
      quality of care.

      At least 3000 patients will be enrolled. The primary endpoint are assessed in-hospital, at
      admission and discharge. Patients will be followed for 1 year after the ACS.
