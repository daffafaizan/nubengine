
    
      The control arm will receive standard preoperative counselling and written consent will be
      taken.

      The NSQIP arm will receive standard preoperative counselling along with the details provided
      by NSQIP Surgical risk calculator and written consent will be taken.

      Anxiety among the patients will be accessed with the use of Preoperative anxiety measured by
      Amsterdam Preoperative Anxiety and Information Scale (APAIS) questionnaire written in Nepali.

      Duration of counselling period will be noted from the start of counselling session till the
      consent is signed.
