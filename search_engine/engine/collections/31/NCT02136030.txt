
    
      Subjects who meet all eligible requirements will be randomized into study group （Lipo-AB）or
      control group（Amphotericin B deoxycholate） in 2:1 ratio. Subjects will be treated with
      induction therapy in study group or in control group with flucytosine for at least 14 days,
      and followed by consolidation therapy for another 56 days after CSF sterilization.
