
    
      Acupuncture is potential to be an alternative treatment for primary insomnia. The purpose of
      this study is to evaluate the clinical effectiveness of acupuncture treatment on primary
      insomnia. This is a randomized single-blinded controlled trial. Patients will be randomly
      assigned to the electro-acupuncture group or the placebo acupuncture group. Patients will be
      put into groups and then compared.
