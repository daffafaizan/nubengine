
    
      The purpose of this research study is to conduct a pilot randomized controlled trial of a
      game-based intervention is able to increase help-seeking-related knowledge, attitudes, and
      behaviors, reduce health risk factors/behaviors, and increase resiliencies among sexual and
      gender minority (SGM) youth. The goals of the proposed study are to: (1) Test the feasibility
      and acceptability of a game-based intervention to increase help-seeking-related knowledge,
      attitudes, and behaviors among SGM youth; and (2) Using a randomized controlled trial, test
      the efficacy of a game-based intervention to increase help-seeking-related knowledge,
      attitudes, and behaviors, reduce health risk factors/behaviors, and increase resiliencies
      among SGM youth.
