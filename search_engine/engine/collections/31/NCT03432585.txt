
    
      This study will use a survey provided on the American Society for Nutrition Family Support
      Grant application webpage for those who are registering to attend the American Society for
      Nutrition annual meeting and who are applying for this particular grant. The survey will take
      about 10 minutes to complete and will provide detailed information for assessment and
      evaluation of conference attendance feasibility and will also provide feedback about
      preferences and barriers related to attendance and this annual meeting.
