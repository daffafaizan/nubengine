
    
      The secondary objectives of this study are:

      A. to describe changes in body image (Image Body Scale Version F) at 3, 6 and 12 months after
      a "remission" consultation.

      B. to describe changes in psychological distress (visual analog scale) at 3, 6 and 12 months
      after a "remission" consultation.

      C. to assess the time of resumption of work (date of last chemotherapy - date of the actual
      resumption of work) after a "remission" consultation.

      D. to evaluate the acceptance rate for the "remission" consultation.

      E. to describe the modes of the observed management, their distribution and the
      characteristics of the patients according to different monitoring arrangements.
