
    
      This study will be carried out on 60 patients with lifelong premature ejaculation and
      non-responding to dapoxetine alone.

      All patients will be equally divided into 2 groups (30 patients each). Group 1 was given
      on-demand 30 mg dapoxetine and 10 mg tadalafil 1 h before intercourse. Group 1 was given
      on-demand 30 mg dapoxetine 1 h before intercourse and apply lidocaine 5% spray on the glans
      penis 10 minutes the wash before intercourse.
