
    
      The primary goal is to quantify cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) volume, diffusion characteristics
      and mechanical properties of brain tissue at two states of arousal:

        1. Resting, awake state

        2. During sleep with low-dose intravenous infusion of dexmedetomidine (titrated to a
           Bispectral index (BIS) of 60-70)
