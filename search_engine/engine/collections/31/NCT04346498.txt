
    
      After the study was explained (risks and benefits) and written informed consent was received
      from participants, an individually controlled randomized crossover clinical trial was
      conducted to examine the effectiveness of Chest-to-Back (CB) skin-to-skin contact (SSC)
      compared with the standard. The trial was done in accordance with the protocol, good clinical
      practice, and the national regulatory and ethics guideline of Ethiopia (which is in line with
      the Helsinki declaration).
