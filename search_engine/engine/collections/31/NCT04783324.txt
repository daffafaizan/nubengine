
    
      This is a randomized controlled trial. A questionnaire and The Postpartum Self-Evaluation
      Questionnaire will be used to collect data. The main hypothesis of the research: Postpartum
      Self-Evaluation Questionnaire total mean score of women who use e-mobile health application
      created according to Roy's Adaptation Model is not different from the total mean score of
      women in the control group.

      Research sample will consist of a total of 64 pregnant women, 32 in the experimental group
      and 32 in the control group. The data will be evaluated with the SPSS (Statistical Package of
      SocialSciences) 24.0 package program.
