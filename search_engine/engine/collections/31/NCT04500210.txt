
    
      The included volunteers, who are devided into two groups either placebo or turmeric extract,
      will be tested using WOMAC questionnaires focussing on pain, stiffness and daily activity. In
      addition different aspects of quality of life will be evaluated and the consumption of rescue
      medication will be recorded in patients personal diary.

      The treatment period is 3 month with the posibility to continue for an additional 3 month.
