
    
      The objectives of this study were to (i) review extant literature on the prevalence of
      physical inactivity (PI) in adolescents of both sex (10-18 years old); (ii) compare its
      prevalence between developed and developing countries.

      The search was carried out using online databases (Biomed Central, CINAHL, EMBASE, ERIC,
      MEDLINE, PsycInfo, Scopus, SPORTDiscus), references cited by retrieved articles and by
      contact with the authors, considering articles published from the establishment of the
      databanks until April 29th, 2010.
