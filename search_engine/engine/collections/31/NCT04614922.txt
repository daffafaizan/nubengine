
    
      The study is run in an outpatient setting at the Department of Physical Medicine and
      Rehabilitation, St. Olavs Hospital, Trondheim University Hospital, Norway. All patients are
      also participants in the observational study: "Minimal and mild head injury: an exercise and
      outpatient follow-up study at St. Olavs Hospital" (REK number 2018/2159).
