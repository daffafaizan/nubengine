
    
      This study will help to quantify heart and kidney ketone uptake with or without an exogenous
      ketone salt with or without food intake in middle-aged adults. Participants will have 3
      different PET scan to perform :

        1. Fasting without exogenous ketone salt

        2. Fasting with exogenous ketone salt supplement

        3. Post-Prandial with exogenous ketone salt supplement.
