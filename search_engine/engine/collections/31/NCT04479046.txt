
    
      The aim of the present study is to evaluate the PMMA crowns regarding the following aspects:

      I. Clinical evaluation of PMMA crowns regarding wear of opposing teeth when compared to
      zirconia crowns and stainless steel crowns after 6 months, and 12 months.

      II. Color stability of PMMA crowns and zirconia crowns after 6 months, and 12 months.

      III. Gingival health of restored teeth after 6 months, and 12 months. IV. Patient's
      satisfaction regarding the appearance and clinical performance of PMMA crowns when compared
      to stainless steel crowns and zirconia crowns through a given questionnaire.
