
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVE:

      I. To increase the number of smokers eligible for lung cancer screening (LCS) who receive
      high-quality educational materials about LCS through tobacco quitlines.

      OUTLINE:

      CALL CENTER STAFF: Participants undergo training consisting of a 60-minute educational
      session.

      CALLERS SUBSTUDY: Participants are referred to lung cancer screening educational materials.
      Participants also complete questionnaires at 1 week and 6 months after referral to
      educational materials.
