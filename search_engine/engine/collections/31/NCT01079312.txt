
    
      80 elective ERCP patients were randomized to anaesthesiologist managed propofol sedation
      (PI-group) or PCS with propofol/remifentanil (PCS-group). Sedation degree was estimated every
      5 min throughout the procedure using Ramsay´s and Gillham´s sedation scores. Total amount of
      propofol was calculated at the end of procedure. Endoscopists´ and patients´ satisfaction was
      evaluated with questionary.
