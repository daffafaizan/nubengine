
    
      Patients who had pedunculated colorectal polyps with heads larger than 10 mm and stalks
      larger than 5 mm in diameter were included. In clip group, hemoclips were applied to the base
      of the stalk, followed by conventional snare polypectomy. In conventional group, conventional
      snare polypectomy was done without any preventive management. Immediate and delayed bleeding
      complications were assessed.
