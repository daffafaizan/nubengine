
    
      Prospectively collect cases with NHL (n=10), having HCV genotype 1b related NHL, who will be
      treated with ASV (200 mg twice daily) + DCV(60 mg once daily) for 24 weeks The patients will
      be observed and followed to determine whether there is regression of NHL after antiviral
      treatment. Conventional chemotherapy should be initiated shortly in cases without regression.
