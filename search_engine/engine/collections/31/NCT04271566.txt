
    
      This study has two parts: A drug utilization study and a Health-related quality of life
      study.

      Drug utilisation study is an open label, cross sectional, descriptive drug utilization
      analysis of the primary cardiovascular drug classes prescribed to coronary artery disease
      patients attending cardiology OPD of a North India hospital.

      Quality of life study intended to evaluate impact of a mobile based patient education program
      on the health related quality of life, physical activity and body mass index of coronary
      artery disease patients. It is a two arm study.
