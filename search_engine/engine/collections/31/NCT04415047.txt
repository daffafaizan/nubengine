
    
      This study will examine the use of TAVR (Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement), which is a
      minimally invasive procedure designed to replace the aortic valve inside the heart. In this
      study, TAVR will be performed using the JenaValve Pericardial TAVR System, which is intended
      to help treat symptomatic severe aortic regurgitation.
