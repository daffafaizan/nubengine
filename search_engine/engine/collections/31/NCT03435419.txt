
    
      The investigators enrolled 274 CL suspects in the study to determine the diagnostic
      performance of LoopampTM Leishmania Detection Kit and CL DetectTM Rapid Test for CL diagnosis
      in Afghanistan. The study was conducted at the National Malaria & Leishmaniasis Control
      Program (NMLCP) Leishmaniasis Clinic in Kabul, Afghanistan.
