
    
      This study aims to increase engagement, detection and management of glaucoma, vision
      impairment, cataracts, and other eye diseases in vulnerable populations living in New York
      City. This study considers disparities by race, ethnicity, gender, geography, and
      socioeconomic status using innovative approaches such as patient navigators. Individuals over
      the age of 40 living in New York City Housing Authority (NYCHA) Housing buildings are
      eligible.
