
    
      Patients that are admitted to hospital with the diagnosis of necrotizing fasciitis of a limb
      will be entered to this study. A questionnaire will be completed for each patient included
      demographic data, admission and diagnosis date, treatment onset, medical history for diabetes
      mellitus, HIV infection, use of immunosuppressor drugs, peripheral vascular disease, cancer,
      renal disease, intravenous drug abuse, and disease outcome that can be recovery, amputation
      or death.
