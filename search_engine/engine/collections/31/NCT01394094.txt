
    
      Following abdominal surgery for treatment of benign gynecologic surgery, paralytic ileus may
      be develops. Gum chewing can promotes the return of bowel function through the cephalic-vagal
      reflex and increased intestinal enzymes secretion. This study will be evaluate effects of
      adding gum chewing to the conventional postoperative feeding protocol on the return of bowel
      function, its related complication, and patients's satisfaction.
