
    
      Unicentric, prospective and randomized study performed between September and November 2020
      with adult patients who sought care at emergency department. The inclusion criterion was the
      exhibition of any tract respiratory symptom. Patients older than 65 years, with chronic heart
      or lung diseases or immunosuppressed were excluded. Eligible patients were randomized 1:1 for
      a brief telemedicine consultation, blinded to subsequent face-to-face evaluation or direct
      face-to-face evaluation.
