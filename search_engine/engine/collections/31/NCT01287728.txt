
    
      Women with symptomatic abnormal vaginal flora (a Nugent score of 4-10 or sup or egal 3 Amsel
      criteria) will be enrolled. Abnormal vaginal flora will be treated with 2g of oral
      metronidazole. Follow-up will be performed at 1 week,1 month and 12 months after treatment.
      Vaginal samples will be tested by quantitative real time PCR to determine the vaginal
      concentrations of A. vaginae, G. vaginalis, and Lactobacillus spp.
