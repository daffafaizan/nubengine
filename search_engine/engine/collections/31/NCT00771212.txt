
    
      This is a Phase IV, multi-center, observational study to describe the use of pain treatments,
      the demographics, clinical characteristics of patients requiring pain treatments, current
      treatments, outcomes of treatments and the reasons for changing pain medication during
      treatment. The study is conducted in an effort to understand the status of pain control in
      Taiwan. Currently in Taiwan, there is no approved documentation showing the most effective
      standard medication for treatment of patients in pain. Observational study with no study
      medication administered.
