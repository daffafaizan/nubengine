
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVE:

      I. To determine the feasibility of a sexual health counseling intervention before and after
      pelvic radiation for women with gynecologic cancers.

      SECONDARY OBJECTIVE:

      I. To preliminarily evaluate the efficacy of a sexual health counseling intervention on
      patient reported outcomes in quality of life, sexual function, and genitourinary symptoms.

      OUTLINE:

      Patients receive sexual health counseling prior to starting and at the completion of
      radiation.

      After completion of study, patients are followed up at 1 and 6 months after radiation.
