
    
      Once parental consent for entrance into the study is obtained, eligible babies will be
      randomized to receive either IV acetaminophen (perfalgen) by continuous infusion at 2.5
      mg/kg/h following a loading dose of 15 mg /kg/ over 20 minutes or rectal acetaminophen at 15
      mg/kg every 6 hours for a period of 3 days.
