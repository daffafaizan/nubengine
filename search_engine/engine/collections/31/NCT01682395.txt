
    
      Participants: Patients 18 years or older presenting to Kamuzu Central Hospital in Lilongwe
      Malawi with sigmoid volvulus will be eligible for inclusion.

      Procedures (methods): This is a prospective randomized trial. Subjects will be randomized
      during surgery to receive resection and anastomosis or mesosigmoidopexy (for non-gangrenous
      sigmoid volvulus), and resection and anastomosis or Hartmann's procedure (for gangrenous
      sigmoid volvulus).
