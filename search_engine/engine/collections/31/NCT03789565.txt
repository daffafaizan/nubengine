
    
      The study included 90 individuals, who were divided into three groups: periodontally healthy
      (PH) (n=30); gingivitis (G) (n=30); and chronic periodontitis (CP) (n=30). GCF and serum
      samples were obtained from each individual, and samples were evaluated using ELISA.
      Statistical evaluation of the data was performed using the SPSS packet data program.
