
    
      Subjects in this long-term follow-up study were recruited among those subjects who completed
      the study 287615 (NCT00508833) and who were enrolled in specific groups. All subjects were
      vaccinated in the study 287615 (NCT00508833) according to a 0, 1, 10 month schedule. No new
      subjects were enrolled and no vaccine was administered in this long-term follow-up study.
      There was a single visit at Year 4 at which blood samples for immunogenicity assays were
      taken.
