
    
      The study will conduct in the Physical Therapy and Radiology Department in a private
      hospital. The Clinical Research Ethics Committee of Gaziantep the univsersity approved
      ethical compliance of the study with the decision number GO 2018-01. The participants will
      inform about the study and their write consent will obtain.

      Demographic information including the name, age, height, body weight and occupation of the
      participants will obtain before the evaluation. All the measurements will perform before,
      soon after the 1st session and after the last session.

      Outcome measures will be muscle strength, muscle flexibility, muscle architectures
      parameters.
