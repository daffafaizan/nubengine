
    
      It will consist of a split-mouth study in patients who require the extraction of the first
      premolars to correct malocclusion. The canine will be retracted using these surgical
      techniques, as well as without any intervention, being this the control side. In addition,
      will be evaluated the inflammatory biomarkers present in the gingival crevicular sulcus
      during the dental movement, as well as data related to pain and discomfort.
