
    
      -  Alterations of neurophysiologic regulation has been suggested in patients with major
           depressive disorder or bipolar disorders

        -  Depressive phase of bipolar disorder shows multi-faceted characteristics different from
           unipolar depression

        -  Quetiapine is an atypical antipsychotic licensed for treatment of bipolar depression

        -  The aim of this study is to investigate the pattern of neurophysiologic dysregulation in
           bipolar depression and its changes induced by quetiapine treatment
