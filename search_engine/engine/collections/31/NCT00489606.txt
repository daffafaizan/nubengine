
    
      During each of the two periods of a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, crossover
      study, 16 healthy men received tamsulosin 0.2 mg daily for 7 days and either a single 100 mg
      of SK3530 or placebo on day7. The blood pressure and heart rate were monitored before and for
      24 hours after SK3530 or placebo.
