
    
      This is a randomized, split face & dorsal hand side by side study. The purpose of the side by
      side study is to determine whether the use of the Impact with the ablative fractional 2940nm
      laser and cosmetics improves the cosmetic outcomes when compared to using just the ablative
      fractional 2940nm laser with the cosmetics.

      This is a non-significant risk aesthetic clinical study. Only cosmetic formulations will be
      used during the study, These cosmetic formulations do not contain any ingredient categorized
      as a drug by the FDA.
