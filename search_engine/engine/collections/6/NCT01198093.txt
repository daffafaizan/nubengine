
    
      The specific aims of this study is (1) construction & maintenance of cohort for Alzheimer's
      disease(AD) and mild cognitive impairment(MCI); (2) study of epidemiology, rate of
      progression, risk factor, prognostic factor; (3) study of biomarker such as
      neuropsychological assessment, peripheral & CSF markers, neuroimaging(both structural &
      functional)
