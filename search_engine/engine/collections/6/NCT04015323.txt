
    
      60 patients with knee osteoarhthritis will be enrolled in the study. Subjects will be
      randomized to 3 groups. All patients will receive interferential currents to both their
      knees, with different carrier frequencies (2, 4 and 8 kilohertz (kHz) respectively). The
      effect of different types of carrier frequencies on pain, 10 meter walk test will be
      analysed.
