
    
      To compare the efficacy of tegafur/uracil (UFT) plus folinate calcium (leucovorin: LV)
      administered orally and UFT plus Krestin (PSK) administered orally enrolling patients with
      pTNM stage IIB or III (UICC Sixth version) colorectal cancer/histological curability A
      (Japanese Classification of Colorectal Carcinoma, Sixth version) as subjects in a multicenter
      collaborative randomized open-label comparative controlled study and consequently to
      determine the usefulness of the above regimens as adjuvant therapy after surgery.
