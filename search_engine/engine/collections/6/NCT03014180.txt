
    
      "In-Clinic LVAT" feature is a non CE-marked feature designed to be activated by the physician
      during 2 hospital follow-ups:

        -  first visit: planned to be conducted 15 days maximum after inclusion

        -  second visit: planned to be conducted between 1 and 3 months after the first visit The
           evaluation consists in the comparison between the threshold value provided by the
           algorithm and the threshold value obtained manually by the physician.
