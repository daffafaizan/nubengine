
    
      This study has 2 arms with a 1:1 randomization. Randomization will be stratified by Japanese
      ethnicity. Half of the subjects in each arm will be of Japanese ethnicity. The other half of
      the subjects in each arm will be non-Japanese. Subjects in both arms will have visits at
      Screening, Day 0 (Baseline), Day 1 (24 hours post first injection), Day 3, Day 14, Day 21,
      Day 28, Day 56, Day 57 (24 hours post the injection on Day 56) and Day 84.
