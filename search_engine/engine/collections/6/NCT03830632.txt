
    
      Plyometric training has been an effective method for the improvement of agility, sprinting,
      and jumping ability and it has also been reported to Improve running economy, joint
      stability, increased joint awareness and overall proprioception and decrease the severity of
      knee injuries. In the Plyometric exercise involves starting, stopping, and change of movement
      directions which contribute to agility development.The athletes use plyometric training
      techniques in many sports to improve explosive power and body strength. The plyometrics is a
      rapid stretch of muscle (eccentric) contraction straight away shortening (concentric)
      contraction in similar muscle tissue
