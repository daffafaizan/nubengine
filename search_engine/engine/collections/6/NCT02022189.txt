
    
      If the patient agrees to be in the study, the patient will be asked to participate in the
      following:

        -  Sign a consent form indicating the patient's willingness to participate.

        -  Provide a signed medical release form allowing the investigator to request your official
           kidney biopsy reports, and also allowing the investigator to request the original slides

           facility.

        -  The investigator will also review your medical record
