
    
      This is a prospective, single-blinded, randomized, paired sample, multi-center clinical
      study. Eight visits per patient are scheduled in this study. The final analysis will be
      conducted after all patients completed the 12-month visit.

      The study devices are CE- (Conformité Européenne, meaning European Conformity) marked
      products and used within their intended use.

      Two centers in Spain will participate.
