
    
      Patients with PEXG under prostaglandine analogue monotherapy with inadequate IOP control are
      treated with 360-degrees 532nm MLT.

      Patients are evaluated at 1 day, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months and 12 months post-MLT. After
      trabeculoplasty patients are maintained in treatment with the same drug regimen as pre-MLT.
