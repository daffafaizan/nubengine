
    
      Trial design: A pilot randomized (allocation ratio 1:1), single blind clinical trial (RCT)
      comparing the effects between the experimental [Robot-Assisted Stair Climbing Training group
      (RASCT)] and control group (Conventional Physiotherapy, CP) in improving stair climbing
      ability and postural control in stroke patients. The examiner will be blind to group
      assignment.
