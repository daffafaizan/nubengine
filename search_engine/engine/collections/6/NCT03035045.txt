
    
      Perioperative and postoperative amniocentesis are often painful which cause concerns to those
      who has to undergo amniocentesis. Anxiety is one of the factor that may prolong operative
      time, resulting in an increase risk of complication from the procedure. Furthermore, pregnant
      women, in order to relief the pain, might require others drugs, which has both maternal and
      fetal side effect. So In conclusion, this study aims to compare efficacy of oral paracetamol
      premedication and oral placebo premedication by using visual analog scale to evaluate pain.
