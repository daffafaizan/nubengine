
    
      Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) is the most common endocrine disorder in women of
      reproductive age. PCOS produces symptoms in approximately 5 to 10% of women of reproductive
      age (12-45 years old) and is thought to be one of the leading causes of the female
      subfertility

        -  The aim of this work is to study the effect of metformin use for reducing early
           pregnancy loss in pregnant patients with PCOS

        -  Research question:

      In pregnant women with PCOS, does metformin use decreases the rate of early pregnancy loss?

      -Research hypothesis: In Pregnant women with PCOS, metformin may reduce the rate of early
      pregnancy loss.
