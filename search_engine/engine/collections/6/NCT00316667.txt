
    
      A population-based random sample of persons living in Copenhagen is drawn from the Danish
      Civil Registration System and invited for a health examination including blood sample
      drawing, measurement of lung function, serologic and genetic biomarkers, questionnaires on
      lifestyle and health. The study will form the basis for research within many different areas
      such as cardiovascular disease, type 2 diabetes, asthma, and allergy.
