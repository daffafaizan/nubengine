
    
      After clinician evaluation, if the child meets the study criteria and after adequate
      communication, the parent or legal guardian voluntarily joins the clinical study, CAR-T
      technique can be used for related treatment, and the long-term therapeutic effect can be
      observed. In this trial, 50 children were publicly enrolled and treated with CAR-T. Patients
      participating in the clinical trial will be tested and assessed in terms of treatment safety,
      efficacy, and duration of response.
