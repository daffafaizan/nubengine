
    
      50 subjects will be enrolled, who show with gingivitis caused by accumulation of plaque
      and/or tartar selected consecutively. Subsequently, subjects will be equally divided and
      randomized into the test group or control group.

      During the three examinations, the subjects will undergo the collection of bleeding and
      plaque indices according to Silness J & Loe H 3,4. Four surfaces per tooth (vestibular,
      lingual/palatal, mesial, distal) will be considered.
