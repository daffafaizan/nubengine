
    
      Our trial seeks to assess the efficacy of the LymphaTech Scanner as compared to the
      Perometer, which is currently used to screen for breast cancer-related lymphedema. The focus
      of the study is to compare the absolute volumes of the upper-extremity as measured by each
      device and to compare the time it takes to measure both arms of a patient using each device
      to assess clinical feasibility.
