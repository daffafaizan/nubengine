
    
      This is a multi-center, Observational study consisting of a one time survey to assess pain
      management satisfaction for patients with non-cancer pain. The purpose of this study is to
      develop a large clinical database for the results of the pain management satisfaction
      questionnaire. All information collected from hospitals will be used to evaluate patient
      satisfaction of pain management and to improve the quality of life for patients with
      non-cancer pain Observational study - No study drug administered
