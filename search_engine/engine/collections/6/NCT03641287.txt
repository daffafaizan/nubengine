
    
      OUTLINE:

      Patients are randomized to 1 of 2 arms.

      ARM I (EXERCISE INTERVENTION): Patients meet with exercise physiologist for 1, 60 minute
      session. Patients then receive individualized exercise prescription with goal of moderate
      aerobic exercise over 150 minutes per week at home for up to 24 weeks. Patients also receive
      telephone-based motivational support by exercise physiologist weekly for 24 weeks.

      ARM II (CONTROL GROUP): Patients maintain habitual levels of physical activity and receive
      general education material about ovarian cancer and survivorship for 24 weeks. After 24
      weeks, patients are offered exercise intervention.
