
    
      Primary Objective:

      To compare 5-year overall survival and 5-year disease-free survival amongst patients who
      undergo a QM-B hysterectomy versus those who undergo a QM-C hysterectomy for early stage
      cervical cancer.

      Secondary Objectives:

      Compare operation time between arms. Compare blood loss between arms. Compare blood
      transfusion between arms. Compare intraoperative complications between arms. Compare
      postoperative complications between arms. Compare quality of lifes between arms.
