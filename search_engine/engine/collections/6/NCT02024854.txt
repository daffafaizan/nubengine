
    
      Power calculations are based on a difference in Bayley Scale of Infant Development III,
      cognitive scale at two years corrected age of about 7,5 mean score =0.5 SD (α=0,05, β=0.80).
      Based on this analysis, 64 preterm infants are needed in each group to yield statistic
      significance. Allowing for withdrawals, the sample size is set to 68 in each group.
