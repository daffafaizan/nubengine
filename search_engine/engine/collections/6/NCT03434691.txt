
    
      Prospective randomized double blinded study. Investigators planned to enroll 60 cases
      diagnosed with septic shock All patients will be sedated with Midazolam and remifentanyl in
      accordance with a local unit protocol.

      After a period of six hours of hemodynamic stability, patients were randomized to receive
      either continuous infusion of Dexmedetomidine at 0.4 Î¼g/kg per hour and remifentanyl (DEX
      group) or a continuous infusion of a Midazolam and remifentanyl (MDZ Group).
