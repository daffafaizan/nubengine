
    
      Study Objectives:

      Primary:

      -To assess the efficacy of hypofractionated radiotherapy followed by immediate surgical
      resection in the treatment of soft tissue sarcoma

      Secondary:

      - To assess the side effect profile, late side effects criteria, and major and minor
      post-operative complication rates.

      Exploratory:

        -  To assess patient related factors which may influence wound complication rates.

        -  To assess patient-reported functional and satisfaction outcomes.

        -  To assess margin status and percent necrosis at time of surgery.
