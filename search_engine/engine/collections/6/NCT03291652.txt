
    
      Potential candidates will undergo a cranial MRI and MR angiography. Patients who are found
      signal void in a relevant intracranial internal carotid artery or middle cerebral artery will
      proceed to a 30-minute TCD monitoring for MES an angiograms will be analyzed by investigators
      blind to subjects' clinical information. All recruited patients will receive standard medical
      therapy throughout the investigation period.
