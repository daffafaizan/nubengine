
    
      Fifty-four patients were given informed consent before enrolling the study. Non-invasive
      blood pressure monitor, electrocardiogram, pulse oxymetry, and Conox probe were set on the
      subjects in the operation room. Baseline hemodynamic is noted. General anesthesia induction
      was done by fentanyl 2.5 mcg/kg and propofol 2 mg/kg. Endotracheal tube intubation was
      facilitated with rocuronium 0.8 mg/kg as muscle relaxant. qNox values right before intubation
      and after intubation is noted, as well as hemodynamic changes in one minute after intubation
