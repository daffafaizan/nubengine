
    
      In the post-hoc analysis the carryover and reverse carryover effect will be analyzed and
      correlated to a number of parameters including, but not limited to: Indication, treatment
      duration, symptom duration, preoperative pain score, stimulation paradigm, gender, age.

      Data will be collected and stored using a dedicated REDCap database under the auspices of
      Aarhus University.
