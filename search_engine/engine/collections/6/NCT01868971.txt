
    
      The EndoAid Ltd. EndoRings is used during endoscopy procedures. The EndoRings is a short
      silicon rubber tube with flexible circular wings that attaches to the distal end of the
      endoscope to facilitate endoscopic therapy.

      The EndoRings is designed to fit specific endoscopes (as designated on the packaging), and is
      supplied sterile following ETO sterilization and is single use only.

      Indications for Use:

      To be attached to the distal end of the endoscope to facilitate endoscopic therapy, to be
      used for the following:

        -  Keeping the suitable depth of endoscope's view field

        -  Helping the endoscope with being inserted into the gastrointestinal tract

      Patient Population: The study population is comprised of patients indicated for colonoscopy.

      Study Design: Single-center study. A single treatment group of 60 patients. No. of Patients:
      Up to sixty (60) treated patients will be enrolled in the study.
