
    
      A total of 52 children with developmental delays will be enrolled. The participants will be
      randomized into two groups, including study group (with insoles) and control group (without
      insoles).

      The participants in the study group will be evaluated at baseline, that is before the
      customized insoles are prescribed. All of evaluations, including physical function, balance,
      and quality of life will be re-evaluated up to 12 weeks after insoles wearing.

      The control group will be evaluated at the baseline and up to 12 weeks.
