
    
      This was a prospective, randomized, two-armed study to assess the efficacy and safety of
      intravitreal conbercept injection plus PRP versus PRP in the treatment of nPDR patients
      with/without diabetic macular edema for a period of 1 year. This was an
      investigator-initiated study performed by department of ophthalmology, Ruijin hospital
      affiliated with Shanghai Jiaotong University School of Medicine.
