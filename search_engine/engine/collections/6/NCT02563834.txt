
    
      A novel Positron Emission Tomography (PET) probe, 16- 18-F-fluoro-4-thiapalmitate, will be
      used to evaluate myocardial atty acid uptake. Studies will be done in humans with type 2
      diabetes mellitus, and in controls. Studies will take place on 2 separate days, under fasting
      conditions and under insulin clamp conditions.
