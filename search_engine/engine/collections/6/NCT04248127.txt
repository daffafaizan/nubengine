
    
      Those qualified for enrollment will be randomized into a double-blind crossover study to
      consume 2 morning servings of a 0.6 cup (150g) of plain yogurt with : A) cane sugar added in
      an isocaloric level as the honey or, B) 1 tbsp of phenolic-rich honey for 4 weeks. A
      one-month washout between periods will be used. The participants will be instructed to
      consume one yogurt in replacement of or as part of breakfast, and as a late morning snack.
      They will also be instructed to not add any additional items to the yogurt. Therefore, each
      study participant will consume 2 tbsp. of honey a day for four weeks, which is a realistic
      amount typically consumed by honey users.
