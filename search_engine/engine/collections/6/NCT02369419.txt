
    
      Methods : Frailty is evaluated before implant ,and defined as <14/17 points using the
      ONCODAGE score, in 150 patients of > 70 years age, selected for CRT according to the ESC 2013
      guidelines. The response to CRT, estimated at 9 months, is defined as an improvement of at
      least 5 % of the LVEF and the absence of HF hospitalization or cardiovascular death.
