
    
      Ongoing pregnancy rate, defined as positive fetal heart action 9 weeks after the first
      positive pregnancy test. Number/diameter of follicles, number of oocytes retrieved, number of
      pronuclear oocytes (referred to as zygotes or pre-embryos in the UK), quality of pronuclear
      stage oocytes, number of embryos transferred, quality of embryos, number of frozen embryos,
      endometrial thickness and morphology on day of HCG administration, estradiol levels at day of
      HCG administration, implantation rate, number of days stimulated with gonadotrophins and
      number of ampoules used, clinical pregnancy rate at 6 weeks after the first positive
      pregnancy test, pregnancy outcome.
