      period of 31 days. Each session lasts 18 minutes.

      At the beginning and end of the study the following measures will be used to assess change in
      fitness, body composition and muscle metabolism. DXA scan, USS of thigh, VO2 peak cycling
      test, short performance battery tests, quality of life questionnaires and muscle biopsy.
