
    
      The sportive performance results from a range of determinations such as: physiological,
      biomechanical, morphological, psychological, social and environmental factors. The
      interactions between athletes, equipment, training and environment influences the development
      of athletes' performance. In the context of the coming Paris 2024 Paralympic Games, the
      performance construction for the exploration, the quantification and the management of all
      factors is an commun objective between sportive team, medical team and researchers.
