
    
      The case-control trial will include 96 participants. 48 of them diagnosed with supraspinatus
      tendinopathy by MRI and 48 healthy subjects.

      In this study the investigators will compare the outcome of tissue elasticity measured by
      sonoelastography, with MRI and conventional ultrasound.

      Patients will be recruited through different radiology department and healthy volunteers will
      be recruited primarily through advertising in local newspapers and social media.
