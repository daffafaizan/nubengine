
    
      The investigators evaluated the efficacy and safety of transbronchial biopsy (TBB) and
      bronchial brushing by endobronchial ultrasonography (EBUS) with a guide sheath (GS) as a
      guide for diagnosing peripheral pulmonary lesions (PPLs) without radiographic fluoroscopy.The
      study is designed as a two-center prospective randomized controlled trial. The participating
      centers are Department of pulmonary medicine and endoscope center, Shanghai chest Hospital
      affiliated to Shanghai JiaoTong University School, China. Department of pulmonary medicine,
      Fukushima Medical University, Japan.Patients are divided into two groups, EBUS-GS-X-ray group
      and EBUS-GS group.Each subject will be randomized to each group.The study is expected to
      enroll 100 patients at 2 centers (China:75, Fukushima:25).
