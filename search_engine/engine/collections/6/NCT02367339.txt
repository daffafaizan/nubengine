
    
      Prospective epidemiological study with voluntary non-medical staff member (i.e. nurses and
      nursing assistants) and medical staff in two French ICUs.

      Assessment of self-reported risk factors for MDR-bacteria colonization. Self-performed rectal
      swab with isolation of ESBL-producting Enterobacteriaceae, identification of bacterial
      species and antibiotic suspectibilities.

      Characterization of Escherichia coli ESBL.
