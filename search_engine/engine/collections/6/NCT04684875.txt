
    
      This is a prospective, non-randomized, multicenter follow-up study to collect long-term data
      on a cohort of patients who participated in the Aerin Medical TP668 study, "Clinical
      Evaluation of Low Power Radiofrequency Energy Applied to the Posterior Nasal Nerve Area for
      Symptomatic Relief of Chronic Rhinitis". The study will be conducted in a maximum of 5
      centers that participated and enrolled patients in the TP668 study.
