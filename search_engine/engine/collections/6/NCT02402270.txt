
    
      The purpose of this Phase 2 formulation screening/proof of concept study is to evaluate the
      safety, tolerability, and efficacy of up to 3 ECP-019 formulations/dosing regimens comprising
      a PEG 3350 Colon Prep Kit, containing electrolytes provided by the foods, in 3 formulations/
      dosing regimens as compared to 2 comparators in a Phase 2 study.
