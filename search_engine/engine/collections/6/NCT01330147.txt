
    
      Patients undergoing a tonsillectomy operation for non-cancerous reasons will be recruited for
      this study. They will be asked to complete two questionnaires (study and sexual practices),
      provide saliva, urine and a blood sample. Following anaesthesia, oropharyngeal brushings and
      a tonsillectomy will be performed and all the samples will be processed for evidence of HPV
      infection.
