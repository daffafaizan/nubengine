
    
      The use of abdominal aortic endoprostheses makes the treatment of infra-renal abdominal
      aortic aneurysms possible, with implantation via the femoral artery, thus avoiding a very
      invasive surgical procedure. The French National Authority for Health requires a 5-year
      follow-up as part of the renewal for reimbursement for these endoprostheses.
