
    
      Part 1: Single-Dose Regimen and Fasted-Fed Crossover - For the single-dose regimen,
      approximately 40 healthy male and female subjects will be enrolled in 1 of 5 single dose
      cohorts (designated as S1 through S5, respectively) in an ascending fashion.

      Part 2: Multiple-Dose Regimen

      - For the multiple-dose regimen, approximately 24 healthy male and female subjects age 18 to
      50 years old will be enrolled in 1 of the 3 multiple-dose cohorts (designated as M1 through
      M3, respectively) in an ascending fashion.
