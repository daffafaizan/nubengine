
    
      This is a retrospective, uncontrolled, multicenter, case history study to determine the
      natural history of visual function in patients with IRD phenotypically diagnosed as LCA or RP
      caused by autosomal recessive mutation in RPE65 or LRAT.

      Up to 60 subjects will be enrolled in this study at approximately 12 study centers in Canada,
      the US and Europe.
