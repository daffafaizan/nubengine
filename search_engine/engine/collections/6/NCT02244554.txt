
    
      This is a randomized, split-tattoo, open-label, single-center study of up to 25 subjects with
      tattoos containing black or dark blue ink, alone or in combination with other colors. It will
      compare the safety and efficacy of nanosecond and picosecond pulse durations with the Cutera
      Picosecond Q-Switched Nd:YAG laser for tattoo removal.
