
    
      The purpose of this study is to the evaluate the use of a close phone-based tele-monitoring
      platform will reflected by less hospital re-admissions, will change their generic and disease
      specific quality of life compared with usual care. Following this; it is also hypothesized
      that this will also lead to less patients' deaths. In addition the patients' satisfaction
      using the telemedicine service will be studied. A Cost-Effective Analysis will evaluate the
      tele-health service compared with the usual care from the health and social perspective.
