
    
      All runners will be examined for: demographical parameters, time of 5 Km run, distance of
      forward jumping with two leg.

      The sample will be divided into 2 research group: research group - all training with
      compression stocking and control group -training without compression stocking. Both groups
      will continue their training program for 8 weeks according to their coach. A second
      examination for the same tests at the end of the 8 weeks.
