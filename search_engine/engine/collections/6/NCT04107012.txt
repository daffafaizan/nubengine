
    
      White spot lesion (WSL) may develop around fixed orthodontic appliances. With clear aligners,
      however, the issue is not controversial. WSL will be monitored in 2 groups of patients; one
      group using clear aligners and one group wearing fixed orthodontic appliances for 3 months.
      QLF images will be taken before treatment (T0) and 3 months after commencement of the
      treatment (T1). The results will be compared between the two groups
