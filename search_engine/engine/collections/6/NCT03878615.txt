
    
      During hepatic surgery, blood and urine samples will be obtained at 3 times during surgery,
      at 3 h after resection of liver, at 24 h after resection of liver and at post operative day
      2, and subsequntly analyzed for the above mentioned substances. Routine labs will be
      monitored until patient is discharged from hospital. During surgery, hemodynamic parameters
      will be monitored by Picco calculations. Urine output and blood loss will be registred.
