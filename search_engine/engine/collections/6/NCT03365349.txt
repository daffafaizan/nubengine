
    
      This randomized prospective pilot study aims at comparing the impact of a "living theater"
      session with a simple "writing workshop " on emotional distress (Problem Areas in Diabetes
      Questionnaire PAID) and illness perception ( Illness Perception Questionnaire IPQR) during a
      5 day education course on Functional Insulin Therapy (FIT). Questionaries at T0 (course day 1
      ) and T1 (3 months later). qualitative interviews in a sub group of patients random G1 and G2
      subgroup at T1, with a general inductive approach to capture all representations
