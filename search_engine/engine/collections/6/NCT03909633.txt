
    
      The investigators want to study the effect of intravenous anesthesia on postoperative
      cognitive function in patients accepted gastroenteroscopy ,no additional medication or
      operation is involved.The investigators assess cognitive function through a series of tests
      for 4 times( within 3 days before the endoscopy ,2 hours after the endoscopy finished,1 week
      after the endoscopy,3 months after the endoscopy),including WCST、DSST、DST、HADS、MoCA,through
      self - contrast,the investigators will conclude whether intravenous anesthesia has bad impact
      on cognitive function or not .
