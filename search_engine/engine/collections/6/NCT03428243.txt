
    
      The purpose of this investigation is to follow-up subjects who have completed participation
      in the Treatment Group of Protocol C-16-TS11 - "Pivotal Study of the TruSculpt Radiofrequency
      Device for Circumferential Reduction" for the long-term (18 months) safety and efficacy of
      the truSculpt radiofrequency (RF) device for circumferential reduction in the abdominal and
      flank region.
