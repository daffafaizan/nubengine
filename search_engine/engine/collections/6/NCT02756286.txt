
    
      The purpose of this study is to build upon the success the novel approach and software for
      analyzing electroencephalography (EEG) data to identify biomarkers of ADHD in adults by
      further refining the method. Then to test the ability of this new assessment tool to
      differentiate between adults with ADHD and healthy controls by comparing the classification
      accuracy of the metrics from the new tool to the classification accuracy of existing
      behavioral tests for ADHD.
