
    
      The study will enroll 264 subjects overall in two groups (randomized 1:1). The study
      follow-up will occur at 1, 6 and 12 months post-stent implantation. All patients will undergo
      angiography assessment (DSA/CTA) at 12 months follow-up. Angiography assessment will be
      performed at baseline (pre- and post-procedure) and 12 months follow-up.
