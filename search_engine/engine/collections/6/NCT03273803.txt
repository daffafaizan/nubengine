
    
      Heart sound recording was done by researchers who were familiar with the use of the app and
      understand the principles of cardiac auscultation.

      Reference heart sounds were recorded using an electronic stethoscope (3M™ Littmann®
      Electronic Stethoscope Model 3200). Study devices included Samsung Galaxy S5 (model SM-G900),
      Galaxy S6 (SM-G920), and LG G3 (LG-F400).
