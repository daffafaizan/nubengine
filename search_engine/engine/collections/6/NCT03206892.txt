
    
      Laparoendoscopic single-site surgery; one of the most recent advances in the field of
      minimally invasive surgery, is the use of a single incision in the umbilicus for laparoscopic
      surgeries, is compared to the conventional multi-port laparoscopy for polycystic ovary
      syndrome in infertile women undergoing ovarian drilling as regards successful surgical
      procedure without conversion to laparotomy or use of an additional port in the single site
      group. in addition to operative time, intra- and post- operative complications and cosmetic
      outcome.
