
    
      The present study proposes to develop nutrition education strategies through multi-sector
      intervention in order to promote the consumption of healthy foods and improvements in the
      health status of the quilombola population.This is a non-randomized cluster trial in four
      quilombolas communities in Southern Brazil (Passo do Lorenço, Algodão, Serrinha do Cristal
      and Nova Palma), combining qualitative and quantitative methodologies.
