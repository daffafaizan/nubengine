
    
      This aim of this study is to determine the prevalence of oral habits among primary Egyptian
      school children in Cairo Governorate, Egypt.

      PICO:

      P: Egyptian primary school children of 7 - 10 years old

      O: Prevalence of oral habits

      Primary Outcome: Prevalence of Oral Habit; Device of Measurement: Questionnaire; Unit of
      Measurement: Binary.
