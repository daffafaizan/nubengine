
    
      A prospective single-arm study designed to further evaluate laparoscopic-assisted gastrectomy
      for advanced Gastric Cancer after neoadjuvant chemotherapy will be performed, to evaluate the
      safety, feasibility, long-term and oncological outcomes. The evaluation parameters are
      perioperative mobility and mortality, perioperative clinical efficacy, postoperative life
      quality, immune function and 3-year survival and recurrence rates.
