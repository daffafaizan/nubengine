
    
      Oral probiotics have previously been shown to prevent necrotizing enterocolitis in preterm
      infants. A few reports of sepsis by a probiotic bacteria have impeded implementation of oral
      probiotic supplementation in most NICUs. Considering the proven immunomodulatory effect of
      maternal probiotics on breastfed infants of atopic mothers, we would like to explore the
      indirect effects of maternal probiotics on preterm infants
