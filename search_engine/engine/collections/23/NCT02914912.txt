
    
      Patients with suspected chronic mesenteric ischemia (included truncus coeliacus compression
      syndrome) referred to vascular surgery department for investigation of splanchnic and
      mesenteric circulation shall inculded in this study.

      In addition to CT angiography patients shall be

        1. investigated with gastroscopy assisted laser Doppler flowmetry and light spectroscopy
           (GALS).

        2. Endoscopic ultrasound

        3. Transserosal laser Doppler flowmetry and lightspectroscopy
