
    
      G-colony stimulating factor(5ug/kg/day)will be administered twice subcutaneously for 3 days
      to three patients with liver cirrhosis. On the day 4th, plasmapheresis will be done to
      collect peripheral blood mononucleated cells

      . And then we are going to infuse the collected peripheral blood mononucleated cells of their
      own through the portal vein of each patients under ultrasonographic guidance.
