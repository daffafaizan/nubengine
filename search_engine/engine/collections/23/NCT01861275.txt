
    
      In prospective study the investigators include all over 18 years old patients who came to
      emergency room for ischaemic stroke. Exclusion criteria is not able to co-operate or need for
      intensive care unit.The investigators shall take 100 control patients who have ischaemic
      stroke and no thrombolysis and 100 study patients who will have thrombolysis. The
      investigators do sleep registration during hospital staying within 2 days after patients have
      felt ill.Follow-up registration will be done after 6 months at home.
