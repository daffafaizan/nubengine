
    
      This study was performed to evaluate the use of salivary gland and labial mucous membrane
      transplantation in patients with severe symblepharon and dry eye secondary to SJS. Labial
      mucous membrane and salivary gland transplantation showed to be a good option in the
      treatment of severe symblepharon and dry eye secondary to SJS. labial mucous membrane,
      salivary glands, symblepharon, entropion, transplantation, graft.
