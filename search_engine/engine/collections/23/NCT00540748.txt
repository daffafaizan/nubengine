
    
      All women seeking surgical abortion before 12 weeks of pregnancy will be invited to
      participate in the study. We will include 400 patients. Written consent is given. Pain is
      measured on a VAS-scale (1-10) 2 hours post operatively and repeated 2 days postoperatively.
      Nausea is categorized in 4 groups, from 1 (no nausea) to 4 (pronounced nausea with
      vomiting)and is registered together with bloodloss also during the hospital stay (2 hours)
      and 2 days postoperatively. The two groups will be compared.
