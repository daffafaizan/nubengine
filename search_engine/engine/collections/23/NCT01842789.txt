
    
      The intent of the TAG system is to assist the gynecologist in getting the tip of the Acessa
      Handpiece to the fibroid by creating an animated image overlay of the movements and
      placement.

      The study will be conducted in two parts:

      Part 1: User Preference Testing - users will be presented with various statements pertaining
      to the features of the system and asked to indicate their degree of agreement.

      Part 2: Time to target - will be measure by fibroid.
