
    
      The study will involve 12 subjects between 4 and 8 years of age, who routinely use
      comprehensive micronutrient supplementation as part of their dietary management.

      Primary Hypothesis:

      FruitiVits is an acceptable source of vitamin, mineral and trace elements for patients on a
      Ketogenic diet.

      Secondary Hypothesis:

      FruitiVits will be well accepted and tolerated when given orally to children on a Ketogenic
      diet.
