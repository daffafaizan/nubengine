
    
      Randomization was divided into two groups by using closed envelope method. Postoperative pain
      scores at rest at 1, 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, and 24 hours with VAS (0 = no pain, very severe pain
      = 10) using Visual Analogue Scale was made. VAS at rest and movement (coughing and taking a
      deep Breath) were evaluated. Patients with VAS> 4 received additional analgesics.
