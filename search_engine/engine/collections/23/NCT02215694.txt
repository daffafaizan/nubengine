
    
      A randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study was conducted on 128 nondiabetic and
      hypertriglyceridemic (Plasma Triglyceride, 150-500 mg/dL) subjects. Over a 12 week test
      period, the probiotic group consumed 2g of powder daily containing dual probiotic strains,
      while the placebo group consumed the same without probiotics.
