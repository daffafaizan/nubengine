
    
      Randomly assigned to one of three groups

        -  Group I : nebulized epinephrine + 0.9% saline + placebo

        -  Group II : nebulized epinephrine + 3% saline + placebo

        -  Group III : nebulized epinephrine + 0.9% saline + dexamethasone

      Group I received two treatments of nebulized epinephrine mixed 0.9% saline and a total of six
      oral doses of placebo, group II received nebulized epinephrine mixed 3% saline and oral
      placebo, and group III received nebulized epinephrine mixed 0.9% saline and oral
      dexamethasone.

      The primary outcome is hospital admission within 7 days after the day of enrollment
