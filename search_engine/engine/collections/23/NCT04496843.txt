
    
      Subjects are patients having a prescription for a one-night polysomnographic consultation in
      order to diagnose potential sleep disorders. The investigator will collect the consent before
      including any patient in this study. The study last one night per patient, during which
      saturation signal recordings and the sleep apnea detection will be made by the HWA09 watch,
      in comparison to the polysomnography.
