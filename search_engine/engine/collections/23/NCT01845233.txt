
    
      Patient with chronic hypoventilation with stable on long term treatment (LTMV) with
      non-invasive ventilation for at least 3 month taking part of our LTMV program will be
      included.

      In addition to clinical examination,report from ventilator software, nocturnal Sp02 and
      daytime arterial blood gas all patients will be investigated with sleep polygraphy and
      transcutaneous Co2.
