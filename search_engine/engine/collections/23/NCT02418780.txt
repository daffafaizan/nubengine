
    
      Specific Aim 1: To assess the feasibility of recruiting and retaining individuals with PD
      into a 6-month randomized controlled trial of Tai Chi exercise.

      Specific Aim 2: To collect preliminary data on the efficacy of Tai Chi on improving gait and
      balance dynamics, physical and cognitive function, exercise activity, self-efficacy, mood,
      and quality of life in individuals with PD.
