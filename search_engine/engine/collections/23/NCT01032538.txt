
    
      The objective of this prospective study is to determine (1) the time course of
      patient-relevant functional outcome evaluated by the Knee injury and Osteoarthritis Outcome
      Score (KOOS) and (2) the time course of range of motion (ROM) the first six years following
      Oxford Medial Unicondylar Knee prosthesis. Improvement of patient self-reported pain and
      daily function during the study period, are of particular interest. Thats it!
