
    
      This is a randomized controlled trial. The pedometer for one week has given to and filling
      one day food intake form asked from all participants. The weights and the heights of the
      participants were measured and the Beck depression scale for primary care, the modify Moresby
      scale, the EQ5D, the MDDQ and the motivation scale filled face to face with all participants.
      In intervention group the steps of the motivational short interview model were; increase
      patient awareness about his/her type 2 diabetes and its difficulties, then discuss
      alternative solutions for the problems the participant has and making decisions for action,
      and the third and fourth interview includes new alternative solutions for new difficulties if
      there is and promotion of self efficacy.
