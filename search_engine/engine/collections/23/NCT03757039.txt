
    
      Habitual contact lens wearers were randomized 1:1:1 to one of three commercially available
      multifocal contact lenses. Progressive addition lens wearers wore their habitual spectacles.
      Subjects were expected to attend 1 or 2 office visits. This study was terminated early by
      Alcon due to slow enrollment and inconsistency in measurement of the primary endpoint.
