
    
      Objective:

      To evaluate origins of Fournier's gangrene as a prognostic value in the morbidity and
      mortality.

      Patients and methods:

      This is a retrospective study where patients from a single hospital from 2007 to 2016 were
      included. They were categorized in 4 groups matching the origin of infection. In every group
      categorizing with the origin of infection and determined severity factors, days of hospital
      stay, Fournier's gangrene severity index and mortality. A statistic analysis will be done
      using lineal multivariable analysis.
