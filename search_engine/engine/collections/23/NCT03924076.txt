
    
      The type of the study is double blind randomize placebo-controlled trial with minimum 72
      subjects which divided into 2 groups i.e. 1) placebo group which will receive UHT milk (125
      mL) and 2) treatment group which will received UHT milk + paraprobiotic Lactobacillus
      plantarum IS-10506 5 x 1010 CFU/day (125 mL). The subjects have to consume one pack of milk
      every day for 90 consecutive days. Before and after intervention (day 30 and day 90), several
      data will be collected i.e. faecal sIgA, blood IgG, IgM, and IgA. The subjects recruited from
      elementary school in Yogyakarta District.
