
    
      Method: This study will recruit 150 peoples with hip fracture from hospitals in Taipei from
      Jan 1, 2014 to Dec 31, 2014. The subjects from wards are randomly divided into group A, B and
      Group C. Each group includes 50 cases of hip fracture. Group C received 14 days
      hospitalization for comprehensive rehabilitation and 6 times home rehabilitation services
      within 3 months; Group B received 14 days hospitalization for comprehensive rehabilitation
      and Group A was control group.
