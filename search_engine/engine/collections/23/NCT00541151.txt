
    
      This study is a post market study to evaluate variables of the implantation, safety, and
      outcomes in a representative population (SUI) indicated for AMS sling systems at various time
      points. The study duration is 2 year with an estimated 200 enrollment. The outcomes of the
      treatment will be evaluated via pad weight test, cough stress test, QoLs,and other patient
      administered questionnaires.
