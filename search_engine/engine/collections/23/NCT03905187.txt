
    
      The purposes of this study is to evaluate the improvement of a stress management involved
      cardiac rehabilitation program on the psychological states, quality of life and clinical
      outcomes of patients after acute myocardial infarction or heart failure. The investigators
      performed modified cardiac rehabilitation program involving stress management on the patients
      who suffered from acute myocardial infarction or severe heart failure who were admitted to
      the CCU. Then the psychological states, quality of life and clinical outcomes were followed
      up.
