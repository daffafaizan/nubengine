
    
      Patients are randomized to either standard care, low FODMAP diet or a normal FODMAP diet.
      Run-in on a low FODMAP diet, thereafter parallel design where one arm is assign a blinded
      food supplement with low FODMAP content and the other arm is assigned a blinded food
      supplement containing FODMAP. A control group is "watchful waiting".

      Symptom reports, blood, and fecal samples are collected.
