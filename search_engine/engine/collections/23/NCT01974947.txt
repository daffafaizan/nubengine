
    
      Assessment of sperm parameters and metabolic syndrome relations in men attending a
      Reproductive Biomedicine centre for an infertility of their couple.

      Secondary objectives are to study relationship between sperm parameters and:

        -  adiposity and insulin resistance

        -  inflammation

        -  oxydative stress in blood and semen

        -  hormonal status Innovative parameters will also be assessed to determine the quality of
           semen (length of fingers, wrist circumference and epigenetics studies)
