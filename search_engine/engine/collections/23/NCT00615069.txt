
    
      The primary objective of this study was to estimate the safety of the 31 mm GORE EXCLUDER®
      AAA Endoprosthesis as compared to open surgical repair when used in the treatment of
      infrarenal abdominal aortic aneurysms. A secondary objective was to compare device
      performance of the original GORE EXCLUDER® AAA Endoprosthesis to the 31mm EXCLUDER when used
      in the treatment of infrarenal abdominal aortic aneurysms.
