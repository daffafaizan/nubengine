
    
      Pain management and adequate anesthesia are of critical importance for the endodontist.
      Traditionally, mandibular teeth are anesthetized via inferior alveolar nerve block (IAN).
      However, this technique provides a marginal success rate of 19-56% in patients with
      irreversible pulpitis.Gow Gates technique introduced in 1973 for anesthetizing of mandibular
      molars with more accuracy, success and safety.
