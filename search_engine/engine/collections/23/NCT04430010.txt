
    
      The goal of this study is to help understand how to successfully implement and sustain
      evidence based programs (EBP) in the education sector. The study will look at how questions
      such as what teacher, program, and school characteristics impact sustainment of the EBP BEST
      in CLASS, what modifications to BEST in CLASS represent a sustained program, and how do
      teacher, program, and school characteristics influence sustainment of BEST in CLASS.
