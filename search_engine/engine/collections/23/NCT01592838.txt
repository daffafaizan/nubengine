
    
      This retrospective study will use data extracted from hospital databases during the period of
      the 1st of June 2004 until the 31st of May 2010. A coded list of all hospitalisations and of
      the different infection tests performed in children will be generated, together with a list
      of Full time equivalents (FTE) in paediatric ward and the number of sick leave days of the
      working personnel.
