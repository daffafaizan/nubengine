
    
      Patient Inclusion/Exclusion Criteria:

        1. New York Heart Association Class II/III HF with preserved ejection fraction (EF) of
           >50%.

        2. Nonsmokers

        3. Subject not taking Coumadin, Xarelto, Prasugrel or Pradaxa. Patients on Plavix for drug
           eluting stent will not be excluded.

        4. Ages between 40-65. We will exclude any women of child bearing potential
