
    
      Study population:

      patients with acute ischemic stroke who were admitted to the hospital within 7 days after the
      initial symptom onset.

      Program:

      There are multifaceted quality improvement interventions including:

        1. Evidence-based clinical pathway

        2. Standard operating procedures (SOP) of performance indicators

        3. A quality coordinator

        4. Monitoring and feedback system of performance measures

      Endpoints:

      Primary outcome:increase of adherence of evidence based treatment in clinical practice;
      Secondary outcome: total mortality, disability, and major cerebrovascular events
