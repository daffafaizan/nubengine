
    
      Routine monitorization (blood pressure, pulse, oxygen saturation with pulse oximetry and
      electrocardiogram) will be applied to all patients taken into the operation room.

      In Group P (TIVA), induction of anesthesia will be achieved with 2 mg/kg propofol, 0.6 mg/kg
      rocuronium, and 1 μ / kg fentanyl. Propofol and remifentanil will be used in the continuation
      of anesthesia.

      In Group S (Volatile), anesthesia will be induced with 2 mg / kg propofol, 0.6 mg / kg
      rocuronium and 1 μ / kg fentanyl. 50% nitrous oxide + 50% oxygen and 2% sevoflurane will be
      used in the continuation of anesthesia.

      All patients will receive 2 ml/kg/hour Ringer's lactate solution during the operation.
