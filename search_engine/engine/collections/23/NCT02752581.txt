
    
      The groups were compared in terms of patellar shock, knee effusion, knee scoring systems,
      range of motion, loss of labour, hospitalization time. Also different arthroscopic procedures
      such as meniscectomy, meniscus repair, synovectomy and microfracture were evaluated in terms
      of need for a suction drain. The controls was at 1st and 15th day, 6th week and 3rd month.
