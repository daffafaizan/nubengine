
    
      Study visit 1: about 16 hours - you will come to this visit fasting.

      You will have an oral glucose tolerance test which takes about 3 ½ hours. An IV line will be
      placed in your arm vein for blood draw purposes and will remain there throughout the testing.
      A blood sample will be drawn 15 minutes and just before you will drink a sugar solution
      consisting of 75 grams of glucose. Blood will be drawn at specific times after you consume
      the drink.

      You will have a second oral glucose tolerance test which takes about 3 ½ hours. An IV line
      will be placed in your arm vein for blood draw purposes and will remain there throughout the
      testing. A blood sample will be drawn 15 minutes and just before you will drink a sugar
      solution. Blood will be drawn at specific times after you consume the drink.
