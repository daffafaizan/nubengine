
    
      This clinical trial uses multicenter, single-arm, prospective trial design,people who met the
      enrollment requirements were performed with Ewata balloon guiding and stent type thrombectomy
      device.The clinical results were compared with those of other historical guiding catheter
      products combined with the stent type thrombectomy device to to evaluate the clinical
      efficacy and safety of Ewata balloon guide catheter in combination with a stent for
      thrombectomy.
