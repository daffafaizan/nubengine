
    
      A conjunctival and palpebral swab will executed at least 14 days before routine cataract
      surgery. Isolates will be identified using standard microbiological protocols. In vitro
      susceptibility will be determined using a breakpoint minimal inhibitory concentrations. In
      case of positivity, the swab will be repeated the day of surgery. Topical antibiotic may be
      used in case of positivity at the discretion of the surgeon. Descriptive statistics will be
      calculated.
