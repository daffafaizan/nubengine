
    
      The purpose of this study is to determine the effect of 7 days supplementation of
      alanyl-glutamine or glycine on the damaged intestinal barrier function, measured by
      lactulose/mannitol test on day 8, in children with persistent diarrhea or malnutrition; on
      diarrhea frequency and duration, weight gain, protein loss, intestinal inflammation, and
      lactoferrin excretion.
