
    
      The prime outcomes were the time in minutes from leave the postoperative care unit to the
      first patient-controlled intravenous analgesia press.The secondary outcomes were to compare
      the following: (1)blood concentrations of ropivacaine was measured at 0.5 h, 1 h, 2 h, 3 h,
      and 24 h after paravertebral nerve block; and (2) the patient's intraoperative opioid use,
      phenylephrine use, fluid intake, urine output, dry mouth score, and 24 h and 48 h visual
      analogue scale.
