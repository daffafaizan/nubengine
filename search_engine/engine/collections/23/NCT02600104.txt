
    
      This is an open-label, multi-center study. Subjects in this study will receive up to four (4)
      facial treatments in 3-8 weeks interval, with the PicoWayTM device-fractional hand piece
      1064nm and/or 532nm according to the study protocol. Subjects will be followed by phone 7
      days post first treatment by study staff, and will return for follow-up (FU) visits at the
      clinic at: 6 weeks and 12 weeks following the last treatment.

      Methodology described in the protocol to evaluate efficacy and safety of treatments will be
      carried out at each visit at the clinic.
