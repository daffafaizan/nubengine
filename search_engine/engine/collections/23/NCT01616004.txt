
    
      10 healthy volunteers will come twice to the pain medicine unit, at 1 visit they'll get N2O
      and in the second visit they'll get air as placebo, they won't know what they're getting.
      During the visit they'll get heat stimulus using QST and will have to report their pain using
      eVAS (scale from 0 to 100 when 0 is no pain and 100 is the worst pain imagine).
