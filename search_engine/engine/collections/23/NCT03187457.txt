
    
      This method validation study will endeavour to obtain evidence for:

        -  Recruitment methodology effectiveness

        -  The usefulness of the "Canestest" test as a screening tool

        -  The time required for the vaginal microbiota (particularly Lactobacillus count) to
           return to normal after treatment of BV infection with metronidazole.

        -  The time required for the Nugent Score to return to normal after treatment of BV
           infection with metronidazole.
