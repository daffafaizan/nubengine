
    
      Structure: Single center, parallel group, single-blind, randomized study There will be three
      treatment groups, two groups receiving a different, cumulative dose of Grass MATA MPL and the
      third group receiving placebo only.

      Duration: The duration of the study from screening to final visit after treatment is expected
      to be approximately 7 weeks. There will be a telephone follow up at 1, 3, 6 and 12 months
      after treatment.
