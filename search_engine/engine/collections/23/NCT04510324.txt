
    
      Single-center, randomized, single-blinded cross-over trial including healthy adult
      participants (N=40, omnivores, aged between 25 and 65 years, and with a body mass index (BMI)
      between 20 and 40 kg/m2 (see participation criteria below). Participants will be randomized
      to either red meat (cow burger) or plant "meat" (plant-based burger). The primary outcome
      will be the within-group change in the TMAO levels.
