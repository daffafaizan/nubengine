
    
      Patients with burned hands will realise prehension test to explore dexterity of hand when
      they wear compressive's treatments and with free hands.

      Each patient will realise each test with all conditions (free hands/ made to measure gloves/
      compressive bandaging).

      Running order of test and condition is randomly defined. Each patient realized this tests
      under the care and devices used at usual care. Realisation of this tests are not influenced
      by the study. Patient realised this tests during reeducation.
