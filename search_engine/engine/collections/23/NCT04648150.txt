
    
      Intervention 1 Name : VERUM

      Description :

      Experimental arm (VERUM): delivery of an active medical device for a period of 2 months. Then
      delivery of a second active medical device for a period of 4 months.

      Intervention 2 Name : SHAM

      Description:

      Control arm (SHAM): delivery of an inactive medical device for a period of 2 months of use
      then delivery of an active medical device from M2 to M6 after inclusion
