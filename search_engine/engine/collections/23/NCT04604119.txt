
    
      Our institution's ICU has served 31 beds for COVID-19 and 21 beds for other patients during
      pandemic with 27 attending physicians and 35 residents. Number of night shifts for attending
      physicians and residents were 3 and 8 per month, respectively. The aim of the study was to
      evaluate the by measuring anxiety level and burnout frequency of HCWs including attending
      physicians, residents and nurses in our institution's ICU.
