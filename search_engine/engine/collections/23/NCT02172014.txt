
    
      Comfort behavior scale consists of 7 components of alertness, calmness, respiratory response,
      crying, physical movement, muscle tone, and facial tension. Because intubated patients cannot
      be evaluated 'crying', however, we use 6 components among total of all 7 components, except
      'crying'.

      Adverse effect of fentanyl include hypotension, coma, and ileus. Unexpected event means the
      situation which study cannot last in, such as withdrawal of consent, necessity of drugs which
      can affect the level of consciousness of the patient, emergent interventional procedure, etc.
