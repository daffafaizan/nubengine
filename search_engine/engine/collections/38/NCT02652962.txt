
    
      This is a cross-over within parallel design. Parallel groups will receive Gelesis200 either 2
      times or 3 times in one day before meals. Within the parallel groups, subjects will
      cross-over to 4 arms: A) Gelesis200 10 min before meals, B) Gelesis200 30 min before meals,
      C) Placebo 10 min before meals, D) Placebo 30 min before meals. Postprandial glucose, insulin
      and subjective appetite ratings will also be measured.
