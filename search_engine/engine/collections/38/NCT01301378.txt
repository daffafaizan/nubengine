
    
      This study compares patients who will undergo Molteno 3 shunt implant surgery with 1 group
      receiving KeraSys patch graft and the 2nd group receiving Tutoplast patch graft. The
      investigators will examine the efficacy and safety of KeraSys in comparison with Tutoplast
      and monitor the long term effectiveness for preventing tube erosion in Molteno 3 tube shunt
      surgery for 1 year.
