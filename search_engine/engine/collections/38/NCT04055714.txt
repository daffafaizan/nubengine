
    
      This study is a prospective, non-significant risk, multi-center trial of patients with
      Eustachian tube dysfunction who are refractory to medical management. Patients will undergo
      balloon dilation of the Eustachian tubes in the office under local anesthesia. We will assess
      assess the peri-procedural and post-procedure pain levels, and determine the proportion of
      subjects experiencing normalization of tympanometry and improvement in quality of of life at
      6 weeks, 12 weeks, and 52 weeks post-treatment.
