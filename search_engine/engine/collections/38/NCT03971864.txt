
    
      Patients with acute coronary syndrome (ACS) who underwent percutaneous coronary intervention
      (PCI) and optical coherence tomography (OCT) are included in this study. Two skillful
      echocardiographers will evaluate peripheral artery plaque characteristics by
      ultrasound.Ultrasound parameters of peripheral artery will be compared among patients with
      different coronary culprit lesion characteristics, such as plaque rupture and plaque erosion.
