
    
      A Prospective, Observational, Multicenter Registry The primary objective of this registry is
      to document the safety and overall clinical performance of the MGuard Coronary Stent System
      in a "real world" patient population requiring stent implantation.

      The secondary objective is to assess the event rate in patient subgroups with specific
      clinical indications and/or vessel and/or lesion characteristics.
