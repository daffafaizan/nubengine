
    
      Mandibular exercises may have behavioral and cognitive action, educating the patient and
      serving as an instrument of control of parafunctional activity. It is a simple, easy, which
      seeks adherence to treatment and has negligible cost. A disadvantage is the need of
      motivation and discipline of the patient in carrying out the therapy daily. The occlusal
      splints, associated with educational guidance and counseling have world indication the
      possibility of promoting teeth protection, joint decompression, equalization of
      electromyographic activity, conscious control of parafunctional activity and strong placebo
      effect.
