
    
      The study will evaluate non-inferiority in clinical improvement in the intensity of
      expression lines over 16 weeks of drug Botulift (Laboratório Químico Farmacêutico Bergamo
      Ltd.) compared with Botox ® (Allergan Inc.). So that the homogeneity of the group opted to
      use in female patients with glabellar wrinkles grade 2 (moderate) and 3 (severe) Scale
      Standard Facial Wrinkles, established by Honecker et al. (2003). The first area of the face
      perceived in mime is the glabellar complex, located in the space between the eyebrows,
      wrinkles eventually show (Almeida et al., 2010).

      Patients will be evaluated at each visit, the doctor Principal Investigator and digital
      photos of the glabellar region at maximum contraction and relaxation will be obtained using
      the apparatus Visia (Canfield Imaging Systems, version 4.0.2) for further analysis by random
      team dermatologists.
