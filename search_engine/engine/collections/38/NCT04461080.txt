
    
      Population: Patients seen by interprofessional primary care teams who are living on estimated
      annual incomes that are below the Canadian Low-Income Cut-Off (LICO)

      Intervention: A Community Health Worker who implements an intervention that addresses a
      number of social needs, including improving income security, access to prescription
      medications, access to housing, reducing social isolation, paying for utilities and access to
      transportation

      Control: Information on community resources on social needs

      Outcome: Change in estimated annual income between baseline and at 6 months
