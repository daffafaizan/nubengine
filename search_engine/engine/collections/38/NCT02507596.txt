
    
      Thirty patients suffering from advanced chronic periodontitis were included in this
      randomized controlled clinical trial. Each subject contributed matched two- or three-walled
      intrabony defects. Patients were equally assigned into the experimental group;
      Nano-crystalline hydroxyapatite silica gel or the negative control group;open flap
      debridement. Clinical and radiographic parameters were recorded at baseline and at 3 and 6
      months postoperatively.
