
    
      The APPEA series is a national multicentric prospective cohort that included children and
      adults in France with acute appendicitis presenting between March 1st and June 30th, 2016.
      Data included demographic characteristics, medical history, symptoms, biology, radiology,
      treatments given during hospitalization, the modalities of the intervention, and any
      complications during a follow-up period of 6 months.
