
    
      The postoperative sore throat is a common complication after the general anesthesia which
      decreases the quality of life. Dexamethasone is known to effective for the prevention of
      postoperative sore throat. The combined effect of the dexamethasone and paracetamol for
      postoperative sore throat, however, is not investigated.

      The investigators want to evaluate the efficacy of the dexamethasone and paracetamol for the
      prevention of postoperative sore throat.
