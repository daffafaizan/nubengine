
    
      This is a non-interventional study, and as such there is no binding treatment strategy,
      diagnosis/treatment procedures or visit schedule. The investigator should administer Mayzent
      as labelled. Routine medical practice will apply to visit frequency and evaluation variables,
      and only these data will be collected by the sponsor as part of the study procedures. The
      investigator should record data in the applicable CRFs at every visit if possible.
