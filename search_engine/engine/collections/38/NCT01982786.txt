
    
      Before a big study is done, a smaller study (called a "feasibility study") is required to
      make sure that patients and physicians are willing to participate in a study comparing the
      two kinds of treatments, and to verify how the radiation therapy is given in different
      centres across Canada.

      The standard or usual treatment for this disease includes treatment with IGRT or IGRT plus
      HDR brachytherapy boost.
