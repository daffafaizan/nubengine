
    
      Design: The Akershus Cardiac Examination (ACE) 1950 Study is a population-based, prospective,
      cerebro- and cardiovascular age-cohort study of all men and women born in 1950 in Akershus
      County, Norway.

      Time schedule: Study inclusion and baseline examinations took place in the period September
      2012 - May 2015. Follow-up and sub-studies are ongoing.

      Current status: As per May 2018, follow-up and sub-studies are ongoing.
