
    
      1. Purpose

             -  To investigate diagnostic performance of CT and MR in intraductal papillary
                mucinous neoplasm with malignant change according to international consensus
                guidelines 2012

        2. Enroll criteria

             -  Patient who is suspected to have pancreas IPMN on imaging

             -  Interval between imaging study and surgery is less than 2 months

             -  Who is scheduled for surgery due to IPMN
