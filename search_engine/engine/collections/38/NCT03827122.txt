
    
      Experimental group we will use BTX-A administration group. All patients (Male/Female) with
      bruxism associated with chronic pain in masseter muscles bilaterally participated in this
      study. The patients age 18-60 years. According to the diagnostic grading system of bruxism
      all subjects will have an assessment including a bruxism questionnaire (i.e., oral history
      taking with specific focus on bruxism habits) plus a clinical examination to evaluate bruxism
      signs and symptoms.
