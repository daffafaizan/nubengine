
    
      Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) is one of the most important complications after Peripherally
      Inserted Central Catheter (PICC) insertion. This study goals are to understand when and why
      DVT develops. Patients will be studied with ultrasound for 4 weeks after PICC insertion, one
      visit a week. The final outcome of PICCs will be collected.
