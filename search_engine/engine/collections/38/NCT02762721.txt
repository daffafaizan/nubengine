
    
      This is a non-interventional, observational study of archived human tissue samples; no
      surgical procedures will be required and no treatment will be provided as part of this study.
      Approximately 150 archived tissue samples obtained from patients with iCCA or mixed HCC-CCA
      will be analyzed for somatic alteration status and will include cholangiocarcinoma-specific
      FGFR2 fusion isoforms.
