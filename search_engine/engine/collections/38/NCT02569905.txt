
    
      154 patients with American Society of Anesthesiologists physical status I-II, who were
      scheduled for colorectal operation under general anesthesia, were randomly assigned to
      receive flurbiprofen (Group F), parecoxib sodium (Group P ) or normal saline(Group S)40min
      before the end of surgery. Hemodynamic parameters were monitored. The occurrence of shivering
      postoperative nausea and vomiting were recorded during the recovery period. And visual
      analogue score (VAS) and ramsy sedation scale (RSS) were also recorded.
