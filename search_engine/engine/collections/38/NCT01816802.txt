
    
      This is a prospective, observational, single-arm, nonrandomized, multicenter study. Data will
      be collected to establish eligibility, at baseline, during the stimulation and in vitro
      fertilization process, during embryo culture, at embryo transfer, at 12-18 days post egg
      retrieval to verify biochemical pregnancy, and then typically at 5-6 weeks gestational weeks
      to verify clinical pregnancy.
