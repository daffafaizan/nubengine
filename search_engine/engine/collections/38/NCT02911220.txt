
    
      Patients with non-atopic asthma will be identified in consultation pulmonology at Laennec
      Hospital (CHU Nantes) from the consultant population to severe asthma.

      These patients will be treated as index case.

      The investigator will conduct a family tree of the index case to identify families where the
      number of healthy individuals and those with non-atopic asthma makes relevant a family
      genetic study. Before initiating the family screening, it is necessary to have notion of at
      least two individuals carrying non-atopic asthma at least two generations in the family.

      Related agreeing to participate in this study will be included.
