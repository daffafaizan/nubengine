
    
      The aim is to track the changes in disease prevalence of people living in impoverished urban
      communities in Lima over the next 10 years.

      Based on data collected in prior years and in an ongoing manner, Ohio University Global
      Health teams will be introducing interventions focused to positively impact disease states
      and trends in this population.

      The current focus is on chronic lifestyle related diseases and their precursors:
      cardiovascular disease, hypertension, dyslipidemia, diabetes, and overweight / obesity.
