
    
      Treatment: Once patients are diagnosed with chronic GVHD, the combination therapy should be
      initiated as soon as possible. 1. Prednisone: 1mg/kg/d po. Taper steroid every two weeks
      according to patient's response. 2. SHR0302 QD po. for at least 28 days. Indication for
      stopping SHR0302 treatment: 1. No response after SHR0302 treatment for 12 weeks. 2. Develop
      life-threatening complication. 3. ANC<0.5×10e9/L or PLT< 30×10e9/L.
