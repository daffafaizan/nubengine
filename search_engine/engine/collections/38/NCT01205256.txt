
    
      Study Procedures Following Surgery:

        -  VAS scores will be recorded at intervals of 1,2,3,4,5,6,8,12,16,20,24, 36, 48 , 72 hrs
           (after extubation).

        -  Blood samples for PK will require 5ml per sample and may be obtained from an existing IV
           or direct phlebotomy and will be obtained at 0min, 5min, 10 min, 15min, 20 min, 40 min,
           1hr, 2hrs, 3hrs, 4hrs, 5hrs, 6hrs, 8hrs, 10 hrs, 12hrs, 24hrs, 48 hrs, 72hrs, and 96hrs
           after study drug administration.
