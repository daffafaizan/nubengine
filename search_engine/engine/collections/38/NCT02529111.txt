
    
      The software "Echonavigator" will be used after the introduction of the percutaneous closure
      of ASD prosthesis. The image fusion on fluoroscopy will then be applied and the bias between
      the generated ultrasound image and the X-ray view of the prosthesis will be measured. The
      center marker visible prosthesis ultrasound and fluoroscopy used as a reference marker.
