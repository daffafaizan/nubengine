
    
      Unlike other organs which show catch up in preterm infants after one year of life, the pineal
      gland shows persistent delay in melatonin production. This field of research has been
      neglected lately although there are published recommendations for decrease of bright light in
      NICUs which prevents melatonin development and production, and also recommendations for
      melatonin treatment in cases respiration recovery in hospitalized preterm infants.
