
    
      Currently, the standard of care is to routinely obtain an X-ray (XR) after every nasogastric
      tube (NGT) placement in the pediatric intensive care unit (PICU). After obtaining informed
      consent and the child's assent, a short (less than 10 minutes) ultrasound (US) scan of the
      abdomen within 2 hours of the routine XR.
