
    
      The target sample of 200 employee participants will be recruited from two healthcare
      organizations. The Beyond Silence program is based on principles of contact-based education,
      and will include six 1.5-2 hour in-person sessions conducted every other week, interspersed
      with five online resource sessions. The Mental Health First Aid training is a standardized,
      12-hour evidence-based mental health literacy training program that has been implemented in a
      range of national and international settings. This program is typically offered over two full
      days.
