
    
      Total of 20 patients were enrolled and randomized in this study. There are two groups, 10
      patients in osteonecrotic device group; 10 incore decompression group. After their baseline
      data has been collected and their surgical procedure has been completed, the following
      postoperative data collection are then continued to 6 weeks, 12 weeks, 6 months, one year,
      and two years' prior from the start of their surgery, in order to understand the new approach
      of the osteonecrotic device's safety in comparison to the core decompression surgical
      procedure.
