
    
      Up to 6 hours before the scheduled arrival time, formula-fed babies may be given formula. Up
      to 4 hours before the scheduled arrival time, breastfed babies may nurse. Up to 2 hours
      before the scheduled arrival time, give only clear liquids. Clear liquids include water,
      Pedialyte®, Kool-Aid® and juices you can see through, such as apple or white grape juice. In
      the 2 hours before the scheduled arrival time, give nothing to eat or drink. Induction of
      general anaesthesia will be administered followed by local anaethesia. Then, Surgical
      prodecure will be done either following the conventional two flap palatoplasty technique or
      the Furlow double-opposing z-plasty technique.
