
    
      This study adopts the method of cross-sectional survey to collect the relevant data of
      Chinese college students' mobile phone addiction and FGIDs (IBS, FD) in the form of online
      and face-to-face questionnaire, so as to understand the situation of Chinese college
      students' mobile phone addiction and the incidence of FGIDs (IBS, FD), and explore the
      correlation, so as to provide new ideas and scientific basis for the prevention, diagnosis
      and treatment of FGIDs.
