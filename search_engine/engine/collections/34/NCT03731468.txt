
    
      80 patients who will be undergoing elective thyroidectomy will be randomly allocated into one
      of two groups ; group A will receive 20 mg isobaric bupivacaine + 8 mg dexamethasone(diluted
      in 5 ml) group B will receive 20 mg isobaric bupivacaine + 1 ml normal saline. block will be
      done under complete a septic condition after induction of general anaesthesia.
