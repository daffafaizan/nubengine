
    
      In this study 445 men and women age 65 and older were enrolled for three years each. Subjects
      were randomized to treatment with 500 mg of calcium s calcium citrate malate plus 700 IU of
      vitamin D daily or placebo. Subjects came to the research center every six months for
      biochemical and bone mineral density measurements. Changes in these measures over the three
      years were determined in each group and compared.
