
    
      Methods In the orthopedic department of our hospital, 38 infants and newborns, (57 feet) ,all
      the patients were under the age of 3 months with congenital clubfoot underwent polyaxial
      braces between Jan, 2011 to Oct ,2013. Polyaxial brace was used for the deformity correction.
      Then, during the following 1-3 years, the Dennis braces were used during the night in case of
      recurrence. Prospective follow-up for a mean duration of 25 months (range, 15-36months) was
      carried out. The efficacy of the treatment was assessed by Pirani's scoring system before and
      after treatment.
