
    
      This study will evaluate the clinical performance of rotating platform and fixed bearing knee
      implants. This will be done by obtaining a series of primary total knee replacements.
      Patients will receive either the P.F.C.® Sigma™ Rotating Platform Cruciate Retaining Knee
      System or the P.F.C.® Sigma™ Fixed Cruciate Retaining Knee System and assignment is
      randomized.
