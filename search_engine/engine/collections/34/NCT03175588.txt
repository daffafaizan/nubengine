
    
      100 people living in Bahcelievler nursing home will be evaluated and volunteers who meet the
      criteria will be included in the study. Persons will be placed in the study groups in
      consideration of their interests. One group will be included in Nintendo Wii program with
      balance board, which is the most frequently preferred game consoles in rehabilitation, and
      video-games will run for 30 minutes two days a week for 8 weeks. The other group will
      participate a program consisting of various physical activities for the same duration; The
      results of both groups will be compared with a control group.
