
    
      105 participants with functional dyspepsia will be allocated into three groups: Low-He point
      group, Mu point group and He-Mu-point combination group.Every participant will receive 20
      sessions of manual acupuncture treatment during 4 weeks. Clinical evaluations will be
      assessed three times, as measured after randomization, 10 and 20 sessions of treatment.The
      fMRI scans will be conducted twice, after randomization and treatment completion.
