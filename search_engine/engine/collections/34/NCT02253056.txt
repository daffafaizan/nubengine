
    
      Participants in the intervention group receive an initial group training for fasting. Then
      all participants will observe one fixed fasting day per week (monday) over a total period of
      eight weeks.

      Participants in the control group receive an initial group training for healthy diet
      according to current German guidelines (DGE). Moreover, they will receive the same offer as
      in the intervention group at the end of the 8-week study period. Until then the control group
      participants follow a regular healthy diet.
