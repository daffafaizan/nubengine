
    
      This study will include 15 patients who are scheduled to undergo partial maxillectomy as part
      of a prescribed treatment program prior to their eligibility for the study. The patients will
      undergo the surgery at the Department of Otolaryngology or Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery and
      will be given an obturator during surgery by a physician from the Internal and Maxillofacial
      Rehabilitation Department of the School of Dentistry .
