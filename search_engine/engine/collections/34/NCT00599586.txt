
    
      The purpose of the study is to testify whether acupuncture is effective for migraine, through
      treating migraine patients for a month, using different acupoints according to literatures of
      treating migraine with acupuncture, and try to provide clinical evidence for the hypothesis
      that"Acupuncture effect is based on meridians, and gathering of meridian Qi is the key
      point."
