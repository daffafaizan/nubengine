
    
      Hyperuricemia patients who have the ability to complete the intervention study in the early
      stage of study were selected as the research subjects. A randomized controlled double blind
      control method was used to study the effect of one year of anthocyanins intervention. Serum
      UA levels, oxidative stress, AGEs, and endothelial function are detecting in patients with
      hyperuricemia who were treated with anthocyanins and placebo. Providing scientific basis of
      anthocyanins for the treatment with hyperuricemia and delaying atherosclerosis.
