
    
      The main objective of the study will be to quantify the rate of subjects seeking help in our
      units meeting the criteria of subject with an ultra-high risk of psychosis (UHR).

      Secondary objectives:

        -  Observe the transition rate of these high risk patients at one year.

        -  Study the socio-demographic and general psychopathology profiles of 12 - 25 year olds
           using the services of CMP, CASADO, mobile team and RCL.

        -  Monitor the development of school and professional integration, the overall functioning
           and self-esteem of subjects at six months and one year.
