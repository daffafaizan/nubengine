
    
      This study is broken up into 5 groups:

      The first is a pre-analytical study to determinate optimum conditions for lipocalin samples.

      The second focuses on intra-individual variations of lipocalin levels in blood and urine.

      The third determinate the normal values of lipocalin in blood and urine. Fourthly, we study
      the role of renal function on serum and urines lipocalin levels.

      Fifthly, we study the role of anti-aldosterone treatment on patients who suffer of primary
      hyperaldosteronism.
