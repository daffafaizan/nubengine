
    
      "Атопик Soothing Cream" is a lipid complex composed of barley grain, Butyrospermum Parkii and
      Argania Spinosa Kernel oil, in a mixture with panthenol and bisabolol.

      Investigators concure that combination of these substances in "Атопик Soothing Cream" will be
      efficacious non-steroidal treatment of Atopic Dermatiti ( AD).
