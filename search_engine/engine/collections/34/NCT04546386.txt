
    
      The role of the optical coherence tomography angiography to detect the changes in retinal
      vascular networks in patients after 48 hours and 1 year from the endoscopic endonasal surgery
      to remove an intra-suprasellar pituitary adenoma compressing the optic nerve.

      The optical coherence tomography angiography allows a detailed and quantitative analysis of
      the retinal vascular networks and to follow their changes over time.
