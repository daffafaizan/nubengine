
    
      All patients in both experimental and control groups received CPT protocol consisted of 30
      minutes of manual resistance exercise (3 sets/10 repetitions each for knee extensors and
      flexors) and 30 minutes of treadmill training, short breaks (1-5 minutes) were allowed,
      depending on the patient tolerance. The training was conducted for 3 days per week for 8
      weeks. The patients in CPT+LMV experimental group received localized muscle vibration at the
      end of each CPT session. The LMV was applied for 10 minutes perpendicularly along quadriceps
      muscle fibers. The frequency set at 30 Hz, amplitude of 2 mm.
