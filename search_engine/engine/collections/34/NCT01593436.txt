
    
      Groups will be assessed by actigraphy, polysomnography and questionnaires for assessing
      stress, depression, anxiety, quality of life, self-pity, subjective states such as sedation
      and other physical or mental attitudes, vitality, emotional state and attentiveness.
      Moreover, prospective memory participants will be evaluated by means of a task as close as
      possible to real life conditions of the volunteers (green) and also the working memory is
      assessed by a test called specific counting span. Menopausal women will be evaluated for the
      presence or absence of bruxism compared to ordinary meditators, all postmenopausal.
