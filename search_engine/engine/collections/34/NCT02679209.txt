
    
      Twenty-two patients suffering from advanced chronic periodontitis were included in this
      randomized controlled clinical trial. Each subject contributed had two- or three-walled
      intrabony defects. Patients were randomly assigned into two groups; bioresorbable membrane or
      bone allograft. Clinical parameters were recorded at baseline and at 3 and 6 months
      postoperatively. Radiographic parameters were recorded at baseline and at 6 months
      postoperatively.
