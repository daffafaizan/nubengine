
    
      After being informed about the study, all patients meeting the inclusion/exclusion criteria
      and giving written informed consent will be randomized in a double-blind manner in a ratio
      1:1 to receive the antioxidant supplementation associated with probiotics (Myo-Inositol,
      D-Chiro-Inositol, folic acid, selenium, vitamin D, Melatonin, Lactobacillus rhamnosus,
      Lactobacillus cripatus and Lactobacillus plantarum) or placebo (folic acid) once daily.
