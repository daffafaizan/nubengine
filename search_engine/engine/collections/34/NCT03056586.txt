
    
      All patients going for vaginal surgery for genital prolapse, stage 3-4, with cystocele,
      rectocele or uterine cervix prominent from the vagina. These patients, after signing an
      informed consent, a vaginal pessary will insert at the end of the operation and will stay for
      4 weeks post-op.

      After 4 weeks the pessary will be removed, and the patient will followed for 2 years, for
      recurrence of prolapse.
