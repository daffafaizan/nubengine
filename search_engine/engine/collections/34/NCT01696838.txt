
    
      The purpose of this study is to determine whether acupuncture or moxibustion therapies are
      effective in the treatment of Crohn's disease. meanwhile, the investigators aim to evaluate
      the efficacy by functional magnetic resonance imaging.

      Through treating quiescent Crohn's disease by electroacupuncture or moxibustion therapies for
      12 weeks, Compare the different brain activation by the different therapies and observe the
      correlation between brain activation and efficacy.
