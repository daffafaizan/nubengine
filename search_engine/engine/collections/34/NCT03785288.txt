
    
      Patients are randomized to Arm 1: HDR vaginal brachytherapy 7 Gy in 3 fractions prescribed at
      5mm. Radiation delivered 4-12 weeks after surgery OR Arm 2: HDR vaginal brachytherapy 4Gy in
      6 fractions prescribed at the cylinder surface. Radiation delivered 4-12 weeks after surgery.
      Participants in both arms will receive standard vaginal dilator for use after treatment (to
      promote healing). 1 and 2 year follow up assessments are performed.
