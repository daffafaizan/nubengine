
    
      100 women were either allocated to received Implanon contraceptive implant, the LNG-IUS or
      TCu380A copper-IUD. The women were allocated according to their preference on each of the
      contraceptive methods.

      half of the women will receive the common counseling at the clinic and the other 50% will
