
    
      This longitudinal observational cohort followed the growth and development of non-Hispanic
      white girls from age 5 to 15 years, with a focus on the development of the controls of food
      intake. Data was collected biennially from girls and their mothers and fathers. Measures
      included anthropometrics, questionnaires and laboratory measures of eating behavior, dietary
      recalls, psychosocial characteristics, and parenting measures.
