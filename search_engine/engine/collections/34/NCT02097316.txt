
    
      The JewelPump (Debiotech) will be compared to the conventional pumps. Patients will treated
      with the JewelPump during 5 days, and with their usual pump during 5 other days.

      The study will be conduct with 15 patients at the following investigation centers : Corbeil
      Hospital, CHU of Strasbourg, and CHRU of Marseille.
