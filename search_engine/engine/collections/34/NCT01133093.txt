
    
      Data collection comprised 3 sequential stages:

        1. computer-assisted telephone interview to obtain information on lifestyle, knowledge and
           attitudes about cardiovascular disease risk factors, and the signs and symptoms of heart
           attack and stroke, subjective health, and morbidity;

        2. first homevisit, to collect blood and urine samples for analysis by a central
           laboratory;

        3. second home visit, to measure anthropometric variables and blood pressure and to
           administer a computer-assisted dietary history; data on functional limitations are also
           collected from participants aged 65 years and older.
