
    
      The investigator targets the patients with knee osteoarthritis. After treatment in 3 groups -
      Cettum treatment, traditional indirect moxibustion treatment, and usual care groups - the
      investigators will compare the effects of pain relieving and daily performing improvement. So
      the investigators will confirm the efficacy of Cettum, and further the best treatment method.
      In addition, the investigators are going to evaluate the safety of abnormal reaction during
      the treatment.
