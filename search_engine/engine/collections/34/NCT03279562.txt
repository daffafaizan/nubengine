
    
      The specific aims and hypotheses of the proposed study are as follows:

      Specific Aim 1: To evaluate the safety, feasibility, acceptability, and preliminary efficacy
      of treatment of alcohol and opioid use disorders combining Nalmefene and recovery coaching
      for rehabilitation of detoxified opioid dependent individuals in Russia.

      Specific Aim 2: To further develop and refine the recovery coaching/counseling intervention
      and to train additional cadre of clinicians and researchers in St. Petersburg, Russia.
