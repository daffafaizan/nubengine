
    
      Tooth erosion is the loss of dental hard tissue due to acidic agents without bacteria
      involvement.

      Mouthguard is a protective appliance use for covering the teeth and gums to prevent the teeth
      from acidic chlorinated water.

      Desensitizing toothpaste is toothpaste containing 8 percent arginine and calcium carbonate
      that can reduce tooth demineralization and promote remineralization.
