
    
      This is an observational, multicenter, cross-sectional study to be conducted at 30 sites in
      India. Endocrinologist/ Diabetologist from multiple private clinics will evaluate the Type 2
      Diabetes patients. The study will be conducted over 9-10 months and will be started after
      obtaining written approval of Independent Ethics Committee (IEC) and written Informed consent
      of the patient. . Each investigative site will be expected to enrol 100 subjects. All the
      procedures will be completed in a single day.
