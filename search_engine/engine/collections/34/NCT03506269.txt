
    
      The survey for was conducted in various location and clinics all over Pakistan. 1200 numbers
      of questionnaires were distributed of which 910 numbers responded from 1st January 2018 to
      28th February 2018. Those who agreed to be part of study where ask a self-structured
      questionnaire related to awareness and symptoms of menopause. Data was analyzed using SPSS
      Version 23.0. Frequencies were calculated for categorical data.
