
    
      Study hypothesis that people are tend to be depressed and the quality of life is affected
      negatively by Covid-19 pandemic. Although Turkey has affected moderately, the immediate
      change on depression and quality of life observable and measurable. It is aimed to
      investigate the depression and quality of life of Turkish society caused by Covid-19 pandemic
      and reveal the relationship between them. According to existing data and perspective of
      psychology and physiotherapy, potential solutions will be aligned.
