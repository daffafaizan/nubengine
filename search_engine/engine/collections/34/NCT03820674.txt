
    
      Energy Conservation+Problem Solving Therapy (EC+PST) Intervention: In this study, the EC+PST
      Intervention will ideally occur twice a week for 6 weeks with each session lasting
      approximately 30 minutes. The intervention will be individually delivered via telehealth;
      therefore, participants will receive the intervention in their home to minimize fatigue from
      the intervention.

      Health Education Intervention: The Health Education Intervention will occur once a week for 6
      weeks with each session lasting approximately 30 minutes. The Health Education Intervention
      will be delivered via telehealth; therefore, participants will receive the intervention at
      their convenient quiet place to minimize fatigue from the intervention.
