
    
      Women, following Caesarean delivery, have even more compelling reasons to receive optimal
      post-operative pain relief, for improved maternal and neonatal well-being. Also, adequate
      pain relief helps the patient to ambulate early and prevent any thrombotic incidents .
      Dexmedetomidine, an imidazole compound, is the pharmacologically active dextroisomer of
      medetomidine that displays specific and selective Î± 2-adrenoceptor agonist activity and
      causes sedation, analgesia without any delirium, or respiratory depression. The mechanism of
      action is unique and differs from those of currently used agents, including clonidine.
