
    
      1. Inclusion criteria 1) age: 18 - 80 2) a patients who had common bile duct dilatation
           because of malignant distal common bile duct obstruction in radiologically 3)
           Hyperbilirubinemia (total bilirubin >= 1.5 mg/dl) 4) inoperable state 5) a patients who
           agree to join this study

        2. Exclusion criteria 1) a patients who can not endure sedation 2) a patients with bleeding
           tendency (PT > 1.5 INR, PLT < 50,000) 3) a patients with systemic infection 4) a
           patients who had hilar obstruction
