
    
      Pilot study to evaluate the effect of the strain Lactobacillus PS11610 on the microbiota of
      the female and male genital tract in couples with fertility problems. Couples who have
      started treatment for artificial insemination or who are on the waiting list for an invitro
      fertilization treatment will be invited to participate. The estimated duration of the study
      will be 7 and a half months.

      Four visits are included in the study period.
