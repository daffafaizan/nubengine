
    
      The investigators also want to:

        -  To find the sensitivity, specificity and accuracy in PET/CT 1h and 3 h in the staging of
           lung cancer (N-/M-stage). Our gold standard is pathology, when not possible - follow-up
           (se Gold Standard 6.1.1)

        -  To compare SUVmax with the expression of GLUT1 and G-6-Pase in tumors

        -  To compare the expression of GLUT1 with the activity of G6Pase

        -  To compare SUVmax, RI, tumor/liver ratio, GLUT1 and G-6-Pase with the occurrence of
           relapse within a year after curative surgery
