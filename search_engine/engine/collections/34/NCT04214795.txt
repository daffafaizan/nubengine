
    
      All infants admitted in the Neonatal Department born with less than 32 weeks of gestational
      age are included.

      Data are prospectively recorded. Perinatal variables (Gestational age, materna condition at
      admission, antenatal steroids treatment, gender and results of histological analysis of the
      placenta) are recorded as well as respiratory status during hospitalization and morbidities
      at discharge. Results of neurodevelopment test performed at 24 months of corrected age are
      also included.
