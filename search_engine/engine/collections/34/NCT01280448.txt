
    
      The current study is a matched case-control study. The experimental group is the patients
      diagnosed as lung cancer at Taipei Medical University- Wanfang Hospital from January 2010 to
      December 2013, and one healthy patient will be matched to each case as the control group. The
      purpose of the current study is to investigate the association between genetic polymorphisms
      and lung cancer in the Taiwanese population and to analyze the correlation.
