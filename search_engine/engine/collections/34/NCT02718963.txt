
    
      -  Design: Prospective study

        -  Inclusion criteria of the patient group: who has a dysphagia symptom and confirmed by
           video-fluoroscopic swallowing study(N=10)

        -  Inclusion criteria of the control group: who does not have any dysphagia symptoms, and
           does not have any underlying disease(N=10)

        -  Setting: Hospital rehabilitation department

        -  Intervention: Participants are applied electrical stimulation by a "Synchronized
           Electrical Stimulation Device(SESD)"
