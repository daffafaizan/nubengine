
    
      A prospective study of the clinical results in women who followed the Long Antagonist
      protocol for Controlled Ovarian Stimulation (COS) versus women who followed Long Agonist
      protocol, in an In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) try. In particular, the Clinical Pregnancy Rates
      (CPR) and Live Birth Rates (LBR) were estimated for the two groups of patients. The number of
      the formed blastocysts was also measured in each group of patients.
