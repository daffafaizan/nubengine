
    
      Primary outcome :

      Post-operative morbidity at 30-day :

      Post operative complications occurred during 30 days post surgery, evaluated with CTCAE V3.0,
      and Complications grade 2.3.4 will be analyzed from day 1, at day 20 up to day 30.

      Evaluation :

      Pre operative : Day -30 to Day 0, Post operative : Day 10, Day 30, Day 90, Day 360.

      Secondary outcome :

      Post-operative mortality, Length of post operative hospital stay, Length of intensive care
      unit stay, QOL assessment, One year survival.
