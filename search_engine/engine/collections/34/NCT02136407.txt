
    
      Serum was collected during 12 months and analysed for 25(OH)D in 540 healthy blood donors.
      The blood donors answered questionnaires concerning vitamin D supplements, smoking, physical
      activity, sunbed use and holidays in sunny locations. Repeated serum samples were also
      collected from thrombocyte donors to study the intra-individual variations in S-25(OH)D.
