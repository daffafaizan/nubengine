
    
      The effect of exercise (EXR) on short-term food intake (FI) and subjective appetite was
      investigated in normal weight (NW), overweight (OW) and obese (OB) boys were randomized into
      4 treatments. Each boy consumed either a non-caloric sweetened control (CON) or GLU drink
      after either exercise (EXR) or sedentary activity. Subjects exercised at their ventilation
      threshold (VeT).
