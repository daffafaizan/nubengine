
    
      42 overweight-obese (BMI 25-35 kg/m2), sedentary females and males aged 45-65yr will be
      enrolled in this study.

      This study will be conducted in 2 randomized intervention groups;

        1. HIIT+ carbohydrate-rich beverage

        2. HIIT+ water.

      Both groups will train 3 times/week under supervision during 12 weeks (period 1) and remotely
      supervised during 40 weeks (period 2). Before, after and during each training period, there
      will be multiple metabolic measurements.
