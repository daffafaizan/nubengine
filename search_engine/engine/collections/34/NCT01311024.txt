
    
      The purpose of this study is to assess the indirect effects of a pneumococcal conjugate
      vaccine administered to infants on vaccinees' elder non-vaccinated siblings aged 3 to 7
      years.

      This study includes a cross-sectional sampling of nasopharyngeal and oropharyngeal swabs in
      2011 and a register-based follow-up study for invasive disease, hospital-diagnosed pneumonia,
      tympanostomy tube surgery and outpatient antimicrobial treatment.
