
    
      The main objective of our study is to assess the effects of protein and prebiotic fiber
      intake on changes in adiposity in an overweight and obese adult population.

      Primary objective - To determine the effect of 12 week protein (10 g/day) or prebiotic fiber
      (16 g/day) intake on changes in body composition, chiefly body fat.

      Secondary objective - To measure changes in appetite following 12 weeks of protein (10 g/day)
      or prebiotic fiber (16 g/day) intake.

      Other outcomes includes changes in quality of life ratings and gut microbiota.
