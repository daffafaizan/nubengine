
    
      A total of 388 students participated in first phase of study and total 60 students with
      moderate academic stress were filtered. Among those only 37 were physically inactive from
      last one month by the assessment through RAPA (Rapid assessment of physical activity). ASS
      (Academic stress scale), Cohen Perceived stress Scale and RAPA questionnaires were used to
      collect data. Three equally distributed groups were given three different modes of physical
      activities i.e light, moderate & vigorous.
