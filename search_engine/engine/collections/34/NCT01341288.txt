
    
      EUCLIDIAN (Endo-Uro Computed Lattice for Intratumoral Delivery, Implantation, Ablation with
      Nanosensing)

      The dose-volume histogram parameters of robotic brachytherapy will be compared to historical
      controls of the study institution. Short-term (<1 yr) health-related quality of life (HRQoL)
      and changes will be assessed using FACT-P and IPSS self-report questionnaires.
