
    
      This data collection protocol is designed to allow data and specimen collection of
      individuals with pediatric and adolescent gynecology (PAG) conditions such as: reproductive,
      endocrine, gynecologic tumors and skin conditions, menstrual abnormalities and congenital
      anomalies of the reproductive tract. Adults and children with pediatric and adolescent
      gynecology-related condition(s) may be enrolled under this protocol to collect clinical data
      and specimen for secondary review and analysis and to provide stimuli for new clinical
      research initiatives.
