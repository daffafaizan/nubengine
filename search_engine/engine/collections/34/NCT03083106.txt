
    
      Evaluate the safety and efficacy of the Zelegent, Inc. Elevo™ Kit Snoring Intervention Device
      (a minimally invasive, barbed, absorbable suture implant) in the reduction of simple snoring
      through subjective evaluation of snoring and objective snoring sound analysis.

      The Zelegent, Inc. Elevo™ Kit Snoring Intervention Device is intended to reduce or eliminate
      simple snoring in the target patient population via minimally invasive implantation of
      specialized barbed, absorbable sutures in the soft palate for the purpose of stiffening by
      way of shortening and lifting the soft palate thereby addressing the root cause of snoring.
