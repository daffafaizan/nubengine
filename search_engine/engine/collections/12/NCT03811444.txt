
    
      This study aims to evaluate the safety of the use of the safety lancets: Haemolance Plus,
      Prolance and MediSafe Solo in the prevention of NSI and to evaluate the user's opinion with
      regards to the handling characteristics of the medical devices.

      The simulated use clinical study will involve healthcare professionals (HCPs) who routinely
      use safety lancets to collect blood samples.
