
    
      Participants will be randomly assigned to 1 of 3 treatment arms: Shape Up Rhode Island (SURI;
      a community-based weight loss campaign), SURI plus an Internet-based behavioral weight loss
      program (SURI + IBWL), or SURI + IBWL + optional group sessions (SURI + IBWL + Group).
      Primary outcome is weight loss at 12-weeks.
