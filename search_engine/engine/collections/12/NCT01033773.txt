
    
      Patients with BG > 200mg/dL presenting to an urban tertiary care hospital ED were enrolled in
      a 4 week prospective intervention with historic self-controls. Subjects returned at 12-72
      hours, 2 and 4 weeks. Diabetes medications (including sulfonylureas, metformin and/or
      insulin) were initiated and/or adjusted at each visit using the intervention algorithm per
      presenting blood glucose and prior diabetes medications. Survival skills self-management
      education and navigation to outpatient services were provided.
