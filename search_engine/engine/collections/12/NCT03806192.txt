
    
      OUTLINE: Participants are randomized to 1 of 2 groups.

      GROUP A: Participants attend 5 psychoeducational counseling sessions over 30-60 minutes via
      telephone.

      GROUP B: Participants attend 5 psychoeducational counseling sessions over 30-60 minutes via
      video teleconference.

      After completion of study, participants are followed up at 4 months.
