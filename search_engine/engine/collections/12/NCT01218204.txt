
    
      This compound has been studied in healthy subjects and subjects with type II diabetes and is
      now being studied in subjects with dyslipidemia. Because many patients with dyslipidemia are
      on statins, it is important to study how GSK1292263 behaves when taken with a potent statin,
      atorvastatin. The cholesterol lowering drug, ezetimibe, is included for comparison.
