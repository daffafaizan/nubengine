
    
      The primary objective is to evaluate the clinical, functional and radiological results in
      patients treated with posterior MIPO for Humerus Diaphyseal Fractures With Extra-articular
      Distal Humeral Anatomical Plate.

      Clinical

        -  Pain

        -  ROM

      Functional

        -  Constant Score

        -  MEPS

        -  QuickDASH

      Radiological

      - Bone union

      Complications

        -  Infection

        -  Nerve injury
