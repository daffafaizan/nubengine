
    
      Remote ischemic preconditioning (RIPC) protocol will be induced at baseline and before each
      dose of anthracycline cardiac surgery and once before induction of anesthesia by 3 cycles of
      5-min upper or lower limb ischemia and 5-min reperfusion using a blood-pressure cuff inflated
      to 15 mmHg above the systolic blood pressure for 5 minutes followed by 5 minutes of cuff
      deflation to 0 mmHg.
