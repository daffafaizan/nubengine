
    
      Vietnamese women aged 30-65 years without medical problems that could interfere with the
      calcium consumption, such as kidney stone, kidney and liver diseases. The women will be
      excluded if they are blinded, deaf, or have general learning disability.

      Outcomes of the study are dietary calcium intake, calcium knowledge, and osteoporosis health
      belief. A quantitative questionnaire is being used to collect these data.
