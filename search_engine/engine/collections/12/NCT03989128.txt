
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. To evaluate patients' perceptions of overall difficulties when filling their opioid
      prescription.

      SECONDARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. To determine cancer patients' perceptions of financial, logistical, and psychosocial
      difficulties when filling their opioid prescription.

      II. To evaluate the association between patient characteristics and patient difficulty
      filling opioid prescriptions.

      OUTLINE:

      Participants complete a survey over 5-10 minutes.
