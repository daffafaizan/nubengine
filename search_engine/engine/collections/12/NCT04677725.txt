
    
      National multicenter registry in Brazil with initial plan of 25 sites from the 5 Brazilian
      regions. It will be coordinated by HCOR Research Institute which will perform regulatory,
      data and site management beyond academic leadership.NEAT is an observational cohort
      (Prospective registry of real world data) of patients with coronary and peripheral artery
      disease in an outopatient setting with 12 months of follow-up with collection of data at
      baseline, 6 months and 12 months.
