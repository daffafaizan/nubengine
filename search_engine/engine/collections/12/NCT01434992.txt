
    
      Our study aimed to achieve the following: (1) describe the H. pylori-related mucosal pattern
      in the gastric corpus using high-definition endoscopy; (2) evaluate the diagnostic accuracy
      for H. pylori detection; (3) find the optimal biopsy site for rapid urease test (RUT); (4)
      validate the inter- and intraobserver agreement in the assessment of endoscopic patterns.
