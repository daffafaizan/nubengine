
    
      Retrospectively identify women who received curative radiation treatment after breast
      conserving cancer surgery, in whom simulation in prone and in supine position was performed.
      Compare the treatment plans for doses to targets and to non-target organs, according to
      clinical parameters of body mass index, size of breast, location of tumor and side.
      Furthermore, evaluate whether a single CT slide is enough or not to predict a dosimetry
      advantage.
