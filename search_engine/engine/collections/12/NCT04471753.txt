
    
      Data were collected according to the Coma Recovery Scale-Revised operation guideline, the sum
      of the highest score on the evaluated subscales was taken as the final score, and the final
      diagnosis of patients was obtained (the highest score of each subscale was recorded). All
      participants were diagnosed by using Coma Recovery Scale-Revised five times at least within 7
      days. According to the results of the Coma Recovery Scale-Revised assessment, minimally
      conscious state and unresponsive wakefulness syndrome patients were distinguished.
