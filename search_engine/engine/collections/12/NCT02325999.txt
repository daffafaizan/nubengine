
    
      Patients with early stage of gastric cancer(T1a/T1bN0M0,NCCN2014.V1) will depart into two
      group randomized double-blind, then experimental group accept Endoscopic Submucosal
      Dissection combine with Laparoscopic Regional Lymph node Dissection ,and control group accept
      Endoscopic Submucosal Dissection only.The follow-up time is from operation to recrudescence.
