
    
      Patients matching the eligibility criteria were divided into two groups (not randomized). One
      group underwent a 7 weeks Low-Energy-Diet before bariatric surgery, the other group
      participated in an intensive lifestyle intervention program for 3 months.

      Data on arterial stiffness, body weight, body composition, blood pressure, blood samples,
      vital parameters are registered in the both groups at baseline, 7 weeks and after 1 year.
