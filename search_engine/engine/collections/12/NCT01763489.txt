
    
      The study will be conducted in 2 steps:

      In a first step, investigators will develop cases vignettes of published RCTs evaluating
      surgical interventions in the field of digestive cancer with its representations of external
      validity according to ASSIST.

      In a second step, investigators will invite a panel of peer reviewers to appraise the
      external validity (applicability) of selected RCTs without and with the graphical tool
      (ASSIST), on a security web site.
