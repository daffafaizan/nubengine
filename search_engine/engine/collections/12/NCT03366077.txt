
    
      This is a double-blinded, randomized, placebo-controlled, parallel-group study evaluating the
      effect of the probiotic on recurrent urinary tract infection (UTI) in adult women recently
      treated for UTI.

      Primary objective The primary objective is to evaluate the effect of oral supplementation
      with probiotic on the frequency of UTI during six months from start of intervention in adult
      women with recurrent UTI.
