
    
      Thirty women with SUI were randomly divided into two equal groups; the control group received
      pelvic floor muscle training , while the study group received the same pelvic floor muscle
      training in addition to laser acupuncture on neurogenic acupoints . The outcome measures were
      pelvic floor muscle strength (PFMS) , SUI severity assessed by Severity Index (SI) , and
      Incontinence Questionnaire-Short Form (ICIQ-SF). The outcome measures were assessed pre- and
      post-rehabilitation program.
