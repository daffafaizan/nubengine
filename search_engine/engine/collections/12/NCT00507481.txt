
    
      Sleep disorders are an important clinical entity affecting outcome in respiratory,
      cardiovascular, metabolic and psychiatric disorders and exacting a potentially significant
      toll on patients at both a personal and socio-economic level.Delirium is a common organ
      dysfunction in the critically ill patients characterized by an acute onset of impaired
      cognitive function [REF] associated with prolonged ICU and hospital stay, nosocomial
      pneumonia, and death.
