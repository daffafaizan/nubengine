
    
      The study is a monocentric, retrospective, descriptive and analytical study , betwen december
      1997 and september 2018 in intensive care unit of university of hospital.

      All patients aged over 18 year and who had received a diagnosis of smoke- inhalation injury
      and were admitted to the ICU of Medical University Hospital , a referral center in Paris
      providing HBO therapy , were potentially eligible for inclusion in this investigation.

      Criteria used for recommending HBO therapy to a patient with transient or prolonged
      unconsciousness, abnormal neurologic findings on physical examination.

      Data were analyzed to identify independent factors that may influence mortality.
