
    
      Randomised Controlled Study with two arms. All cases following up for post operative
      Hirschsprungs surgery are included in this study. First arm will receive a probiotics in the
      form of sachet for 6 months. Second arm will receive a multivitamin as placebo. Comparative
      analysis between the two groups for baseline and followup prevalence of Hirschsprungs
      associated enterocolitis.
