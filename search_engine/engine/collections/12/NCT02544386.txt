
    
      Each patient must answer several questionnaires about their calcium intake and its ability to
      perform everyday tasks. They will also have 3 blood samples (15 ml each time), 3 measure of
      bone by 3D micro-tomography and 2 MRI examination. These examinations and sampling will take
      place after 30 days after stroke onset (except for MRI) and again after three months and six
      months after the stroke (the same day as the medical follow-up visit).
