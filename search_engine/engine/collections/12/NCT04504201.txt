
    
      The overall goal of this research is to collect essential input for the future development of
      a quality improvement intervention to foster "Patient-Center Communication about Healthy
      Weigh in clinical practice

      Primary Objective:

        1. Understand patient-centered perspectives on health weight communication with oncology
           clinicians by conducting focus groups

           Secondary Objective

        2. Understand oncology clinician perspectives on patient center communications for healthy
           weight interactions through semi-structured interviews.
