
    
      Complex distal radius fractures are a common problem treated by orthopaedic surgeons. The
      purpose of this study is to compare the outcome between complex distal radius fractures
      treated with open reduction and internal fixation using a volar fixed angled plate versus and
      external fixator versus DRV. The hypothesis is that the volar fixed angled plate will have a
      superior outcome both radiographically and clinically.
