
    
      At least 50 volunteers between the ages of 18 and 50, will be enlisted, with the need to
      perform one of the interventions described above and that fall within the criteria of
      inclusion.

      The study will be carried out in accordance with the Helsinki Declaration on In Vivo Studies
      and will be initiated with the approval of the Internal Review Board of the University of
      L'Aquila.

      The protocol follows the guidelines of the CONSORT Statement for parallel studies and will be
      recorded on the data base clinicaltrials.gov.
