
    
      The actual mortality of LVFR is close to 100%. Despite the mortality rate for LVFR has been
      decreasing with the development of reperfusion therapy, the serious complication remains a
      difficult challenge. The coronary anatomy distribution associated with LVFR has an
      interesting phenomenon. From the perspective of different coronary distribution, we can
      better known the risk factors related to LVFR.
