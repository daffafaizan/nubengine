
    
      This randomized controlled trial included data from 120 cases in the Pediatric Health and
      Diseases Department of Istanbul, during March 2020-August 2020 (parental involvement group, n
      = 46; parental presence group, n = 42; parental absence group, n = 32). Information form,
      State-Trait Anxiety Inventory and FLACC scale were used to collect data.
