
    
      This study will be on music use as musicotherapy "receptive " or " passive ", using hearing
      aid for listening in order to relax and reduce the anxiety and pain state of the patient.
      This care will be focused on the acute stress and pain during the dental care.

      Another major secondary purpose will consist to explore the anxiolytic effect of the
      musicotherapy on diverse stress markers such as heart rate, respiratory rate, salivary
      cortisol ratio and electro-dermal intensity (RED).
