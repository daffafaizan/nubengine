
    
      Patients undergoing coronary artery bypass graft surgery are at risk of postoperative
      pulmonary complications, which lead to increased postoperative morbidity and mortality.

      A few studies have demonstrated that preoperative physical therapy has advantages over
      postoperative care in patients undergoing cardiac . In this way, this study aim to
      investigate the effects of preoperativeinspiratory muscle training in patients at high risk
      of postoperative pulmonary complications who were scheduled for coronary artery bypass graft
      surgery.
