
    
      the purpose of the present study was to design a randomized, double-blind, controlled study
      to evaluate functional recovery effect of Helminthostachys zeylanica(L.)Hook after surgical
      operation in patients with ankle fracture. The primary outcome measure was the scores change
      of American Orthopaedic Foot and Ankle Society (AOFAS) Ankle-Hindfoot scale, and tape
      (figure-of-eight) methods. Secondary outcome measure was the level changes of bone metabolism
      in peripheral blood, four diagnostic method, and the changes of ankle X-ray findings.
