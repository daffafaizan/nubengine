
    
      This pharmacokinetic profile will be obtained in only in patient using Advate® (INN-octocog
      alfa) or Adynovi® (INN- rurioctocog alfa pegol), both recombinant intravenous FVIII products,
      with myPKFiT®. This patients will be followed during the period of 12 months, and the
      pharmacokinetic will be measured collecting at least 2 blood samples, in which the presence
      of factor VIII will be determined by the usual clinical methods: chromogenic method or
      coagulative method.
