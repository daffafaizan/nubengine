
    
      We will ask the participants to join this research on the department of obstetric outpatient
      department, and give the consent form to sign. Then we will allocate the participant into the
      experiment group or the control group, furthermore, we will give the sheet which includes the
      information about peanut ball usage, like the destination, method, position, duration, and
      quick response code of the video.

      When they finish the admission process in the delivery room, we will give the peanut ball to
      the participants who is in the experimental group, and the participants of the control group
      will receive the routine care of delivery room.
