
    
      Prospective randomized double-blind single-center preliminary study aimed to demonstrate a
      possible greater analgesic efficacy of the PENG block compared to the iliac fascia block (FIB
      block) in elderly patients (age > 65 years) suffering from traumatic fracture of the proximal
      femur in emergency room.

      The study will cover patients aged > 65 years suffering from a proximal traumatic fracture of
      the femur who access the emergency room of Santo Spirito Hospital in Rome (ASL Roma 1).
