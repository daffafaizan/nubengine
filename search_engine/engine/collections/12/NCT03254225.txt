
    
      This study evaluates the inffluence of 16 weeks of resistance training on heart rate
      variability using time and frequency-domains by ECG tracks. Will be assessed the blood
      pressure using the ambulatory blood pressure monitoring method of hypertensive elderly
      patients. All volunteers will be divided in two groups, one will participate in a protocol of
      sixteen weeks of resistance training prescribed with a relative workload of 50% of 1 maximal
      repetition, an the ontrol group will remain sedentary.
