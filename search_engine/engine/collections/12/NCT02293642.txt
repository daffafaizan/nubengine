
    
      Aim of this study is to translate the questionnaire and to examine the psychometric
      properties of BOMET-QoL-10 in a German population. Correlations with the EORTC QLQ-C30/BM22
      at the time of enrolment and throughout the study aim to verify that the BOMET-QoL-10 is
      reliable, valid and able to detect stabilization or changes in quality of life.
