
    
      The CO10 EPF paediatric cohort includes therapeutic, clinical and biological detailed
      questionnaires. The data are collected by the doctors every three months to 2 years and then
      every 6 months until to 18 years or every year by a simplified questionnaire if medical
      follow up continues outside of a participating site.

      After 18 years of age, the patients are eligibile for the ANRS CO19-Coverte cohort.
