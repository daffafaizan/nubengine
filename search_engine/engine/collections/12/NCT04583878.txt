
    
      Subjects will be identified from the clinical setting and approached to participate in this
      observational study where participants will be enrolled at 3 different sites and referred
      from several more sites and have: cardiopulmonary exercise testing and pulmonary function
      testing at The Institute of Exercise and Environmental Medicine (IEEM), Cardiac MRI and MRI
      for pulmonary perfusion at Children's Medical Center and MR Spectroscopy and MR for Muscle
      Perfusion at the Advanced Imaging Research Center (AIRC) performed in Dallas over a 3 day
      research visit at week 12 and Month 12. Blood is collected for biomarkers at these visits and
      multiple questionnaires are completed by participants.
