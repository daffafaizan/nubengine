
    
      This is a prospective cohort study that will newly enroll approximately 150 MSM in Kuala
      Lumpur, Jakarta, and Bangkok and follow them prospectively with a group of 280 MSM who are
      previous participants of a study entitled "Multidisciplinary services to enhance HIV testing
      and linkage to care among MSM" (MSM VCT study) in Jakarta, Bali, and Bangkok. Newly enrolled
      MSM will have three study visits at baseline, month 6, and at month 12. MSM who are previous
      participants of the MSM VCT study will only have one study visit.
