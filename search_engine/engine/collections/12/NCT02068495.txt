
    
      This special drug use surveillance was designed to investigate the safety and efficacy of
      long-term use of candesartan cilexetil/amlodipine besilate combination tablets (Unisia
      Combination Tablets) in participants with hypertension in the routine clinical setting.

      The usual adult dosage is one tablet (8 mg/2.5 mg or 8 mg/5 mg as candesartan
      cilexetil/amlodipine besilate) administered orally once daily.
