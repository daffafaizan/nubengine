
    
      this is a cross-sectional, questionnaire-based study. It is conducted on a random targeted
      sample of medical students at medical schools in Universities of Jordan, classified by their
      academic years (from the 1st to the 6th academic year). A score of 10 points was constructed
      to reflect the perspective about the items in each group of variables. SPSS version 25.0 was
      used for the statistical analysis.
