
    
      Background: This study aimed to determine the effect of progressive relaxation exercises
      (PMR) on dyspnea and anxiety level in individuals with COPD Methods: The study was conducted
      in the pretest-posttest randomized-controlled trial and completed with 44 COPD individuals,
      22 interventions and 22 controls. PMR were applied once a day for four weeks in the
      intervention group in addition to the standard treatments. The control group received
      standard treatment. For data collection Questionnaire Forms, Patient Follow-Up Schedules,
      Modified Borg Scale (MBS), Modified British Medical Research Council Dyspnea Scale (mMRC),
      COPD Assessment Test (CAT), and Beck Anxiety Scale were used.
