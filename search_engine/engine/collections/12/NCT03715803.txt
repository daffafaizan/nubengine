
    
      The main objective of this study is to compare the initial outcomes and complication of two
      meshes implanted through a single incision to treat anterior and apical prolapses, Calistar A
      and a second-generation low weight mesh called Calistar S (Soft). In such sense, objective
      and subjective parameters will be retrospectively tested to demonstrate the safety and
      effectiveness of this products. Safety will be assessed by register of complications and
      effectiveness will be evaluated by pelvic floor reconstruction and quality of life
      improvements.
