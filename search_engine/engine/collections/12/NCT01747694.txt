
    
      The respiratory muscle training is an important part of pulmonary rehabilitation. Subjects
      will be recruited from the chest medicine clinic of a medical center in Northern Taiwan,
      randomly assigned to control and experimental groups by severity status for a 12-week muscle
      training intervention.The outcome measures will be performed to evaluate respiratory
      function, depression status and quality of life at baseline, 4, 8 and 12 weeks of training.
