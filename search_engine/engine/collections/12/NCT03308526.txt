
    
      Sepsis remains a leading cause of death in the intensive care units and in all age groups
      worldwide.sepsis was defined as the host's immune response(systemic inflammatory response
      syndrome) to injury and/or infectious stimuli in the presence of a known(or strongly
      suspected) infection .early onset neonatal sepsis occurs in utero or might become infected
      when exposed to potentially pathogenic bacteria,viruses,or fungi during passage through the
      birth canal.
