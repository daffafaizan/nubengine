
    
      Patients who have irreversible pulpitis and need pulpotomy will be selected randomly from the
      out patient clinic. they will be assigned randomly to different study and control groups.
      pulpotomy will be performed in the second primary molar and 1 of the five materials will be
      put in the pulp chamber (aloe vera, nigella sativa, thymus vulgaris, turmeric and
      formocresol). It will be covered with a restoration and a stainless steel crown. Patients
      follow up will be done after 1,3,6 and 12 months to assess clinical and radiographic signs
      and symptoms.
