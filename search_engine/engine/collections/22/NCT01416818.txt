
    
      Depression as the most common non-motor comorbidity of Parkinson's disease (PD), have a
      negative impact on disability and quality of life.

      Traditional Chinese Medicine has shown effects on several depression. It is not known if TCM
      also is effective for depression in PD. This study is to investigate "Xiaoyao Pill", an
      antidepressant in TCM, on depression in PD.

      A total of 60 patients with depression in PD will be randomized to each of the three arms.
