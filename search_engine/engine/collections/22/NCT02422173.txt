
    
      This is a randomized sham-controlled trial reported following the 2010 CONSORT guidelines.
      The subjects were randomly allocated into 1 of 4 groups in a double-blinded parallel study
      design. Each patient received 10 sessions (5 consecutive days for 2 weeks) of real or sham
      stimulation at 2 mA intensity and current density equivalent to 0.05 A/m2.
