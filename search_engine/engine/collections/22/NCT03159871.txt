
    
      A Randomized prospective single blinded trial. The investigator seek to evaluate the surgical
      outcome of Stratafix barbed suture compared to standart Vicryl sutures in reducing the
      suturing time during C-Section. In addition, the need of additional sutures to control
      bleeding after the 2- layer closure will be estimated.These will be the primary outcome
      measures. The secondary outcome measures will be factors related to the blood loss at the
      time of the uterine closure, surgical complications, duration of hospital stay, postoperative
      infection (endometritis) and wound infection..
