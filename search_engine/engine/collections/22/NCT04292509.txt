
    
      10 pregnant women with type 1 diabetes will be included . Participants will be randomized 1/1
      between 12-30 weeks of pregnancy to SAP with predictive stop before low during two weeks or
      predictive stop on low. Participants will be cross-over to the other group after two weeks
      (from stop before low to stop on low or vice versa). During the study, participants will be
      asked to measure blood ketones three times per day.
