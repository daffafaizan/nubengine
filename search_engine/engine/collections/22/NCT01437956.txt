
    
      Prospective, monocentric, case-control and transversal study Primary end-point: correlation
      between serum KL-6 level and severity of the bronchiolitis, evaluated by a clinical scoring
      system established at the time of the admission in Paediatric Emergency Unit Secondary
      end-points: respiratory virus detected by EIA and RT-PCR, phylogenetic study of rhinovirus,
      length of hospitalisation, immediate morbidity and mortality, risk of asthma at the age of
      three years old.
