
    
      This is a prospective observational cohort conducted at the Universitary hospital of Caen
      over a total period of 5 years.

      Patients with a symptomatic genital prolapse will all be offered the installation of a
      pessary. If they agree to participate in the study, they will be asked to respond to
      validated questionnaires: symptom questionnaires (PFDI-20, ICIQ-SF, USP), a sexuality
      questionnaire (PISQ-12), a quality of life questionnaire (PFIQ-7, BIS) and Satisfaction
      (PGI-I), several times: Before the installation of the pessary, at one month, at 6 months,
      then annually over 5 years
