
    
      Cord Blood will be collected at birth if possible and peripheral blood will be collected at 6
      month of age (5 to 10 days after receiving their standard 6 month immunizations) and at 12
      months of age prior to their 12 month immunization. Additionally, stool samples will be
      collected within the first week of live and once monthly through 12 months of age.
      Breastfeeding mothers will collect breast milk once monthly at the same time as the stool
      specimen during the period of breastfeeding.
