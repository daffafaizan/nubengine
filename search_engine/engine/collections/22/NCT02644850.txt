
    
      To evaluate the feasibility of using CZT SPECT as an "all-in-one" technique to provide
      information on both left ventricular ejection fraction (LVEF) and right ventricular ejection
      fraction (RVEF) derived from a single examination and compare with the values from
      traditional planar ERNA, FP-RNV and echocardiography.
