
    
      The main objective is to evaluate the protein intake of the pregnant woman in the 3rd
      trimester of pregnancy in 3 populations:

        -  Women with BMI ≥ 18.5 and <30 kg / m² (group 1)

        -  Obese women (BMI ≥ 30 kg / m²) (group 2)

        -  Women operated on a bariatric surgery (group 3)
