
    
      Obese pregnant women (BMI > 30) are randomized to exercise, diet or control (1:1:1) in order
      to reduce weight gain during pregnancy. Shortly after birth, tissue composition will be
      measured (DEXA)in 200 infants (65 from each group). The amount of fat tissue is subsequently
      analyzed between the groups and compared to results from another 65 infants of lean mothers
