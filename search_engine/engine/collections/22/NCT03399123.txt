
    
      Background & Aims:Liver cancer is an disease that threatening human health .Laparoscopic
      hepatectomy is more and more widely used.It is very important to reduce the bleeding during
      laparoscopic hepatectomy.The investigators found that low tidal volume can effectively reduce
      intraoperative bleeding.The aim of this study was to explore the effect of low tidal volume
      on blood loss and gas exchange during laparoscopic hepatectomy
