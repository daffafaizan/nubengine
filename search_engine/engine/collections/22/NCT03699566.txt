
    
      The study was conducted on 60 healthy volunteers ranging in age from 20 to 36 years. 27 woman
      and 33 men participated in the study. Respiratory muscle strength, trunk muscle endurance,
      pulmonary function test values and physical activity levels were evaluated for the subjects
      participating in the study.Respiratory muscle strength and respiratory function tests were
      performed with a Cosmed Pony Fx portable spirometer. Trunk muscle endurance of the
      individuals were evaluated by prone bridge, side bridge, flexor endurance and sorensen test.
