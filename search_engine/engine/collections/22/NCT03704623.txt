
    
      Eligible patients will be randomized to one of the two treatment groups. Group 1 patients
      will receive IV 1 g of Paracetamol while group 2 patients will receive 40 mg of Parecoxib IV.

      The Visual Analogue Scale (VAS) will be calculated on presentation and at 30 minutes after
      analgesia administration. Patients who do not respond to an initial dose of the analgesia
      will receive rescue analgesia in the form of Morphine, 01.mg/kg, IV.
