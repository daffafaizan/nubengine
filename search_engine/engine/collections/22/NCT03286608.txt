
    
      The study sample includes 1,329 subjects followed for up to 12 years fro dementia after
      dietary assessment. The aim is to identify a pattern of polyphenol intake (based on 26
      flavonoid and non-flavonoid polyphenol subclasses ascertained using a 24-hour dietary recall)
      associated with the risk of dementia and AD over 12 years in the 3C Bordeaux cohort.
