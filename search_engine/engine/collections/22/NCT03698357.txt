
    
      Thirty individuals with stroke are recruited and allocated to 2 groups in this study. Fifteen
      participants in group A will undergo 30 minutes a day and 3 days a week interactive video
      balance-based exercise intervention for four weeks. Another 15 participants allocated to the
      group B will receive 30 minutes a day and 3 days a week conventional rehabilitation for four
      weeks. Time up go test (TUG), Berg Balance Scale (BBS), Modified Falls Efficacy Scale (MFES),
      Motricity Index (MI), Functional Ambulation Category (FAC) were assessed before and after
      intervention in both groups
