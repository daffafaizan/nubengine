
    
      An observational cross-sectional study was conducted using telephone interview method in
      Diabetes Management Centre (DMC), Services hospital, Lahore. The study participants were 1282
      diabetic patients selected randomly from electronic medical record (EMR) database of the
      Centre. A structured questionnaire was used to evaluate parameters which included patients'
      demographic information; diabetes self-care and general health profile; COVID-related
      questions and its impact on quality of life (QoL); financial and social support issues.
