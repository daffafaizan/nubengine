
    
      Eligible patients are randomized pre-operatively into either a standard dressing or NPWT for
      the groin incision.

        -  1 The control group receive a standard sterile gauge dressing over the groin incision.
           The dressing is removed on post-operative day #2 and the wound is inspected for a SSI or
           dehiscence, followed by daily dressing changes and wound inspections until discharge.

        -  2 The intervention group will receive a NPWT dressing which will be applied in the
           operating room under sterile conditions. The NPWT dressing will be removed on Post
           operative day 5. The wound is inspected for a SSI or dehiscence, followed by daily
           dressing changes and wound inspections until discharge.
