
    
      Open-label, randomized controlled trial comparing three different treatment maneuvers in a
      mechanical rotational chair (ROTUNDUM-chair) in treating BPPV. Patients diagnosed with BPPV
      will be randomized to one of three maneuvers in the ROTUNDUM-chair:

        1. Epley's maneuver

        2. Semont maneuver

        3. 360 degree vertical rotation
