
    
      Healthy subjects were randomly allocated to receive a single dose of either four 600 mg red
      yeast rice capsules or one 20 mg lovastatin tablet; after 7-day washout period, they received
      a single dose of the alternative drug. The subjects were fasted at least 10 hour before
      dosing. The investigational products were administered with 240 mL of water with the subject
      in an upright position. The blood samples were collected at prior to the drug administration
      (T0), and 0.5, 1, 1.5, 2, 2.5, 3, 4, 6, 8 and 12 hours after dosing.
