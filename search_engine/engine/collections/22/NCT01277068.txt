
    
      It is a monocentric, prospective, comparative study. After identification of the eligible
      subjects, the kinetics (production and clearance rates) of the intestinal TRL will be
      performed in the hospital, using a stable isotope method (5,5,5-D3-L-Leucine), in 3 groups of
      obese patients (12 patients per surgery procedure), before and 6 months after the surgery
