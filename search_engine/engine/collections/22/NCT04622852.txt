
    
      From a cohort of patients treated for displaced Proximal humeral fractures (PHF) in the
      period July 2017 - August 2019 patients with the 11-B2 and 11-C2 PHF according to AO/OTA 2007
      fracture classification were selected according to age and gender to be compared with
      patients operated with a Philos plate within the same fracture group, age and gender to be
      matched for adverse events:

        1. Radiographic evidence of penetration of pegs (ALPS-PHP) or screws (Philos) into the
           glenohumeral joint (primary outcome) and

        2. Avascular head necrosis / sinking / need of re-surgery

        3. Clinical PROMS
