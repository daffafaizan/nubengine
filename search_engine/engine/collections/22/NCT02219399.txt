
    
      This was a trial of supplementation with DHA during pregnancy to increase gestational length.
      Subjects were provided with 300 mg, 600 mg or placebo (olive oil) in a randomized design from
      week 24-26 of pregnancy until delivery. Gestational age, birth weight and birth length data
      were collected and infants growth at 2 and 4 months and cognitive development at 4 months and
      1 year were measured.
