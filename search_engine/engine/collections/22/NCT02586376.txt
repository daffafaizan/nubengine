
    
      This study will be conducing in a large hospital that is reference in kidneys disorders
      treatments. 16 patients will be randomized for placebo, 4J, 6J, or 8J low-level laser therapy
      (LLLT). The maximal voluntary contraction (MVC) will be test by handgrip test before and
      after de LLLT. One-week interval between the LLLT will gave to reduce the influence between
      the treatments. Moreover the endurance test will be use to evaluate the muscle oxidation
      impact of the LLLT.
