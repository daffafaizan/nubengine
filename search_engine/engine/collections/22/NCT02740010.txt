
    
      The study aims at evaluating the visual performance of the EDOF MINI WELL® in qualitative and
      quantitative terms of the progressive vision. The cohort of subjects includes all patients
      submitted in the six involved Centers. for the surgical procedure for cataract extraction or
      Refractive Lens Exchange (RLE) using the MINI WELL® with a pre-operative visit (from the day
      - 60 to day -1 before surgery) and a post-operative follow-up visit performed 30-60 days
      after implant.
