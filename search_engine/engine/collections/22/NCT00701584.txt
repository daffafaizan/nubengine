
    
      Women matching the eligibility criteria are consented at breast cancer follow-up hospital
      visits. Blood and urine samples are then collected and questionnaires are provided for the
      participants to take away and return within 4 weeks. The process of sample collection and
      questionnaire provision is repeated for visit at years 3 and 5.

      Year 2 and 4 study visit vary in that questionnaires including a 7 day food diary are sent to
      participants at home to be filled in 7 days before their scheduled follow-up appointment. At
      hospital, blood and urine samples are collected as usual.
