
    
      Eligibility criteria:

        1. Inclusion criteria:Blood cultures demonstrated E.coli or K.pneumoniae among
           nonhospitalized patients or within 48 h after hospitalization.

        2. Exclusion criteria:Patients with hospitalization in the past 1 month.

      Outcome measures:

        1. Risk factors for community-onset bacteremia due to ESBL producing E. coli or
           K.pneumoniae

        2. Microbial and clinical efficacy of commonly used antibiotics for community-onset
           bacteremia due to ESBL producing E. coli or K.pneumoniae
