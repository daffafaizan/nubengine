
    
      Jitongning Tablet is a kind of traditional Chinese medicine(TCM), consists of Cortex
      Eucommiae, Duhuo(Radix Angelicae Pubescentis), Zhichuanwu(Radix Aconiti) , Rhizoma Corydalis,
      Radix Paeoniae Rubra, Radix Puerariae and Radix Glycyrrhizae. This study is being conducted
      to evaluate the efficacy and safety of Jitongning Tablet in patients with active axial
      spondyloarthritis (SpA) (Syndrome Of Kidney Yang Deficiency and Biood Stasis Stagnation),
      when compared with placebo and to explore the optimal dosage.
