
    
      Retrospective evaluation of partial knee replacements (medial UNI, lateral UNI,
      patellofemoral replacement, combined UNI+ patellofemoral replacement, combined medial and
      lateral UNI) performed in the Joint Replacement Department of the IRCCS Istituto Ortopedico
      Galeazzi.

      evaluation includes: clinical examination, functional results, survivorship at 1, 2, 5 , 10
      and 15 years.
