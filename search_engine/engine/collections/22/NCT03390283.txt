
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. To determine the frequency of unsafe storage, use, and disposal of unused and currently
      used opioids by parents and/or caregivers of patients 18 years and younger at MD Anderson
      Cancer Center.

      II. To explore potential association between other covariates with the unsafe storage, use,
      and disposal of opioids.

      OUTLINE:

      Participants complete online survey on an iPad over 20 minutes.
