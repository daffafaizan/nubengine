
    
      The volunteers are submitted to 10 exams (electrocardiogram, polysomnography, blood
      collection, body composition, rest metabolic rate, DXA, abdominal and carotid
      ultrasonography, lung function evaluation, ergospirometric test) 3 times a year (baseline,
      middle and at the end of the year). They also answer nutrition, sleep and psychology
      questionnaires.
