
    
      Children up to 18 years of age, on parenteral nutrition, with a direct bilirubin level of 2
      mg/dL or greater will be eligible to receive Omegaven at a maximum dose of 1 g/kg/day.

      Up to 200 children will be eligible for enrollment.

      Direct bilirubin levels and other labs will be monitored as well as growth parameters.
