
    
      The present study is a clinical, opened trial and single-center.

      The patients will be treated by six laser diodes, localised cooling system and rhythmical
      intake massage device. Application of the massage will be performed for 8 weeks in a total of
      16 treatment sessions: twice a week.

      A set of photographs will be taken at visit 1 (baseline), visit 2 and 3 with standardized
      positions and lighting conditions.
