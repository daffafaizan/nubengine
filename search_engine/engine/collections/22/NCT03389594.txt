
    
      Accuracy Of CBCT affected by many factors. On of the most factors the affect the image
      quality is voxel size. Larger voxel size give a higher accuracy of the images but with higher
      radiation dose.

      The study is to assist the accuracy of surgical guides designed from CBCT with different
      voxel sizes (voxel size 0.4mm & 0.2mm)
