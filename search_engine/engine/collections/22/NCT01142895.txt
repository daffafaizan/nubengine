
    
      These are the detailed descriptive planned outcomes of this useability study.

        -  To demonstrate that human-interface design mitigations lead to effective, efficient, and
           safe use.

        -  Validate that foreseeable misuse does not lead to a safety hazard or patient injury.

        -  Validate the effectiveness and comprehension of critical warnings and alerts, including
           vibratory, auditory, and textual cues.
