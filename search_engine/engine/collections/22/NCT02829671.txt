
    
      This project is based on clinical, neuropsychological and biological datas routinely
      collected in the Department of Emergency Psychiatry and Post Acute Care. This is a one year
      follow-up prospective study.

      Over three years, 600 depressed patients will be recruited.

        -  First visit (inclusion): clinical, biological and neuropsychological assessment

        -  Follow up visits (3, 6 and 12 months): clinical assessment only
