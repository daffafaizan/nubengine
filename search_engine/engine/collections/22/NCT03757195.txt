
    
      Patients suffering from anterior maxillary vertical and horizontal bone loss will be treated
      using a xeno-graft mixed with the same patient plasma that extracted from his own blood
      during the operation this graft will be stabilized by collagen membrane and surgical tags.

      Patients will be follow up for 6 months to measure amount of bone loss of the xeno-graft
      during this period post operative using C.B.C.T
