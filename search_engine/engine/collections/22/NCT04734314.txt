
    
      This randomized controlled trial is to investigate the effect of Vojta method on balance and
      quality of life in patients with chronic non-specific low back pain.

      Forty patients (male and female) complaining of chronic non -specific low back pain was
      recruited to share in this study

      . The recruited patients were randomly recruited into two groups; group A (control group) and
      group B (study group).

      Group A: received conventional treatment of back pain which included; TENS 20 min and hot
      packs 10 min .The total treatment session is 30 min three session per week for four weeks
      Group B: received conventional treatment of back pain in addition to Vojta method. The total
      treatment session is 40 min.

      Patient in both groups performed 10 sessions, 3 session per week
