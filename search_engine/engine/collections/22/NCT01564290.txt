
    
      Background: Acute Gastroenteritis is one of the most and frequent disease in the childhood,
      considering the advances in treatment, Lactobacillus Rahmnosus has being well described as a
      probiotic who reduces the number of days of hospitalization and also de the severity.

      Objectives: To evaluate the effect of the probiotic yogurt in the treatment of children who
      are between 10 months and 3 years old with acute non-dysenteric diarrhea.

      Methods: A randomized clinical trial, controlled, double-blind. The investigators will have
      two groups of patients : One group will receive Lactobacillus Rahmnosus in a yogurt
      presentation and the other one will receive Sacharomyces boulardii in a lyophilized form ;
      the parameters of evaluation are going to be the number of diarrhea stools by day, number of
      vomiting episodes, hours with fever, nutritional and immunization state.
