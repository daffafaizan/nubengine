
    
      The aim of this study was to compare the blood lactate levels in patients receiving typical
      or atypical antipsychotics.

      Subjects included sixty patients with psychotic disorder were assigned to treatment for 6
      months with haloperidol (typical antipsychotic), N=30 or olanzapine (atypical antipsychotic),
      N=30. Blood lactate levels, other metabolic parameters, and scores on the extrapyramidal
      symptom rating scale (ESRS) were assessed.
