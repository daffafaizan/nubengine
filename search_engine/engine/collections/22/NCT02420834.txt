
    
      The efficacy of different classes of artificial tears (non-pharmaceutical) will be examined
      in patients that report dry eyes by prescribing each category of treatment [0.40% Sodium
      Hyaluronate (Clinitas Soothe), 0.15% Sodium Hyaluronate (Hyabak), 0.25%
      Carboxymethylcellulose, electrolyte balanced (Theratears), and Phospholipid liposomal spray
      (Tears Again)] for a month in turn following a short wash-out period and to examine their
      tear film and ocular surface after each one.
