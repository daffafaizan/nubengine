
    
      To characterize the attitude of pre menopausal patients with breast cancer regarding the
      risks of infertility based on the following variables:

        -  Age at diagnosis

        -  Number of children

        -  Will of having children

        -  Marital status

        -  Scholarity

        -  Period of time from the diagnosis

        -  Time of the last menstruation period

        -  Chemotherapy administration

        -  Clinical staging at the diagnosis

        -  Pathological evaluation

        -  Documentation of the cancer therapy
