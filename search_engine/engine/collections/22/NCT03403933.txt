
    
      Population of the study: 47 cardiopathic patients with hypovitaminosis D supplemented with
      calcifediol to reach vitamin D levels> 30 ng / ml within 6 months. The purpose of this study
      is to evaluate the improvement of the ejection fraction or GLS (global longitudinal strain)
      calculated by speckle tracking at 6 months in cardiopathic patients with vitamin D deficiency
      during functional recovery after major orthopedic surgery.
