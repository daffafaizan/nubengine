
    
      Subjects with various types of cancer underwent 68Ga-PSMA-11 PET/CT either for an initial
      assessment or for recurrence detection. Tumor uptake was quantified by the maximum standard
      uptake value (SUVmax). The sensitivity, specificity, positive predictive value (PPV),
      negative predictive value (NPV) and accuracy of 68Ga-PSMA-11 PET/CT were calculated.
