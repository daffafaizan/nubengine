
    
      Patients supported by chyme reinfusion will be followed for 3 days pre chyme reinfusion and
      for 7 weeks during chyme reinfusion. Three days prior chyme reinfusion, baseline
      characteristics will be determined, an ileal biopsy, blood, chyme and urine will be
      collected. Furthermore, blood, chyme, faeces and urine will be collected at the first day of
      chime reinfusion. These materials will be collected again at week 1, 3, 5 and 7 weeks after
      initiation of chyme reinfusion. A second ileal biopsy will be performed at week 3.
