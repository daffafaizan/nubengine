
    
      To assess the knowledge, attitude and practice the structured knowledge questionnaire,
      attitude 5-point likert scale and expressed checklist tool were prepared. A sample size of
      318 was calculated by power analysis and eligible couples were selected by Purposive sampling
      technique from rural and urban area of Ambala Haryana, India.

      Each eligible couples (separately husband and wife) data was collected through Self- Report
      technique.one time assessment only done.
