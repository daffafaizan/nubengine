
    
      This is a prospective, non-randomized, study of the clinical outcomes of participants that
      receive the Stryker Triathlon® Total Knee System for Total Knee Arthroplasty implanted using
      ShapeMatch® Cutting Guides. Sub-study A aims to quantify the reproducibility of
      intra-operatively positioning the cutting guides onto the affected femoral and tibial joint
      surfaces.
