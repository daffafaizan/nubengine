
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. Examine the efficacy of messaging for adjuvant hormone therapy compliance promotion
      (mAHT-CaP) in a randomized control trial (RCT) design.

      SECONDARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. Conduct mediator analyses of intervention efficacy..

      EXPLORATORY OBJECTIVES:

      I. Explore whether age (=< 45 versus [vs.] > 45 at diagnosis) and race/ethnicity (white vs.
      non-white) moderate intervention effects on medication adherence and symptom distress.

      OUTLINE:

      PHASE I: Participants attend focus groups on adherence to hormone therapy.

      PHASE II: Participants are randomized to 1 of 2 groups.

      GROUP I: Participants receive text messages twice weekly for 6 months to remind and motivate
      participants about adjuvant hormonal therapy (AHT) adherence.

      GROUP II: Participants receive usual care.

      After completion of study, participants are followed up at 3, 6, and 12 months.
