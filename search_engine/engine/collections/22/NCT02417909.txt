
    
      There are two groups in this study : healthy volunteers and teenagers suffering from obesity.

      Patients and healthy volunteers will come to the nutrition clinic center Naturalpha where the
      patient will complete a questionnaire. The included patients will also complete a
      questionnaire privately (without the presence of the parents), they will also be weighted and
      measured.

      No further tests or questionnaires will be given after the enrollment day.
