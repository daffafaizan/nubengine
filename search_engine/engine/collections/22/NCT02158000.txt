
    
      Objectives: To notify the effect of aspiration of excessive endometrial fluid in addition to
      Diosmin in cases undergoing intracytoplasmic sperm injection (ICSI) cycles.

      Study design: Prospective randomized controlled clinical trial.

      Patients and Methods: 200 women with excessive endometrial fluid by transvaginal ultrasound
      during ICSI cycles are randomly scheduled into two equal groups. In group A, one tab every 8
      hs Diosmin ( 500mg) beginning from the day of ovum retrieval in addition to aspiration of the
      fluid by an IUI catheter ; while in group B (control group), no thing is given or done. The
      main outcome measures are presence of the fluid on the day of embryo transfer and the
      pregnancy rate on those patients.
