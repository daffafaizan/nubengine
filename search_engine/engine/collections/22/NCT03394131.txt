
    
      Carpal tunnel syndrome (CTS) is a common peripheral entrapment neuropathy in Rheumatoid
      arthritis patients proved by nerve conduction studies and ultrasound parameters, this study
      aims to evaluate the effect of hydro-dissection of median nerve inside CT twice injections
      with interval 2 weeks using insulin and 10 cc saline versus hyalase and 10 cc saline versus
      10 cc saline, ultrasounic guided as an effective treatment of CT
