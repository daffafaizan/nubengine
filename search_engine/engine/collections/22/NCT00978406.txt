
    
      Objectives:

      -To compare different ways of collecting information about food choices and opinions from
      adults.

      Eligibility:

        -  Individuals between the ages of 20 and 70, recruited from a database of volunteers who
           wish to participate in research studies.

        -  Volunteers must have high-speed Internet access.

        -  Individuals who have previously been involved in a research study will be excluded.

      Design:

      -Participants will come to a research study center for three consecutive meals (approximately
      45 minutes per meal). On the following day, participants will return to the study center for
      an interview about their food choices and opinions (approximately 45 minutes).
