
    
      This study is a Randomized Clinical Trial deigned to investigate the effects of including
      Contingency Management to a standard public ambulatory treatment in Brazil on treatment
      attendance, retention in treatment, reduction of crack cocaine use and promotion of crack
      cocaine abstinence for crack dependent individuals seeking treatment for there addiction. In
      total 65 subjects will be randomized to receive Standard Treatment Alone or Standard
      Treatment plus Contingency Management. Both interventions will last for 12 weeks.
