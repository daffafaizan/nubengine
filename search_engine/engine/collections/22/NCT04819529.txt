
    
      A randomized clinical trial with an experimental-control will be implemented, considering the
      prospective multicenter group, with parallel groups, in a 1:1 ratio, in 3 Chilean hospitals.

      A control group will has a standard analgesia, sedation, delirium and mobilization (ASDM)
      measures or an intervention group will have early and intensive OT plus ASDM.

      The intervention group will receive 20 OT sessions, mainly twice a day, which considers a
      predefined protocol of actions according to the patient's condition
