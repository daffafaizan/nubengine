
    
      Single dose: The subjects will be randomized to receive a single dose of OxyNorm®
      immediate-release capsules 5, 10, and 20mg.

      Multiple dose: The subjects will take 3 days OxyNorm® capsule 10mg, once every 6 hours, 10mg
      per dose. In the morning of the 4th day, the subjects will take single dose of OxyNorm®
      capsule 10mg. On the 5th day, all the subjects complete the study.
