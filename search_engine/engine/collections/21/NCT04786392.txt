
    
      The absorption the macular carotenoids has shown variability between different supplements,
      and food sources such as egg and spinach. Investigation of the absorption of different forms
      of food (e.g. cooked, raw, blended) is not as well studied. Investigating the absorption of
      lutein from different forms of food is important to be able to prescribe lutein from forms of
      food that will be optimally absorbed.
