
    
      Primary Objective To assess efficacy of EXPAREL TAP blocks in improving pain scores for 48
      hours postoperatively, and in reducing total oral morphine equivalents (OME) use, compared to
      standard Intrathecal opioid administration (IT).

      Secondary Objective Assess the length of stay (LOS), postoperative ileus (POI) incidence, and
      the use of intravenous patient controlled analgesia (PCA) in patients that had EXPAREL TAP
      blocs compared to IT.
