
    
      In this International multi-center prospective observational study, 250 patients with severe
      symptomatic aortic stenosis (AS) and eligibility for transfemoral TAVI with ACURATE Neo
      Bioprosthesis, will be pre-procedurally selected for participation in an early discharge
      protocol in which patients are discharged within 48 hours after uncomplicated TAVI.
