
    
      Acute postoperative pain may predict the development of chronic pain.Recently, the analgesic
      effect of lidocaine, as a part of a multimodal analgesia, has been evaluated in many studies.
      They have shown that lidocaine may have a role in the postoperative pain management, as an
      adjunct.

      Acute postoperative pain after laparoscopic cholecystectomy is complex in nature.
      Postoperative pain is the main complaint and the primary reason for delay discharge after
      laparoscopic cholecystectomy.The study includes patients undergoing laparoscopic
      cholecystectomy.
