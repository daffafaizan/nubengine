
    
      Eligible participants will be followed up until the completion of a 12-month period. All
      participants will be followed up every 3 months. An additional follow up visit at month 1
      will be made for participants who PrEP is initiated at entry visit (those who already
      received PrEP prior to the study will not need the additional visit).

      Study Sites

      The study will take place at the following community-based clinics:

        1. SWING DIC, Bangkok, Thailand

        2. RSAT DIC, Bangkok, Thailand

        3. SWING DIC, Chonburi, Thailand

        4. Sisters DIC, Chonburi, Thailand

        5. Mplus DIC, Chiang Mai, Thailand

        6. RSAT DIC, Songkhla, Thailand
