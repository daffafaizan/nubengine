
    
      This is an RCT aimed at testing different population-based approaches to increasing
      colorectal cancer screening. The investigators will randomize a cohort of employees between
      the ages of 50-64 to 3 arms of email communications: a.) Usual email communication; b.)
      Active choice- usual care + the ability to pre-commit directly through a web form; c.)
      Financial incentive- usual care + active choice + financial incentive for completion.
