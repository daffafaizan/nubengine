
    
      Preliminary Evaluation of Safety, Tolerability, and Efficacy of XT-150 for the Treatment of
      Osteoarthritic Pain.

      Subjects for whom replacement knee surgery is recommended will be enrolled in the study. A
      single injection of a DNA plasmid with a variant Interleukin-10 (IL-10) transgene will be
      injected into the synovial capsule of the knee.

      This is a first in human, dose-range, safety and efficacy study.
