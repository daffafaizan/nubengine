
    
      This prospective randomized open label clinical trial will be carried out on 90 male patients
      undergoing elective idiopathic gynecomastia surgery in Tanta University Hospitals.

      Study design:

      Patients will be randomly allocated into three equal groups by computer generated sequence
      through sealed opaque envelopes:

      Group C: 30 patients will receive bilateral tumescent local anesthesia as a control group.

      Group TPVB: 30 patients will receive bilateral US guided thoracic paravertebral block.

      Group TIPB: 30 patients will receive bilateral US guided thoracic interfascial plane block.
