
    
      This study assessed the effect of tailored eradication therapy according to Clarithromycin
      resistance in Helicobacter pylori patients.

      The patients with gastric or duodenal ulcer by endoscopy were registered at the study and
      inspected Rapid Urease test, Biopsy, IgG-Hp antibody.

      The tailored eradication therapy was prescribed to patients that confirmed Helicobacter
      pylori positive in two or more of among the three tests for 14 days. Through this, This study
      is to assess the effectiveness of eradication rate and to evaluate safety and tolerability of
      Ilaprazole 10mg qid treatment
