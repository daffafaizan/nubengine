
    
      The primary objectives of this study include:

      (i) The detection of patients with serum liver biochemistry abnormalities less than 8 weeks
      after intake of RZZY Capsule; (ii) The record of overall individuals with demographics,
      underlying diseases, physical status, medication information, clinical laboratory index, and
      so on; (iii) The specimen collection of surveiled individuals. (iv) The attempt to establish
      a predictive model to screen susceptibilities to RZZY Capsule.
