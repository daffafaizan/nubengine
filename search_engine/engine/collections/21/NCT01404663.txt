
    
      Our study evaluate the side effects of bone marrow derived CD133 cells transplantation in
      4-12years old patients with cerebral palsy.first all the patients undergone a comprehensive
      evaluation(physical exam, serology tests,EEG,MRI,GMFM66).

      After bone marrow aspiration,autologous stem cells prepare for each patient. In operation
      room ,the cells are transplanted by intrathecal injection. all the patients would be under
      observed to notice acute side effects and 3,6,9 and 12 months after injection they will be
      followed and evaluate by comprehensive tests.
