
    
      PROTOCOL OUTLINE:

      Patients are given sodium citrate over a 2 hour infusion on day 1. Blood is drawn at times
      -30, -15, 0, and every 10 minutes thereafter during the infusion. On day 2, patients are
      given calcium gluconate over a 2 hour infusion. Blood is drawn at times -30, -15, 0, and
      every 10 minutes thereafter during the infusion.
