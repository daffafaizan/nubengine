
    
      In this study forty-eight subjects will be voluntarily recruited into the study according to
      inclusion criteria. Participants will be randomly assigned to Vibration Foam Rolling + PNF
      stretching, Non-Vibration Foam Rolling + PNF stretching, only PNF stretching and control
      group. Subjects will be evaluated before, immediately after and 30 minutes after
      intervention. Hamstring flexibility will be evaluated with Active Knee Extension Angle (AKEA)
      Test and functionally Sit and Reach test. Thigh skin temperature will be evaluated with
      thermographic imaging.
