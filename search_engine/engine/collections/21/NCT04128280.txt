
    
      1. Study purpose: To investigate the therapeutic effect of transurethral dilation of
           prostate with a columnar balloon on patients with chronic obstructive prostatitis

        2. Study design: A randomized, controlled, multi-center clinical trial

        3. Study subjects: Patients with chronic obstructive prostatitis

        4. Number of subjects: 180

        5. Surgery treatments:

      Test group: Patients will receive a surgery: transurethral dilation of prostate with a
      columnar balloon.

      Control group: Patients will receive a surgery: transurethral incision of bladder neck.
