
    
      The study is a randomized clinical pilot trial that assesses preliminary intervention
      efficacy of a two-session health coaching intervention relative to brief advice among
      pre-operative elective surgical patients and evaluates intervention acceptability. Study
      subjects will be outpatients at a large health system in the midwestern United States and
      fulfill the study's inclusion criteria. Subjects will be randomized to one of two
      intervention conditions: Health Coaching or Brief Advice.
