
    
      Pancreatic fistula (PF) grade C, defined by the International Study Group of Pancreatic
      Fistula (ISGPF) is associated with a life-threatening complication after
      pancreaticoduodenectomy(PD). The rate of grade C PF has been reported 2.5-6%, however, it is
      critical complication after PD. A large number of enrolled patients will be needed to clarify
      the risk factors for grade C PF. Therefore, this study design is prospective large
      observational study at multi-center in Japan and Taiwan.
