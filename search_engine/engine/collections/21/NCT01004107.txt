      This is a prospective, randomized, masked, controlled study to investigate the effectiveness
      of Radiesse® Dermal Filler for hand treatment, comprised of a 3-month main study followed by
      an open-label extension (OLEX) study through 12 months. At 3 months, untreated controls
      received Radiesse Dermal Filler for hand treatment and were followed for the remainder of the
      study. All study subjects were eligible for retreatment 6 months after initial treatment, and
      all subjects were followed through 12 months from study enrollment in the OLEX.
