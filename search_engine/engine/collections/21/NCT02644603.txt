
    
      212 patients are randomly recruited from inpatients of Shanghai Zhongshan Hospital, Eastern
      Hepatobiliary Surgery Hospital, RenJi Hospital, Anhui Provincial Hospital, Subei People's
      Hospital of Jiangsu Province and Affiliated Tumor Hospital of Nantong University from
      February 2016 to July 2016. Randomly assigned about half of the patients to receive Enhanced
      Recovery After Surgery(ERAS) protocol and the other half to receive conventional treatment.
      Evaluate the safety and efficacy of the ERAS protocol.
