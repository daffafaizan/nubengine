
    
      Interim analysis will be done after 20 subjects are enrolled in order to assess the 1st arm.
      Following the analysis of the first phase results, and per need, 30 more subjects may be
      enrolled under a different regimen (2nd arm). Second Interim analysis will be done in order
      to assess the 2nd phase results. Following the analysis of the 2nd phase results, and per
      need, up to 30 additional subjects may be enrolled under a third regimen (3rd arm).
