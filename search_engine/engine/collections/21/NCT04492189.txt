
    
      Investigators will recruit 40 patients with eating disorders. All patients were initially
      assessed in the daily hospital of the Endocrinology, Diabetes, Nutrition Department
      (Montpellier University Hospital) from January to march 2020.

      All participants will performed a phone interview to assess eating disorder symptoms,
      caracteristicus of containment, 1 month after the beginning of the containment, and 1 month
      after the end of the containment.
