
    
      This retrospective study included 536 cases who were followed with various diagnoses in
      intensive care units between 1 Ocak 2015 ile 1 Ocak 2016, covering the six months period,
      retrospectively. Sakarya University's Faculty of Medicine Review Board
      (71522473/050.01.04/113) approved the study after approval of the research ethics committee
      (. Medical record forms and electronic medical record system of the hospital were used in the
      analysis of medical data with regard to age, sex, HBsAg, anti-HCV, AST, ALT, bilirubin,
      creatinine levels.
