
    
      The purpose of the present study is to gain insight into the histopathological changes of the
      uncinate process in patients with chronic rhinosinusitis through comparison of its mucosal
      and bony elements with those of the normal uncinate process without chronic inflammation
      (unpublished data: Gilead Berger, Ephraim Eviatar, Tatiana Kogan, Roee Landsberg).
