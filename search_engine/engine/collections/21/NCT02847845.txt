
    
      Verify the efficacy and safety after taking red ginseng for chronic fatigue syndrome or
      idiopathic chronic fatigue patient. They were verified through not only survey like VAS
      change about fatigue, fatigue severity scale, Chalder fatigue severity questionnaire,
      SRI-short form , BDI , ISI EQ-5D-5L, ginseng subjective symptoms questionnaire but also an
      anti-oxidant test and a salivary cortisol test after 6 weeks after taking red ginseng or
      placebo drugs.
