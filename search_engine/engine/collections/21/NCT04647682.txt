
    
      The 30 second chair test, balance with joint-feet and semi-tandem stance, and 4 meters
      comfortable walking test were evaluated. Depending on their score, a specific PA program was
      selected and explained. The program was printed, explained and lefted in the room. This
      intervention included 5 different PA programs.

      Final evaluation (one day before leaving the unit) recording physical performance (same
      physical tests used for inclusion evaluation), HAD score, ADL and self-satisfaction of the
      program.
