
    
      The investigators compare the ICP of two standard Spiegelberg ICP-probes in children, which
      underwent an operation and Implantation of an external ventricle drain or a subdural ICP
      Monitoring devise. The second ICP probe is fixed epicutaneously on the skin over the frontal
      fontanel. The hourly measured ICPs within 24 hours postoperatively were compared with the
      Bland-Altman two methods analysis.
