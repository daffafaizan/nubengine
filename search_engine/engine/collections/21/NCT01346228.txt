
    
      The first goal is to see how helpful the Smart Phone application is in helping veterans with
      panic symptoms continue to improve after you complete treatment.

      The second goal of this study is to find out how much veterans like the Smart Phone
      application.

      The third goal is to see how practical it is to use the Smart Phone application as a form of
      follow-up care. We want to see how much time is needed to provide technical support in using
      the application.
