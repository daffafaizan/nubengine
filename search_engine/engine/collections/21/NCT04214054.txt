
    
      This study was designed as a randomized controlled clinical trial, the study population
      consisted of 20 patients with missing maxillary posterior teeth and residual bone height
      5-7mm. The sample was selected conveniently according to a list of inclusion and exclusion
      criteria, the participants were allocated randomly into two equal groups. All patients had
      sinus lift using piezosurgery device with simultaneous implant placement. In group I, blood
      clot was the only filling material. In group II, moldable, self-hardening calcium phosphate
      was the filling material. Clinical and radiographic evaluations were done through 6 months
      postoperatively.
