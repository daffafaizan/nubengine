
    
      This is a prospective, cross-sectional, observational study quantitatively assessing

        1. challenging behavior in children with MPS, an orphan, progressive, neurodegenerative
           metabolic disorder, and

        2. parental coping strategies for this challenging behavior.

      Patients registered at the German Society for Mucopolysacchardoses giving written informed
      consent are eligible. The study is based on questionnaires. IRB approval was obtained.
