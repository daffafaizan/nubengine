
    
      To assess the effectiveness of two anesthetic techniques by measuring the pain through visual
      analog scale (VAS), to obtain saturation prostate biopsies.

      Anesthetic technique (control) - Intravenous sedation with Midazolam, Fentanile and Ketamine.

      Anesthetic technique (intervention) - Regional Mepivacaine infiltration of periprostatic
      region.
