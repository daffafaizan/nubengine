
    
      The objectives of this study are to:

        1. Compare the image quality of the fundus autofluorescence (FAF) photographs between DRI
           OCT Triton (plus) and TRC-50DX retinal camera.

        2. Compare the image quality of the fluorescein angiography (FA) photographs between DRI
           OCT Triton (plus) and TRC-50DX retinal camera.
