
    
      To comprehensively evaluate the effect of an AFO on ambulatory function and loading in
      hemiplegic gait. This study is addressing important questions regarding the effect of an AFO
      on gait and introduces technology for quantifiable objective assessment of AFO effectiveness.
      It will attempt to identify if AFO wear compliance increases community ambulation and walking
      efficiency bilaterally. Research is needed to fully understand how compliance affects
      mobility outcomes.
