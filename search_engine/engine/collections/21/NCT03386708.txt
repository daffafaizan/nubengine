
    
      This is a random, open label, and parallel controled experiment. 40 patients are selected and
      sign consent forms, then divided into four groups. Doctors collect the basic information of
      patient (including age,height, mental condition, vital sign, history of disease,
      pharmaco-history, and so on.), evaluate the symptom of healing Poor after Uterus Injury
      (Menstrual conditions, Visual analogue scale, pregnancies).

      All patients receive laboratory and image examination as baseline. Then, cell treatment will
      be given based on the clinical protocol. Doctors have follow-up visit on 1, 2, 3, 6, and 12
      month after treatment, and do efficacy evaluation.
