
    
      Objectives

        1. To assess energy expenditure and body composition by stable isotope techniques in
           children with Severe Acute Malnutrition

        2. To evaluate the potential usefulness of body composition assessment as a predictor of
           outcome in children with SAM, based on stable isotope technique and multi-frequency
           bioelectrical impedance analysis (BIA)

      Inclusion criteria

        -  Parents of children of age 6 months to 3 years

        -  Resident of study area

      Exclusion criteria

        -  Children with congenital malformations

        -  Chronic debilitating illnesses

        -  Refusal by parents to enroll in study
