
    
      1. Cancer pain is a common symptom exists in malignant tumor patients, which bother the
           patients and decrease the quality of life.

        2. The goal of the study is that using the real-time monitoring and intervention system
           will alleviate cancer pain better than the standard cancer pain treatment.

        3. The cloud computing concept is developed by Sun Yat-sen University cancer center, and
           accord with clinical practice.

        4. The subjects receive the real-time monitoring and intervention system will install the
           software and report in the contents of cancer pain in the software correspondingly to
           the doctors, and advices will be given by the software as well.

        5. The quality of life and overall survival follow-up is also required.
