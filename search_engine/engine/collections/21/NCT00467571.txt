
    
      Children with chronic idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura who have Helicobacter pylori
      infection are enrolled. Helicobacter pylori infection is diagnosed by urea breath test.
      Patients who have Helicobacter pylori infection will be randomized into 2 groups: control and
      treatment groups. The treatment group will receive 2 antibiotics and proton-pump inhibitors
      for 14 days for eradication of infection. Repeated urea breath test at week 6-8 after
      treatment. Blood tests for platelet count will be performed every month for 6 months in both
      groups. Platelet count at 6 months in both group will be compared.Blood for platelet antibody
      will be performed at 3 and 6 months. Control group will receive treatment for Helicobacter
      pylori at the end of the study.
