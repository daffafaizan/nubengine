
    
      The secondary objectives of this study are to evaluate before versus after the intervention
      of clowns therapists:

      A. Changes in the quality of life at two days after the intervention (using the ESAS).

      B. The quality of life two days after the intervention via the McGill Quality of Life
      Questionnaire.

      C. The quality of life of patients after the intervention as assessed by semi-structured
      interviews conducted by the department psychologist.

      D. The quality of life of caregivers after the intervention as assessed by semi-structured
      interviews conducted by the department psychologist.

      E. Employee satisfaction at the end of the intervention period in the department and after
      debriefing as assessed by semi-structured interviews conducted by the department
      psychologist.
