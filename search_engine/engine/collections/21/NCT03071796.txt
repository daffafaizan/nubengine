
    
      The double-blind, randomized intervention study is conducted for 12 weeks and includes two
      groups (placebo versus verum) with same number of participants.

      The investigator aims to determine the effects of multivitamin supplement on blood and vital
      parameters which includes blood pressure, pulse, heart rate, variability of heart rate,
      vitamin D, vitamin B12, folic acid, homocysteine, HbA1c. Furthermore the investigator will
      access life quality, mood and perception of stress through the following questionnaire POMS,
      PSS-10, SF12 & SF36. The investigator will evaluate whether the multivitamin supplement
      changes the frequency of diseases like cold which represents the function of the immune
      system.
