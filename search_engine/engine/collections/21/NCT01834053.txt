
    
      This study is Single arm, Single Centre trial study the Safety and Efficacy of Bone Marrow
      Derived Autologous mononuclear cells (MNCs) ,to be conducted for 36 months in patients with
      diabetes Mellitus in India,Primary outcome measure are Improvement in cognitive and
      Psychiatric Symptoms and Improvement in Jerky,random, and Uncontrollable Movements called
      Chorea.
