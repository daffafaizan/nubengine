
    
      PROTOCOL OUTLINE: This is a randomized study. Patients are randomized to one of three
      treatment arms. Patients in arms I and II receive one of two doses of paromomycin
      intramuscularly once a day for 3 days. Patients in arm III receive streptomycin
      intramuscularly once a day for 3 days. All patients then begin a course of standard therapy
      for tuberculosis.
