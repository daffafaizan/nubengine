
    
      The objective of this study is to perform a prospective, multi-center (min 2 sites),
      non-randomized study to evaluate the safety and efficacy of the Spur system and compare
      treatments arms (if applicable) in treating subjects with infrapopliteal disease who are at
      risk for amputation.

      The study protocol will ensure consistency in performing the procedure, patient management
      and results of the procedure. Safety and efficacy will be evaluated during index procedure
      through one (1) year follow up
