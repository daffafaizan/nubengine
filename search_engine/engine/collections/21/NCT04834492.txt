
    
      We reviewed retrospectively 67 hips of 50 patients which underwent CoC, cementless THA with
      transverse subtrochanteric osteotomy between 2008 and 2011. Clinical and radiological data of
      the hips were examined. Clinical results were evaluated using the Harris Hip Score (HHS) and
      the Western Ontario and McMaster Universities Osteoarthritis Index (WOMAC).
