
    
      Qualifying and consenting IVF patients will have approximately half of their developing
      embryos cultured in droplets covered with oil. The other approximate half of their embryos
      will be cultured in small enclosed containers. Embryo assignments will be at random.
      Regardless of culture system, all embryos will be cultured from the bipronuclear stage to the
      blastocyst stage in the same (Global) media, without the usual refreshing of media on day 3.
