
    
      This study focus on four hands: (1) to investigate different types of gestational sac
      implantation for pregnancy in caesarean women of childbearing age;(2) to explore the
      relationship between abnormal placenta planting disease in middle-late pregnancy and the
      different types of scar pregnancy (3) to explore the the value of ultrasound and serological
      markers for early diagnosis of placenta planting disease from scar pregnancy; (4) to compare
      the end and the influence of health economy of different termination method on scar
      pregnancy.
