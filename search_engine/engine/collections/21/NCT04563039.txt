
    
      The AEROLITH Clinical Study will evaluate the safety and effectiveness of Applaud Acoustic
      Enhancer when used in conjunction with conventional ureteroscopic laser lithortipsy (URS-LL)
      in the treatment of subjects with urinary stones. The clinical study is a prospective,
      multicenter, two-arm, randomized, double blinded study. A total of 196 subjects will be
      enrolled in this study at up to 15 investigational sites located in the U.S.
