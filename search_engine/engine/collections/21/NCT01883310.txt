
    
      The inclusion criteria will be:

        -  males and females, community dwelling, age 18 or older;

        -  diagnosis of multiple sclerosis in a stable phase, which will be defined as a chronic
           progressive or relapsing-remitting pattern with no relapse during the last 3 months;

        -  moderate gait impairments referred to Expanded Disability Status Scale between 4 and 5,5
           (this EDSS scores refers to people with restricted ambulation who do not need walking
           aids).
