
    
      PROTOCOL OUTLINE:

      Patients undergo punch skin biopsy obtained from the upper arm or back. Studies of
      fibrillin-1 synthesis and cellular processing, and FBN1 mutational analysis, linkage
      analysis, and genetic mapping are performed on the tissue samples. Patients from all major US
      ethnic groups (Caucasian, Hispanic, African Americans) will be recruited for the study in the
      next 2 years.
