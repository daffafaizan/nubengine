
    
      The goal of the study is to assess the feasibility, safety, and acceptability of the
      Jintronix system, as well as provide preliminary evidence regarding the clinical efficacy for
      post-stroke rehabilitation.This is a pilot parallel randomised single-blinded controlled
      trial, with patients who have had a stroke randomly allocated to one of two groups: (1) usual
      rehabilitation services and additional training with Jintronix system (treatment group) or
      (2) usual rehabilitation services only (control group).
