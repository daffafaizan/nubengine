
    
      With this prospective registry, all patients with aortic stenosis, mitral and aortic
      regurgitation will be followed. The investigators will find followed patients not yet
      operated or until surgery, but also patient after surgery according to the presence or
      abscence of guidelines criteria.

      It will permit to compare different kind of management of patients with valvular heart
      disease and to better define guidelines criteria for surgery.
