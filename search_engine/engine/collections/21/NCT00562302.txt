
    
      This is a multi-centered, randomized trial in patients receiving lung biopsies. Patients will
      be randomized to either receive or not receive a Bio-Seal Biopsy Track Plug after a lung
      biopsy per standard hospital protocol. This study is designed to demonstrate safety and
      efficacy of the Bio-Seal Biopsy Track Plug in reducing pneumothorax rates post lung biopsy.
