
    
      Procedure: Femtosecond laser assisted implantation of intracorneal ring segments in
      keratoconus patients Uncorrected visual acuity (UCVA), best corrected visual acuity (BCVA),
      refraction, keratometric (K) readings of the anterior and posterior surface, corneal
      asphericity (Q value) of the anterior and posterior surface, and anterior and posterior
      elevations are evaluated using a corneal imaging device preoperatively and at 1, 3, 6 and 12
      months after surgery.
