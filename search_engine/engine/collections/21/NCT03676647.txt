
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. To validate a novel epigenetic thyroid nodule diagnostic test, diagnostic DNA methylation
      signature (DDMS) in a retrospective-prospective study with leftover of fine needle aspiration
      biopsies.

      OUTLINE:

      Previously collected FNA aspiration specimen samples are analyzed via DDMS assay.
      Participants undergo FNA aspiration for collection of tissue samples for analysis via DDMS
      assay.

      After completion of study, patients are followed up yearly for 5 years.
