
    
      Sixty patients (72 sides) with medial or low condylar fractures were treated with surgical
      intervention. Thirty-six patients (42 sides) were treated with minor parotid anterior
      approach, and the other 24 patients (30 sides) were treated with retromandibular approach.
      The data about the operations and complications were recorded. The follow-up periods were
      3-12 months.
