
    
      Subjects are to be enrolled in this clinical study if they are 18-65 years old. A maximum of
      25 subjects will be enrolled at 1 study center. Subjects will receive up to 6 treatments
      (minimum of 4 treatment visits with an optional 5th and 6th treatment) on the face. Subjects
      will return for follow up visits at 30 and 90 days post last treatment. All subjects will
      receive a phone call 1 week after each treatment.
