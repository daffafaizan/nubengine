
    
      Validated methods were used to gather data on the prevalence of LBP, LP and PGP,
      anthropometric and socio-demographic characteristics, history of LBP, obstetrical history,
      physical activity before and during pregnancy, mattress and sleep characteristics,
      disability, anxiety, and depression. Separate multiple logistic regression models were
      developed to identify the variables associated with LBP, LP and PGP.
