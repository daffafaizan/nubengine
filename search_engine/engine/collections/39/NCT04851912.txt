
    
      The purpose of this study is to gather longitudinal data to examine how speech features and
      symptoms change over different intervals of time and the reliability of repeated, high
      frequency test administration. Participants will complete a variety of speech and language
      tasks using the Winterlight Assessment (iOS app) everyday for 30 days. The following is the
      list of assessments that participants will complete; 1) Daily assessment of mood, 2)
      Winterlight assessment which contains a series of verbal response tasks where participants
      are recorded through a tablet app, and 3) Clinical assessments.
