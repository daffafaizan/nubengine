
    
      A prospective cohort study on consecutive patients scheduled for hepatectomy was planned.
      Fibroscan was performed during the preoperative workup. Accuracy of the liver stiffness and
      steatosis measured by Fibroscan in predicting the presence of cirrhosis, of steatosis, and
      the development of postoperative complications was assessed by ROC curve, and multivariate
      analysis.
