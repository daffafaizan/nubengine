
    
      The objectives of this study are to evaluate the safety and efficacy of Naftin (naftifine
      hydrochloride) Cream, 2% applied once daily for 2 weeks, compared to its vehicle in the
      treatment of pediatric subjects aged 2 to 17 years and 11 months with positive potassium
      hydroxide (KOH), positive dermatophyte culture, and clinical signs and symptoms of tinea
      corporis.
