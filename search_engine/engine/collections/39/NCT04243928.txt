
    
      The use of Kinesio Taping might influence the cutaneous receptors of the sensory motor
      system, resulting in the improvement of voluntary control and coordination in a physiotherapy
      program for children with CP. (Yasukawa et al., 2006).

      The study will be conducted To investigate the effect of kinesiotape applied on dorsi fexors
      muscles on improving balance in spastic diplegic children .
