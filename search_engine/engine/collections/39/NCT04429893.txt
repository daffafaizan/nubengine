
    
      Patients will be randomly into two equal groups (forty patients each) according to the
      adjuvant added to the local anesthetic (bupivacaine) in the serratus anterior plane block
      using a computer-generated random numbers concealed in sealed opaque envelopes. Group B: The
      patients will receive 20 ml of 0.5% bupivacaine plus 5 ml 0.9% normal saline with total
      volume 25 ml. Group BM: The patients will receive 20 ml of 0.5% bupivacaine plus 150 mg
      magnesium sulphate in 0.9% normal saline with a total volume of 25 ml.
