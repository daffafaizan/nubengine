
    
      This research study's procedures include screening for eligibility, participant designation
      of a caregiver and a series of questionnaires.

      - It is expected that about 180 people undergoing treatment for multiple myeloma and up to
      180 of their caregivers will take part in this research study.

      -- The questionnaires are completed one time only and measure quality of life, mood, coping
      strategies, and prognostic understanding and can be completed in the hospital, clinic, over
      the email, or telephone with assistance provided as needed.Questionnaires take approximately
      20 minutes to complete.
