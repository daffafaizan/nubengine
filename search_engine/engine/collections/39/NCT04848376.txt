
    
      The purpose of study is to confirm the safety and and performance of the A-SPINE's products
      for using 2 years. This post-market clinical follow-up study use questionnaires such as
      Visual Analog Scale (VAS), the Neck Disability Index (NDI), or the Oswestry Disability Index
      (ODI) to evaluate the clinical improvement effects. Subjects will be evaluated at
      pre-operation, 6 months, 12 months, and 2 years post treatment. Finally, analyze the clinical
      outcome, fusion rate, and adverse events.
