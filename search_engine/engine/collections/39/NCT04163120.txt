
    
      Fourteen overweight women with diagnosis of Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) will undergo to
      a low calorie ketogenic Mediterranean diet with phyoextracts (KEMEPHY) for 12 week. Changes
      in body weight, body mass index (BMI), fat body mass (FBM), lean body mass (LBM), visceral
      adipose tissue (VAT), insulin, glucose, HOMA-IR, total cholesterol, low density lipoprotein
      (LDL), high density lipoprotein (HDL(, triglycerides (TGs), total and free testosterone,
      luteinizing hormone (LH), follicle stimulating hormone (FSH); dehydroepiandrosterone sulfate
      (DHEAs), estradiol, progesterone, sex hormone binding globulin (SHBG) and Ferriman Gallwey
      score will be evaluated.
