
    
      Data will be collected on all patients who were discharged with HMV between October 2014 and
      August 2015 at Thammasat University Hospital. Data include sex, age, co-morbid diseases,
      clinical features, causes of HMV, mechanical ventilator and complimentary equipment
      characteristics, caregiver characteristics, ventilator and patient care costs, and morbidity
      and mortality outcomes of patients.
