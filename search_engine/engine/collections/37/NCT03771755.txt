
    
      Approval from Ethical Committee of Faculty of Medicine Universitas Indonesia was acquired
      prior conducting the study. Patients undergoing orthopedic low extremity surgery were
      screened. Eligible subjects were then randomized into treatment group (intravenous ibuprofen)
      and control group (intravenous acetaminophen). In case required, patients from both group
      will be given Morphine (PCA) 1-2 mg every 5 minutes.
