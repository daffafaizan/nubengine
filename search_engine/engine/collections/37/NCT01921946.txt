
    
      After subjects have signed informed consent voluntarily, they go through screening period for
      within 21 days.

      As period I, subjects of Part A take fimasartan for 7 days and subjects of Part B take
      rosuvastatin for 7 days.

      And then, after wash out for 7 days, as period II, subjects of both Part A and B take
      fimasartan and rosuvastatin for 7 days.

      At each period, subjects of Part A have blood sampling 2nd, 4th, 6th day before medication,
      7th day before and 0.25, 0.5, 1, 1.5, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 24, 36, and 48hour after
      medication(17 times in each period, 34 times in total).

      At each period, subjects of Part B have blood sampling 2nd, 4th, 6th day before medication,
      7th day before and 1, 1.5, 2, 2.5, 3, 3.5, 4, 5, 8, 12, 24, 48 and 72hour after medication(17
      times in each period, 34 times in total).
