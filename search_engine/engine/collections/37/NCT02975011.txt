
    
      This is a prospective study to the aim of investigating the effectiveness and safety in
      patients with neurogenic claudication diagnosed as lumbar stenosis by lumbar MRI and
      receiving integrative Korean medicine treatment (herbal medicine, Chuna manipulation, bee
      venom pharmacopuncture and pharmacopuncture, acupuncture, electroacupuncture, and cupping) at
      3 locations of Jaseng Hospital of Korean Medicine (Bucheon, Daejeon, Busan (Haeundae))
      through assessment of pain, functional disability, and quality of life with 1-year follow-up
      period
