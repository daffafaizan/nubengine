
    
      This is a cross-sectional (studies in which the presence or absence of disease or other
      health-related variables are determined in each member of the study population or in a
      representative sample at 1 particular time) observational study (a scientific study to make a
      clear and easy understanding of the cause and effect relationship) to investigate the health
      related QoL in Korean female participants with chronic constipation having received treatment
      previously by using EQ-5D (EuroQol 5 Dimension), SF-36 (Short Form Health Survey) and PAC-QoL
      (Patient Assessment of Constipation) questionnaires. The duration of study will be 6 months.
      Participant's safety will be monitored throughout the study.
