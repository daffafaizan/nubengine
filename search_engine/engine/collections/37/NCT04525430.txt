
    
      Aim: The purpose of this study was to investigate how pregnant women's childbirth
      self-efficacy was affected by childbirth education and birth plans applied with the
      education.

      Methods: The research was carried out at seven Family Health Centers in Turkey as a
      randomized controlled trial. The pregnant women constituting the sample were divided into 3
      groups. Group 1 was given only childbirth education (Group-ED), group 2 was given childbirth
      education and was subjected to a birth plan (Group-ED&P), and group 3 was the control group,
      each including 51 pregnant women.
