
    
      Patients with necrotic teeth with apical periodontitis will be selected according to the
      eligibility criteria and patients are then randomized to either single visit or multiple
      visit using triple paste intracanal medicament. Postoperative pain will then assessed using
      numerical rating scale after 1, 2, 3 and 7 days post-instrumentation. Radiographic healing
      will be assessed every 3 months up to one year.
