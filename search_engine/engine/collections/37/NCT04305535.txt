
    
      Multicentric, parallel, randomized, double blind and controlled clinical-nutritional study of
      6 months of intervention and 3 study groups of treatment: Experimental group (peptidic diet
      with a mix of probiotics); Experimental group with placebo (peptidic diet with a placebo);
      and Control group (control with a polymeric nutritional oral supplement with a placebo) to
      evaluate the effect on the nutritional status, inflammatory markers and the intestinal
      absorption
