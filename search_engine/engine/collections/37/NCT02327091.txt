
    
      A total 122 elderly women were enrolled to this study. It was determined subjects with
      handgrip strength less than 22 kg and measured using handheld dynamometer. There were 95
      subjects fulfilled the eligible criteria. These subjects were randomized into two groups, one
      group of 47 subjects received alfacalcidol 0.5 mcg/day and the other group received placebo.
      Each subject was given calcium 500 mg/day. After 12 weeks of intervention, 88 subjects had
      the second muscle strength measurement (7 dropped out).
