
    
      Our study will address the following specific aims: (1) examine the impact of a lifestyle
      intervention on gestational weight gain and postpartum weight loss in overweight and obese
      women, (2) examine the impact of the intervention on physical activity (PA), dietary intake,
      and quality of life (QOL), (3) examine race differences in total gestational weight gain, PA,
      dietary intake, and QOL; and (4) examine the impact of the intervention on offspring
      adiposity.
