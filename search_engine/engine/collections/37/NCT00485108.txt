
    
      Patients who have been chosen to undergo SLT for glaucoma will be randomized to receive one
      of prednisolone 1%, ketorolac 0.5% or artificial tears four times a day for 5 days following
      administration of the laser treatment. The IOP will be measured at various time points
      following SLT: 1 hour, 2 days, 1 week, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, and 1 year.
