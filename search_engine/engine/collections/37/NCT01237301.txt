
    
      This study involves the use of the following 2 glucose monitoring methods to measure your
      blood sugar (glucose) levels and help manage type 2 diabetes:

        1. Self Monitoring Blood Glucose (SMBG): blood glucose is measured 4-7 times each day using
           finger sticks and an blood glucose meter.

        2. Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM): blood glucose is measured continuously via the CGM
           device. This device has been approved for use by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration
           (FDA).
