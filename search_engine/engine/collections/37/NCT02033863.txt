
    
      Prospective, non-randomized, multi-center confirmatory observational study for the treatment
      of subjects with the need for vascular occlusion for the following conditions:

        1. Cohort A: Varicocele arising from the spermatic vein(s) and pampiniform plexus, with or
           without concomitant diagnosis of infertility or subfertility

        2. Cohort B: Pelvic varices in females for the treatment of pelvic congestion syndrome
           (e.g., pelvic venous incompetence).
