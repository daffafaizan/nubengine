
    
      Prospective, observational, multicenter study in ARF patients (minimal pulse oximetry -SpO2-
      of 92% with an minimal oxygen flow of 6 L/min , respiratory rate > 25/min (20/min if use of
      accessory respiratory muscles) with a compatible etiology. Primary outcome measure is
      increase in ventilatory support (non-invasive positive pressure ventilation (NPPV) or
      endotracheal intubation (ETI)) within the first 24h following BAL.
