
    
      Following signing an informed consent patients who are scheduled for gynecological
      laparoscopy surgery for benign disease will randomized to general vs. combined general and
      spinal anesthesia.

      During the postoperative period data regarding pain level as evaluated by visual analogue
      scale (VAS), number of doses of pain relief drugs, satisfaction and number of day in
      hospitalization will be collected from patient charts.
