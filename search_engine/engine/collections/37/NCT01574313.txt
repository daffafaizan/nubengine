
    
      Ten patients were randomly assigned to receive stellate ganglion block. These ten patients
      made up the experimental group. Another ten patients in the control group were assigned to
      receiving one dosage of oral medications: 0.25mg of erispan@ (fludiazine) , 25mg cephadol@
      (diphenidol), and 200mg kentons@ (tocopherol nicotinate).
