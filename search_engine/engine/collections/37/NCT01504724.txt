
    
      This prospective randomized study included 30 patients (60 eyes) with severe nonproliferative
      diabetic retinopathy or non-high-risk proliferative diabetic retinopathy. They had weekly PRP
      treatments in 3 sessions and they were randomly assigned to IVB group who had adjunctive IVB
      within 1 week before first PRP and control group who had only PRP. CMT, RNFL, and
      best-corrected visual acuity (BCVA) were measured
