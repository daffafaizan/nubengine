
    
      To study the effectiveness of MPM in ridge augmentation with implant insertion compared with
      autogenous bone graft.

      -PICO Population(P) : Patients with defective posterior maxillary alveolar ridge.
      Intervention (I) :Implant placement and ridge augmentation with MPM. Comparator (C) :
      Autogenous bone graft. Outcome (O) : Patient satisfaction - Implant stability.
