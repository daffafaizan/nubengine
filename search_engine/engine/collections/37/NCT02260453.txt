
    
      Patients candidates for CEA, with no history of CAD, a normal electrocardiogram (ECG), and a
      normal cardiac ultrasound were randomized in two groups. In group A (n = 216) all patients
      underwent coronary angiography before CEA +- coronary artery revascularization. In group B (n
      = 210) CEA was performed without coronary angiography.
