
    
      Device accuracy will be assessed based on the acceptance criteria of ISO 15197:2003 which
      states: "The minimum acceptable accuracy for results produced by a glucose monitoring system
      shall be as follows: 95% of the individual glucose results shall fall within 15 mg/dL of the
      results of Reference method at glucose concentrations ≥ 4,2 mmol/L (≥ 75 mg/dL)".
