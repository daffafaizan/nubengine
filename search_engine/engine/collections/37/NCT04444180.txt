
    
      This study is conducted according to the tenets of the Declaration of Helsinki. All
      participants are recruited at the Shanghai Mental Health Center (SMHC) or its affiliate, the
      Shanghai Psychotherapy and Psychological Counseling Center (SPCC) with written informed
      consent, approved by the Research Ethics Committee of SMHC. For those younger than 18 years,
      both the adolescents and their next of kin or legal guardians are provided informed consents.
      The outpatients are recommended by doctors to participate in our CHR screening. After being
      screened and interviewed, those who meet the inclusion criteria are informed and signed
      written informed consent. Once the subjects are recruited, clinical evaluation, VHI and MCCB
      test will be conducted. In addition, CHR individuals will be followed up at one-year node.
