
    
      Changes in the sensory, neurological, and musculoskeletal systems in older adults affect
      several motor tasks,including postural balance. Also osteoarthritis contribute to this
      process. Older people can gain balance disorders. The flamingo balance exercise is a full
      lower body workout utilizing many major muscle groups. Therefore investigators aimed to
      determine the effect of flamingo exercise combined with kinesthetic ability trainer or alone
      in patients with balance impairment due to osteoarthritis
