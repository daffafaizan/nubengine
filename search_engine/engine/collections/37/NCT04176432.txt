
    
      Methods:

      During 2015-2016 106 patients aged 80 years or above underwent emergency laparotomy at
      Haukeland University Hospital.

      Primary outcome was mortality at 30 days. Secondary outcomes were mortality at 90 days, 1
      year, postoperative complications and level of care at discharge.

      The Medical records were reviewed in detail and mortality and postoperative morbidity were
      registered. Post-operative complications were defined according to the European Perioperative
      Clinical Outcome (EPCO) definitions and included if they were Clavien Dindo grade 2 or above.
