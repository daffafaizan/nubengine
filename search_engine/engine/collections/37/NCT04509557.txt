
    
      [18F]PSMA PET/CT is conducted at the 2nd screening and only PSMA PET/CT positive patients can
      be enrolled (PSMA RADS 4 or more).

      Dose is administered by differentiated into 5 groups (6 subjects each) and sequentially
      elevated starting from a low dose to a high dose (50±5 mCi, 75±8 mCi, 100±10 mCi, 125±13 mCi,
      150±15 mCi).
