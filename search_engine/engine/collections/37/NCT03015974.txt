
    
      A total of 1200 patients diagnosed as primary IgA nephropathy with nephrotic proteinuria will
      be enrolled among 25 pediatric nephrology medical centers nationwide, according to the
      following protocol.

        1. Establishment of registration database online.

        2. Participants will be enrolled according to the inclusion criteria and exclusion
           criteria.

        3. The following data will be collected prospectively, including demographic data, clinical
           symptoms, physical examination, laboratory examination, renal pathology, treatment
           protocol and follow-up.

        4. SPSS software (version 14.0; SPSS, Inc., Chicago, IL, USA) will be used for statistical
           analysis. P value less than 0.05 will be considered significant.
