
    
      The purpose of the study is to compare the diagnostic accuracy of the ZIO xt , MoMe,
      TECHNOMED pocket ECG and Nuubo to the R-test, our current standard clinical practice, in
      detection of AF burden, in patients with history of Paroxysmal Atrial Fibrillation and have a
      dual chamber pacemaker or ICD in situ, with sensitivity and specificity of AF detection
      calculated from the implanted pacemaker monitored beat to beat holters.
