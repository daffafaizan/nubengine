
    
      This is a multi-center, prospective cohort study. This study plans to recruit 200 subjects.
      The suitable subject will be enrolled and there are seven visits through the 6-month study.
      Efficacy parameters are the positive incidence of open food challenge test, growth and
      development index (Weight, Height, Head circumference;), and the improvement of CMPA symptoms
      (such as SCORAD, gastrointestinal tract, respiratory, comfort scores).
