
    
      This study is a three arm randomized controlled clinical trial. Eligible children will be
      selected from the outpatient clinic of Pediatric Dentistry and Public Health Department,
      Faculty of Dentistry, Alexandria University after securing necessary consents. One hundred
      and fifty five teeth will be randomly allocated into 3 groups fifty two each. Group I will be
      assigned to Hall technique Group II will be assigned to atraumatic restorative treatment
      using Glass ionomer cement, and Group III will be assigned to a conventional restoration and
      will serve as a control.
