
    
      Only cases of initial diagnosis of HighGradeT1 and with a complete transurethral endoscopic
      resection (TUR) of bladder tumor) can enter this protocol. HighGradeT1a will only receive
      standard BCG treatment (induction and maintenance). HighGradeT1b will undergo a second
      transurethral endoscopic resection (TUR) after the induction of Bacillus de Calmette-Guerin
      (BCG) and then continue mantenaince BCG and standard follow-up.
