
    
      Participants are adult women suffering from PTSD or Complex PTSD (n=56) that are randomized
      to three months of weekly (12) sessions of trauma modified GrpMI therapy or no treatment for
      an equal amount of time (wait list control).

      Primary outcome is pre, post and 3 months follow-up measures of PTSD checklist 5 (PCL-5).

      Secondary outcome is pre, post and 3 months follow-up measures of dissociative experience
      scale (DES), positive state of mind scale (PSOM-S), Hopkins symptoms checklist (HSCL-25),
      somatoform dissociation questionnaire (SDQ-5), and reactivity and recovery of different
      physiological measures related to arousal regulation ability in a script driven imagery
      setting.
