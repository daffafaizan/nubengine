
    
      This study will assess course of patients with FSHD in accordance with usual care performed
      within the Department of clinical physiology at the University hospital of Montpellier. This
      prospective longitudinal study include an initial assessment. Then the follow up of patients
      will be monitored at 6 months and annually for a period of six years. The eventual objective
      will be to develop a FSHD disease cohort in care at the Montpellier hospital.
