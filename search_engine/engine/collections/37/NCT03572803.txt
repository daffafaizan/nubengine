
    
      Experimental study randomized with double blind, whose purpose is to compare three protocols
      in those who apply different protocols of physical therapy to themselves in three groups of
      intervention with patients with chronic lateral epicondyle tendinopathy.

      There take part in this study people of both sexes, with ages between 18 and 65 years, all of
      them present chronic lateral epicondyle tendinopathy.
