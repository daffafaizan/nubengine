
    
      After screening,each participant undergo baseline pulmonary function test(Jaeger Master
      Screen Baby Body, Germany)and post-bronchodilator measurements that performed 10min after
      200ug of salbutamol administered using a metered-dose inhaler and a spacer.

      The participants undergo baseline lung function test and bronchial dilation tests on the
      second and third visits with one-month interval,and whenever there is an exacerbation.
