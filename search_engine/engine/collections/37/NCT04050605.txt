
    
      The aim of this prospective study is to evaluate the clinical performance of habitual wearers
      of Clariti Toric lenses after a refit with Avaira Vitality Toric lenses for 1-month of daily
      wear.

      This is a prospective, subject masked; bilateral, 1-month refit study comparing the fitting
      characteristics of Clariti Toric silicone hydrogel lenses, (somofilcon A); against Avaira
      Vitality silicone hydrogel Toric lenses (fanfilcon A).
