
    
      This randomized controlled trial is to determine the effect of shock wave therapy in the
      treatment of primary dysmenorrhea.

      females are randomly assigned into 2 groups: group A SWT treatment plus dietary modification
      during PD (n=25） GROUP B:dietary modification only (n=25）;

      Participants in group B will receive dietary modification for three successive months

      The numerical rating scale (NRS) and prostaglandin blood level will be recorded and evaluated
      before and after treatment.
