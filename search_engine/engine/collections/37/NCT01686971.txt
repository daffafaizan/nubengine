
    
      Cancer patients on finishing therapy will be interviewed by a registered nurse by a approved
      questionaire asking about his /her feeling needs and demands.

      Efforts to help them will be trid

      It is well known from studies that survivors have unmet medical and non-medical needs that
      are not met, this includes physical, emotional and practical issues. The purpose of this
      study is to study them and investigate the methods how to alleviate them.

      All patients with breast and colon cancer that are refered to the Rambam Oncology center will
      be investigated.
