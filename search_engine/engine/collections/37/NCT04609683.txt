
    
      Study subjects scheduled for an EP procedure who have signed an informed consent form (ICF)
      will be admitted to the study. Prior to the EP procedure (e.g. in a hospital room or
      operating room at NYU Medical Center), either an employee of NYU or Hydrostasis will place
      sensor on subject's bicep, forearm, or wrist, start the sensor and connect the sensor to
      mobile app to start data collection.
