
    
      2 groups of 10 healthy participants each are included. 1 group aged 18 to 40 and a group of
      participants above 60 years of age.

      The functional MRI sequences are performed consecutively in a protocol set up on a 1,5 T MRI
      scanner.

      In totl, 3 identical scans are being performed. 2 scans on day 1 followed by another scan 1-2
      weeks later. Before and after each scan, cortisol and copeptin is controlled in a blood
      sample to quantify the level of physiological stress.
