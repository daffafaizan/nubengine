
    
      Poor dietary habits of Irish adults has been accompanied by epidemics of obesity and
      metabolic disorders such as type II diabetes leading to a substantial predicted increase in
      the prevalence of chronic diseases such as cardiovascular disease.Hypertension is a major
      contributing factor to the development of cardiovascular and metabolic diseases. Dietary
      factors play a significant role in the prevention and/or treatment of hypertension and
      therefore the development of foods with antihypertensive properties could prove beneficial.
