
    
      An open label, randomised controlled trial to assess the pharmacokinetics and
      pharmacodynamics of intramuscular, intravenous and oral solution administration of tranexamic
      acid in women giving birth by caesarean section. 120 women (30 receiving oral liquid, 30
      receiving intramuscular, 30 receiving intravenous and 30 receiving no TXA who have at least 6
      evaluable PK samples will be randomised.
