
    
      This is a multi-center observational study. Treatment and follow-up visits will be done as
      per standard of care.

      The purpose of this study is to demonstrate that use of the FRED/FRED Jr Embolic device in
      intracranial aneurysm treatment is safe and effective when assessed at 1, 6 and 12 month post
      procedure.

      100 patients will be enrolled over an 18-month recruitment period. All patients will be
      followed for 12 months.
