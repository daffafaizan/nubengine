
    
      Critically ill patients, presenting Glasgow Coma Score 5 or lower, will be submitted to the
      Computed Tomography Angiography (CTA) of the skull assessment, and follow a clinical
      evaluation for brain death, additionally, transcranial Doppler would be applied as the gold
      standard for brain death.

      We will exclude patients whose do not can be submitted to contrast injection, or if a brain
      death clinical evaluation has been performed prior the CTA.
