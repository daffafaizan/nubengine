
    
      The investigators divided two groups. (total number = 100 subjects)

        1. with probiotics group - 50 subjects

             -  Participants included in this group will be taken probiotics for 1 month after
                bowel preparation for colonoscopy.

        2. without probiotics group - 50 subjects

             -  Participants included in this group will not be taken probiotics for 1 month after
                bowel preparation for colonoscopy.
