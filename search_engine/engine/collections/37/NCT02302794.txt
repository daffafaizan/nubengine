
    
      Study type：Interventional Study phase：Phase III Objectives of Study：to evaluate the long-term
      efficacy and safety of oncology between D2 laparoscopic approach distal gastric resection
      (distal gastrectomy, D2 lymph node dissection) with the current standard surgical treatment
      model D2 open approach distal gastric resection (distal gastrectomy, D2 lymphadenectomy).
