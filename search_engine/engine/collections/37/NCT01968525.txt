
    
      After breathing for 3 minutes, anesthesia induction was conducted. The endotracheal tube
      position was confirmed after anesthesia induction. Patients were left apneic with the
      endotracheal tube open to room air. The non-hypoxic apneic period was recorded as the time
      (in seconds) taken for the drop in pulse saturation to 90% or an apneic period of 10 min
      elapsed, whichever was earlier.
