
    
      Patients randomized to either arm of the study will be required to undergo a lung
      transplantation education and counselling session with their CF physician prior to
      randomization. Subsequent to this session patients will be randomly allocated to receive: 1)
      usual care plus the patient decision aid or 2) usual care. For purposes of this study, usual
      care will include a booklet entitled, "Cystic fibrosis and lung transplantation" written by
      the Canadian Cystic Fibrosis Foundation plus any other written or educational material that
      the local CF centre normally provides.
