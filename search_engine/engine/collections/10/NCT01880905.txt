
    
      Female patient who are going to undergo laparoscopy/laparotomy for any gynecological reason
      will ask to participate. During the operation the uteroovarian ligament length will be
      measure by sterile ruler (calibrated plastic device 1 mm in diameter).

      The length of uteroovarian ligament will compared between the patient who will diagnose with
      ovarian torsion (study group) and the other women (control group)
