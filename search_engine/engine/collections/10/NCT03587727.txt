
    
      A metabolic study that will be performed prior to and 12 months following bariatric surgery.
      The study will include 31-phosphorus magnetic resonance spectroscopy to measure phosphate
      concentrations in the liver; a 4 hour mixed meal tolerance test, an intravenous arginine
      test, Abdominal Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) for visceral and hepatic fat, Magnetic
      Resonance (MR) Elastography of the liver, indirect calorimetry, body composition assessment
      with Bodpod, serum metabolomics and hepatic tissue mitochondrial measures from the time of
      surgery only.
