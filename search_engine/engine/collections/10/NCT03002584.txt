
    
      Observational (non-interventional) study with a cross-sectional part for a subset of adult
      subjects with a single IGQ completion and a longitudinal cross-over part for a subset of
      subjects with test-retest IGQ completion for validation of electronic version of IGQ.

      300 eligible subjects (100 in each of the 3 countries: France, UK & Spain):

        -  180 with IBS diagnosis (60% of recruited subjects)

        -  120 from General population (40% of recruited subjects)
