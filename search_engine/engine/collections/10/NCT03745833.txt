
    
      In this study, investigators hope to 1) assess acceptance of VR mindfulness among inpatient
      adolescents and young adults with eating disorders 2) assess effects of VR mindfulness on
      perceived abdominal pain and 3) determine the impact of VR mindfulness on anxiety scores.

      This study is designed to test the feasibility, acceptability and effectiveness of VR
      mindfulness in patients as they undergo various medical and nutritional therapies to treat
      their conditions.
