
    
      Osteoarthritis of the knee joint is a very common disease in middle aged and elderly
      patients. This study evaluates the efficiency of different commonly used substances for
      intraarticular, non-operative treatment in early stages of this disease.

      240 patients receive an fluoroscopic guided injection in the knee joint: 60 patients get
      injection with steroids 60 patients get injection with prp 60 patients get injection with
      hyaluronic acid 60 patients get injection with placebo (iopamiro contrast media)

      Primary outcome: - clinical outcome (pain using numeric rating scale)

        -  Magnetic Resonance imaging (cartilage defects etc.)
