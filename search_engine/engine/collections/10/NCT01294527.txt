
    
      Eligible patients will undergo an acoustic window assessment using transthoracic
      echocardiography. Patients with adequate acoustic windows will undergo implantation of the
      WiCS-LV system.

      Patients will undergo evaluations pre-hospital discharge, and at one month, 3 months, and 6
      months post implantation. Extended follow-up will be obtained via a registry at 1, 2, 3, 4,
      and 5 years post implantation.
