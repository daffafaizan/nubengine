
    
      The purpose of the study is to evaluate the concentrations of antibiotics (daptomycin,
      vancomycin, cefotaxime, ceftriaxone, cefepime) in serum and periprosthetic tissues(bone and
      synovial joint) used as empirical treatment during reimplantation of infected hip and knee
      prostheses (1-stage or 2-stage management).
