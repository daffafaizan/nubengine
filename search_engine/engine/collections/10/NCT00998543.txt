
    
      None of the participants in this study will receive any vaccination as part of the study. All
      participants will provide blood samples for immunogenicity testing at the 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5
      year anniversaries of vaccination. Safety will be assessed for up to 5 years after
      vaccination, including follow-up of reactions that occurred during Study VVL04 (NCT
      00258947).
