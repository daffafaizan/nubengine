
    
      Primary Outcome Measure:

      Number of patients who receive a letter and patient navigation services and get screened
      compared to the control group.

      Secondary Outcome Measures:

      Proportion (%) of patients preferring FOBT vs.colonoscopy; Predictors of CRC screening and
      screening preference.

      Subjects: Ambulatory patients (men-women aged 50 years and above) recruited from primary care
      practices at the Group Health Centre, Sault Ste Marie, Ontario. All patients will be screened
      for eligibility and those who are eligible and agree to participate in the study will be
      randomized to either of the two study arms: intervention group or control group.
