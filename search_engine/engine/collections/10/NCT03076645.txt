
    
      The primary purpose of this study is to assess a person with epilepsy's level of usage of a
      mobile messaging application for peer support. The secondary purpose is to understand what
      characteristics of their group drive different levels of usage and improvements in
      self-management.

      Participants can participate as much or little as they want for a minimum of six weeks.
      Participants will complete an initial and a final questionnaire.
