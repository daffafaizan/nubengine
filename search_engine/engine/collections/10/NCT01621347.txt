
    
      Data of all consecutive pregnant women included in the adherence enhancing program of the
      Pharmacy, Department of ambulatory care & community medicine, are collected. In this program,
      adherence is measured and enhanced by electronic monitoring, pill count, motivational
      interviewing and interdisciplinary collaboration. Electronic monitoring data are collected
      during pregnancy and postpartum, and compared. Barriers and facilitators are collected using
      interview's marks.
