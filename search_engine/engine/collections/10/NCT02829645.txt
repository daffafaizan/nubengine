
    
      The investigators propose to assess, with a cohort of patients with ED the prognostic value
      of cognitive abnormalities associated with ED (cognitive flexibility and central coherence)
      They will recruit 370 patients suffering from a current ED (anorexia, bulimia, binge eating
      disorder, Eating disorder not otherwise specified).

      Each patient will be reassessed every year for 3 years.
