
    
      Thirty (30) intrabony defects of 18 patients (9 males, 9 females) were randomly divided in
      two treatment groups: test (n = 15) and control (n = 15). The intrabony defects were filled
      with A-PRF+ (n=15) in the test group, respectively with EMD in the control group, and fixed
      with sutures to ensure wound closure and stability.
