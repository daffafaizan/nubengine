
    
      Aim 1: To determine whether differences exist between people with healthy eyes and patients
      with POAG in seeing under dim illumination (Low Luminance Questionnaire) and to determine
      whether such differences are associated with life space.

      Aim 2: To determine whether differences exist between people with healthy eyes and patients
      with POAG in seeing under dim illumination (objective measures of visual function) and to
      determine whether such differences are associated with life space.
