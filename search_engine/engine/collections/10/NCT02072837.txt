
    
      Blood samples were obtained by venipuncture in the peritoneal dialyzed patients and control
      group in a twelve hours fasting state.In hemodialyzed patients blood was drawn just before
      the start of the mean weekly dialysis session also in a twelve hours fasting state from the
      vascular access.

      All samples were received once at baseline time ( in the start of the study ). Peripheral
      systolic and diastolic blood pressures were calculated as an average of 10 measurements
      during a renal replacement treatment month in the starting time of the study.

      Also, electrocardiographic examination, echocardiography and the calculation of
      ankle-brachial blood pressure index became once in the start of the study.
