
    
      This was an 8-week yoga intervention, focused on demonstrating the preliminary feasibility
      and efficacy of yoga for individuals with chronic traumatic brain injury. The study was a
      mixed-methods longitudinal study. Assessments were conducted 8 weeks prior to the yoga
      intervention and then just following the 8 week yoga intervention. The objectives of this
      feasibility and efficacy study was to 1) further develop, standardize, and manualize the yoga
      for the traumatic brain injury intervention and 2) assess change in balance, balance
      confidence, body responsiveness, pain, physical ability, and quality of life.
