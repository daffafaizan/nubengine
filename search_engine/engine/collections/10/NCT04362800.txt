
    
      The main objective of this study is to evaluate the effect of 10 days (i.e. 50 hours) of
      early HABIT-ILE (HABIT-ILE) on the overall motor function of children aged 1 to 4 with
      bilateral cerebral palsy in comparison to 10 days (50h) of spontaneous overall motor activity
      including usual rehabilitation (control group).
