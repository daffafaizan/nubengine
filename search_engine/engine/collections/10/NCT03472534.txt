
    
      This study was designed to assess the cumulative irritation potential of diacerein 1%
      ointment in comparison with vehicle ointment, 0.2% sodium lauryl sulfate (SLS) (positive
      control), and 0.9% saline (negative control) on normal skin of healthy volunteers using a
      cumulative irritancy patch test (CIPT) procedure applied once daily for 21 days.
