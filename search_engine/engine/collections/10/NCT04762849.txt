
    
      This study compares the loss of weight after the laparoscopic banded one anastomosis gastric
      bypass with the use of a shape-memory ring and standard laparoscopic one anastomosis gastric
      bypass.

      Inclusion Criteria:

        -  BMI from 30 to 50 kg / m2;

        -  Ages Eligible for Study: 18 Years to 55 Years

      Outcome Measures: Change of body mass index [ Time Frame: Baseline, at 12, 24, 36 months
      after surgery ]
