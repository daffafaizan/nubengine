
    
      Patients are assessed with Mini Mental Status Examination. Postictal reorientation time is
      measured. There is also a weekly rating of depression depth during the series. After the 6th
      ECT; Mini Mental Status Examination For a subgroup of patients; extensive blood-test samples
      are taken on the day of the 6th ECT.

      Some of the patients receive continuation-ECT once monthly after the series. In these months
      patients receive treatment as usual (naturalistic observation).
