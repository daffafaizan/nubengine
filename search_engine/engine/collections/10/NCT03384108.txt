
    
      Twenty healthy adult study participants between the ages of 18 and 60 years will be recruited
      to participate in this pilot study that will include two groups: a) Ex Vivo group (10
      volunteers) and b) In Vivo group (10 volunteers). Volunteers will be recruited to either
      group in a sequential alternating fashion.

      In vivo 13C SIRM studies evaluating the utilization of glutamine into the TCA cycle of normal
      bone marrow plasma cells.
