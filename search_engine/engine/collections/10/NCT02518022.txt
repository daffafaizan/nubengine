
    
      Participants will get two times a height, weight an gender based amount of beer.

      One time (Standard) there will be no Insulin given for the beer. Second time (Intervention)
      half of the carbohydrates containing in the beer will be covered with Insulin, additionally
      the basal rate will be set to 50% for 12 hours.

      All the time, Glucose will be monitored by continuous subcutaneous Glucose Monitoring.
