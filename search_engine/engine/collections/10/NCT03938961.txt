
    
      Data extracted from the computerized ICU system (PDMS): age, Simplified acute physiology
      score (SAPSII), weight, BMI, Nutrition Risk Score (NRS-2002), daily energy, protein, and
      glucose intakes, Energy balance, blood glucose (dysglycemia and Glu variability) and lactate,
      24hr-insulin (Goal Glu 6-8 mmol/l).

      Outcome variables: length of stay (LICU), muscle strength (MRC), vital status at 90 days.

      Patients will be followed in detail until ICU discharge and for vital status until day 90
      after admission Data will be presented as mean or median, univariate and multivariate
      analysis will be performed.
