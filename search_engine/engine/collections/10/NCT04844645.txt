
    
      Healthcare interventions delivered via personal mobile device have been shown to be
      acceptable to users, who have identified benefits such as convenience, access to personalized
      information, greater awareness of own health. Integrating mini program into the cancer pain
      care may be an effective strategy to improve cancer pain patients self-management.

      To design, construct, and test the mini program in patients managing cancer pain, evaluate
      the total remission rate of pain and the improvement in medicine compliance to improve pain
      management for cancer pain patients, and assess patient acceptance of the mini program.
