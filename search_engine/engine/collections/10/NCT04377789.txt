
    
      Novel Coronavirus is defined to be the cause of COVID-19, recently. It's known that COVID-19
      goes with excessive immune reaction of human body in severe cases. Quercetin is reported to
      be effective on treatment and prophylaxis of other SARS like coronavirus infections, as a
      strong antioxidant and scavenger flavonoid without any adverse events. Upon this data, the
      investigators hypothesize that quercetin can be effective on both prophylaxis and treatment
      of COVID-19 cases. Therefore, the aim of this study to evaluate the possible role of
      quercetin on prophylaxis and treatment of COVID-19.
