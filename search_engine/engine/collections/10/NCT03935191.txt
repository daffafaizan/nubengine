
    
      Primary Study Objective:

      To determine the accuracy of Dexcom G6 CGM System glucose readings in reference to
      arterialized venous sample YSI glucose measurements in pregnant women with diabetes mellitus.

      Secondary Study Objective:

      To assess the safety of the Dexcom G6 CGM System, through characterization of device-related
      Adverse Events (AEs), in pregnant women with diabetes mellitus.
