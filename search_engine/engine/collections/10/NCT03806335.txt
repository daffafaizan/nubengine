
    
      The study will be conducted prospectively, using a randomized double-blind design. Adult
      patients scheduled for total or partial tonsillectomy with or without adenoidectomy starting
      from January 2019 till January 2020 will be included.Patients will be allocated randomly into
      two equal groups using the sealed envelope method. Both groups will receive general
      anesthesia. Then, one group will receive pre-incision infiltration of 2.5 ml local anesthesia
      mixture in each tonsil. The second group will have placebo infiltration.
