
    
      In this controlled randomised pilot study the patients are allocated to 1. a ketogenic low
      glycemic load treatment from the outset of the study for 24 weeks or 2. enhance their regular
      diet with an initial 7-day fasting followed by a Mediterranean diet pattern until the study
      end or 3. stay on their regular diet (control group) from the outset of the study for 24
      weeks. The investigators will then assess the differences between the groups.
