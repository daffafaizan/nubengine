
    
      The study is a case-controlled observational trial. Sixty patients will be divided into 2
      groups (good or poor sleepers) depending on their first postoperative night Bispectral index
      data. Firstly, this study aims to characterise the gut and lung microbiota in patients with
      lung cancer treated with surgery. Secondly, it aims to evaluate microbiota and its influence
      on plasma kynurenine.
