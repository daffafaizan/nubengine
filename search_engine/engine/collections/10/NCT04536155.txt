
    
      The study will be performed at Centre for the Study and Treatment of Pain. The patients with
      chronic pain will participate to a group programm of therapeutic education.

      The programm consists of 9 workshops about psycho-corporal techniques performed in group.

      The patients will complete questionnaries before beginning the program, at the end of the
      programm, 6 months after the end of the program, and 12 months after the end of program.

      The questionnaries will be SF36, HADS, CPAQ, and digital pain scale.
