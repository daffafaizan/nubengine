
    
      This study is divided into 2 sequential phases for a total of 5 visits. Phase I of the study
      is the open-labeled Screening/Eligibility Phase, which includes a Screening Visit followed by
      2 Eligibility Visits. Phase II of the study is the randomized, double-masked Treatment Phase,
      which includes 2 on-therapy visits: Visit 4 (at Week 2) and Visit 5 (Week 6, Exit Visit).
