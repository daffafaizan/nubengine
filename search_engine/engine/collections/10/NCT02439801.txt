
    
      In the study, 75 ASA I-II patients aged 30-65 years will be randomized into 3 groups to
      receive 0 cmH2O PEEP (Group-1), 5 cmH2O PEEP (Group-2) and 8 cmH2O PEEP (Group-3) in PVC
      mode. In all patients underwent general anesthesia; hemodynamic parameters, Ppeak, Pplateau,
      compliance values as well as Fisevo/Fexpsevo ratio at 1 MAC and times from 1 MAC to 0.3 and
      0.1 MAC will be recorded. In addition, preoperative and postoperative pulmonary function
      tests will be compared in all patients.
