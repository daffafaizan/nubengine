
    
      This is a non-inferiority study of Omega 3 versus Soy Isoflavones, being each patient its own
      control from the baseline.

      In order to determine the difference observed in the evolution of intensity, frequency and
      the total number of hot flashes in 4 months in patients treated with Omega-3 vs Soy
      Isoflavones, is planned to include at least 100 patients, 50 per treatment arm.

      It si estimated a standar deviation of 0.5%, and a 10% lost follow-up rate.
