
    
      1. Primary Objective Estimate the percent of patients with at least one deficit who agree to
      at least one referral. "Interest" in the intervention will be determined by this metric. This
      agreement will be determined within the 1-2 weeks of consenting to the study through
      interactions between patients and the Study Team.

      This study will recruit 100 participants in 2 years. These participants will complete the
      geriatric assessment and be given recommendations based on results. The study team will
      follow the medical record to determine if the participants complied with the recommendations
