
    
      Prior to surgery, all patients undergo upper endoscopy , x-ray "time barium" manometry, 24
      hours pH registration. Moreover Quality of Life instrument (RAND-36), GSRS (Gastrointestinal
      Symptom Rating Scale) and a dysphagia score (Eckardt score) are completed by all patients.

      Follow-up with surveys 3, 6, 12, and 36 months (GSRS, RAND-36 and Eckardt score).

      At 3,12 and 36 months an x-ray "time barium" is performed and after 12 months a gastroscopy
      and at 24 hours esophageal pH monitoring are performed.
