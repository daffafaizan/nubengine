
    
      This study is a prospective, randomized, multi-center, post-market investigation of the
      effectiveness of Ambu® aScope™ 4 Cysto (single-use cystoscope) as compared to routine
      flexible reusable cystoscopes (standard of care) for ureteral stent removals. The
      investigation will include approximately 102 subjects (randomized at a 1:1 ratio).
