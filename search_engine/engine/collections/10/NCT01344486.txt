
    
      Randomized controlled trial (RCT) comparing carbon dioxide to full conditioning (addition of
      oxygen, nitrous oxide, humidification and temperature control, dexamethasone and Hyalobarrier
      gel) Women undergoing laparoscopy for endometriosis, myomectomy or adhesiolysis will be
      randomized to either group and will be compared for pain (VAS scores); inflammation (White
      blood cells, CRP) and adhesions scored by second look laparoscopy.
