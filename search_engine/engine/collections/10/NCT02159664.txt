
    
      Didgeridoo practice has been shown to improve Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) severity. The aim
      of our study is to assess the changes in upper airways obstruction levels, evaluated by sleep
      endoscopy, before and after 3 months of Didgeridoo practice in moderate non obese OSA
      patients. Severity of OSA will also be documented by polysomnography.
