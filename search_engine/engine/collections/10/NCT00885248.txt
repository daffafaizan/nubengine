
    
      Compared to dynamic phase pancreatic CT, integrated PET-CT will be evaluated for sensitivity,
      specificity and accuracy of diagnosing and staging pancreatic cancer while the gold standard
      for pancratic cancer is operative specimen. If the operation can't be performed, EUS-FNA of
      the pancreatic mass or lymph node and additional imaging modality will be preferred to
      laparotomy.
