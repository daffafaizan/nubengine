
    
      In this prospective clinical trail, 43 patients with plantar ulcer grade 1 or 2 (wagner
      scale) were randomized to one of two off-loading modalities: total contact cast or
      custom-made temporary footwear.outcomes assessed were wound surface area reduction (square
      cm.)and time to wound healing (days0 at 2,4,8 and 16 weeks. to evaluate safety, possible side
      effects were recorded at each follow-up visit.
