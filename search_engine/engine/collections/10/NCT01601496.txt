
    
      This study was a prospective, single-arm, multicenter study conducted in Germany and Austria
      to evaluate the FUSION Vascular Graft in patients with peripheral arterial occlusive disease
      undergoing above-the-knee bypass. Follow-up visits were performed at 30 days, 6 months, 12
      months and included patency and post operative complications not associated with bypass
      patency.
