
    
      This study was conducted with 270 patients who were the COVID-19 test was positive and
      isolated in their homes during post-acute periods.While one of the groups (n=135) was asked
      to remain isolated at home by providing patient education,a PR program was applied for 4
      weeks in addition to the patients in the other group(n=135).PR consists of patient education,
      breathing, in-house mobilization and range of motion exercises.As the evaluation
      parameters,pre-treatment and post-treatment dyspnea levels with Borg scale,muscle pain with
      visual analog scale(VAS),functional capacities with sit-up test,chest expansions with tape
      measure,respiratory frequency,fatigue level with fatigue severity scale,and emotional status
      with hospital anxiety and depression(HAD) scale were evaluated.
