
    
      Eligible participants are: 1) 18-65 years of age; 2) current episode of 2-4 weeks of LBP; 3)
      self-reported physically demanding work; 4) express concerns about the ability to maintain
      their current job.

      The exclusion criteria are: 1) pregnancy; 2) server somatic or psychiatric diseases; 3)
      cancer or metastatic disease
