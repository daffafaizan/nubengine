
    
      In this pilot study, the objective is to determine if there is an increase in MoCA (Montreal
      Cognitive Assessment), MFRT (Modified Functional Reach Test), and FMA-UA (Fugl-Meyer
      Assessment Upper Extremity) scores of participants who receive the VR intervention in
      addition to traditional inpatient rehabilitation standard of care (experimental group) versus
      participants who receive the traditional standard of care (control group).
