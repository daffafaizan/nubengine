
    
      In this study Low intensity shockwaves (1/10 the ones used in Lithotripsy) are delivered
      directly to the penile tissue using a specially designed shockwave device and probe. Shock
      wave therapy is applied twice a week for three weeks , then a 3- week interval with no
      therapy followed by another 3 week period of twice a week shock wave therapy. Before and
      after sexual function questionnaires and objective measures of erectile function will be
      performed.
