
    
      Primary Objective: To evaluate 68Ga PSMA 11 PET/MRI and 68Ga RM2 PET/MRI for biopsy guidance
      in patients with suspected prostate cancer.

      Exploratory Objective:

        -  Correlation of 68Ga PSMA 11 uptake and Gleason score at biopsy

        -  Correlation of 68Ga RM2 and Gleason score at biopsy
