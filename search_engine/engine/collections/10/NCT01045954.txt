
    
      Subjects with stable type 1 diabetes mellitus on multiple daily insulin injections including
      basal and bolus will be selected in order to determine the correct insulin dosing formulas.
      Subjects will be placed on continuous glucose sensor (CGMS) and an isocaloric diet. Subjects
      will have the CGMS downloaded daily and the insulin doses adjusted if necessary. Subject will
      do four SMBGs daily to calibrate CGMS and on last day a 7-point SMBG. subjects will be on
      lispro and glargine insulin. Basal insulin will be once a day.
