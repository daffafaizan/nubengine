
    
      Participants with gastrointestinal stromal tumors(GIST) were divided into favorable and
      unfavorable sites according to the anatomical site of the tumor, and this study aims to
      validate the overall postoperative morbidity and mortality rates between favorable site
      receiving laparoscopic resection of GIST and that of unfavorable site under the currently
      standard surgical therapy. Main end of study: 3-year disease free survival rate
