
    
      In the study，155 patients from multiple center with periprosthetic femur fracture after hip
      arthroplasty are planned to be recruited. All the patients are treated surgically according
      to Vancouver classification，and their fracture union，pain in fracture site and lower
      extremity length are evaluated at 6 months and 1 year postoperatively．Also Harris scores are
      used to assess hip function at the 2 time points.
