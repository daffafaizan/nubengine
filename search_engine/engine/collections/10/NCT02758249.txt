
    
      The patients were allocated randomly to receive propofol or sevoflurane. Also, a total of
      18ml of blood sample was obtained for total 3 times in consecutive order.

        1. immediate before anesthesia induction

        2. postoperative 1 hours

        3. postoperative 24 hours

      Immune cells isolation from patients peripheral blood mononuclear cells. Next, immune cell
      were co-culture with human cancer cell line (MCF-7) for 24 hours. investigation for immune
      cell or cancer cell survival by flow cytometry.
