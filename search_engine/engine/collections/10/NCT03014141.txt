
    
      The study is a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled crossover study it involves a
      total of 8 visits with a 14 day run-in period for total study duration of 84 days. A washout
      period of 2 weeks will occur in between the 2, 28 day treatment periods. The effects of an
      oat bran beverage for breakfast on bowel function will be tested after 2 week and 4 weeks of
      intake.
