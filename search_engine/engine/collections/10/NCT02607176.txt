
    
      The objective of this study is to evaluate safety and efficacy of Heweizhixie Capsule in
      patients with diarrhea.This is a Phase 4 Open Label trial, enroll 2400 patients with diarrhea
      Primary Outcome Measure:Percent of subjects with treatment Success

      Secondary Outcome Measures:

      Number of unformed stools passed per 24 h period Time (hours) from first intake to the last
      unformed stools Change from baseline of Leeds dyspepsia questionnaire score Percent of
      subjects with AE.
