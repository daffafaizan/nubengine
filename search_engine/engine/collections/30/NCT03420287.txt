
    
      Alveolar bone grafting will be performed at 6 to 12 years of age as following :

      Group (A):Will undergo grafting with anterior iliac crest bone,(Control group). Group
      (B):Will undergo grafting with Mineralized Plasmatic Matrix prepared from allogenic bone
      graft (study group).

      In all patients, this will be the first bone graft to be placed in the alveolar cleft. All
      patients will be monitored clinically and radiographically (by cone beam C.T) for up to 6
      months after surgery.
