
    
      A multi-center trial examining the effects of progressive resistance group exercise compared
      with an active control group (relaxation). A person-centred model of exercise was used. The
      intervention was performed twice a week for 15 weeks and was supervised by experienced
      physiotherapists.

      Secondary aims off this multi-center study was to improve the understanding of pain
      mechanisms in fibromyalgia and how these are affected by physical exercise. Please see
      references of published articles for details.
