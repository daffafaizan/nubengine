
    
      The study will be carried out on healthy volunteers, aged 18 to 65 and self-declared
      sensitive, even intolerant to the consumption of milk.

      The main objective of the study is to assess the impact of milk Î²-caseins (A2A2 vs A1A2) on
      the digestive comfort felt during the consumption of milk in subjects declaring that they do
      not tolerate milk.
