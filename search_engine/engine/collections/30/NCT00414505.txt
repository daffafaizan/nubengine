
    
      Recently the Studies of auricular acupuncture or Common Acupuncture on obesity have been
      conducted. Saam acupuncture, the Korean unique acupuncture, is famous with its high efficacy
      but not established by the scientific way yet. Thus, we tried a double-blind study on the
      effect of sa-am acupuncture on obesity using intradermal acupuncture.
