
    
      The TriClip™ bRIGHT EU post-approval study (PAS) study is designed to confirm the safety and
      performance of the TriClip™ device in a contemporary real-world setting. The bRIGHT PAS study
      is a prospective, single arm, open-label, multi-center, post market registry, conducted to
      satisfy condition of CE Marking for the TriClip™.
