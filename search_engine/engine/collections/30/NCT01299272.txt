
    
      In order to enter the Stabilization Open-label Period, participants must have met remission
      criteria, defined as a Montgomery-Asberg Depression Rating Scale (MADRS) total score ≤10 at
      Week 8. In order to enter the Double-blind Randomization Withdrawal Period, participants must
      have met randomization criteria, defined as a MADRS total score ≤10 at Weeks 18, 19, and 20.
