
    
      Specifically, the investigators propose to test the primary hypothesis that the use of
      AppliedVR software in Pico G2 4K headsets decreases acute postoperative pain scores (with a 1
      point difference considered clinically important) compared to sham treatment on a 0-10 scale
      15 minutes after each use in the first 48 hours after surgery or hospital discharge,
      whichever comes first.
