
    
      This is a double blinded RCT comparing the effect of insulin on wound healing in both
      diabetics and non diabetics.

      The study will run in primary care setting in Bahrain. The aim of this study is to heal wound
      in an innovative way. and the Objective: to evaluate the effect of topical insulin
      administration on wound healing.

      All adult patients with skin wounds attending primary health care center will be included in
      the study. Patients with acute and chronic wounds of the upper and lower extremities will be
      eligible to participate.
