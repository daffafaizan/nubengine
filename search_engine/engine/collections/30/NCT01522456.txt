
    
      Adult subjects with acne vulgaris will receive tretinoin gel 0.1% on one side of the face and
      adapalene 0.1% / benzoyl peroxide 2.5% gel on the other side of the face once daily for a
      period 3 weeks (22 days). Tolerability assessments (erythema, dryness, scaling,
      stinging/burning)on both sides of the face will be taken on each visit, on each weekday from
      from baseline to day 22. The investigator and subjects will respond to user preference
      questions on days 5, 12, 19, and 22.
