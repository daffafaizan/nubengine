
    
      Multicenter observational study collecting data from a routine ultrasound screening for AAA
      at the waning of echocardiograms performed during a specific day. All of the cardiologists
      practicing echocardiography are invited to participate in this study, each cardiologist
      should include echocardiography in 10 patients examined for any reason and will be considered
      referring cardiologist.

      There will be no follow-up, the idea is to have a database with measures of abdominal aortic
      aneurysms and therefore.
