
    
      The purpose of the study is to testify whether acupuncture is effective for functional
      dyspepsia, through treating functional dyspepsia patient for a month, using different
      acupoints according to literatures of treating migraine with acupuncture, and using Itopride
      as controlled group, and try to provide clinical evidence for the hypothesis that"Acupuncture
      effect is based on meridians, and gathering of meridian Qi is the key point."
