
    
      The aim of the study is to determine the effect of regular pain assessment on pain management
      interventions applied and patient outcomes among patient with severe dementia.

      A cluster randomized controlled trial will be conducted. A cluster is defined as a single
      independent nursing home. The clusters will be randomly assigned to either the experimental
      or control condition. Appropriate information will be given to both the intervention and the
      control group. All nurses in the experimental conditions will be taught how to administer the
      DOLOPLUS-2. The study will start after the initial education session. During the 3-month
      study period, the participating nursing staff will be asked to complete the DOLOPLUS-2
      regularly. The control groups will receive standard methods of care. Data collection at
      baseline and at 4, 8 and 12 months.
