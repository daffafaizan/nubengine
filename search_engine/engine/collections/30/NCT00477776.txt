
    
      The study aims to determine the breastfeeding initiation and duration rate of a cohort of
      pregnant women with gestational diabetes(diet-controlled) and insulin dependent and
      pre-existing diabetes, assess the impact of a post natally administered galactogogue,
      metoclopramide on the milk volume production and timing of lactogenesis II in diabetic women
      on diet control and insulin and determine the prolactin response to lactation among diabetic
      women on diet control and insulin.
