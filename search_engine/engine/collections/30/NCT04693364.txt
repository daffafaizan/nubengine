
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVE:

      I. To determine feasibility and explore the acceptability of 3 dimensional (3D) printed
      breast models as a decision aid in breast cancer treatment decision making.

      OUTLINE:

      Patients complete questionnaire over 5 minutes about difficulties in making decision about
      breast cancer treatment, then participate in a consultation with regular care doctor and
      study doctor/study staff using the 3D breast model. Patients then complete questionnaires
      over 5-10 minutes about their opinions on the breast model and different breast surgical
      treatment options available.
