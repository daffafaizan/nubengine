
    
      A web-based, biometric-linked vaccination record, cell-phone reminder and compliance-linked
      incentive software platform to provide robust and universal access of vaccinations. The
      investigators will implement this platform in a low-resource settings with the following
      features: a) Web-based for robust and universal access. b) Biometric-linked for positive
      identification. c) Digital storage and reporting for transparent view of program operations.
      d) Global Positioning System (GPS)-linked, allowing rapid assessment of vaccination status of
      communities. e) Increasing vaccination uptake and coverage by automated text message
      reminders and compliance-linked incentives.
