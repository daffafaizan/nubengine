
    
      Description of the treatment regimen: Bortezomib: 1.3 mg/m2 as i.v. bolus injection, given on
      days 1, 4, 8, and 11 of each treatment cycle; Rituximab: 375 mg/m2 infusion, day 1 of each
      cycle; Dexamethasone: 40 mg per day orally (days 1 - 4) of each treatment cycle. Treatment
      will be given for a total of 6 cycles (every 21 days), followed by maintenance treatment with
      rituximab (375 mg/m2 every two months for 4 times)
