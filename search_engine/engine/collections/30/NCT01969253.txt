
    
      Primary Objectives:

      - To analyze incidence rate of lymphedema using tape measurements, perometre, bioimpedance,
      spectroscopy.

      Secondary Objectives:

        -  To determine the risk factor of lymphedema in postoperative breast cancer.

        -  To understand that lymphedema has an effect on quality of life and physical function in
           postoperative breast cancer.

        -  To complete uestionnaire about physical and quality of life in postoperative breast
           cancer with ALND patients.
