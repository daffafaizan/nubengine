
    
      The study will evaluate the clinical performance of rotating platform and fixed bearing
      cruciate ligament substituting implants by obtaining a series of primary TKAS. Patients will
      receive either the P.F.C.® Sigma™ Rotating Platform Cruciate Substituting Knee System or
      P.F.C.® Sigma™ Fixed Cruciate Substituting Knee System and assignment is randomized.
