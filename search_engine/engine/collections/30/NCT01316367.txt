
    
      Assignment to the Health Promotion Education (HPE) will be random, three centers will use
      conventional HPE (the control group) and the remaining five will use PRECEDE-type HPE.
      Eligible patients are selected from a list of DM2 patients by each professional using random
      sampling, until the predetermined sample size is attained.

      Conventional HPE is defined according to the recommendations of the Spanish Ministry of
      Health National Conference on Diabetes Mellitus, and was complemented by the criteria for
      good care of the Madrid Primary Healthcare Service for the promotion of healthy lifestyles
      among adults for 2004-2007
