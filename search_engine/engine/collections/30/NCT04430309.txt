
    
      A single-blind, 2-arm randomized controlled trial investigating the benefits of Baduanjin
      exercise on middle-aged and older patients with schizophrenia. A total of 48 participants
      will be assigned to either 12 weeks of 60-minute two sessions per week Baduanjin exercise
      group or a treatment as usual group. Primary outcomes are physical fitness and cognition
      functions. Secondary outcomes are dual task performance and daily functions. Evaluation will
      be completed at baseline, immediately after treatment and 4-week follow up.
