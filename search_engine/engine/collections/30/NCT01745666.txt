
    
      65 patients with KCNQ1 or KCNH2 mutation presenting QTc interval <470msec and 65 patients
      without KCNQ1 or KCNH2 mutation presenting QTc interval <470msec will be included.

      At the end of the inclusions, two experts will examine examens results without knowing in
      which arm (mutated or not) the patient belongs.
