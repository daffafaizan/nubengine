
    
      Study type: observational Estimated enrollment: 40 participants Observational model: cohort
      Time perspective: prospective

      Inclusion Criteria:

      Assigned for exercise and education due to knee osteoarthrosis Age 18 years or older at the
      time of recruitment Able to read and understand Danish

      Exclusion Criteria:

      Not mentally able to reply to the questionnaire

      Patients are tested with and without the brace before starting the exercise therapy and
      education program and re-tested with and without the brace after completing the treatment
      program (6 weeks) and again at 12 weeks.
