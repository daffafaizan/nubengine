
    
      Fifty patients underwent total knee replacement were included in the study. The patients (11
      male and 39 female) were classified into two equal groups:- The patients received 15 mL plain
      bupivacaine + 2 mL (BS), or 8 mg dexamethasone (BD). The total volume injected in both groups
      was 17 ml.

      Postoperative records:

        1. Pain score (NRS) at base line, then every 4 hrs for 24 hrs.

        2. Frequency of rescue analgesia.

        3. Postoperative side effects like nausea, vomiting, and over sedation.
