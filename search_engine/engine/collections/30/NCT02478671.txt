
    
      In this study, the diameter of each subjects' radial artery will be measured using MRI
      imagery at varying degrees of radial artery compression by adjusting the amount of air
      injected into a Terumo TR Band. In addition, using pulse oxymetry, the perfusion waveform
      will be measured and recorded also while the subjects have a TR Band in place on their
      wrists.
