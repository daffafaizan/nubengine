
    
      OUTLINE: Patients are randomized to 1 of 2 arms.

      ARM I: Patients receive the Quit2Heal app and are encouraged to use it frequently. During the
      entire 12-month follow-up period, the app will remain fully available anytime study
      participants wish to use it.

      ARM II: Patients receive the QuitGuide app and are encouraged to use it frequently. During
      the entire 12-month follow-up period, the app will remain fully available anytime study
      participants wish to use it.

      After randomization, patients are followed up at 3, 6, and 12 months.
