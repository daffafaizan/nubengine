
    
      Methodology:

        -  Education and alerting physicians to the early detection of cancers of oral cavity and
           coordination of networks involved for 1 year.

        -  Incentive period for the screening by the tobacconist who distributes with every package
           of cigarettes bought a flyer of invitation to the screening.

        -  Carrying out screening examinations by general practitioners with completion of the
           customized examination forms, orientation towards a specialist, data collection and
           evaluation.
