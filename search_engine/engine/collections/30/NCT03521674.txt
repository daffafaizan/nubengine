
    
      Women will be randomised into two groups. Foley catheter was applied to one group and double
      balloon catheter to the other group. After catheter expulsion or if the catheters did not
      spontaneously expulsed after 24 hours, i.v. oxytocin infusion will be started. Abortions will
      be considered successful if there will be no need of another method for abortion.
