
    
      Retrospectively study reviewing 34 patients operated with dual mobility cups in total hip
      arthroplasty between January 2009 and June 2012 at Sundsvall Hospital.

      Indications for surgery is recurrent dislocation or patients at risk for dislocation after
      total hip arthroplasty suffering osteoarthritis or femoral neck fracture.

      Patients are followed recording complications, reoperations, functional outcome (Harris hip
      score and EQ5D) in june 2012.
