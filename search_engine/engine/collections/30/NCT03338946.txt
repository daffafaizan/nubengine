
    
      Subjects with CIEDs undergoing evaluation for TIA/stroke, HF or syncope will be reviewed for
      inclusion/exclusion criteria. CIED interrogation, demographics and time of interrogation will
      be collected. Review of interrogation and medical record will be done to evaluate for
      actionable events including CIED programming, initiation or change in medications,
      admission/discharge or decision for further testing.
