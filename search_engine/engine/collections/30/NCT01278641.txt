
    
      A controlled randomised trial (CRT). 60 men who fulfil the ACR-criteria for Fibromyalgia or
      Chronic Widespread Pain (Wolfe 1990) participate in the study.

      40 patients (in the city Uddevalla) are randomised into two groups; low intensive temperate
      pool exercise program, and graded strength training program. The study period is 12 weeks.

      20 patients (in the city Alings√•s) make a reference group who is asked to continue their
      normal activities during the study period.

      Primary outcomes are fatigue (using the Multidimensional fatigue inventory and VAS for global
      fatigue)and muscle strength (using Steve Strong, Isobex and the Grippit). Several other
      self-administered instruments was used.
