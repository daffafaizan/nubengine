
    
      .The purpose of this study is to assess the safety and efficacy of brodalumab at two
      different doses compared with placebo in participants with moderate to severe plaque
      psoriasis. A second purpose of this study is to assess the safety and efficacy brodalumab at
      two different doses compared with ustekinumab in participants with moderate to severe plaque
      psoriasis. A third purpose of this study is to assess the safety and efficacy of 4
      maintenance regimens of brodalumab.
