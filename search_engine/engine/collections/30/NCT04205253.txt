
    
      Suspension laryngoscopy (SL) is a surgical procedure for both diagnostic and therapeutic
      purposes. In this procedure, a rigid direct laryngoscope is inserted orally, and the tongue
      and the base of the tongue are compressed.The tongue is an ideal organ for evaluation with
      ultrasonography. This study aimed to detect the occurrence of tongue edema through
      ultrasonography examination due to the rigid direct laryngoscope in SL procedures.
