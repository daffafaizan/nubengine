
    
      Thirty diabetic patients (17 male, 13female) with foot burn injury, with their age ranged
      from 40 to 55 years. They will randomly assigned into two equal groups. Group (A) will
      receive low vibrational training in the form of whole body vibration for 8 weeks (n=15),
      while group (B) will receive Yoga exercises for 8 weeks (n=15) in addition both groups will
      receive their routine medical care and physical therapy rehabilitation including balancing
      exercises. Dynamic balance will be evaluated through Biodex balance system (BBS);and balance
      confidence will be measured by the Activities-specific Balance Confidence Scale (ABC) before
      the starting and after the end of treatment for all patients in both groups
