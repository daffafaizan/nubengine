
    
      Patients with indication for endarterectomy will be presented the present protocol. If
      inclusion criteria are fulfilled, patients will have a 18F-FDG PET/CT + carotid US performed
      6 to 2 days before endarterectomy and 68Ga-NODAGA-RGD PET/CT + MRI performed the day before
      endarterectomy. Histopathological sample will be analyzed to measure plaque inflammation.

      All imaging results and histopathology results will be pooled and compared.
