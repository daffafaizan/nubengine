
    
      Radiometabolic Therapy (RMT) with 177Lu PSMA 617 in advanced castration resistant prostate
      cancer (CRPC): efficacy and toxicity evaluation. Single-center, prospective, non controlled,
      open label, phase II trial. The main objective of this study is to evaluate the Disease
      Control Rate (DCR) and the safety as co-primary objective.

      The secondary objectives are: late toxicity, PFS, OS, biochemical response and dosimetry.
