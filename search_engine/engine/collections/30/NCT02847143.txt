
    
      Healthy adult male will be recruited. Their attachement style will be assessed through the
      Attachement scale interview (ASI).

      They will have 2 fMRI sessions, during which emotional images will be presented.

      One session will be preceded by a nasal administration of oxytocin and the other session will
      be preceded by a placebo nasal administration. The order of both administrations will be
      randomized.
