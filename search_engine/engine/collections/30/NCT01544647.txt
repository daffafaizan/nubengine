
    
      This is a monocentric randomised non inferiority trial comparing 2 spa therapy protocols in
      symptomatic knee OA.

      In the first group, 4 treatments (massages, showers, mud and pool sessions) are provided 6
      days a week during 3 weeks. In the second group, the same 4 treatments are provided 3 days a
      week during 3 weeks then patients will follow an exercise program 3 days a week during 3
      week.

      Data will be collected at inclusion, 3 and 6 weeks and 3 and 6 months.
