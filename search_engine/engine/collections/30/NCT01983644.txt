
    
      RECO is a novel, self-expanding stent retriever, designed to yield rapid flow restoration in
      acute cerebral ischaemia. The proposed study is a multicenter, randomized, prospective,
      parallel-group, open-label clinical trial with a blinded outcome, to test the RECO(a novel,
      self-expanding stent retriever), as compared with Solitaire FR stent retriever.
