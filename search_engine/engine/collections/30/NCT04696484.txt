
    
      Participating patients will be asked to complete two online surveys. The first survey, which
      includes the coopeRATE Prompt intervention questions, is completed 1-4 days before their
      upcoming healthcare visit. After completing this survey and before their visit, patients'
      intervention responses are shared with their physician and, if requested, other members of
      their care team. The second patient survey is completed within 24 hours after their
      healthcare visit. Participating clinicians are asked to complete a single online survey
      within one month after completion of patient data collection.
