
    
      In this study, 1000 cases of essential hypertension in changping district, Beijing, who are
      currently taking oral antihypertensive drugs and still have poor blood pressure control (>
      130/80mmHg) are included. The duration of the study is 3+ (30±2) days, during which time the
      changing of antihypertensive drugs are avoided and the types and doses of drugs taken daily
      are recorded. Baseline blood pressure and heart rate are measured 2 times a day for 3 days.
      And then the blood pressure and heart rate are measured before and after remote ischemic
      conditioning for 200mmHg, twice a day, 30±2 days in a row to evaluate the efficacy and safety
      of remote ischemic conditioning on essential hypertension.
