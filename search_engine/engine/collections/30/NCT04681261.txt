
    
      During the Covid-19 pandemic, the occupational balance of individuals change and the
      sedentary activities preferences, that pose a risk for health, increase.The outbreak also
      produced effects such as fear of sickness and death in addition to changing daily life and
      work routines, occupational balance and social participation. This study was planned to
      investigate occupational balance, fear of Covid-19 and agoraphobia in adults during the
      Covid-19 pandemic.
