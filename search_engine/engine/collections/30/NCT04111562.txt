
    
      Cranioplasty is surgical repair of a cranial bone defect, important to cover defects
      following skull trauma for restoration of function, cosmoses and protective effects.
      Cranioplasty is the surgical procedure in which the autologous skull, synthetic materials
      (titanium, methyl methacrylate, polyetheretherketone implants, hydroxyapatite, and
      bioceramics are used to repair the skull defect
