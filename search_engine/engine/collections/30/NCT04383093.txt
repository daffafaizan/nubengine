
    
      75 Consecutive men presenting with ED and LUTS suggestive of bladder prostatic obstruction
      were enrolled. Patients were divided into two groups according to MetS presence or absence.
      All subjects were then treated with tadalafil 5 mg/die plus tamsulosin 0.4 mg/die for 12
      weeks. Patients were re-evaluated after 12 weeks of treatment with Uroflowmetry and PVR,
      IPSS, IPSS QoL, OAB-q and IIEF-5 and comparison were made in and between groups.
