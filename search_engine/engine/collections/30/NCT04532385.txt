
    
      A 12 week randomized double-blind, placebo-controlled trial was conducted in patients with
      T2DM to evaluate the effect of green tea extract (sunphenon 90D, TAIYO international,
      Minneapolis, US) at a 400 mg/12 hrs dose or calcined magnesia (400 mg/12 hrs) on lipids,
      anthropometric variables, arterial stiffness and inflammatory cytokines.
