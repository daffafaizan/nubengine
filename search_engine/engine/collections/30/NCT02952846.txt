
    
      Patients < 18 years of age treated in an intensive care unit for 5 days or more with infusion
      of opioids and/or benzodiazepines, are eligible for inclusion. Patients are included at the
      time tapering of these drugs is initiated. Part one of the study is observational. Drugs
      administered, complications, Comfort score and WAT-1 (Withdrawal Assessment Tool-1) score is
      recorded. Before part two of the study, an algorithm for tapering of analgosedation is
      implemented, and the data set is obtained as in part one.

      For part three of the study, focus group interviews will be used.
