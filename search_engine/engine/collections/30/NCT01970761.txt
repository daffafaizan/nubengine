
    
      Methods: From February 2008 and June 2013, a total of 36 patients received autologous fat
      grafting in scar areas. Patients who Douleur Neuropathique 4 Questions (scores >4/10) were
      enrolled this study. The Visual Analogue Scale (VAS), and Neuropathic Pain Symptom Inventory
      (NPSI) were used to evaluate pain preoperatively and postoperatively after 1 week, 4 weeks,
      and 24 weeks. The mechanism of trauma, duration of allodynia, fat graft volume, and total
      follow-up time were recorded.
