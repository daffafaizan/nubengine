
    
      Primary objective: To evaluate a strategy of Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)
      management to reduce the number of hospital days in patients with severe Gold III/IV COPD: a
      Home-Based COPD Management Program versus usual patient education and follow-up.

      Secondary objectives:

        -  To assess the clinical outcome and the health-related quality of life,

        -  To assess safety,

        -  To evaluate medico-economic impact.
