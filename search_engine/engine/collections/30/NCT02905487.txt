
    
      ADIPOGEST is a cross sectional study in which mothers are divided into 2 groups : exposed and
      unexposed to GDM, matched on pregestational BMI, date of delivery and child gender.

      Mothers are recruited in the maternity ward of the University Hospital in South of Reunion
      Island.

      It is planned a conveniance sample of 20 mother-child pairs per group.
