
    
      This comparative study was carried out in 28 PWS children and 18 controls. The enrolled PWS
      children were volunteers out of a pool of approximately 100 PWS children followed at Samsung
      Medical Center [median age 8.0 years, interquartile range (7.0-10.5 years), BMI: median 24.2
      kg/m2 (23.0-27.5 kg/m2)] and 18 obese healthy children [median age 9.0 years interquartile
      range (7.0-11.0 years), BMI: median 24.2 kg/m2 interquartile range (23.1-27.6 kg/m2)].
