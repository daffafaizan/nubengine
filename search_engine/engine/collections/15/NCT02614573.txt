
    
      The secondary objectives of this study are to:

      A. Estimate the intra-patient variability of capillary INR measures.

      B. Estimate the time spent in the target INR (TTR) window of dependent elderly patients in
      nursing homes, under AVK treatment, and with a capillary INR monitoring strategy.

      C. Estimate the number of venous thromboembolic events and bleeding events over the 6-month
      observation period.
