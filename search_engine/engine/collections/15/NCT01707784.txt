
    
      Hypotheses in this study, 3 primary hypotheses will be tested.

        1. Women with gestational diabetes have more life events compared to pregnant women without
           gestational diabetes.

        2. Women with gestational diabetes have higher perceived level of stress and anxiety
           compared to pregnant women without gestational diabetes.

        3. Women with gestational diabetes have higher measures of cortisol levels in the saliva
           and in the nailfolds and higher plasma copeptin levels compared to pregnant women
           without gestational diabetes.

      Secondary objectives:

      1. To test the link between these stress measures and the fasting and stimulated glucose
      levels during the oral glucose tolerance test.
