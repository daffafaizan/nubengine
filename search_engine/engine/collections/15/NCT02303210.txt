
    
      This is a controlled, single blinded and randomised comparative study which is conducted
      monocentric at Hoerzentrum Oldenburg, Germany. The focus is to test two frequency lowering
      techniques with an amount of 30 moderate to severe, symmetrically sensorineural hearing
      impaired adult subjects. The subjects, all of them native German speakers, individually
      conduct a number of laboratory standard tests and home trials (questionnaires) during 6
      months including seven appointments of 2 hours.
