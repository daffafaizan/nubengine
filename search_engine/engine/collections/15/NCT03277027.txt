
    
      Monitor the physical activity, health related quality of life, parenting stress according to
      GMFCS level in children with cerebral palsy in Korea.

        1. Physical activity and quality of sleep are monitored by wearing Actigraph for a week.

        2. Health related quality of life is checked by parents withChild Health Questionnaire
           Parent Form 50 (CHQ-PF50).

        3. Parenting stress is monitored with Korean Parenting Stress Index-Short Form (K-PSI-SF)
           by parents.
