
    
      The squat procedure in each condition will be repeated 10 times on a treadmill. The treadmill
      will be used to adjust the degree of decline in a standard manner. There will be a metronome
      to keep the speed of exercise in the same rhythm. All squats will be performed with a
      weighted barbell. The weight of it will be set as in ratio 30% of the participant's body
      weight
