
    
      Clinical randomized controlled double-blind study with healthy subjects to investigate the
      effect of low and high fat of meal on absorption of vitamin D supplement. Participants were
      randomly in two groups according to sex and BMI.

      In the same date, each group of participants received a meal and dietary supplement of
      vitamin D3 (cholecalciferol) after the first blood sample and a urine sample. Others samples
      were collected in the seventh and fourteenth day after intake the supplement.
