
    
      A favorable uterine medium is necessary for embryo implantation, but endometrial receptivity
      can be negatively affected by ovarian stimulation . Study of endometrial receptivity is
      especially limited in the context of IVF cycles .

      Biopsy of the endometrium cannot be considered during the treatment, and this has encouraged
      the development and use of ultrasonography and Doppler-like non-invasive methods to evaluate
      ovarian response to the stimulation and uterine receptivity .
