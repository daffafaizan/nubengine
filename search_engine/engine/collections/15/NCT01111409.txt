
    
      Assuming success of the VFIX anatomical observational study (protocol 300-08-009), in this
      pilot study approximately 20 subjects (excluding the Device Run-In (DRI) subjects) will
      undergo the VFIX procedure to evaluate the safety and effectiveness of the VFIX device in
      maintaining apical support for at least 6 months, in women with symptomatic apical prolapse.
