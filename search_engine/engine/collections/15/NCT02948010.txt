
    
      Existing Adult Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) therapy users will be recruited for
      an overnight polysomnography (PSG) sleep study. The participants will use the investigational
      CPAP device and be connected to a PSG during their session. The device performance during the
      session will be assessed. Outcomes of the clinical investigation include downloadable device
      data reports, device error reports and participants perception questionnaires and PSG data.
