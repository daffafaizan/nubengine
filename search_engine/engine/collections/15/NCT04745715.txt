
    
      The investigators collected the data from from the investigators' center between January 2011
      and October 2020. The study included all non-small cell lung cancer patients with surgically
      excised brain metastasis. The investigators analysis the correlation of gene mutation and the
      disease course. The investigators use Kaplan-Meier regression to analyze the overall
      survival, and the difference between the groups was calculated using log rank test. Cox
      proportional hazards method was used to create the regression model and estimate the hazard
      ratios.
