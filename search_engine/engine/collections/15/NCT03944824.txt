
    
      The primary objective of this study will be to test the feasibility and efficacy of a 3 month
      exercise physiologist consultation program. Specifically, the investigator's aims will be to:

        1. Test the feasibility of a 3-month exercise physiologist led program

        2. Measure the effect of an exercise physiologist collaboration and intradialytic exercise
           on the physical function of HD patients using the 30 second STS test, handgrip strength
           test [required for frailty scale], 8-foot TUG test and patient physical activity based
           off of the Low Physical Activity Questionnaire.
