
    
      Patients will be informed about the study and potential risks. All patients giving written
      informed consent will undergo a screening visit to assess the eligibility criteria.

      Patients who meet the eligibility requirements will perform a pre-treatment visit in order to
      assess their healthy state. Also, patients will undergo to the radiographic visit (RX and TX)
      as request by clinical practice.

      All the patients will be treated with arthrotomy mini-open technique with bone allograft at
      the site of the shoulder lesion.

      After surgery all the patients will be followed up to 24 months through clinical and
      radiographic visits.
