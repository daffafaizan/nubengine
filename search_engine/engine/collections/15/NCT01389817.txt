
    
      To determine the effects of short term (3 month) near-infrared light NIR-LED therapy on
      anatomic and functional abnormalities of LHON as assessed by a wide variety of ophthalmologic
      tests, including visual acuity, optical coherence tomography, pattern electroretinography
      (pERG) N95 retinal ganglion cell peak, fERG-PhNR (flash ERG - Photopic Negative Response),
      and fundus photography.
