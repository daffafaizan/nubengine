
    
      Automatic processes refered to implicit attitudes measured through the implicit association
      test (IAT) and single category (SC-IAT). Controlled processes refered to the variables of the
      Theory of Planned Behavior measured by questionnaire. Self regulatory capacities refered to
      the concept of impulsivity measured by questionnaire and executive functions measured through
      the STROOP task and Wisconsin Card Sorting Task.

      Physical activity and sedentary behaviors are measured by questionnaire and accelerometry.
