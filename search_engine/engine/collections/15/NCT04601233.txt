
    
      Volunteers will be treated weekly with Xyosted 75 mg (given subcutaneously) for 3 months
      during which they will have 3 study visits, 6 weeks apart. The Baseline visit will include
      providing a blood sample, completing questionnaires, receiving training on the Xyosted
      auto-injector, and undergoing a rectal exam for participants 55 years and older. Visits 2 and
      3 will also include collecting a blood sample and completing questionnaires. At Visit 3, the
      rectal exam for those age 55 years and older will be repeated.
