
    
      This study divided into 2 groups: daily room disinfection and after-each-case room
      disinfection. Each group was also subdivided into general anesthesia or non-general
      anesthesia gastroscopy, and with or without ventilation consultation room.

      Samples collection include: operation unit air (six-stage sieve percussion air sampler for
      sampling, airflow rate 28.3L/min, sampling time 4 min); workstation's mouse (wiping sterile
      cotton for sampling); endoscopist's gown, control panel buttons, and patient's bed headboard
      (contact plate for sampling).
