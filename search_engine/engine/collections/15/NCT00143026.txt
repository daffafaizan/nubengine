
    
      This study examines the effect of treatment of levodopa/entacapone on quality of life, as
      measured by the Parkinson's Disease-Questionnaire 8 (PDQ-8), in Parkinson's disease patients
      with no or minimal, non-disabling motor fluctuations. Treatment with
      carbidopa/levodopa/entacapone will be compared with treatment with standard formulation
      levodopa/carbidopa.
