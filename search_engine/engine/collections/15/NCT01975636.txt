
    
      A baseline (predose) fecal sample and a baseline (predose) urine sample will be obtained at
      the clinic prior to dosing on Day 1.

      On Day 1, subjects will begin the Treatment Phase. Subjects will be released from the clinic
      after at least 10 days since dosing and as soon as the following conditions are met:

        -  greater than 90% of the administered radioactivity has been recovered, and

        -  less than 1% of the administered radioactive dose is recovered in two consecutive
           24-hour samples each of urine and feces
