
    
      Men with apnea-hypopnea index greater than 30/hour will be included and randomly assigned
      into three groups: sodium-restricted diet, or diuretic, or control. The intervention will
      last one week. The diuretic group will receive combined spironolactone 100mg plus furosemide
      20mg daily. The diet group will receive a regimen with a maximum intake of three grams of
      sodium per day. The control group will receive a placebo pill and will maintain all eating
      habits.
