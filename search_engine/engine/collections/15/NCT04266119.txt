
    
      The study will be a two-group, parallel randomized control trial (RCT), with a pre-test,
      post-test and repeated, control group design.

      he overall aim of the study is to test the effectiveness of the online HOPE intervention in
      enhancing mental health literacy, on depression literacy, psychological well-being and in
      reducing stress among youths aged 18 - 24 living in Singapore.
