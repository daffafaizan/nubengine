
    
      Gastroesophageal variceal bleeding is the most common and life-threatening condition in
      patients with portal hypertension, which are susceptibility to bacterial infection. However,
      the prophylaxis use of antibiotics remained uncertain and lack of high level evidences.

      The purpose of the study is to evaluate the efficacy of Rifaximin in the treatment of
      cirrhotic gastroesophageal variceal bleeding.
