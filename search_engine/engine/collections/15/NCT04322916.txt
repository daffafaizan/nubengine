
    
      Prospective multicenter follow-up study which examines the short- to long-term post-market
      clinical data on the safety and performance of the RM Pressfit vitamys cup. In total 675
      participants in 9 clinics are included in this international multicenter study.

      The primary endpoint of the study is the Harris Hip Score (HHS) 2 years after surgery.

      The following parameters will be collected during the regular clinical and radiological
      follow-up: Harris Hip Score, radiographic evaluation and detection of adverse events.
