
    
      A total of 75 patients with SIS were randomly classified into the following three groups:
      kinesiotaping (KT), exercise (EX) and sham-kinesiotaping (sham-KT). Each group was underwent
      two weeks treatment program. The patients were then evaluated in terms of pain analyzed using
      the visual analog scale (VAS), joint range of motion, Quick-Disabilities of the Arm, Shoulder
      and Hand (Q-DASH) questionnaire before and after treatment. In addition, supraspinatus tendon
      (SsT) thickness and acromiohumeral distance (AHD) parameters were measured using
      ultrasonography (US). All parameters were measured before and after treatment.
