
    
      Effects and side effects in patients undergoing radiotherapy will be collected over the next
      five years (2015-2020). It is planned to include 600 to 650 new patients referred for
      radiotherapy per calender year. It is estimated that 3000 patients will be included at the
      end of the study after five years. Patient Information on diseae and treatment will be
      collected and analyzed. Common statistical algorithms will be performed for outcome analysis.
