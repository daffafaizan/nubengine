
    
      Healthy volunteers are administrated single-dose over the period I and II (Crossover) of
      CKD-4101 1000mg and Mycophenolate mofetil capsule 1000mg.

      Mycophenolate mofetil capsule is performed using a blood sample and conducting some tests
