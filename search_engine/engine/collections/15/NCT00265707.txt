
    
      The study will comprise 85 to 100 subjects with blood pressure values ranging between 90 and
      170mmHg -systolic and 65-95 -diastolic.

        -  Three sets of measurements will be performed on each subject. The measurement with the
           tested device placed between twh determinations with a mercury sphygmomanometer.

        -  Each measurement will be performed simultaneously by two examiners specially trained.

        -  Informed consent will be obtained from all participants.

        -  The results will be evaluated by a standard statistical plan ANSI/AAMI SP-10 2002.
