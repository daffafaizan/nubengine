
    
      This is a Open label, balanced, randomized two-treatment, two-period,two-sequence, crossover
      bioequivalence study with at least 07 days washout period between each drug administration
      under fed conditions.52 (+6 standby) healthy, adult, human subjects were enrolled. A single
      oral dose of 40 mg of pantoprazole delayed release tablet will be administered along with 240
      mL of drinking water within 30 minutes after serving a high fat breakfast.
