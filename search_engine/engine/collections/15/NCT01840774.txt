
    
      This is a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, within subject 2x2 factorial,
      repeated measures study. Research participants will play two versions of the Virtual Reality
      based video game "Snow World" and be given thermal and electrical stimulus. Each of two study
      visits will be identical with the exception of whether or not the participant will receive an
      iv infusion of a low dose pain medication or a saline placebo.
