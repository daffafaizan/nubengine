
    
      Induction of labour was done with oxytocin. An infusion of 30U oxytocin diluted in 500ml
      normal saline was prepared and given through infusion pump at initial rate of 3mU/min.
      Subsequently dose was increased 3mU/min every 45 min till adequate uterine contractions were
      established.

      Epidural analgesia was provided wherever feasible or Injection Morphine 2-5mg was given
      intravenously for pain relief.
