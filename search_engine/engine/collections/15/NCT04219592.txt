
    
      IgG4 levels will be obtained from medical records of 80 SSc patients between the years
      2000-2019. These will be compared to IgG4 levels from sera of 80 healthy donors (from Israel
      blood-bank - MDA). Correlations between IgG4 levels and the following will be made: age, sex,
      disease type (limited or diffused SSc), disease duration, medications.
