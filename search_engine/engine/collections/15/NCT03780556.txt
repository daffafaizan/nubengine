
    
      Prospective, randomized, double blind clinical study including 120 patients with acute renal
      pain admitted in emergency department. They were randomly assigned to one of two groups using
      a computer-generated table. Group L received lornoxicam 8 mg IV plus 0.15 mg.kg-1 ketamine
      and Group P received pethidine 50 mg IV. Parameters were observed at baseline and after 0,
      15, 30, 45 and 1 hour of drug treatment. The efficacy of the drug was measured by observing:
      patient rated pain, time to pain relief, rate of pain recurrence, the need for rescue
      analgesia, adverse events and functional status.
