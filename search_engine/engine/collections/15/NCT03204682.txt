
    
      The first step consists of the inclusion of 12 patients followed by the filling by each
      patient of the RedCap software of five simple numerical scales for 4 weeks . Realization of a
      brain and medullary MRI to allow neuronavigation. Realization of 5 sessions of rTMS in 5 days
      during the first week then evaluation (physical examination, questionnaires) in consultation
      on post-stimulation. Then followed for 28 days in total through self-assessment of pain that
      will be filled daily on REDCap and last consultation on D28 for further evaluation
