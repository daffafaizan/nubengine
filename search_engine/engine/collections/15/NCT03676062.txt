
    
      44 patients participating in the study were divided three groups. The patients in the
      stabilization exercise group were instructed to perform hotpack, Trans-cutaneous Electric
      Nerve Stimulation (TENS) and spinal stabilization exercises, the patients in the yoga group
      were instructed to perform yoga program consisting of breathing, relaxation and flexibility
      exercises. The patients in the home exercise group were instructed to perform stabilization
      exercise. The severity of the pain evaluated through visual analog scale, functional status
      and quality of life evaluated through Oswestry Disability Index(ODI), Nottingham Health
      Profile (NHP), respectively. Assessments were repeated before and after the treatment.
