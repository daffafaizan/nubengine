
    
      Infants will be followed up at age 12 months, 3 years and five years. At each visit the
      following will be assessed:

      Age 12 months

        -  Speech development (canonical babbling)

        -  Audiological assessments

        -  Growth

      Age 3 years

        -  Speech development

        -  Audiological assessments

      Age 5 Years

        -  Speech development

        -  Audiological assessments

        -  Dentofacial development (Soft Tissue ANB and Maxillary arch constriction score using
           modified Huddart Bodenham scoring)
