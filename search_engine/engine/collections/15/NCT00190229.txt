
    
      The protocol test the role of 2 modalities of treatment (steroid vs. steroid and
      cyclophosphamide) for severe systemic form of Henoch-Schonlein purpura. The severe forms
      include extracapillary glomerulonephritis, myocarditis, pneumonitis, occult involvement,
      severe gastro-intestinal bleeding. No other randomized trial has been performed in adult
      patient for this indication.
