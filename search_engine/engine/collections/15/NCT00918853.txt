
    
      Primary objective:

        -  Evaluation of the prognosis role of the invasion of the margins on the overall survival
           after a standard resection of adenocarcinoma of the head of the pancreas.

      Secondary objectives:

        -  Evaluation of the prognosis role of the invasion:

             -  Of margins on the overall survival.

             -  Of each margins (distal pancreatic margin, gastric, posterior, retro-venous,
                retro-arterial) on the overall survival.

             -  Of the ganglionic group of the superior mesenteric artery and the other nodes.
