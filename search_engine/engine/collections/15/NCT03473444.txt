
    
      Informed consent will be obtained from all patients and their attendant coming to clinics.
      Those who will agree to be part of this study were asked a questionnaire about co-morbidity
      and symptoms.

      Uric acid levels will be assessed by using UASure Blood Uric Acid Monitoring System. Male
      with uric acid levels greater than 7 mg/dl and female greater than 6 mg/dl were classified as
      hyperuricemia.
