
    
      This study consist of two steps. Step 1: determination of the SPF of the test products which
      will be performed as a single-center, randomized, evaluator blind, intra-individual
      comparison, no treatment and positive controlled clinical study as per ISO24444:2010. And
      Step 2: determination of the UVAPF of the test products which will be performed as a single
      center, open-label, negative and positive controlled technical test as per ISO24443:2012
      (using test plates for assessing UV transmittance of four developmental sunscreens).
