
    
      This is a multicentre, cross-sectional, observational study, aiming to investigate the
      prevalence and control situation of dyslipidimia in China post ischemic stroke patients
      within 6-12 months from attack. Patients will be screened consecutively, eligible subjects
      will be interviewed by investigator and finish a questionire during the visit. Investigator
      will collect relevant medical history, physical exam results and lab test results including
      blood lipid (i.e. Triglyceride, Total Cholesterol, LDL-C, HDL-C) and glucose(HbA1c if
      available).Approximately 5000 post ischemic stroke patients within 6-12 months from attack
      will enter into the study. Fifty centres from different regions of China will participate in
      this study.
