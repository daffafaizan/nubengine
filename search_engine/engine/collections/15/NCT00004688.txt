
    
      PROTOCOL OUTLINE:

      Patients receive mercaptopurine IV on days 1-3, carmustine by continuous infusion on days
      3-5, and streptozocin IV over 20 minutes on days 2-5. Patients receive treatment every 42
      days, for up to 4 courses.
