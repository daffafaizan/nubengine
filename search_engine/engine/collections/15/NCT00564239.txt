
    
      Internalizing disorders represent a large group of psychological disorders among children who
      are referred to psychological treatment. Anxiety, Depression and somatoform disorders in
      children and adolescents increase the risk of mental disorders in adulthood. Some english
      programs have proved the efficacy of CBT-G prevention.This study will test the efficacy of
      CBT-G in German children. The role of parental involvement is currently subject to scientific
      discussions. The impact of an additional parent-group training will be investigated.
