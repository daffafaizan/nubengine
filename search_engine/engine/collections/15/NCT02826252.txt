
    
      The VENTASWITCH study will be a local, prospective and retrospective, observational,
      case-crossover study. Data are collected and downloaded from the I-Neb AAD (Adaptive Aerosol
      Delivery) System. Two data collection periods are planned: period one; data collection from
      the last 3 months using Ventavis (Iloprost) 10 μg/mL (V10) before the index date of switching
      to Ventavis (Iloprost) 20 μg/mL (V20) (retrospective part) and period two; data collection
      for 3 months using V20 from index date (prospective part).
