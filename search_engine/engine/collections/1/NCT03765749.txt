
    
      Prospective cohort study is conducted for monitoring outcomes and risk factors among
      community-onset bacteremia caused by third generation cephalosporin resistant
      enterobacteriaceae.

      Primary endpoint is mortality rate among those who received initially inappropriate
      antimicrobial which switch to appropriate antimicrobial later when culture and antimicrobial
      susceptibility test reported among community-onset bacteremia caused by third generation
      cephalosporin resistant enterobacteriaceae.
