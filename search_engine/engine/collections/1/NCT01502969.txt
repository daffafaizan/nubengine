
    
      The investigators evaluate the vaccine schedule consisting of 2 doses of Rotavin-M1,
      10e6.3ffu/dose at 2 month interval in children in two study sites, Thanh Son- Phu Tho and
      Thai Binh city-Thai Binh provinces.

      This vaccine dose and schedule was chosen based on results from the previous dose-escalating
      study (NCT01377571).
