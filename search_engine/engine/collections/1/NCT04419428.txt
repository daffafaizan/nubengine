
    
      Prospective observational multicenter registry study for chronic obstructive pulmonary
      disease patients who require noninvasive ventilation in the home setting as part of standard
      medical care. The registry includes one in person screening or baseline visit, monthly
      follow-up phone calls (monitoring for medication changes, unscheduled visits and adverse
      events) and device data downloads, and a final in person study visit.
