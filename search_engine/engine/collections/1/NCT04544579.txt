
    
      This study was designed combination of retrospective and prospective methods, and focused on
      distal false lumen Aneurysmal Expansion of Aortic Dissection (DAEAD) . To formulate clinical
      data for the risk assessment of DAEAD, and best intracavitary treatment or long-term efficacy
      evaluation, we combined the results of genetic testing, evaluating the missense mutation,
      computer simulation of blood flow dynamics analysis, and clinical imaging analysis.
