
    
      This study is a multi-centered, daily wear, monthly replacement, bilateral, randomized in a
      ratio of two to one (2:1) Test to Control, lens blocked (subjects remain in the same lens
      brand throughout the study), licensed investigator and subject masked, concurrent controlled
      study with a study duration of approximately three months evaluating performance equivalence
      of Invigor A (test) over Invigor B (control) contact lenses.
