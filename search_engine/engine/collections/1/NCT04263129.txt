
    
      In the context of end-stage tracheobronchial disease (in particular tracheal or carinal
      indication) this technic propose a therapeutic alternative, in the context of bronchial
      replacement it allows to avoid pneumonectomy.

      The advantages are to reduce mortality at 90 days, functional consequences and long-term
      complications.

      In this observationnel study, data were collected prospectively in the medical records of all
      patients who have undergone a trachea or artificial bronchus transplant.
