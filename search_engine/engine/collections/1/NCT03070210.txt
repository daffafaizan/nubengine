
    
      Inclusion criteria include: age>18 yrs, ability for informed consent, chronic daily
      pancreatic-type abdominal pain.

      Exclusion criteria include: pregnancy, malignancy, recent acute pancreatitis (within 2
      months), elevated INR (>1.5) or low platelet count (<75 cells/mm3), allergy to eggs or
      "caine" anesthetics.
