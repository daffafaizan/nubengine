      treatment to slow the spread of the disease and relieve some symptoms of cancer.

      Palbociclib is a new type of drug for prostate cancer. Laboratory tests show that it may help
      slow the growth of prostate cancer. Palbociclib has been shown to help patients with breast
      cancer but it is not known if the drug is useful for treating prostate cancer.
