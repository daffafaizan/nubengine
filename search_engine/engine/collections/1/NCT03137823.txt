
    
      This study will examine 2 groups. first group of 50 children aged 3-18 years and the second
      group of adults aged 18-65 with a clinical diagnosis of tonsillitis and centor criteria of 2
      or more (fever, exudates on tonsills, no cough, enlarged lymph nodes).

      In each patient we'll take 2 cultures - the first from the tonsills and the second from the
      bucal surface. both of them will be sent to MEGA-LAB .

      the treatment will be based only on the gold standrad culture from the tonsills.
