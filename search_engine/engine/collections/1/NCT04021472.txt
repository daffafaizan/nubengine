      The investigators are preparing to conduct a one-group pilot trial evaluating Veterans who
      receive automated text messaging. Completion of this pilot trial will provide preliminary
      insights regarding efficacy and feasibility of a text messaging program on self-efficacy,
      goal commitment, and health confidence. It will also provide data on how best to incorporate
      health coaching into the adoption and use of connected care technologies for health behavior
      and behavior change.
