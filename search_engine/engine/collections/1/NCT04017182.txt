
    
      All patients age between 18-65 year old who undergoing surgery under general anesthesia and
      superior vena cava central venous catheter installation anticipated are eligible. The tips of
      superior vena cava central venous catheter was confirmed by Chest x-ray. Next 1 hour after
      skin incision, 1 ml of venous blood sample is taken from superior vena cava central venous
      catheter of right external jugular vein then send to central laboratory for venous blood gas
      analysis. The demographic data will be recorded. Correlation between superior vena cava
      central venous blood and right external jugular venous blood oxygen saturation will be
      determined the primary outcome and partial pressure of carbon dioxide will be determined as a
      secondary outcome.
