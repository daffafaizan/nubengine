
    
      This study is to describe the long-term (3 to 5 years after the acute coronary syndrome index
      event) antithrombotic management patterns in a real-life setting for patients hospitalized
      with an acute coronary syndrome (i.e. ST-segment Elevation Myocardial Infarction,
      Non-ST-segment Elevation Myocardial Infarction, Unstable Angina) in China.
