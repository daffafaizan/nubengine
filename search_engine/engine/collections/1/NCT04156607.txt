
    
      Balance is one of the most problematic issues in children with DS. Mechanoreceptors of
      plantar region of the foot helps to regulate human erect posture and balance. Studies shows
      that inputs from foot sole give a positive effect in improving balance both in healthy and
      neurologically demaged subjects. Kinesio tape is a supportive method in physiotherapy with
      it's proprioceptive input giving effects. It is aimed to show the acute effects of Kinesio
      tape on balance parameters in children with DS.
