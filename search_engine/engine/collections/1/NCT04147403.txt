
    
      The objectives of the study are as follows:

        1. To evaluate the perioperative safety of extended total mesopancreas excision(eTME) for
           patients who diagnosed with resectable pancreatic head adenocarcinoma.

        2. To demonstrate the prognosis of extended total mesopancreas excision(eTME) for patients
           who diagnosed with resectable pancreatic head adenocarcinoma.
