
    
      Validation of the Italian Bt-DUX:

      Cross sectional study among patients of the Istituto Ortopedico Rizzoli, Bologna, Italy, will
      carry out. All patients who underwent a surgical intervention due to a malignant bone tumour
      in the leg will be identified through hospital records and a survey consisting of different
      QoL questionnaires will be executed:

      Bt-DUX, EORTC QLQ C-30, The Toronto Extremity Salvage Score (TESS).
