
    
      All patients included will be assigned into two different groups according to the surgical
      approach,one group is named "NP" with normal perfusion,and the other is named "RP" with
      retrograde perfusion.

      For all the patients,we will obtain 0.5ml blood from both peripheral arterial and aortic root
      for blood gas analysis at some specific time points.

      We will focus on the differences of arterial oxygenation between peripheral arterial and
      aortic root at each group.At the same time,we will analyse the impact of artificial
      ventilation on it.
