
    
      This randomized, crossover study requires participants to arrive to the lab 12 hours fasted,
      consume an oral liquid supplement and complete gastrointestinal tolerance questionnaires as
      well as produce samples for breath hydrogen and methane analysis at various time points over
      a 4 hour time period. Subjects will also be asked to rate gastrointestinal symptoms via a
      questionnaire at various time points throughout the study.
