
    
      100 investigation centers in metropolitan France will participate in the study, targeting
      2000 included patients (20 surgical interventions in each center). The duration of inclusion
      in each center should last at most 2 weeks.

      The selected surgical interventions will be those performed with a high frequency, practiced
      in all types of hospitals (public, private, university hospital or not), and requiring
      anesthesia of at leat 30 minutes (general, epidural or intrathecal anesthesia).
