
    
      HIV-uninfected & HIV-infected participants who enroll on this study will be asked to provide
      blood samples for 18 months. These samples will be used to assess T-cell responses and
      presence of herpesvirus(es).

      Primary Objective

      Characterize phenotypic and functional features, including TCR repertoires of HIV-specific
      CD8 T-cell responses and exhaustion in HIV-positive humans with and without concomitant
      herpesvirus infections.
