
    
      Inclusion criteria

      Inclusion criteria of healthy older adults : Age > 60, without dysphagia, comorbidity

      Inclusion criteria of young control group

      : Age < 60, without dysphagia, comorbidity

      No prior history of swallowing difficulty or history of central nervous system disease, and
      none were taking medication that are known to affect swallowing.

      Surface EMG was performed at bilateral suprahyoid, thyrohyoid, sternothyroid and pharyngeal
      constrictors. The latency of activation and termination was checked.
