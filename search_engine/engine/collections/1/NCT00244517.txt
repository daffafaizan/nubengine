
    
      In part I, 60 healthy young subjects free from cardiovascular disease will be examined. In
      part II, 50 patients with impaired diastolic but preserved systolic function, i.e., patients
      with arterial hypertension and ejection fraction > 50%. Doppler echocardiography will be
      performed at baseline before induction of anaesthesia, during anaesthesia in the
      spontaneously breathing patient, and in the mechanically ventilated patient.
