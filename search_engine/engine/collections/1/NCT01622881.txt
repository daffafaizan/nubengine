
    
      Drug combination are frequently used to relieve postoperative pain. Nefopam can inhibit
      serotonin, dopamine, and norepinephrine reuptake through central mechanisms. Several studies
      have demonstrated analgesic efficacy of nefopam in the postoperative period. The purpose of
      this study is to ascertain the analgesic effect and tolerance of intravenous nefopam in
      combination with fentanyl based patient-controlled analgesia after kidney transplantation.
