
    
      The main objective of this study is to evaluate subject satisfaction with the treatment
      regimen comprising Benzaknen® 5% Gel in association with 2 cosmetic products including a foam
      soap, Dermotivin® Soft Liquid soap, and a non comedogenic moisturizer, Cetaphil® Dermacontrol
      Moisturizer SPF30, in patients with mild to moderate acne, after 12 weeks of treatment.
