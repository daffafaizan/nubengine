
    
      This study is a randomized controlled trial in infants less than 35 weeks gestation admitted
      to the Newborn Intensive Care Unit at Children Hospital in Saltillo Coahuila Mexico.

      Infants receive at 24 hours of life; sodium (5mEq/kg/day) versus less than 1mEq/kg/day.
      Weight, serum and urine sodium, serum chloride, serum and urine creatinine, serum chloride,
      bicarbonate and glucose are monitored daily during the first 3 days of life. Patients are
      assessed for hyponatremia, hypernatremia, weight change, sepsis, necrotizing enterocolitis
      and intraventricular hemorrhage.
