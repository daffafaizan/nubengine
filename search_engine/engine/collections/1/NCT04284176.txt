
    
      Two protocols are designed to be carried out at home. The AOT protocol includes 15 set of
      daily life upper limb uni/bimanual activities (8 sets for bimanual activities and 7 sets for
      unimanual activities).

      The MTAO group include 6 mirror therapy activities and the same activities that are included
      in the AOT protocol.

      A pilot study will be conducted for sample size calculation, assessing the AHA and
      Jebsen-Taylor scales, before and immediately after the intervention.
