
    
      To study the effect of twice daily dual bronchodilation (Aclidinium Bromide/Formoterol
      Fumarate 340/12 mcg) versus once daily single bronchodilation (Tiotropium 'respimat' 5 mcg)
      in patients with COPD on 24-hour static and dynamic hyperinflation, spirometry respiratory
      symptoms and sleep quality.
