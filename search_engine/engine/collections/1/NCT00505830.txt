
    
      4 groups of participants will be screened with the questionnaires: Group 1: 50 adolescents
      with AS or high functioning autism (HFS) diagnosed by psychiatrists using established
      criteria and with an IQ >85.

      Group 2: 50 adolescents with AS or classical autism diagnosed by psychiatrists using
      established criteria 70<IQ<84.

      Group 3: 50 adolescents with psychiatric disorders. Group 4: Sample of 50 healthy adolescents
      selected randomly from 200. The questionnaires are given to the parents of adolescents during
      the first meeting. Parents are invited to send them back to the clinical investigation centre
      of Lyon (France).

      Multicentre study: 8 clinical centres and 18 paediatricians
