
    
      Study sample: 64 children, 8 to 14 years old, with diagnosis of generalized or separation
      anxiety.

      Training: 32 of these children will undergo balance training and the other 32 children will
      undergo motor training of power and flexibility. Training will last 7 weeks, twice per week,
      1 hr each session.

      Tests: Balance and anxiety tests will be applied before training period, immediately after
      the last training session and again 2 months after the last training session.

      Tests will include standard balance performance tests and questionnaires of anxiety.
