
    
      Purpose: In this study, authors describe a new surgical technique for the treatment of
      refractory DME. The technique consists of vitrectomy with ILM peeling with subretinal
      injection of Ranibizumab.

      Methods: This is a prospective interventional non-comparative study including patients with
      refractory DME. Included patients were subjected to the new surgical technique of pars plana
      vitrectomy with sub-retinal injection of Ranibizumab.
