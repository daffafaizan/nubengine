
    
      Clinically stable, premature babies (birth weight between 500 and 1,250g) will be randomized
      to receive metoclopramide (0.1 mg/kg IV q8h)or an equal volume of placebo for 7 days.A
      transition feeding schedule will begin within the first 72 hours of life. The time to full
      feeds for the 2 groups will be analyzed.
