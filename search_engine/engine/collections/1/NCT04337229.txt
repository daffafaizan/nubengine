
    
      This study; It will be carried out with the aim of developing the artificial intelligence
      method, which allows automatic determination of comfort levels of newborns. Facial and body
      movements of newborns will be recorded by camera, and images will be processed in computer
      environment by using artificial intelligence techniques. As a result, it is planned to create
      a technology that determines the comfort level of the newborn quickly and simply and can be
      used by the mobile device.
