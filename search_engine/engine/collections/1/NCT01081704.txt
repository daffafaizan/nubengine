
    
      This is a randomized (study medication assigned by chance), open-label (both the physician
      and subject know the assigned study medication) study to assess the safety, tolerability,
      immune response, pharmacokinetics (what the body does to the drug), of ustekinumab (Stelara).
      The study population will consist of 24 healthy male participants in China. Participants will
      receive a single dose of either 45mg or 90 mg ustekinumab. Participants will be in the study
      for up to 16 weeks. Safety assessments will be performed throughout the study and include
      obtaining and evaluating laboratory tests, vital signs (e.g., blood pressure), and the
      occurrence and severity of adverse events. A single dose of 45 mg or 90 mg ustekinumab.
