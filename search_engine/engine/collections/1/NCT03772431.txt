
    
      Using random digit dialing (RDD) sampling techniques, participants were randomized to one of
      four arms: 1) male narrator and informational survey introduction, 2) male narrator and
      motivational survey introduction, 3) female narrator and informational survey introduction,
      and 4) female narrator and motivational survey introduction,male motivational, female
      informational, female motivational) which were then followed by a noncommunicable disease
      risk factor survey. This mobile phone survey was sent as an interactive voice response (IVR).
      In IVR surveys, participants use their touch tone key pad to answer pre-recorded questions.
      (i.e. If you are male, press 1; if you are female, press 2). This study was conducted in both
      Bangladesh and Uganda.
