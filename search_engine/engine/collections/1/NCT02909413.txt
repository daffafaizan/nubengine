
    
      The postoperative cognitive dysfunction (POCD) of school-age children (6-14 age) after
      operation draw more attention from both society and family. It is well known that, compare
      with sevoflurane, desflurane could improve cognitive function in elderly patients. But
      whether Desflurane could improve postoperative cognitive function compare with Sevoflurane
      for the school-age children, there is no consistent conclusion.
