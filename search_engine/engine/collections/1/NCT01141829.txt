
    
      DESIGN: CASE-CONTROL STUDY METHODS: We are going to determine the A-930G and C242T p22phox
      genotypes in patients with OSA and healthy subjects recruited from sleep unit of Son Dureta
      University Hospital, (Palma de Mallorca, Spain). 8-Isoprostane is going to be measured as an
      oxidative stress marker with an 8-isoprostane EIA kit (Cayman Chemicals Company, USA) in
      plasma samples.
