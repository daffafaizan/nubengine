
    
      Patients entering the six-week Treatment Period will receive once daily oral dosing of
      arhalofenate during Weeks 1 and 2 (Days 1 through 14), combined once daily oral dosing of
      arhalofenate and febuxostat during Weeks 3 and 4 (Days 15 through 28), and once daily oral
      dosing of febuxostat during Weeks 5 and 6 (Days 29 through 42). In addition, all patients
      will receive once daily oral dosing of colchicine throughout the Treatment Period.
