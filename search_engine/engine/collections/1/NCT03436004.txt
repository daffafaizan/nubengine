
    
      Given the discrepancy in the results of the published studies, the main objective of the
      present study would be to evaluate, through a multicentric design and with a sufficiently
      large sample of patients, if there are relevant differences in the ADR and in the MAP between
      the colonoscopy performed with a device specific (Endocuff visionTM) versus standard
      colonoscopy.

      Secondarily, the number of adenomas, the number of advanced adenomas and the number of
      serrated lesions in both groups.
