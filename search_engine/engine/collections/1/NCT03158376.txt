
    
      Randomized, double blind study enrolling 200 adults undergoing thoracotomy. Patients receive
      either 75 mg hydroxyzine before surgery and placebo 3 times daily for 10 days or 1200 mg
      gabapentin before surgery continued for 10 days. All patients receive also a thoracic
      epidural analgesia.

      Assessment of chronic pain at 3 month with an evaluation by a physician (pain intensity, pain
      quality and analgesia requirement).

      Assessment of acute pain in the early postoperative period (pain intensity, pain quality and
      analgesia requirement).
