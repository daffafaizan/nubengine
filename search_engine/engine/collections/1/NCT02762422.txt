
    
      84 Fe-replete, non-anemic subjects were randomly assigned to one of three experimental serial
      phlebotomy procedures designed to induce Fe loss or RBC loss. Brachial artery reactivity
      (BAR, %) in response to transient oxidative stress induced by oral methionine was measured
      with high-resolution duplex ultrasound imaging before and after study phlebotomy procedures.
