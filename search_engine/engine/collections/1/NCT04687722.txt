
    
      This study is a Randomized Control Trial, which will be conducted in Shifa Tameer-e-Millat
      University (Department of Rehabilitation Sciences). the calculated Sample size is 24
      individuals using Epitool with 95% Confidence Interval (CI) and power 80%. Individual will be
      screened out according to inclusion criteria. Individuals will be allocated randomly into two
      groups, 12 in High Intensity Aerobic training group and 12 in Low Intensity Aerobic Training
      group by sealed envelope method. High Intensity Aerobic Training will be use in experimental
      group. Low Intensity Aerobic Training will be used in Control group. Assessment will be done
      on baseline day 1 prior to the intervention and after 8 weeks of training.
