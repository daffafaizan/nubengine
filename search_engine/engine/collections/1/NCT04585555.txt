
    
      The primary objective of the Accuryn Registry Study is to is to track and analyze changes in
      physiologic data streams using the Accuryn Monitoring System and correlate these changes to
      the occurrence of acute kidney injury (AKI) following cardiovascular surgery intervention(s).

      The secondary objective is to determine the incidence of IAH and ACS in the cardiovascular
      surgery population and the correlation of these diagnoses to the diagnosis of AKI.
