
    
      Women who are informed about the study and provide informed consent will be randomized to one
      of two groups: supplement and placebo. Group assignment will be a 1:1 ratio to supplement
      (455mg of blackcurrant standardized to 50mg anthocyanins in 2 capsules/day) or placebo (2
      capsules/day). Outcomes will be assessed at baseline and again on day 70.
