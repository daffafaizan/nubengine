
    
      Patients submitted to cranioplasty since 2008 to 2019 will be retrospectively analyzed.

      Will be considered for analysis all cases treated after decompressive craniectomy. Will be
      excluded patients with incomplete clinical or imaging records.

      Main factors determined as a possible influence on complications after cranioplasty will be
      recorded focusing on patients characteristics, craniectomy characteristics, surgical timing,
      and cranioplasty materials.

      Patients' records will be review by two investigators and data will be verified by a third
      investigator. records will be anonymously recorded.
