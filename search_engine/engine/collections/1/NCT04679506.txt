
    
      This study is a prospective, open, multiple -centered, sing-arm trial. The major aim of this
      studies to evaluate the efficacy and safety of CLAE regimen (cladribine + cytarabine +
      etoposide) in the treatment of relapsed/refractory T-ALL/LBL. The study will include 50
      subjects to receive CLAE regimen for reinduction chemotherapy.
