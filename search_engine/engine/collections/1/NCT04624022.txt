
    
      Prospective non randomized case series study included 50 eyes. The study is conducted on
      patients attending to the oculoplastic unit of ophthalmology department of Minia university
      hospital. Informed consents were obtained from the participants in this study. The study was
      adherent to the tenets of declaration of Heleniski.

      Anterior segment optical coherence tomography(AS-OCT) was performed for the included patients
      after perforated plug implantation (during the first operative weak)
