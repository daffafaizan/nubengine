
    
      48 patients with primary intestinal ischemia admitted in 4 consecutive years (2006-2009) in
      Department of Gastrointestinal Surgery, Aalborg University Hospital. Control group consists
      of 82 patients operated in the same department and in the same time range.

      Abdominal CT scans are described with respect to intestinal diameter, changes in intestinal
      wall, extra intestinal changes and vessel pathology.
