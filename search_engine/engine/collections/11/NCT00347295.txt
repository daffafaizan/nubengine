
    
      It is intended to recruit 240 insomnia outpatients in this study, 120 patients receiving
      Brotizolam + Estazolam placebo (Test Group) and 120 patients given Estazolam + Brotizolam
      placebo (Control Group).

      Study Hypothesis:

      Brotizolam can be safely used in insomnia patients in China. And Brotizolam 0.25mg is
      non-inferior with Estazolam 1-2mg in treatment of insomnia patients in China
