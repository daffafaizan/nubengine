
    
      Eligible subjects were divided into two randomised controlled trials with the name of "mild"
      or "moderate" low back pain trials (interventions) and also mild vs natural course (NC) and
      moderate vs. NC interventions.

      Pain, disability and quality of life were collected up to 2 years by self-administered
      questionnaires and sickness absences were gathered from electronical records for at least 4
      year's follow up time.
