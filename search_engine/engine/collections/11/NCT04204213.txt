
    
      112 patients with end stage osteoarthritis knee had enrolled into a customised
      multidisciplinary education program that consisted of one hour healthcare education seminars
      followed by another hour of 8 Section Brocade (Baduanjin) sitting Tai Chi classes for 4
      consecutive weeks. Followup measures were taken at baseline, 3 and 6 months post course
      commencement using the International Physical Activity Questionnaire (IPAQ), Western Ontario
      and McMaster Universities Osteoarthritis Index (WOMAC), SF-36v2 Health Survey and the Client
      Satisfaction Questionnaire (CSQ-8).
