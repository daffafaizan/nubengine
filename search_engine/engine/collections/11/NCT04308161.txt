
    
      The study was designed as randomized, controlled, clinical trial. patients who were
      undergoing to receive radiotherapy were divided into three groups: Group I: was given
      conventional treatment.Group II : was given topical oral gel of vitamin D. Group III: was
      given topical oral gel of vitamin D in combination with the conventional treatment.

      All patients were clinically evaluated at the start the radiotherapy, three weeks and six
      weeks later for pain and oral mucositis severity.
