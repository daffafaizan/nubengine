
    
      Surgical patients often have varying degrees of preoperative anxiety.The aim of this study
      was to determine the incidence of preoperative anxiety and related predictors, and to
      evaluate the effect of anxiety on anesthesia and postoperative pain and prognosis. The study
      also showed that prostaglandin, catecholamines, cortex Effects of alcohol and inflammatory
      cell level on postoperative pain in patients with preoperative anxiety.
