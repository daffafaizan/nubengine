
    
      Single-centre, open-label, randomised, two-way cross-over study consisting of 2 periods
      separated by a washout period of 14 days or more. In one period, subjects received three
      single-doses of 1 g paracetamol separated by 6 hours and 1.5 hours after the last paracetamol
      dose a single-dose of 50 mg OPC was administered.In the other period, a single-dose of 50 mg
      OPC was administered alone.
