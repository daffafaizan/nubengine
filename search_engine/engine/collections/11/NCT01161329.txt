
    
      Hife include functional exercises consisting of everyday tasks challenging leg strength,
      postural stability, and gait ability. All exercise shall be performed in weight-bearing
      positions, eg squats and walking over obstacles. HIFE are performed twice a week during 1 h.
      for three months in a group with 6-7 seven patients with stroke. Two physiotherapists lead
      the group and one physiotherapist select exercises for each participant according to their
      functional deficits. The exercises will be progressively increased in load and difficulty.
