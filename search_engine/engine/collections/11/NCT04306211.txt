
    
      124 patients with BMI> or equal to 35kg.m2 or documented Obstructive Sleep Apnea scheduled
      for an EUS with Propofol sedation will be randomized to CPAP vs nasal mask for oxygenation
      during their procedure. Patients will be monitored every 2 minutes during the procedure and
      in the recovery area until discharge. If patients have difficulty or refuse to wear the mask,
      2 or more simultaneous or non-synchronous adverse events, change in heart rhythm or ST
      changes on EKG they will immediately changed to the other group. The ansethesiologist,
      gastroenterologist & patient will complete post-procedure questionnaires regarding their
      opinion of the oxygen delivery system.
