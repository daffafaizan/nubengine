
    
      After a single oral administration of 2 mg tolterodine in 31 healthy subjects (6 with the
      heterozygous mutation of HNF-4A G60D vs 25 subjects with wild type of that whose CYP2D6
      genotype and gender were matched with variant group), blood were collected for 24hrs. Assay
      of tolterodine and the metabolite, 5-hyroxymethyl tolterodine was conducted using LC/MS/MS.
