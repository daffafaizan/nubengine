
    
      TSN2898 is an inhibitor of stearoyl-CoA desaturase-1 (SCD-1), an enzyme present in sebaceous
      glands and a key regulatory enzyme in lipogenesis. This study will assess the safety and
      efficacy of TSN2898 topical gel and matched gel vehicle, applied daily on the face for the
      treatment of moderate to severe acne vulgaris.
