
    
      The main objective is evaluation and validation of social cognition battery, characterizing
      the psychometric properties (validity, sensitivity to change) measures in a cohort of
      schizophrenic patients. Other objective is to demonstrate that the various components
      operating at T0 and T12 months of schizophrenic patients, as an external validator, are
      significantly explained by social cognition performance given the third known factors :
      neuropsychological, sociodemographic, disease course, and clinical status. Search and model
      relationships between the measured variables.
