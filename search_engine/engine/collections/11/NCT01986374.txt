
    
      The study is in pilot phase, uncontrolled. The risks appear to be minimal and there are
      conflicting new reports being announced.

      A small amount of fluid with HCG is injected into the uterus. HCG is a potent signal to the
      uterus for preparation for implantation.

      This is not a funded study. Three published studies suggest the approach is valid; one
      refutes the concept.
