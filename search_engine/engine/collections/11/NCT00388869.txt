
    
      Every patient aged 30-100 referred for abdominal CT examination with IV contrast injection
      will be included in this study (total 300 patients.Prior to the examination relevant clinical
      data including history of hypertension and predisposing conditions for atherosclerotic
      disease, will be investigated. The patient will undergo conventional CT with no special
      procedures. The images will be review by the radiologist for the presence of renal arteries
      calcifications, renal arteries stenosis,plaque location, plaque burden and characterization
      and secondary renal pathology.

      This study will not change the examination technique and is not associated with any
      inconvenience to the patients.
