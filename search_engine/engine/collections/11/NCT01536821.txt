
    
      Participants will be asked to complete a family history questionnaire, which will gather
      information about their family history of Parkinson's disease and related disorders. They
      will be asked to complete a single Study Visit, during which they will be asked to do some or
      all of the following:

        1. Complete questionnaires regarding Parkinson's disease symptoms, medical history, mood,
           sleep, mental status, and activity level.

        2. Be given a brief standard neurological examination.

        3. Be given a scratch and sniff smell identification test.

        4. Be asked to give a sample of approximately 2 tablespoons of blood.

        5. Be asked to give a urine sample of approximately 1 tablespoon of urine.
