
    
      Subjects will be randomized to intervention group and control group. The experiment is
      designed as randomized cross-over control trial. The primary outcome included 8 measures:
      Barthel Index-based Supplementary Scales, Canadian Occupational Performance Measure,
      Fugl-Meyer Assessment, Balance Computerized Adaptive Testing, Mini Mental State
      Examination-2nd edition, Mini Nutritional Assessment, Stroke Impact Scale, and Stroke
      Self-Efficacy Questionnaire.
