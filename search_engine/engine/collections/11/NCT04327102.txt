
    
      First, the Chinese version of hypertension health literacy scale was used to evaluate the
      health literacy of inpatients in a hospital, and then the critical and lack of health
      literacy patients were selected as the research objects.

      Then, taking a place in Huzhou City of Zhejiang Province as the research site, a general
      hospital was randomly selected. According to the ratio of male to female = 1:1, the
      hypertension patients who meet the inclusion criteria were screened until the intervention
      group and the control group reached the required sample size. Patients were randomly assigned
      to the intervention group and the control group.

      After the patients signed the informed consent, the patients were intervened. Finally, data
      collection and statistical processing are carried out
