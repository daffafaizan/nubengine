
    
      In the inpatient pediatric care area (PICU, PCCU, PIMU, ACU), when an x-ray is performed for
      any reason and a patient has a CORTRAK feeding tube, a provider from the research team will
      re-insert the small, individualized, flexible stylet back into the patient's feeding tube and
      document the position of the tube on the CORTRAK display monitor. The x-ray will be obtained
      by current standard of care and the tube location on x-ray will be compared to the tube
      location on the CORTRAK monitor. The x-ray will only be obtained as part of the routine care
      decided by the medical team, the patient will not receive any additional x-rays for this
      study.
