
    
      150 mothers will participate in this study. The study is using 4 questionnaires at infants
      ages of 1 month, 1 year, 2 years and 4 years. The questionnaires are involving communication
      skills,grandparents support, stress feelings, psychological parameters, parity functioning,
      infant temperament, self assessment, maternal and infant medical background and twins and
      brotherhood evaluation.

      a comparison will be done between mothers to preterm twins and mothers to single full term
      infants.
