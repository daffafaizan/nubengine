
    
      This is a double-blinded, placebo-controlled, randomized trial that will recruit patients
      categorized into two study arms. Patients in the gabapentin arm will receive gabapentin 600mg
      taken pre-operatively and 300mg taken three times a day for 3 days, while patients in the
      placebo arm will receive a placebo drug taken pre-operatively three times a day for 3 days.
