
    
      The PBC patients have been enrolled in the study for UDCA treatment more than 1 year, who is
      divided into the UDCA total response Group and the other poor response group according to
      Paris-I/II standard .The feces of two groups of patients were collected and the differences
      of microbial polymorphism of fecal microorganism in two groups were observed. The metabolic
      differences of bile acids and short chain fatty acids in serum and feces of two groups of PBC
      patients were studied by means of metabolic proteomics.
