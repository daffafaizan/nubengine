
    
      Multicenter, epidemiological, prospective, randomized, sequential study. The introduction of
      an educational program and a package of clinical measures (specific monitoring and
      pharmacological reversion) in the incidence of NMB will be evaluated by evaluating variations
      in this incidence of NMB, the rate of compliance with the preventive measures and the
      perception of the Safety in the perioperative, through pre and postoperative survey.
