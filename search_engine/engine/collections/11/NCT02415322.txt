
    
      Participants:

      Total 3,000 children will be enrolled the cohort and followed up for 4 years.

      Procedures:

      These students will be surveyed annually between 2015 and 2019. Surveys were conducted in
      classrooms by trained interviewer who had been trained by the research team assisted by the
      teachers.

      Measurements

        -  usage and frequency of internet, internet game, and smartphone game etc.

        -  interpersional relationship; with family members, peer group, and teacher etc.

        -  personal characteristics; self esteem, social support, depression, anxiety,
           hyperactivity etc.

        -  Socio-demographic factors
