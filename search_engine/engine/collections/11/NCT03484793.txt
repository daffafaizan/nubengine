
    
      This paper focuses on "Big data" in the knowledge base, using "Data minig" study of DM
      (Disease-Medication) and MM (Medication-Medication) of relevance to develop associated
      decision resources system-"the intelligent safety system" (Advanced Electronic Safety of
      Prescriptions,AESOP Model), and test the system in the clinical environment in hospital can
      assist physicians when open orders reduce medication errors, the system is named "AESOP
      Model".
