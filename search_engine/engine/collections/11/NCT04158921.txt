
    
      The objectives of this study are to:

        -  Test the hypothesis that patients with diabetes mellitus treated with insulin and
           elevated blood glucose will achieve better glycemic control after using the
           Control:Diabetes mobile app.

        -  Test the hypothesis that patients with diabetes mellitus treated with insulin and
           frequent hypoglycemia will achieve lower frequency of hypoglycemia after using the
           Control:Diabetes mobile app.
