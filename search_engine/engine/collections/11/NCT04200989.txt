
    
      Eligible subject undergo a baseline blinded oral peanut challenge. Then the peanut ILIT as
      described above. Pretreatment with antihistamines, leukotriene modifier and albuterol before
      each treatment.

      Following the three ILIT peanut injections a repeat blinded oral peanut challenge is
      performed.

      Blood is drawn at each of the 5 study visits for a variety of tests trying to elucidate the
      mechanism(s) of the induction of tolerance to peanut.
