
    
      Adult patients who have a head and neck cancer and are planned to receive chemotherapy and
      radiotherapy will be invited to participate in the study. Patients who are not in very good
      health, have diabetes or kidney failure will not be considered for the study. Also patients
      who can not undergo MRI scan or are allergic to contrast injection given prior to the scan or
      have had any other form of cancer (except skin cancer) will also be excluded from the study.
