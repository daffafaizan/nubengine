
    
      Detailed Description : The study was conducted as an Open label, balanced, randomized,
      two-treatment, two-sequence, two period,single-dose, crossover oral bioequivalence study of
      Ibuprofen and Diphenhydramine citrate 200 mg/38 mg caplets of Dr. Reddy's Laboratories
      Limited, India and Advil@PM, of Wyeth Consumer Healthcare, USA., in normal, healthy, adult,
      human subjects under fasting conditions. A total number of forty (40) subjects were enrolled
      in the study and all the subjects completed both the periods of the study.
