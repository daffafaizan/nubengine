
    
      This is an 8-week program consisting of weekly yoga therapy classes in a public studio. The
      classes are led by a pediatric occupational therapist who is also a certified internationally
      accredited yoga therapist (C-IAYT). The initial and final sessions will include an
      Rheumatologist MD evaluation including a physician global assessment and joint damage
      assessment (JADI) and visual assessment (VAS). A Visual Analog Pain Scale will and Peds
      Quality of Life Questionnaire-Arthritis Module 4.0 will be administered. Each yoga class is
      75 minutes to include a set-sequence of postures including breathing exercises and final
      corpse pose (final relaxation).
