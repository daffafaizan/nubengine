
    
      Proposed is an open label trial of tattoo machine microneedling in ten patients with
      androgenic alopecia. Ten healthy male patients older than 18 years will undergo 6
      microneedling sessions. Over the course of six months, subjects will undergo up to six
      treatment sessions (one session every 30 day). Patients will be evaluated at 8 visits over 8
      months with the first visit for screening purposes.
