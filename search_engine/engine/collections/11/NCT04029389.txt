
    
      Three groups will be formed. After ESWT therapy, if the patients still suffer from pain (> 5
      points, visual analog scale(0-10points), participants will be included in the study. One
      group will receive conversational therapy. Second group will be treated with local
      corticosteroid injection. And third group will be treated with tibial nerve block.
      Before-treatment, after one week, after six weeks and after 3 months evaluation of the
      patients will be performed.
