
    
      The study was approved by Ethics Committee of Indonesia University. It was a single-blind
      randomized clinical trial at Cipto Mangunkusumo Hospital - Jakarta during August to September
      2018. 72 subjects were recruited and randomly assigned into 2 groups. Patients in group B
      were given 0.15% mouthwash with benzydamine hydrochloride prior to procedure while patients
      in group C were given mouthwash with 15 ml of water. Cumulative propofol consumption per
      minute per kg body weight, and incidence of postoperative sore throat were measured for each
      group.
