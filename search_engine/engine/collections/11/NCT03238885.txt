
    
      We propose to develop and validate a radiomics model for individualized pCR evaluation after
      CRT in patients with LARC. We plan to use both pre- and post-CRT MRI data to construct the
      predictive radiomics model for evaluating if LARC patients achieve pCR after CRT. The
      ultimate aim is to select appropriate LARC patients for omission of surgery.
