
    
      Primary objective To analyze with the aid of CGM the blood sugar profiles of children
      receiving dual-wave versus standard bolus for dinner.

      Primary endpoint To compare time in range (TIR) during 2 weeks receiving dual-wave bolus
      versus standard bolus for dinner.

      Trial design This is a 28-day long prospective randomized open-label single-center crossover
      clinical study with a 14-day long run-in phase.

      Trial population A total of 30 subjects with type 1 diabetes.
