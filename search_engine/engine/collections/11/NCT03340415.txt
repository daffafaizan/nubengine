
    
      Introduction:

        -  Rehabilitation plans after hallux valgus/bunion surgery is varied

        -  From a patient's perspective, full weight bearing is paramount for quality of life and
           return to work/study

        -  No high-level study showing the optimal time to resume full weight bearing available

      Hypothesis:

      Early weight bearing (at 2 weeks post-op) does not affect outcome after the Endoscopic Distal
      Soft Tissue Procedure for Hallux Valgus correction

      Trial Design:

        -  Parallel group

        -  Random allocation

        -  Operating Surgeon blind to randomisation

      Study Setting:

        -  General Regional Hospital

        -  Single Centre
