
    
      This study is to follow the infants that were enrolled in Beginnings study which purpose was
      to characterize physical growth, body composition, dietary intake, neurobehavioral
      development, and brain function of infants and children fed mostly breast milk, milk based
      formula, or soy based formula during their first year of life up to age 6. This study will
      call back those children when they turn 14 to compare growth, sexual maturation, and
      development between the three diets.
