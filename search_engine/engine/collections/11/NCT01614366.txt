
    
      To compare the different dose of DM combine with amlodipine in the treatment of mild to
      moderate essential hypertension, and to define the optimized dose of DM in the combination
      therapy.

      The purpose of this study is to compare the antihypertensive efficacy and tolerability of
      different dose of DM combines with amlodipine treatment, further to explore the optimized
      combination dose of DM.
