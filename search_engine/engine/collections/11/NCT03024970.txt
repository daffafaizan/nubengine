
    
      This will be, prospective, multicenter, double-masked, randomized, bilateral, 1 month
      cross-over, dispensing study comparing the test lens against the stenfilcon A control lens
      with a study duration of approximately two months.

      Each subject will be randomized to wear either the test or control as a matched pair first
      and subjects will be randomized based on the order in which the subject is enrolled and
      qualified into the study.
