
    
      This study involves fasted and post-prandial components.

      Fasted Study:

      Participants will provide fasting blood samples pre-and post-intervention [4-week
      administration of supplement or placebo] in Treatment Period I. Participants will again
      provide fasting blood samples pre-and post-intervention [4-week administration of supplement
      or placebo, opposite to Treatment Period I] during Treatment Period II.

      There will be a 4-week washout period during the two Treatment Periods.

      Post-prandial study:

      Participants will also complete a post-prandial study pre-and post-intervention during each
      Treatment Period.
