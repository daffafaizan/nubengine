
    
      After transurethral bladder resection, large-sized foley catheter have been used for drainage
      of urine and residual blood. Because of urinary catheterization, patients usually experiences
      catheter-related bladder discomfort defined as a burning sensation with an urge to void or
      discomfort in the supra-pubic region caused by catheter- related bladder irritation.

      Dexmedetomidine is an alpha-2 antagonist and used as an analgesic agent. The investigators
      try to evaluate The efficacy of dexmedetomidine for the treatment of catheter-related bladder
      discomfort
