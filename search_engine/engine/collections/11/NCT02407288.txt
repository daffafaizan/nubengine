      of Ruffini and colleagues (2009) in which rTMS was used to target the left orbito frontal
      cortex to calculate the number of patients to be recruited. With a risk alpha of 5%, a power
      of 80%, the number of patients to be included was 46 with an anticipated rate of 10% of lost
      to follow-up patients. (calculation was made on the
      http://www.spc.univ-lyon1.fr/mfcalc/NSN/assistant.htm)
