
    
      In the present study, 75 patient, whose ages ranged between 18-30 and have an impacted lower
      third molar which is in vertical or mesiyoangular position and has class I and II ramus
      relationship and class B and C depth, will included. 75 patient will divided randomly into
      three groups as CGF, A-PRF and Control. After third molar surgery CGF and A-PRF will prepared
      and applied to the extraction sockets in CGF and A-PRF groups. nothing will applied to the
      control group. Post operative pain, edema and trismus will measured at 2nd and 7th days.
