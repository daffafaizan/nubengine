
    
      -  We conducted a well-designed study to investigate the effectiveness of PAT on motor
           function, activities of daily living (ADL),and health related quality of life(HRQOL)
           perspectives for children with CP.

        -  A convenience sample of 27 children was recruited for the study from the outpatient
           clinics of the Department of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation of two tertiary
           hospitals: Chang Gung Memorial Hospital and Taipei Veterans General Hospital.

        -  The study was designed as a single blinded, prospective, case control study.

        -  The pediatric aquatic therapy group got more improvementon motor performance measured by
           GMFM-66.
