
    
      Oligohydramnios refers to decrease in amniotic fluid and it is defined as a deepest fluid
      pocket of less than 2 cm or an amniotic fluid index of 5 cm or less. Oligohydramnios
      complicates 0.5% to 8% of pregnancies, and 12 % if postdates When it occurs in first half of
      pregnancy it is associated with birth defects while in second half it causes poor fetal
      growth, malpresentations , FHR decelerations , MAS , Fetal suicidal syndrome and increases
      cesarean section and management and prognosis depend on gestational age
