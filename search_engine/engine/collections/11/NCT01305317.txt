
    
      This study is a randomized controlled trial of statins effect on serum IgG, IgA and IgM level
      on chronic hemodialysis patients in Bandarabbas. The study is carried out in Hormozgan, Iran.
      Seventy four patient were randomized either to receive atorvastatin in addition of usual
      treatment or not. The primary outcomes were serum IgG,IgA and IgM level. The secondary
      outcome was drug side effects.
