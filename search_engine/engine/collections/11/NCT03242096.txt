      coated SeQuent®SCB balloon balloon (sirolimus 4.0 μg/mm²)

      SeQuent®Please balloon or SeQuent®Please NEO balloon (paclitaxel 3.0 μg/mm²)

      Duration of intervention per patient: minutes

      Follow-up per patient: 30 days telephone call; 6 months angiographic + 12 months clinical
