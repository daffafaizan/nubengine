
    
      This study will be a randomized, controlled and partially blinded study with three arms: 1)
      press tack needle acupuncture (ACU) and routine postoperative analgesic care, 2) press tack
      placebo acupressure (SHAM) and routine postoperative analgesic care, and 3) only routine
      postoperative analgesic care (CONTROL).
