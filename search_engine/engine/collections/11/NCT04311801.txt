
    
      Inclusion and exclusion criteria evaluated during the pre-anesthesia visit. After obtaining
      informed consent, patients were referred to OCT examination in the Ophthalmology clinic. The
      examination was performed in the 48 hours before the surgery. Between the fourth and the
      seventh postoperative day, the patients underwent control OCT examination. The results of OCT
      examinations, baseline clinical data and the data on the course of surgery and postoperative
      care were recorded for further analysis.
