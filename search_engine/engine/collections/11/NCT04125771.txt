
    
      Diclofenac is a proven, commonly prescribed nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that
      has analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antipyretic properties, and has been shown to be
      effective in treating a variety of acute and chronic pain and inflammatory conditions.

      . As with all NSAIDs, diclofenac exerts its action via inhibition of prostaglandin synthesis
      by inhibiting cyclooxygenase-1 (COX-1)
