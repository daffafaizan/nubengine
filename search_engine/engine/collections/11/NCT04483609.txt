
    
      The aim of this study is to reveal the Turkish cultural adaptation, validity and reliability
      of the SF-Qualiveen scale. In our study, Sf-Qualiveen and Urogenital Distress Inventory
      (UDI-6) scales will be used. Pre-testing will be done in 10 patients with multiple
      sclerosis.Then, the validity and reliability of the scale will be performed in 80 patients
      with multiple sclerosis.
