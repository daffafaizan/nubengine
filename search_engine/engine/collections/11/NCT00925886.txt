
    
      Patients with Cataract are treated with phacoemulsification of the lens with an implantation
      of an intraocular lens. Besides cataract, almost 25% of these patients have some degree of
      Astigmatism; when it is higher than 1 diopter, it is possible to neutralize it with an
      implantation of a toric intraocular lens.
