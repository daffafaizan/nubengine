
    
      This will be a prospective, randomized interventional study conducted at the National
      University Hospital under University Orthopaedics and Hand surgery Cluster during the period
      of December 2015 to December 2018. Study participants: The study will include 80 patients
      with complete Anterior Cruciate Ligament (ACL) tear undergoing primary arthroscopic
      reconstruction with ipsilateral hamstring autograft after written informed consent to
      participate in the study.

      The subjects will be divided into two groups A) 4-strand hamstring or B) 5-strand hamstring
      group by block randomization using an online randomization generator
      (http://www.graphpad.com/quickcalcs). All the subjects will undergo the procedure by
      otherwise identical technique performed by single surgeon. At 1 and 2 years functional and
      subjective outcomes will be assessed.
