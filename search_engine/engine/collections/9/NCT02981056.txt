
    
      This is a single-center, single-blind, parallel study in healthy, full-term, male or female
      infants aged 0-6 months at the start of the study. The objective of the study is to
      investigate the effects of different skincare regimens on infants (0-6 months old at the
      start of the study), specifically focusing on moisturization effect. Outcome measures include
      moisturization, skin pH, skin barrier, physiological parameters, reactivity and skin
      microbiome.
