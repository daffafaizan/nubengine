
    
      During the procedure of placing a gastrointestinal gastrostomy tube (GIG tube) or GI
      jejunostomy tube (GIJ tube), the gastroenterologist or interventional radiologist will
      replace the manufacturer's external, adjustable skin disk with the external, adjustable
      Constant Pressure Skin Disk (CPSD) by sliding the former off and the latter on to the feeding
      tube.
