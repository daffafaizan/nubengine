
    
      This study will be conducted on:

      Eighty patients suffering of verruca (male and female) divided randomly into three groups:
      group A, B&C :

      Group A: 30 patients will undergo photodynamic therapy using intralesional 4%methylene blue
      solution and IPL.

      Group B: 30 patients will undergo IPL only. Group C (control group):20 patients will undergo
      intralesional saline.
