
    
      Women following bariatric surgery are included at least 6 months after childbirth to evaluate
      retrospectively pregnancy and puerperal time. For mental health issues questionnaires, a
      structured clinical interview (SCID) and a psychological interview give an insight to past
      and present status. In order to evaluate weight progress and nutrient deficiencies routine
      follow-up care data of bariatric surgery is screened. In order to assess the course of
      pregnancy and child development data is extracted from pregnancy and child's examinations
      reports. Furthermore medication adherence, quality of life and sleep are registered by
      questionnaires.
