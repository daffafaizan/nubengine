
    
      While the specific impact of obstructive sleep apnea (OSA) on quality of life is well
      established, the impact of CPAP therapy on quality of life remains unclear. Yet, improving
      the quality of life of OSA patient is one of the main targets of treatment.

      Both exercise and healthy diet are recommended in the management of overweight and obesity.
      This should improve quality of life as well as comorbidities associated with sleep apnea.
