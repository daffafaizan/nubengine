
    
      Maternal weight is measured close to delivery and BMI is calculated. Birth weight, length,
      head circumference, Apgar score and glycaemia are recorded at birth. An EEG are performed on
      the newborn within 48 hrs after birth.A maternal blood sample is collected at admission to
      measure maternal glycated hemoglobin (A1C). Cord blood samples are collected in the umbilical
      vein after delivery.
