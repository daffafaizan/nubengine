
    
      Randomized single-blinded clinical trial with convenience sampling of the anesthetic efficacy
      of a single 30-minute application vs three 10-minute applications of Lidocaine (4%),
      Epinephrine (0.1%), and Tetracaine (0.5%) topical gel (LET) prior to suturing simple
      lacerations in children aged 7-17 years seen in the Emergency Department.
