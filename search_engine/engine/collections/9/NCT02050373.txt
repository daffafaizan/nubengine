
    
      This is a randomized clinical trial in which the proposed analysis will be performed from
      saliva samples of patients transplanted bone marrow, divided into two different groups
      (control and laser) and collected at different times during the hospitalization of the
      patient. the group laser receive prophylactic laser therapy, in order to evaluate the effects
      of LLLT in inflammatory mediators (cytokines pro- and anti-inflammatory) as well as their
      effect on modulation of oxidative stress products and cell damage (nitric oxide,
      myeloperoxidase, interleukin-8).
