
    
      This study will be done inside Cairo university hospitals, it will involve a number of CP
      donors who were recovered from COVID-19 infection and were treated at Cairo University
      isolation hospital. The CP donation process will take place at CUH blood bank ,the number of
      the donors will be determined at the end of the study as it will vary according to the
      donation process .

      The study will also include sixty seven /life threatening COVID-19 patients admitted to Cairo
      University isolation hospital.
