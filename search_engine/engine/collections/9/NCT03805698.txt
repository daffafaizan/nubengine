
    
      Patients with TFCC tears will undergo treatment with arthroscopic debridement, as is standard
      of care, followed by treatment with PRP (24 subjects) versus no treatment with PRP (24
      subjects). Outcome measures will include Modified Mayo Wrist scores (pain scale scores, grip
      strength, wrist range of motion, functional status) and Patient-rated Wrist Evaluation (PRWE)
      scores.
