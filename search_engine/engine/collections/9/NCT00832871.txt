
    
      This is a compassionate use of mifepristone treatment for patients with conditions that could
      respond to an antiprogesterone agent, including:

        -  Meningioma.

        -  Breast cancer

        -  Colon Cancer

        -  Endometrial Stromal Sarcoma

        -  Bilateral Chronic Central Serous Retinopathy

        -  Cushing's Syndrome

        -  Metastatic Adrenocortical Cancer

        -  Ovarian Cancer

        -  Other conditions as determined by the attending physicians
