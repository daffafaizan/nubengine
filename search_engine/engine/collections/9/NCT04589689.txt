
    
      This study will collect survey and biomedical data to assess this program in a randomized
      controlled trial with 165 families (including an adolescent and parent/caregiver) will be
      enrolled, complete surveys, provide biomedical data via continuous glucose monitors and
      receive a 6-week psychosocial intervention. The results of this study will inform future
      intervention redesign to provide more judicious interventions to be disseminated across
      diabetes care.This study will evaluate the relative efficacy of each of the individual
      intervention components and also identify the mechanisms of actions (mediators) that are most
      impacted by these types of interventions as well as most linked to long-term outcomes for
      adolescents.
