
    
      Thirty patients who participated in the study were randomly divided into two groups. First
      group (n=15), conventional physiotherapy program and exercise, second group (n=15), in
      addition to the conventional physiotherapy program and exercises, scapula exercises were
      applied. Patients were evaluated before and after treatment by VAS, algometer, hand
      dynamometer, EMG, UEFS, PRTEE and DASH questionnaires.
