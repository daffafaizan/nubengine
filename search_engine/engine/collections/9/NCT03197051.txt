
    
      Syndecan-1, Heparan sulfate serve as biomarkers of glycocalyx degradation. Both biomarkers
      will be measured 2 times, before the anesthetic induction, and immediately after weaning from
      cardiopulmonary bypass, during the valvular heart surgery.

      Postoperative acute kidney injury up to postoperative 48 hours, Composite morbidity and
      mortality up to postoperative 30 days will be checked.
