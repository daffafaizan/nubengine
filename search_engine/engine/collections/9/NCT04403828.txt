
    
      This is a cross-sectional, online survey-based study administered to Dentist in Egypt. A
      20-item questionnaire will be developed and distributed using Google Forms.

      Participants were recruited through social networking websites and applications (Twitter,
      Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp). The questionnaire will be conducted from 24 May
      2020 and until 30 june 2020.

      The survey will be delivered in Enlish. Participants will be able to complete the survey only
      once and will be allowed to terminate the survey at any time they desired. The survey will be
      anonymous and confidential. An introductory paragraph outlining the purpose of the study will
      be posted along with the survey.
