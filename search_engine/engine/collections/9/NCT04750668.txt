
    
      A total of 80 subacute stroke participants will be randomly assigned to either experimental
      and control groups. Participants in the experimental group will receive multisensory balance
      training while those in the control group will maintain their regular care. All participants
      will be assessed their levels of visual dependence, motor ability, balance and gait, vertigo
      symptom and activities of daily living before and after intervention program as well as at
      3-month follow-up.
