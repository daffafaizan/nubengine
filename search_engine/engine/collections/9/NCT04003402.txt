
    
      Participants will be admitted to the clinical unit on day -1 of each period and will be
      residential for 6 days/5 nights. Participants will be discharged from the clinical unit on
      day 5 of each period on the condition that all required assessments have been performed and
      that there are no medical reasons for a longer stay in the clinical unit. Participants will
      return to the clinical unit on days 7 and 10 for pharmacokinetic blood sampling.
