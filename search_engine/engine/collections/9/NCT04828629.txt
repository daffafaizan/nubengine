
    
      About 100 adult patients hospitalized in the Department of Cardiology and Congenital Heart
      Diseases of Adults who had recovered from COVID-19 will be included in the study. Selected
      prognostic factors will be analyzed, among others: demographic data of the patient, physical
      examination, symptoms, co-morbidities, results of selected laboratory tests, lung imaging
      (X-ray or computed tomography) selected electrocardiographic and echocardiographic data,
      patient's cooperation with the doctor, treatment applied, parameters in the spiroergometric
      study, body mass analysis. One year after inclusion in the study, a control hospitalization
      will be performed. The same factors will be analyzed.
