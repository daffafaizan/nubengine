
    
      The intervention consists of five components: Recruitment of peers and peer supporters;
      training of peer supporters; match making of peers and peer supporters; individual
      face-to-face meetings between peers and peer supporters; and ongoing supervision and network
      meetings for peer supporters. The peer support meetings are held approximately every second
      week for six months and contain three activities: 'Social and emotional support'; 'Assistance
      in daily management'; and 'Linkage to healthcare services'
