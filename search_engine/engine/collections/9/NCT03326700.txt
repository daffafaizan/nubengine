
    
      176 men with unilateral inguinal hernia were randomized into two groups. Group T (n=88)
      underwent TEP and Group L(n:88) underwent LH repair. Patients demografigs, perioperative
      findings were recorded. For all patients preoperative, postoperative 7th,30th and 90th day SF
      36 and IIEF were filled up.
