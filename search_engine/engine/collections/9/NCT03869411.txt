
    
      Participants with diabetes will attend an 12-week aerobic exercise intervention or home
      exercise if the exercise test result of the maximum rate of oxygen consumption is fair or
      poor. Assessments will be taken before and after the training program, which including muscle
      strength, stiffness, quality, microcirculation and exercise capacity. Comparisons between the
      pre-training and post-training condition will be made for each groups. And the assessments
      also compare with the healthy control group.
