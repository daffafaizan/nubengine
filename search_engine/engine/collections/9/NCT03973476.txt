
    
      Sample: 220 primiparous mothers with access, during the 8 weeks postpartum, to a mobile phone
      to contact the midwife randomization of 110 mothers to the experimental group and 110 to the
      control group. The mothers of the experimental group will be offered the mobile phone number
      to contact their midwife after delivery. The mothers of the control group will receive the
      usual attention of the portfolio of services that includes the spontaneous visit in the
      center, the telephone call in the center, the home visit and the health education groups.
