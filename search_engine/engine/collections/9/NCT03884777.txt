
    
      This is a descriptive study. The data will be analyzed retrospectively . Inclusion of 416
      cases of influenza in myasthenic patients treated in the intensive care unit of Garches
      Hospital between January 1, 2009 and December 31, 2017.

      Patients who meet the inclusion and non inclusion criteria and do not object to participate
      in the study will be collected in a database for analysis.
