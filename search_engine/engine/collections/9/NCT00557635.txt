
    
      All patients will be treated for tibia or femur pseudo-arthrosis. At the beginning of
      chirurgical intervention, autologous bone marrow will be sampling and will be concentrated
      during this one.

      Meanwhile osseous matrix (Ostéopure™ ) will be incubated in autologous blood serum and
      finally implanted in the fracture site. And after (at the end of chirurgical intervention),
      concentrate autologous bone marrow will be injected in the same place.
