
    
      De---identified patient---level data will be collected on patients presenting to a
      participating intensive care unit or emergency department with severe sepsis or septic shock
      over a 24 hour period from 00:00 to 24:00 November 07, 2013. De---identified data that is
      already collected as part of routine clinical care will be collected for this study. Data to
      be collected includes hospital and ICU characteristics, patient characteristics, severity of
      illness, and adherence to SSC bundle elements and mortality. It will take between 30---60
      minutes to collect and enter data for each patient admitted with severe sepsis or septic
      shock over the 24 hours study period.
