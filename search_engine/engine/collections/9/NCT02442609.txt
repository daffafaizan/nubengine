
    
      This is a prospective study with 23 centers ( France, Suisse, Belgium, Canada) including
      before surgery: adult (> 18y), no emergency procedure. Questionaires (8 questions) are
      distributed to the patient before surgery and after they were prepared by surgeon. After
      collecting all questionaires, questionaires are analysed by an independant center. The mean
      question is the use of internet for anesthesia information. More than 8000 patients have to
      be enrolled during the study period over 23 centers.
