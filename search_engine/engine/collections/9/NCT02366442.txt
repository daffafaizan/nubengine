
    
      Examine if there are associations among balance, cognitive and affective function during
      multitasking activities in Veterans with the diagnosis of TBI.

      To determine if an association exists between balance performance and the degree of community
      re-integration in Veterans with TBI.

      To determine which of the three systems, balance performance, cognitive dysfunction or
      affective dysregulation, is the strongest predictor of community re-integration in Veterans
      with TBI.
