
    
      Including a German sleep committee a new questionnaire has been created to ask patients about
      their travel Habits. Only travels over a onenightstay were of interest. All detailed
      Information about the Destination has been asked. A Special Point of interest were the
      Impacts of traveling. For each Patient a doctor completed the medical history including sleep
      related breathing disorder and relevant medical conditions e.g. orthopedic Impacts.
