
    
      Volunteers over 65 years old (n=40) are supplemented either with carnitine or placebo for 24
      weeks. Before the start, in the mid-point, and after finishing the supplementation the
      following primary outputs variables are performed: body composition analysis (InBody720),
      maximal isokinetic knee extensor peak torque (Biodex System 4 Pro), blood cytokines and
      carnitine concentration, blood lipid profile.
