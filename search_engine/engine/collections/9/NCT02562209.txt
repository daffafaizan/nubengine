
    
      Increasing the protein consumption promotes weight loss through several mechanisms. Diets
      high in protein promote energy expenditure through increased postprandial thermogenesis,
      increase total daily expenditure and increase satiety as compared to carbohydrate. The
      present study aims to evaluate the effects "High Protein Atkin's Complete (lacto) VEgetaRian
      Diet (Acronym; 'PACER' diet) on weight, body composition and metabolic profiles in obese
      Asian Indians subjects living in north India.
