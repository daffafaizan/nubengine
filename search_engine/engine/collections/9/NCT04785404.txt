
    
      In primary skin closure group skin will be closed with interrupted polypropylene sutures
      while in delayed skin closure group wound dressed with saline-soaked gauze daily using
      aseptic technique and will be allowed to heal by secondary intention or delayed primary
      closure latter on. The wound will be observed daily until the discharge of the patient from
      the hospital and followed later at the out-patient department for four consecutive weeks.
      Wound infection will be assessed according to criteria of the U.S. Centers for Disease
      Control and Prevention (CDC) within at least 30 day post-operatively or until complete
      healing of the site.
