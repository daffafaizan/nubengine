
    
      -  After the development of a techinique to lyophilize corneas, we randomly assingned 20
           patients with keratoconus and visual acuity of < 20/60, who signed the consent term and
           divided them into 2 groups. One group was transplanted with lyophilized corneas and the
           other group with optisol corneas.

        -  Patients are being followed for 6 months with visual acuity, refraction, topography,
           orbscan, pachmetry, ultrasound biomicroscopy and confocal microscopy.

        -  Results are going to be statistically analysed by SPSS program.
