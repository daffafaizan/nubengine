
    
      Oral moisturizing jelly is a novel edible, gel-based artificial saliva. It has been proven
      effective in reducing dry mouth sign and symptoms in elderly patients with xerostomia. This
      randomized control trial intends to determine if continuous use of oral moisturizing jelly
      may be effective in reduction of signs and symptoms of dry mouth, improvement of biochemical
      properties of saliva, subjective swallow ability, appetite, tolerance to spicy food, energy
      intake and reduce umami taste recognition threshold.
