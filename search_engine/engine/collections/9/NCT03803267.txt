
    
      Erector spinae plane block and quadratus lumborum block are analgesic techniques suitable for
      open colorectal cancer surgeries. postoperative pain score, serum levels of biomarkers of
      stress (cortisol and CRP), primary hemodynamics, time to first rescue analgesic request, the
      total amount of rescue analgesic consumption and postoperative nausea and vomiting are the
      parameters of comparison between both techniques.
