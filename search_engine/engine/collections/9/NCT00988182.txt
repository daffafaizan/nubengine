
    
      A randomized, cross-over design study was conducted. Whey protein preloads (5-40 g) and
      hydrolysate whey protein (10 g) were provided in 300 ml water. At 30 min following
      consumption, subjects were fed a preset pizza meal (12 kcal/kg, experiment 2). Satiety,
      insulin and blood glucose were measured at baseline and selected intervals pre- and
      post-meal.
