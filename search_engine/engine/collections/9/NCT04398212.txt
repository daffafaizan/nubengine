
    
      CVS-F3 is a survey form consisted of up to 30 questions regarding Computer Vision Syndrome
      (CVS) complains, thereafter a randomized sample of students will be subjected to complete
      ophthalmic examination including visual acuity and refractive measurements, intraocular
      pressure measurements, Slitlamp and fungus examinations, mfERG examinations. the sudents will
      be requested to decrease their screen time and the examinations will be repeated one month
      later.
