
    
      CNI-1493-CD05 is an open-label extension study of CNI-1493-CD04. In the CD05 study, patients
      are eligible for up to 5 courses of semapimod 60 mg IV x 3 days every 6 - 8 weeks. Primary
      objective is assessment of the efficacy of cumulative doses of semapimod measured by decrease
      in Crohn's Disease Activity Index (CDAI). In addition, the safety of repeated courses was
      measured by recording adverse events over time.
