
    
      It is a prospective, comparative, randomized, multicentric, french, open study. Patients with
      ARDS will be enrolled and will be allocated to Liberal Oxygenation arm (LO) or to
      Conservative Oxygenation arm (CO) during the invasive mechanical ventilation procedure in
      ICU.

      In LO arm, objective of PaO2 is 90 to 105 mmHg. In CO arm, objective of PaO2 is 55 to 70
      mmHg.
