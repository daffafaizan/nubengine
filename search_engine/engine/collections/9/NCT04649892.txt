
    
      This will be a double blind controlled study in which the investigators will select a
      specific sample of individuals diagnosed with a video game disorder representing a picture of
      genuine dependence on this technology. The sample will be submitted to the use of naltrexone
      over a period of 12 weeks and it wiil be assessed whether there was a response to this
      intervention and whether that response can be predicted through attentional bias analysis
      using the Eye Tracking device.
