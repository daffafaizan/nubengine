
    
      In this observational, prospective and multicentre clinical trial, investigator seek to
      determine the prognostic value of liver volume during an acute decompensation. Investigator
      plan to enroll admitted adult patients with acute decompensation of an alcoholic cirrhosis
      (abstinent or not), followed for one year or until death or liver transplantation. Liver
      volume will be evaluated using CT-scan or MRI reconstructions.
