
    
      This study proposes to test and compare the surgeon's impression/confidence in identifying
      ureteral jets using 3 other methods in contrast to standard diagnostic cystoscopy: 1)
      cystoscopy with Dextrose 10% solution, 2) cystoscopy with phenazopyridine and 3) IV sodium
      fluorescein 4) standard cystoscopy with saline.
