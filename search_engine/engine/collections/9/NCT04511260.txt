
    
      The treatment will include: A treatment of the vaginal canal using the NuEra Tight VRF small
      area handpiece. A treatment of the introitus and vestibule using the NuEra Tight VRF small
      area hand piece (optional).

      This is a single center, prospective, open label, single arm study. Following screening
      visit, eligible subjects will be enrolled into the study. Each subject will receive 3
      treatments, 4 weeks apart and 2 Follow Up (FU) visits, at 1, and 3 months following the last
      treatment.
