
    
      Doing laparoscopic cholecystectomy for patients with acute cholecystitis as an early
      intervention after patient admission ( first group) or to do it after a while of treatment
      and conservation as a delayed cholecystectomy (second group) points of study include the
      hospital stay, interval for antibiotic, the easiness of the operative maneuver reflected by
      operative time, conversion and intraoperative complications.
