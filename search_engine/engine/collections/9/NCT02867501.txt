
    
      Patients with aortic aneurysm and patients with PAOD and Controls will be investigated in the
      following manner: Duplexsonography with assessment of the arterial diameters in the aorta and
      leg arteries. Duplexsonography with assessment of venous reflux in proximal and deep veins of
      both legs Duplexsonography testing of venous distension in the common femoral vein, the great
      saphenous vein and the femoral vein.
