
    
      This will be a Phase 1, double blind, placebo-controlled, dose-escalation study conducted at
      a single center to 1)evaluate the safety and tolerability of single and multiple doses of
      ISIS 353512 administered intravenously and subcutaneously, 2)evaluate the pharmacokinetic
      profile of single and multiple doses of ISIS 353512 administered intravenously and
      subcutaneously, and 3)evaluate the pharmacodynamics of ISIS 353512 in lowering constitutive
      levels of c-reactive proteins in healthy volunteers.
