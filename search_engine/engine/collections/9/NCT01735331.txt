
    
      This study is a prospective,observational,single-center study.Ethical approval was obtained
      from the Institutional Review Board.This study will be completed 30 patients with Habitual
      Abortus.

      Endometrial vascular endothelial growth factor levels will be measured using Flk-1 / Kinase
      Domain Receptor / Vascular endothelial growth factor receptor-2, Ab-1 (Rabbit PAb), Vascular
      Endothelial Growth Factor (VEGF) Ab-7 (VG1), Cluster of Differentiation 34 (Endothelial Cell
      Marker) Ab-1 (QBEnd/10), Vascular endothelial growth factor receptor-1 (N-term) (FLT1)
      antibody kits. All assays will be performed according to the manufacturer's instructions.
