
    
      This is a randomized, controlled, multicenter study to assess and compare implant stability
      of Trabecular Metal (TM) dental implants and Tapered Screw-Vent (TSV) implants during the
      early stages of healing i.e. 21 +/- 2 days and 42+/- 2 days, as well as their short term
      clinical outcomes.
