
    
      Proper history, examination and investigations are carried out to diagnose causes of
      infertility. Office hysteroscopy will be performed during the Luteal phase of the proceeding
      menstrual cycle using a rigid hysteroscope. Controlled ovarian hyper stimulation-embryo
      transfer (COH-ET) using the standard long protocol of induction used in Ain Shams University
      Maternity Hospitals ART (assisted reproductive techniques) unit.

      Biochemical pregnancy will be determined by a positive pregnancy test performed 2 weeks after
      embryo transfer and clinical pregnancy will be defined by the presence of a gestational sac
      using transvaginal ultrasound performed 6 weeks after embryo transfer.
