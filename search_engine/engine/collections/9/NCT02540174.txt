
    
      -  Study proposal at the first medical consultation for therapeutic management of a first
           upper aerodigestive tract cancer or lung cancer.

        -  Selection criteria validation

        -  Collection of an informed consent

        -  Randomization :

      Arm A: Experimental arm : Addiction consultation to the overall cancer treatment

      Arm B: standard of care

      The completion of questionnaires , the measurement of exhaled carbone monoxide rate using a
      CO tester for all patients, and the integrated addiction treatment among patients randomized
      in Arm A will not interfere with the medical treatment of cancer.
