
    
      This is a multicenter, randomized controlled trial of negative ion generation versus
      light-emitting diode phototherapy for seasonal affective disorder. Participants appropriate
      for the study are subjects with a DSM IV diagnosis of Major Depression, with seasonal
      pattern, winter type. Subjects were seen at Yale University,New Haven,CT University of
      British Columbia,Vancouver,BC Groningen University Hospital,Groningen, The Netherlands, Royal
      Ottawa Hospital, Ottawa,Ontario, McGill University, Montreal,Quebec, University
      Hospital,Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. The trial is 4 weeks in duration.

      This trial has now been completed.
