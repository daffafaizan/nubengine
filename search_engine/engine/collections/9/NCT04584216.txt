
    
      A prospective study is conducted to evaluate the effect of the steam eye mask with acupoints
      stimulation on eye fatigue and dry eye symptoms in VDT users. The effects and safety of the
      single therapy by the moist heat of approximately 40 degree C for 20 minutes with eyebrows
      acupoints massage for the first 3 minutes are evaluated in the single application study, and
      the effects and safety of the repeated therapy by the moist heat applied and massage
      acupoints on eyebrows once per working day for two weeks are evaluated in the repeated
      application study.
