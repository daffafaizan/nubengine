
    
      Research hypothesis :

      In women undergoing diagnostic hysteroscopy, warm saline distension media may be effective in
      decreasing the pain during the procedure .

      Research question :

      In women undergoing diagnostic hysteroscopy, does warm saline distension media decrease the
      pain during the procedure ?

      Aim of the work :

      - This study aim to assess the efficacy of warm saline distension media in decreasing the
      pain in women undergoing diagnostic hysteroscopy
