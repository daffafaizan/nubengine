
    
      A cross-sectional prevalence study will be carried out, taking patients with a diagnosis of
      uterine fibroid in two hospitals; blood level of vitamin D will be taken to determine the
      proportion of patients with deficit and insufficiency; In addition, the number, size and type
      (according to the PALM-COEN classification) of the uterine fibroids in each patient will be
      analyzed.
