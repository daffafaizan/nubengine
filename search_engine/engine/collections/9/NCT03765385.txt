
    
      Information about modifications of the study protocol (September 2020): Experiences of
      exercising and its effects were at the 9-month follow-up collected through individual
      interviews instead of focus group interviews (secondary outcome measures no. 31). Due to
      Covid-19, at the 9-month follow-up all outcome measurements besides the questionnaire
      (secondary outcome measures no 9, 10, 11, 12, and 14) and interviews (secondary outcome
      measure no 31) were terminated for 37 of the participants.
