
    
      This clinical trial is performed in postoperative orthopedic unit. In this study, 120
      patients were enrolled who had an epidural anesthesia; 60 patients who developed PDPH joined
      the treatment group (A) and received the SP block. They are assessed before the procedure by
      NRS and at 30 mins, 2h, 4h, 6h, 12h, and 24h after the procedure. Patients are also assessed
      by TCD before and after the block. The control group (B) included 60 patients with no PDPH.
