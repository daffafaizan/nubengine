
    
      The purpose of this study is to assess the efficacy and performance of the C2 Focal
      Cryoablation System in patients with BE. The study will involve up to 50 subjects with each
      subject receiving cryoablation therapy of all visible BE using the C2 System Focal Ablation
      System. Length of ablation will be 10 seconds. The ablations will be performed on Barrett's
      epithelium with a maximal length of 6 cm.

      At the time of endoscopic follow up, biopsies of the treated area will be taken and submitted
      for analysis according to Histopathology Protocol.

      This study is:

        -  Prospective

        -  Multi-center

        -  Non-randomized
