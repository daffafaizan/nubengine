
    
      The investigators will conduct a retrospective study to determine the prevalence of diabetes,
      comprehensively describe the management of diabetes, and to evaluate the impact of quality of
      care and glycemic control on clinical outcome in elderly subjects admitted to two large
      long-term care facilities: Grady Health System (GHS) and Veterans Administration Medical
      Center (VAMC) in Atlanta, Georgia between 1/01/08 to 12/31/08.
