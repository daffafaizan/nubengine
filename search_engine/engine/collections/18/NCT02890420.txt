
    
      For several decades the eating behavior has been changing. That note is obviously more true
      for children, with the arrival of new recipes, not really healthier for all. This study
      permit to evaluate the characteristics of children in third year of preschool, during a year,
      in 16 schools in Thionville (north-eastern France) by the way of the annual medical visit.
      The aim of the research is to estimate the healthy weight corresponding with a height
      considering the gender.
