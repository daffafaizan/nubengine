
    
      Sanjie Analgesic Capsule is a Chinese Traditional Patent Medicine. It is a botanical extract
      from the raw materials of the Resina Draconis , Panax Notoginseng, fritillary bulb and coix
      seed, which are derived from a classic prescription of traditional Chinese medicine.

      Sanjie Analgesic Capsule was approved by CFDA in 2004, produced by means of modern technology
      by Jiangsu Kanion Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. The study is a multi-center, double -blind,
      placebo-controlled, randomized in Chinese subjects with endometriosis-associated pain to
      assess treatment effect and safety in the subjects treated with Sanjie Analgesic Capsule
      versus subjects treated with placebo.
