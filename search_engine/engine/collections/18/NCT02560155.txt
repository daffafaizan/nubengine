
    
      All participants will be assessed for eligibility during preoperative othopedic consultation.
      2-3 weeks prior operation date participants will be screened for Staph. aureus carriage by
      nose-swab. According to nose-swab results randomizaton and allocation to study arm will be
      performed.

      After one and three months post-operatively participants will be asked by phone interview if
      surgical site infections occured. Results have to be confirmed by orthopedic surgeon if
      possible.
