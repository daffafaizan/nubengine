
    
      Comparison between two techniques for root coverage divided in two groups the first (test
      group) VISTA technique in conjunction with PRF-membrane and the second (control group)MCAF
      technique in conjunction with PRF-membrane for treating Miller's class I and II gingival
      recession defects.

      Clinical Periodontal Records will be taken at baseline, 3 months and 6 months respectively.

      Visual analogue scale for pain during one week after operation. Patient satisfaction record
      using questionnaire after 6 months.
