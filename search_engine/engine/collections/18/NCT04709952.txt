
    
      During the clinical trial period, a total of 4 to 5 outpatient visits are made, and changes
      are evaluated through neuropsychological examination by visiting at 2, 4 and 6 weeks based on
      the baseline time point. In addition, in the case of patients who agree after 6 weeks of
      participation, an additional 6 weeks of high real stimulation is used to conduct an open
      extension study.
