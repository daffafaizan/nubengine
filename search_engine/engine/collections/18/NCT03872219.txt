
    
      Newborns will start to be exposed to biodiversity intervention or placebo at the age of two
      months. The proof of concept trial will be double blind. Intervention will start at the age
      of 2 months and it will end when children become 12 months old. Children will be randomized
      to the two arms. IgE sensitization is the primary outcome at the age of two and three years.
