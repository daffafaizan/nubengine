
    
      This is a cross-over intervention assessing the effect of autogenic drainage on ventilation
      inhomogeneity in patients with cystic fibrosis.

      Electrical impedance tomography and multiple breath nitrogen washout will be performed
      before, immediatly after and 2h post intervention. Each participant will receive either
      aerosoltherapy alone or in combination with chest physiotherapy on 2 consecutive days.
