
    
      The goal of this multicenter clinical observation is to validate short-, mid-, and long-term
      outcome (efficacy and safety) of the NANOS neck preserving hip stem.

      Effectiveness Measures:

        -  Evaluation of function, range of motion (ROM) and pain as assessed by Harris Hip Score,
           HOOS Score [4-6], UCLA Activity Rating [7]

        -  Radiographic changes as defined by radiolucent lines, osteolysis, hypo- and hypertrophy,
           implant loosening or migration

      Safety evaluations:

        -  Intra- and peri-operative device-related adverse events (AE) and complications up to
           discharge

        -  Postoperative AE up to 10 years
