
    
      Sixty primipara undergoing elective cesarean delivery were randomly assigned to receive
      remifentanil 1 μg kg-1 (R1) or 0.5 μg kg-1 (R0.5), or saline (control) over 15 s with
      induction of anesthesia using propofol 2 mg kg-1 and suxamethonium 1.5 mg kg-1.The
      investgators assessed the average value of BIS at the time period of before, induction ,
      intubation, skin incision, peritoneum incision , uterine incision , and delivery. The
      investgators also determined the neonatal Apgar scores, and conducted umbilical artery blood
      gas analysis.
