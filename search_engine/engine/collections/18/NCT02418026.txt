
    
      Nonpharmacological approaches for pain management, either as a primary method or as a
      complement to pharmacological approaches, are suggested for a better perinatal management.
      The investigators assessed the use of conversational hypnotic induction and therapeutic
      suggestions during cesarean section as a complement to pharmacological strategy. Women giving
      birth through scheduled or unplanned cesarean section and who accepted hypnotherapy session
      were compared to a control group.
