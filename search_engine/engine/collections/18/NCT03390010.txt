
    
      Many approved drugs are used in prophylaxis against postpartum hemorage from these
      medications are the (syntocinon combined with methergine ) that are known as active
      management of labour drugs .

      Moreover there is misotac , that also guards against postpartum hemorage .

      All these medications act in a different way to cause uterine contraction post delivery to
      prevent uterine atony .

      -Here we are comparing the additive value of misotac intrauterine to syntocinon and
      methergine in comparison to the usually used syntocinon plus methergine .
