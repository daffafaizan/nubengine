
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. Examine the associations between measures of self-efficacy, quality of social support, and
      financial literacy with the outcome measure of financial toxicity.

      II. Identify the feasibility of collecting these measures in a Spanish-speaking cancer
      patient population.

      OUTLINE:

      Participants will complete a survey packet that is estimated to take about 30 minutes. The
      survey packet will be collected at baseline and at 6 months.
