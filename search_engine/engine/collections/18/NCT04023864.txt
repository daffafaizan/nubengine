
    
      This study was a 12-week, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled human trial. 80
      subjects were randomly divided into Platycodon grandiflorus extract(GCWB107) group and a
      placebo group. It is to evaluate the changes in the displayed evaluation items when taking
      Platycodon grandiflorus extract(GCWB107) extract once a day, in comparison with taking a
      placebo.
