
    
      Fang yi qing feng shi granules is the chinese patent medicine, which contains 8 kinds of
      chinese medicine .

      Fang yi qing feng shi granules was approved by China Food and Drug Administration (CFDA) in
      2005, produced by means of modern technology by Maoxiang Group Jilin Pharmaceutical Co.,
      Ltd.The study is a multi-center, double -blind, placebo -controlled, randomized in Chinese
      subjects with Rheumatoid Arthritis to assess treatment effect and safety in the subjects
      treated with Fang yi qing feng shi granules versus subjects treated with placebo.
