
    
      The Enhanced Recovery Program has a series of observation data contains hospitalization days,
      adverse events, drainage, extubation, visual analogue scale(VAS) scores and the number of
      analgesic using.The investigators plan to enroll 60 patients and divide them into two groups
      to compare the influences of different chest tube managements for enhanced recovery program
      after video-assisted thoracoscopic lobectomy.
