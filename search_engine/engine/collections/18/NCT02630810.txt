
    
      Traditionally, oral intake after CS was depend on insurance of intestinal movement, since
      uncomplicated CS does not affect the intestine and the mother needs to care of her infants,
      lactates, discharged from hospital, early oral hydration may enhance bowel movement, so the
      investigators need to evaluate the safety of early oral hydration after CS.

      This study compare the effects of introducing water during the 1st hour or after 6 hours on
      bowel movement after CS.
