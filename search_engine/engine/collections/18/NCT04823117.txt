
    
      This study's aim was to determine the effect of music therapy on symptoms and functions in
      patients with fibromyalgia syndrome (FMS). Women diagnosed with FMS for at least 6 months
      included in the study. 16 patients were in the music treatment group and 51 patients were in
      the control group. Patients in music treatment group were given a music CD which includes
      nature sounds recommended by the Turkish Psychological Association. The patients were
      evaluated with Fibromyalgia Impact Questionnaire (FIQ) before and after the treatment.
