
    
      This study was 8 weeks, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled human trial. 120
      subjects were randomly divided into ethanol extracts of Porphyra tenera(PTE10) group or
      placebo group. The investigators measure Natural Killer cell activity, Cytokines(IL-2, IL-6,
      IL-12, IFN-γ, TNF-α), questionnaire scores of upper airway infection.
