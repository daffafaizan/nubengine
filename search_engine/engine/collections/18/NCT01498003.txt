
    
      According to the results of 12.6% occurrence of peri-procedural myocardial infarction after
      long stent implantation by previous report[International Journal of Cardiology 2009;134:
      231-237], the investigator hypothesize the 50% reduction of peri-procedural MI by using
      tirofiban. Three hundred and sixty-nine patients in each group are needed to reach 80% of
      power with Î± 0.05.
