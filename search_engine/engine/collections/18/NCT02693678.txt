
    
      Delayed onset muscle soreness (DOMS) can have an impact on the proprioception of knee joint.
      The study aim is to assess the effect of manual massage, diathermy and sham diathermy on the
      proprioception of the knee joint in patients affected by DOMS.

      Proprioception will be measured with "Technique" an App available on appstore , on the basis
      of the work on knee proprioception by S M Mir 2008 , and data about pain with numbering pain
      rating scale (NPRS) and pre-post treatment and global impression of change (PGIC) will be
      acquired.
