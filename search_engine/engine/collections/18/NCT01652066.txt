
    
      probiotics are "live microorganisms which when administered in adequate amounts confer a
      health benefit on the host" . many health effects are associated with probiotics , but
      various degrees of evidence support the benefits associated to probiotics consumption .
      Impact on defences and the immune system has been shown with several strains and this may
      result in improved protection against some infections or improvment of vaccine efficacy .

      In its scientific requirments for health claims related to immune function, EFSA indicates
      that it is generally accepted that higher vaccination responses are beneficial.
