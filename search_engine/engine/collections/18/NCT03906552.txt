
    
      Massage is a systematic and scientific procedure applied by using hands or special tools. As
      for acupressure, it is a method applied by stimulating the acupuncture points using hands and
      fingers by applying pressure on the meridians in body.Both methods are reliable, economic and
      easy to learn and apply. Review of the related literature indicates studies on the
      effectiveness of acupressure and foot massage on reducing pain in neonates.
