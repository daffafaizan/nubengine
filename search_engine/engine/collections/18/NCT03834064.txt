
    
      The proposed intervention is comprised of four components : (a) planned action including a
      behavioral contract Activity 1) with caregivers and action planning for oral health (Activity
      2), (b) caregiver capacity building with didactic training (Activity 3), observational
      learning and caregiver practice (Activity 4); (c) environmental adaptations with location of
      oral health activities and reminder posters - physical environment (Activity 5) and
      administrative oral health support- (social environment (Activity 6); and (d) reinforcement
      with caregiver coaching (Activity 7).
