
    
      All patients that are surgical candidates with endometrial cancer will be screened for this
      study. Two measurements from each eye will be taken in the following positions: awake at
      rest, supine before induction, after induction on anesthesia, after insufflation of abdomen
      with CO2 in supine position, in trendelenburg position, in deep trendelenburg position,at the
      end of procedure,prior to awakening in supine position and one last reading 45-60 min after
      awakening in supine position. The time, blood pressure, heart rate, peak airway pressure,
      plateau airway pressure, end tidal desflurane, end tidal CO2, total IV fluids administered,
      and blood loss.
