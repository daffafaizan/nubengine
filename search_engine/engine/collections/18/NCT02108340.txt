
    
      Patients diagnosed with acute appendicitis from the emergency department of Hippocration
      General Hospital and treated with appendectomy will be evaluated with microwave radiometry
      for changes in the temperature of the appendix before its removal.

      Subsequently, the results of microwave radiometry for the patients confirmed by the
      histopathology report to have acute inflammation of the appendix, will be compared with those
      of the ultrasonography used for the diagnosis of acute appendicitis.
