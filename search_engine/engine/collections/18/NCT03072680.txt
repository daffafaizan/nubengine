
    
      The design will permit two comparisons:

      After the first week of the intervention, participants will answer the assessment. It will
      permit to compare BPS PAST, BPS FUTURE and CONTROL conditions (pre-post).

      After the second week, participants will answer the assessment and then it will be possible
      to compare BPS PAST + BPS FUTURE combination, BPS FUTURE, and CONTROL conditions.
