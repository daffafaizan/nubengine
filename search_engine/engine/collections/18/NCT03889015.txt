
    
      The subjects will be assigned randomly into two equal groups to receive either probiotic
      yogurt or xylitol-containing chewing gum for three months. The probiotic yogurt is (Activia;
      Danone, Egypt) containing Bifidobacterium (7x107 CFU/g). The subjects assigned to probiotic
      yogurt group will be instructed to eat 100 g of the provided yogurt 10 minutes after dinner
      and not to brush their teeth until one hour later. The subjects assigned to the
      xylitol-chewing gum group will be instructed to chew the provided gum three times daily for
      five minutes after each meal.
