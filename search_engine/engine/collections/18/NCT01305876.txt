
    
      Participants: children in 4th and 5th grade at an urban elementary school, identified by the
      special education director and the teaching staff, as having emotional behavioral disorders
      including anxiety, depression, aggression, conduct disorder, hyperactivity and attention
      difficulties. These grades have a combined student population of 160 students. Enrolled over
      two years.

      Inclusion/Exclusion criteria:

      Eligible if identified by the teaching staff or special education director as having an
      emotional behavioral disorder. Exclusion criteria: inability to comprehend English
      sufficiently to complete written assessments and follow the yoga intervention.

      Pre- and post-intervention assessments (teachers)

        1. Behavior Assessment Scale for Children (BASC).

        2. The Swanson, Kotkin, Agler, M-Flynn & Pelham Rating Scale (SKAMP)
