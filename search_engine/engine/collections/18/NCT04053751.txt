
    
      Patients will be monitored for two days. On the first day, the patient will be followed up
      with an open system and the second day closed suctioning system will be used, or the patient
      will be followed up with a closed system on the first day and followed with an open system on
      the second day. The patients are randomly allocated on the first follow up days. The
      follow-up period has two consecutive days. suctioning will be performed for 12 hours in one
      day. The number of suctions will be recorded. The amount of secretion will be accumulated in
      the mucus collection vessel. At the end of the day, the secretion will be weighed with
      precision weighing.
