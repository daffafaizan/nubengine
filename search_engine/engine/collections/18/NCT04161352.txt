
    
      The current study is a observation study in the real world setting. The 500 patients in
      compliance with Enrollment Criteria will be as the objects of observation. The procedure of
      study include the period of screening, treating and follow-up. The duration of study is about
      two and half year. The data entrying will be done by CRC, using the EDC system. CRA will
      audit the quality of data regularly.
