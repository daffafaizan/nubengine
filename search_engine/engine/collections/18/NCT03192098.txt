
    
      A randomized controlled prospective clinical trial assessing the efficacy and safety of
      progressive esophageal dilation (up to 6mm) compared to esophageal dilation according to the
      rule-of-3 in patients with severe benign esophageal strictures. Patients will be followed up
      for 6 months. When recurrent dysphagia occurs within 6 months the patients will be dilated
      according to the previously allocated strategy.
