
    
      This study is a randomized crossover study in which patients will be randomized to start with
      or without a mobile device assisting the patient or caregiver in using the device. The
      investigators aim to enroll 20 patients across 3 sites. Each patient is expected to
      participate in the study for 12 weeks. After the first 2 weeks of participation, the patient
      will either begin using the mobile device to assist or will stop using the mobile device for
      the next 2 weeks. after the initial 4 weeks, the patient will choose to continue with or
      without using the mobile device.
