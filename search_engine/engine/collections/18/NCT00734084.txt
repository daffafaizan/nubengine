
    
      Primary endpoint - to demonstrate the suitability of the developed instrumentation to safely
      and accurately implant the preservation Unicompartmental Knee using postoperative
      radiographic limb and component alignment data.

      Secondary endpoints - to demonstrate the survivorship and performance of the Preservation
      Unicompartmental Knee using the American Knee Society clinical and radiological assessment,
      Oxford Knee Score and survivorship analysis.
