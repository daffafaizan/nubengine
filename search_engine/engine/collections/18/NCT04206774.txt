
    
      The BIO|STREAM.ICM submodule will recruit a subset of patients with syncope with uncertain
      origin and cryptogenic stroke without history of atrial arrhythmia from the existing
      population enrolled in the BIO|STREAM.ICM registry. The submodule study is designed to
      provide clinical evidence from patients in France on safety and efficacy of the BIOMONITOR
      System.
