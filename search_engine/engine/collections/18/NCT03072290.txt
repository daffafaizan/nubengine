
    
      This is a prospective, single-blinded randomized controlled study to determine the efficacy
      of low dose corticosteroid in patient with CTS. Patient with CTS were randomly assigned to
      group receiving ultrasound guided steroid injection with different dosage of triamcinolone
      acetonide (Shincort) mixed, 1ml 10mg (10mg/ml) or 1ml 40mg (40mg/ml) with 1 ml of 2%
      lidocaine hydrochloride. The follow up at 6 and 12 weeks includes Boston Carpal Tunnel
      Questionnaire, nerve conductive study and VAS pain score.
