
    
      Participants will be randomly assigned to one of three intervention arms. Two brief
      stimulation periods will be conducted on day one. One brief stimulation will be scheduled in
      the morning and a second brief stimulation will be scheduled in the afternoon. A final
      stimulation will be conducted on day two. Direct Current Stimulation will be applied for 20
      minutes for each stimulation period. Brief neurocognitive testing will be conducted during
      each stimulation session.
