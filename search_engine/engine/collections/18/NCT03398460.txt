
    
      This study will evaluate:

        1. Burnout rates among nurses and ancillary staff with the Abbreviated Maslach burnout
           inventory. This is a well validated tool that is used to evaluate symptoms of burnout
           such as denationalization, emotional exhaustion and lack of personal accomplishment.

        2. Burnout rates at the beginning of and at the end of the house staff's ICU rotation to
           gauge the effect of an ICU rotation on burnout rates amon medical trainees.

        3. Determine whether the introduction of a bereavement card will improve symptoms of
           burnout as gauged by the abbreviated maslach burnout inventory

        4. Staff perceptions and response to the introduction of a bereavement
