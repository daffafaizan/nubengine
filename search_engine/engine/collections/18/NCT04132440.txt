
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. Explore the perceptions of non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD), including knowledge,
      current practices for diagnosis and management, and additional disease-related perceptions
      amongst both primary care physicians.

      II. Explore the perceptions of NAFLD, including knowledge, diagnosis and management, and
      additional disease-related perceptions amongst Hispanic patients.

      OUTLINE:

      Patients and physicians complete a questionnaire over 5 minutes and an interview over 30-45
      minutes about thoughts on NAFLD, including what they know about NAFLD and its diagnosis,
      management, and monitoring, and other thoughts on NAFLD.
