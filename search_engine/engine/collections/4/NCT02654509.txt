
    
      This study is being conducted to collect safety and immunogenicity data for the Eastern
      Equine Encephalitis (EEE) vaccine. To determine the frequency of adverse events in this
      Eastern Equine Encephalitis (EEE) vaccine study for all intent-to-treat subjects will be
      evaluated. Also the frequency of clinically confirmed and documented cases of Eastern Equine
      Encephalitis (EEE) disease among vaccinated subjects compliant with titer schedule after
      working with Eastern Equine Encephalitis (EEE) virus.
