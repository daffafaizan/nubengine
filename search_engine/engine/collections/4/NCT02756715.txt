
    
      The patients were allocated randomly to receive propofol or desflurane. Also, a total of 6ml
      of blood sample was obtained for total 6 times in consecutive order.

        1. Preoperation (immediate before anesthesia induction)

        2. Immediate before tourniquet off

        3. 5 min after tourniquet off

        4. PACU

        5. postoperative 6 hours

        6. postoperative 24 hours

      Serum in blood will be obtained and level of serum cytokine will be evaluated by
      enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay
