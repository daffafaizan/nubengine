
    
      This study is looking at information already collected of transplant outcomes (short, medium
      and long-term complications and quality of life). This will specifically look at:

        1. 10 year follow-up of steroid-free maintenance immunosuppression

        2. A cross-sectional study of infection after kidney transplantation (with emphasis on
           infection in steroid-free recipients

        3. An evaluation of the Delayed Graft Function (DGF) nomogram in Thymoglobulin treated
           recipients at the University of Minnesota
