
    
      Participating Children will be recruited from the outpatient's clinic of pediatric dentistry
      department , Faculty of Dentistry Ain Shams University and will be randomly allocated to one
      of the following groups; Indirect pulp capping, direct pulp capping, partial pulpotomy or
      pulpotomy. TheraCAL PT, a dual cured tricalcium silicate cement will be used in all groups as
      the pulp capping material. Children will be followed every 6 months for a period of 2 years
      to evaluate clinical and radiographic success.
