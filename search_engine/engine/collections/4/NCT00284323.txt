
    
      Prospective, single center, randomized clinical study. Study design is random patient
      assignment to selective intracoronary administration of adenosine or control immediately
      before restoration of coronary artery patency in patients presenting with an acute ST
      segment-elevation myocardial infarction (STEMI). Randomisation will be stratified for the
      duration of symptoms (< 4 hours vs > 4 hours).
