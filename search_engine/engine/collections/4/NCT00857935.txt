
    
      A Phase 2b, randomized, single-site, investigator/evaluator blind, two-arm, parallel-group
      pilot study that tested the operational elements of an "Actual Use" study designed to
      evaluate the safety and efficacy of a 10-minute treatment and no-nit combing regimen of
      NatrOVA Creme Rinse 1% versus NIX Creme Rinse in subjects with at least a mild case of head
      lice.
