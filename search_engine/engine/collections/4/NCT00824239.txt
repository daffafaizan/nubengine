
    
      A comparison of intermittent sedation - keeping analgesia with fentanyl and midazolam or
      propofol as boluses if agitation (SAS 5 or more) after adequate analgesia - and daily
      interruption of sedation - analgesia with fentanyl and sedation wiht midazolam or propofol
      continuously with daily interruptions every morning until patients awake and can follow
      simple commands. Sedation is restarted in half previous dosage if agitation (SAS 5 or more)
      occur.
