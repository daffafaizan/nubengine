
    
      The study aims to assess concentrations of the different fatty acids in the erythrocyte
      membrane before and after administration of a compound with high quality omega3 (EPA and DHA)
      in two different doses, compared with control group . The period of evaluation and management
      is four months, one month for the proposed renewal of red blood cells. The composition of the
      diet, the sports calendar, caloric expenditure and level of training is determined. During
      the study process it is excluded subjects with injuries or disease processes and ensure that
      no one take any supplement or modify workouts or diet.
