
    
      With the Westernization of the diet and insufficient exercise, Taiwan's population of
      obesity, diabetes, and hyperlipidemia has increased in recent years, and the prevalence of
      the non-alcoholic fatty liver disease has gradually increased. Although weight loss, dietary
      adjustments, and certain drug treatments can delay the deterioration of the disease; however,
      weight loss and dietary adjustment are not easy. Moreover, drugs also have side effects, and
      there is currently no convenient and effective treatment for people who have non-alcoholic
      fatty liver disease. The purpose of this trial is that investigating and evaluating the
      effects on fatty liver, liver fiber, and metabolic indexes after subjects using Shen Pu Yang
      Gan Wan.
