establishment of a turkey flock free of 'n' strain mycoplasmamycoplasma of the 'n' strain are frequently isolated fromturkey poults affected with airsacculitis but free ofmycoplasma gallisepticum.  research on the 'n' strain hasbeen impeded by the presence of a high proportion ofturkey poults with airsacculitis in all breeding flocksexamined, by the difficulty of culturing and identifyingthe mycoplasma strains, and by the lack of a practicalserologic test.  this communication describes the establishmentof a small nucleus of 'n'-free turkeys from a commercialbreeding flock known to carry 'n' mycoplasma.hens and toms for the production of 'n'-free poults wereselected from the parent flock by serology and culture.
