
    
      Each cohort will include 30 patients. Complete blood count in all patients will be done daily
      in all patients. Bone marrow aspiration cytology will be done in some selected patients
      especially those with lymphopenia less than 1000 per cubic mm.

      Correlation of the degree of lymphopenia with the status of the patients will be done. Also
      correlation will be done about the relation of recovery of lymphopenia and status of the
      patients.
