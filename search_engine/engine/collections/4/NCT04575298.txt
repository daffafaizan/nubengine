
    
      This is an acceptability study to evaluate the gastrointestinal tolerance and compliance over
      a 7-day period, of Amino acid formula with HMOs, which is a hypoallergenic amino acid for
      infant formula for the dietary management of cows' milk allergy.

      The acceptability data from a minimum of 15 participants will be collected in order to submit
      an application to the Advisory Committee on Borderline Substances (ACBS) and the HSE for the
      Ireland to be reimbursed under the GMS and community drug schemes
