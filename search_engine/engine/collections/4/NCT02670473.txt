
    
      This is a 30 subject, prospective, dispensing, bilateral wear, subject-masked study,
      reviewing the refit characteristics in a group of enfilcon A wearers switched to the
      fanfilcon A lens. Subject's habitual enfilcon A lenses will be evaluated at the first vist
      and then re-fitted with a pair of fanfilcon A lenses for 4 weeks of daily wear. After the
      dispensing visit, subjects will return for evaluations at 1 week, 2 weeks, and 4 weeks.
