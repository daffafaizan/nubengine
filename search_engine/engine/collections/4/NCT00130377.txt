
    
      The clinical cell registry includes data on thoracic and cardiovascular diseases including:

      Acute myocardial infarction, Chronic Angina, Heart Failure, Wound Healing including Sternal,
      Peripheral Arterial Disease, Critical Limb Ischemia, Type 2 Diabetes, COPD, Asthma.
      Neurological disorders registry is available to specifics sites if requested. Our own
      biological data at the University of Utah including co-localized data with Society of
      Thoracic Surgeons also is a part of this program. Cell based therapies that are autologous
      and allogeneic are included in the registry
