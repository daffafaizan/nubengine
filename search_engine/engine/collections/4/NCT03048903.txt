
    
      Patients were randomly divided into two groups, using rhubarb powder or placebo. Rhubarb is
      commercially certified rhubarb(Rheum palmatum, Sichuan origin, provided by the pharmacy of my
      hospital), ground into a powder, over 80 mesh steel mesh, Two days before 6g, three times /
      day, oral or nasal feeding, two days later reduced to 3g, three times / day, oral or nasal
      feeding; Another group of patients taking placebo, with the same dose of Rhubarb; The overall
      course of the two drugs was a week.
