
    
      This is a 12-week, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial comparing maca root (3.0 g/day)
      versus an inactive placebo in female outpatients with SSRI/SNRI-induced sexual dysfunction
      whose depression had remitted with SSRI treatment. Outcome measures include improvement in
      sexual functioning as per the Arizona Sexual Experience Scale (ASEX) and the Massachusetts
      General Hospital Sexual Function Questionnaire (MGH-SFQ).
