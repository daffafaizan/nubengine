
    
      35 subjects with chronic cough plus 25 subjects without any cough will be asked to
      participate. The study consists of two visits in the outpatient clinic of Kuopio University
      Hospital, department of respiratory medicine, 2 - 7 days apart. They will fill in the
      Leicester Cough Questionnaire and undergo two different cough provocation tests, in random
      order: Mannitol test and citric acid test. If a chest x-ray has not been taken within 6
      months, it will be taken.
