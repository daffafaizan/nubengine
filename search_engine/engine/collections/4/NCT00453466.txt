
    
      A randomized controlled trial (N=141) with two control groups (one individual
      psychotherapeutic treatment and the other a usual care group treatment) was conducted in a
      violence treatment center in the South of Israel The treatment protocol designed to empower
      battered women (EBW) was conducted with 12 groups over a period of two years. Assessments
      were made before, immediately following and at an 8 month follow up following the
      intervention.
