
    
      This is a nationwide qualitative study of 20 to 40 physicians who provide abortion services
      as part of their scope of practice. Providers will participate in a semi-structured interview
      using an interview guide that addresses 4 domains of interest including: (1) initiation of
      abortion services, (2) barriers to abortion provision, (3) facilitators of abortion provision
      and (4) acquired expertise and coaching for success.
