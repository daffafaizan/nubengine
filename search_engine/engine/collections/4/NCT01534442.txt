
    
      GLP-1 is a importent enterogastron and incretin hormone. Rapid degradation of GLP-1 by
      dipeptidyl peptidase 4 (DPP-4) suggests that GLP-1 may act locally (through vagal afferents)
      before being degraded. We aimed to clarify the role of vagal innervation on the incretin
      effect.
