
    
      After receiving consent inform from parents, 40 children scheduled for orthopedic surgeries
      will be randomized to one of two groups: epidural group and intravenous group, both will
      receive 1 mg kg-1 S(+)-ketamine. All patients will receive caudal block anesthesia with
      marcaine. Cardiovascular monitoring will be assessed during operation. Follow up will
      continue for 24 hours after caudal block. Duration of analgesia, first time of analgesic
      request and complications will be recorded by an orthopedic assistant that is blinded to
      study. Data will be analyzed statistically by Chi square, t test and nonparametric tests.
