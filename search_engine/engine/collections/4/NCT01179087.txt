
    
      All patients with end-stage respiratory failure who are referred for lung transplantation and
      are older than 18 years old are enrolled.

      We collect:

        -  Medical history

        -  Blood pressure

        -  Anthropometrics

        -  Pulmonary function

        -  Arterial blood gas

        -  Questionnaire (QOL, ESS)

        -  Sleep diary

        -  Polysomnography
