
    
      It is expected to determine for the first time an optimal recovery duration that would
      maximize the walking capacity of LEPAD patients.

      In the medium term :

        -  To give indications to the LEPAD patients to manage their pain in the community without
           lower their physical activity.

        -  To limit the functional decline of LEPAD patients.

        -  To influence the quality of life and cardiovascular mortality. This would deserve
           furthers studies.
