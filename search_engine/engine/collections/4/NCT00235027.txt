
    
      Specific Aim 1: Increase routine identification of outpatient adverse drug events (ADEs)
      through development of a computerized ADE detection monitor.

      Specific Aim 2: Use basic computerized outpatient prescribing to reduce preventable ADEs in a
      diverse array of outpatient settings.

      Specific Aim 3: Use advanced decision-support within computerized prescribing to reduce the
      frequency of preventable ADEs, medication errors, and potential ADEs.
