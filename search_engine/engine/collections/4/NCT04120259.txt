
    
      Diabetes Mellitus is one of the oldest diseases known to men and has been treated with
      multiple agents,organic and with the current pharmacological advancement,with multiple
      synthetic drugs as well.

      Metformin has been considered first line for the treatment of Type 2 Diabetes, however
      presents itself with a side effect profile of GI distress, Vitamin B12 deficiency,to name a
      few.

      Vinegar has been used as therapeutic agent for centuries for multiple ailments including
      diabetes. This study aims to evaluate the combined effects of drug and natural therapy and
      compare it with conventional therapy.
