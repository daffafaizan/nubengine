
    
      Transfusion of blood products has many disadvantages. Erythropoietin is used to increase red
      blood cells production and decrease such disadvantages.In some studies anti inflammatory
      effects is shown for Erythropoietin.

      One of the most problems in the intensive care unit is stress response. we are going to
      evaluate effects of erythropoietin on oxidative stress and inflammation in trauma patient in
      intensive care unit.
