
    
      The investigators tested the effects on serum Arg and ADMA of a) a single load of ingested
      dextrose, protein, fat or alcohol, each consumed within 10 minutes; b) a single episode of
      physical exercise; c) a 12 weeks lifestyle modification program comprised of a standard
      dietary and physical activity counseling in obese or T2DM subjects on circulating Arg and
      ADMA (n=43)
