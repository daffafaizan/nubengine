
    
      Delirium is prevalent after hip fracture surgery. Delirium has been linked to postoperative
      sleep disturbances. Zolpidem is a non-benzodiazepine hypnotic that preserves REM sleep. The
      present study investigates the incidence of delirium in hip fracture patients randomized to
      Zolpidem treatment 5 mg vs placebo in the perioperative phase.
