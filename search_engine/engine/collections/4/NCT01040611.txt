
    
      The experimental study was conducted on a medical center of North Taiwan. 66 patients with
      Tuberculosis were included in this study. Patients who fit the inclusion criteria were
      randomly assigned to either experimental or control group. Patients in experimental group
      could choose a piece of CD and listen to music with earphone for 30 minutes. Patients in
      control group just rest for 30 minutes. The procedure was implemented once a day, and
      sustained for total 5 days.
