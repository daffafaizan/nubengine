
    
      'Niti-S SPAXUS Stent' is a newly designed lumen-apposing fully covered self-expandable metal
      stent. It is indicated for use in patients with pancreatic pseudocyst. Consecutive subject
      data should be collected at discharge, stent removal (day 30 or 60), and day 20 post Niti-S
      SPAXUS stent removal.
