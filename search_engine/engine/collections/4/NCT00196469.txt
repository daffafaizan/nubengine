
    
      Title: Randomized double blind trial comparing heparin and placebo as additives to continuous
      infusion in intensive care neonates for prevention of mechanical ventilation

      Primary endpoint: Percentage of neonates requiring mechanical ventilation in both groups

      Secondary endpoints: Duration of dependency on mechanical ventilation; major bleedings,
      incidence of heparin-induced thrombocytopenia and of anti-platelet factor 4/heparin
      antibodies
