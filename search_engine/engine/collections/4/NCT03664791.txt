
    
      The Vanguard rotating platform knee prosthesis (Vanguard ROCC) was launched in March 2009.
      Therefore there is a need to show the safety and performance of the Vanguard ROCC at long
      term.

      A maximum of 10 sites will be involved in this study. This number of clinical centers will
      permit assessment of consistency among a multitude of investigators.
