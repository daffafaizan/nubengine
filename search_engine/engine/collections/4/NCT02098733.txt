
    
      This a special drug use surveillance on long-term use of pioglitazone/glimepiride combination
      tablets (Sonias Combination Tablets) designed to investigate the frequency of adverse drug
      reactions in patients with type 2 diabetes mellitus in the routine clinical setting.

      The usual adult dosage is one tablet of Sonias administered orally once daily before or after
      breakfast (15 mg/1 mg or 30 mg/3 mg of pioglitazone/glimepiride).
