
    
      This study compares the time needed to extubate of two anesthesic techniques, 1) Total
      Intravenous Anesthesia guided by Bispectral Index (BIS), 2) Spinal Anesthesia with minimal
      opioid dose for cardiac surgery.

      Identify the best anesthesia technique to do ultrafast track extubation can minimize the risk
      of infection associated.
