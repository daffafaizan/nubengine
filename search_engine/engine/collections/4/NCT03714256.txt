
    
      The purpose of this study is to validate the user interface of a pediatric power mobility
      device by establishing that the product is safe and effective use for the intended users. The
      human factors validation study will be conducted in children with mobility impairments as
      categorised by Hays (1987) in the age range of 6 months to 36 months under realistic use
      conditions.The study is an open label, un-blinded, non-randomized study which consists of one
      visit.
