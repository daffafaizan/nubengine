
    
      A randomized controlled clinical trial on 50 hybrid hips age above 70 years to the described
      two surgical procedures will be performed at Aarhus University hospital by two orthopaedic
      consultants. Randomization in 10 blocks of ten patients distributed with five patients for
      Palacos Cement and five patients for Hi-Fatigue Cement.

        -  25 CPT femoral stems fixed with Hi-Fatigue Bone Cement.

        -  25 CPT femoral stems fixed with Palacos Bone Cement.

      All patients will be operated by the postero-lateral approach. The post-operative treatment
      and training will not differ from the standard.
