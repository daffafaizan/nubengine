
    
      The therapeutic efficacy of Ursodeoxycholic Acid 300 mg tablet and capsules was measured
      comparing the liver enzyme parameters: alkaline phosphatase, gamma glutamyl transferase,
      alanine aminotransferase, aspartate aminotransferase and total bilirubin in three times:
      before treatment begins, in the middle of treatment and at the end of treatment.
