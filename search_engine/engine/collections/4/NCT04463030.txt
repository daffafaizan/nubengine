
    
      A randomized, cross-over study design will be used to evaluate the effects of consumption of
      4 different doses of liposomal vitamin C (0, 1, 2, and 5 grams). The study is of 5 weeks'
      duration, with evaluation of a different dose at each of week 1, week 3 and week 5 with a one
      week washout between each dose.
