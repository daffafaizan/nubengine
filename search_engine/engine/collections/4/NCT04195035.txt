
    
      Randomized comparative trial that will be performed in Kasr Al Ainy hospital, Cairo
      University, Cairo, Egypt.ASA II patients from 20 to 50 years of age, undergoing minor surgry
      were included in this study. 40 patients will be allocated into 2 equal groups using
      computerized software and enclosed in sealed opaque envelopes:

      GQ ( n=20 ): Where Air-Q intubating laryngeal airway will be used for ventilation &
      intubation through fiberoptic bronchoscope.

      GA ( n=20 ): Where Ambu-Aura intubating laryngeal mask will be used for ventilation &
      intubation through fiberoptic bronchscope.
