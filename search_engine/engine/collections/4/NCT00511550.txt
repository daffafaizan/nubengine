
    
      This study establishes and evaluates a system to supply peripheral blood stem cell
      (PBSC)products for use in unrelated donor hematopoietic stem cell (HSC) transplantation. The
      protocol describes processes for donor identification, education and evaluation. Procedures
      for administration and monitoring of the stem cell mobilizing agent filgrastim are included.
      The study also describes procedures for the collection of PBSC products by leukapheresis and
      includes provisions for indefinite donor follow-up.
