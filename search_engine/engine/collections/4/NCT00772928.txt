
    
      This is a 2-staged study. Stage I of this study is designed to compare the immune responses
      elicited by an infant series (3 doses) of Pentacel™ when given at different times from or
      concurrently with Prevnar®.

      Stage II is designed to describe the immune responses elicited by a 4th dose of Pentacel™
      (all antigens) when given at different times from or concurrently with Prevnar®.
