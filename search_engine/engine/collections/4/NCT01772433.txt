
    
      This study will enroll 60 female patients suffering from chronic low back pain. the study
      will run in single blind mode that only patients will kept unaware of treatment.

      The duration of treatment is 10 days. patients will be assigned randomly to olive oil or
      placebo group. every day, they will take phonophoresis on lower back using phonophoresis gel
      as placebo or olive oil as treatment for 10 day. the questionnaires will be filled in
      baseline visit and day 10. any side effect of drugs or technique will be recorded
