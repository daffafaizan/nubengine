
    
      Eligibility criteria:

      Age 6 - 16 yrs

        -  IgE-mediated milk allergy

        -  and milk provocation within 3 months prior to or an documented accidental milk contact
           with clear immediate symptoms

      Outcome measures:

        -  number of patients with successful desensitization (195 - 200 ml milk per day)

        -  number of patients with adverse events
