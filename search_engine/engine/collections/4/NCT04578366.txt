
    
      This study will be a Randomized Controlled Trail conducted at Horizon hospital, Lahore. The
      study will be completed within the time duration of Six months. Consecutive sampling
      technique will be used to collect the data. The sample size of 44 patients will be taken in
      this study to find the effects of ESWT on pain, ROM, functional mobility. Patient will be
      divided into two groups. Group A will be treated with ESWT with conventional therapy and
      Group B will be treated with conventional therapy. Constant Shoulder Score (CSS) and the
      Oxford Shoulder Score (OSS) tools will be used to measure the outcomes of the study.

      One session of treatment per week for four weeks will be given. Pretest, posttest at 4th week
      and followup of 24th weeks will be evaluated.
