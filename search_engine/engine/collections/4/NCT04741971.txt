
    
      This is single blinded intervention study. The investigators will invite mother to
      participate in the project after giving birth. At week 0, mother who meet the eligibility
      criteria will be randomized in a single-blind study in a 1:1 ratio to probiotics with Vit. D3
      (five drops per day) or Vit. D3 (placebo) (single drop per day) for 90 days. The follow-up
      will be at the 1, 3 and 6 months all the subjects.
