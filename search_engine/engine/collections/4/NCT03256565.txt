
    
      All women with SUI and underwent Q-tip testing were reviewed. Patient characteristics, degree
      of prolapse determined by Pelvic Organ Prolapse Quantification system, Q-tip angle, the
      results of urodynamic studies, the Urinary Distress Inventory (UDI-6) Questionnaire and
      Incontinence Impact (IIQ-7) Questionnaire were reviewed from their medical records. Spearman
      correlation test, and univariate and multivariate backward stepwise linear regression
      analysis were performed as appropriate.
