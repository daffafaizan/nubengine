
    
      Patients who fulfill the international definition of unknown primary tumors can be included,
      except:

        -  Young men (<55 years) with growing mid-line tumors where a germ cell tumor could be
           expected will not be included.

        -  Patients with lymph-node metastases with squamous cell carcinoma in the head and neck
           region will not be included.

        -  Women with adenocarcinoma in lymph nodes in the axilla will not be included.
