
    
      In this randomized clinical trial , two groups will be included to compare the effectiveness
      of a treatment modality of botulinum toxin-A injection , in the first group , on gingival
      display during smiling ,patient satisfaction , lower face esthetics ( upper lip length ,
      upper lip vermillion length , interlabial gap , nasolabial angle) and the postoperative side
      effects . the comparator group will receive treatment by modified lip repositioning surgery
      ,to compare the results of the intervention group that can possibly obtained without
      undergoing surgery.
