
    
      This randomized, single-blind, controlled trial included 40 patients who are discharged after
      ACL reconstruction.

      The intervention group(n=20) was instructed and educated about 6 week home rehabilitation
      program using a wearable device(exoRehab)

      The controls(n=20) was instructed and educated about 6 week conventional home rehabilitation
      program

      Two weeks after surgery and six weeks after discharge, evaluation is performed.

      The purpose of this study is to develop and apply a rehabilitation program for patients who
      are discharged after ACL reconstruction using a wearable joint rehabilitation exercise device
      (exoRehab).
