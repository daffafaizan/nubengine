
    
      The patients undergoing spinal anesthesia for lower extremity surgery will be studied.
      Flurbiprofen axetil 100mg will be injected intravenously. CSF 1ml and blood 2ml will be
      obtained simultaneously every 5 minutes after intravenous injection in 50 min( T1~T10).CSF
      and blood samples will be used to detected S-flurbiprofen and R-flurbiprofen concentrations
      by using Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry. And the CSF/blood S-flurbiprofen and
      R-flurbiprofen concentrations ratio were calculated.
