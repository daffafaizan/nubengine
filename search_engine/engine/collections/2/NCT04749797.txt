
    
      Objective: To assess the effectiveness of analgesia by scalp nerve block with various agents
      in the first 72 hours following elective craniotomy.

      The investigators will employ a randomized, single-blinded, prospective study design.
      Participants will be randomized 1:1:1 into one of three treatment groups: Bupivacaine,
      Liposomal Bupivacaine, and Saline.
