
    
      This will be a single centre, three day, randomised, examiner blind, two treatment arm,
      parallel design, stratified (by maximum baseline Schiff sensitivity score of the two selected
      test teeth), controlled study, in participants with at least two sensitive teeth that meet
      all the criteria at the screening and baseline (pre-treatment) visits. Dentinal
      hypersensitivity will be assessed at baseline (pre-treatment), post-treatment and after 3
      days twice daily brushing
