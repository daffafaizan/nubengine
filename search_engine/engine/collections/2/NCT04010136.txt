
    
      The study consists of two phases:

        1. Randomized trial with General Practitioners (GP) to determine the role of two different
           training programs and a structured tool in the identification of geriatric patients with
           palliative care needs.

        2. Cross-sectional, analytical study of the prevalence and patterns of geriatric patients
           with Palliative Care (PC) needs, managed in primary care in Portugal's Center Healthcare
           Administrative Region.
