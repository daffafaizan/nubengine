
    
      Enrolled patinets will receive two cycles of Toripalimab(240 mg administered as an
      intravenous infusion over 30 minutes per 3 weeks), paclitaxel(135 mg/m2) and carboplatin(AUC
      5) administered as an intravenous infusion per 3 weeks. Giving the drugs on the first day of
      each cycle.
