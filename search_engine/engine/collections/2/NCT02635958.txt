
    
      This study will use a stratified design. Eligible participants will be categorized with BMI
      category to receive InBody followed by iDXA. A total of 176 participants will be placed in 1
      of 4 arms (n = 44 per arm) based on their BMI: 1) 18.5-24.9; 2) 25-29.9; 3) 30-34.9; and 4) ≥
      35. Within these arms, the 44 participants will participate in an InBody 770® body
      composition measure as well as an iDXA body composition measure.
