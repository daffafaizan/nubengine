
    
      The purpose of this feasibility study is to evaluate the safety and efficacy of the
      epicardial / endocardial combined procedure using the nContact Numeris®-AF Guided Coagulation
      System with VisiTrax® epicardially and the St. Jude Medical Therapy™ Cool Path™ Ablation
      Catheter endocardially to treat longstanding persistent Atrial Fibrillation (AF) patients.
