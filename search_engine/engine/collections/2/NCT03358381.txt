
    
      This is a multicenter, double-blined, randomized controled clinical trial. 98 patients from 2
      clinical center will be recruited with primary knee osteoarthritis, undergoing a primary TKA.
      The patients will be randomly divided into two groups , the measured resection group and the
      gap balance group.And the gap balance technique in this study will be achieved with a
      ligament tensor used during TKA. Femoral component rotation will be assessed from the
      preoperative and postoperative CT scans.
