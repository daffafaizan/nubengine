
    
      The aim of this study was to evaluate the effects of night sleep disorder and sleeplessness,
      excessive hours spent on flight and UV radiation exposure over ovarian function in flight
      attendant women.

      On the third day of the menstrual period, evaluate the number of antral follicle count will
      be examined and the level of hormones showing the ovarian functions will be measured in the
      blood sample given by the participant.
