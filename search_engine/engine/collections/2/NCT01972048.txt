
    
      Specific aims over the three-year period include the following: (1) The first year will be
      devoted to intervention and protocol development. A major emphasis in year 1 will be
      developing the community advisory board (CAB), conducting a series of pre-intervention focus
      groups, and working with pertinent persuasive technology consultants to develop and
      pilot-test the intervention. (2) The second year and the first half of the third year will
      focus on delivery of the intervention. (3) the second half of the third year will be devoted
      to data analysis and dissemination.
