
    
      A comparison of external cephalic (ECV) success rates when ECV performed with full urinary
      bladder or not. The study is prospective and randomised. Both nulliparous and multiparous
      women will be enrolled in the study, however each group will be randomized separately either
      to full urinary bladder or not. Written informed consent will be obtained prior to inclusion
      in the study.
