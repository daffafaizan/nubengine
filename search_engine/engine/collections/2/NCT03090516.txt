
    
      The assessment of cognitive scales included Minimum Mental State Examination(MMSE),
      Alzheimer's Disease Assessment Scale-cognition(ADAS-cog), Neuropsychiatric inventory(NPI),
      quality of Life of Life in Alzheimer's disease(Qol-ADL), activities of Daily living(ADL),
      Geriatric Depression scale(GDS).Biochemical test included cholesterol, triglycerides, and low
      density lipoprotein.
