
    
      STUDY DESIGN:

      Multicenter, prospective, randomized (1:1), controlled study comparing the artificial nucleus
      to ProDisc. After implanting artificial nucleus in two patients during the training session,
      enrollment of randomized patients shall begin. The study is expected to be completed in four
      years.

      NUMBER OF INVESTIGATIONAL SITES and INVESTIGATORS:

      10 - 20 investigational sites, 1 to 2 investigators per site, approximately 20-40 patients
      per site
