
    
      The aim of this study is to compare the effect of operator experience on the ability to use
      fiberoptic-guided intubation in children less than two years old, with and without the use of
      an air-Q as a conduit. The ease and time for placement of the air-Q, fiberoptic grade of
      laryngeal view, time for fiberoptic tracheal intubation, and peri-operative complications
      will also be assessed.
