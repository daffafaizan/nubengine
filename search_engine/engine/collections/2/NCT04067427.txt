
    
      To explore the potential of a daily app-based mindfulness mental training in improving AF
      symptoms, assessed by self-reported outcome measures, i.e. the AF 6 questionnaire, AF Effect
      on QualiTy of Life (AFEQT) and Patient Reported Outcome Measurement Information System
      (PROMIS) Global Health questionnaires, as well as in reducing AF burden in 7-day-Holter,
      within the first 12 weeks following AF ablation.
