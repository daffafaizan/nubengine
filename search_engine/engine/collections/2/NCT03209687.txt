
    
      The study will be conducted in the IVF unit of Cairo University. Females undergoing ICSI
      cycles in the age between 20 and 40 years will be allocated to one of 2 groups. Long agonist
      protocol will be used. Allocation to either one of 2 groups will be done on the day of
      oocytes retrieval. The first group will take (in addition to the routine care) daily human
      menopausal gonadotropin drug injection for 2 weeks. The second group will receive the routine
      care.
