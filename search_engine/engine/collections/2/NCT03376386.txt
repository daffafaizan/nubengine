
    
      If there is a complete metabolic response on the FDG-PET after 2 weeks, the entire tumor will
      receive 70 Gy. If there is a complete metabolic response after 4 weeks of treatment, the
      residual FDG-avid area will receive 74 Gy. If there also is no complete metabolic response
      after 4 weeks of treatment, the residual FDG-avid area will receive 78 Gy.
