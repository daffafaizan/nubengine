
    
      After laparoscopic gallbladder removal patients are randomized to have a suction drain
      positioned in the subhepatic space or to have a sham drain in the subhepatic space. The
      primary outcome measure will be the presence of subhepatic fluid collection at
      ultrasonographic examination on the first postoperative day. Secondary outcome measures will
      be postoperative abdominal and shoulder tip pain, use of analgesics, nausea, vomiting and
      morbidity
