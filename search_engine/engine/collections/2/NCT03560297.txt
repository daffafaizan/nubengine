
    
      This pilot study evaluates the impact of the SeRenade Parent-Child Music Class Program on
      families of children with and without autism spectrum disorders (ASD). This study examines
      the impact of this program on social development in children with ASD, parental stress and
      efficacy, and parent-child relationships. A primary goal of this study is to examine multiple
      levels of analysis including families' experience within and outside of the music class
      program.
