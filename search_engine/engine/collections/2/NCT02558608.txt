
    
      Wedge resection of the lung is usually used in the treatment of primary spontaneous
      pneumothorax. And the pleural treatment also might be used. But part of secondary
      pneumothorax patients who had undergone surgical, the bullae can be found especially in the
      apical of lung, even if the pleural have been treated in some way.This subject provides a new
      way of thinking and method to solve the problem of recurrent spontaneous pneumothorax.
