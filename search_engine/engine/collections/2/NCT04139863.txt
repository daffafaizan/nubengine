
    
      Adolescents (14-15-year-olds) are randomly divided to control group and test group. Chairside
      mouth rinse test for aMMP-8 is performed for the test group. Two groups are compared with
      each other.

      Participants are asked to fill a questionnaire which consists of questions concerning oral
      hygiene habits, tobacco products, alcohol, drug and snuff use.
