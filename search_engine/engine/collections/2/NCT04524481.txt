
    
      The aim of this study is to evaluate the prevalence of osteoporosis in adult patients with
      haemophilia. Subsequently, the ascertained prevalence of osteoporosis in patients with
      haemophilia will be compared to the latest published data of osteoporosis in Germany.
      Furthermore, the impact of severity of haemophilia, comorbidities as well as the role of both
      physical activity and individual joint condition as influencing factors on the presence of
      osteoporosis will be analysed by this study.
