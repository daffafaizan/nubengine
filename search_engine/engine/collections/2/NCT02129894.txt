
    
      Inclusion: All patients admitted to labor and delivery

      Consented patients who had a cesarean delivery will be randomized using a number table to
      receive or not receive an abdominal binder.

      Outcomes: On postoperative days 1 and 2, patients were asked to complete a pain visual analog
      scale and the validated Symptom Distress Scale. Postoperative hemoglobin and hematocrit were
      also recorded.
