
    
      This study is designed to explore the drug interaction between ketoconazole and ABT-869 to
      determine the potential effect of ketoconazole on the metabolism of ABT-869. ABT-869 will be
      taken alone or in combination with ketoconazole. The safety of a single dose administration
      of ABT-869 when administered alone and in combination with ketoconazole will be assessed.
