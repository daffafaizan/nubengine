
    
      Methods.46 maintenance hemodialysis patients will be given three stages of diuretic
      intervention: 100mg furosemide every day for 1 month, 200mg furosemide every day for 1 month,
      and 100mg furosemide combined with 25mg hydrochlorothiazide every day for 1 month. The
      24-hour urine volume, 24-hour urine sodium, 24-hour urine potassium, blood pressure,
      OH(overhydration) and dry weight will be collected. OH will be measured by bioelectrical
      impedance.
