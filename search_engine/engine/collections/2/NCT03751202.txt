
    
      A bioequivalence study of a single dose of the fixed-dose combination of fluticasone
      propionate and salmeterol xinafoate inhalation powder administered from Fluticasone
      propionate 500 mcg and Salmeterol xinafoate 50 mcg inhalation powder/Respirent
      Pharmaceuticals (test-Τ) as 2 inhalations and ADVAIR DISKUS® 500/50 mcg inhalation powder/GSK
      (reference-R) in healthy volunteers under fasting conditions. The study will be one-center
      crossover, randomized, 2-period, 2-sequence (RT and TR), single dose, laboratory-blinded.
