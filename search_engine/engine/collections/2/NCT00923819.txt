
    
      The study has two intervention arms:

      Experimental: Group A Surgery; laparoscopic gastric bypass

      Active comparator: Group B Patients from Child Obesity Registry of Vestfold who get standard
      conservative treatment in a multidisiplinary team at Morbid Obesity Center.

      All patients are referred to Morbid Obesity Center from other hospitals or general
      practionairs in Norway. The procedure will take place at the Vestfold Hospital Trust The
      participants are included for 2 years follow-up with visits to a multidisciplinary team every
      3rd month at the Morbid Obesity Center. Total follow-up time is ten years.
