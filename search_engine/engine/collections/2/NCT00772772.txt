
    
      Your participation in this study requires:

        -  4 visits to the outpatient clinic (including 1 screening visit)

        -  Providing a blood sample (less than 5 tablespoons) and a urine sample at each visit

        -  Taking a test to measure how leaky your gut is. This test requires that you drink a
           small amount of liquid (about 4 ounces) and then collect your urine for 6 hours after
           drinking the liquid.
