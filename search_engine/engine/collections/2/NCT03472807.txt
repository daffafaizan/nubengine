
    
      -Primary objective : Our aim is to identify new mutations and genes involved in paediatric
      cancer predisposition associating developmental delay by WES of a sub-cohort of patients
      included in the TED database.

      -Secondary objectives :

        1. Describe inherited predisposition to cancer.

        2. Improve genetic counselling processes.

        3. Initiate clinical exome sequencing in childhood cancer treatment.
