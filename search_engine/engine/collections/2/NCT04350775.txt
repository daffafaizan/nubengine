
    
      Subjects will be randomized to intervention group and control group.The primary outcome
      included eight measures, Canadian Occupational Performance Measure, Instrumental Activities
      of Daily Living-3 Dimensions and Activities of Daily Living Rating Scale III, Mini Mental
      State Examination-2nd edition, Participation Measure-3 Domains, 4 Dimensions, Work Behavior
      Inventory, Schizophrenia-Quality of Life questionnaire and Beck Depression Inventory-II.
