
    
      This study is a post surgical, data collection effort in order to gather definitive, Quality
      of Life(continence, impotence) information regarding the outcomes of patients who have
      undergone either Laparoscopic vs. Prostatectomy surgeries for Prostate Cancer. As part of the
      study, 200 subject's medical records will be retrospectively reviewed and 100 current
      subjects will be prospectively contacted. The information collected may benefit patients who
      undergo a prostatectomy
