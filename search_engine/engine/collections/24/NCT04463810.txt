
    
      Previous studies have indicated that chronic HIV infection has resulted the diastolic cardiac
      dysfunction. In this study, 500 long-term virologically controlled HIV-infected individuals
      will be followed for two years, and evaluated at 0, 24, 48 and 96 weeks of follow-up. At each
      visiting point, ultrasound evaluation of carotid and vertebral arteries and the heart
      function will be carried out and compared.
