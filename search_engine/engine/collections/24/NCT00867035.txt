
    
      Subjects in trial had oral and periodontal exam and scored the Ramfjord teeth(#3, 9, 12, 19,
      25, 28) for Plaque Index(PlI), Gingival Index(GI), probing depth (PD), recession. A tongue
      coating index is scored and sample of coating on dorsum taken for culture of total viable
      count and percentage of black sulfide-producing colonies on anaerobe agar with lead acetate
      added. Subjects given hygiene instruction and instruction on use of scraper, then rinse with
      20ml assigned mouthwash 30sec. Breath assessed by organoleptic means and mouth air sample
      concentration of hydrogen sulfide, methyl mercaptan in portable gas chromatograph at 0, 1, 2,
      and 4 hours. Subjects use scraper and mouthwash twice daily for one week and return for final
      exam.
