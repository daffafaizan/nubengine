
    
      Does comforting conversation during colonoscopy improve on patient satisfaction, compliance
      and pain management. A randomized controlled trial on comforting conversation or standard
      communication during colonoscopy. Using a mixed methods model, a qualitative study will be
      conducted to clarify what patients deem as important or effective conversation during
      colonoscopy. A randomized controlled trial will hereafter be conducted, where the results
      from the initial study will be utilized in the conversation during colonoscopy.
