
    
      This project intends to transplant human embryonic stem cells derived retinal pigment
      epitheliums into subretinal space of patients to treat dry age-related macular
      degeneration(dry-AMD). Through the statistical analysis EDTRS, BCVA, OCT, ERG, Fluorescein
      angiography, Ophthalmic AB ultrasound changes between before and after the treatment to
      assess the safety and efficacy of RPE transplants to treat dry AMD.
