
    
      This is a prospective observational study conducted in involutional ptosis patients, which
      will be submitted to instillation of a single drop of 10% phenylephrine in one eye(Group 1),
      2 drops (G2) or 4 drops ( G3).

      Video camera will record the images of both eyes before and after drug application at 3, 10,
      15 and 30 minutes. The images will be edited to analyze upper and lower lid height.
