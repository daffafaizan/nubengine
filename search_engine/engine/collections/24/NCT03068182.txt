
    
      A one hundred thirty male volunteers were equally and randomly assigned to; Group-I:
      Sixty-five participated in an arm crank moderate intensity exercise for 40 minutes. Group-II:
      Sixty-five participated in a treadmill moderate intensity exercise for 40 minutes. Halter
      heart rate monitor and automatic sphygmomanometer were used to measure heart beat intervals
      and arterial blood pressure respectively.
