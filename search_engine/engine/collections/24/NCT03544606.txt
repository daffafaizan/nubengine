
    
      Patient and methods

      Type of study:

      Prospective double blinded randomized placebo-controlled clinical trial.

      Study setting:

      The study will be conducted at Cairo university in labor ward of Kasr El Ainy Hospital.

      Study period:

      approximately 6 months from June 2018 to December 2018.

      Study population:

      Patients will be recruited in this study those attending labor ward at kasr el ainy hospital
      for induction of labor after 39 weeks of gestation.
