
    
      According to sample size calculation 37 diagnosed Multiple Sclerosis patients and 37 healthy
      individuals will be included.The demographic characteristics of the participants initially
      will be taken and Expanded Disability Status Scale score of those of MS will be recorded.
      Then, fatigue severity and perception, physical activity level, balance, peripheral muscle
      strength, functional mobility, functional exercise capacity will be evaluated. Primary
      outcomes are fatigue and physical activity level; secondary outcomes are balance, peripheral
      muscle strength, functional mobility, functional exercise capacity.
