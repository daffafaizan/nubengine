
    
      OUTLINE: Participants are randomized to 1 of 2 arms.

      ARM I: Participants use Actify app for smoking cessation for 8 weeks. Participants also
      receive motivational messages and smoking cessation information via text messages.

      ARM II: Participants use United States (US) Clinical Practice Guidelines (USCPG) app for
      smoking cessation for 8 weeks. Participants also receive motivational messages and smoking
      cessation information via text messages.

      After completion of study, participants are followed up at 6 months.
