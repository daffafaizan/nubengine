
    
      This is a prospective, non-randomized study of a convenience sample of Centura Health
      physicians.This study will not include any drugs or devices. The physician-oriented
      intervention will consist of a two-day weekend retreat that focuses on the application of
      CREATION Health principles in order to reduce burnout and perceived stress and improve
      well-being and empathy. There will be one four-hour session about each of the following
      CREATION Health principles: Choice, Trust, Interpersonal Relationships, and Outlook.
