
    
      The study is composed of eight successive visits. Each visit consists of the consumption of
      one individual oil or a mixture of oils (20 g of coconut oil or 10 g of coconut oil + 10 g of
      MCT 60-40 or 20 g MCT 60-40 or 10 g coconut oil + 10 g tricaprylin or 20 g tricaprylin or 10
      g coconut oil + 10 g triheptanoate or 20 g triheptanoate) and a 8h follow-up during which
      repeated blood samples are taken for laboratory analysis (plasma beta-hydroxybutyrate,
      acetoacetate, glucose, free fatty acids, triglycerides, total cholesterol, lactate and
      insulin).
