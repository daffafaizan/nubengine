
    
      A retrospective chart review was performed to examine a total of 162 patients received the
      same brand of implants for primary breast augmentation under sedative anesthesia (propofol
      infusion) in a single surgeon's practice. Asian patients' demographics, preoperative and
      postoperative measurements, surgical technique (single antibiotic breast irrigation), implant
      type, size, texture, soft tissue coverage, implant placement, incision approach,
      complications, and incidence of reoperation were documented.
