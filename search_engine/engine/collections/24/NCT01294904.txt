
    
      Renal elimination of hormones and peptides. By using a human transplantation model the
      investigators will study changes in hormonal levels before and after a successful
      transplantation up to 12 months. Hormones: testosterone, SHBG (sex hormone binding globulin),
      LH (luteinizing hormone), FSH (follicle stimulating hormone), estradiol and inhibin B.

      Analysis of sperm quality, concentration, motility, morphology and as well DNA-fragmentation
      index (DFI) in men with chronic kidney disease. In women the investigators will study the
      maturation of follicles via vaginal ultrasound. Controls are persons from a study at Dept of
      Reproductive Medicine.
