
    
      Patients with proved tracheobronchitis who are mechanically ventilated for more than 48 hrs,
      will undergo a CT scan to rool our pneumonia that may not be seen on a routine chest x-ray.

      After ruling out pneumonia by CT scan, patients will be randomized into 2 groups.
      Intervention group will receive 7 days of treatment with Piperacillin/Tazobactam. A control
      group will receive nothing.

      Primary endpoint wil be the rate of new pneumonias and secondary endpoint will be ICU Length
      of stay, days on mechanical ventilation and 28 day mortality.
