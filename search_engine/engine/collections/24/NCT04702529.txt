
    
      Soliton's Rapid Acoustic Pulse(RAP) device is an electrohydraulic (EH) device using Soliton's
      Rapid Acoustic Pulse (RAP) technology developed to minimize the appearance of scars through
      both micro-disruption of the scar matrix leading to scar remodeling and down regulation of
      the 'fibrotic fibroblasts'
