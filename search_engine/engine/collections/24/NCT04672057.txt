
    
      A total of 108 individuals within the age range of 20-65 and 36 of whom are right hemiparetic
      (Group1; 16F, 20M), 36 of whom are left hemiparetic (Group2; 16F, 20M) and 36 of whom are
      healthy (Group3; 19F, 17M) have been included in the study. BTS G-Walk Gait Analysis System
      has been used to assess the spatio-temporal gait characteristics.
