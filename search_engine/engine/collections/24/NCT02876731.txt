        -  repeat both exams systematically if one of the initial tests showed a suspicious or
           doubtful abnormality

        -  final diagnosis by a committee of practitioners using the whole patient's file

      Scheduled Project inclusion duration of 36 months (possible adaptation of duration in order
      to obtain the number of required patients)
