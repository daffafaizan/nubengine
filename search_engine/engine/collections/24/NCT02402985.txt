
    
      2x20 patients with Diabetes mellitus type 2 are randomise by a blind computer assist for an
      equal allocation in both groups (animal protein vs. plant protein). The food components for
      both groups are 40% carbohydrates, 30% fat and 30% protein. The carbohydrates are correspond
      with a high fiber content and a low blood sugar effectiveness of carbohydrates (glycemic
      index).
