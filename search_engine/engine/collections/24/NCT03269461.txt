
    
      The first 20 patients enrolled will be evaluated by angiography and Optical Coherence
      Tomography 3 months after stent implantation. The following 20 patients will be evaluated by
      the same methods 2 months after the implantation and the last 20 patients after 30 days.

      All patients will be clinically followed at 30 days, 6 months, 1 and 2 years after index
      procedure.
