
    
      This is a non-interventional, retrospective study of participants with CD and CPF in a
      clinical practice setting.

      The study will enroll approximately 100 participants.

      The study will have a retrospective data collection to select and analyze information from
      EMRs processed by an AI based analytics framework that uses machine learning and NLP
      methodologies.

      The study will be structured in two sequential phases: Phase 1 (validation process) and Phase
      2 (data collection and NLP). All participants will be enrolled in one observational group.
