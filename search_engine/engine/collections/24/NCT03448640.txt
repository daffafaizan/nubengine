
    
      Purpose: To determine effect and vital change after topical application of phenylephrine 2.5%
      and tropicamide 0.5% three times for retinopathy of prematurity (ROP) examination in preterm
      infants.

      Methods: Pupillary diameter, blood pressure, pulse rate, and oxygen saturation were monitored
      before, and after up to 24 hours 60 ROP screening examinations.
