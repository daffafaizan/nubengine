
    
      Subjects will undergo four euglycaemic clamp procedures in order to determine the extent of
      glucose disposal. The first clamp will be performed prior to the commencement of the first
      study drug administration, the second one following two weeks of study drug, the third after
      a two week washout period, prior to commencement of second study drug administration and the
      fourth after two weeks of the second study drug
