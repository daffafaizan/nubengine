
    
      This study aimed to compare 3 doses of ocytocin in bolus injection and contnious perfusion
      during C section. these doses determined by: high, intermediate and low doses, were
      administred to parturients during elective or urgent C section under spinal anesthesia.

      The aim was to determine the lowest efficient dose to prevent post partum hemorrage and with
      the lowest incidence of maternal side effects.
