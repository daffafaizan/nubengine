
    
      Study center:

      A total of 50 subjects were to be included in 1 site in Iceland.

      Methodology:

        -  Group Loceryl Nail Lacquer+ Cosmetic Varnish:

             -  Loceryl Nail Lacquer to be applied once weekly for 12 weeks on all affected
                toenails,

             -  Cosmetic varnish to be applied once weekly for 12 weeks on all affected toenails
                and/or all toenails.

        -  Group Loceryl Nail Lacquer alone:

           - Loceryl Nail Lacquer to be applied once weekly for 12 weeks on all affected toenails.

        -  All eligible subejct with no clinical sign of clinical aggravation of Onychomycosis at
           week 12, could continue to apply Loceryl Nail Lacquer alone, once a week for 15
           additional months (or less in case of complete cure).
