
    
      This study aims to develop an adjunctive novel treatment intervention using yoga in opioid
      agonist maintained patients to effectively treat the co-occurring disorders of non-malignant
      chronic pain and opioid dependence (POD).

      Specific Aims:

      1) To conduct a pre-pilot phase with 8 patients with POD receiving opioid agonist maintenance
      treatment.
