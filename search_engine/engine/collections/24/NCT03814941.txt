
    
      At first, the authors evaluated the accuracy of the traditional, manually completed
      paper-based documentation, which sets a benchmark for the accuracy that the electronic
      documentation and database provides.

      At the second portion, the investigators used the paper-based documentation as a standard to
      compare the electronic documentation. The incidence of artifact was double checked by two
      investigators.

      At the third portion, the authors calculated the artifact incidence in the AIMS database,
      using paper based and electric document anesthesia records as the standard respectively.
