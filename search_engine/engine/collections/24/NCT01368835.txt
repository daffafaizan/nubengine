
    
      The primary objective of this study is to demonstrate the effectiveness of the Ulthera™
      System for the non-invasive dermatological treatment to obtain lift and tighten the cheek
      tissue.

      The secondary objective of this study is to demonstrate the effectiveness of the Ulthera™
      System for the non-invasive dermatological treatment for improvement in jawline definition
      and submental skin laxity at 3 and 6 months compared to baseline based on the consensus of
      the three masked reviewers.
