
    
      In Part A, healthy participants were randomized to receive a single dose application of
      BOS-475 or vehicle cream. Following a washout period, participants received 7 days of
      repeated dosing with BOS-475 or vehicle cream on the back, followed by 7 days of repeated
      dosing of BOS-475 or vehicle cream over the facial area (excluding eye lids and areas around
      the mouth).

      In Part B, participants with mild to moderate stable plaque psoriasis affecting up to 15% of
      their body surface area were randomized to receive 6 weeks of topical treatment of BOS-475 or
      vehicle cream.
