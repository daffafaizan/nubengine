
    
      Seal-G and Seal-G MIST Surgical Sealants are intended for the reinforcement and protection of
      gastrointestinal anastomosis. The Seal-G and Seal-G MIST devices share the same Alginate
      based sealant technology and intended purpose. The devices only differ in their method of
      application, The Seal-G device is designed for spread application by extra-corporal approach,
      and the Seal-G MIST device is designed for spray application by intra-corporal approach.

      The proposed Clinical Investigation aims to collect clinical evidence on the devices' usage,
      including safety, performance and usability.
