
    
      This study will collect perioperative pain-related data of elderly patients in multi-centers,
      including: preoperative general data, intraoperative anesthesia and surgical data, clinical
      laboratory data, postoperative pain and intervention, postoperative complications and quality
      of life. Analyzing these collected data to find the high-risk factors of postoperative pain
      in elderly patients and to establish an early-warning model of perioperative pain in elderly
      patients, so as to improve the ability of assessing the risks of postoperative pain in
      elderly patients and providing an early warning.
