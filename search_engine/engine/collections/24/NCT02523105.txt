
    
      The study will be conducted in two arms:

      In arm I, participants diagnosed with depression. In arm II, healthy participants.

      Arm I - Participants that need to start or switch their pharmacological antidepressant
      therapy will be recruited. Their clinical status will be evaluated and their EEG
      characteristics will be collected.

      Arm II - Clinical and EEG characteristics of Healthy volunteers will collected on a similar
      timetable.
