
    
      There are three group with 10 participants each one. The group one will do hamstring
      eccentric exercise 12 series per 6 repeats. The group two will do three proprioception
      exercise 2 series per exercise and leg. And third group won't have add some exercise to their
      normal training.

      All participants will do the exercises during 12 weeks. Before this 12 weeks they will be
      valuation with different tests, and this tests will have to repeat after this 12 weeks and 3
      weeks after finish the exercises.
