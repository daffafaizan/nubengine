
    
      This study comprised 2 major stages: 1) Cross-cultural adaptation and 2) psychometric
      properties testing.

      Cross-cultural adaptation stage:

      The FRI was cross-culturally adapted to Thai following the well-accepted guideline for the
      process of cross-cultural adaptation of self-report measure.

      Psychometric properties testing stage:

      The patients with back or neck pain completed the questionnaires at the first visit, the
      second visit and at 2-week (or at discharge or when reporting 80% improvement from the first
      visit).
