
    
      This is a multicenter, prospective and randomized trial. Objective: To determine if the Chest
      physiotherapy (CP) associated with conventional medical treatment (CT) improves functional
      sequelae secondary to pleural infectious disease, defined as an increased of 15% in Vital
      Functional Capacity (VFC). Adult patients with diagnosis of pleural infection will be
      included and randomized into two branches: control group - only CT and interventional one -
      CT plus CP.
