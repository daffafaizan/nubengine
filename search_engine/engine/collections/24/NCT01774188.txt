
    
      Select 40 cases of patients (80eyes) who had counter pulsation treatment at Enhanced External
      Counter Pulsation (EECP) Specialist in First Affiliated Hospital of Sun Yat-sen University
      during Apr 2012 - Mar 2013. Separated the patients into 2 groups according to having glaucoma
      history or not.The cases whose eyes have erythralgia and who have received corneal surgery or
      intraocular surgery in recent one month are excluded.
