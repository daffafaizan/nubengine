
    
      Every center which has patient at risk can participate. Main inclusion criteria : French
      childhood cancer survivor (5 years or more of delay with the treatment) - becoming adult -
      with a past history of radiotherapy at the age of 20 years or before, with a significant dose
      irradiation on the thyroid or the breast

      The inclusion of patients are validated by one team

      Thyroid screening programme is based on US each 2 years Breast screening programme is based
      mainly on MRI each year
