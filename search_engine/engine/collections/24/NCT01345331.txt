
    
      The Brigham and Women's Pain Management Center is conducting a research study for chronic
      pelvic pain patients due to endometriosis. The study will assess the effect of
      electro-stimulation for the treatment of Chronic Pelvic Pain. You may be eligible if you have
      chronic pelvic pain due to endometriosis, are between the ages of 21- 64, and not taking
      opioid medication (such as morphine or oxycodone). The study will require two visits, each
      lasting about 2 hours. The study takes place at the Brigham & Women's Pain Management Center,
      850 Boylston St., Chestnut Hill. Please contact Chrissie Cahalan at 617-732-9014 or
      ccahalan1@partners.org if interested.

      Vitaly Napadow, Ph.D., Lic.Ac. Assistant Professor Dept. of Radiology, Massachusetts General
      Hospital, Harvard Medical School
