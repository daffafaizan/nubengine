
    
      This walnut oral immunotherapy (OIT) study is a randomized, blinded, placebo controlled study
      based on previous experience at Arkansas Children's Hospital Research Institute (ACHRI) using
      OIT in food allergic subjects. The study will enroll 30 (20 in the active treatment and 10 in
      the placebo treatment arms) children and adults with walnut allergy and a second tree nut
      allergy.
