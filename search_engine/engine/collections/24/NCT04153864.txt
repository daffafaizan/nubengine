
    
      Eligible participants will be randomly assigned to the same BA treatment for perinatal
      depressive and anxiety symptoms in one of four arms: 1) In-person specialist; 2) Telemedicine
      specialist; 3) In-person non-specialist; 4) Telemedicine non-specialist. For the duration of
      COVID-19 pandemic period, participants were only randomized to 1) Telemedicine
      non-specialist; 2) Telemedicine specialist.
