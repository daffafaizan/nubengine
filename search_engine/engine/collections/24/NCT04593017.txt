
    
      To investigate the short term effect of probiotic oil on plaque acidogenicity among
      orthodontic patients and to investigate the effect of probiotics on mutan streptococci and
      lactobacilli in plaque and saliva.

      The null-hypothesis is that: The effect of probiotics would not differ from placebo-treated
      control group.

      This product might decrease the chance of getting caries but also cooperation and cost
      effectiveness should be considered
