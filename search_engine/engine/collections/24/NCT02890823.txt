
    
      A single-blinded prospective, randomized study undertaken at epilepsy clinic of King
      Chulalongkorn Memorial Hospital. The patients with hypovitaminosis D were included and
      divided into two groups according to the type of AEDs use. Patients receiving each AEDs type
      were randomly assigned to receive vitamin D3 1000, 3000 or 6000 IU once daily. The mean
      increment in serum 25(OH)D levels were measured at 8 and 16 weeks.
