
    
      Capsimax™ is a capsicum extract in a bead technology to avoid oral and gastric burning.
      Capsimax™ 2mg, Capsimax™ 4 mg and placebo was initially given as a single dose on an empty
      stomach with measures of metabolic rate, and satiety before and after and food intake at 1
      hour after Capsimax™.
