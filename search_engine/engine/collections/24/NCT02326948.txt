
    
      In order to estimate region-specific risk factors for gallbladder cancer (GBC) in Jeju
      island, Korea the investigators performed a case-control study. Among 124 GBC patients and
      7088 health controls, the investigators randomly selected 78 cases and 78 age-sex matched
      controls (1:1 matching) and compared their demographic features, combined diseases, and
      personal histories about known risk factors until now.
