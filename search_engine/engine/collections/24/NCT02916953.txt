
    
      This research study will be conducted as a post-CE Mark prospective multi-center study within
      Europe. In total 60 patients will be treated with the OSSURE LOEP kit.

      Follow-up visits will be conducted ten (10) and forty-two (42) days after the procedure. In
      addition, all subjects will have the option to consent for a long term follow-up evaluation
      at twelve (12) months post-procedure.
