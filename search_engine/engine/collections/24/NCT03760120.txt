
    
      Investigational device: PEP-MASK=Positive Expiratory Pressure delivered through a face mask.

      Standard Operating Procedures used for evaluating CF lung disease and ventilation
      inhomogeneity.

      Sample size: Based on preliminary data collected on seven subjects who performed nitrogen
      multiple-breath washout test before and after PEP-MASK, assuming a variation of 20% of Sacin,
      the study requires a sample size of 18 subjects.
