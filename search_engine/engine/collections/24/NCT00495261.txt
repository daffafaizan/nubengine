
    
      Hypothesis The mindfulness-enhanced CBT (MCBT) will report significant reductions in the
      frequency of gambling, gambling urges, gambling cognitions and gambling-related expenditures
      compared to the relaxation-enhanced CBT (RCBT) at end-of-treatment and at the 3-month
      follow-up.

      Significance

        1. feasibility of mindfulness interventions for problem gamblers

        2. tentative data for the effectiveness of the intervention

        3. an additional clinical tool to treat problem gamblers

        4. basis of a larger, grant-funded study obtain more definitive data
