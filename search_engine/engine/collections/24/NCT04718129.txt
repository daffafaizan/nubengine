
    
      This is a randomized controlled trial testing an 8-session, coached, app-based mindfulness
      intervention on adolescents' reports of affect as measured with ecological momentary
      assessment (EMA). Participants will include 120 youth ages 12- to 17-years-old randomized to
      either the Youth Mindful Awareness Program (YMAP) or to an assessment only control condition.
      Participation is open to all genders and youth of all races and ethnicities.
