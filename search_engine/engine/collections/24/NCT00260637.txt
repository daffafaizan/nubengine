
    
      Various studies have reported that this acupuncture able to relieve symptoms of chronic
      prostatitis (CP)/ chronic pelvic pain syndrome (CPPS). However, controversy exists on the
      efficacy of this technique due to poor experimental designs and lack of placebo controls in
      these experiment. This study is the first randomised controlled study which will evaluate if
      acupuncture for 10 weeks will help improve symptoms of CP/CPPS
