
    
      This study will compare the need for analgesia (Hydromorphone PCA) of two groups of patients
      post-VATS. The placebo group will only have a PCA for post-operative analgesia, witch is the
      standard care for patient post-VATS. The experimental group will have a intrathecal injection
      of a mix of morphine and sufentanil before the induction of anesthesia.
