
    
      This study is a multicenter, prospective, randomized parallel arm, investigator initiated
      trial.

      The aim of study is to compare oral sulfate solution (OSS) with 2L polyethylene glycol (PEG)
      with ascorbic acid (Asc) for bowel preparation in elderly people.

      99 participants will be enrolled in each group (198 in total). The primary outcome is the
      efficacy of bowel preparation. Secondary outcomes are tolerability, satisfaction, and safety.
