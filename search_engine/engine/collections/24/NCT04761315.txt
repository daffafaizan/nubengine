
    
      The presence of interatrial block (IAB) is associated with the development of atrial
      fibrillation (AF). The aim of this study was to determine whether P-wave duration and
      presence of IAB before the implantation of a cardiac implantable electronic device (CIED) are
      associated with the presence of atrial high rate episodes (AHRE), during long-term follow-up.
