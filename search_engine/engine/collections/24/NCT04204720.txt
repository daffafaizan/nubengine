
    
      A total of 164 caudal epidural injections were performed in patients with disc herniation or
      spinal stenosis on lumbosacral region. Patients were randomly allocated to Group Whitacre
      (n=82) and Group Chiba (n=82). Patients in Group Whitacre received caudal epidural injection
      using Whitacre type needle and those in Group Chiba received the procedure using Chiba type
      needle. Intravascular injection was assessed with blood aspiration and angiography during
      real-time fluoroscopy.
