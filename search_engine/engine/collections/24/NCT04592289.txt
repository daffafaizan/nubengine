
    
      The design involves random allocation of eligible patients to full bowel preparation or only
      mechanical bowel preparation in 1:1 ratio. After that rectal resection is performed in both
      groups.

      Short-term outcomes are assessed in 30 day period after surgery. This is a superiority trial
      evaluating statistical superiority. Rate of surgical site infection is anticipated to
      decrease from 12% (data from local registry) to 6%. For power of 80% enrolment of 622
      patients is required.

      The intent-to-treat principle is used for the data analysis.
