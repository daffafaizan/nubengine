
    
      The VergenixTM STR device is an advanced Soft tissue repair device, made of lyophilized Type
      I rhCollagen and Calcium Chloride that will be mixed with autologous PRP prior to injection
      for the repair of non ruptured tendon injuries (Tendinopathy). Three (3) ml of the mixed
      solution will be injected into the common extensor tendon.
