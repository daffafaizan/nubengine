
    
      The overweight and obese primary school students from 1-5 grades will be recruited according
      to "Body Mass Index Reference for Screening Overweight and Obesity in Chinese School-age
      Children". The participants with will be allocated with schools as the unit to four groups:
      tailored intervention based on monitoring physical activity and dietary behaviors, exercise
      intervention group based on the Happy 10 program, nutrition education intervention group. The
      three intervention groups received the designed interventions for the duration of 1 year.
      Before and after the intervention, we will carry out a questionnaire survey, a physical
      measurement, a fitness test, and a metabolic markers detection
