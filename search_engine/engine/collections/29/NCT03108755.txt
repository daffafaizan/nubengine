
    
      There will be a residential period for Parts 1 and 2. Part 1: Eligible participants will be
      residential for a single period of 5 days and 4 nights. Participants will be discharged on
      day 4 on the condition that all required assessments have been performed and that there are
      no medical reason for a longer stay in the clinical unit.

      Part 2: Eligible participants will be residential for a single period of 18 days and 17
      nights. Participants will be discharged on day 17 on the condition that there are no medical
      reason for a longer stay in the clinical unit.
