
    
      Pre-donation sampling pouch of fresh and stored frozen plasma collected within the last 5
      years will be tested for HEV IgG serology at the National Reference Laboratory of the Blood
      Transfusion Services SRC in Berne Collected data will be used to determine HEV
      seroprevalence, actual and in the last 5 years, in the Canton Ticino and to investigate trend
      and pattern of the HEV infection (seroprevalence by different regional areas, comparison with
      national HEV data, and correlation with age, sex and place of residence).
