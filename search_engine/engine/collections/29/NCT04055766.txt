
    
      The doctor completes the admission note within 24 hours after the patient is admitted to the
      hospital. After informed consent is given to the patient who meet the inclusion criteria,
      open the DeepDoc to fill in the patient identification information. Then take the picture of
      the initial diagnosis of the admission note page and upload it to DeepDoc, fill the doctor's
      initial diagnosis into the DeppDoc. And put the patient's clinical data points into the
      DeepDoc to get a DeepdDoc diagnosis.
