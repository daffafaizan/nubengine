
    
      The continuous nalbuphine infusion dose starts at 100ug/kg/h, if the BPS scores reaches 3-4,
      the continuous infusion dose would be decreased by 20 ug/kg/h for next patients. If not,
      increased by 20 ug/kg/h.

      The nalbuphine bolus is injected 5 min before turning the patients and the dose (starting at
      0) is increased by 0.05 mg/kg, prior to each subsequent turn to lateral decubitus until a BPS
      score of 3-4 is obtained.
