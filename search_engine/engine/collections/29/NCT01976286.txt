
    
      Participants in this study will be patients who are clinically diagnosed with at least a 2 x
      2 cm patch of melasma on each side of their face (forehead or cheek). One half of the
      subject's face will be randomly selected to receive 4 treatments of 30% glycolic acid peels
      and the other half of the face will receive 4 treatments of 30% salicylic acid peels/ weeks
      0, 4, 8, and 12. Follow up visit will be at week 16. This study is a pilot study designed to
      determine feasibility of these procedures.
