
    
      Aims of the study are to compare the impacts of two short 7-day contrasted nutritional
      interventions (the Mediterranean diet (MED) and the Canadian diet (CAN)) on blood metabolites
      (endocannabinoids) after a maximal aerobic test. A 3 weeks washout between the two diets is
      planned. Gut microbiome at each test will also be evaluated.
