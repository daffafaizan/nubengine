
    
      This is a prospective, multi-center, four-arm study designed to evaluate the clinical and
      radiographic outcomes in patients diagnosed with cervical degenerative disc disease (DDD) and
      treated using the Vectra-T Cervical Plate and the Advanced ACF or CorticoCancellous Allograft
      Spacer made by Synthes Spine Company (Paoli, PA). This is not an investigational study (IDE)
      and no investigational or experimental devices or procedures are included in this study.
