
    
      The aim of the study is to evaluate the quality of follow-up and treatment by means of
      consecutive data collection and anonymised analysis of all glaucoma patients of a tertiary
      university hospital glaucoma service.

      Parameters being analyzed are:

      Age, sex, refraction, family history, known duration of glaucoma, highest intraocular
      pressure before treatment, systemic and topical medication, previous ocular surgery.

      24h Goldmann-applanation tonometry, biomicroscopy, gonioscopy, Heidelberg Retinal Tomograph
      (HRT), glaucoma diagnosis with Scanning-Laser-Polarimetry (GDx), optical coherence tomography
      (OCT), Pentacam, Ocular Response Analyzer (ORA), Corvis ST (Corneal Visualization with the
      Scheimpflug Technology), perimetry
