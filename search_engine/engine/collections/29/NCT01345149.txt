
    
      Obese pregnant women with a BMI>30 are randomized to Diet + Exercise, Exercise or control
      (1:1:1) in order to reduce maternal weight gain to less than 5 kg during pregnancy.

      The effect on the children of mothers in this study is evaluated in the study: Is it possible
      to reduce the Number of Macrosomic Infants With Abundant Fat Tissue by Lifestyle Intervention
      during Pregnancy (NCT01235676)
