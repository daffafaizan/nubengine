
    
      The study was conducted in two government maternity hospitals in Lucknow. Neonates enrolled
      in the pre intervention phase were followed up for 6 weeks+15 days but did not recieve the
      study intervention. While, the mothers of the neonates enrolled in the post intervention
      phase recieved the study intervention within 48 hours of institutional delivery.Enrollment
      and follow-up procedures were same in both the phases.
