
    
      In this study, 54 patients (18 of them RA, 18 of them PsA and, 18 of them asymptomatic
      healthy group) were included. The patient group was selected from the patients who came to
      the Izmir Katip Celebi University Ataturk Training and Research Hospital Rheumatology
      Outpatient Clinic for routine control.

      Participants were matched according to age and gender. It was recorded demographic and
      clinical data.

      Grip and pinch strengths were evaluated within the scope of hand function evaluation. Wrist
      joint position sense was evaluated along with hand functions.
