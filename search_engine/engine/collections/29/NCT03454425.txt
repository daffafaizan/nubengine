
    
      A Prospective, Randomized, Sham Controlled Study to Evaluate the Safety and Efficacy of
      ExAblate Subthalomotomy for the treatment of Parkinson's disease motor features.

      The objective of this prospective, randomized, double-blind (to subjects, examining
      neurologists and external video-based examination by Movement Disorders neurologist), two-arm
      study (ExAblate treated arm Vs ExAblate Sham treated control arm) is to confirm the efficacy
      of ExAblate Model 4000 Type 1 System for the treatment of Parkinson's disease (PD) motor
      features and to further demonstrate safety.
