
    
      Effective treatments for severe drug addiction are limited. China centers employing
      compulsory detoxification, reported a 3 year abstinence rate of only 15%. For the methadone
      maintenance therapy, relapse often occurs promptly after cessation of methadone. A Malaysian
      reports about taking naltrexone or buprenorphine treating,showed average relapsed time of 2
      or 3 months,respectively.

      Assessment for psychological craving degree of addiction patients is also a difficult thing.
