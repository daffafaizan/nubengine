
    
      Nutrition therapy of inflammatory bowel disease should follow the principle of sequential
      treatment of nutrition, which is the preferred scheme with enteral nutrition, from slow to
      fast, from weak to strong, from the elements to the whole protein on the steps of the gradual
      transition, in order to improve enteral nutrition in patients with tolerance and compliance
      and improve patient quality of life and long-term survival rate, reduce the social burden of
      disease, patients with good economic and social benefits.
