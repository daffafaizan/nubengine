
    
      A total of 90 patients who were diagnosed as unilateral vestibular weakness were included in
      this prospective randomized controlled study. They were randomized into three groups each
      consisting of 30 patients. Home based vestibular rehabilitation program was given to all
      groups. Fall risk yielded by posturography, Berg balance scale, Timed Up-and-Go test, vertigo
      visual analogue scale and Dizziness Handicap Inventory were used for comparison.
