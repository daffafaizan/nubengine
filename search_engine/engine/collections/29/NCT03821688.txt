
    
      150 morbidly obese patients divided into to equal groups, group A underwent single
      anastomosis sleeve jejunal bypass with biliary limb length of 200 cm from the
      duodenal-jejunal flexure and group B underwent mini gastric bypass, group C underwent sleeve
      gastrectomy. All patients were followed for at least one year. all cases were evaluated as
      regard BMI, complications, nutritional status and obesity-related comorbidities.
