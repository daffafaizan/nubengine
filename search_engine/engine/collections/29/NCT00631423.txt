
    
      All Patients are assessed using a standardized questionnaire and details concerning VTE risk
      factors, concomitant disease, medical conditions, diagnosis and therapy of IVC thrombosis
      were registered. A blood sample is taken to test for inherited and acquired thrombophilia. In
      addition, duplex ultrasound examination and digital photoplethysmograph are performed to
      assess postthrombotic syndrome.
