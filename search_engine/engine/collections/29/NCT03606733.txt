
    
      A custom made 30 gauge needle offering 1000 micron penetration of the sclera at the parsplana
      with the help of gentle pressure on the sclera will help to inject medication such as
      triamcinilone or VEGF blockade agents (Ziv aflibercept or Bevacizumab) in the potential
      Suprachoroidal space which offers direct effect of injected drug on the retina and choroid
      sparing both crystalline lens, trabecular meshwork and other ocular tissues, hence improving
      efficacy of the drug upto six times and reduce potential complications such as cataract and
      glaucoma.
