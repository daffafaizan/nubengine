
    
      Background. Child Overweight and obesity are a world public health problem, in low income
      countries is more. One difficulty in the management is the poor adherence to physical
      activity, some studies show that active video games could improve this.

      Objective: Establish the effect of the introduction of active video game (Nintendo Wii) on
      fitness, anthropometry and adherence to treatment, as part of an intervention program for the
      management of childhood obesity in children over 5 years of age with obesity Methodology: A
      clinical trial, randomized and controlled. children between 5-10 years with a diagnosis of
      overweight or obese, were divided into two groups. (Wii group), received 3 weekly sessions of
      one hour of physical activity by an active video game, the other group (control group)
      received 3 weekly sessions of one hour of physical activity by static exercises.
