
    
      150 patients were enrolled in this study and only 97 patients were finally analyzed for
      primary outcomes measures of Oswestry disability index and Roland-Morris Questionnaire and
      secondary outcome measures of Visual analogue scale for back pain and leg pain as well as
      length of hospital stay and time to return for daily activities. Assessments done at
      different periods of 2 weeks, 3 months, 6 months, 1year, 2 years and 3 years.
