
    
      This feasibility study of a randomized controlled trial, aims to evaluate the feasibility of
      a 12-week sleep program consisting on aerobic exercise and cognitive-behavioral therapy. In
      this study the investigators wish to evaluate the program as well as the randomized
      controlled trial research procedure.

      In total 20 participants will be recruited and randomized to either the intervention group
      (n=10) or control group (n=10).The intervention group will participate in two exercise
      sessions each week and four sessions based on cognitive-behavioral therapy for insomnia.
