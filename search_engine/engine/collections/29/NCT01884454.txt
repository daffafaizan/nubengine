
    
      In this study will be recruited volunteers 25-50 years of age with a body mass index (BMI)
      20-25 kg/m2 (normal) that after performing polysomnography, will be distributed in the
      following groups: healthy or diagnosed with obstructive sleep apnea syndrome (OSA).
      Volunteers without OSA will be submitted to total sleep deprivation (SD) or selective REM
      sleep deprivation for 48 hours. After SD, these volunteers will be allowed to sleep for 48
      hours (period rebound). Blood samples will be taken for hormonal analysis (testosterone,
      luteinizing hormone, follicle-stimulating hormone, progesterone and cortisol)and gene
      expression. The semen collection for evaluation of sperm count (concentration, viability and
      sperm morphology) in three different periods, baseline collection, after SD protocols and the
      end of rebound period. Individuals diagnosed with OSA also have three collections of blood
      and semen samples for analyzes.
