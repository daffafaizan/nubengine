
    
      Men and women with elevated waist circumference aged between 18-75 yrs will be recruited from
      the Winnipeg (Manitoba, Canada) area to participate in a two arm crossover, randomized study;
      each treatment period will be 4 weeks in length, with a 4 week washout period separating each
      treatment. Participants will be randomized to consume either i) almonds, or ii) omelettes at
      15% of energy. Each of the treatment products will contain equal levels of calories.
