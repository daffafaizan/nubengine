
    
      The secondary objectives of this study are:

        -  Determine whether the current recommended doses of caspofungin achieve the
           Pharmacokinetic/Pharmacodynamic (PK/PD) target for this molecule.

        -  Determine whether the current recommended doses of micafungin achieve the PK / PD target
           for this molecule

        -  Describe the peritoneal concentrations of echinocandins in secondary peritonitis
           complicated with septic shock

        -  Identify via modeling PK / PD parameters and based on monte Carlo simulations the
           optimal dosing regimen for caspofungin and micafungin in this population
