
    
      Descriptive explorative study of three population showing sicca symptoms (Systemic sclerosis,
      Gougerot-Sjogren syndrome, Sicca-Asthenia-Polyalgia syndrome).

      Patient will be enrolled in the rheumatology department of University hospital of
      Clermont-Ferrand (France).

      Salivary glands ultrasonography will be performed on all patients enrolled by the same expert
      and blinded to the diagnosis of the patient. Echostructure of the two salivary glands will be
      graded 0 to 4 and the size calculated.
