
    
      In this study, 912 cases of ischemic stroke in 48 hours were included in 5 centers in China
      according to the principle of random, double-blind and parallel control .The experimental
      group receive basic treatment and remote ischemic conditioning for 200mmHg, 2 times per day
      for 7 consecutive days .The control group receive basic treatment and remote ischemic
      conditioning control for 60mmHg, 2 times per day, 7 days in a row . Two groups will be
      followed up for 90 days to evaluate the efficacy and safety of remote ischemic conditioning
      therapy.
