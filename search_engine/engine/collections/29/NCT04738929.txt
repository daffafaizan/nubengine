
    
      Twenty male subjects (10 normal weight; 10 obese) consumed breakfast meals containing skimmed
      milk, fat-free white cheese, bread and EVCO (25 g) or EVOO (25 g). Visual analog scale
      evaluations, resting metabolic rate measurements and selected blood parameters analysis
      (glucose, triglyceride, insulin and plasma peptide YY) were performed before and after the
      test breakfast meals. In addition, energy intakes were evaluated by ad libitum lunch meal at
      180 min.
