
    
      The investigators target the patients with perennial allergic rhinitis. After treatment in 2
      groups (Cettum treatment and acupuncture treatment) the investigators will compare the
      effects of relieving symptoms and improving quality of life. So the investigators will
      confirm the efficacy of Cettum, and further the best treatment method. In addition, the
      investigators are going to evaluate the safety of abnormal reaction during the treatment.
