
    
      AMG 386 is a medication made to stop the growth of blood vessels in cancer tissues. Cancer
      relies on new blood vessels to bring it oxygen and nutrients to grow. Docetaxel is currently
      approved by the FDA for advanced urothelial, hormone-refractory prostate, breast, non-small
      cell lung, gastric, and squamous cell carcinoma of the head and neck.
