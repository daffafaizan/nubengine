
    
      The objectives of the study were to:

        1. To study knowledge and perceptions regarding JSSK and RBSK programs in the
           patients/patient attendants, nursing students and in-service nurses attending LHMC and
           associated hospitals.

        2. To compare knowledge and perceptions regarding JSSK and RBSK programs in the
           patients/patient attendants, nursing students and in-service nurses attending LHMC and
           associated hospitals.

        3. To identify factors contributing to knowledge regarding JSSK and RBSK programs in the
           patients/patient attendants, nursing students and in-service nurses attending LHMC and
           associated hospitals.
