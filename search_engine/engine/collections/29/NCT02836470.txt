
    
      This will be a multicenter, randomized, double-blind, parallel, placebo-controlled,
      proof-of-concept, adaptive design, Phase 2 study to evaluate LB1148 for return of
      gastrointestinal function and reduction of post-operative ileus and intra-abdominal adhesions
      in subjects undergoing elective bowel resection surgery with or without a planned stoma.
