
    
      We consider relevant conduct this study because the poisoning scorpion bite represents a
      public health problem.

      This is a randomized clinical trial, which included 120 subjects of both sexes aged 15 and 60
      years old and residents of the state of Guanajuato, divided into 2 groups: 60 patients
      received antiscorpion serum of Birmex and 60 patients receive other commercial serum.
