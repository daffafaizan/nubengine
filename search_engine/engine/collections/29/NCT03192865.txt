
    
      This prospective observational study will screen patients with body mass index (BMI) ≥30
      kg/m² undergoing acromioplasty or supraspinatus tendon repair. Occurrence of post-operative
      hemidiaphragmatic paralysis will be observed using M-mode ultrasonography and its
      consequences on patient ventilation: arterial oxygen saturation, dyspnea, success of
      ambulatory procedure. Causes of diaphragmatic paralysis will be analyzed.
