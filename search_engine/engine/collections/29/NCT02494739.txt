
    
      Eighteen healthy adult volunteers of both sexes consumed yogurt (400g per day) enriched with
      polyphenols (10mg/100g yogurt) or yogurt (400g per day) not enriched with polyphenols for two
      weeks in a double-blind, crossover, randomized manner.There was a washout period of at least
      10 days. Physiological data and blood samples were collected before and after consumption of
      each yogurt.
