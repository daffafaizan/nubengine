
    
      -  Monocentric prospective interventional study.

        -  Intervention is assisted HHD, defined as: rope-ladder cannulation at the patient's home
           provided by a trained private nurse at each dialysis session, and connection to the
           Nxstage System One

        -  pilot study, with main objective defined as feasibility of the investigator's assisted
           HHD program. Pilot study usually concerns a small population, allowing us to develop
           thereafter a bigger study if the results are encouraging.

        -  primary end-point criteria defined as definitive cessation of HHD with transfer to an
           other dialysis modality.
