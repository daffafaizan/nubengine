
    
      This study will compare the application of :

        -  hemostatic collagen sponge

        -  collagen sponge sealed with abio-adhesive material

        -  Platelet rich fibrin

        -  physical barrier (stent) on the palatal donor sites with the purpose of which one of
           these will result in least postoperative pain after epithelialized gingival graft (EGG)
           harvesting.
