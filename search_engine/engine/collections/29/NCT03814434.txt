
    
      Periimplantitis (PI) is an inflammatory process that results in tissue attachment bone loss
      around the implant. Periodontal disease, smoke habit and type 2diabetes mellitus have been
      recognized as potential risk factor indicators that might lead to complications establishment
      and progression around dental implants. PI treatment predictability has been shown limited
      and influenced by factors not totally clarified. The aim of this study is to evaluate the
      reparative response after PI surgical treatment at clinical and radiographic levelsin risk
      factors patients: type 2 diabetes mellitus, smokers and chronic periodontal disease.
