
    
      Paraoxonase 1 (PON1), an enzyme associated with high-density lipoprotein, is reported to have
      antioxidant and cardioprotective properties. And all-trans-retinoic acid revealed the
      ameliorating atherosclerosis effects in many reports.There maybe some correlations between
      retinoic acid and PON1 activity and the study aim to prove it.
