
    
      The patients will be randomized into two groups. All patients will receive prewarming with
      forced-air warming device for 30 minutes. All patients will receive warmed fluids during
      intraoperative period. One group will receive warming with only forced-air warming device and
      the other group will receive warming with both forced-air warming device and water mattress.
      Timpanic, esophageal and peripheral temperature monitoring will be performed.
