
    
      This is a prospective epidemiological observationnal study involving a sample of 300
      ambulatory surgical centers among 1000 french centers.

      In a first step, a survey on the specific organization and care management of the structure
      will be carried out.

      In a second step, data on the management of 3000 patients admitted in these structure will be
      performed. The will allow an analysis of practices in a selection of acts (which seem to be
      more frequent and more relevant). Data from three different questionnaires on pain
      management, PONV prevention and treatment and VTE prevention will be recorded for each
      patient.
