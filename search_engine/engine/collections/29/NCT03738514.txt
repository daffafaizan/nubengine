
    
      A total of 120 subjects were divided into 3 groups randomly. Group 1 was given the dentifrice
      containing 5% fluoro calcium phosphosilicate ,Group 2 was given the dentifrice containing
      potassium nitrate and group 3 was given placebo. Sensitivity scores (VAS score) were recorded
      at baseline, 2 weeks, 6 weeks.
