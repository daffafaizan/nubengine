
    
      Objective: To develop a scoring model from multiple airway assessment to predict difficult
      intubation.

      Methods: This will be a retrospective analytic study. All airway assessment data from medical
      records of patients aged more than 18 years old underwent general anesthesia in Srinagarind
      hospital from January 2012 to December 2014 will be used to develop a new scoring prediction
      model.
