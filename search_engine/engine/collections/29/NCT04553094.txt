
    
      The main objective is to show that the quality of life of patients with Marfan syndrome can
      be improved by personalized training at home. The goal is to be able to offer new non-drug
      management based on physical activity to these patients.

      Improved quality of life; Improvement of muscle strength by a muscle strengthening protocol;
      Improvement of cardiovascular function parameters. Regular coaching throughout the study.

        1. / Assessment of muscular capacities

        2. / Assessment of cardiovascular and respiratory capacities

        3. / Response to psychometric questionnaires

        4. / Participation in a training circuit (training protocol)
