
    
      A cross sectional study for assessment of pelvic floor functions after 6 months to 4 years
      after normal vaginal delivery or Cesarean section Four separate entities were evaluated using
      internationally recognized and validated questionnaires:

        1. Pelvic organ prolapse evaluated using the Pelvic Organ Prolapse Distress Inventory

        2. Urinary functions were evaluated using the International Consultation on Incontinence
           Questionnaire Short Form

        3. Fecal incontinence was evaluated using Wexner Fecal Incontinence Score

        4. Sexual functions were evaluated using female sexual function index
