
    
      The 3 series of measurements

        1. Longitudinal section: maximum longitudinal length, width at the hilum, maximum width,
           width at the upper third, longitudinal cross-sectional area

        2. Transverse section at the hilum : thickness, width, hilar cross-sectional area

        3. Largest transverse section : thickness, width, maximum transverse cross-sectional area

      Clinical and biological patient data collection:

      age, gender, date of transplantation, serum creatinine and glomerular filtration rate (GFR),
      kidney transplant placement (right or left iliac fossa), acute illness or usual follow-up
