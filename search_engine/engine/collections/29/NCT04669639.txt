
    
      Suprascapular nerve block is considered a phrenic nerve sparing approach that recently
      introduced as alternative for interscalene brachial plexus nerve block in shoulder surgery.
      However, some researchers reported that Suprascapular nerve block alone results in an
      ineffective analgesia. Erector Spinae Plane block is supposed to be able in providing
      adequate analgesia in shoulder surgery
