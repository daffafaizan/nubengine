
    
      OUTLINE: Patients are randomized to 1 of 2 arms.

      ARM I: Patients undergo acupuncture during chemotherapy infusion on day 1 and fluorouracil
      pump disconnect on day 3 of each biweekly chemotherapy infusion over 12 weeks. Patients also
      undergo self-administered acupressure over 11 minutes daily for 12 weeks and undergo standard
      of care oral cryotherapy.

      ARM II: Patients undergo standard of care oral cryotherapy.
