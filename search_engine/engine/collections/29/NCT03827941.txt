
    
      In this study, investigators will examine the treatment effect of transcutaneous electrical
      nerve stimulation at auricular area with vagus nerve distribution on high-functioning
      individuals with autism. Specifically, investigators will choose two ear acupoints: heart and
      shenmen. Participants will be randomized to either 1 Hz or 20 Hz tVNS group (up to 5 times
      per week) for three weeks.
