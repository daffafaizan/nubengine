
    
      This is a retrospective and prospective, non-randomized study of the Ceramic on Metal(CoM)
      device. This study will follow each subject through 10 years postoperatively. A total of 250
      subjects will be enrolled in the investigation comprised of approximately 150 new PAS
      subjects and approximately 100 subjects recruited from the investigational device exemption
      study to the PAS.
