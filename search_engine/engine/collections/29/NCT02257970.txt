
    
      Part 1 is feasibility, exploratory, open-label study of ketoprofen, to document effects.

      Part 2, is open-label trial of ketoprofen to document histological response. Part 3 is
      double-blind randomized trial of receive placebo or ketoprofen to evaluate safety and
      efficacy.

      We will try to determine how the study drug affects the body tissue by obtaining tissue
      biopsies (small pieces of skin from the arm or leg) before treatment and after treatment.
