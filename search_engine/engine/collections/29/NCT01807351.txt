
    
      We aim at find out the progression of impaired fasting glucose (IFG) to type 2 Diabetes
      Mellitus (T2DM) among patients in general practice setting. We can understand the progress of
      the target patients so as to improve patient education, improve patient motivation in
      lifestyle modification.We are going to find out the annual incidence of Type 2 DM
      developement among the patients with and without hypertension Hypertensive patients with
      impaired fasting glucose are more likely to develop type 2 diabetes mellitus compare with
      normal tensive patients
