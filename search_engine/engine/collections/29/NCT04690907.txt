
    
      Pregnant women are recruited for the study from 18 maternity care clinics in Northern Savo
      and Northern Karelia area. The participants will be randomized into intervention and control
      groups. All the participants will have access to the study web-pages, which includes written
      material of healthy diet during pregnancy as well as all the study material (4 day food
      records, Three factor eating questionnaire (TFEQ R-18) and Binge Eating Scale (BES)). In
      addition, intervention group will get two virtual dietary counseling sessions by a dietician.
      The information on participants' gestational weight gain and the result of oral glucose
      tolerance tests will be collected by the public health nurses after birth.
