
    
      Physical condition will be evaluated in obese patients before surgery and 1, 4, 6, 12, 18 and
      24 months after surgery.

      Between 1 and 4 months after surgery, participants will be assigned to one of the
      postoperative care groups:

        -  Control

        -  Control + protein supplementation

        -  Training in aquabike

        -  Training in aquabike + protein supplementation

        -  Bicycle training

        -  Bicycle training + protein supplementation
