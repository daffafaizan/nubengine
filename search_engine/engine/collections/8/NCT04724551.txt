
    
      From January 1, 2012 to January 31, 2020, consecutive patients underwent PD performed at
      Institut Paoli-Calmettes for various pathologies. Data were entered prospectively into a
      clinical database (NCT02871336).

      The cohort was split into 3 groups:

        -  a training cohort ;

        -  an internal validation cohort (Paoli Calmettes institute);

        -  an external validation cohort (Rennes Pontchaillou Hospital).
