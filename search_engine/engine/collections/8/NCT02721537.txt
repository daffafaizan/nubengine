
    
      Healthy collegiate athletes (football, soccer, rugby, hockey, and volleyball) will take
      Nicotinamide Riboside (NR) or placebo twice daily for 84 days. Pre- and post-intervention
      evaluations include physical assessment, blood tests for safety and toxicity monitoring,
      blood tests for biomarkers, neurologic testing, quality of life questionnaires, and the 31P
      MRI exam.
