
    
      Patients with moderate-to-severe preoperative pain are selected. Patients receive either a
      single-dose of 50 mg diclofenac potassium (group A) or a placebo tablet as a control (group
      B). All patients receive the assigned premedication one hour before single-visit root canal
      treatment. Postoperative endodontic pain is measured at the following time points: 6 ,
      12,24,48 h after treatment. The incidence of analgesic intake is also recorded.
