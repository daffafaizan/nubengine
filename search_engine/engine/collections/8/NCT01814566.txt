
    
      It is very common that Chinese Medicine Injection used in hospitals in mainland China.
      However safety problems rose in recent years. There could be many uncertain factors influence
      Chinese Medicine Injection in clinical practice.

      In order to ensure the safety of public drug use and lower drug-induced risks, a registry
      study for DanshenDuofensuanyan injection safety surveillance with 30000 patients will be
      conducted from Jan.2013 to Dec.2015.

      Eligibility criteria Patients who will use DanshenDuofensuanyan injection in selected
      hospitals.
