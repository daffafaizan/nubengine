
    
      Sixty- five male and female diabetic patients with peripheral neuropathy will be selected to
      participate in this study. They will be equally divided into two groups Group-I: Twenty five
      patient with diabetic peripheral neuropathy will be exposed to low intensity pulsed magnetic
      therapy Group-II: Twenty five patients with diabetic peripheral neuropathy will be exposed
      for 24 treatment sessions of laser therapy Group-III 15 patient received only medical
      treatment
