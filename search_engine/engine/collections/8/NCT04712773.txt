
    
      Participants are given oral and written information about the course of the trial, associated
      risks and benefits and about the handling of the personal data on the day before the surgery
      by one of the investigators. Participants are also given written information about patient's
      rights and insurance, the aim of the trial and its course. 40 adult cardiac surgery patients
      will be recruited for the study. NOL and SPI values will be measured during the cardiac
      surgery procedure. All patients receive oral premedication and standardized anesthesia.
