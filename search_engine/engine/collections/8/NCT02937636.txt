
    
      This clinical study will be carried out in healthy adult volunteers with moderate gingivitis.
      Treatment effect will be determined by evaluating the efficacy, in a Chinese population, of a
      dentifrice containing 0.454% w/w stannous fluoride to control gingivitis and supra-gingival
      plaque following 6 and 12 weeks twice daily brushing, compared to a fluoride control
      dentifrice. During the 12 week treatment period, participant will brush with their allocated
      study product twice daily.
