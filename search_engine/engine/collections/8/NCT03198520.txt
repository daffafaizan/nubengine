
    
      Cohort study conducted in the United States & United Kingdom to assess Oral Health Related
      Quality of Life (OHRQoL) in patients receiving the study polymer Removable Partial Denture
      (RPD) as compared to their baseline Cobalt Chrome (CoCr) Removable Partial Denture (RPD) Oral
      Health Quality of Life (OHRQoL).
