
    
      Design and setting: Double-blind randomised crossover studies in a university teaching
      hospital

      Patients: 30 patients with stable Heart Failure (NYHA I-III) and 15 healthy volunteers

      Interventions: All 45 subjects will be exposed to dilute diesel exhaust (PM2.5 of 300 mg/m3),
      filtered diesel exhaust (filter mask), or filtered air

      Main outcome measures: Endothelial function, heart rate variability, six-minute walking test
      and blood samples for inflammatory factors
