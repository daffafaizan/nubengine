
    
      The purpose of the study is to investigate the effects of systemic treatment with
      Ng-monomethyl-l-arginine in congestive heart failure and healthy controls on:

        -  glomerular filtration rate and renal plasma flow

        -  blood pressure and heart rate

        -  plasma levels of vasoactive hormones

        -  lithium clearance

      The study is randomized and placebo controlled
