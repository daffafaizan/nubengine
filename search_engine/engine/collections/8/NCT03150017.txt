
    
      A pilot prospective, single blinded, randomised controlled trial with two arms an
      internet-delivered CBT-PMP for chronic pain post spinal cord injury (SCI) and usual care.

      The CBT-PMP will contain 8 sessions over 8 weeks, with outcomes assessed at baseline, post
      intervention, and 6 months follow-up.

      Participants will be adults with chronic pain (pain > 3 months), regular internet access and
      not undergoing any other psychological treatments.

      Following the study those in the intervention strand will be invited to complete a focus
      group to explore the subjects' overall experience of the online CBT-PMP.
