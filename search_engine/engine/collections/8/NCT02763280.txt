
    
      The investigators performed a 12-week, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled human
      trial to evaluate the efficacy and safety of ginseng extract on improvement of Bone
      metabolism in menopausal women. The investigators measured Serum Osteocalcin, Urinary
      Deoxypyridinoline, DPD/OC ratio, Serum CTX, NTX, Ca, Phosphorus, BSALP(vone specific-alkaline
      phosphatase), P1NP(Procollagen type 1 N-terminal propeptide), WOMAC index(Western Ontario and
      McMaster Universities Arthritis).
