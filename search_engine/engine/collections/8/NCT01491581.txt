
    
      Design: double-masked randomized control trial Place: three schools in South Vietnam, Can Tho
      district, South Vietnam Subjects: School children aged 6 to 10 years old, from first grade to
      third grade of 3 primary schools.

      Duration of intervention: 6 months (consumption of Goody or "control bar")

      Study groups: 2 groups:

      Group 1: children will receive micronutrient enriched milk bar. Group 2: children will
      receive the same milk-based food except for the micronutrient fortification.
