
    
      The first purpose of this study is to validate the Finnish version of the Musculoskeletal
      Tumor Society scale and the Toronto Extremity Salvage Score upper extremity sections. The
      second purpose is to chart the functional capabilities and health-related quality of life
      (HRQoL) of upper extremity sarcoma patients who have undergone limb salvage surgery with
      local or free flaps.

      Patient hospital records are retrospectively reviewed to chart the demographic and clinical
      data.

      The recruitment of patient takes place at the outpatient clinic of Department of Oncology,
      Helsinki University Hospital. The study includes a cross-sectional assessment at one year
      follow-up with five validated questionnaires. A follow-up is conducted three years after
      surgery in the outpatient clinic.
