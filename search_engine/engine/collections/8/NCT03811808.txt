
    
      Study Procedures:

      Participants and caregivers will attend a multidisciplinary MSA clinic one day every four
      months. In addition to the standard of care, they will be asked to complete both online and
      paper questionnaires, including the following:

        -  UMSARS (Unified MSA Rating Scale)

        -  MSA QOL

        -  CES-D

        -  OHQ

        -  Compass-31

        -  BIC

        -  UPDRS

        -  SARA

        -  MDS Scale

        -  CGI

        -  MoCA

      Participants will be part of the study as long as they are a patient of the MSA clinic, and
      will be contacted for follow-up information up to five years.
