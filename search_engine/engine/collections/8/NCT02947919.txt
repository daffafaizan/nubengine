
    
      60 patients aged 5-7 years, ASA (American Society of Anesthesiologists) status I-II,
      scheduled for general surgery at the Hospital of the Catholic University and associate
      institutions, will be considered for inclusion. 30 will receive music as intervention and 30
      will received usual treatment. Salivary cortisol samples will be obtained preoperatively, in
      the surgical ward (OR), and in the post-anesthesia care unit (PACU). Secondary outcomes will
      be M-YPAS (Yale Preoperative Anxiety Scale) for children, and STAI (State-Trait Anxiety
      Inventory) for parents scales.
