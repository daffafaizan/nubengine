
    
      The primary end point of the study is post-operative complications defined as systemic and
      local complications, including the vascular complications e.g., graft infection, thrombosis
      etc.

      Secondary end points are as follows,

        -  health related quality of life examined and evaluated with the help of pre and
           post-operative survey performed with the help of SF36v2 questionnaire and EQ5D5L.

        -  peri operative stress response (during operation) measured/ analysed with the help of
           stress hormones changes

        -  periopersative inflammatory response

        -  cost utility analysis

        -  cost differences
