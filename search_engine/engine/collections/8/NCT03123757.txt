
    
      To evaluate the determinants of mobility an its correlation with quality of life, we
      conducted a study that evaluated patients ability to perform the 6-minute walk test and their
      corresponding quality of life measured by the EQ-5D. Cognition was evaluated used the
      Callahan six-item screener, and additional measures of physical performance were assessed
      using the Short Physical Performance Battery. Participants disclosed self reported physical
      function and physical activity. Additionally, other participant characteristics including
      vital signs, medication history and healthcare utilization were determined as well as social,
      economic and health related information was collected.
