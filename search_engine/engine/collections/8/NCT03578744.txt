
    
      Platelet-rich fibrin is a fraction of plasma that provides a rich source of growth factors
      and may enhance the stabilization and revascularization of the flaps and grafts.

      Hyaluronic acid is a naturally occurring non-sulfated high molecular weight glycosaminoglycan
      that forms a critical component of the extracellular matrix and contributes significantly to
      tissue hydrodynamics, cell migration and proliferation. Hence its administration to
      periodontal wound sites could achieve beneficial effects in periodontal tissue regeneration.

      Amniotic membrane is being used for various regenerative procedures as it is a rich source of
      various collagen and non-collagen proteins such as laminin, fibronectin and proteoglycan.
