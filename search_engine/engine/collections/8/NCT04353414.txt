
    
      This is a phase IV, randomized, single-blind, single-center study comparing postoperative
      pain, opioid usage and Post-Anesthesia Care Unit (PACU) recovery time for patients who
      receive a Transmuscular Quadratus Lumborum block (TQLB) plus pericapsular injection versus
      pericapsular injection (PCI) undergoing primary hip arthroscopy.
