
    
      PROTOCOL OUTLINE: This is a randomized study. Children are randomized to receive either 35
      hours of intensive one-on-one behavioral treatment in the home and neighborhood for 2 years
      or individualized, in home parent training for 6 months.

      A common group of tests to evaluate autism are administered at intake, 12 and 24 months into
      treatment, and when patients reach age 6.
