
    
      Participants will be randomized allocated to two groups: robot-assisted hand combined with
      traditional occupational therapy (OT) group and traditional OT group. All participants
      underwent six weeks, 3 sessions per week, and 60 minutes per session training program. Motor
      Evoked Potential (MEP), Fugl-Meyer Assessment (FMA), Modified Ashworth Scale (MAS), Box and
      Block Test (BBT) and Purdue Pegboard Test (PPT) will be assessed before and after
      intervention in all participants.
