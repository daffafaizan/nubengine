
    
      Biop system's safety and performance in increased detection of high-grade lesions of the
      cervical epithelium in women scheduled for colposcopy.

      This is a multi-center, prospective, open label, non-randomized study to train the Biop
      System's algorithm and evaluate the safety and performance of the Biop system in women
      undergoing cervical colposcopy.
