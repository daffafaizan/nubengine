
    
      This is a single center, non-randomized study. Patients who meet the eligibility criteria
      will be treated with the sirolimus coated modified Bx VELOCITY Balloon-Expandable Stent
      mounted on the Raptor OTW SDS. Patients will be followed for five years post-procedure, with
      all patients having a repeat angiography at 6 months, 18 months, and 48 months.
