
    
      The study is aimed to evaluate the efficacy and safety of navigation bronchoscopy guided
      transbronchial ablation for treating inoperable malignant lung tumor. The study is designed
      as a single-center prospective trial. The participating centers are Department of pulmonary
      medicine and endoscopy, Shanghai Chest Hospital,Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China.
      Patients are divided into two groups, including radiofrequency ablation group and microwave
      ablation group. Sixty patients are expected to be enrolled into the study with 30 patients in
      each group.
