
    
      -  Design: Prospective study

        -  Inclusion criteria of patient group: who has a cerebral palsy and GMFCS level 3,4,5

        -  Intervention: applied medication Botulinum A injection (meditox 200unit)

        -  Main outcome measures: The investigators will measure for finding evidence by checking
           the x-ray photograph, hip adductor and muscles pressure test and electromyography(EMG
           test) test of hip adductor, abductor and spine muscles.
