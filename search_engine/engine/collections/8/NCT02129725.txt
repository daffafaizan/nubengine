
    
      Participants enrolled in the study titled " Renin-angiotensin and fibrinolysis interaction in
      humans: effect of long-term PDE5 inhibition on glucose Homeostasis, (Specific aim 2)" will be
      offered the opportunity to participate in this sub-study.

      We will obtain skeletal muscle biopsies from 16 subjects who are enrolled in specific aim 2.
      Eight subjects will be in the sildenafil group and 8 subjects will be in the placebo group.
