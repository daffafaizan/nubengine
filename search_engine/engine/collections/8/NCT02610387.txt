
    
      In a randomized, sham-controlled, double-blinded clinical study, hemiplegic chronic stroke
      patients are randomized to receive active tDCS delivered to lesioned leg motor cortex (CZ) or
      sham combined with balance training with Biodex balance system.

      Experimental group receive tDCS stimulation (2mA) for 20 min, 5 sessions in five days paired
      with 20 min balance training.
