
    
      33 healthy postmenopausal women were randomized to 6 wk seaweed then 1 wk seaweed plus soy or
      6 wk placebo then 1 wk placebo plus soy. Blood and urine samples were collected at the end of
      each treatment period and analyzed for estrogen, homocysteine, antioxidants, insulin-like
      growth factors and thyroid hormones. Urine was analyzed for phytoestrogens and iodine.
