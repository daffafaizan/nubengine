
    
      Patients with Hand Eczema will be asked to wash with either a CLn BodyWash or Cetaphil Daily
      Facial Cleanser. Patients and study staff will be blinded. Hand swabs will be done prior to
      the wash and after 20 minutes. The average time spent in the study for each patient should be
      less than 2 hours unless an Adverse Event occurs.
