
    
      PET/MRI scans will be performed with a whole-body PET/MR imaging system capable of
      simultaneous PET and MR imaging (SIGNA, GE Healthcare, Milwaukee, WI). Approximately 45-60
      minutes after IV injection of 8 mCi of 18F-FSPG, PET emission scanning and MRI acquisition T1
      weighted scanning will begin imaging both whole body and the heart.
