
    
      Low back pain (LBP) is a very common health problem worldwide and a major cause of disability
      which affecting performance at work and general well-being. LBP is a complicated condition
      which affects the physiological and psychosocial aspects of the patient. The muscles of the
      low back provide the strength and mobility for all activities of daily living. The purpose of
      this study is to find out the additional effect of global re-education program with hamstring
      stretching in patients with low back pain.
