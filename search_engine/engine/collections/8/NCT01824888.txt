
    
      Before the intervention, the bactericidal activity of JUC was validated in accordance with
      the prEN 12054. A total of 15 volunteers were recruited, hands were washed immediately prior
      to the intervention. Then, both hands of each volunteer were immersed into a contamination
      fluid of E. coli. Then, hand rub was performed with JUC or alcohol. Culture samples were
      collected before and after the hand rub by rubbing the fingertips and thumb tips of each hand
      ont he base of a Petri dish containing appropriate medium. The culture inoculation,
      incubation, appropriate dilutions, and colony count were performed.
