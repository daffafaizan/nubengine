
    
      30 patients undergoing retroperitoneoscopic renal or adrenal surgery were included in this
      study.Their PaCO2, PetCO2, and PTCCO2values were measured at 3 time points before and 30min,
      60min after pneumoperitoneum and calculated the different between each measure(PetCO2 and
      PTCCO2) and PaCO2. Agreement among measures was assessed by the Bland-Altman method.
