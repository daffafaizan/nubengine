
    
      A pre-post test trial was conducted in 120 elders with xerostomia. All subjects received a
      total of 50 ml (10ml x 5 times) OMJ per day. Subjective and objective dry mouth scores,
      salivary pH, buffering capacity and satisfaction were monitored at 2 weeks and 1 month use,
      compared to baseline. The data were analyzed by Repeated Measure ANOVA.
