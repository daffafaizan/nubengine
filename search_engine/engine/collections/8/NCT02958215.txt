      minutes, with blood pressure and heart rate recorded three times, with a three-minute
      interval between measurements to obtain mean baseline levels and this will be repeated in an
      upright position to record the same measurement.Orthostatic hypotension (Orthostatic
      hypotension "OH" is defined specifically as a 20mmHg drop in systolic, and/or a 10mmHg drop
      in diastolic blood pressure within 3 minutes of standing.
