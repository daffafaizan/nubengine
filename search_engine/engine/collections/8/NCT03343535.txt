
    
      Inclusion

      At least one of the following:

        -  Donor PaO2/FiO2 ≤ 300 mmHg at the time of the offer; or

        -  Expected cross-clamp time > 6 hours for the second lung; or

        -  Donor after Cardiac Death (DCD donor); or

        -  Donor age ≥ 55 years old
