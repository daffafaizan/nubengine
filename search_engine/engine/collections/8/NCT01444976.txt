
    
      Seventy-six patients with an indication of TEE will be recruited, the age range will be 18-83
      years. In Group Topical (T) there are 26 patients who had the procedure under topical
      pharyngeal anesthesia, in Group Midazolam (D) there are 25 patients who will receive
      midazolam and in Group H there are 27 patients receiving hypnosis. All patients will have an
      IV access; throughout the procedure heart rate, rhythm ECG, peripheric O2 saturation will be
      monitored with a non-invasive monitor, blood pressure measurements were taken every 3
      minutes. Rhythm Holter recordings will be obtained from all patients and TEE will be
      performed.
