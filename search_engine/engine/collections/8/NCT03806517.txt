
    
      The investigators are planning to complete this study between January and February 2019. This
      study will be performed at Ordu University Education and Research Hospital, Neurology
      Department.

      All patients admitted to the outpatient clinic will be evaluated in terms of inclusion
      criteria. The control group consisted of people who do not have any orthopedic, neurological
      or metabolic disorders which may affect the BMD.
