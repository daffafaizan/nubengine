
    
      The investigator performed an observational study on the national population of anaesthesia
      residents. A questionnaire was sent to all residents composed with validated questionnaires
      analyzing psychological distress (Hospital Anxiety and Depression Scale (HADS), Copenhagen
      Burnout Inventory (CBI), Perceived Stress Scale (PSS), Karasek's questionnaire; work-related
      questions (work hours per week, night shift per month, application of safety rest after night
      shift, average time to start and end work, break time and time for food per day; two
      semi-open work-related questions (main stressor during internship, professional orientation);
      a free verbatim.
