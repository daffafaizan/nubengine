
    
      Demographic data, duration of disease, routine biochemical tests and currently used
      medications were recorded. Disease activity was assessed with Disease Activity Score 28 (DAS
      28). Visual Analog Scale (VAS) was used to evaluate pain levels. Health Assessment
      Questionnaire (HAQ) was used to assess activities of daily living. Beck depression inventory
      (BDI) was used to investigate the signs of depression in patients. Sleep quality was
      considered to be poor in patients with a Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index (PSQI) global score
      of 5 and greater.
