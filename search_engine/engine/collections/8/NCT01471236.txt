
    
      Agili-C implant is a bi-phasic, porous resorbable tissue regeneration scaffold for the
      treatment of focal articular cartilage and for osteochondral defects. Up to 50 patients are
      to be included in the study. Patients suffering from isolated, single and focal cartilage or
      osteochondral defect at the knee joint are considered for study inclusion.
