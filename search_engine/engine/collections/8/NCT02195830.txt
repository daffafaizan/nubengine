
    
      The study is an interventional cohort study using a convenience sample of adult patients in a
      university teaching hospital Emergency Department.

      Eligible participants who consent to partake in the trial will have an ultrasound assessment
      of their inferior vena cava performed and the IVCCI will be calculated at enrolment.

      Care will be provided in line with the published guidelines on sepsis management from the
      Surviving Sepsis Campaign and the College of Emergency Medicine.

      A sample size calculation has been performed which indicates that 112 patient with severe
      sepsis or septic shock will need to be enrolled.
