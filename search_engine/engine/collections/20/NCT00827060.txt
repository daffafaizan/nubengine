
    
      Primary endpoint:

        -  initial Procalcitonin values of patients with Acute Respiratory Tract Infections

      Secondary endpoints:

        -  correlation of initial Procalcitonin values to classify Acute Respiratory Tract
           Infections

        -  correlation of initial Procalcitonin values to subjective severity of Acute Respiratory
           Tract Infections

        -  correlation of initial Procalcitonin values to antibiotic- prescriptions

        -  correlation of initial Procalcitonin values to frequency of admission to hospital

        -  correlation of initial Procalcitonin values to frequency of referrals (radiology,
           pneumology)
