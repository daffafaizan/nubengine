
    
      A randomized prospective study conducted in The Neurosciences Hospital, Baghdad, from January
      2016 to January 2018, patients had either GKR treatment or treated with MVD. The pain
      evaluated pre- and post-operatively using the Barrow Neurological Institute Pain Intensity
      scale (BNIPI), visual analog scale (VAS) and Brief Pain Inventory Facial (BPI-Facial) scoring
      systems. In GKR procedure, the trigeminal root entry zone targeted with a radiation dose of
      80 Gy. MVD was performed using retro-sigmoid approach. Follow-up period for two years
