
    
      Trampoline accidents are increasing since a few years. In Rennes hospital, pediatric
      emergencies are experiencing a subjective increase in admissions for trampoline trauma with
      increasingly early ages and severe trauma. The aim of this study is to compare trauma related
      to others sport to trauma due to trampoline in pediatric emergencies of Rennes.

      In parallel, retrospective data analysis will be performed on the same data during the year
      2008, to compare with a period before the explosion of trampoline sales.
