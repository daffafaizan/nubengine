
    
      The purpose of this study is to evaluate long-term performance of the Attain Performa™ LV
      Leads. This evaluation is based on the number of lead-related complications occurring during
      the study compared to the number of leads enrolled in the study. The leads will be followed
      for 5 years after implant. This study is required by FDA as a condition of approval of the
      Attain Performa™ LV Leads. This study is conducted within Medtronic's post-market
      surveillance platform.
