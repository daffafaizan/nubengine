
    
      Anxiety is present due to many gait in the preoperative period. In studies that investigated
      anxiety levels in the preoperative period was found that 60-80% of the patients had
      significant anxiety. The present study is aimed to investigate the effect of preoperative
      anxiety level on anesthesia preference of cesarean patients. After approval, patients
      enrolled in the study is invited to complete the Pain Catastrophizing Scale questionnaire,
      Beck depression and Beck anxiety scale. After surgery, The effect of anxiety on the choice of
      anesthesia will be evaluated (general or spinal anesthesia).
