
    
      The study will be performed in emergency departments and ICUs of university hospitals. 15
      emergency departments and 36 ICUs of APHP (the public hospital system of the city of Paris)
      will be involved.

      The study will assemble the data from 50,000 to 80,000 patients in total.

      Eligible patients are admitted in ICUs between 01/01/2007 and 12/31/2011.

      Extraction of the patients' data will be performed with processing software utilised in APHP,
      i.e. Healthcare Information System Program (PMSI, Urqual, Gilda...etc.).

      Collected patients' data span the period from admission in emergency department or medical
      department unitl hospital discharge.
