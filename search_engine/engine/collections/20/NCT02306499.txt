
    
      Study approval: The study will be submitted for approval by Research and Ethical committees
      at Faculty of Medicine, Sohag University.

      This study will include cryopreserved sperm from infertile azoospermic men, with proven
      diagnosis of varicocele (clinical & sonographic), which will be used for ICSI in an ART
      program in Sohag .

      Patients personal and medical history and socio-demographic data will be retrieved from their
      saved medical files.

      Study design: Prospective, controlled clinical study. Control group: ICSI cases using
      cryopreserved sperms from infertile azoospermic men due to etiologies other than varicocele.
      Cases with known female factor infertility will be excluded.

      Outcome Measures: fertilization rate, embryo quality, chemical pregnancy and clinical
      pregnancy.
