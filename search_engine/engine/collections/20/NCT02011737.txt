
    
      Prospective, Single-center, Double blind, Randomized controlled trial

      Treatment

      - Oral Naftopidil 75 mg once daily are given to the patients who are scheduled for shockwave
      lithotripsy (ESWL) because of ureteral or renal stones.

      Follow up Assessments including X-ray(KUB), visual analogue pain scale, are done 2, 4, and 6
      weeks after the ESWL.
