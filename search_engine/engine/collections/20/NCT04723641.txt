
    
      Forty children were randomly assigned to one of two groups of equal number, control and study
      groups. Random assignment of children was conducted into two stages. Stage one involved
      instructing three physical therapists who were working in the pediatric outpatient clinic of
      the Faculty of Physical Therapy, Cairo University, pediatric outpatient clinic of the Faculty
      of Physical Therapy, Badr University as well as Prof. Dr/ Kamal Shoukry's pediatric
      rehabilitation center to report all children who fulfilled the inclusion criteria of the
      study and had no exclusion criteria.
