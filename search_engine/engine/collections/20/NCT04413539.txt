
    
      This is a retrospective study. Patients or parents of minor patients living in France and
      suffering from SCD will be informed about the study and enrolled during their usual
      follow-up, or by patients' associations. They will be asked to answer an anonymous survey
      online relating to their whole life-course with SCD and exploring different aspects of the
      consequences of the disease on their life. It contains 4 different sections:

        1. socio-demographic questions to establish a respondent profile

        2. health status of the respondent to establish the severity of the disease

        3. education, professional life, material and economic situation

        4. quality of life through MOS SF-36 questionnaire This is a one-shot survey, which will
           take approximatively 20 to 30 minutes to answer by the respondent.
