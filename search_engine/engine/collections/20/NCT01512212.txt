
    
      The author group of this investigation produced a new thyroid fine needle aspiration biopsy
      (FNAB) apparatus and patented it from Turkish patent Institute. FNAB indicated nodular goitre
      patients are the subjects of this research. First, the investigators will perform standart
      FNAB after this procedure will perform FNAB with new apparatus. The investigators will
      evaluate the aspirates according to number of cells and malignity ratio of cells.
