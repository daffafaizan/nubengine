
    
      The surveys will address questions in the following domains: 1) Diabetes diagnosis and
      treatment; 2) Carbohydrate-restricted diet; 3) Insulin regimen; 4) Other diabetes related
      care; 5) Diabetes complications; 6) Other related health and healthcare; 7) Patient provider
      interactions; 8) Socio-demographics. Drafts of these surveys are included in the appendix of
      this protocol.
