
    
      Demographic data, including ethnic origin, age an gender will be recorded. The type of
      Convulsions (Simple or Complex), duration, and time since the fever was noticed will be also
      recorded. Another data that will be summarized included: previous antibiotic treatment,
      physical examination with special emphasis on neurological examination and meningeal signs,
      routine laboratory analysis including blood count and cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) results and
      the final diagnosis including the cause of fever.
