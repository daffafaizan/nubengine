
    
      This is a single-center, 2 treatment periods, examiner-blind, randomized, 7 day crossover
      study in adult participants with a complete maxillary denture. This is a method development
      study to investigate the changes in the level of denture plaque, microbial counts, microbial
      composition and stain on the maxillary dentures after daily denture cleanser use versus
      dentures that are cleaned weekly.
