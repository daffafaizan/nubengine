
    
      Participants in the two intervention groups will exercise at home with supervision from
      physiotherapist week one, two, four and eight. They will then be contacted for telephone
      follow-up month five, seven and twelve after the start of training. The control group is
      encouraged to live as usual.

      All participants are assessed at baseline, after three, 12 and 24 months from inclusion in
      the study.
