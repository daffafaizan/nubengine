
    
      The primary objectives of this study are to:

        1. Compare the image quality of images between the SPECTRALIS with Flex Module and the
           SPECTRALIS.

        2. & 3. Assess the agreement in the ability to identify abnormalities between the
           SPECTRALIS with Flex Module images and the SPECTRALIS images.

      The secondary objective is to evaluate any adverse events found during the clinical study.
