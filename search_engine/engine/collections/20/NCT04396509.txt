
    
      The Maternal Attachment Inventory (MAI) measure maternal love attachment (9). Muller tested
      the MAI questions in two phases; maternal adaptation and maternal attachmentEach item is
      evaluated by a 4-way to 26-point Likert-type scale ranging from "always" to "never". Always
      is calculated as (a) = 4 points, often (b) = 3 points, sometimes (c) = 2 points and never (d)
      = 1 point. The lowest score which could be obtained from the scale that measures maternal
      emotions and behaviors indicating love is 26, the high score is 104, and a high score
      indicates a high maternal attachment (12).
