
    
      After approval, patients enrolled in the study is invited to complete the Pain
      Catastrophizing Scale questionnaire, Distress Tolerance Scale and Beck anxiety scale.After
      surgery, VAS scores of the patients will be evaluated (in the recovery room, 2,6,12 and 24
      hours). and patients with VAS> 3 will receive 2 mg of morphine.The relationship between total
      analgesic consumption and the stress tolerance will be evaluated.The study will be performed
      with 69 sleeve gastrectomy patients(BMI>30).
