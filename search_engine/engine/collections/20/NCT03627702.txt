
    
      18 people were examined. In all subjects, were assessed the risk of developing bedsores
      according to the scale Dutch Consensus Prevention of Bedsores CBO. All bedsores were
      irradiated with a Bioptron lamp (20 sessions, twice a day). Photo and measurement of bedsores
      size and Torrance score, were made:on the first, 9, 18 and 36 day of therapy.
