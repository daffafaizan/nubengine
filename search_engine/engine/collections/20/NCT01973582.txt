
    
      This study is aimed to delineate the hepatitis B seroepidemiology among young adults with or
      without neonatal hepatitis B immunization, among young adults with hepatitis B booster in the
      university period, among the mothers of hepatitis B vaccination cohort. The results of this
      study will provide important information to help government implement hepatitis B vaccine
      booster policy in the adolescents and young adults.
