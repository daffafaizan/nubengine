
    
      In this multi-center, randomized, open pilot study the therapeutic effect of two prarallel
      groups treated with either Riciguat or Macitentan shall be determined by evaluating the
      change in systolic and diastolic RV function within 12 weeks after first drug intake in order
      to plan a larger Phase II study.The method used to determine the RV function will be the
      "Conductance Method".
