
    
      12 tertiary care centers will enroll all AF comers. Details of baseline clinical, EKG, echo,
      and lab data will be documented for all patients. Management is according to the physicians
      discretion. Patients will be followed up for 1 year for clinical endpoints including
      cardiovascular death, stroke, heart failure, systemic embolization and bleeding..
      Anticoagulants and other cardiovascular medications will also be documented.
