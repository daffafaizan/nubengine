
    
      This study applied to determine the effect of physiotherapy exercises on correction the
      remaining ocular deviation which still present following the strabismus surgery and also its
      effect on amplyobia.

      Different type of physiotherapy exercises was applied to experimental group for 12 weeks
      beside the medical treatment applied post-surgery while the control group received
      traditional treatment only.

      The near and far angles of ocular deviation were measured before and after the treatment for
      evaluation of strabismus and measuring visual acuity for evaluation of amplyobia.
