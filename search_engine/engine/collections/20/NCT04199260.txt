
    
      300 patients will be included in approximately 50 Spanish sites. The study comprehend a
      maximum of 3 visits. At visit 1, patient's writted informed consent will be collected,
      patient's selection critera will be checked, and patient's medial history data will be
      collected. At visit 2, 6 months after the first visit, the primary and secondary objectives
      will be evaluated. For those patients who finish treatment after 6 months, this will be the
      final visit. Only in that cases where, by usual clinical practice, the doctor indicates
      patient should continue under treatment, visit 3, wich will take place 12 months after
      streatment stars, will be the last visit for the patient under study.
