
    
      This is a randomized-controlled trial in the patients undergoing
      esophago-gastro-duodenoscopy. The patients are randomized by computer into two arms:
      nebulized lidocaine and lidocaine spray. Both drugs are applied 15-20 minutes prior the prior
      the procedure. Only one endoscopist participate in this study. After the procedure finishes,
      the investigators evaluate the outcome and satisfaction of patients and endoscopist.
