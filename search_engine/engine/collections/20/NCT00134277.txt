
    
      Dilatation and recanalisation of the distal veins (infragenual popliteal artery, tibiofibular
      trunc, posterior tibial artery, anterior tibial artery) by classical balloon dilatation or
      cutting balloon with or without stenting or by endovascular laser. This intervention happens
      only once; the duration of the intervention depends on the procedure and the original
      injuries(30 - 120 minutes).
