
    
      The study will test the subject questionnaire aimed to be used in a full general population
      prevalence study to screen potential subjects with rosacea.

      Questionnaire will be self-administrated to Dermatology outpatients.

      Testing will be performed at the practice of 2 Dermatologists experts in rosacea who will
      evaluate subjects' answers and propose fine-tuning of the questionnaire content, if needed.
