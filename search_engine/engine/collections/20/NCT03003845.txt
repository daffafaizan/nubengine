
    
      The study is investigator-initiated, prospective, study. There will be no change in care.

      Patients who have had a diagnosis of IC made by cystoscopy and hydrodistension and still have
      pain will be recruited for this study.

      Patients will have blood samples taken at the visit they sign the consent form and 6 months
      later.

      The study staff will call and remind the patient of their 6 month visit.
