
    
      Testing and counseling for HIV, syphilis and other sexually transmitted infections is
      provided to consenting individuals.

      Components: interactive counseling program, self sampling (finger prick) under supervision,
      questionnaire (on a tablet computer) to assess risks of HIV acquisition, understanding of HIV
      transmission routes and pre-exposure prophylaxis, willingness to take pre-exposure
      prophylaxis if at risk of HIV, and satisfaction with regard to services provided.

      Referral to appropriate care facility of participants diagnosed with infection. HIV negative
      participants with significant risk of HIV are advised to come back for HIV retesting within 3
      to 6 months and informed about pre-exposure prophylaxis.
