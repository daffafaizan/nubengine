
    
      Wheezy infants were tested for gastro-esophageal reflux disease using combined MII-pH, EGD,
      LLMI and BAL pepsin. Wheezy infants with abnormal MII-pH or reflux esophagitis were given
      domperidone and omeprazole for 12 weeks then re-evaluated for symptoms control and wheeze
      exacerbations recurrence.
