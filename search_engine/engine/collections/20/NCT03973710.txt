
    
      Probiotics are live microorganisms that have been associated with multiple health benefits.
      However, its protective role in adults has long been controversial. This study aims to
      examine the effect of 12-week probiotics supplementation on the prevention of hypertension in
      Chinese adults. By understanding the mechanisms by which probiotics exert this beneficial
      effects, we can better control the rising prevalence of hypertension, which is a major risk
      factor for cardiovascular diseases.
