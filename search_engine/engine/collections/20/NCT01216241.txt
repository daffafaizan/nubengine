
    
      To determine whether the percentage of neutropenic subjects that become afebrile by five days
      after fever first develops is higher when daptomycin compared to placebo is added at the same
      time as anti gram-negative therapy. The five day time point is selected because it is at this
      point where it is recommended to add antifungal therapy to the treatment regimen if the
      patient is still febrile.
