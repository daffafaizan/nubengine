
    
      The laser handpiece (probe) will be deposited in the vaginal canal and the laser energy will
      be delivered through the handpiece. Subjects will receive a total of three (3) treatments
      every six (6) weeks (+/-1 week). Subjects will return one week (+/- 3 days) post the first
      treatment, as well as three (3) months (+/- 2 weeks) and twelve (12) months (+/- 2 weeks)
      post the third treatment.
