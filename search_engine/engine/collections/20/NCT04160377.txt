
    
      This study aims at investigating the special effectiveness of antidepressant effect of
      Fluvoxamine for melancholia. The investigators also aim to assess the effect of Fluvoxamine
      on the multimodal magnetic resonance imaging(MRI), blood cytokines, feces bacteria flora and
      neuropsychological performance in depression patients with melancholic features. The
      investigators further aim to identify the predictors of Fluvoxamine's antidepressant effeect
      using the above techniques.
