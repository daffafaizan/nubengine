
    
      Septi-PICC is a prospective single-center study of infections related to PICCs setting at the
      South tertiary site of the University Hospital of La Reunion over one year with a follow-up
      of 6 months per patient.

      All the complications such as thromboembolic, are collected during the follow-up.

      It is planned a convenience sample of 400 patients over 18 years of age, except pregnant
      women.
