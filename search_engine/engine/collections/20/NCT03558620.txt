
    
      Evaluation of effects of an endoscopic bite block on mask ventilation. In the present study,
      expiratory tidal volume and minute ventilation are measured under the same pressure
      controlled mode in ventilator (Pressure 15cmH2O, Respiratory rate 15/min, I:E=1:1) with three
      kinds of holding a mask. ( by one hand, one hand with an endoscopic bite block and two hand
      hold.)
