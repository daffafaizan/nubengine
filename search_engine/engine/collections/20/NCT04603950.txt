
    
      From October 2020 to January 2021, 40 knee osteoarthritis patients with flexion contracture
      between 5 ° and 10 ° total knee arthroplasty are enrolled in this study. 20 patients are
      treated with Adductor Canal Block + Infiltration between the Popliteal Artery and Capsule of
      the Knee, and 20 patients are treated with Adductor Canal Block alone.
