
    
      In this sturdy, the deep block is applied when the surgeon requests for deep muscle
      relaxation to improve surgical field condition in the surgery such as laparotomy known that
      the deep block is helpful and in the other surgeries, the moderate block is maintained. The
      investigators assess the postoperative sore throat and hoarseness and compare the effect of
      muscle relaxation depth in the deep block group and moderate block group in terms of the
      incidence and severity of postoperative sore throat and hoarseness.
