
    
      Prospective, observational, longitudinal study from Sept 2019 to Feb 2020 of all adult
      patients who required invasive mechanical ventilation treated at General Hospital of Zone 11
      IMSS in Piedras Negras Coahuila.

      Independent variable: Lung protective ventilation, Mechanical power, Driving pressure
      Dependent variable: Hospital Mortality, Sequential Organic Failure Assessment (SOFA)

      Also will be registered:

        1. Primary diagnosis

        2. Compliance with lung protective ventilation

        3. Complications associated to mechanical ventilation

        4. Quality of attention indicators for MV patients as defined by Semicyuc.

        5. Real mortality vs Expected mortality analysis
