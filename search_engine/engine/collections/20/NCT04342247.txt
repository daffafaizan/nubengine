
    
      In the study, we have two group: First group consists of patients with testosteron deficiency
      and there are sex and age-matched healthy control subjects in the second group. We will
      measure the parameters of both groups at baseline. And first month and 3rd month we will
      remeasure the values of the parameters by enhance depth optical coherence tomography. The
      results will be compared between the groups.
