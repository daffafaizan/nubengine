
    
      To compare the success and complication rates of super-mini-percutaneous nephrolithotomy
      (SMP) and Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL) for the treatment of renal stones ≥20
      mm in children.An international multicentre, prospective, observational study. Pediatric
      cases with renal Stones ≥20 mm who undergo ESWL or SMP will be evaluated and compared
      proespectively. The total number of departments planned to be included in the study is 10 and
      the number of cases is 180.
