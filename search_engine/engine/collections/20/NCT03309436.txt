
    
      This study receives patients from 2017 August to 2018 June who undergo ART treatment at
      Shanghai Jiai Genetics & IVF Institute and taken either CC or GnRH antagonist
      protocol(control group) for ovulation induction.

      The investigators will record every patients' age, BMI, serum E2, P, LH level, infertility
      factors and pregnancy outcomes, counted the implantation rate and clinical pregnancy rate,
      and then used SPSS Software x2 test for statistical analysis, the significance was set at
      p<0.05.The investigators will also make a correlation analysis about the serum hormone level
      and pregnancy outcomes.
