
    
      Based on the previous work, this project optimizes the TCM Symptoms and Symptoms Scale.
      Through the cohort study method, 194 patients with acute ischemic stroke were evaluated for
      neurological impairment, motor dysfunction, TCM Symptoms and Symptoms Scale, combined with
      the patients' head MRI. , Plasma BDNF, MMP-9 and other biomarkers, screen TCM syndromes
      related to stroke outcome indicators, and adopt targeted comprehensive acupuncture and
      moxibustion program intervention and follow-up to establish a stroke-specific intervention
      outcome prediction model with TCM characteristics. A curative effect evaluation system of
      acupuncture and moxibustion in the treatment of acute ischemic stroke is formed to provide
      clinical evidence.
