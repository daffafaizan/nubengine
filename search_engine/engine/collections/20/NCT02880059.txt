
    
      Patient presenting to the outpatient center for neurological of neurosurgical management of
      intracerebral aneurysm will be recruited. Sleep apnea will be assessed by questionnaire and
      out of center polygraph/polysomnography. Medication, comorbidities and blood pressure will be
      recorded. Change in aneurysm size and blood pressure medication will be evaluated annually
      for up to 5 years. Frequency of subarachnoid hemorrhage and increase in aneurysm size will be
      assessed.
