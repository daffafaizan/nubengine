
    
      Migraine attack is one of the most leading causes presentations to emergency department.
      Patients with migraine attack seek urgent care to cease their pain. There are so many
      interventions defined in the medical literature that can be used in migraine attacks.
      However, as a IV drug, dexketoprofen; little known whether IV dexketoprofen is superior to
      placebo or not. In the present study we aimed to determine the effects of IV dexketoprofen in
      migraine attack in emergency department.
