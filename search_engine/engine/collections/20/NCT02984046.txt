
    
      Prospective, monocentric, case-control and study Primary end-point: correlation between serum
      CC16 level and severity of the bronchiolitis, evaluated by a clinical scoring system
      established at the time of the admission in Paediatric Emergency Unit. Secondary end-points:
      correlation with urinary CC16; correlation with risk factors for bronchial epithelial
      aggression, viruses, immediate morbidity and mortality. Study of serum SP-D and sRAGE levels.
