
    
      Evaluation of the Impact of dexmedetomidine as an adjunct for local infiltrations analgesia
      and ultrasound-based regional anaesthesia as pain medication in regard to patients' opioid
      need the first 48 hours after the total knee replacement operation. Secondary objectives are:
      Pain assesment, Pain medication necessary, functionality of the prothesis, satisfaction,
      wellbeing, postoperative discomforts, complications (delirium, allergic reactions,
      cardiopulmonary decompensation), procedure specific time.
