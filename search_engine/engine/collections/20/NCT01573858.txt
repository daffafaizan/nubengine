
    
      This is a multicenter, randomized, controlled, 2 by 2 factorial clinical trial for Chinese
      women with PCOS.

      Primary outcomes:

      - Live birth rate

      Secondary outcomes:

        -  Ovulation rate

        -  Ongoing pregnancy rate

        -  Multiple pregnancy rate

        -  Miscarriage rate

        -  Hormonal profile

        -  Metabolic profile

        -  Side effect profile
