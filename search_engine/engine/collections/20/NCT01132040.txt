
    
      An open label, balanced. randomized, two-treatment, two-period, two-sequence, single
      dose,crossover, bioequivalence study of Primidone 50 mg tablets of Dr.Reddy's Laboratories
      Limited, Generics, India comparing with that of Mysoline (containing Primidone 50 mg) tablets
      of Yamanouchi Pharma Technologies Inc, Norman, OK in healthy, adult, human subjects under
      fasting conditions with a washout period of 14 days.
