
    
      Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease or asthma patients who have positive response in
      bronchodilator test will be consented and enrolled, patients come back for the second
      bronchodilator assessment within one to three days after the initial bronchodilator test.
      Albuterol with different concentration will be provided to the patients via HFNC, patients
      will be assessed by spirometry after each concentration until bronchodilator response is
      positive and does not improve after the next dose.
