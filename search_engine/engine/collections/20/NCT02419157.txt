
    
      The purpose of this study is to evaluate the clinical effectiveness of non-carious cervical
      lesions (NCCLs) restored with two self-etching adhesive systems: Clearfil SE Bond (Kuraray
      Noritake, Japan) and Xeno V + (Dentsply De Trey, Germany) applied with or without 36%
      phosphoric acid enamel etching. Assessments were at baseline, 3, 6, 12, 18 and 24 months of
      clinical service.
