
    
      In this study all eligible patients (Based on the inclusion and exclusion criteria) were
      randomly allocated into two study groups by a Stratified Permuted Block randomization method:
      group A received platelet rich plasma, group B (control group) received only placebo.

      All of the patients underwent a standard long protocol for knee osteoarthritis. All patients
      with grade II,III and IV of radiographic knee OA are selected. On average, in each instance,
      the amount of platelets in the peripheral blood is 4 to 6 times the baseline level.

      Group A: 1-3 cc injection of placebo Group B: 1-3 cc injection of PRP All patients received
      monthly injection for 3 times.
