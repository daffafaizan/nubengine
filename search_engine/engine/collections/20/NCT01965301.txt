
    
      This is a single center, Double-blind, placebo controlled, randomized study to assess the
      safety, tolerability, pharmacokinetics and pharmacodynamics of BP1.5375 after single oral
      administrations ranging in healthy male subjects (Part 1) followed by the assessment of the
      effect of 3 single different BP1.5375 oral doses on polysomnography in comparison to 50 mg
      diphenhydramine and placebo in healthy male subjects (Part 2).
