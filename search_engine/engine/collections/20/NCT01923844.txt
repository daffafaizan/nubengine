
    
      Preterm infants with severe Respiratory Distress Syndrome (study group) were compared to
      preterm infants without Respiratory Distress Syndrome(control group). Infants in the study
      group were randomized to receive either poractantalfa or beractant.

      Perfusion index (PI) and transcutaneous CO (TCO) values were measured before (Tp) and 0 (T0),
      5 (T5), 30 (T30), 60 (T60), 360 (T360) minutes after surfactant administration in the first
      six hours of life.
