
    
      Treatment: Randomized to one of two treatment arms. Group One: Basic active exercise program
      at home. Group Two: Instruction in the same active exercise program along with additional
      exercises using exercise equipment and stretching exercises. Range of Motion measurements
      will be taken at the first session, 1 month, 6 weeks, 3 months, 6 months and 1 year. Patients
      will complete demographic form, functional questionnaire, and QOL questionnaire.
