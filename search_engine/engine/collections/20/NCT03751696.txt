
    
      Participants will be recruited through the following routes: 1) by our partner
      non-governmental organizations, 2) by our research team through community outreach or
      training activities, 3) online screening tool, and 4) referral from organizations that
      provide service to women (e.g., Maternal and Child Health Clinics of Department of Health).
      Eligible subjects with informed consent provided will be randomly assigned to coaching group
      or SMS self-help tips. To test the effectiveness of the provided intervention programs,
      symptoms assessments and data collection on demographics and psychosocial data will be
      carried out during baseline, immediately, and 3 months post-intervention.
