
    
      The intervention is designed as a psychosocial intervention, targeted both patients and
      spouses.

      The intervention consists of individually targeted meetings with a specialized DBS nurse
      several times during the first six months og DBS. Patients and spouses likewise have to fill
      out a diary for use in the meetings.

      All meetings have specific topics, addressing aspects of challenges in adjusting to DBS, and
      the aim is to off patients and spouses a tailored intervention, supporting each individual
      based on an individual assessment.
