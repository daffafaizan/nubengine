
    
      ARM 1 - 10 prepubertal, 12-17 years old boys with no prior exposure to TRT will receive
      single dose of 5.5 mg on day one and single dose of 11 mg on day 2 with repeat blood draws to
      assess serum levels of testosterone and metabolites.

      ARM 2 - 10 Tanner Stage 3, 12-17 years old boys on TRT with bone age >= 13 years will receive
      single dose of 11 mg on day one, and single dose of 11 mg in the morning and a second 11 mg
      dose in the afternoon on day 2 with repeat blood draws to assess serum levels of testosterone
      and metabolites.
