
    
      It will be a double-blind randomized multiple cross-over trial with 3 treatments:

        -  CLA;

        -  Industrial trans fatty acids (as a positive control);

        -  Oleic acid (Cis 18:1, the monounsaturated fatty acid in unhydrogenated vegetable oils)
           as a reference fat.

      Each volunteer receives each diet for three weeks, in random order, for a total of 9 weeks.
      Three weeks is sufficient to reach new stable lipoprotein levels.
