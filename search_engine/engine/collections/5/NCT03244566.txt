
    
      From June, 2018, a total of 110 patients with thoracic esophageal carcinoma will be recruited
      in 4 hospital in China. Participants with resectable esophageal cancer will have PET/CT scan
      before three-field lymphadenectomy. Lymph nodes will be recored according to the anatomy
      site. Lymph nodes metastasis diagnosed by PET/CT and by postoperation pathological
      examination will be compared to evaluate the role of PET/CT in guiding the extent of
      lymphadenecomy and surgical approach.
