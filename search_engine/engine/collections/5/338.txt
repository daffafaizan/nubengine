1013. hand eczemathe clinical aspects of 106 cases of handeczema are reviewed.  the clinicaltypes are defined  nummular eczema, nickelallergy, atopic dermatitis,hyperkeratotic dermatitis of the palms, handeczema post partum, mycotic eczema,contact dermatitis, occupational eczema andidiopathic eczema.  the resultsof the patch tests, the influence ofpsychological and psychiatric factors,the effect of water and cleansers, thediagnosis, treatment and prognosis arediscussed.  in 39% no significant causativefactor could be found.  inmany cases where causative factors couldbe identified these were probablyno more than contributory, superimposedupon an underlying idiopathic eczema.
