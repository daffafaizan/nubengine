
    
      In a selected group of patients with repeated implantation failure, endometrial injury in the
      preceding cycle may improve ICSI outcome.

      Little is known about the efficacy of this procedure in improving intrauterine insemination
      outcome.

      Also little is known about the effect of this procedure if done in the stimulation cycle
      weather in ICSI or intrauterine insemination cycles.
