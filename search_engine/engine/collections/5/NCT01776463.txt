
    
      This is a single centre, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel-group, and
      food effect study.This is three parts study. The purpose of the first part (Part 1) is the
      evaluation of the safety, tolerability, and pharmacokinetics after single ascending doses of
      Z-360.The second part (Part 2 ) is the evaluation of the pharmacokinetics of food effect of
      Z-360.The third part (Part 3) is the evaluation of the safety, tolerability, and
      pharmacokinetics after multiple ascending doses of Z-360.
