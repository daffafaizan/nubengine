
    
      A Phase 2, Randomized, Double-Blind, Placebo- and Comparator-Controlled Trial to Evaluate the
      Safety and Efficacy of Combination Intravenous Pregabalin and Acetaminophen Compared to
      Acetaminophen and Placebo in Subjects Undergoing Bunionectomy

      Up to 80 subjects will be randomized (32 in each active treatment group, 16 placebo).

      To evaluate the efficacy of combination PGB and Ofirmev administered IV vs. placebo for pain
      control in subjects undergoing bunionectomy.

      The placebo will be the saline solution.
