
    
      Study group consists of patients with cancer who are qualified for home enteral nutrition in
      Nutritional Counselling Center Copernicus in Gdansk. The control group will receive home
      enteral nutrition and placebo. The experimental group will receive home enteral nutrition and
      Lactobacillus plantarum 299v. The study is prospective and double-blind. The parameters for
      evaluation of nutritional status are: BMI, body mass analysis, level of total protein and
      albumin in blood serum, total lymphocyte count, NRS 2002 method. Assessment of the quality of
      life and tolerance of enteral diet will be done by questionaires.
