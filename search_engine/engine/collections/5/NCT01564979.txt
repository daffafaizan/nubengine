
    
      Botulinum toxin type A has been used for hemifacial spasm since 1986. of Botulinum toxin type
      A can be subcutaneously injected at pretarsal or preseptal portion of orbicularis muscle.
      Blepharoptosis has been reported less frequently with pretarsal technique, however dry eye
      was noticed in one patient treated with pretarsal botulinum toxin type A for cosmetic
      purpose.
