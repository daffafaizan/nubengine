
    
      The objectives are:

        1. - To measure the phenomenon of vaccine hesitancy in Mexican patients with RD from two
           Health Institutions in Mexico City.

        2. - Translate and adapt the "Oxford Covid-19 vaccine hesitancy scale" to patients with RD
           and validate this version in a population of patients with RD from two Health
           Institutions in Mexico City

        3. Explore factors associated with the phenomenon of vaccine hesitancy.
