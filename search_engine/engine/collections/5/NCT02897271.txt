
    
      This retrospective chart review study will analyze the demographics, medical history,
      clinical features, and pathological data of patients with Eosinophilic Gastrointestinal
      Disorders (EGIDs) other than Eosinophilic Esophagitis (EoE) alone, including Eosinophilic
      Gastritis (EG), Eosinophilic Gastroenteritis (EGE), and Eosinophilic Colitis (EC).
