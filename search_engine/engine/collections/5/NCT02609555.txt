
    
      Perineal Rectopexy is performed by an incision in the intersphincteric space followed by
      dissection till the sacral promontory then Proctoscope is inserted to complete the dissection
      .Mesh is inserted and fixed to the sacrum with laparoscopic tacker ( or can be left unfixed)
      then postanal repair is conducted in two layers ( levatorplasty then sphincteroplasty) and
      finally Ana cerclage is done with polyglactin 0 sutures
