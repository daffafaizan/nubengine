
    
      To investigate in patients having rheumatoid arthritis (RA), or axial spondyloarthritis (AS)
      or digital osteoarthritis (DA) practices and beliefs about influence of food and diet on
      their rheumatic disease.

      200 patients of each disease will be included in this cross-sectional non interventional
      study Factors associated with specific practices or specific beliefs will be determined.

      Only questionnaires are used in this study.
