
    
      The aim of this study is to explore the effectiveness of Mifepristone for cervical ripening
      before high dose Oxytocin drip in midtrimester abortions. After informed consent, all the
      women will be randomized for Mifepristone or placebo. 48 hours later, high dose oxytocin drip
      will be started and we will examine the success rate to induce abortion, the duration from
      starting oxytocin till evacuation of uterus and side effects.
