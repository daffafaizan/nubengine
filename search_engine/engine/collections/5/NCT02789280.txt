
    
      The main goal of this study was to compare the effect of a one-session unsupported Internet
      intervention on participants' clinical symptoms (depressive and anxiety symptoms) and related
      variables (mood, confidence and motivation).

      Method: A total of 275 adults residing in the United States will take part in a randomized
      controlled trial. Participants were randomly assigned to an experimental condition based on
      Behavioral activation or a one week wait list condition. They will be follow-up one week
      after consenting.
