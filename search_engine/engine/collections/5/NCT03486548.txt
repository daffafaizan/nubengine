
    
      After informed and consent, all patients will be devided into two groups, Sciatic group
      (group S) and Controlled group (group C). All patients will receive adductor canal block,
      then patients in group S will receive Sciatic nerve block with low concentration bupivacaine
      and dexamethasone while partients in group C will receive sham block. All patient will
      receive spinal anesthesia and intraoperative periarticular injection.

      Postoperative pain score, muscle strength, total morphine consumption and adverse events will
      be record in case record form.
