
    
      Patients will be recruited after admission to the hospital for surgery. Randomization will be
      performed using computer-generated random number tables in opaque sealed envelopes prepared
      by an anesthesiologist who will be not part of the study

      Patients will be randomly divided into two groups using a computer generated random number
      chart. Group S received scalp block with 20 ml of 0.5% bupivacaine, whereas Group C received
      scalp block using 20 mL normal saline and will be considered as control group.
