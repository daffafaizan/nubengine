
    
      An open-label randomized trial conducted in one centre to compare the efficacy of
      ultrasound-guided caudal and interlaminar corticosteroid injections for the treatment of
      lumbar disk herniation-related sciatica. 30 male and female patients will be included in each
      group. The primary outcome measure will be a 30% decrease in lower limbs pain as assessed by
      a visual analog scale.
