
    
      Patients undergoing total hip arthroplasty are randomized to posterior approach (PA) and
      anterolateral approach (ALA) groups. The patients are evaluated preoperatively and at 6 and
      12 months postoperatively, with flexion, extension and abduction strength measurements and
      the Harris Hip Score (HHS). The physiatrist performing isokinetic tests and the patients are
      blinded to the study groups.All data are calculated as mean and standard deviation values.
      The Student's t-test is used for statistical analysis of the patient data. Statistical
      calculations are performed using SPSS vn.22.0 software (IBM SPSS Statistics for Windows,
      Version 22.0. Armonk, NY, USA).
