
    
      A total of 200 population is recruited, individuals are divided into four groups according to
      a periodontal examination and a general physical examination: healthy individuals
      ,periodontitis, renal disease patients with healthy periodontium, and renal disease patients
      wth peridontitis. Fasting blood samples and gingival crevicular fluid are collected from all
      individuals, and inflammatory markers are analysed for the four groups.
