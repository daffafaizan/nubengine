
    
      The endoscopic sphincterotomy with needle knife papillotome is a well-established advanced
      endoscopic technique in difficult cannulation cases. Choledocholithiasis impacted at the
      ampulla of Vater is occasionally seen. Inflammed surrounding mucosa frequently accompanies.
      Whether sphincterotomy with a needle knife increases the bleeding complications is to be
      elucidated in this study.
