
    
      Each subject completed a preoperative examination of both eyes, implantation of IOL at the
      operative visit for each eye, and up to 5 postoperative visits (each eye examined at Day 1-2,
      with binocular visits at Month 1, Month 3, and Month 6 after the second implantation). The
      second implantation generally occurred within 30 days of the first.
