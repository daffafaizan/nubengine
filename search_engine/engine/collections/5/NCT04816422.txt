
    
      50 patients was included and allocated into 2 groups, group A consisting of 25 patients who
      received PNF techniques for trunk to improve trunk control. Group B consisting of 25 patients
      who received conventional trunk exercises to improve trunk control. Trunk control was
      assessed pre and post treatment with the help of Trunk Impairment Scale and ICU mobility
      scale. Data was collected by ICU mobility scale & Trunk impairment scale
