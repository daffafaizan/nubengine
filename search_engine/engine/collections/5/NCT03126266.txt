
    
      Study therapy will consist of radiation therapy (RT) given over 17 treatment days (for 30.6
      Gy in fractions of 1.8 Gy) or 20 treatment days (for 36 Gy in 1.8 Gy fractions), depending on
      the time from completion of the first course of RT. Treatment days will generally be
      weekdays, not including statutory holidays.
