
    
      All patients will be recruited from the orthopedics inpatient wards and emergency department
      at Mulago National Referral Hospital. Only patients who have sustained an orthopedics injury
      will be included in this study, and the severity will be recorded for future references. No
      Protected Health Information (PHI) will be collected throughout the study.

      Steps

        1. Identify patients from Mulago Teaching Hospital

        2. Enrollment of patients into survey study

        3. Administer survey to patients at 1st meet up

        4. Administer survey to doctor-in-charge?

        5. Compilations of survey results into excel spreadsheet. Participants will be assigned an
           ID. The study team does not intend to collect any PHI.

        6. Statistical Analysis.
