
    
      The study was conducted on 40 eyes of 24 patients have acquired inflammatory punctal stenosis
      and 20 eyes of 10 patients of normal asymptomatic subjects as control group. We studied the
      external punctal diameter, visibility of the internal punctum and punctal depth before
      treatment using AS-OCT, and then patients were re-evaluated after one month of treatment with
      preservative free methylprednisolone 5% eye drops.
