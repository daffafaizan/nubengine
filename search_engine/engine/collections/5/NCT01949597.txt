
    
      Patients of Dexeus Universitary Institute that are having a laparoscopic surgery for deep
      endometriosis will participate in the study. A patient information sheet will be provided and
      written consent will be obtained. Patients who give written consent will participate in the
      trial. All patient information will be confidential and only be available to researches
      involved in the study.

      Only three expert surgeons of the Gynecological Department of Dexeus Universitary Institute
      will participate in the study.
