
    
      Vitamin D level is measured on the day of HCG administration. According to Vitamin D level,
      patients are divided into 3 groups: A, Deficient, <20 ng/mL; B, Insufficient, 20-29.9 ng/mL;
      C, Replete, >30 ng/mL.

      Ongoing pregnancy rate are compared among these three groups after controlling age, number of
      embryos transferred, ect.
