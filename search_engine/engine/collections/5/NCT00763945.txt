
    
      Aims of this registry stdy are

      documentation of the characteristics of all patients representing to the RWTH Aachen
      University Hospital (UKA) with STEMI or NSTEMI documentation of the invasive and adjuvant
      medicinal therapy of STEMI and NSTEMI at UKA documentation of the in-hospital mortality
      documentation of the procedure-associated complications documentation of therapeutic
      principals for patients with STEMI or NSTEMI at UKA and investigation conformity of treatment
      with already established guidelines
