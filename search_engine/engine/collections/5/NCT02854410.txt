
    
      Thirty patients diagnosed as nasopharyngeal carcinoma will be recruited. Nitrate
      supplement(Sodium nitrate: 0.5 mmol/kgbodyweight,bid) or placebo(sodium chloride: 0.5
      mmol/kgbodyweight,bid) will be provided to the patients from 7 days before radiotherapy to
      one month after the finish of radiotherapy . The salivary flow and plasma nitrate levels will
      be determined before the course of nitrate supplementation and radiotherapy,before the course
      of radiotherapy,post the course of radiotherapy immediately and one month after the finish of
      radiotherapy.
