
    
      This study will concern two populations:

        -  pregnant women attending hospital during pregnancy monitoring and / or being
           hospitalized in one of the maternity wards associated with the project,

        -  health professionals in charge of the follow-up of these pregnant women and their
           childbirth.

      The study will include:

        -  500 pregnant women: 300 in Nantes and 200 in Brest

        -  150 health professionals.
