
    
      In order to investigate the relation between meibomian gland massage outcomes and signs and
      symptoms of dry eye, randomized prospective study will be conducted. Patients, who arrive to
      Barzilai Medical Center out patients' clinic, are diagnosed with MGD 2-3 and signed informed
      consent form shall be included in the trial, then randomized to study group or control group.

      The study group will receive meibomian glands massage and artificial drops PRN, whereas the
      control group will receive sham massage and artificial drops. Each visit at the clinic will
      include dry examinations and questionnaire.

      Follow up examination are due after one week, monthly visits up to 4 months, then after half
      year, 8 months and one year.

      Statistical methods: in order to evaluate to treatment effect T-Test analysis will be
      calculated, significant result is when P<0.05.

      Data will be recorded into to electronic sheets. Subjects' confidentiality will be assured by
      masking any identifying details and a random number will be assigned prior results analysis
      stage
