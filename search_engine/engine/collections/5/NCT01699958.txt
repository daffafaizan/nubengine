
    
      This study will refine, evaluate the feasibility, and estimate the effect of a brief
      problem-solving intervention to promote healthy eating and activity habits in adolescents.
      Intervention participants will receive 2 sessions of individual behavioral counseling on
      problem-solving skills utilizing educational videos, handouts, and worksheets. Intervention
      participants will be asked to generate unique health goals and practice utilizing
      problem-solving skills to achieve these goals.Control participants will receive standard of
      care. Outcomes will explore the ability to shorten the intervention, integrate the
      intervention into busy outpatient clinics, and estimate the effect of the intervention on
      confidence to maintain or improve healthy habits and to improve problem-solving skills.
