
    
      Randomized controlled trial of n-3 Polyunsaturated Fatty Acid-Enriched Therapeutic Food for
      Severe Malnutrition.

      Description of Study Design: Randomized Controlled Trial

      Study Duration: 6 months (Trial); 12 months (All trial-related activities)

      Description of Intervention: Provision of ready to use food (RUTF) enriched with flax seed
      oil, with or without fish oil, compared to conventional-composition RUTF. Interventional
      product will be given until nutritional recovery to a maximum of 12 weeks.

      Design: Flax-enriched or standard RUTF allocation is randomised and double blind. Fish oil
      allocation is randomised within those receiving flax-enriched RUTF and is open label.

      Location: Kilifi District, coastal Kenya.
