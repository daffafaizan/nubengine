
    
      Setting:

      Thrombosis Service at HHS - General Hospital. This center monitors the warfarin treatment for
      1300 patients in the region. These patient regularly go to a laboratory where they live. Test
      results (INR-results) are faxed to the Thrombosis Service, which calls the patient the same
      day to inform them of the results, how to continue dosing the warfarin and when to go for the
      following blood test.
