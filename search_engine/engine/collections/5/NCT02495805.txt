
    
      Patients meeting the criteria: American Society of Anesthesiologists (ASA) class I and II,
      that are scheduled for elective total knee arthroplasty, will be recruited into the study.
      Subjects will be randomized into 2 groups: those who will receive a continuous proximal
      adductor canal block (ACB) and those who will receive a continuous femoral nerve block (FNB).
