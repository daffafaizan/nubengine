
    
      In a prospective observational study at a university teaching hospital, the mid-luteal
      (peri-implantation) endometrial thickness and volume, uterine artery pulsatility index (PI)
      and resistance index (RI), endometrial and subendometrial vascularization index, flow index,
      and vascularization flow index, and serum estradiol and progesterone levels were compared
      between spontaneous and clomiphene citrate stimulated cycles in the same group of 50 patients
      with unexplained infertility.
