
    
      The cases divided into two groups those spinal anesthesia group ( group:1; n:30 ) and general
      anesthesia group (group:2; n:30). The preoperative and intraoperative mean arterial pressures
      (MAP), heart rate (HR), arterial oxyhemoglobin saturation (SpO2), intraoperative blood loss
      values of pregnants and 1st-5th minute APGAR scores, preductal and postductal SpO2, perfusion
      index, heart rates, body temperature, cerebral and renal regional oxygen saturations of
      newborns after birth have been recorded.
