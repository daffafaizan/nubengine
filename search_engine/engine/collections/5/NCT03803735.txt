
    
      This register is a registration of all children entering hospital based pediatric oncology
      units, working in selected hospitals in French speaking Africa. The data collected includes:
      demographic and socioeconomic status as well as clinical status and outcome.

      The register also collects information on vital status, treatment abandonment and loss to
      follow-up.

      Data, collected locally is entered on line using the Research Electronic Data Capture
      (REDCap) program. Data are stored under the responsibility of the IT department at Gustave
      ROUSSY in Paris- Villejuif.
