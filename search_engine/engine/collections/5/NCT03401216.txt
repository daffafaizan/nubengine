
    
      Patients with stable, unstable angina and non-ST-elevation ACS will be included in this
      study. Patient will undergo coronary angiography for coronary anatomy assessment and
      estimation indications for PCI . After screening an OCT-guided PCI with extra long SYNERGY
      stent implantation will be provided in all patients. Patient will be divided into 2 groups of
      follow-up and will be followed within 3 and 6 month after procedure. Final clinical follow-up
      will be assessed at 12 month for all patients. At each follow-up visits the data regarding
      clinical events, coronary angiography and OCT-imaging will be collected.
