
    
      Ethosuximide is a inhibitor of low-voltage-sensitive T-type calcium channels(T-VSCCs). It has
      higher selectivity to T-VSCCs and can enter cerebrospinal fluid through the blood-brain
      barrier, inhibit T-VSCCs on the lateral habenular nucleus neurons, and then inhibit the
      cluster discharge of neurons, resulting in a rapid antidepressant effect.
