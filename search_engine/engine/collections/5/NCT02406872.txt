
    
      This is an multi-center，double-blinded，parallel and control study using Remimazolam or
      propofol for anesthesia in operations.Subjects are randomized to different treatment groups
      (including 3 for Remimazolam Tosilate and 1 for propofol) and anaesthetics are pumped
      contimuously into bodies.Analgesics and muscle relaxants are permitted during
      operations.Efficacy，safety and pharmacokinetic/pharmacodynamic profiles of Remimazolam
      Tosilate are to be evaluated.
