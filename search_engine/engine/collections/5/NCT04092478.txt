
    
      Interspinal distance will be measured by ultrasonography by 3 different positions,
      respectively, for the patients who will undergo elective surgery and meet the study criteria
      and give voluntary consent.

      This evaluation process is to record and compare the measurement results. Patients will not
      undergo any interventional procedures.

      Patients who have difficulty in positioning due to hip, femur, knee and tibial fractures or
      arthrosis and who cannot be communicated will not be included in the study.

      One hundred of patients over the age of 18 who will undergo elective surgery will be included
      in the study.
