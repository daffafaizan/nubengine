
    
      Prospective data collection of a consecutive series of elderly patients with large B-cell
      lymphoma newly diagnosed, receiving before the start of treatment Multidimensional Geriatric
      Assessment (VGM) through the use of Activity of Daily Living (ADL), Instrumental Activity of
      Daily Living (IADL) and Cumulative Illness Rating Scale for Geriatrics (CIRS-G) to be defined
      in 3 categories:

        -  elderly FIT (ADL 6; IADL 8; 0 score= 3-4, < 5 score=2)

        -  elderly UNIFIT (ADL 5; IADL 6-7; 0 score= 3-4, 5-8 score=2; ≥ 80 fit)

        -  elderly FRAIL (ADL ≤ 4; IADL ≤ 5; 1 score= 3-4, > 8 score=2; ≥ 80 unfit)
