
    
      40 pregnant smoking and 40 pregnant nonsmoking women will be included at the end of the 2nd
      trimester.

      Assessments: smoking characteristics, plasma cotinine, DHPG, DOPAC, 5HIAA concentrations
      reflecting MAOA activity and platelet MAOB activity will be measured just after inclusion
      (end of 2nd trimester), just before delivery in venous maternal blood and just after delivery
      in cord blood.

      The newborns' behavior (wellbeing) will be assessed every 8 hour after birth for 2 days.

      Comparisons will be made between smoking and nonsmoking women and their newborns.
