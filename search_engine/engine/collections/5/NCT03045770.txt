
    
      In previous studies, we found that mFOLFOX(a Combination of Oxaliplatin, Fluorouracil),
      mFOLFIRI(a Combination of Irinotecan, Fluorouracil), FOLFPTX (a Combination of Paclitaxel,
      Fluorouracil) are active in patients with AGC or EGJA.This study is being done to find out
      which one has the best efficacy.
