
    
      Assessing Ketone levels achieved after varying doses of Medium Chain Triglyceride (MCT) oil
      in three study populations: <65 year healthy, 65 and older healthy, and Alzheimer's disease.

      Each subject attends for 4 study days, receiving incrementally larger MCT doses at each visit
      for 0g, to 14g, to 28g to 42g.
