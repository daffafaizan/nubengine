
    
      Up to two hundred un-identified PillCam SB movies that were created between the year 2008 and
      the date the study was approved by the IRB, will be collected from 2 clinical sites, Sourasky
      and Sheba medical centers during up to 3 years and transferred to Given Imaging.
      Demographical data and medical history will be collected. Capsule transit time in the
      different sections of the GI will be evaluated by internal readers.
