
    
      This is a substudy of the ongoing randomized trial entitled: Metabolic Changes Due to
      Iatrogenic Hypogonadism in Patients With Prostate Cancer: Orchiectomy vs. Triptorelin
      (EudraCT number: 2013-002553-29). 20 consecutive patients are anticipated to participate
      regardless of assignment to either orchiectomy or triptorelin.
