
    
      All patients will start taking 50 mg of betahistine dihydrochloride on treatment day 1 (after
      baseline visit). Starting on day 4, the daily dose will be increased by adding a 50 mg dose.
      If tolerated, the daily dose will be titrated to 200 mg. At the end of week 3, the dose will
      be titrated to 300 mg.
