
    
      Women with heavy periods and with a diagnosed bleeding disorders are prescribed cyclokapron
      pills to be taken during their periods. Their periods are assessed at 3 and 6 months by
      filling out a pictorial blood assessment chart. If the drug appears to be working with no
      adverse effects the patients can continue to take the medication as long as it is available
      with follow up with the investigator every 6 months.
