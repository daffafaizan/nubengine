
    
      Specific aims will guide the proposed project:

        -  Aim 1: To determine the feasibility of the proposed home-based PR program. We will
           additionally address adherence and appropriateness of the program.

        -  Aim 2: To determine improvements in quality of life and mental health of the proposed
           home-based PR determined.

        -  Aim 3: To evaluate if the average number of daily step counts increase after the
           home-based PR program
