
    
      Through the analysis of interview of the mothers that have lost their infant during pregnancy
      or just after birth, the study aims to draw a picture of the pregnancy cares offer and
      pregnancy conditions in Reunion Island.

      This study is an non interventional case-control study. Socio-economic conditions, pregnancy
      care and prevention are screened to identified the major cause of foetal or new-born death in
      Reunion Island. These data are still lacking and will be useful to identify which public
      health actions that should be organized.
