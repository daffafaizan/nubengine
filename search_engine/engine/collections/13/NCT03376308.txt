
    
      The study was conducted in patients who underwent surgical procedures under general
      anesthesia by obtaining consent from the Adnan Menderes University Medical Faculty Ethics
      Board and written consent from the patients and then being admitted to the ASA I-II age group
      18-70 years of age.

      Patients with peripheric vascular disease, vasculitis, rheumatologic disease,
      non-cooperative, under the age of 18 and over 70, ASA III and above, allergic to general
      anesthetic agents and refusing to participate in the study were excluded from the study.
