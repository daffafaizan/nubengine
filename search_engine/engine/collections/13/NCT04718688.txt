
    
      Method

        -  Single case experimental study with multiple baselines across individuals and behaviors

        -  14 individual sessions of CO-OP intervention.

        -  Two goals trained, one control goal.

        -  Different assessments used : Canadian Occupational Performance Measure (COPM), the Goal
           Attainment Scales (GAS), the Children's Cooking Task (CCT) and parent- and
           teacher-ratings of the Behavior Rating Inventory of Executive Functions (BRIEF)
           questionnaire.
