
    
      Patients with previous fertilization failure will be divided into 2 groups:

      Group 1 (control): Will undergo classic ICSI procedure in half the MII oocyte of the same
      case.

      Group 2 (study group): The other half of the MII oocyte of the same case will undergo:

        1. Polar body removal.

        2. ICSI procedure with injection of the spermatozoon in the same zona opening (done by
           laser for polar body removal).

      Results: Will be collected with evaluation of number of 2PN zygotes, Day 1, Day 2, Day 3, Day
      5 embryos in all the cases.
