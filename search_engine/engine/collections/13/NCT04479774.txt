
    
      Total 28 sample size out of which 27 were divided into control and experimental group.
      Inclusion criteria was Unilateral chronic stroke with asymmetrical stance, Patients with
      score of 3,4 and 5 will be included according to FAS (Functional Ambulation Scale), Ability
      to understand and follow instructions, Both male and female, Any type of stroke, and Age
      40-60. Written and informed consent was obtained from participants of study. Six weeks of
      exercise training was combined with compelled body weight shift technique via shoe lift of
      0.6 cm thickness for experimental group and for control group training was performed without
      shoe lift.
