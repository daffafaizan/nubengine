
    
      The purpose of this feasibility study is to collect preliminary performance and safety
      information and develop procedural optimization for the Torax Medical Inc. Magnetic
      Esophageal Sphincter device (MES) in reinforcement of Esophageal Sphincter function to treat
      Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD). The information from this study will be used to
      support the design and conduct of an expanded clinical trial.
