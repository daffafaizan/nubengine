
    
      The outcomes are:

      - Bayley Scales of Infant and Toddler Development, Third Edition (in Finnish in Finland and

      At the age corresponding to term (40 gestational weeks)

        -  Auditory event related potentials (AERPs) in electroencephalography (EEG), in Helsinki
           cohort

        -  Auditory event related magnetic fields in magnetoencephalgraphy (MEG) in Karolinska
           cohort

        -  Parental stress with The State Trait Anxiety Inventory (STAI)
