
    
      40 patients with deep second-degree burn involving the lower limbs and trunk (TBSA: 35-40%),
      will be randomly assigned either into the experimental group, received whole body vibration
      (WBV) plus the routine physical therapy program or the control group, received the same
      routine physical therapy program. All treatment interventions will be applied at a frequency
      of three sessions per week for 8 weeks. Anteroposterior stability (APS), Medial lateral
      stability (MLS), Time up and go test (TUG) test, and Berg balance scale will be the outcome
      measures of the study.
