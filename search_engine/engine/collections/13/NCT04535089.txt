
    
      Site of study:

      This study will be carried out in neurosurgical operating rooms of Zagazig University
      Hospitals.

      b. Sample size: The sample size was calculated using OPEN EPI program assuming that the mean
      postoperative opioid intake among dexmedetomidine group was 0.3±0.2 mg/kg and among lidocaine
      group was 0.58± 0.4 so at power of study 80% confidence interval 95%, the sample size was
      calculated to be 42 cases (21in each group ).
