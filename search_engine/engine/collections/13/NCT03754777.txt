
    
      A modified ERAS (mERAS) protocol was investigated. The study is a prospective, randomized
      nonblinded. All patients underwent LA. Modified ERAS protocol included patient informing,
      limitation of drainage use; intraperitoneal anesthesia with long-acting anesthetics;
      low-pressure pneumoperitoneum; early mobilization and oral nutrition. Pain level was assessed
      in rest using visual analogue scale (VAS). The primary endpoint was postoperative length of
      stay (pLOS).
