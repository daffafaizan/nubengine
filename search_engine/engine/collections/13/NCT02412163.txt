
    
      The Ellipse IM HTO Nail is intended for open wedge proximal tibial osteotomies. The Ellipse
      IM HTO Nail is an intramedullary nail that operates on the principles of distraction
      osteogenesis.

      The Ellipse IM HTO Nail is used in valgizing HTO procedures to correct varus malalignment.
      The Ellipse IM HTO Nail is surgically placed in the intramedullary canal of the tibia. It is
      lengthened telescopically using the non-invasive external remote control.
