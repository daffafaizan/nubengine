
    
      Sixty volunteers (aged 20-80) who gave a written consent before entering the study, were
      randomized in two groups of thirty subjects each. The skin prick test with histamine was
      performed on the ventral forearm, 10 cm from the elbow, before and after supplement
      administration, as well as three times daily for 12 weeks of chungkookjang(35g/day) or
      placebo(35g/day)intake.
