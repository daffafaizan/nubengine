
    
      Local pediatric primary care practices are beginning to implement universal screening. The
      investigators plan to assess feasibility and acceptability of screening by querying parents
      of children in order to determine their expectations, perceptions, and opinions about the
      screeners. Study activities include a brief, anonymous survey given to all families in clinic
      who complete the screener. The investigators will also use data from electronic health
      records to examine the impact of screeners on referrals and healthcare utilization. The
      investigators will be examining correlations between demographics and preferences indicated
      in the survey and via electronic health record data, among other relationships.
