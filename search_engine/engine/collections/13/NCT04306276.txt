
    
      The primary outcomes of the study are clinical pregnancy rate and live birth rate after
      reaching 24 week's gestation.

      Secondary outcomes of the study are: changes in the diameter and volume of the largest
      endometrioma, total gonadotropin dose administered, number of mature oocytes collected,
      number of two-pronuclear (2PN) embryos, number of blastocysts.
