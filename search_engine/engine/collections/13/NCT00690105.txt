
    
      Treatments were to be applied twice daily over all lesions on the face (except for the
      eyelids) until clearance, for a maximum of 3 weeks and then, in case of uncleared residual
      lesions, once daily for up to 3 further weeks. In the event of a flare-up the treatment was
      to be resumed twice a day until the end of the study.
