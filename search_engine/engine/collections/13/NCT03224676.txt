
    
      A total of 150 adult patients were selected from surgical, orthopedic and medical wards to
      participate in the study 50 patients from each ward. All the adult patients were selected
      using convenience sampling technique. Self-introduction and establishing rapport with the
      subjects was done. Purpose of the study was explained to the subjects, the confidentially of
      their responses was assured, and informed consent was taken prior to the study. The data was
      collected by Modified Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index and Structured Perceived Sleep
      Distractors Performa.
