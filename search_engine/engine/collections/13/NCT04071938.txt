
    
      This model will lead to awareness, recognition and interactive communication between GPs and
      specialists and guidelines on the shared-role relationship.

      The study interventions are educational modules on bladder, bowel, skin-care, and pain
      management which will be provided to participating GPs by Spinal Cord Injury specialists. In
      addition visits by specialized nurses to the participating GPs at their practices to support
      the establishment of specialized care on-site.
