
    
      Eligible patients will be randomized to one of two treatment arms: Hardware removal at 6
      months or Hardware retention for a minimum of 2 years.

      Consenting patients having previously undergone open reduction and internal fixation of
      Lisfranc injuries with an anatomic reconstruction within 6-8 weeks will be randomized to one
      of two treatment arms: removal hardware (RH) or hardware retention (HR).

      Outcome will be assessed at 6 weeks, 12 weeks, 6 months, 1 year, and 2 years from enrollment.
