
    
      The most common health complications associated with laryngomalacia were: 1. Gastroesophageal
      reflux which was presented by arching, choking, gagging, feeding aversion and continuous
      crying 2. Frequent unilateral eye infections 3. Repeated choking, frequent vomiting and poor
      weight gain 4. Repeated upper respiratory tract infections (viral and bacterial), coughing
      and cyanosis 5. Repeated secretory otitis media 6. Aspiration pneumonia
