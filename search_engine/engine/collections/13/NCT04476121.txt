
    
      The main objective was to compare the effects of using two different methods in the treatment
      of dry socket (alveolar osteitis), which is a postoperative complication of dental
      extraction. The first was Nipas, applied, if dry socket was diagnosed following a tooth
      extraction. In case of subsequent extraction of homonymous tooth and reoccurrence of dry
      socket, patients were treated with application of PRF.
