
    
      15 patients on antiretroviral therapy with darunavir/ritonavir 600/100 mg twice a day during
      four weeks will be recruited. The patients will receive treatment with Echinacea purpurea for
      14 days, 500 mg every 6 hours. A 12 hour pharmacokinetic curve will be obtained at baseline
      and on day 14.
