
    
      This 26 week study will be conducted at one site. The site will enroll 24 subjects. Subjects
      will be randomized to 1 of 2 treatment regimens:

      Regimen A: vaginal ring used continuously for approximately 90 days Regimen B: vaginal ring
      used cyclically for approximately 90 days: used for ~28 days, then removed,washed and stored
      for 2 days. The same ring will be used for 2 additional cycles in a similar fashion.
