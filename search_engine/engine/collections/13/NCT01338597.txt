
    
      To create a working place in the chest wall is an inevitable step of endoscopic
      thyroidectomy. Large subcutaneous area was considered a drawback of endoscopic thyroidectomy.
      The purpose of this study is to investigate whether subcutaneous dissection area has
      influence on clinical outcome of endoscopic thyroidectomy, such as post-operative pain,
      complication rate and post-operative discomfort.
