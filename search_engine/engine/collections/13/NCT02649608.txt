
    
      The study comprised 5 cohorts (Cohorts 1 to 5), with each cohort consisting of 3 patients
      with Parkinson's disease (men and/or women). Each patient will be treated for 3 or 4 days,
      with increasing dose each day.

      Dosing regimen will be decided at a dosing conferences. Dose levels can be increased,
      maintained or reduced both between cohorts but also within same cohort. The results are
      presented by dose level and reflect the actual doses administered.

      A follow-up safety visit was scheduled approximately 7 days after the last dose of IMP.
