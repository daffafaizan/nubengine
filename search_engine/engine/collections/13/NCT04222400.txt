
    
      Comparison of the feasibility and results of the following frailty assessment tools in
      advanced heart failure patients treated with ventricular assist device Implantation or
      awaiting heart transplantation in Eurotransplant-Status "high urgency":

        -  Evaluation of the muscle mass in the preoperative CT-Scan

        -  Bioelectrical Impedance Analysis

        -  6 Minute Walk Test

        -  Grip Strength Measurement

        -  Questionnaires: EQ5D-5L, Rockwood Frailty Scale

      The assessment will be conducted prior to surgery, after surgery and at 6 Month Follow-up
