
    
      Patient with distal radius fractures are after removal of the cast are recruited form Bergen
      Emergency Department, where the are randomly divided into two groups with concealed
      allocation. Patient will be treated 3 times a week, for 3 week. After removal of the cast,
      the patients pain threshold, swelling, AROM, strength and function is measured. The patients
      will meet for following-up controls until 26 weeks after injury.
