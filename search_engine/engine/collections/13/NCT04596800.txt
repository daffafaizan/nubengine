
    
      Prospective, interventionist and randomized controlled trial in a 1: 1 ratio, open to
      multidisciplinary team and blind to surgeons and anaesthesiologists. The aim is test the
      effectiveness of a multimodal prehabilitation protocol in patients who will undergo
      gynecological surgery.

      The multidisciplinary prehabilitation program will be applied to the intervention group. For
      the group participating in the prehabilitation and for the control group, specific
      recommendations for gynecological cancer defined by the Enhanced Recovery After Surgery
      (ERASÂ®) guidelines will be applied.
