
    
      Cross-sectional, non-interventional study on healthcare for psoriasis in patients and their
      treating physicians. Clinical data on severity as well as subjective estimates on disease
      burden, quality of life etc. will be collected. Data collection is planned at multiple,
      representative sites in fourEuropean countries with different healthcare systems with the aim
      to identify system-dependent and -independent barriers in guideline-compliant psoriasis care.
