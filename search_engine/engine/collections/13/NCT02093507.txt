
    
      The newborn with metatarsus adducts are included in the study and divide in 2 groups by
      random.Group A with parents'manipulation of the foot and group B without manipulation of the
      foot.The parents'foot manipulation is trained by pediatric orthopedic surgeons to correct the
      deformity.The effectiveness of the manipulation is measured by percentage of no deformity of
      the foot in newborn after 6 months.
