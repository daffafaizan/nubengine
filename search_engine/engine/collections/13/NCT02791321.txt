
    
      Calendar :

      This study will take place during 60 months. 4 first months will be devoted at the ethic and
      administrative issues. Inclusion will take place during 26 months. Follow-up will take 5
      years. Last months will be used for data analysis and articles submission.

      Perspectives :

      This study will be the first describing frailty evolution during aging in patients suffering
      from ADS-MR. It will be useful for creating a new, simple and pragmatical frailty screening
      tool specific for ADS-MR patients in order to detect earlier which patient are the most
      frail.
