
    
      The objective of this US pivotal trial is to investigate the safety and effectiveness of The
      LimFlow System for creating an AV connection in the Below The Knee (BTK) vascular system
      using an endovascular, minimally invasive approach to arterialize the pedal veins for the
      treatment of chronic limb-threatening ischemia (CLTI) in subjects ineligible for conventional
      endovascular or surgical limb salvage procedures.
