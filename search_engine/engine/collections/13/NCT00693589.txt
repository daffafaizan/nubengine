
    
      Study design: 36 pts with CAD or PAD are randomly assigned to either rosuvastatin 10mg/d or
      vitamin supplementation with folic acid 1mg, vitamin B12 0.4mg and B6 10mg/d for 6 weeks in a
      double-blinded design. After 6 weeks all pts receive rosuvastatin and vitamin supplementation
      in combination for additional 6 weeks.
