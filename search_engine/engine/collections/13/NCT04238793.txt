
    
      This is a first-in-human, multi-center, double-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel-group,
      escalating single-dose design study to determine the safety, tolerability, and analgesic
      activity of KLS-2031 administered by transforaminal epidural injection to subjects aged 18 to
      75 years with neuropathic pain due to Lumbosacral Radiculopathy.
