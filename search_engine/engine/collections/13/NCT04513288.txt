
    
      Strategy :

      Enteral administration of citrulline for 5 days versus iso-nitrogenous placebo. Amino acid
      assay and immunological parameters (monocytic expression of HLA-DR, MDSCs, cytokines /
      chemokines, lymphocyte number and phenotype, apoptosis and lymphocyte proliferation and
      mitochondrial function and T lymphocyte repertoire) will only be carried out on patients
      included in Rennes (60 patients).
