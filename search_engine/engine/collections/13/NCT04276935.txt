
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. To develop a Spanish-language version of the anal cancer/high-grade squamous
      intraepithelial lesions outcomes research (ANCHOR) high-grade squamous intraepithelial
      lesions (HSIL) health-related quality of life (HRQoL) index (HQI) using state-of-the-art
      measure development methodology that captures the most important HRQoL symptoms and concerns
      of those persons diagnosed with anal HSIL and either treated or untreated for anal HSIL.

      OUTLINE:

      Participants take part in cognitive interviews in Spanish over 45-60 minutes.
