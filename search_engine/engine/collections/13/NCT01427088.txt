
    
      Subjects who have been enrolled for this study will take baseline evaluation for
      sociodemographic data and serum level of BDNF, plasma Prolaction, and magnesium, etc. And
      then, weekday regular stimulation through rTMS will be applied on DLPFC of brain. After the
      completion of stimulation for 2 weeks, the change or response of serum BDNF will be analyzed
      and compared.
