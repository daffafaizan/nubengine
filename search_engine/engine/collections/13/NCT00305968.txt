
    
      The patients are enrolled in endoscopic clinics in 7 hospitals in Norway. They are randomized
      to receive intervention now (intervention group) or in 6 months (control group). The
      intervention is a 6 hour education about the irritable bowel syndrome, held by a experienced
      gastroenterologist at the local hospital.

      Elements in this "school-day" includes symptoms, epidemiology, pathophysiology, treatment,
      nutritional advices.

      The patients are evaluated at time: 0 months and 6 months (intervention group and control
      group), 12 months (control group) and after 2 years (both groups).
