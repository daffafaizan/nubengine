
    
      The study will be conducted on the patients seeking medical care in the orthodontic
      department Cairo University, for six months all patients with class II and class III
      malocclusion will be recruited based on Wits appraisal measured from the cephalometric X-ray
      taken routinely during orthodontic examination. Then a group of class I arch length
      discrepancy patients will be used as control. A blood sample will be withdrawn and taken to
      the Cairo university laboratory to analyze the blood group and RH .a statistical analysis
      will be performed to detect a relationship between skeletal malocclusion and the blood groups
      as compared to the control
