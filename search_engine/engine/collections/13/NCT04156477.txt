
    
      All subjects are, in random order, tested on three separate occasions with intake of weight
      adjusted and taste matched drink of i) a ketone ester, and ii) an isocaloric and -volumetric
      glucose drink, or iii) an isovolumetric tap water drink.

      Subjects are studied for five hours with baseline and blood sampling on multiple occasions. A
      satiety questionaire are included.

      Subjects are recruited through notice on website for this purpose. Sample size calculations
      were performed prior to inclusion (n=10). According to protocolized statistical plan, missing
      data will not be replaced and no imputation will occur. Data will be analyzed by applying a
      repeated measurements multilevel model with intervention, time and their interactions as
      categorical factors. Effects size are adjusted for small sample size.
