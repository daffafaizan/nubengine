
    
      The investigators propose UltraSound Elastography as an imaging method which allows for a
      better evaluation of soft tissue and organ lesions compared to the conventional US modalities
      used nowadays in the dayly clinical routine.

      This is a prospective, single-center, non-randomised, non-blinded trial with US Elastography
      of Patients referred to our institution to assess soft tissue and organ lesions. All patients
      will undergo next to the clinical indicated conventional US-examination, US Elastography
      after informed consent. After completion of these three examinations, the participation in
      the study is completed.
