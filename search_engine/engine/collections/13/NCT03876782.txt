
    
      Participants were enrolled from the Cataract & Presbyopia Service of the University General
      Hospital of Alexandroupolis in a consecutive-if-eligible basis. Apart from keratometric
      values, IOL power calculations will be compared for 8 IOL models (Acrysof SN60WF, SN60AT,
      SN6ATx, TFNT00, TFNTx0, SV25T0, SV25Tx and Tecnis ZCB00) using 4 formulas (SRK/T, Holladay 1,
      Hoffer Q and Haigis).
