
    
      This is a double-blind,randomised,placebo-controlled clinical trial.We recruit patients that
      suffer from non-erosive reflux disease and then divide them into two groups:treatment group
      and control group.Treatment group take Jianpi Qinghua granule and half-dose omeprazole for 4
      weeks,control group take a normal dose of omeprazole for 4 weeks.Finally we investigate the
      safety of Jianpi Qinghua granule combined with half-dose omeprazole in treatment of
      non-erosive reflux disease(NERD),and contrast the efficacy of Jianpi Qinghua granule with
      omeprazole.
