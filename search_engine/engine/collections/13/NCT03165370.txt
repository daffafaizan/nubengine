
    
      Insomnia is a common sleep disturbance, there were more than a quarter of people with sleep
      disorders, the prevalence with more and higher, seriously affecting the health and quality of
      life. The Athens Insomnia Scale (referred to as AIS) can help physicians more accurately
      diagnose and understand the state of insomnia patients. The purpose of this study was
      investigated the correlation among the disease status, treatment and AIS assessment, hoping
      enough traditional Chinese physician and Western physician's clinical reference. Methods:
      This study used the cross-sectional questionnaire survey with the convenience sampling
      method. Screened 200 patients according to the diagnostic criteria for insomnia and conducted
      a survey from the local clinic at Miaoli County.
