
    
      All patients will be given guidelines for adopting dietary habits and healthy lifestyles to
      achieve a regular and physiological depositional rhythm and will be given a study supplement
      and a control supplement, alternatively, for 2 time periods of 3 weeks separated by 2 weeks
      of wash out.

        -  Supplement under study: mixture of food fibers of plant origin, in a proportion that
           contributes 75% soluble fiber / 25% insoluble fiber.

        -  Control: Placebo supplement: maltodextrin and plum flakes
