
    
      The study was carried out with schoolchildren living in a non-fluoridated area, with (A) or
      without (I) active caries lesions, were randomly allocated into 3 groups according to the LD
      used over 12 months: Group 1 (n=39-A/38-I): 550µgF/g-pH4.5, Group 2 (n=33-A/35-I)
      1100µgF/g-pH7.0, Group 3 (n=33-A/34-I): 550µgF/g-pH7.0.
