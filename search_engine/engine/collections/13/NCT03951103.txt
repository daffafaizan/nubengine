
    
      A multicenter, international, non- interventional, retrospective and prospective medical
      chart review study. Data will be collected from medical records for patients diagnosed with
      haemophilia A who have been, or who are currently, treated with rFVIIIFc for ITI . The study
      will be descriptive in nature and report on baseline characteristics, treatment and outcomes
      for patients who have been, or who are currently, treated with rFVIIIFc for ITI.
