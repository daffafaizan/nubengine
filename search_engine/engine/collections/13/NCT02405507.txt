
    
      This is a crossover, double blinded, placebo controlled randomized trial. The study will
      compare the effect of consumption of wheat breads with or without wheat germ on healthy
      individuals, during 2 intake periods of 4 weeks each and 5 weeks washout before crossover.

      The study will focus on the effects of wheat breads supplemented with wheat germ on
      gastrointestinal discomfort, blood cholesterol and postprandial glycaemic response in healthy
      human volunteers.
