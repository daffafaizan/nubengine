
    
      The effectiveness and safety of nebulized magnesium sulfate (using isotonic solution in a
      dose of 1024 mg/hour) is compared with intravenous magnesium sulfate (200 mg/kg over 30
      minutes, followed by 50 mg/kg/hour) in treating mechanically ventilated neonates with severe
      persistent pulmonary hypertension of the newborn.
