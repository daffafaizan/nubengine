
    
      This is a prospective, non-randomized, single center study to collect data on fusion and pain
      reduction following implantation of the SImmetry device. Up to 15 total subjects will be
      implanted and followed for 24 months. Data will be collected pre-operatively, at implant,
      discharge, and at specified follow-up time points (6 weeks, 3 months, 6 months, 12 months, 24
      months.)
