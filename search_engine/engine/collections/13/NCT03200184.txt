
    
      All cases of hepatitis C whether cirrhotic, post organ transplant, co-infected with HIV or
      hepatitis B, active drug abuse, on immune suppression and from all genotypes will be
      included. Patients will be treated by a single daily dose of a fixed-dose combination pill of
      400mg sofosbuvir and 60 mg daclatasvir for 12 weeks. If a patient is cirrhotic, defined by
      liver stiffness > 12 kilopascal, either weight based ribavirin will be added or the treatment
      duration will be prolonged to 24 weeks.
