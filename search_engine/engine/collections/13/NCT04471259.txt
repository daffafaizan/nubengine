
    
      Patients. A retrospective (medical records), level 3 evidence study was designed to evaluate
      the outcomes of patients who underwent endoscopic surgery for Haglund's deformity. The
      present study was approved by the Peking University Third Hospital's ethics committee.
      Written informed consent was obtained from all patients, parents or guardians of the patients
      prior to the present study. The rights of the patients were protected.

      Diagnosis. Patients were designed to be diagnosed based on subjective complaints, combined
      with clinical and radiological examinations. Radiological measurements for this disease
      include the Fowler-Phillip angle, calcaneal pith angle, parallel pitch lines, Chauveaux-Liet
      angle and the X/Y ratio.
