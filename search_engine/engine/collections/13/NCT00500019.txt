
    
      Objective: To evaluate the uterine flora at the time of elective and non-elective cesarean
      sections and to study the relationship to the development of postpartum fever and/or
      endometritis.

      Methods: Eight-hundred women who will be delivered by cesarean section will be enrolled in
      this study. Cultures will be obtained during the surgery from the open uterine cavity after
      removal of the placenta. Data regarding postpartum morbidity would be collected.
