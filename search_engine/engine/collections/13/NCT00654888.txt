
    
      Only patients with BK symptomatic (with pain) were submitted to automated lamellar
      keratectomy (ALK).

      Complete ophthalmological examination including UCVA,BSVA, biomicroscopy, tonometry,
      esthesiometry, UBM pachymetry, impression cytology and pain questionnaire were performed in
      preoperative, postoperative of one, seven, 30, 90, 180 days and one year.
