
    
      The data represented in this module is a pooled analysis of the following 3 studies:
      NCT00567489 (protocol ID 31998), NCT00567398 (protocol ID 34202), NCT01219959 (protocol
      ID51067). Given that the glucose content of the PD solutions is similar, the pooling of the
      results were considered a valid method to answer the underlying research questions.
