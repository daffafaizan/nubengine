attempts to transmit wuchereria bancrofti to cats and to a toque monkeythere are no records of the larval development of wuchereria bancrofti inexperimental animals.  in attempts to transmit periodic w. bancrofti todomestic cats and to a toque monkey we recovered from one of the cats aninfective larva that had undergone some development.  this finding wasreported in a preliminary note (dissanaike and niles, 1963); the present papergives the experimental details and describes the larva.
