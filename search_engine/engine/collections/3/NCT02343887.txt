
    
      This is an open prospective comparative monocentric study, pilote, that randomised 60
      patients in three groups of 20. This study will select 18 to 55 years old patients, with HIV
      infection controlled under antiretroviral therapy stble for 12 months and cognitive
      impairment objective on two neuropsychological evaluations separated from 6 months at least.
      All selected patients must have been explored by cerebral RMN or scanner
