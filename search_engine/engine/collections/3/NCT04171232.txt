
    
      A total of 30 children with spastic hemiplegic cerebral palsy would be recruited and randomly
      divided into three groups, 10 in each group. The demo-graphical data would be collected
      through a general demographic questionnaire. Outcome measures would include Goniometer to
      determine the Range of Motion (ROM), Manual Muscle Testing (MMT) to determine the upper limb
      muscle power/strength, disability of Arms, Shoulders, and Hand (DASH) questionnaire to assess
      the function of upper extremity, and Pediatric Balance Scale to determine the body balance.
