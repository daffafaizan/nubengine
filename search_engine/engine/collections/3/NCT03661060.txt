
    
      Multicentre, cross-sectional, prospective, non-interventional, observational study designed
      to evaluate the level of adherence to current ESC guidelines recommendations in both acute
      and chronic HF patients enrolled in each participating center and to verify if a specific HF
      educational intervention composed by an educational web based program of data collection and
      face to face meetings is able to improve adherence to guidelines recommendations evaluated
      during a second enrollment period.
