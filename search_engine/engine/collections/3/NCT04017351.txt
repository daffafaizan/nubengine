
    
      Eligible subjects will be randomized into to two groups. One group of subjects will be given
      access to the AIVA will be instructed to ask, at home, questions regarding the planned
      surgery to the AIVA application on their mobile phone. The other group will be instructed to
      call to the Standard of Care call center with questions related to their treatments.
      Researchers will follow up with all subjects from both groups for a 1 year amount of time.
