
    
      The aim of the present study is to determine whether usage of mesh with larger pores,
      compared with mesh with smaller pores, would result in decreased rate of chronic pain after
      open inguinal hernia repair. The patients are randomized into two study gropus receiving mesh
      with different pore size. Clinical follow-up examination will be performed 1 week, 1 month, 6
      months and 3 years after the operation.
