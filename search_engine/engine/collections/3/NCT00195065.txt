
    
      1. Through a series of open-ended questions we will explore and build a better
           understanding of how different patient populations view asthma and the difficulties they
           face in changing behavior. In addition the responses to such questions should help us to
           better understand and anticipate some of the barriers and issues that participants may
           face in successfully changing their behaviors.

        2. The second objective is to use the responses obtained to inform how an intervention of
           positive affect induction and self-affirmation should be operationalized and tailored.
