
    
      This study is going to clarify if there are some socioeconomical or psychosocial factors that
      might influence child's type 1 diabetes mellitus glycemic control. Study population consists
      of 300-350 diabetic children aged 1-16 years who have diabetic controls at Tampere University
      Hospital and their families. Required information is going to be collected with 2
      questionnaires, wich families are kindly asked to fill in.
