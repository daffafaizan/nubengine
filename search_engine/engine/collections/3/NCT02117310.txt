
    
      This study is being done to determine if it is feasible to use angiography during endo-nasal
      surgery. During the angiography indocyanine green (ICG) will be injected into the patients IV
      tubing to help see the arteries. ICG is routinely used in vascular surgeries on the brain but
      not for endo-nasal surgery. The purpose of this study is to see how well intraoperative
      angiography using ICG can be done during endonasal surgery.
