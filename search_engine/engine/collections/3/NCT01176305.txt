
    
      Venous thromboembolism (VTE) still causes maternal death in the western world. The purpose of
      this study is to estimate the relative and absolute risk of VTE at different gestational ages
      in pregnant women compared to non-pregnant women.

      All Danish Women 15 to 49 years old in the period January 1995 through December 2005, free of
      previous VTE and current use of oral contraceptives are included.Non-pregnant women are
      controls.
