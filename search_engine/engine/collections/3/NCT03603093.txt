
    
      Esophagogastroduodenoscopy (EGD) become more and more common in diagnosing gastroenterology
      diseases. And there are indications for EGD. Here, investigators want to find which symptoms
      can strongly support for positive endoscopy findings. So, investigators design the study to
      record the symptoms in detail and diagnosis, and then analysis symptoms and the rate of
      endoscopic findings.
