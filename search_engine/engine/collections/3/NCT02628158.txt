
    
      92 eyes with epiretinal membrane were performed vitreous surgery, epiretinal membrane removal
      and internal limiting membrane (ILM) peeling. Presence of hyperfluorescence in FAF at the
      macular area and best-corrected visual acuity (BCVA) was evaluated preoperatively and at
      postoperative 1, 3, and 6 months with ultra-wide angle imaging.
