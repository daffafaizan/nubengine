
    
      This study will use a 8 week parallel group design. 100 patients diagnosed as hypertrophic
      cardiomyopathy accompanied with depression will be into experimental group and control group.
      The experimental group received 8 times of psychological treatments for once each week; the
      control group did not receive any antidepressant treatment. Follow-up assessments will be
      performed at baseline, week 4 and 8.
