
    
      Primary objectives: To evaluate, in objective way, the usability of the future alpha version
      of the APDS device by testing the sensitivity and specificity of its results achieved from
      running it on the collected recorded videos of Colonoscopy screening tests of the general
      population.

      Secondary objectives: When the APDS would be ready for field installation (Beta Version), to
      install it in the Gastroenterology institute of the medical center, in order to evaluate, as
      part of a continuation study, the physicians satisfaction from the APDS performance.
