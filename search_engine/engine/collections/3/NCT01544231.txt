
    
      The secondary objectives of this are:

        -  to study plasma NGAL levels on admission to the emergency ward as a marker of morbidity
           and mortality by considering the following factors: duration of hospitalization, need
           for extracorporeal blood purification, ICU admission, death.

        -  to study the delay between the onset of the proposed cause of rhabdomyolysis and
           treatment initiation

        -  to study the delay between treatment initiation and the occurrence of ARF.
