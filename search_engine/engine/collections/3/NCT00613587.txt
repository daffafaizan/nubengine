
    
      Non-smoking allergic asthmatics will be verified using pulmonary function, skin prick
      testing, exhaled nitric oxide and serum IgE. If qualified, blood will be drawn, treated with
      study medication and then stimulated with the allergen to which the subject is allergic.
      Mediators including Histamine, IL-13, IL-4, and leukotrienes will be measured.
