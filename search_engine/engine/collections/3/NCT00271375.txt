
    
      Primary objectives of the research were:

        1. to evaluate user satisfaction with and perceived utility of the "Caring for You, Caring
           for Me" caregiver educational program among formal (VHA staff) and informal (family)
           caregivers who undergo the program;

        2. to assess effects of the program upon psychosocial and physical well-being of informal
           caregivers over a 1-year period .
