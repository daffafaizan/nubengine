
    
      The purpose is to evaluate exercise performance in patients with Chronic Obstructive
      Pulmonary Disease (COPD) using the 6-minute walk test (6MWT) after supplementing with higher
      doses of dietary NO3- compared to previous studies, as beetroot juice for seven days. The
      secondary purpose is to evaluate oxygen consumption of submaximal cycling, amendments in
      blood pressure, and physical activity level.
