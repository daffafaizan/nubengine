
    
      This trial is a prospective, single-centre, randomized controlled, and non-inferiority study
      in which all men with localized prostate cancer. This study aims to determine whether
      intraperitoneal laparoscopic radical prostatectomy without drainage tube is non-inferior to
      intraperitoneal laparoscopic radical prostatectomy with drainage tube in the safety in men
      with a loacalized prostate cancer.
