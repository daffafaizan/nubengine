
    
      OBJECTIVES:

      OBJECTIVES: Determine whether Traditional Chinese Medicine Formula LC09 can treat or reduce
      the severity of capecitabine-caused hand foot syndrome.

      Evaluate the potential toxicity of Traditional Chinese Medicine Formula LC09. OUTLINE: This
      is a multicenter, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study. Patients are randomized
      to 2 treatment arms.

      Arm I: Patients receive Traditional Chinese Medicine Formula LC09 by soaking their affected
      hand and feet 20 min twice daily.

      Arm II: Patients receive placebo by soaking their affected hand and feet 20 min twice daily.
