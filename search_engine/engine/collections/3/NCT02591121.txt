
    
      Healthy volunteers who meet eligibility criteria and have provided informed consent will be
      enrolled in this study. Each participant will be required to donate 1 blood sample via
      standard venipuncture into an EDTA (lavender top) tube for testing on one occasion. The
      maximum trial duration for each participant is one visit/occasion. Sample collection will
      take place within a single clinical session. The maximum trial duration for each participant
      is one clinic session.
