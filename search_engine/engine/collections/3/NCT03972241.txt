
    
      In the first year, it is to compare the functional difference of biomechanics and motor
      control between the healthy and the Parkinsonian patients. In the second year, it is to
      examine the immediate and short-term effects of functional foot insole and the kinetic
      training on daily activities in Parkinsonian patients. In the third year, it is to examine
      the hand tremor during drawing, and the coordination during reaching in Parkinsonian
      patients.
