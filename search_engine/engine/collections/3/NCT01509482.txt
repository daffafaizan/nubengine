
    
      During the past decade IR has been recognized as a major contributor to the pathogenesis of
      chronic anovulation, hyperandrogenism and metabolic abnormalities associated with PCOS .
      Theoretically IR in men may result in a similar reproductive abnormalities ( chronic
      hypospermatogenesis ) as well as other metabolic abnormalities similar to what happens in
      PCOS in women .
