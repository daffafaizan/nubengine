
    
      This study is a prospective ,multiple center study about the safety and efficacy of
      WeFlow-Arch Modeler Embedded Branch Stent Graft System, it is expected to complete the
      implantation of 80 patients in 23 centers within 12 months, and interim follow-up was
      conducted before discharge, 30 days after surgery, 6 months after surgery and 12 months after
      surgery, long-term follow-up will be performed at 24 months postoperatively, 36 months
      postoperatively, 48 months postoperatively and 60 months postoperatively.
