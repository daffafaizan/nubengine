
    
      Atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease is markedly increased in chronic kidney disease. There
      are a lot of risk factor, along with conventional risk factors. Serum onemtin-1 is a
      adipokine and suggested as a new marker for vascular disease.It has some anti-atherogenic
      properties. Studies investigating serum omentin-1 level have usually conducted in patients
      with diabetes mellitus.
