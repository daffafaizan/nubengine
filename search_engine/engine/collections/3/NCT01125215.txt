
    
      Painful diabetic neuropathy is the most common cause of neuropathic pain. 0.075% topical
      capsaicin has been used to treat the pain. Because of high concentration, conventional
      capsaicin topical preparation causing burning sensation and required several time of
      applications per day.

      The nanoparticle of capsaicin topical preparation was developed with expectation of remaining
      efficacy, minimizing burning sensation and improving convenience of use.
