
    
      This is a double blinded study whereby both the surgeons and patients are blinded from the
      sedative agent used. Patients admitted to UKMMC requiring ERCP will randomized into 2 groups
      after evaluating the inclusion and exclusion criteria. Patients will be divided into two
      arms, Midazolam and Ketamine group respectively. Before the initiation of the the scope,
      patient would be given specified dose of sedation accordingly. All the parameters and outcome
      would be measured during and after the procedure.
