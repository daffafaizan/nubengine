
    
      A RCT (Randomized Controlled Trial)to evaluate the role of 0.9% NACL (sodium chloride) IV
      fluids during an acute renal colic. The 2 arms will include -

        1. 2 liters of 0.9% NACL IV fluids with pain killers

        2. only pain killers The object is to find out if the IV fluids has any advantage in the
           care of renal colic. Does it helps to relive the pain? Does the stone eject earlier?
           Will there be fewer interventions?
