
    
      The aim of this project is to demonstrate the feasibility and the medico-economic impact of a
      spinal muscular atrophy neonatal screening in a European country. We propose a
      3-year-neonatal screening program in Southern Belgium (Wallonia-Brussels Federation) where
      there is 59.000 newborns/year, among whom 6 are affected by spinal muscular atrophy.
