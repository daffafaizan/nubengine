
    
      Twenty-two patients with ischemic stroke, subcortical, involving the internal capsule, will
      be included.

      Each patient will be compared to himself, on day zero to ten and three months.

      An MRI will be performed between day zero and ten, then three months of infarction sequences
      with traditional threeD enabling acquisition and tractography and The measurement of cortical
      thickness is in mm The measurement of fractional anisotropy of the pyramidal tract A clinical
      evaluation will be done between Day zero and Day ten then three months, using a neurological
      score (NIHSS), a score of functional disability (Rankin) and more analytical and sensitive
      tests such as dynamometer, the Finger Tapping test and test new plugs.
