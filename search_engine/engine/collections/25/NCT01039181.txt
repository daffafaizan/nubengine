
    
      -  OUTLINE: This is a dose-limiting toxicity study of calcitriol.

        -  EVALUAITON: During the initial phase, 14 patients will be accrued for this study. If the
           number of patient's response to calcitriol and 5-fluorouracil/mitomycin C/leucovorin is
           less than 1/14, the study will be stopped. However there is one patient who responses to
           calcitriol and 5-fluorouracil/mitomycin C/leucovorin, the new 14 patients will be
           accrued during the secondary phase of study.
