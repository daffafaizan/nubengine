
    
      Enrolled patients were randomly divided into 2 treatment groups. In the first group (IMAP)
      the IMA was preserved sectioning the sigmoid arteries near colonic wall while, in the second
      group (IMAS) the IMA was sectioned immediately below the origin of the left colic artery.
      Incidence and severity of sexual dysfunction and QoL were assessed by four validated
      questionnaire administered 6,12 months and 5 and 8 years after surgery.
