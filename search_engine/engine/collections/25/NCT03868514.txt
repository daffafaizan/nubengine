
    
      This international, multicentre, non-randomised, observational clinical device investigation
      will be performed to obtain post market information on TiLOOP® Bra Pocket surgical meshes for
      a period of up to two years. In particular, on patient reported satisfaction (BreastQTM),
      cosmetic outcome and the rate of complications.

      The objective of the clinical Investigation is to establish the efficacy and safety of the
      TiLOOP® Bra Pocket.

      The Investigation will be performed in ten clinical centres in Germany and Austria.
