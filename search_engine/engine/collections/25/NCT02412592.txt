
    
      HBeAg positive chronic hepatitis B patients achieved HBeAg seronconversion by interferon
      treatment will be enrolled and observed for 156 weeks. Serum HBV DNA, HBsAg, anti-HBs, HBeAg,
      and anti-HBe will be measured every 3 months during the observation period, liver function
      tests were performed every 3 months also. The liver ultrasonic examination would be taken
      every 3-6 months. The primary endpoint is sustained viral response, and secondary endpoints
      is HBV DNA reaction and clinical relapse defined as virla relapse with abnormal ALT during
      observation period.
