
    
      Most of patient with epiphora have different causes of nasolacrimal duct obstruction . This
      study aims to examine how important routine lacrimal sac biopsy is during endoscopic
      dacryocystorhinostomy surgery.

      The study included 50 patients with chronic unilateral epiphora. All patients underwent
      endoscopic dacryocystorhinostomy with nasolacrimal duct biopsy. Histopathologic analysis was
      performed for each specimen to assess the nature of the pathology.
