
    
      In this trial oral and dental status of 30 children (ages 5-12) who suffer from ADHD and
      treated with ritalin will be evaluated. Parameters that will be checked are the following:
      DMFT - decay, missing, filling teeth for dental status, GI - gingival index, PI - plaque
      index for periodontal status. OJ - over jet and OB - over bite for inter-maxillary relations
      and susceptibility for dental trauma. In addition these parameters will be collected for 30
      age and sex match healthy children.
