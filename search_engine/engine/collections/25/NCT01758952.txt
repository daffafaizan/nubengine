
    
      Patient population: Prospective cohort study of 10,000 adults undergoing non-cardiac,
      non-neurological surgery.

      Screening and enrollment: Consecutive patients undergoing (elective or emergency)
      non-cardiac, non-neurosurgical surgeries will be recruited.

      Monitoring, follow-up, and data collection: Usual treatment will be provided. Demographic
      details will be recorded. Patients will be visited regularly in hospital. Patient will be
      reviewed for neurologic deficit using the mNIHSS. Brain imaging will be performed to confirm
      stroke event. Follow-up at 30 days after discharge will be done to ascertain if there is any
      adverse outcome.
