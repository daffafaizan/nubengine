
    
      Postoperatively, the investigators analyze the patients' subjective and objective recurrent
      rates. The investigators compare the quality of life by filling out the preoperative and
      postoperative scoring questionnaires (PISQ 12, PFDI-20, PFIQ-7).The electrical physiological
      changes will be focused on. The investigators also use Overactive Bladder Symptom
      Score(OABss) to assess the lower urinary tract symptoms.
