
    
      Omentum is adipose tissue lying under inner anterior wall Omentectomy is routine procedure in
      treatment of gynecological malignancy especially ovarian cancer and some advanced stage
      endometrial cancer. Perilipin, Leptin, Adipolipin, Adiponectin, Resistin, Visfatin, Estrone
      secrete from adipose tissue. These regulate matabolic process.

      We aimed comparison of Perilipin, Leptin, Adipolipin, Adiponectin, Resistin, Visfatin,
      Estrone levels derivated from adipose tissue pre-postoperatively in patient diagnosed with
      gynecological malignancy performed omentectomy or not. Blood sample will study after 4000 rpm
      centrifugal for 10 minutes
