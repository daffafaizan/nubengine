
    
      The specific research aims of this proposal are:

      Aim 1: Develop and test a group curriculum for parents of children (age 6 to 18 years) with
      ADHD to increase parental knowledge about ADHD and self-confidence in managing issues related
      to their child's functioning in school and home.

      Aim 2: Develop and test a group curriculum for children (age 6 to 18 years) with ADHD to
      teach social and educational skills to improve adaptive functioning at home and school.

      Aim 3: To assess any added benefits to the parents, children and providers (related to group
      visit logistics and satisfaction) the group visit model has over usual care.

      Aim 4: To assess whether the group visit model can be done efficiently and effectively in the
      setting of an actual general pediatric practice.
