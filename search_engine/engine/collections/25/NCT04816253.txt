
    
      Impacted Mandibular third molar will be extracted under local anesthesia Through standard
      surgical flap elevation, after extraction and wound irrigation a material facilitate healing
      will be placed. According to material will be placed patients will be divide into 3 group:
      control ( no material, second group hyaluronic acid, third group ( honey).. then wound will
      be closed. Postoperative pain ( through visual analog scale) , edema, total dose of
      analgesic, mandibular movement (interincisal distance in mm) will be evaluated preoperative,
      1,2,3,7, and 10 day after extraction
