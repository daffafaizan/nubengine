
    
      In this way, the main objective of this study is to characterize the oral language in a
      population of fifteen subjects with Möbius syndrome with age ranging from two to thirteen
      years old, from both sexes, seen in one of the three main center for genetic diagnosis in the
      state of Ceará. The present work is a descriptive study of a cross-sectional design conducted
      from February to December/2002, using a clinical evaluation of language components including
      morphology, syntax, semantics and pragmatics.
