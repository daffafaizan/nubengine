
    
      Methodology: Multicentric French national cohort with prospective collection of data and
      constitution of biobank, in patients hospitalized in a context of acute liver failure without
      known cause at admission. Follow-up during 3 months for each patients. The primary objective
      is to determine predictive factors of early mortality. This study is an observational cohort
      and patients will received standard care during the follow-up.
