
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. To assess whether an educational brochure and online program will increase sun-protective
      behaviors among African-American patients who present at the general dermatology clinic.

      SECONDARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. To assess whether the educational brochure and online program will improve skills for skin
      self-examinations among African-American patients who present at the general dermatology
      clinic.

      OUTLINE: Participants are assigned to 1 of 2 arms.

      ARM I: Participants receive a skin cancer educational and preventive brochure distributed by
      the National Cancer Institute and watch a 30-minute online tutorial video called X-Plain
      about skin cancer, preventative behaviors, and skin self-examinations.

      ARM II: Participants receive an educational brochure as in Arm I.

      After completion of study, participants are followed up at 30 days.
