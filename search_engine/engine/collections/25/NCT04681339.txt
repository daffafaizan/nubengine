
    
      An observational study to document the antibiotic treatment rate of children hospitalized for
      community acquired pneumonia at a pediatric department with longstanding practice of
      restrictive antibiotic prescribing. Patients will be enrolled consecutively and treated
      according to in-house standard operating procedure. Antibiotic treatment rate, severity of
      disease and medical complication rate in antibiotic and non-antibiotic managed children,
      frequency of predefined factors in physician decision making on antibiotic use, and parental
      satisfaction will be recorded. Parents will be contacted at least 4 weeks after discharge to
      inquire about recurrence or readmission.
