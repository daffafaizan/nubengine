
    
      This is a Randomised Controlled Trial informed by prior feasibility and pilot work
      (ClinicalTrials.gov IDs: NCT03509792, NCT04185155). The primary outcome is the number of
      intrusive memories of the traumatic event(s) (week 5). The intervention is delivered remotely
      (e.g. via smartphone) with potential remote support (e.g. by phone) to medical staff in
      Swedish hospitals.
