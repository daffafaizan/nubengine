
    
      Breast milk contains a variety of nutrients that are beneficial to the early development of
      infants, such as lipids, Vitamins and so on. At present, the level of these nutrients in milk
      and infant formula are lower and which are present in a different form from that in breast
      milk. Therefore, this study was designed to investigate the level of nutrients in breast milk
      of Chinese mothers and learn about its related factors, as well as exploring the
      relationships between these nutrients and the early development of infants.
