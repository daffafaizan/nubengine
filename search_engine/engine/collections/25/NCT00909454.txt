
    
      To determine the feasibility of African American teenagers taking a daily vitamin D
      supplement over a 4 month period in terms of compliance and practical implementation.

      To determine the differences in response in blood 25-OH D level between those randomly
      assigned to taking a 400 IU supplement versus 2000 IU per day and to determine if there are
      differences in blood 25-OH D level response depending on subject gender and overweight/obese
      versus healthy-weight status.
