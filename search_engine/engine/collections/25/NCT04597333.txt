
    
      Nulliparous women with obstetric indications for induction of labor who were induced with a
      dinoprostone insert and failed to go into labor after 24 hours from the beginning of labor
      induction and a BISHOP score of less than 5 will receive a thorough explanation regarding the
      study and sign informed consent. The eligible women will be randomized to 1 of 2 groups.
      Induction with a cervical ripening balloon versus induction with a 2nd dinoprostone insert.
      Relevant demographic and medical information will be gathered from the patients' electronic
      files.
