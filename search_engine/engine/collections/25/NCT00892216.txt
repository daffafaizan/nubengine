
    
      The rationale is to do a prospective study in patients who have been diagnosed with Pectus
      Excavatum and who are undergoing a NUSS procedure (pectus excavatum correction) and to
      evaluate the effect of Acupressure on the post-operative outcome of PONV in these patients.
      Also, to determine whether there are any secondary outcomes related to pain scores and length
      of hospital stay.
