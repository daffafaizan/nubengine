
    
      The healthy control group will comprise volunteers who are at least 18 years of age, have no
      history of pre-existing lung disease, and report no respiratory illness in the four weeks
      preceding enrollment.

      The symptomatic deployers who have undergone lung biopsy and meet the case definition for
      deployment-related lung disease. A case of deployment-related lung disease is defined as the
      presence of unexplained chest symptoms in a deployer who, on a surgical lung biopsy, is found
      to have bronchiolitis or granulomatous pneumonitis without other known cause.
