
    
      The JETi Registry is an open label, prospective, non-randomized, multi-center observational
      registry including 250 patients who meet eligibility from up to 30 sites worldwide. Patient
      participation will be 12 months including post procedure follow-up contact at 10 days, 3, 6
      and 12 months. The study duration is estimated at 36 months.
