
    
      The main aim of the study is to compare the effects of in-patient treatment with a group
      based intensive out-patient treatment. It is a randomized controlled comparison study
      including patients with concurrent eating disorder and personality disorder. 18 patients will
      receive an in-patient treatment lasting one year. 18 patients will receive intensive
      out-patient treatment consisting of two-weekly group sessions, lasting one year. Personality
      disorder, eating behavior, symptoms, and function will be followed for two years.
