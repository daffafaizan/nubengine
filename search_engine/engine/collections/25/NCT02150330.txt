
    
      This study was designed as a prospective case-control study to assess the gene expression of
      cumulus cells after DHEAS supplement. Patients with diminished ovarian reserve under ovarian
      hyper-stimulation protocol were evaluated at Kaoshiung Veteran General Hospital from January
      1, 2013 through October 31, 2013. Approval for the study was obtained from the hospital's
      ethic committee, and informed consent was obtained from all patients (VGHKS13-CT11-17)
