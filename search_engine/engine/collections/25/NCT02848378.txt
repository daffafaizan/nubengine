
    
      Oxidative stress is the disequilibrium between the formation of free radicals and antioxidant
      defense mechanisms via damaging cellular macromolecules (DNA, protein, lipid, etc..) which
      are susceptible to oxidative damage.

      Advanced oxidation protein product is identified as a new marker of protein oxidation.

      Monocyte chemoattractant protein-1 is a major chemoattractant for lymphocytes, monocytes and
      macrophages.
