
    
      Healthy, consenting and informed patients of both gender under 55 years of age with apical
      periodontitis will be enrolled as cases. Healthy, consenting and informed patients of both
      gender under 55 years of age without apical periodontitis will be enrolled as control. The
      study has been designed to assess the serum level of lipopolysaccharide-binding protein
      (LBP), TNF-α, IL-1β, IL-6, IL-8 and the platelet factor 4 (PF4) by ELISA assay. PF4 will be
      dosed also in platelets.
