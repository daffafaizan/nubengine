
    
      In this clinical trial study, our case group will receive I-BiTTM in the format of three
      playing games which have been designed suitable to the age of our cases. The mechanism of
      I-BiTTM has been designed in three playing games with inducing more foveal stimulation for
      the amblyopic eye through presentation of movable targets with more detailed. In addition,
      unmovable targets will presented to the non-amblyopic eyes. Playing games were continued 30
      minutes in each session for 5 days in each week within one month.
