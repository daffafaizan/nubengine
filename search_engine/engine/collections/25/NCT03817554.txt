
    
      Peritoneal dialysis patients diagnosed with restless legs syndrome will be divided into
      experiment group and control group, and will be prescribed with pramipexole and placebo
      respectively.After 12 weeks, we will compare IRLSSG（International RLS Study Group Rating
      Scale）、MOS（Medical Outcomes Study ）Sleeping Scale、Self-Rating Anxiety Scale and Self-Rating
      Depression Scale before and after the prescription.
