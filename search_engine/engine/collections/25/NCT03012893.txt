
    
      To identify the accurate level of upper thoracic spine is essential maneuver during breast
      surgery and axillary node dissection. Palpation C7 spinous process is most common technique
      using to identify the level. However, the accuracy was lower than 50%. Ultrasonography has
      been investigated to identify the cervical nerve roots by differentiating the transvers
      process of C6 and C7. However, there was no study on the accuracy of using ultrasound to
      identify cervical spine level before.
