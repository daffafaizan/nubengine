
    
      The purpose of this study is to examine whether the expression levels of a novel class of
      molecules, called microRNAs, are associated with acute graft-versus-host disease frequency
      and severity. Acute graft-versus-host disease is a Acute GVHD is a medical condition that can
      become very serious. It happens when the donor cells attack and damage your tissues after
      transplant.
