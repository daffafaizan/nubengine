
    
      Primary outcome:

      Incidence of complete miscarriage (complete expulsion of the products of conception with no
      need for surgical intervention within one week from the first dose of misoprostol)

      Secondary outcome:

      Need for surgical evacuation of the products of conception

        -  Incomplete expulsion of the products of conception (incomplete miscarriage).

        -  Considerable bleeding necessitating immediate surgical evacuation. Maternal morbidity

        -  Major side effects (Sepsis, considerable vaginal bleeding leading to hemodynamic
           instability or necessitating blood transfusion)

        -  Minor side effects (fever, rigors, nausea, vomiting) Patient's compliance and adherence
           to treatment Hemoglobin and hematocrit deficit
