
    
      The IHCIS is Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) compliant with
      encrypted member and provider identifiers; date of birth is restricted to year of birth.
      Based on year of birth and the prescription sales claim date, age at the time of the
      prescription(s) will be categorized as less than 9 months (definitive) and less than 9 months
      (uncertain).
