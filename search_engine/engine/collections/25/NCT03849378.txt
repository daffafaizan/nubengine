
    
      Previous studies have indicated that Panax Ginseng C.A. Mey Extract may have the ability to
      improve liver function. Therefore, the investigators conduct a randomized, double-blind,
      placebo-controlled study to investigate the effects of the Sargassum Horneri Extract in
      patients with skin hypersensitivity for 12 weeks; the safety of the compound are also
      evaluate. The Investigators examine SCORAD(SCORing of Atopic Dermatitis) score, PGA (Patient
      Global assessment) score, IGA (Investigator Global assessment) score, total IgE, eosinophil
      counts, ECP, IL-4, IL-31, and TARC at baseline, as well as after 6 and 12 weeks of
      intervention. One hundred adults were administered either 1,000 mg of Sargassum Horneri
      Extract or a placebo each day for 12 weeks.
