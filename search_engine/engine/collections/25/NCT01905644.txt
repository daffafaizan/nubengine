
    
      The secondary objectives of this study are:

      A. Compare the anal incontinence score (measured by the Wexner score, qualitative and
      quantitative assessment) at 3 months between the two groups.

      B. To assess the safety of perineal ultrasound in the group with "ultrasound" using a visual
      analog scale for discomfort

      C. Assess the inter and intra operator consistency of perineal ultrasound snapshot readings

      D. Evaluate and compare the rate of infectious complications of perineal sutures between the
      two groups at 3 months.

      E. Evaluate and compare the quality of life (questionnaires PFIQ, PFDI, SF36, Euroqol)
      between the two groups at 3 months.
