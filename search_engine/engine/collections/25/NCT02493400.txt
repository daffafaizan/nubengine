
    
      Patients who have completed study will be offered a follow-up after 3 months regarding
      physical fitness, physical activity, heart rate and HR-QoL. Physical fitness will be measured
      by a symptom limited bicycle test and a muscle endurance test. Physical activity will be
      measured by accelerometer and the International Physical Activity Questionnaire (IPAQ) and a
      physical activity diary, HR-QoL will be measured by short form (SF-36).
