
    
      Participants are defined as persons who have endoscopically confirmed on gastric or duodenal
      ulcer(including scar stage) ,gastritis and dyspepsia confirmed to be H.pylori positive
      patients in the biopsy and UBT test. For 7 days, Participants treated as Esoprazole based
      triple regimen therapy therapy including Esoprazole 20mg，Clarithromycin 500mg and Amoxicillin
      Cap(Amoxicillin) 1000mg BID. After treatment, The healing rate was evaluated in the UBT test
      and Biopsy at 49±5days from the first day dosing
