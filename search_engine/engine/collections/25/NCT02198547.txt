
    
      Patients are randomly divided into two groups: group "M" (Ropivacaina 7,5 mg/ml with Mg 4
      mg/Kg)and group"C" (without Mg).

      Standard monitoring is applied to each patient in perioperative period. An indipendent
      observer collect data: time to perform block, sensitivity onset,motor onset, pain during
      surgery and complication.

      In the post-operative period till 24 hours for each patient is evaluated off-set time, pain
      and analgesic consumption.
