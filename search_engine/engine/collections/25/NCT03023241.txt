
    
      Collection and analyses of bladder biopsies, bladder washings, blood and urine samples of
      patients with bladder pain syndrome/interstitial cystitis (BPS/IC), overactive bladder
      syndrome (OAB) and bladder-healthy controls. Analyses include ELISA, RT-qPCR, DNA sequencing,
      and immunohistochemistry techniques. Differences between patient groups are statistically
      evaluated.
