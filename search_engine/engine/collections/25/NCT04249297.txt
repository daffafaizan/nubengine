
    
      Research on significant factors (predictors) of pregnancy rate in In Vitro Fertilization
      (IVF) and Intracytoplasmatic Sperm Injection (ICSI) cycles in Russian population according to
      actual national and international guidelines in patients using oral dydrogesterone for luteal
      phase support in assisted reproductive technology. Investigating the strength of the
      relationship and assessing coefficients of impact on the pregnancy rate.

      Creating a predictive table of Clinical Pregnancy in IVF and ICSI cycles and 4-5 most impact
      predictors.
