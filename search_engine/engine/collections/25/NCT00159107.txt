
    
      The objective of this multi-center study is to optimize the long-term treatment of patients
      with alcohol dependence. This investigation should contribute to the issue of effective
      relapse prevention. Each treatment condition is lasting 6 months: a) Behavior Group Therapy +
      Acamprosate, b) Behavior Group Therapy + Placebo and c) support and counseling + Acamprosate.
      Follow-up examinations carried out 3 and 6 months after the end of treatment.
