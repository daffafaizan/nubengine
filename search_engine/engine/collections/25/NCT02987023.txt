
    
      The study is a follow up of NCT01844362. Uterus transplantation will be performed from live
      donors to patients with uterine factor infertility. If will be performed before
      transplantation. Organ procurement from the donor will be performed by robotic assisted
      laparoscopy. Transplantation will be by laparotomy. Embryo transfer will be done 10-12 months
      after transplantation. After birth of 1-3 children the uterus will be removed by
      hysterectomy.
