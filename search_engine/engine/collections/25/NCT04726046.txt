
    
      529 patients performed elective LCC at Chonbuk National University Hospital between April
      2014 and August 2015. Total 509 patients were enrolled by inclusion criteria. They were
      randomized studied by comparing with antibiotic group (n=249, AG, cefotetan 1g, 1
      dose/prophylactic) and non-antibiotic group (n=260, NAG) by table of random numbers. The
      clinical variables were pre and post-operatively blood tests enclude WBC, ESR, CRP, body
      temperatures, symptoms and imaging of chest x-ray to evaluate the infections.
