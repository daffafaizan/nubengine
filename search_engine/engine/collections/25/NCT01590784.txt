
    
      Measurements:

      The Thai version of Calgary depression scale of schizophrenia (CDSS), Positive and Negative
      Syndrome Scale (PANSS), Hamilton Rating Scale for Depression-17 (HAM-D 17), Montgomery-Asberg
      Depression Rating Scale (MADRS), the Thai abbreviated version of World Health Organization
      quality of life (WHOQOL-BREF-THAI).
