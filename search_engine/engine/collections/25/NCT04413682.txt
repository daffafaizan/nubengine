
    
      Participants are randomly assigned to the structured supervised exercise or structured
      home-based groups. Interventions in Group 1 (n=15) includes one session per day, 3 days a
      week over a 12-weeks structured exercise program under the supervision of a physical
      therapist. Group 2 (n=15) includes a 12-week structured home-based exercise program checked
      and controlled through telerehabilitation three times a week. All measurements pre-post
      intervention.
