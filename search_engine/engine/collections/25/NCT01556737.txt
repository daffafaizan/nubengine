
    
      Intervention study with two substudies; each substudy is a double blind placebo controlled
      crossover intervention study. The first substudy has two groups: 'high daidzein' supplement
      versus placebo (n=18) and vice versa (n=18); the second substudy also has two groups 'high
      genistein' versus placebo (n=18) and vice versa (n=18).
