
    
      It is a translation and validation of a quality of life scale.

      Step 1: Translation and cultural adaptation (Forward and back translation, committee review,
      pre-testing with 10 patients for content and face validity).

      Step 2 : Psychometric testing : Test-retest with a 2 day interval, correlation with clinical
      scores - the Myasthenic Muscle Score and the (MG-ADL) score and with a global quality of life
      score (WHOQOL-BREF).
