
    
      The objective of this study is to assess the safety and efficacy of Ocular Iontophoresis with
      Dexamethasone Phosphate 40 mg/mL 7.5 mA-min at 2.5 mA and Ocular Iontophoresis with
      Dexamethasone Phosphate 40 mg/mL 10.5 mA-min at 3.5 mA compared to placebo for the treatment
      of the signs and symptoms of dry eye.
