
    
      The objective of this study is to collect post market data on safety and performance of the
      Atlas Knee System in patients with osteoarthritis (OA) of the medial knee compartment through
      a 24 months post-operative follow-up period. This study is designed as a post-market,
      prospective, multicenter, single arm study. The study population will consist of adult
      subjects older than 25 years of age, with a diagnosis of symptomatic and radiographic OA of
      the medial knee compartment.
