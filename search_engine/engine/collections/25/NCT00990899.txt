
    
      This is an exploratory, cross sectional study involving identification of the children of the
      age between 10 years and 17 years diagnosed with primary hypertension who have not been
      previously diagnosed and or treated for sleep disordered breathing with the revised pediatric
      sleep questionaire (PSQ) for sleep disordered breathing. The subjects that have shown
      interest will be mailed the informed consent/assent form to tbe reviewed. The consent/assent
      form will be handed to the child and all their questions and concerns will be answered in a
      comprehensible manner.
