
    
      The lipoprotein response will be performed in 15 healthy individuals recruited by newspaper
      advertisements. A member of the study team will explain the study in detail on visit 1, and
      if the individual is interested in participating, informed consent will be obtained and
      preliminary screening will occur. On visit 2 participants will receive a high fat liquid meal
      after an overnight fast. Hourly blood samples will be taken for 5 hours, followed by a
      subcutaneous teduglutide injection and simultaneous glucose drink. Following the injection
      and drink, blood samples will be taken every 15 minutes for the first hour and every 30
      minutes for the subsequent 3 hours.
