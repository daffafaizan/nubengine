
    
      Stage Ⅰ is a cross-sectional study to record selected information and biospecimen of more
      than 200 colorectal cancer cases( at least 160 stage Ⅲ cases,40 stage Ⅳ（synchronous
      metastasis）).Stage Ⅱ is a nested case-control study. 200 eligible cases enrolled in
      phaseⅠwill be followed up for 5 years to record recurrence, metastasis and to collect
      biospecimen . Each case with the study outcome will be matched with the corresponding control
      according to the proportion of 1:1 according to age, sex and other conditions, and further
      analysis will be made.
