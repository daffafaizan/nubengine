
    
      This is an open label within-subject feasibility study, in 20 patients with Alcohol Use
      Disorder who have recently undergone detoxification. All patients will receive MDMA-Assisted
      drug therapy. This study aims to assess if MDMA-Assisted Psychotherapy can be delivered
      safely and can be tolerated by patients with alcohol use disorder post-detoxification.
      Outcomes regarding abstinence from alcohol, quality of life and psychosocial functioning will
      be evaluated.
