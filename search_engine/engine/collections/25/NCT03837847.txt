
    
      Participants will be randomized to either 12 weeks of weekly telephone health coaching calls
      followed by 12 weeks of living a normal life or 12 weeks of living a normal life (in addition
      to 6 brief educational guides and 6 non-health related calls), followed by 12 weeks of health
      coaching over the phone.

      Questionnaires and an activity monitor worn for one week will be used at baseline, 3,6 and 12
      months.
