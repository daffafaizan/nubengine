
    
      CARECA-CHF study is a non-randomized, prospective, single-arm study, multicentric study.
      Patients with stable chronic heart failure [New York Heart Association (NYHA) class II/III]
      will be enrolled ambulatory. The program will take place during a period of 06-08 weeks.

      The aim of this study is to assess the effect of a cardiac rehabilitation program on patients
      with stable chronic heart failure in a resource-limited country.
