
    
      This will be a a randomized, single-blind, placebo-controlled, single-dose, dose-escalation
      study. Five escalating dose groups are planned, with 8 subjects per dose group, randomized in
      3:1 ratio to receive a single dose of MOD-6031 or matching Placebo (6 MOD-6031 to 2 placebo
      subjects). The initial MOD-6031 dose will be 20 mg, followed by single doses of 50 mg, 100
      mg, 150 mg and 200 mg. Each subject will receive a single study drug injection in the morning
      (dosing day designated as Day 0) and will be monitored for 30 days.
