
    
      Patients are referred to Mayo Clinic Rochester with an establish diagnosis of EoE and are
      nonresponsive to proton pump inhibitor (PPI) medical therapy. Eligible patients will then
      meet with one of three investigators complete the Mayo Dysphagia Questionaire-30 Day (MDQ-30)
      following which a standardized Allergy Patch testing (APT) will be conducted. Thereafter, a
      standard clinically indicated Six Food Elimination Diet treatment completed. Patients will
      follow up with one of three investigators following the elimination diet who will be blinded
      to the results of the APT. During this visit responders and nonresponders will be identified
      and nonresponders will complete a directed elimination diet based on APT results.
