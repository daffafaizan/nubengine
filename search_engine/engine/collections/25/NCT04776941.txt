
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. To evaluate the feasibility of the virtual study for cancer survivors. II. To
      preliminarily assess the impact of the intervention for cancer survivors.

      OUTLINE: Patients are randomized to 1 of 2 arms.

      ARM I (INTERVENTION): Patients complete questionnaires over 30 minutes about their mood,
      health, and income at baseline, and 1, 3, and 6 months. Patients also read brief positive
      messages and write essays about their experiences over 30 minutes (non-stop) once weekly (QW)
      for 3 weeks.

      ARM II (CONTROL): Patients complete questionnaires over 30 minutes about their mood, health,
      and income at baseline, and 1, 3, and 6 months. Patients also read brief neutral messages and
      write essays about neutral topics over 30 minutes (non-stop) QW for 3 weeks.
