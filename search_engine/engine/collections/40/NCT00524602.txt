
    
      The purpose on this study is to show the utility and the safety by using the remote magnetic
      navigation of ablation catheters with the Stereotaxis equipment at ablations of

        -  atrial fibrillation, atrial flutter and other arrythmias

        -  childrens arrythmias

        -  arrythmias of patients with pacemaker or ICD (Implantable Cardiac Defibrillator)
