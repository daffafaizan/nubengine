
    
      Intervention: Arm B (control): chemotherapy alone, regimen according to each center. Arm A
      (experimental): surgery (complete tumoral resection; R0) followed by chemotherapy, regimen
      according to each center. Statistical Analysis: A power analysis showed that to assure a
      significance level of 0,05 and a beta error 0.20. 168 patients are necessary in each arm. It
      has been estimated a loss rate of up to 10%.

      Differences between groups will be analyzed by t, U, X2, exact test and survival will be
      assessed according to Kaplan and Meier method. Evaluation of safety of the trial will be made
      in the middle of the study statistically
