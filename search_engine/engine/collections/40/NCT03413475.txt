
    
      Patients are selected at the beginning of their prosthetic rehabilitation and selected
      patients give their consent for the participation at the clinical trial. The filling of the
      GOHAI questionnaire is performed in 3 steps:1- before treatment, 2- after teeth extractions
      and the delivering of immediate complete denture or without immediate prosthetic
      rehabilitation and 3-during inspection visits after the delivery of the definitive denture.
