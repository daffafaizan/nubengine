
    
      Aim of the work

      We aim to examine the prevalence of KC among patients already diagnosed by endocrinologist
      with TGD.

      Materials and methods

      Our Screening for KC will include evaluation of the demographic data and complete
      ophthalmological examination of the patients using:

      VA Refraction K reading Slit lamp examination Pentacam Study design Epidemiological study

      Inclusion citeria All patients diagnosed as thyroid gland dysfunction Control group

      Exclusion criteria Uncooperative patients
