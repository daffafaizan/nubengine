
    
      This study tests the impact of an innovative combination of a fortified beverage (a diet cola
      containing green tea extract, soluble dextrin fiber, and caffeine) and an online support
      weight loss program in a multi-center randomized clinical trial. The study compares the
      impact of all combinations of the beverage and the online behavioral modification treatment
      program over 12 weeks at three treatment center in the US and EU.
