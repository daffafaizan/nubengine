
    
      The treatment protocols of MWA and SR were according to the Chinese and international
      guidelines. MWA need to ablate all tumors including at least 2mm safe margin except for
      tumors adjacent to thyroid capsule. SR basic protocol is lobectomy + central lymph node
      dissection. All patients need to receive levothyroxine therapy to keep TSH lower than 0.5
      mU/L. Patients in both groups underwent regular medical imaging and laboratory tests 1 month,
      3-month, 6-month, 12-month, 18-month, 24-month and 36-month after treatment.
