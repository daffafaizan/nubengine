
    
      Patients undergoing FBS for lung cancer diagnosis purpose will be enrolled in the study.
      During FBS Fine Need Aspiration (FNA) will be performed. Four samplings by FNA will be
      performed in each patient. Two samplings will be processed with thin prep technique and two
      samplings with the traditional procedure. To avoid sampling procedures-related bias, the
      first harvested sample will be randomly tested with Thin Prep or Traditional technique.
