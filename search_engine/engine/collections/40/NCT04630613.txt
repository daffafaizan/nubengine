
    
      This will be a cross sectional study and will include male and female, young (18-40-years)
      well characterized patients with early (eGFR>90mL/min/1.73m2) ADPKD. Patients with different
      classes of disease severity will be selected using the ADPKD imaging classification. The
      study will include 60 class-1 (A-E) patients (N=12 per each sub-class).
