
    
      Single blind pilot trial assessing the efficacy and side effects of active training or
      osteopathic manipulative treatment in ALS.

      Interventions will be administered for three months (plus one month without intervention) by
      specialized personnel.

      Primary outcome measures will include: exercise tolerance and muscle oxygen extraction
      capacity, side effects. Secondary outcome measures will include: pain, QoL and disease
      progression scales.
