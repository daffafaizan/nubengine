
    
      The main objective of the study is to determine if implication of epoxy-eicosatriénoïques
      acids (EETs) and NO during cutaneous post-occlusive hyperemia differs between patients with
      Raynaud phenomena and healthy volunteers, by studying hyperaemic postocclusive response after
      microinjection of fluconazole and L-NMMA at the dorsal side of the fingers.
