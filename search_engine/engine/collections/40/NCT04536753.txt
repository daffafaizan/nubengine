
    
      This is a retrospective cohort study of pregnant women taken from 3 groups; women with a
      suspected LGA fetus (LGA), women with diabetes (DM) and a control group of women that
      underwent induction of labour at or after 40 weeks. Scan accuracy using GROW and WHO charts
      in the LGA and DM cohorts was assessed using ROC curves and outcomes between the cohorts was
      compared.
