
    
      In a 3 years period adult patients with obstructive epiphora were evaluated clinically by
      Probing /irrigation of lacrimal passages to detect anatomical level of obstruction.

      The main site of obstruction was the common canaliculus where 2/3 of patients had canalicular
      problems. The second common site of obstruction is nasolacrimal duct followed by the puncti.
