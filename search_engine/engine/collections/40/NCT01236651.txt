
    
      Comparison Between Meperidine and Drotaverine Regarding the Effect on the Duration of the
      First Stage of Labor in Full Term Primigravidae

      Protocol of Thesis Submitted for partial fulfillment of master degree in Obstetrics &
      Gynecology

      The aim of this work is to compare and to evaluate the efficacy of drotaverine hydrochloride
      versus meperidine hydrochloride on the duration of the 1st stage of labor.
