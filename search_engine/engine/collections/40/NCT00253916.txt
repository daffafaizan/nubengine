
    
      A total of 220 men with a diagnosis of prostate cancer, scheduled to receive radiation will
      be recruited. The study is a parallel 3-group design with groups stratified for intended
      duration of ADT ( greater or less than 12 weeks) and random assignment to aerobic, resistance
      or Wait List control groups. The dependent variable of interest will fatigue measured at 24
      weeks by the 13 item FACT-Fatigue. Dependent variables of secondary interest will be toxicity
      of radiation, body composition, muscular fitness, cardio-respiratory fitness, metabolic
      fitness, and prostate specific QOL. All exercise sessions will be performed 3 times per week
      at the Ottawa Hospital regional Cancer Center
