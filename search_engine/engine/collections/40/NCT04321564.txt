
    
      The beck depression and anxiety scale consists of questionnaire questions asked to the
      patient, and the value on 17 indicates advanced depression.

      The BDI inventory has maximum of 63 and minimum of 0 scores:

      (0-10): Are considered normal ups and downs (11-16): Mild mood disturbance (17-20):
      Borderline clinical depression (21-30): Moderate depression, (31-40): Severe depression, Over
      40: Extreme depression. A persistent score of !17, requires psychiatric treatment
