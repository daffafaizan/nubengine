
    
      Study participation by invitation. Patients will be invited to participate after completing
      all necessary clinical visits including lab studies, counseling, and participation in
      educational programs. During the pre-operative visit patients interested in participation
      will complete all necessary consent documentation, during surgery tissue collection will
      include subcutaneous & omental fat, jejunum (Gastric Bypass), gastric remnant (sleeve
      gastrectomy), and liver biopsy. Post surgery patient will be observed for weight loss and
      resolution of co-morbidities. At designated times post surgery when lab studies are ordered
      additional blood/urine samples will be collected and analyzed.
