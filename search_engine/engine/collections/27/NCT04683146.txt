
    
      The investigators conducted a crossover clinical trial to evaluate the antiseptic
      effectiveness of surgical hand antisepsis using propan-ol-1 60% by using the reference
      antiseptic product propan-ol-1 60% as control versus a surgical hand antisepsis with addition
      of a solution of alcohol, chlorhexidine digluconate and potassium sorbate in order to test if
      pass the standard european norm 12791. Samples will be taken from the hands of each volunteer
      after a surgical hands antiseptic.
