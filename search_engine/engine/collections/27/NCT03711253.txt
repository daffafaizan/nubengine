
    
      Individuals with suspected acute HIV infection willing to start immediate ART treatment will
      be given the ART regimen (bictegravir 50mg + tenofovir alafenamide 25 mg + emtricitabine 200
      mg fixed dose combination) on the day of diagnosis. Those patients beginning immediate ART in
      the ED who are willing to have additional laboratory specimens drawn in the ED and become
      part of a cohort to receive ART and have ongoing biological specimens obtained and will be
      followed for 48 weeks.
