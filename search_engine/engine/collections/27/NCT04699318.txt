
    
      In group A dartos flap is raised from foreskin of the patient and applied as a single layer
      after tubularization in between neourethra and skin . In group B dartos flap is raised from
      the foreskin of patient and divided in two equal halves.Both flaps are placed as two layers
      in between skin and neourethra.After surgery patient is kept in ward for 10 days with
      stent.Patient is followed up in OPD on after 7th day after discharge for outcome.
