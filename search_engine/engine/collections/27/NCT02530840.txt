
    
      In this study the investigators use 3 interventions in diabetic patients and one control, to
      find what is the best intervention to improve adherence to healthy life style and medication
      therapy. the 3 intervention as followed: 1) meetings and follow-up by the medical team; 2)
      meetings and follow-up by trained peers; 3) system for sending SMS, operate according to
      algorithm.
