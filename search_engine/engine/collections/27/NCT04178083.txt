
    
      The aim of this study was to compare the results of laparoscopic sacrohysteropexy,
      laparoscopic modified lateral suspens9ion and laparoscopic pectopexy between January 2017 and
      December 2019 in our clinic.(Patients with at least 12 months of the date of surgery will be
      called for examination.)

      We will evaluate Pelvic Organ Prolapse Quantitative System (POP-Q), Prolapse Quality of Life
      Questionnaire (PQoL), Pelvic Organ Prolapse Symptom Score (POP-SS), Visual Analogue Score
      (VAS), routine gynecologic USG, failure, complication, operation time .
