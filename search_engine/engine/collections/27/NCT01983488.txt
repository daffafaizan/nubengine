
    
      Patients will be included if they are treated for uveitis and have had repeat follow-up. Data
      will be gathered from the entire length of the patients follow-up.

      The main outcome measures will be:

      Change from baseline of Best corrected visual acuity (BCVA)at 10 years Causes of vision loss
      (≤6/15, VL) and severe vision loss (≤6/60, SVL) at 10 years Number of clinical visits at 10
      years.
