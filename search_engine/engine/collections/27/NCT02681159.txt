
    
      Over the forthcoming two years, the investigators will study in details critical incidences
      that may occur to patients in the perioperative period like; acute hypoxic event, acute major
      hemorrhage, acute airway obstruction and others that may endanger the life or one of the
      patient organs and whether the patient survive the acute event or die.

      These critical incidences will be collected and all information about it will be known in
      order to be studied and to know were was the defect and eventually to improve the safety of
      practice of anesthesia and surgery.
