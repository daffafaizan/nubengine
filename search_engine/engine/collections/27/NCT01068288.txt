
    
      The ability of clinical practice guidelines to improve clinical practice and optimize
      resource utilization continues to be substantiated in the literature. To be effective,
      clinical practice guidelines must be developed from reliable and reproducible data.

      This trial prospectively compares expectant management versus immediate laparoscopic or open
      appendectomy for perforated appendicitis in children with a delayed diagnosis. The primary
      outcome measure is length of hospital stay.
