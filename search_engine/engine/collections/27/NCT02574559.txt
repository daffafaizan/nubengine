
    
      A pilot-test, eight week, Cognitive Based Compassion Training (CBCT) program for caregivers
      of children with developmental delays that is comprised of eight two-hour weekly long
      sessions with a certified instructor followed by a twenty minute long meditation session.
      Sessions will focus on developing attention and stability of mind through focused attention
      training; cultivating insight into the nature of mental experience; cultivating
      self-compassion; developing equanimity; developing appreciation and gratitude; developing
      affection and empathy; realizing aspirational compassion; and realizing active compassion.
