
    
      Study Objective:

        1. The main Objective: To explore the effects of different statins lipid-lowering treatment
           on glucose metabolism in patients with impaired glucose regulation in a large sample of
           Chinese population;

        2. The secondary Objective: To obtain real-world evidence of the clinical efficacy and
           safety of statins lipid-lowering therapy in Chinese patients.

      Study Design: The study was an open-label, multi-center network register-based follow-up
      study.

      The total sample size: 10000 participants.
