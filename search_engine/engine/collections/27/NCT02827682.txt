
    
      Participants will be randomly assigned, in a 1:1 ratio, to receive IoC monitoring or BIS
      monitoring during the operation as the criteria for adjustment of analgesics use. The
      randomization sequence will be computer-generated, and randomization will be performed in
      blocks and will be stratified according to participating center. The number of dialysis and
      patient's recovery after the transplantation will be recorded. The participation of each
      patient is scheduled till they discharge from hospital.
