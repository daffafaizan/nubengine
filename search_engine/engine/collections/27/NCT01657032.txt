
    
      ESPGHAN agreed to use probiotics, with proven efficacy, and smectite in treatment of AGE as
      an adjunct to standard rehydration therapy. Among probiotics Lactobacillus GG were found to
      be beneficial in meta-analyses. Treatment with LGG was associated with a significant
      reduction in diarrhea duration.

      A recent review systematically evaluated the efficacy of smectite in treating acute
      infections diarrhea in infants and children.
