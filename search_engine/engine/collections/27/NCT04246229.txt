
    
      A randomized Controlled trial comparing the use of transcutaneous bilirubinometry to serum
      bilirubin measurements (via blooddraws = standard care) in neonates born after a gestational
      age > 32 weeks and < 15 days of age treated with Phototherapy for neonatal hyperbilrubinemia.
      Main outcome measurement is the number of blooddraws for measurement of bilirubin. Secondary
      outcome measurements are duration of phototherapy and hospitalisation.
