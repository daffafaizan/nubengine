
    
      Utilizing acustimulation, the effect of PC 6 "Neiguan" (acupuncture point Pericardium 6) has
      been studied in PONV . Conventional acupuncture has not been tested yet in laparoscopic
      cholecystectomy.

      The incidence of postoperative vomiting during the first 24 hours is the main outcome.
      Secondary outcomes include: complete response rate, total rescue ondansetron dose used and
      patient satisfaction with PONV management.
