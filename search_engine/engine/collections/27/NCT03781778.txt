
    
      Participants are randomized to 1 of 2 groups.

      GROUP I (ACTIVE GROUP): Participants eat a diet consisting of resistant starch foods daily
      for 8 weeks. Study foods are in addition to their own usual daily diet.

      GROUP II (CONTROL GROUP): Participants eat a diet consisting of regular corn starch foods
      daily for 8 weeks. Study foods are in addition to their own usual daily diet.
