
    
      Retrospective study to analyze tissue samples of patients after thyroid surgery at Claudius
      Regaud Institute Toulouse, France, for thyroid cancer or thyroid aggressive lymphoma or
      benign thyroid pathology or other head&neck pathology necessitating total thyroidectomy
      despite normal thyroid.

      Analysis focused on cholesterol metabolites quatification by mass spectrometry in liquid and
      gas phase.
