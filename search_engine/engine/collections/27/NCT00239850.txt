
    
      The aim of this study is to evaluate 3 different treatments of obesity with gastric bypass.
      Two groups will be treated in different clinics and 1 group will be treated at our outpatient
      clinic. Sixty patients will participate in each group. Behavioural changes and weight loss
      over 4-5 years will be followed. We will also analyse changes in hormones, mental status,
      sexual behaviour, effect of training and quality of life.
