
    
      The effect of exercise (EXR) on short-term food intake (FI) and subjective appetite was
      investigated in normal weight (NW), boys were randomized into 4 treatments. Each boy consumed
      either a non-caloric sweetened control (CON) or GLU drink after either exercise (EXR) or
      sedentary activity. Subjects exercised at 25% above their ventilation threshold (VeT).
