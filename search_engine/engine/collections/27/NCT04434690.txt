
    
      Investigators will enroll 136 participants with primary bilateral symptomatic knee
      osteoarthritis that will refractory to conservative treatment who will undergo bilateral TKA
      under single anaesthesia after 15.06.2020. Investigators will include patients who were less
      than 75 years old, with no history of malignancy, without severe extra-articular deformities,
      and without the systemic inflammatory disease, severe cardiac insufficiency, and morbid
      obesity (>40 kg/m2 or 35 or more and experiencing obesity-related health conditions). An
      informed consent form will be obtained from all patients.
