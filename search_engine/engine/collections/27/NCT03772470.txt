
    
      Using random digit dialing (RDD) sampling technique, participants were randomized one of four
      airtime incentive amounts contingent on them completing the noncommunicable disease risk
      factor survey. This mobile phone survey will be sent as an interactive voice response (IVR).
      In IVR surveys, participants use their touch tone key pad to answer pre-recorded questions.
      (i.e. If you are male, pres 1; If you are female, press 2). This study will be conducted in
      both Colombia and Tanzania.
