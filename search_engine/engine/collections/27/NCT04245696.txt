
    
      A multi-center, blinded, non-randomized, non-controlled study

      Up to 60 eligible participants will be enrolled at up to three (3) sites. Participants will
      receive one (1) treatment in the submental/neck area. Participants will complete follow-up
      visits for clinical evaluation at Day 14, 1 Month, 3 Month, and 6 Months after the study
      treatment. Standard photography will be obtained at Screening to the 6 Month follow-up, and
      3D photography will be obtained at Screening, Month 3, and Month 6 visit.
