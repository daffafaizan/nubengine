
    
      The study will include three phases: screening, treatment and follow-up. Screening: during
      this phase subjects eligibility will be evaluated after informed consent signature. Endoscopy
      and Urea Breath test will be performed in addition to the baseline routine evaluations.

      Treatment: Subjects are randomly assigned to treatment and will be treated for 8weeks.

      Follow-up: includes one visits. Eradication of H. Pylori will be confirmed through urea
      breath test(UBT).
