
    
      In this study, Nursing care based on Kolcaba's Comfort Theory will be applied for women
      undergoing HSG. Before the HSG , the women will be met at the appointment time and the women
      will be given care during the procedure.Up to 15 minutes after the procedure will be with
      women. There will be 2 intervention groups and one control group. In the first intervention
      group, virtual reality glasses will be applied during the procedure, Mobile-assisted
      education and consultancy will be provided in the 2nd intervention group. This mobile
      assisted education and consultancy starts 48 hours before HSG shooting.The hsg capture will
      continue until it ends. Hospital care standards will be applied to the control group, the
      third group.
