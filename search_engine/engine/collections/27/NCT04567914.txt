
    
      This cross-sectional,case-control clinical study included 40 patients diagnosed with AIS and
      40 age and gender -matched healthy individuals. AIS group was evaluated for localization of
      curvature and degree of curvature. Ultrasonographic measurements and were performed of
      bilaterally abdominal muscle thickness in both groups. Spirometry evaluation was performed in
      both groups. Intragroup and intergroup data were compared.
