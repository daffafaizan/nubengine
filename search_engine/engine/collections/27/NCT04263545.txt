
    
      A randomised controlled feasibility study to trial a novel intervention to help NHS staff
      stop smoking compared to standard care. Participants will attend one-on-one interviews and a
      support group where they will undertake CO monitoring and mini-spirometry. They will complete
      questionnaires about their quality of life, other health problems and their general state of
      health. They will attend the support groups on a weekly basis and have access to support
      outside of these times.
