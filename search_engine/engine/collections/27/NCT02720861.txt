
    
      This is an cross-sectional observational study in Chulalongkorn hospital, to determine the
      prevalence of genetic polymorphism on RNF213 rs112735431 gene in non-cardioembolic ischemic
      cerebrovascular disease. Patients who are potentially eligible for study participation will
      be identified through a chart review of patients who were admitted in Chulalongkorn Hospital
      for acute ischemic stroke treatment.
