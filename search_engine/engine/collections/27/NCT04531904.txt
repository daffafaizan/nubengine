
    
      The PERFECT Study is a prospective, multi-center, single arm, post-market clinical follow-up
      study.

      The objective of this post-market clinical follow-up study is to assess the performance of
      the EMBOVAC Aspiration Catheter in the treatment of acute ischemic stroke in a real-world
      clinical setting.

      The study will also report on clot characteristics and clinical outcomes.
