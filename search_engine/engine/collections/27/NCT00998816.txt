
    
      A total of 200 patients scheduled for lateral thoracotomy will be randomly assigned to
      receive either placebo or 150 mg pregabalin (Lyrica)1 hour prior to surgery, 12 hr after
      surgery and then every 12 hr for an additional 10 days. While in the hospital, patients will
      be assessed for pain intensity, satisfaction and side effects. Patients will be re-assessed
      at 2, 4 and 6 months following surgery.
