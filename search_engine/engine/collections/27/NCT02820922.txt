
    
      This retrospective study included patients of all ages who have undergone emergency
      orthopedic surgery between January 2015 and December 2015 after approval of the research
      ethics committee. Medical record forms and electronic medical record system of the hospital
      were used in the analysis of medical data with regard to age, sex, ASA score, anaesthesia
      technique, comorbidities, surgery diagnosis, length of surgery, complications and admission
      to intensive care unit after surgery.
