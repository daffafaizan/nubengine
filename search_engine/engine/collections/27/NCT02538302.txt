
    
      Nocturnal enuresis is among the most common disorders in children. Several pharmacological
      and non-pharmacological treatments are available for nocturnal enuresis. Studies for reaching
      the best pharmacological treatment for this disorder are continuing. The aim of current study
      was to compare the efficacy and safety of Minirin and oxybutynin for treatment of nocturnal
      enuresis in children in Bandar Abbas in 2014.
