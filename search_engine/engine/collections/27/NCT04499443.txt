
    
      This trial includes single-dose studies and multiple-dose studies, The single-dose study
      included six dose groups of 10 mg, 30 mg, 60 mg, 100mg, 150 mg, 200 mg, 250 mg and 300 mg.
      Based on the results of a single dose, select 1 to 3 doses to conduct multiple dose studies.
      To evaluate the tolerance of GST-HG131 tablets in healthy subjects in single and multiple
      administrations, pharmacokinetic characteristics, drug metabolism and transformation, and the
      effect of food on GST-HG131 pharmacokinetics.
