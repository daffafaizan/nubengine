
    
      To evaluate the acute and chronic effects of smokeless tobacco (ST) on the anterior segment
      parameters using optical biometry. Observational, comparative, cross-sectional study Place
      and Duration of Study: Department of Ophthalmology, Kahramanmaras Sutcu Imam University.

      The study included 53 right eyes of 53 subjects (study group), who used ST and 54 right eyes
      of 54 healthy subjects without ST (control group). Following a full ophthalmological
      examination, baseline measurements were taken with optical biometry and these measurements
      were repeated at 5, 30 and 60 mins after using ST.
