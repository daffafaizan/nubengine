
    
      This is a non inferiority cluster randomized trial on three Emergency Departments (ED) and
      five Intensive Care Unit (ICU) from three urban academic hospital in Paris, France. The
      investigators evaluated the quality of CC with a low-fidelity manikin and its electronic
      feedback device. The investigators tested subjects on a 6-minutes CC-only CPR scenario,
      including 2 minutes of pause. Physicians were tested either on a control day then after a
      night shift, or after a night shift then on a control day.
