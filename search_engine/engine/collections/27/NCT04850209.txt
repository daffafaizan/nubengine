
    
      There are two randomized parallel groups in this study, control group and Wechat group.All of
      the patients will be treated with C-BQT( lansoprazole, bismuth, amoxicillin and
      clarithromycin ). In control group,the patients will be educated by oral and written way
      about the regimen. In Wechat group, the patients will be educated by Wechat oral and written
      way. Then, the eradication rates, compliance, adverse events, patients' satisfaction and
      experience will be evaluated.
