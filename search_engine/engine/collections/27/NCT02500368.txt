
    
      The order of lens wear (test lens first, control lens second or control lens first, test lens
      second) will be selected according to a randomization table.

      Both test and control lenses will be used in a daily wear modality for 1 week. It is
      anticipated that this study will involve following visits: Baseline (screening and dispense
      of 1st study pair of lenses, visit combined), 1-week visit (follow-up of the 1st study pair
      of lenses and dispense the 2nd study pair of lenses) and 2-week visit (1 week follow-up of
      the 2nd study pair of lenses).
