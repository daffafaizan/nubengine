
    
      This project aims to do the following:

        -  To evaluate the operational feasibility of integrating an enhanced health education and
           nutrition package into the existing curriculum of the National Program for FP/PHC (LHW
           Program)

        -  Impact of the implementation of the full package of evidence-based nutrition
           interventions on the nutrition status, morbidity patterns and overall survival patterns
           in the district
