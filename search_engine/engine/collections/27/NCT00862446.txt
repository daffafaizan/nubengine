
    
      Babies in the newborn intensive care unit with evidence of liver damage from Total Parenteral
      Nutrition (TPN), as indicated by a direct bilirubin of greater than 2.5 , and who will not be
      fed for at least another month will be offered the trial. they will be changed from the
      intralipid to Omegaven as the source of their fat. We will continue routine monitoring of
      their nutrition and liver function.
