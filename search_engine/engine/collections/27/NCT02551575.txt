
    
      Three arms were included in this study, and treatment with MTX and HCQ is defined as active
      comparator. Treatment of TCM，including Qingre Huoxue granule, Qingre Huoxue external
      preparation, MTX placebo and HCQ placebo, is defined as one experimental arm. Treatment of
      integrative medicine，including Qingre Huoxue granule, Qingre Huoxue external preparation, MTX
      and HCQ, is defined as another experimental arm.
