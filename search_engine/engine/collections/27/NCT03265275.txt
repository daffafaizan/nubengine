
    
      The study will be conducted at the orthopedic department, Akershus University Hospital, as a
      prospective observational study and will include approximately 200 consecutive hip fracture
      patients. The patients will be evaluated once daily for the fulfillment of predefined
      discharge criteria. If the discharge criteria are not met or if the criteria are met but the
      patient is not discharged in spite of this the underlying reason(s) are recorded.
