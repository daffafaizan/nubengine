
    
      TRIUMPH study will utilize the multiprogram trial stimulator or invisible trial system with
      BurstDR for the trial evaluation period and the Prodigy or Proclaim Elite family of
      neurostimulators with tonic or BurstDR waveform at permanent implant and follow-up for pain
      control and improvement of psychosocial function. The follow-up visits will be scheduled at
      3, 6, 12, 18, and 24 months post permanent implant
