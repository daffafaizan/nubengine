
    
      Participants in this study were selected from the Korean Metabolic disorders & Obesity Study
      in Elementary School children (KMOSES), the main aim of which was to comprehensively assess
      obesity-related metabolic risk factors and to evaluate clinical outcomes in Korean children
      prospectively. Korean children who were participated in a school-based health examination,
      were reexamined for the 3-year follow-up assessment.
