
    
      A one month-long project monitoring an obstetric anesthesia practices in obstetric/anesthesia
      departments throughout the Czech Republic.

      Electronic Case Report Form (eCRF) is used to collect data on all obstetric anesthesia
      procedures in peripartal period and obstetric and anesthesia complications. All consecutive
      cases in each participating center during the study period are recorded. Each record is
      related to parturient and contains the following sections: history, form of labour analgesia,
      type of anesthesia for Cesarean section and anesthesia in third stage of labor.
