
    
      The aim of this study is to evaluate the long-term safety of on the onset of menopause. To
      investigate the effect of Opportunistic Salpingectomy on the age of menopause, we will
      compare age of menopause in women who underwent sterilization through opportunistic
      salpingectomy with a control group consisting of women who underwent sterilization by tubal
      ligation or who had no sterilization.
