
    
      Randomized, single-center, waiting-list control group study: Introvision as preventative in
      Migraine

      Randomized, single center, waiting-list group control study to evaluate the effect of
      Introvison, a mental and emotional self regulation method to reduce headache days in patients
      with migraine, migraine and tension type headache and chronic migraine. The number of
      headache days 3 months after completion of the last session of Introvision will be compared
      with the number of headache days of the waiting list group in the month before the course to
      learn Introvision.
