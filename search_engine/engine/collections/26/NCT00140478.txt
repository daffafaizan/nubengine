
    
      Patients will receive mifepristone, 200mg orally once a day. One treatment cycle is 28 days
      long. Patients will remain on treatment unless their cancer gets worse or they develop
      intolerable side effects.

      At the end of each cycle a physical examination, routine blood tests, and hormone levels will
      be performed.

      After every 3 cycles, one or more of the following will be performed: bone scan, chest x-ray,
      CT scan or MRI.
