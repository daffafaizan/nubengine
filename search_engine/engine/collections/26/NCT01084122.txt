
    
      The main objective of this study is to evaluate the safety and efficacy of intravenous iron
      sucrose in patients with hip fracture and surgical intervention to prevent perioperative
      anemia.

      It will also determine whether intravenous iron sucrose administration improve outcomes as
      Haemoglobin values, transfusional needs, postoperative complications, length of hospital stay
      and cost-effectiveness.
