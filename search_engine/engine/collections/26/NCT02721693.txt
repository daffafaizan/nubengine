
    
      Patients with COPD will perform a cardiopulmonary exercise test (CPET) on treadmill until
      exhaustion, 8-12 minutes. Venous blood will be drawn before exercise test and 15 minutes, 3
      hours and 24 hours after test for analysis of high sensitive Cardiac troponin T (hs-cTnT).
      The investigators will also measure arterial bloodgas, oxygen uptake, carbon dioxide
      consumption, ventilation, blood pressure, ECG, heart rate and ask for dyspnea-score.
