
    
      This study will divide into two groups, single and multiple doses groups. In single dose
      group, the subjects will receive one, two and four red yeast rice capsules under fed state in
      period one to three, respectively. In multiple doses group, the subjects will receive red
      yeast rice capsules under fed state twice daily for 4.5 days. There is a minimum of a 5-days
      washout period before crossover of treatments.

      In single dose treatment, the blood samples will be drawn prior to the dosing, and 0.5, 1,
      1.5, 2, 2.5, 3, 4, 6, 8 and 12 hours after the dosing.

      In multiple doses treatment, the blood samples will be drawn prior to the 1st dosing, prior
      to the 7th dosing, prior to the 8th dosing, prior to the 9th dosing, and 0.5, 1, 1.5, 2, 2.5,
      3, 4, 6, 8 and 12 hours after the 9th dosing.
