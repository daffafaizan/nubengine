
    
      The practice of physical exercise has numerous health benefits, preventing the appearance and
      development of cardiovascular diseases and various types of cancer. Exercise causes changes
      in the vascular, muscular and pulmonary systems, etc. One of the benefits that can result
      from exercise modification of the intestinal microbiota and its metabolic profile. In order
      to identify the potential changes that exercise has on the intestinal microbiota and its
      metabolic profile, the purpose of this study is to identify changes in the plasma and fecal
      metabolome and in fecal microbiota associated with a bout of exercise
