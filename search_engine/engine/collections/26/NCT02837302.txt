
    
      This study is a multicenter retrospective cohort study involving thirteen centers in Korea
      nation-wide. The inclusion criteria are liver cirrhosis patients with Child-Pugh score 8 to
      10. The major exclusion criteria are abnormal serum creatinine level, and hepatocellular
      carcinoma with viable tumor. The investigators analyzed improvement of Model for End-Stage
      Liver Disease score, Child-Pugh score, incidence of cirrhosis-related complications, and
      event free survival.
