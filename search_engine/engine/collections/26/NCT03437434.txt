
    
      4 point scleral fixation of PC- IOL (Posterior Chamber-PC) BunnyLens with Gore-Tex sutures is
      a surgical technique for implanting IOL in patients with capsular support absence, due to
      trauma, abnormal anterior chamber, IOL dislocation, or ciliary zonule defect.

      The 4 point fixation enables to align the PC- IOL with minimal vertical and horizontal
      deviation;The Gore-Tex sutures maintain stable scleral fixation with no suture erosion or
      breakage.
