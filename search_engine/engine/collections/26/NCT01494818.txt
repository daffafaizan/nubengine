
    
      The subjects attended the enrollment visit having worn their habitual silicone hydrogel
      contact lenses for at least six hours on the day of the visit. The enrollment visit was
      scheduled so that the subjects' habitual lenses were at least 14 ± 3 days old for the
      subjects replacing their lenses bi-weekly and at least 30 ± 5 days old for those replacing
      their lenses monthly.
