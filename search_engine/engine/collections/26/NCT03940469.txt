
    
      Sixty patients were enrolled and divided into three groups 20 each, Group C received 35ml
      levobupivacaine+2ml normal saline under ultrasound guided interscalene block. Group H
      received 35ml levobupivacaine+8mg dexamethasone under ultrasound guided interscalene block.
      Group D received 35ml levobupivacaine+100umg dexmedetomidine+1ml normal saline. The primary
      outcome was the postoperative pain which was assessed by Visual analog score(VAS), the
      secondary outcomes were the onset and duration of sensory and motor block, the total amount
      of analgesic requirement, and blood cortisol level.
