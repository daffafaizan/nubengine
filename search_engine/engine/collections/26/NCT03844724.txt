
    
      The purpose of this study was to evaluate the safety and efficacy of ZENFlow™ drug-eluting
      PTA balloon dilatation catheter (paclitaxel 3±1 μg/mm2 balloon surface area) in the treatment
      of iliac artery, femoral artery and popliteal stenosis or occlusive lesions compared with
      conventional PTA balloon dilatation catheter.
