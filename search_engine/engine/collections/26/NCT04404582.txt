
    
      Clinical data of adult patients in integrated ICUs of 6 hospitals of Fujian Province China
      from January 2015 to July 2019 were collected, who had been examined for sputum, urine,
      blood, pleural effusion, ascites, cerebrospinal fluid and wound secretions and other body
      fluid samples. And the results showed that Klebsiella pneumoniae was positive, including
      demographic data, underlying disease status, drug sensitivity test, infection-related
      indicators, life support, prognostic indicators.
