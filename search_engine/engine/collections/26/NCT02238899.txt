
    
      The Register is based on the study HIT 2000 that recruited patients until 31.12.2011. All
      german patients with intracranial medulloblastoma, CNS-PNET, ependymoma can be included in
      the register. Object of the register is to maintain the quality of diagnostic standard by
      using of central review (Neuroradiology, Pathology, and cranio spinal fluid (CSF) cytology).

      Furthermore, the register should enable to continue the collection of epidemiologic data and
      biological material (tumor material, CSF, and blood) for associated studies.

      The register gives recommendations for the therapy of the patients, but these are not
      mandatory.
