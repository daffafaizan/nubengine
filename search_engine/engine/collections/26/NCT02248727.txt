
    
      This is a 30-subject, prospective dispensing study comparing the clinical performance of the
      subjects' habitual Avaira sphere lenses following a refit with Clariti Elite sphere lenses.
      Subject's habitual Avaira sphere lenses will be evaluated at the first visit and then
      re-fitted with a pair of Clariti Elite sphere lenses for 4 weeks of daily wear. After the
      dispensing visit, subjects will return for evaluations at 1 week, 2 weeks and 4 weeks.
