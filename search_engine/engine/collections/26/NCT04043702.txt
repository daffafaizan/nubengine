
    
      The aim was to show if a combination of topiramate and empagliflozin have the greatest weight
      decreasing effect in comparison to each drug alone and to group with no treatment( control
      group).

      Materials and methods 200 obese patients 4 groups. had been monitored in a private clinic,
      each group 50 patients number, with 35 females and 15 males who were already on treatment.
