
    
      The study duration is 1 month and 2 days. It is possible to add 2 additional months
      (optional).

      The 2 first days (from D-3 to D0) correspond to the pre-selection period. These 2 days are
      followed by the follow-up month (from D0 to D30). It is possible to add an optional follow up
      of 2 months (form D30 to D90).
