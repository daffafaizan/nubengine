
    
      A prospective, cohort study conducted in Pediatric Neurology Clinic, Children's Hospital,
      Faculty of Medicine, Ain Shams University. Forty children with sCP with sleep and seizure
      problems, aged 2-12 years were enrolled. The children received crushed Melatonin 3-mg tablets
      daily, 30-minutes before bedtime for 3-months. Children's sleep habits questionnaire (CSHQ)
      Arabic version, polysomnography, Chalfont epilepsy severity score, and EEG were done at
      enrollment and after the 3-months. The ethical approval number is (FMASU MD 202/2016).
