
    
      This is a six-month, multicenter, randomized, double blind, parallel-group,placebo controlled
      study. Patients take Imotun capsules or placebo once a day, between the meals. Patients are
      allowed to take study drugs during the meal in the case of intolerant reflux of oil smell
      from GI. If patients do not tolerate the pain, they are able to take Celecoxib as a rescue
      medication during 6 months.
