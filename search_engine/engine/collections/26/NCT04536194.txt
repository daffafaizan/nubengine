
    
      After intubation under general anesthesia, radial arterial is cannulated and pressure
      transducer is connected to MostCare(core technique PRAM). CI, SVRI, SVI, SVV, dp/dt and HR
      are recorded before and after a infusion of dopamine or norepinephrine to elevate 10% of mean
      arterial pressure. Changes of these hemodynamic data are compared.
