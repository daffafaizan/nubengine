
    
      This is a prospective, multi-center, observational cohort study and seeks to enroll 10,560
      participants with pulmonary nodules smaller than 3 cm in diameter from 21 hospitals in China.
      All participants will be followed up with chest CT or low-dose computed tomography (LDCT)
      scans for 2-3 years ( baseline, 3 months, 6 months, 12 months, 24 months and 36 months).
      Their blood samples, CT or LDCT scans data, and clinical data will be collected at each visit
      and Formalin-fixed paraffin-embedded (FFPE) tissues will be collected when participants who
      receive pneumonectomy or percutaneous lung biopsy.
