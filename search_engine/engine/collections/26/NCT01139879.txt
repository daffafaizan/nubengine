
    
      Subjects will be recruited from those presenting to the wound clinic and meet the inclusion
      criteria. A total of 30 evaluable subjects will be recruited for this study from up to 2
      wound care clinics /home health agencies (15 in Home Care and 15 in Extended Care). Subjects
      will be followed by weekly wound assessments for a period of 12 weeks or until their wounds
      heal. The outcome measurement is the rate of healing over the 12 week period.
