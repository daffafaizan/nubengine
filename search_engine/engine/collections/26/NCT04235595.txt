
    
      CTM and TENS each relieve dysmenorrhea pain, but they do so by different. The electrotherapy
      modality that is most commonly used in the treatment of dysmenorrhea is TENS. It has been
      reported in studies that high-frequency TENS is more effective in reducing pain than either
      low-frequency TENS or placebo TENS.
