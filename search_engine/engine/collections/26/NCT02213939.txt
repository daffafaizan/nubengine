
    
      Patients, who have an elective myocardial revascularization and give there written consent
      will be enrolled to the study. Demographic, intraoperative, and postoperative data will be
      collected prospectively. Furthermore, blood samples (1. before induction of anaesthesia 2. at
      the end of CPB 3. 6 hours after surgery 4. 24 hours after surgery 5. 3-5 days after surgery)
      will be analyzed.
