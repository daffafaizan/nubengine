
    
      We will randomize 300 consecutive patients undergoing colonoscopy for screening or
      surveillance purposes to receive one of four bowel purgatives. These purgatives include

        1. Split dose 4 liter polyethylene glycol with electrolytes (PEG+E): Brand name GoLytely

        2. Split dose 2 liter polyethylene glycol with ascorbic acid (PEG+Asc): Brand name:
           MoviPrep

        3. Split dose sodium picosulfate, magnesium oxide, and anhydrous citric acid (P+MC): Brand
           name Prepopik

        4. Split dose sodium sulfate, magnesium sulfate, and potassium sulfate solution (sulfate):
           Brand name Suprep
