
    
      Ambispective (retrospective and prospective) observational register of patients with
      cutaneous melanoma on lower limbs are grouped in 1) proximal (thigh), 2) medium (leg), and 3)
      distal (ankle and foot) location. Analysis of melanoma-specific overall and disease-free
      survival will be made, as well as multivariant analysis for prognostic variables including
      anatomical location.
