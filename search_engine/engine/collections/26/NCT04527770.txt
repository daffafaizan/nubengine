
    
      Entrapement syndrome - namely median nerve compression in carpal tunnel syndrome - causes
      chronic pain that can be refractory to medical treatment . Ultrasound guided hydrodissection
      is an option for alleviating pain . Recurrence is the main side effect of injection. We try
      in this research to prevent recurrence rate . Midazolam and dexamethasone both are used in
      chronic pain . Midazolm act by GABA mimetic action while dexamethasone act by decrease
      interleukin formation
