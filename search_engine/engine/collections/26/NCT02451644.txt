
    
      Many studies have investigated the matter until today and still no reliable information about
      the relationship between physical activity and / or its intensity and the pregnancy rate
      after embryo transfer.

      All patients that would take part in the study will be given a pedometer to measure steps for
      a month.

      Measuring steps will begin preparation for embryo transfer and two weeks after. Collected
      information about pregnancy rate (BHCG positive or negative).
