
    
      To evaluate the effect of mosapride on DPG by 13 C - octylic acid breath test.

      IF C13- octylic acid be absorbed by mucous membrane of small intestine, octylic acid will be
      oxidated to CO2.Patients eating food containing C13, and then collect their expired gas at
      15, 30,45,60,75,90,105,120,150,180,210,240min, analyse the mass of C13 by mass spectrometer,
      then can calculate the gastric emptying rate.
