
    
      Assess the impact of guidance on breastfeeding duration of exclusive breastfeeding in the
      first six months of a baby's life.

      110 pairs of mother / baby - Recruitment: Private Hospital of Porto Alegre.

      The first stage was carried out in the maternity - Orientation: Breastfeeding Importance
      Handle Correct, Positioning mother and baby, socioeconomic data and demographics. For the
      intervention group held three home visits:

      10th day, 1st and 2nd month of the baby's life. At 6 months were made telephone contact for
      both groups.
