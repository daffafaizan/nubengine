
    
      The primary objective of this study is to compare an Immunosuppressive regimen with 10 days
      of corticosteroids with a regimen with only an optional intra-op bolus of corticosteroids
      with regard to incidence of new onset Diabetes Mellitus as per the American Diabetic
      Association (ADA) criteria at any point up to 24 weeks after kidney transplantation.
