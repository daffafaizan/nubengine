
    
      In a proof of concept 5 groups are compared in preserving soft- and hard tissue after tooth
      extraction in the esthetic zone:

        1. Mucograft® Seal + Bio-Oss®

        2. Free Gingival Graft (FGG) + Bio-Oss®

        3. Free Gingival Graft + Gelatine sponge (Spongostan®)

        4. Spongostan®+ Mucograft® Seal

        5. Spongostan®

      Volumetric changes of hard and soft tissues are recorded , 2/4/8 weeks and 3/6/12 months
      after extraction.
