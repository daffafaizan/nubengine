
    
      The aim of this study is to assess the efficacy of endovascular treatment (balloon
      angioplasty and/or stenting) for the improvement of clinical symptoms in multiple sclerosis
      patients measured by Extended Disability Severity Score (EDSS), Multiple Sclerosis Impact
      Scale-29 (MSIS-29), Fatigue Severity Scale (FSS) and heat intolerance
