
    
      45 individuals were subdivided into 3 groups, 15 patients suffering from oral premalignant
      lesions, 15 patients suffering from oral malignant lesions and 15 individuals having age,
      gender and periodontal status matched with both patients groups acting as a control group.
      Serum and whole unstimulated saliva samples were collected from all the included groups in
      order to determine chemerin and MMP-9 in serum and saliva utilizing the ELISA technique.
