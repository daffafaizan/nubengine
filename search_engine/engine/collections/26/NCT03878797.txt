
    
      Effective airway management is the primary concern of the anesthesiologist. The conventional
      method of determining the insertion length of the endotracheal tube, 21 cm for women and 23
      cm for men, shows a high incidence of endobronchial intubations in the analyzed
      population.The aim of this study is form a new and safe practice habit by passing the
      endotracheal tube with the balloon just 2 cm below the vocal cords during intubation.
