
    
      This study is observational and multicentric.In a first part, patients will be
      retrospectively included. Baseline clinical and biological data obtainedat the time of MPN
      diagnosis of MPN with normal CBC following venous or arterial thrombosis will be recorded and
      follow-up data will be collected in an e-CRF. In a second part, patients will be included
      prospectively and diagnostic and follow-up data will be collected. Whether or not to initiate
      treatment with a cytoreduction treatment is left to the discretion of the clinician.
