
    
      59 persons with TMD diagnosed by the dentist and with cervical myofascial pain, aged 18-30
      years will be randomly allocated, 3 groups. The participants were randomly allocated only
      exercise group (EG) (n=20), low-level laser therapy plus exercise group (LLLT) (n=17) and
      manual pressure release plus exercise group (MPR) (n=22). The cases were evaluated and
      treated by the same physiotherapist for 4 weeks (12 sessions).

      Pain, pain pressure threshold, temporomandibular joint (TMJ) mobility and disability, forward
      head posture, head rotation, neck disability, psychological status and quality of life were
      evaluated. Evaluations were repeated before and after treatment.
