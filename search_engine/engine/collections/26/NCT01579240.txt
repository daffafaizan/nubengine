
    
      Based on the treatment group users self reported fitness level, activity goals, and
      self-identified barriers to exercise, the program helped users select exercise activities in
      the areas of endurance, flexibility, strengthening, and balance enhancement. They returned
      weekly for automated video and text support and education, with the option to change or
      increase in their exercise plan, and on-going problem solving to overcome barriers to
      exercise.
