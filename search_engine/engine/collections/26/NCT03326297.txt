
    
      Infant colic is a problem that affects 10-30% of babies in their first months of life. This
      research project aims to verify the effectiveness of manual therapy on infant colic. The
      sample is divided in three groups, performing in the firts one osteopathy manual therapy; in
      the second one, measures to support and education to the family; and in the third, there is
      no specific intervention.
