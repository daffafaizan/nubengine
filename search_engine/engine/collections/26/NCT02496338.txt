
    
      The investigators applied a menopausal health training program for Husbands of 45-60 years
      women. The content of menopausal health training program was about the nature of menopause,
      signs and symptoms, complications, treatment option and health behavior during transitional
      period. Training program lasted 3 sessions. The investigators assessed quality of life and
      marital satisfaction using Hildich marital satisfaction questionnaire and Menopause Quality
      of Life questionnaire(MENQOL), respectively, three month after intervention.
