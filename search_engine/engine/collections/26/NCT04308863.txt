
    
      Thirty permanent mandibular molars of patients with signs and symptoms of irreversible
      pulpitis will be selected. They will be randomly allocated into two groups, Group 1(Chitosan
      scaffold/ MTA pulp dressing materials), Group 2 (MTA pulp dressing material). A single visit
      full pulpotomy procedure will be carried out. Chitosan scaffold and / or MTA will be used as
      the pulp dressing materials. The teeth will be then restored with base of Glass ionomer
      cement followed by composite restoration.
