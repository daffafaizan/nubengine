
    
      The aims of the study is to investigate whether consumption of preload isovolumetric (220 ml)
      spice-based beverages contained total polyphenol concentration to 185 mg gallic-acid
      equivalents followed by white wheat bread challenge (contained 50 g available carbohydrate)
      might affect postprandial glucose metabolism, inflammatory markers, appetite-related gut
      hormones, plasma antioxidant capacity, as well as appetite sensations in healthy subjects.
