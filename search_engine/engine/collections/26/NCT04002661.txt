
    
      This pilot randomized placebo controlled study will include 60 men (30 treatment and 30
      placebo). ARM 1 will take flibanserin 100mg orally every night and ARM 2 will take a placebo
      orally every night.

      There will be four study visits and the study duration is approximately 4 months.
      Participants will have physicals at each visit. Blood draws for tests will be done at 3
      visits and up to 4 questionnaires will completed at each visit. Study drug will randomized.
