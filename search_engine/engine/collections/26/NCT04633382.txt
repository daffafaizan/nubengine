
    
      A prospective randomized study on the effect of fast-track surgery on the immediate
      postoperative results after various reconstructive and restorative operations on the bile
      ducts. In the Department of Surgical Hepatology and Transplantation, a prospective randomized
      study will include patients (about 50) with planned reconstructive and restorative operations
      on the bile ducts for malignant and benign diseases of the bile ducts.
