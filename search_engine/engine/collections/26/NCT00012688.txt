
    
      Background:

      Diabetes is a prevalent and costly disease among users of VA care. Goals of diabetes care
      include the prevention and/or delay of complications via adequate glycemic control.
      Improvements in periodontal status may also improve diabetes control.

      Objectives:

      This project will determine the efficacy of a program of periodontal screening and therapy in
      improving the level of glycemic control in poorly controlled diabetics.

      Methods:

      Status:

      Subject accrual is complete. Data collection is ongoing.
