
    
      Forty-five patients diagnosed with acquired brain injury, with over one year's evolution and
      independent gait over a distance of 10 meters, with our without the use of an orthopedic aid,
      were selected and randomized in 3 groups. Dry land therapy group (DT group), which received
      sessions including exercises for gait, trunk mobility and exercises involving the upper and
      lower limbs; Experimental group (EG group) , which received Ai Chi aquatic therapy; Combined
      group (DT+EG group), which received alternate sessions of therapy on dry land and aquatic
      therapy with Ai Chi.
