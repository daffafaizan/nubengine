
    
      This study will be carried out on sixty females with polycystic ovarian syndrome (PCOS) who
      were selected from the Outpatient Clinic of Gynecology at Qasr Al Aini University Hospital.
      Their age ranged from 17-28 years, body mass index was 25-30 Kg/m2. They experienced
      hyperandrogenism, hyperinsulinemia and amenorrhea not less than 6 months.

      PCOS females will be randomly assigned into two equal groups: group (A) (control group)
      consisted of 30 women who received low caloric diet (1200 Cal/day) and group (B) (study
      group) consisted of 30 women who received the same diet regime and cryolipolysis
