
    
      Patients recruited in the study will be primigravida at term with obstetric or medical
      indication for labour induction. These patients will be either booked attending antenatal
      clinic referred for induction of labour or emergency admissions in labour room.

      A total of 100 women will be randomly selected for the study. I. Women will be given 20 ml
      (20 µg) of misoprostol solution orally every 2 hourly until adequate uterine contractions
      occurred (3 contractions per 10 min lasting 30-40 s).

      II. The induction regimen included application of Vagiprost 25 µg tablet in the posterior
      fornix of the vagina every 4 h (up to 6 doses) after determination of the Bishop score.
