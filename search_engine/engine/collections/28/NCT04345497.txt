
    
      This is a randomized, controlled, parallel study. Eligible participants will be randomly
      allocated (at 1 :1 ratio) to one of two groups: DSF group or control group.The total expected
      duration of the study is 97 days.

      There are two periods in the study:

      i) "Run-in Period" (Study Days -6 to -1): participants will wear the CGMS:Continuous Glucose
      Monitoring System (FreeStyle Libre Pro) and will be asked to follow and track their diet and
      physical activity on a daily basis.

      ii) "Intervention Period" (Study Days 0 to 90): participants will be randomized into one of
      the two groups (n = 125 per group): DSF group or control group.
