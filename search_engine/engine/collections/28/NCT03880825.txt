
    
      This study will enroll healthy male and female subjects to evaluate the effect of
      levoketoconazole on the PK of a single 500 mg dose of metformin. There will be 3 sequential
      treatment periods, and all subjects will receive metformin only in Period 1, escalating doses
      of levoketoconazole in Period 2, and concurrent administration of metformin and
      levoketoconazole in Period 3.
