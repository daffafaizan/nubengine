
    
      1. Inclusion criteria: patients with intrauterine adhesions (AFS score >9 points); age 21
           to 40 years old, with fertility requirements.

        2. Study design:prospective, randomized controlled cohort study, patients with intrauterine
           adhesions were randomized into two groups, the control group and the intervention group
           (intraoperative and postoperative 1 week postoperative PRP). The surgical procedures and
           postoperative medication regimens were the same.

        3. Compare the biochemical pregnancy rate, clinical pregnancy rate, abortion rate and live
           birth rate after hysteroscopic resection in PRP group and control group, and explore the
           effect of PRP on pregnancy outcome after intrauterine adhesion.
