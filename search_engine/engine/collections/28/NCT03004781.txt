
    
      The first format was a one-variable intervention manipulating parental self-efficacy beliefs.
      The second format was a two-variable intervention manipulating both parents' self-efficacy
      beliefs and emotion coaching practices. The two interventions shared exactly the same design,
      consisting of eight parent group sessions. Effect on children's EB and observed behaviors
      were evaluated through a multi-method assessment at three points (pre-test, post-test and
      follow-up).
