
    
      This study will be conducted in Australia and New Zealand. A total of 10 scheduled visits are
      planned. The visits include a screening visit, 2 operative visits, and 7 postoperative
      visits. Both eyes will be implanted. The second eye implant will occur within 7-21 days of
      the first eye implant. Individual subject participation is expected to last 6-7 months.
