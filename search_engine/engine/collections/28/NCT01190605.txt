
    
      Following recruitment and completion of informed consent, a participant will be given a
      questionnaire to look at factors that help or hinder access to HIV care after delivery. After
      finishing the initial questionnaire the participant will get a Wal-Mart gift card for $15.
      The participant may then be asked to complete a longer interview for which she would receive
      an additional gift card for $25. The combined results will be used to design an
      interventional study to improve entry into HIV care after delivery.
