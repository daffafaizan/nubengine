
    
      295 pregnant women will formed the cohort, during all pregnancy and two months after
      delivery. The Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale was used to evaluate depressive symptoms
      at 2 months postpartum. The LBP will be evaluated based on self-administrated questionnaire,
      and the patients with LBP will be classified in to lumbopelvic groups according to mechanical
      assessment of the lumbar spine, pelvic pain provocation tests, standard history, pain
      intensity on visual analogue scale, spread of pain through pain drawings and Oswestry index
      was used to evaluated the disability. Logistics regression analysis will performed to explain
      the predictors of depression.
