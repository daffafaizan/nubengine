
    
      This study is comprised of 2 parts. Part 1 includes participants with severe renal impairment
      and normal renal function. Part 2 includes participants with mild and moderate renal
      impairment and normal renal function. Participants will be residential for a single period of
      14 days/13 nights. Participants will be discharged from the clinical unit on day 14 on the
      condition that all required assessments have been performed and that there are no medical
      reasons for a longer stay in the clinical unit.
