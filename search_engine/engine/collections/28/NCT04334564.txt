
    
      In this clinical study, the visual field, including MD, MS, LV, and vision score, are the
      main efficacy indicators. HRT and visual electrophysiological examination are the secondary
      efficacy indicators. Safety indicators based on laboratory tests and total frequency and
      incidence of adverse events. To evaluate the effectiveness and safety of Ginkgo biloba
      capsules for glaucoma patients with intraocular pressure control.
