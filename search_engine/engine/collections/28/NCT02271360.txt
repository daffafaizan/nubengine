
    
      Two hundred and twenty women at risk of ovarian hyperstimulation syndrome during ICSI cycles
      will be randomly scheduled into two equal groups. In group A, (Calcium Dobesilate group), 1
      cap / 8 hs Doxium ( 500mg) will be given at day of HCG injection and for 21 days; while in
      group B (Cabergoline group), 1 tab/day Cabergoline( 0.5 mg) will be given at day of HCG
      injection and for 8 days.
