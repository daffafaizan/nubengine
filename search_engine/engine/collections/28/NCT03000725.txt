
    
      The goal of this study is to adapt Project UPLIFT for Hispanic adults with epilepsy and
      depression. A 2-arm randomized control trial will be conducted to evaluate the feasibility,
      acceptability and effects of Project UPLIFT versus usual care (UC) in Hispanic patients with
      epilepsy.

      The specific aim of this study is: To evaluate the feasibility, acceptability and short- and
      long-term effects of the adapted intervention on depression and other outcomes in 72 English-
      and Spanish-speaking Hispanic epilepsy patients.
