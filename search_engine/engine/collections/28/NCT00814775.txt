
    
      Participant will be randomly assigned to receive one of 2 conventional airway devices - the
      Fastrach Laryngeal Mask Airway or the CTrach Laryngeal Mask Airway. This study will compare
      the time required for successful intubation between the two devices.

      Postoperative visit will be performed to assess the participant's clinical condition and to
      receive participant's feed back.
