
    
      Patients will be randomized into 2 groups:

        -  Vit E group: Vitamin E acetate ointment will be applied over staple lines and
           anastomoses in Roux-en-Y gastric bypass.

        -  Control group: Vitamin E will not be applied

      Postoperative pain will be assessed 24 hours after surgery, by means of visuala analogic
      scale (ranging from 0 to 100mm).
