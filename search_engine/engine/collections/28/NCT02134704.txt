
    
      Subjects participating in the study must wear sensors that will be arrange at the shoulders,
      spine, pelvis and legs. Subjects will perform under the direction of the investigator,
      different tasks of a total duration of 45 minutes.

      During the period of experimentation, the data collected will be:

        -  the positions of each one of the sensors in all three planes of space,

        -  the ground support forces
