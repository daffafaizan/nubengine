
    
      This is a single-center, unblinded, non-randomized, non-controlled study designed to follow a
      total of up to 15 qualified and consenting subjects to receive one bipolar fractional
      radiofrequency microneedling treatment under an IRB approved protocol. Up to 15 subjects will
      be enrolled and treated at UT Southwestern in the Department of Plastic Surgery. Subjects
      will be identified from the clinical practice of Dr. Jeffrey Kenkel, Department of Plastic
      Surgery.
