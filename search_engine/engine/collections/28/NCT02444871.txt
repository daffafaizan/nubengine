
    
      Before the change of the standard operating procedure (SOP) with the implementation of the
      application of IgM-enriched immunoglobulins to patients with severe sepsis and septic shock
      conventional inflammatory and coagulation parameters, viscoelastic and aggregometric
      measurements as well as the endotoxin activity was measured in 15 patients. After the change
      of SOP the same parameters were recorded for additional 15 patients.
