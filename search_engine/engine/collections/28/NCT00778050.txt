
    
      A single oral dose of the test or reference product was administered to volunteers on two
      separate occasions under fasting conditions with at least a 7 day washout between the doses.
      Twenty-six healthy volunteers were randomly assigned to the test or the reference products.
      Food and fluid intake were controlled during each confinement period.

      Twenty six (26) healthy subjects (16 males and 10 females) were enrolled in the study, of
      which 25 subjects completed the clinical phase of the study.
