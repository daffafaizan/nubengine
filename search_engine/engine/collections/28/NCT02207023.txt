
    
      Participants recruited in this study will be randomly assigned to either the Healthy
      Lifestyle Training Program, or Attention Control Memory Training Program.

      Individuals in the Healthy Lifestyle Training Program will each receive 7 lifestyle coaching
      telephone-sessions (30-60 minutes) with a trained lifestyle coach over a 6 month period.
      Individuals in the Memory Training Program will each receive 7 memory coaching telephone
      sessions (30-60 minutes) with a trained memory training coach over a 6 month period. 100
      evaluable subjects will be recruited (124 to accommodate for 20% dropout).
