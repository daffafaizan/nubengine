
    
      Inclusion criteria:

        1. adults with equal to or greater than 18 year-old

        2. confirmed as acute intracranial artery dissection based on the clinical and radiological
           diagnoses

        3. informed consent

      Exclusion criteria:

        1. contraindicated for MRI scanning and contrast media usage

        2. refuse the enrollment

      Outcome measures

        1. Serial follow-up with intraindividual comparisons and interstage comparisons

             -  Radiological features: intimal flap, double lumen, aneurysmal dilatation,
                intramural hematoma, luminal stenosis, contrast enhancement degree, diameters,
                length, wall thickness, eccentricity index

             -  Clinical features: mRS, NIHSS, drug

        2. Demographics
