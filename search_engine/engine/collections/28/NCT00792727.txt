
    
      The objective of this study is to demonstrate the short-term multiple dose efficacy and
      safety of HKT-500 in subjects with pain caused by mild to moderate osteoarthritis of the
      knee. The goal of the study is to gather additional information about the analgesic efficacy
      and safety of repeated, 24-hour applications of 2 HKT-500 patches per knee for a total of 28
      days in subjects with pain caused by mild to moderate osteoarthritis of the knee.
