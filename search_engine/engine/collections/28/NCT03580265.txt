
    
      Laser acupuncture is combined low level laser with acupuncture points. Investigators perform
      a prospective randomized, single-blinded study to investigate the efficacy of laser
      acupuncture in patients with carpal tunnel syndrome. Patients were randomized into
      intervention and control group. Participants in intervention group received six-sessions of
      laser acupuncture and six-sessions of sham laser acupuncture was given in control group. All
      participants used wrist splint at night. The evaluation was performed pretreatment as well as
      on the 2th, 4th, 8th and 12th week after the treatment.
