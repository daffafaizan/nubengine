
    
      This study will be performed for proving the efficacy of AMARGOL®, a phytomedicine product,
      under the Brazilian law for registration and revalidation RDC 14/2010.

      AMARGOL® indications are for non specific digestive complaints as laxative and
      anti-dyspeptic.

      Is a mixture of 7 plant extracts: Aloe ferox, Angelica archangelica, Alpinia officinarum,
      Cinnamomum zeylanicum, Gentiana lutea, Commiphora myrrh and Rheum palmatum.

      Placebo controlled test
