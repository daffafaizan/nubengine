
    
      Patients were randomized to receive one of two types of enteral nutrition formula: soybean
      oil used enteral nutrition (control), vegetable (canola, flaxseed) derived n-3 fatty acid
      enriched enteral nutrition (n3EN). Blood samples were collected on the first day and 14 days
      after consumption of each formula and anthropometric parameters were collected. Hematology
      and biochemical values were analyzed and NK cell activities and serum cytokine productions
      were measured.
