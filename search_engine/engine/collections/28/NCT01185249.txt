
    
      This is a quasi-experimental pilot study with prospective cohort design. Study subjects will
      be weighed in the early morning and then again in the evening for a minimum of three days.
      Analysis will be accomplished on data by analysis within-subjects data utilizing the paired t
      test. Other data to be collected includes dates of hospitalization, demographics, dose and
      timing of medications, lab results and documentation of comorbid conditions.
