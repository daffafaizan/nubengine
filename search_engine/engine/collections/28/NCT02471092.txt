
    
      1. A total of 33 males and females will participate in the study. Capillary blood samples
           will be collected from all participants to measure plasma glucose over a 3.5 hour
           postprandial study.

        2. Intravenous blood samples will be collected from a subset of 12 (6 males and 6 females)
           participants for determination of insulin, satiety hormones, acetaminophen (gastric
           emptying rates), and determination of amino acid profiles.
