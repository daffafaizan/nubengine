
    
      Ten patients were studied (5 - GI - not taking A. blazei - and 5 - G2 - taking A. blazei),
      from both sexes, with positive Anti-VHC and a healthy nutritional state, admitted into in the
      viral hepatitis ward. Diagnosis and treatment for hepatitis C concurred with the regulations
      of the Ministry of Health and nutritional evaluation was performed in 3 moments according to
      anthropometrical, bioimpedance, biochemical and dietary standards, with duration of 6 months.
      The consumption of A. blazei (10g/day) in dehydrated powder was concomitant with antiviral
      therapy (conventional or pegylated interferon associated with ribavirin) with duration of 5
      months.
