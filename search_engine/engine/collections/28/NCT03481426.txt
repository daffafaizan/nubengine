
    
      Musculoskeletal symptoms, especially back pain, account for the majority of days lost.

      In order to enhance the health and working careers of people with MSD, it is important that
      work can be arranged in such a way that they can remain in work life despite MSD.In the field
      of work ability promotion, various stakeholders; the worker, employer, professionals in
      occupational health services (OHS) interact to optimize actions and practices to prevent
      absences from work and promote remaining at work. This study aims to investigate
      participatory working practices and modles of co-operation among OHS and workplaces.
