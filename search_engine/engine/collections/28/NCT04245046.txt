
    
      This study was an open-label, comparative, multiple-dose, fixed sequence steady state trial
      to compare the pharmacokinetic of candesartan cilexetil, atorvastatin as atorvastatin calcium
      trihydrate, amlodipine as amlodipine besilate given as a multiple dose under fasting
      conditions in the absence and presence of each other.

      Bioanalysis of candesartan, atorvastatin and amlodipine is performed by LC/MS/MS method.
