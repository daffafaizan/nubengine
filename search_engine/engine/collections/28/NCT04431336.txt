
    
      To understand the clinical characteristics, treatment options and prognosis of aortic
      dissection or aneurysm in young people，the study plan will include young people younger (≤50
      years) with aortic dissection or aneurysm from 2020.6. Then collect their clinical data and
      conduct follow-up studies, in order to understand younger aortic dissection or aneurysm
      patients.
