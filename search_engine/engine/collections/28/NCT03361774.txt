
    
      This will be a single center, 8 week, randomized, controlled, examiner blind, two treatment
      arm, parallel design, stratified (by maximum baseline Schiff sensitivity score of the two
      selected test teeth), method development study, in participants in good general health, with
      pre-existing self-reported tooth sensitivity, and at least two sensitive teeth (diagnosed
      clinically) that meet all study criteria at the screening and baseline (pre-treatment)
      visits.
