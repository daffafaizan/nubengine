
    
      For this cross-sectional study, 81 patients were recruited by convenience at the Clinical
      Hospital (HC) of the Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais (UFMG), in Brazil.

      COPM was applied to identify the main occupational performance problems reported by
      participants. The COPM is an individual measure of self-awareness about the problems faced
      when carrying out the activities. It identifies problems in the areas of self-care,
      productivity, and leisure, and measure the individual's performance and satisfaction
      regarding these activities
