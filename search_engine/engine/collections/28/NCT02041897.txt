
    
      First, the investigators will send only EUS images of patients with PCL to diagnosis
      committee that is consisted with 8 EUS specialists from different countries.The diagnosis
      will be collected. Then the investigators send the results of EUS-FNA. The diagnosis and
      suggestions will be collected again. The investigators will calculate how much diagnosis has
      been changed because of results of EUS-FNA. The investigators will also follow up these
      patients for 5 years at least to find if these patients benefit from EUS-FNA and
      complications such as needle tract seeding metastases.
