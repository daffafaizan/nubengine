
    
      A cloud-based, biometric-linked vaccination record, mobile-phone reminder and
      compliance-linked incentive software platform to provide robust and universal access of
      vaccinations. The investigators will implement this platform in a low-resource settings with
      the following features: a) Cloud-based for robust and universal access. b) Biometric-linked
      for positive identification. c) Digital storage and reporting for transparent view of program
      operations. d) Global Positioning System (GPS)-linked, allowing rapid assessment of
      vaccination status of communities. e) Increasing vaccination uptake and coverage by
      intelligent and subject-aware automated reminders and compliance-linked incentives.
