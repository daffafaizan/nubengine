
    
      This survey was designed to evaluate the safety and efficacy of long-term use of
      azilsartan/amlodipine combination tablets LD & HD (Zacras Combination Tablets LD & HD) in
      hypertensive patients in daily medical practice.

      For adults, one azilsartan/amlodipine combination tablet (20 mg/2.5 mg [for LD tablets] or 20
      mg/5 mg [for HD tablets] of azilsartan/amlodipine) is administered orally once daily.
      Azilsartan/amlodipine combination tablets should not be used as the first-line drug for the
      treatment of hypertension.
