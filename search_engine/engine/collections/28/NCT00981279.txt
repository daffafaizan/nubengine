
    
      Objective: To delineate the epidemiological profile of HIV seropositive patients on
      antiretroviral therapy at the Clinical Hospital of the Federal University of Goiás.

      Methods: The present study is documental and analitical descriptive and it was conducted at
      the Clinical Hospital of the Federal University of Goiás, trough data collection of 222
      records of individuals in drug therapy. Data were collected regarding the personal aspects,
      aspects related to the infection and information about the most used therapeutic.
