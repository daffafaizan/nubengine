
    
      This study is a secondary analysis using data from participants in the IBD Partners and
      SPARC-IBD cohorts. Data received from IBD Partners consists of patient-reported outcomes
      gathered via online surveys (baseline and 6-month follow-ups). Data received from SPARC-IBD
      consists of patient-reported outcomes gathered via surveys (baseline and follow-ups) as well
      as data from clinical records.
