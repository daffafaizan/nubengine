
    
      Two groups of patients diagnosed with gestational diabetes mellitus. Once the selection
      criteria are verified, they will be assigned randomly in one of the two arms,one of them will
      recive 5,000 IU of vitamin D and the other one placebo (talcum food grade). Baseline
      measurements of vitamin D, glucose,insulin, HBA1c, calcium, phosphorus. At the end of the
      eight weeks will be determined again the aforementioned biochemical parameters.
