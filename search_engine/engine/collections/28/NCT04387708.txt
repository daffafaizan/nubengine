
    
      Interviews will be recorded, made anonymous and transcribed verbatim. The resulting text will
      be subjected to an inductive thematic analysis. The investigators will analyse the interviews
      to identify themes. Then, based on the preliminary results, higher key themes will be
      proposed, i.e. the themes most present and salient in the material. They will look for a
      concordance between the different members of each family. Several profiles may be proposed
      based on the patient's and parents' experiences and will be presented in the form of a
      thematic tree.

      The study will be proposed to all eligible patients in order to obtain as much material as
      possible.
