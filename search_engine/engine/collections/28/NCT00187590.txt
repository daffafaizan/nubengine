
    
      One year retrospective look at outcome measures followed by a one year trial of a phone call
      after each ER visit in half of the group. Outcomes measured are cost, ER visits,
      hospitalizations, death, length of stay. Adults only, English or Spanish, questionnaire on
      depression, appointments, drug use with referral to case management services, drug rehab
      services, or followup appointments. Time for intervention is recorded to estimate cost.
