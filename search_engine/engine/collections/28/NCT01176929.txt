
    
      This is a randomized, controlled and prospective trial comparing an experimental group (usual
      treatment + interventions) to a control group (usual treatment only). All participants are
      evaluated after one year of monitoring. The search duration is 2 years (one inclusion year
      and one year of follow-up). It is planned to include 330 patients, 165 patients in each
      group.

      The program (experimental group) includes three interventions:

        -  A series of three telephone calls ( the second week , one month and three months after
           discharge from hospital )

        -  A systematic telephone contact with the referring physician

        -  A telephone helpline for people who made a suicide attempt and referring physicians.
