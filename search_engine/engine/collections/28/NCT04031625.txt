
    
      The metastatic ColoRectal Cancer (mCRC) Database base contains data collected during the
      regular clinical patient management (sociodemographic, clinical, pathological, radiological,
      biological, disease evolution, ...). This database allows easy access to information in a
      centralized and structured way. This cohort is used to gather insights for research purposes.

      All data collected come from medical files of patients managed at Centre Georges Francois
      Leclerc, Dijon, France.
