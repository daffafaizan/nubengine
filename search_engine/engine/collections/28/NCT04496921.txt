
    
      The investigators propose a pilot study (carried out to assess its feasibility on a larger
      scale) to evaluate if a supplement of vitamin K improves vitamin K serum levels in CF
      patients by comparing:

        -  Group A: a vitamin K supplement of 2mg, every day for 6 months

        -  Group B: a vitamin K supplement of 7mg, twice a week for 6 months
