
    
      This sectional study included 100 patients with non-anemic FMS diagnosis and 100 patients
      with non anemic non FMS diagnosis whose ferritin levels were measured within the last four
      weeks. The participants were evaluated using Fibromyalgia Impact Questionnaire (FIQ), Beck
      Anxiety Inventory (BAI), Beck Depression Inventory (BDI) and Pittsburg Sleep Quality Index
      (PSQI) as well as with a questionnaire form about descriptive characteristics. Serum ferritin
      level of <30 ng/mL was considered iron deficiency.
