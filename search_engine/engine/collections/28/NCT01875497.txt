
    
      Fifty four Brazilian healthy military without hyperglycemia, diabetes mellitus and endocrine
      disease were enrolled in this study. All subjects were high physically military trained. The
      Ethical Committee of the Universidade do Estado do Rio de Janeiro in Brazil (COEP008/2011)
      approved the study protocol. Informed consent was obtained from each subject after
      explanation of purpose and risks of all procedures used.
