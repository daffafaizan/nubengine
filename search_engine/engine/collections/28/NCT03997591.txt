
    
      Program Ready2E.A.T. consists on the training of specific upper limb tasks, including:

        -  reaching during attention tasks

        -  reaching during memory tasks

        -  reaching during sequential motor tasks

        -  reaching during fine hand movements

      This program has been tested as a measure of functional status (cognitive and motor
      habilities) in population with different risks; It also has been tested as an effective
      program for reeducation in cognitive affected population.
