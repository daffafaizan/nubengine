
    
      Between January 2014 and July 2017 a RCT was conducted at Sorlandet Hospital Kristiansand,
      Norway. The trial was approved by the regional ethics committee (2013/1853/REK) and
      registered at ClinicalTrials.gov (ClinicalTrials.gov Identifier NCT02028468). This study is a
      follow-up on the same study population.

      150 patients (70 to 90 years) with a displaced femoral neck fracture was randomized to be
      operated With either anterolateral og Direct lateral surgical approach. Investigators want to
      compare the radiological features of the femoral stem between the two Groups.
