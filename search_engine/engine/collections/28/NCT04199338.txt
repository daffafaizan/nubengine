
    
      In the study, which will last for 3 months, the patients in the intervention group will
      receive pre-test Web-based training based on the Self-Care Deficiency Theory. And an
      intervention group will be expected to use the website weekly. Also weekly reminder messages
      will be sent regularly.

      Following the final test, individuals in the enterprise group will be asked to evaluate their
      views on the website in writing with the WAMMI test.
