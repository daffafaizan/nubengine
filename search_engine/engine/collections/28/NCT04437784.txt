
    
      This prospective study included 180 patients with bilateral primary inguinal hernia;
      randomized by sealed envelopes into 3 groups; each includes 60 patients. Group I treated by
      laparoscopic trans-abdominal pre-peritoneal (TAPP) repair using 2 separate meshes, Group II
      treated by open pre-peritoneal (PP) single mesh repair, while Group III treated by bilateral
      Lichtenstein repair. The 3 groups were compared regarding: operative time, postoperative
      complications, postoperative pain, 3 years-recurrence rate and patient's satisfaction.
