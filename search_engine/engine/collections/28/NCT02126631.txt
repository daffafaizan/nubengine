
    
      The purpose of this research is to simplify the system of recording ECGs. Carnation
      Ambulatory Monitoring (CAM) System is a small patch system that can be worn during most
      activities include showering. Each patient will be his or her own control, and will wear the
      CAM and Holter systems simultaneously for 24 hours. Both systems will then be sent out for
      analysis.
