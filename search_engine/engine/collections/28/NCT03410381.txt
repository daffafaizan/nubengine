
    
      100 patients having recently attempted suicide or having suicidal ideation will be recruited.
      They will be asked to use the application EMMA during 6 months and will be followed up during
      this period.

      An optional qualitative study conducted on 25 patients will allow to evaluate more finely
      their subjective experience of the use of the application.

      Initial visit (inclusion) : clinical, biological and neuropsychological assessment and
      installation of the application EMMA.

      First visit (1st month) and second visit (3rd month) : clinical assessment

      Last visit (6th month) : clinical, biological and neuropsychological assessment and optional
      qualitative interview.
