
    
      The sample is to be recruited online (using platforms such as MTurk and Prolific). The
      participants will not the topics of study prior to enrollment because this research will be
      listed online only as "research." Subjects are randomized to messages. The subjects will then
      be asked questions to measure intentions for future behavior.

      When asked about future behavior, the response options are "yes" or "no" or scaled. Basic,
      socio-demographic variables, such as age and gender, will also be measured. The study will
      include at least 100 per cell.
