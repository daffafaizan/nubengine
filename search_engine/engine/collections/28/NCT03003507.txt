
    
      Randomized, cross-over, controlled, double-blind clinical trial without washout. Adult
      patients admitted to the ICU who presented capillary glycemia values> 180mg / dl with a full
      and exclusive enteral diet. They were randomized and observed for 4 days (2 days with each
      diet: with / without fructose). Capillary glycemia was measured 4 / 4h.
