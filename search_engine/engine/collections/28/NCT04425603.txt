
    
      The clinical evaluations will be based on standard, functional and pain parameters Harris Hip
      Score pre-operatively, and at 6 months, 3, 5, 7.5 and 10 years post-operatively. Postal
      Oxford questionnaire preoperatively, and yearly thereafter. The objective of this study is to
      analyse the clinical and radiographic responses and the complication rates for patients
      undergoing primary total hip arthroplasty using the Anthology Hip Replacement System
      (Anthology Hip System).
