
    
      Patients with newly diagnosed achalasia have been submitted to either treatment with
      pneumatic dilatation or surgical myotomy. Minimal follow up five years.

      During follow up patients have been interviewed with regular intervals, by use validated
      questionnaires. The level of symptom control has been assessed as well as the scoring
      cumulative incidence of treatment failures.

      Health Related Quality of Life (HRQL) has been regularly checked.
