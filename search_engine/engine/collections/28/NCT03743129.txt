
    
      Anlotinib (AL3818) is a kind of innovative medicines approved by State Food and Drug
      Administration（SFDA:2011L00661） which was researched by Jiangsu Chia-tai Tianqing
      Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. Anlotinib is a kinase inhibitor of receptor tyrosine with
      multi-targets, especially for VEGFR1、VEGFR2、VEGFR3、FGFR1/2/3、PDGFRa/β c-Kit and MET.
