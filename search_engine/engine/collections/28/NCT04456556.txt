
    
      This observation case series study included 5 patients referred to the Ophthalmology
      Department, Minia University with severe ophthalmic manifestations in the form of combined
      endophthalmitis and orbital cellulitis. On doing the work-up of covid-19 infection according
      to the recommendations of Egyptian Ministry of Health, patients proved to be infected by the
      novel Corona virus. In addition to the Corona work-up, all patients were subjected to full
      ophthalmological evaluation, ocular ultrasound and orbital CT examination.
