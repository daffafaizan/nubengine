
    
      A total of 100 participants who will receive total knee arthroplasty will be recruited in
      this study. Fitbit ONE, the activity tracker, will be used for all of them to monitor the
      daily steps during the rehabilitation, to be exact, before and at 6 weeks, 3 months, 6
      months, 1 year after TKA. Moreover, pain score, self reported questionnaire will also be
      estimated at those time points.
