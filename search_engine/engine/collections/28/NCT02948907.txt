
    
      Patient enrolment and data acquisition is to be carried out on a prospective basis. Patients
      with hilar and/or mediastinal lymphadenopathy and indication for EBUS-TBNA are enroled. It is
      planned to evaluate 100 lymph nodes by elastography and THI. All patients will undergo
      diagnostic bronchoscopy at one study centre in Heidelberg.
