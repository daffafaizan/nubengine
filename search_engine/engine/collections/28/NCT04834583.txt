
    
      All manual therapists that are members of a professional organization in Sweden will be
      invited to participate. The study started in November 2020 with follow-ups planned after 3,6
      and 12 months.

      All data-collection is taking place with digital questionnaires.

      There are three main areas of interest:

        1. Work environment (knowledge about viral contageon, protection, availability of
           protective measures, disturbances in care for patients)

        2. Health (physical and psychological, how many caught the virus?)

        3. Economy (income, costs, turnover)
