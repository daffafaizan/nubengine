
    
      In the center of the analyses are the perioperative consumption of transfused red cell
      concentrates, platelet concentrates and frozen fresh plasma in children and adolescents. The
      distribution by subject area is also examined, demographic distribution and indicators on
      transfusion. With the introduction of the Patient Blood Management for adults at the
      University Hospital in 2013 have become potentially also result in changes in transfusion
      practice in children, which should also be investigated.
