
    
      The purpose of this study is to determine what the effect of a common ankle manipulation on
      the supporting ligaments of the ankle.

      Design: The researchers will conduct a quasi-experimental study with a method of convenience
      sampling. The design of this study will allow to determine what the direct effect of a common
      a commonly used physical therapy ankle manipulation is on the length of the soft tissue
      connection between the smaller bone in the lower leg and the heel bone and the smaller bone
      in the leg and the ankle bone.
