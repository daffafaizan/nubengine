
    
      In women who underwent laparoscopic unilateral ovarian cystectomy, the effect of SurgiGuard@
      was assessed by randomization, using SurgiGuard@ and Bipolar electro cauterization, followed
      by comparative evaluation of hemostasis, ovarian function preservation, safety using
      postoperative Hb, anti-mullerian hormone (AMH), pelvis ultrasonography (USG), and physical
      examination
