
    
      A prospective cohort study investigating 208 women with PCOS giving birth after singleton
      pregnancies at Odense Univesity Hospital during 2003-2011 and a date-of-childbirth matched
      control group of 1040 women. Anthropometric data from children is recorded as well as data on
      maternal parity, age, pre-gestational BMI, and adverse obstetric outcomes.
