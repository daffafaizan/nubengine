
    
      In healthy volunteers and subjects with dietary regulated diabetes mellitus, glucose, protein
      mixture or fat emulsion will be served individually and on a separate occasions a mixed meal
      will be served. Samples will be taken during the following 300 min. Concentrations of the gut
      hormones glucose-dependent insulinotropic polypeptide and glucagon-like peptide-1, insulin
      and glucagon will be determined.
