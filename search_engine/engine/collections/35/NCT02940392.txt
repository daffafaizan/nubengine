
    
      Subjects with BPH symptoms were treated with the Rezum device with a range of thermal
      energies (as measured by calorie output) to optimize the setting for maximum lesion size with
      minimal intra and post-procedure discomfort. Lesion size and ablated tissue resorption rate
      was followed post-procedure at 1 week, 1 month, 3 months, and 6 months.
