
    
      An important determinant of activities of daily living performance is standing balance.
      Therefore, poor balance, or postural stability, is significant predictors the risk of fall.
      After a fall, the patient can experience psychological, physical, social, economic, and
      sometimes fatal effects. The injuries can reduce the patient's mobility as well as
      independence and influence quality of life.Patients with chronic stroke wearing functional
      insoles and sham insoles to challenge balance tests.
