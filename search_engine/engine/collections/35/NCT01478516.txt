
    
      Autologous plasmin enzyme has been used to liquefy the gel structure of the vitreous body and
      to decrease the adherence of the posterior vitreous cortex to the inner limiting membrane in
      clinical studies. The investigators performed intravitreal autologous plasmin enzyme for
      macular edema. in addition, the investigators collected vitreous body in macular edema and
      analyzed fibrinolytic system.
