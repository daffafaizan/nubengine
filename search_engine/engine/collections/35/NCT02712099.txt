
    
      -  This is a prospective study which will include 50 patients who will be selected from the
           outpatient and inpatient obstetric Ain Shams university maternity hospital.

        -  All patients will undergo the following :

             1. History taking

             2. Clinical examination.

             3. For each patient, the whole placenta will be scanned in a systematic fashion using
                both 2D grayscale and 2D power Doppler ultrasound then displayed by 3D TUI to
                determine whether those patients suspected of having advanced invasive
                placentation.
