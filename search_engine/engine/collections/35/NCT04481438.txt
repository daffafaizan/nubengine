
    
      Frailty is a complex elderly problem and a precursor of functional degradation in elder
      adults. The cause of frailty is multifactorial. Regular exercise intervension could improve
      frailty condition and general functional fitness. The acupunch exercise program was developed
      based on Jing-Luo theory of tranditional Chinese Medicine. The aim of this study will to test
      the effects of acupunch exercises on the frail older adults in long-term care facilities.
