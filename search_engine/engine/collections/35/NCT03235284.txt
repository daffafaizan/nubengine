
    
      Patients with Parkinson's disease present motor dysfunctions such as stiffness, tremor,
      postural instability and bradykinesia, which leads to alterations of balance, necessitating
      specialized physiotherapeutic treatment. In this way this study aims to determine the
      effectiveness of two tools used in physiotherapy, kinesiotherapy and the Nintendo Wii. 45
      patients will be evaluated through the Berg Equilibrium Scale, Evaluated by the Dynamic Gait
      Index and the Timed up and go and PDQ-39. Later patients will be randomized to receive 16
      sessions of Kinesiotherapy or Nintendo Wii alone or together, twice a week, for 50 minutes at
      the Neurociencias Outpatient Clinic of Teacher Magalh√£es Neto - FUBA. After this period the
      patients will be reassessed to verify the effects of the techniques.
