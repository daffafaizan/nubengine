
    
      This study will enroll up to 202 subjects (assumes 15 % exclusion/drop-out rate). The target
      number of subjects is 75 active TB (recruited from subjects presenting at TB clinics) and 100
      active TB excluded (recruited from subjects in the community).

      All subjects enrolled in this study will be men or women, aged 18 years or older.

      Duration: 1 year
