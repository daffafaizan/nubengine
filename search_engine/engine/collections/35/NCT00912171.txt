
    
      The aim of the project is to evaluate whether leukotriene is less, equally or more efficient
      than nasal steroid in children with adenotonsillar hypertrophy and with mild and moderate
      OSAS checking if leukotriene administrated for 24 weeks improves overnight oximetry,
      polysomnography and checking if leukotriene as anti-inflammatory of upper airway really
      reduces the tonsils and adenoids dimensions.
