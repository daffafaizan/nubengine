
    
      Multicentric, parallel, randomized, double blind and controlled clinical-nutritional study of
      8 weeks of duration and 2 study groups of treatment: Experimental group (experimental
      nutritional oral supplement); and Control group (control nutritional oral supplement) to
      evaluate the effect on the nutritional and functional status, the quality of life and body
      composition of patients with cancer and malnutrition undergoint specficic treatment
      (chemotherapy, radiotherapy, inmunotherapy).
