
    
      This prospective randomized controlled double-blinded study was conducted with 50 liver
      transplantation donors between 18-65 years who were scheduled to undergo right hepatectomy
      surgery. Cases to whom TAP block with ultrasound was applied after standard general
      anesthesia were determined as Group 1 and cases to whom only standard general anesthesia was
      applied, were determined as Group 2.
