
    
      CardioCHUVI is a retrospective study aim to analyze all consecutively patients admitted by
      acute coronary syndrome to University Hospital Alvaro Cunqueiro of Vigo, between January 2010
      to January 2018

      The objective of the project was to study cardiac and bleeding complications, such as
      reinfarction, heart failure, atrial fibrillation, stroke, and bleeding events, together with
      all-cause and cardiac mortality
