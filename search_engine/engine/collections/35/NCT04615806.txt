
    
      This study will be accepted in esophageal cancer patients with Radical Esophag-ectomy as the
      research object.We will divide them into two groups: experimental group for injection of
      indocyanine green group and control group for injectable in-docyanine green group.We will
      compare with the accuracy,false positive rate and false negative rate,sensitivity,
      specificity and related indicators of intraoperative lymph node cleaning,in order to explore
      the common position of esophageal cancer sentinel lymph node,guidance of esophageal cancer
      lymph node cleaning thoroughly.
