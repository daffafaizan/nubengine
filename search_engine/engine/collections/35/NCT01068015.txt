
    
      National sentinel surveillance data show that HIV infections are increasing fast among men
      who have sex with men(MSM) in China. In some cities HIV prevalence among MSM is higher than
      10%.It is urgent for China to explore innovative intervention to reduce HIV/AIDS transmission
      among this population.

      The purposes of this study are:

        1. To evaluate the effectiveness of comprehensive interventions to decrease the HIV/STIs
           incidence among MSM population in China. The comprehensive intervention components
           include male circumcision, POL intervention, HIV testing and counseling and condom
           promotion.

        2. To determine the acceptability of male circumcision among MSM population in China.
