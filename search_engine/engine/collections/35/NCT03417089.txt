
    
      The falciform ligament is hanging down in the upper abdomen and usually impede the work
      during laparoscopic upper GIT surgery, especially in obese patient, so the aim of this study
      is to evaluate the impact of routine suspension of falciform ligament during mini gastric
      bypass, which is advanced laparoscopic procedure, by comparing the outcomes in 2 groups of
      patient in the fist group the investigators will routinely suspend the falciform ligament and
      the other group the investigators will working without suspension
