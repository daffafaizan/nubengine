
    
      This study investigated whether the supplementation of barley β-glucan is reduce the visceral
      fat as well as the serum low-density lipoprotein cholesterol (LDL-C) and total cholesterol
      (TC) in mildly hypercholesterolemic subjects. In a 12 week double-blind, controlled study,
      subjects were randomized to one of treatment group(3.0g/day as β-glucan) or placebo group.
