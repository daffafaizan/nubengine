
    
      The reduction in refractive cylinder compared to pre-operative corneal astigmatism will be
      assessed using pre- and postoperative subjective refraction evaluation. Units of the
      measurement: diopters.

      Monocular best corrected distance visual acuity will be assessed using ETDRS charts. Units of
      the measurement: logMAR.

      There are no complications related to the BunnyLens TR implantation beyond those that might
      be expected in standard cataract surgery. The complications will be reported using Ocular
      condition forms included in the CRFs.
