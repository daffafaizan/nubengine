
    
      The scalp is the most frequently affected body region in psoriasis. Scalp Psoriasis can
      impair the quality of life greatly due to pruritus. This is however not usually investigated
      as a primary outcome of treatments.

      This study will investigate the effects of apremilast on the pruritus and quality of life and
      other patient reported outcomes in patients with scalp psoriasis.
