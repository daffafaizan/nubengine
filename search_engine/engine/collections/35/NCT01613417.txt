
    
      Adult patients were given two MRI exams, one after injection of ProHance (0.1 mmol/kg) and
      one after injection of Gadovist/Gadavist (0.1 mmol/kg). The exams were performed with
      identical equipment and imaging parameters with the order of the two exams randomized and
      2-14 days between the first and the second MRI exam. Image data was evaluated by 3 expert
      neuroradiologist blinded to the agent administered and patient clinical data.
