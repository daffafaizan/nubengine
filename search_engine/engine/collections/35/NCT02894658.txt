
    
      Objectives To determine whether a single treatment with thermal pulsation relieves dry eye
      symptoms (primary objective) and improves objective findings of meibomian gland dysfunction
      in patients with Parkinsons disease.

      Methods and Measures

      Design

        -  Randomized, controlled trial

        -  Patient eyes will be randomized to either receive a single thermal pulsation treatment
           the FDA-approved Lipiflow thermal pulsation system or to use traditional eyelid hygiene
           or to use traditional eyelid hygiene
