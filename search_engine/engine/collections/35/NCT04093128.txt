
    
      This study will be carried out to examine the effect of herbal tea in recruited subjects in
      an intervention single-group pretest-posttest study for a period of 7 weeks. Subjects will
      consume twice a day for a period of 7 weeks an herbal tea of (carob, anise, wild thyme, and
      eucalyptus leaves) before eating. The total sample (n=30) adults aged (19-57 years) , body
      mass index BMI range (19-35 kg/m2)
