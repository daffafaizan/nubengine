
    
      All patients were submitted to office blood pressure measurement, Ambulatory blood pressure
      monitoring and 24-hour Holter monitoring.

      LDL cholesterol (mg/dL), triglycerides (mg/dL), creatinine (mg/dL), serum uric acid, serum
      sodium, serum potassium, plasma aldosterone concentration (PAC) and plasma renin activity
