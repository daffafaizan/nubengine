
    
      90 patients scheduled for thoracic cancer surgeries, allocated in 3 groups for serratus
      anterior block (SAB) and erector spinae block (ESB) and control group. During anaesthesia:
      total intraoperative fentanyl required will be recorded. After surgery, all patients will be
      connected to PCA device containing morphine solution, the amount of morphine consumed during
      the first 24 hours will be recorded. Other secondary outcomes are: numerical rating scales
      and postoperative pulmonary functions.
