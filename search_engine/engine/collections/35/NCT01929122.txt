
    
      Objectives:

        1. To learn about how increased cooked navy bean powders or rice bran dietary intake affect
           the stool microbiota of colorectal cancer survivors and healthy adults.

        2. To determine whether cooked navy bean powders or rice bran consumption modulate
           microbial metabolism.

        3. To observe dietary changes in the blood, urine and stool metabolite profiles and
           inflammatory cytokines.
