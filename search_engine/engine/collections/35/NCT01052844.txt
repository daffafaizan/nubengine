
    
      This was a prospective, double-blind, placebo-controlled study conducted at our institution
      (Faculdade de Medicina da Fundação ABC and affiliated Hospitals) from April 2009 to April
      2010. Patients and personnel involved in the study were blinded to the assigned treatment.
      The study was approved by the ethics committee of our institution. All the patients provided
      written informed consent.
