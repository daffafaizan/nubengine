
    
      Eligibility Criteria:

      Adults > 18 yrs with chronic central or peripheral neuropathic pain at the St Joseph's Health
      Care Pain Clinic between June and December 2009.

      Outcome measures: All side effects spontaneously reported. Sedation, nausea, dizziness by
      Visual Analog Score Q 15 minutes; hemodynamic data q 5 minutes during and after infusion for
      30 minutes.

      Daily diary of side effects and Visual Analogue score of Pain for 7 days post-infusion.
