
    
      This is a monocentric prospective cohort study with minimal risk and constraints. Ovarian
      reserve is compared between two groups: 30 PLWH, at time of diagnosis and 6, 12 and 18 months
      after initiation of an antiretroviral therapy, and 30 HIV negative women attending medically
      assisted procreation consultation for male infertility, age, BMI and smoking habits matched.
