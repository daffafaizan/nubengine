
    
      Reducing the rest time after diagnostic cardiac catheterization for three hours does not
      increase the complications concerning to the procedure, compared to the rest of five hours.
      The aim of this study is to compare through a randomized clinical trial to reduce the rest
      time in bed for five hours after elective diagnostic cardiac catheterization, with a 6F
      sheath with a transfemoral approach, for a period of three hours of the occurrence of
      bleeding, hematoma, pseudoaneurysm and a vasovagal reaction.
