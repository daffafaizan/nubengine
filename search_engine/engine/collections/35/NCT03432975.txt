
    
      The study will be conducted at the Department of Periodontology, Cliniques universitaires
      Saint Luc. The patients will be informed in detail of the nature and course of study, and
      will sign informed consent prior to engagement in the study. Patients diagnosed with GChP
      based on the current classification of the American Academy of Periodontology will be
      included. Detailed medical, periodontal and dental history will be obtained. Those who will
      fulfill the inclusion/exclusion criteria will be invited to participate in the study.
