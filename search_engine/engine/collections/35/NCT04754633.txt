
    
      A total of 764 adult patients who were diagnosed with COVID-19 inpatient of researchers'
      hospital 2020 were included in this study. The findings were recorded as typical,
      indeterminate, and atypical by the RSNA guidelines and as classical COVID-19, possible
      COVID-19, indeterminate, and non-COVID-19 by the BSTI classification. The data obtained
      according to both classification systems were compared with each other.
