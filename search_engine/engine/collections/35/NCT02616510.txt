
    
      Cardiovascular risk profile will be assessed in women with PCOS and POI > 45 years of age,
      consisting of:

      fasting serum and urine analyses blood pressure, length, weight, waist circumference, hip
      circumference ECG carotid intima media thickness cardiac ultrasound

      spare serum, plasma and urine will be stored in -80 degrees celsius biobank.
