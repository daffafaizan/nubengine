
    
      Mindfulness-ACT-based interventions aren't widely used in Finnish work health care a the
      moment. This study examines the possibility of using this kind of intervention for reducing
      job burnout and stress and also promoting well-being at work. Muupu-program is standardized
      and can be administered in varying settings. If intervention is effective, it can have an
      impact on Finnish practices when treating job burnout and work-related stress.
