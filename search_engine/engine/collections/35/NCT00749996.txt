
    
      This study is a multi-center, prospective, randomized, controlled trial. Patients will be
      randomized in 2 groups:

      Investigational group: a single level herniectomy procedure, followed by placement of the
      DIAMTM Spinal Stabilization System; Control group: a single level herniectomy procedure;
      After surgery, patients will be followed for a 2 years period with required follow up visits
      at 6 weeks, 3 months, 6 months, 1 year, 2 years and 4 years (optional visit).
