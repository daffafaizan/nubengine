
    
      In the past, the role of laminaria and prostaglandins in cervical dilatation and reducing
      intraoperative complications of cervical dilatation have been proven . Misoprostol is a
      synthetic analogue of prostaglandin E1 used to treat and prevent gastric ulcers, which is now
      widely used in obstetrics and gynecology. Its applications in obstetrics and gynecology
      include medical abortion in the first and second trimesters of pregnancy.
