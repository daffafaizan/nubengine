
    
      The investigators retrospectively reviewed patients who underwent elective or emergent
      endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography at semi-prone position under pharyngeal
      anesthesia (lidocaine spray) with routine monitoring (including electrocardiography,
      non-invasive blood pressure, peripheral capillary oxygen saturation) and standard sedation
      protocol (midazolam 0.02 mgkg-1; fentanyl, 1 mgkg-1; propofol 1 mgkg-1) between 2011 and 2016
      after approval of local ethics committee of Umraniye Training Hospital.
