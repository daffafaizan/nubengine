
    
      This retrospective study is conducted at Health Sciences University Turkey, Sisli Hamidiye
      Etfal Training and Research Hospital Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology. Electronic
      database was searched for patients who were treated for tub-ovarian abscess during the last
      10 years (January 2010- November 2020). Patients' biometric data, admission date and
      duration, medical and surgical treatment modalities were recorded using an Excel sheet.
      Patients will be evaluated on the basis of the treatment they receive, length of treatment,
      if surgically treatment the type of surgery performed. Hospitalization length, readmission,
      recurrence will be compared between treatment modalities. A change in management strategy
      over the years if any will be reported.
