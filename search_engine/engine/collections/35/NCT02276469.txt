
    
      A randomized controlled multi center trial is conducted, where patients receive usual care in
      the control group and usual care with additional peer support for 6 month in the intervention
      group. Psychosocial outcome criteria are collected pre intervention, after 6 month
      intervention and at one year follow up. Days till hospitalization and days spent in hospital
      are collected for one year before recruitment and one year after recruitment.
