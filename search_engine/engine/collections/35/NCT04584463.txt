
    
      Pre-hospital factors associated with poor neurologic outcome are well known. Meanwhile,
      in-ICU factors for the first 24 hours may impact neurologic outcome.

      This observational study aim to study the factors such as ECLS, NSE dosage, therapeutic
      hypotermia... and their association with neurologic outcome in patient with myocardial
      infarction due to instable coronaropathy complicated by an out-of-+hospital cardiac arrest.

      This is designed to be a observationnal, prospective, multicentric national french study
