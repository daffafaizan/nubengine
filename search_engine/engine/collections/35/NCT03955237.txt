
    
      Lactate ringer (LR) solution with % 2 glucose was infused to the patients with preoperative
      blood glucose level lower than 60 mg/dL, LR solution with % 1 glucose was used for the
      patients with preoperative blood glucose level between 60-90 mg/dL, LR solution without
      glucose was used for the patients with preoperative blood glucose level higher than 90 mg/dL
