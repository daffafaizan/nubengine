
    
      The trial was planned with 100 healthy female volunteers between 25 and 55 years of age.

      Treated group: Face device 20 minutes per day, 5 days per week for 12 weeks.

      Control group: No NMES.

      Both Groups: No changes to usual beauty routine, or use of beauty products with an anti-aging
      claim, or changes to usual diet or exercise habits over the course of the 12 week study
      period.

      Assessments:

      Ultrasound measurements of the zygomaticus major muscle were planned at Baseline, and at the
      end of Weeks 6 and 12.

      Psychometric questionnaires at Baseline, end of Weeks 6 and 12.
