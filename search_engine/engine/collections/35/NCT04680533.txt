
    
      This study is a single center, randomized, subject and assessor-blind (i.e., modified,
      double-blind), trial to compare the effects of two types of TENS delivered via wireless
      device on chronic peripheral edema using a minimal-dose The treatment period will be 3 weeks
      in duration. After the treatment period, all subjects will be asked to stop using the device
      for up to 3 weeks or until their symptoms have returned to within 80% of baseline levels
      (i.e., Washout period). Once their symptoms return all subjects will receive the
      commercially-available TENS device to use for an additional 3 weeks (i.e., Extension period).
