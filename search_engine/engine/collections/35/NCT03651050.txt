
    
      The specific aims of this proposed study include:

      Aim 1. To develop a P-MHDT (by employing qualitative focus-group interviews and user-centered
      design to enhance usability).

      Aim 2. To train mental health professionals and community health workers (CHWs) for the
      P-MHDT approach of child mental health promotion in Uganda (including monitoring impacts of
      training on CHWs' program implementation competency).

      Aim 3 (11-24 month). To estimate impacts of the P-MHDT on FBOs and families (i.e.,
      caregivers' mental health literacy, practices, and children's mental health) when implemented
      in the real world FBO settings.
