
    
      The goal of this study is to adapt and evaluate the efficacy of an existing empirically
      supported, family-based affect management intervention to be delivered via telehealth to CWI
      youth in out-of-home placement and their caregiver of origin. The study involves iteratively
      adapt the intervention with feedback from youth and caregiver participants and stakeholder
      partners, as well as evaluate the feasibility, acceptability, and preliminary effectiveness
      of the intervention.
