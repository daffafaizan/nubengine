
    
      The data contained in hospital registry were collected, categorized into demographic,
      previous medical history, the use of NSAID as maintenance therapy or its recent
      administration before diagnosis of COVID-19 disease, presenting manifestations, laboratory
      and CT findings, applied therapies or interventions. Outcomes will be categorized as survival
      and non-survival outcome. Survivors will be subdivided as complete recovery or recovery with
      getting new morbidities. Mortality was subdivided as total hospital and ICU mortality. Data
      will be statistically analyzed in relation to outcomes
