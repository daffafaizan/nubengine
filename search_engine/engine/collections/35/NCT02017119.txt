
    
      Lactulose is a first line treatment for HE, but the adherence to this treatment is relatively
      low due to side effects. Lactulose-paraffin recently available in the market can be used for
      the treatment of HE, and possibly have fewer side effects.

      The aim of this study was to compare the adherence of the treatment of lactulose and
      lactulose-paraffin.
