
    
      Main objective: to evaluate the relationship between PINI score and biomarker related to
      PINI, and 28 days mortality in ICU

      Secondary objectives:

        -  to evaluate the relationship between PINI score (and biomarkers related to PINI) and
           morbidity in ICU : SOFA score, duration of ventilation, duration of antibiotherapy,
           duration of extra renal epuration, length of stay

        -  to evaluate the relationship between PINI score (and biomarkers related to PINI) and
           nutrition in ICU (type of nutrition, calorie content)
