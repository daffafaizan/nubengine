
    
      This retrospective observational study will be conducted at Benha University Hospital, Benha,
      Egypt; after approval by the local ethical committee of Faculty of Medicine, Benha
      University, Egypt.

      We will include in this retrospective study all pediatric patients who were admitted with
      confirmed COVID-19 infection with age range from birth to 18 years during the period from
      April 2020 till July 2020. The diagnosis of COVID-19 infection is based on positive
      SARS-CoV-2 RNA detection in throat swab specimens by PCR.

      All data including demography information, contact history, previous history, clinical
      symptoms, clinical examination on admission, and hematology and laboratory information will
      be obtained from inpatient medical records.
