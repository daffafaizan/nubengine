
    
      Inclusion criteria: all participating patients have been diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes, are
      between 25 and 70 years of age, have a regular primary care physician, and have HbA1c levels
      > or = to 7.3.

      Exclusion criteria: Axis I and II psychiatric disorders.

      Primary outcome: HBA1c at 6 months follow up.

      Secondary outcome: Centre for Epidemiological Studies Depression Scale
