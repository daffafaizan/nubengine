
    
      This study will evaluate and compare the effectiveness of a supervised resistance muscular
      exercise (RME) , walking program (WP)and control group (just observation) in women with FMS
      during 16 weeks, on decrease of pain.

      Participants will undergo an assessment including medical some questionnaires to assess their
      FMS symptoms at baseline, after 8 weeks, 16 weeks and 28 weeks.
