
    
      This study aimed to evaluate the effect of topical application of hyperoxygenated fatty acids
      (AGHO), PrevOmega, at the umbilical trocar level after laparoscopic cholecystectomy with
      prosthesis placement, regarding its non-application, on the infection rate. of the surgical
      site (ISQ) and eventration of the trocar orifice (EOT), in patients with risk factors for ISQ
      AND EOT.
