
    
      PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:

      I. Obtain Comprehensive Score for financial Toxicity (COST) questionnaire data from the
      patients who are undergoing any form of mastectomy (with or without breast reconstruction).

      II. To determine which patients are at higher risk of financial toxicity while pursuing
      breast reconstruction.

      III. Assess if financial toxicity or distress is associated with worse BREAST-questionnaire
      (Q) performance as well as quality of life.

      IV. Identify relationships between coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) related productivity
      losses and financial toxicity.

      OUTLINE:

      Patients complete up to 4 electronic questionnaires over 15 minutes before surgery and at 6,
      12, and 24 months after surgery.

      After completion of study, patients will be followed up for 1 year.
