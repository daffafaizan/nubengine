
    
      The present study will have two experiments, one is a cross-section study and the other is
      intervention study.

      As for cross-section study, typical developmental children and children with ADHD will
      perform the inhibitory task with non-emotional and emotional stimulus while recording their
      brain waves.

      The intervention is twice a week for 5 weeks and before and after the intervention, children
      with ADHD will randomly assign to either an exercise group or control group. They will
      perform the inhibitory task while recording their brain waves.
