
    
      Twenty individuals (14 females and 6 males) aged between 10-24 with the diagnosis of
      idiopathic scoliosis will be participated in the study. Individuals underwent 1 hour exercise
      program 3 times a week for 6 weeks. During the rest of the week, same exercise program was
      given as a home program for at least half an hour a day and individuals will followed up.
      Individuals' subjective pain intensity will be assessed with Visual Analogue Scale (VAS),
      pressure pain threshold by algometer, body awareness by Turkish version of Body Awareness
      Questionnaire and quality of life by Turkish version of Scoliosis Research Society (SRS)-22
      scale. Measurements will be performed before and after the 6-week exercise program.
