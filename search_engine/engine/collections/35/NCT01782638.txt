
    
      A randomized, double-blind, parallel-group study 2 groups of patients: 10 patients with
      electrodes placed in STN and 10 patients with electrodes placed in Forel fields.

      1 group of healthy paired-control (n=20).

      Patients will be evaluated without and with treatment on two mornings (J0 and J0+1day).

      In each condition of treatment, 3 conditions of stimulation were tested: without stimulation;
      frequency 25Hz, frequency 130 Hz.

      Evaluations consist on :

        -  motor evaluation (UPDRS)

        -  gait evaluation (to walk on a gait mat)

        -  Stand-Walk-Sit Test
