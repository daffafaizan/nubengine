
    
      There have been several studies regarding efficacy of peri articular infiltration. IPACK
      block is a novel technique gaining popularity where one injects local anesthesia safely under
      ultrasound guidance in the interspace between the popliteal artery and capsule of the knee.

      There have been no studies till date comparing the efficacy between IPACK block and
      Periarticular Infiltration (PAI).
