
    
      Participants will be randomly assigned using a random number generator to either the virtual
      reality condition, or to the standard procedure that includes using books and/or watching
      movies as a standard method of distraction.

      Participants will take part in the study during their scheduled research venipuncture. The
      venipuncture lasts approximately 15-30 minutes. Pre and post venipuncture questionnaires for
      both participant and parent will take 15 minutes to complete. Total study time is between 30
      and 45 minutes. Questionnaires completed by research personal will take 15 minutes to
      complete.
