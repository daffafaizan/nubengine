
    
      The purpose of this pilot study is to determine the feasibility and acceptability of
      randomizing adults with overweight and obesity to an exercise intervention of either
      prescribed morning aerobic exercise (AM-EX) or prescribed evening aerobic exercise (PM-EX)
      progressing to 2000 kcal/wk. Specifically, the purpose of this study is to assess
      recruitment, retention, and adherence to the exercise interventions.
