
    
      A phase one and two study. part one is performed as a single arm unblinded uncontrolled study
      in 16 obese patients undergoing awake intubation (investigational pill). The second part is a
      phase two study randomized single blinded study in 100 patient attending a day case endoscopy
      unit for oesophagogastroduodenoscopy (investigational pill versus lidocaine suspension).
