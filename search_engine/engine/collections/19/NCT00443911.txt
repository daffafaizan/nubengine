
    
      Rheohemapheresis will be performed in 5 cycles. One cycle consists of 2 rheohemapheresis
      treatments on day 1 and day 4. A cycle lasts for 4 weeks and will be repeated every fourth
      week. During each treatment > 75% to 100% of patients total plasma volume has to be
      filtered.General medical reviews, ETDRS and Radner visual acuity assessment, ophthalmologic
      examinations, ocular imaging with OCT 3, autofluorescein imaging and infrared imaging,
      multifocal ERG, ocular echography, and blood examinations will be performed.
