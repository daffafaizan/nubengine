
    
      This is a prospective diagnostic test study. Inclusion criteria are all consecutive patients
      with parotid gland masses referred for treatment at our center, from January 2003 to December
      2007.

      The gold standard for the diagnosis of malignancy is the surgical pathology report of the
      parotidectomy specimen.

      Cytologist and pathologist will be blind. Diagnostic accuracy is determinded by Bayesian
      analysis.
