
    
      Patients are after baseline PSG 1 included and randomized to either UPPP or TE. They are
      stratified according to tonsil size, those with size 2 in one group and those with 3-4 in
      another. They all undergo surgery within two months and thereafter PSG 2 after 6 months and
      PSG after 4 years. At PSG they fill in questionnaires and measurements of blood pressure.
