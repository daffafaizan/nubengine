
    
      Samples stored at the Biomedical Research Institute of A Coruña, Xerencia Integrated
      Management of A Coruña (University Hospital Center of A Coruña), belonging to the collection
      of the line of research "Oral Health and Systemic Relationship" with registration nº 2017/104
      will be used for the realization of the present study.

      Nucleic acid extraction and histological analysis of the samples will be performed to
      evaluate the functional epigenetic modifications associated with peri-implant diseases, in an
      exploratory manner.
