
    
      This study will involve performing ultrasound guided subparaneural popliteal sciatic nerve
      block for 40 patients by using either Lidocaine or Levobupivacaine. Both are commonly used
      local anaesthetics for regional anaesthesia in daily clinical practice. Patients will be
      enrolled from the elective orthopaedic surgical list at Prince of Wales Hospital after
      informed consent.
