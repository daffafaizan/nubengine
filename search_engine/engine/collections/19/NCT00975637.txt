
    
      The study evaluated the efficacy of AMG 827 compared with placebo as measured by the percent
      of improvement in PASI score at week 12. Subjects were randomized ina 1:1:1:1:1 ratio.
      Subjects randomized to receive AMG 827 received 70, 140, or 210 mg at day 1 and weeks 4 and
      8.
