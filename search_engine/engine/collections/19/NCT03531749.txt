
    
      Since Patients HIV+ suffer oxidative stress, microencapsulated of red pomegranate juice will
      be evaluated for decreasing oxidative stress in HIV+ patients it will be compared with
      patients treated with ascorbic acid. The supplementation will take 90 days and blood samples
      were taken four times: at the beginning of the study and every 30 days thereafter.
      Antioxidant activity in the blood serum was measured by the DPPH and ABTS+ methods lipid
      peroxidation was measured as stress oxidative markers
