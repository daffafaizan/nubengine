
    
      This is a multicenter prospective observational study collecting data on over 5000 CAG
      patients admitted to department of cardiology in 12 hospitals from January 2013. Data will be
      collected for more than 1 year on all patients undergoing CAG with or without PCI older than
      18 years without baseline end-stage renal failure needing renal replacement therapy or renal
      transplantation. Data to be collected includes demographic information, admission diagnoses
      and co-morbidities, biomarkers and details on preventive hydration and medications used
