
    
      Transversus abdominis plane (TAP) block has been shown to provide postoperative pain relief
      following various abdominal and inguinal surgeries. The aim of this randomised controlled
      trial was to assess the effect of transversus abdominis plane (TAP) blocks on opioid
      requirements and median hospitai stay, postoperative nause and vomitting in patients
      undergoing laparoscopic cholecystectomy.
