
    
      A prospective, phase II-III study, which will include a series of cases of 30 patients. The
      hospitals involved will be Bellvitge University Hospital and Hospital del Mar.

      Efficacy was evaluated according magnetic resonance image and biopsy of the breast 1 month
      after the procedure.

      Patients will be followed up over a 2-year period to assess cosmetic results, short and
      long-term complications and possible recurrences.
