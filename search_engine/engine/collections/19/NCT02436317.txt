
    
      This is a Prospective, Double-center, randomized clinical trial. The purpose of the trial is
      to evaluate the benefit of adding POC US to the management of inpatients admitted to the
      internal ward with respiratory, cardiovascular or hemodynamic abnormalities.

      This is a stage 1 pilot trial aimed to provide better understanding on the study population
      and study groups. This trial will comprise a total of 60 patients, 30 subjects on the
      intervention group and an equal number on the control group. The results of this pilot trial
      will be to design a larger trial and calculate a required sample size.
