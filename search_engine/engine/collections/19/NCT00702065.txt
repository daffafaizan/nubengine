
    
      This is a multi-center, multi-regional observational,24 month study.

      Health-related quality of life data from the MusiQoL instrument and SF-36 instrument will be
      collected at baseline, M6, M12, M18 and M24 in patients with MS. In addition, physical health
      outcomes such as expanded disability status scores (EDSS) will be collected every 6 months.
