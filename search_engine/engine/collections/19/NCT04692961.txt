
    
      A total of twenty-three patients will be recruited at National Taiwan University Hospital
      Yunlin Branch. All of them will receive fibula flap or peroneal flap harvest operations. At
      the time points of pre-operation, 1-month post-operation, 3-month post-operation, and 6-month
      post-operation, the muscles of both legs of each patient will be evaluated by elastography,
      and the function of both legs will be evaluated by American Orthopedic Foot and Ankle Score
      (AOFAS). Paired t-test will be used to compare the results of each evaluations.
