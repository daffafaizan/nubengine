
    
      At baseline, the timed 360° turn test, Berg Balance Scale, Timed Up and Go test, Functional
      Reach Test, Four Square Step Test, and One-legged Stance Test is applied to the Multiple
      Sclerosis group. The timed 360° turn test is repeated after seven days from the first
      application in patients with Multiple Sclerosis. Healthy controls are going to perform only
      the timed 360° turn test.
