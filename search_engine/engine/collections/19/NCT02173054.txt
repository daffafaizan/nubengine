
    
      The investigators team research with patients by separate patients into 3 groups and compare
      among each other. In the first group use only adapalene gel, the second group use adapalene
      gel with moisturizer containing three substances mentioned above, and the last group use
      adapalene gel with placebo moisturizer. To compare outcomes among 3 groups, the investigators
      will measure degree of redness, scale, oily face, moist, number of acne, quality of life, and
      satisfaction after using moisturizer.
