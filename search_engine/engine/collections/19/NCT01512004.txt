
    
      This is a multi-center, randomized, doubled-blind, double dummy clinical study, in comparison
      with Tolterodine Tartrate Extended-Release Tablet as active parallel control to evaluate the
      efficacy and safety of Propiverine Hydrochloride Extended-Release Capsule in the treatment of
      Overactive Bladder in Chinese population with urgent micturition, frequent micturition and/or
      urge urinary incontinence.
