
    
      This prospective non-interventional study aims to evaluate via two questionnaires the
      evolution of perceived stress in general practice residents of two centres in France, Rouen
      and Caen. The two questionnaires contain the Perceived Stress Scale, as well as items
      regarding socio-demographic data and stress factors. The first questionnaire is to be
      distributed during the epidemic COVID-19 wave and the second questionnaire at the end of the
      epidemic COVID-19 wave.
