
    
      This study is performed in healthy adults, older than 18 years, with no gastro-intestinal
      complaints. The intervention group receives personalized dietary advice (PDA) based on their
      current food pattern, preferences and selfregulation capacity. The PDA aims to compute
      high-fiber alternatives for products that subjects currently use and that are close to their
      current eating behavior, to help increase dietary fiber intake. This PDA will be provided
      using an online webportal. The control group only receives a general dietary advice, e.g. (a)
      flyer(s) containing information on fibers from the Netherlands Nutrition Center and the MLDS.
      The intervention group will also receive this general advice next to the PDA.
