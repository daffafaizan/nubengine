
    
      At immediate, 1, 24, 48, and 72 hours post-test patch removal (date & time of each assessment
      will be recorded), each test site will be scored for erythema and oedema by the trained
      investigator using the ISO 10993 scoring system.

      Primary Endpoint The irritation of the test product according to the Cutaneous Irritancy
      Index value. The test product should be graded as 'non-irritant' and confirmed by the study
      dermatologist.
