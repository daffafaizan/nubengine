
    
      Tonsillectomy with or without adenoidectomy is the most common major surgical procedure
      performed in children. Although it's a relatively simple procedure, may present complications
      as hemorrhage and death. The mortality associated to the surgery range between 1/1.000 and
      1/27.000 in the literature, with approximately 30% due to hemorrhage.

      Tranexamic acid is an antifibrinolytic hemostatic indicated to control bleeding in cases of
      hyperfibrinolysis or defective hemostasis diseases. It's major indications are prostatic
      surgery and menorrhagia.
