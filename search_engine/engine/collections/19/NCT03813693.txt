
    
      This is a pilot randomized controlled clinical trial, composed of patients submitted to
      potentially contaminated elective surgeries, randomly assigned to an intervention group,
      consisting of those who used towels impregnated with pre-operative CHG 2% and control group,
      composed of by the pre-operative bath with 2% conventional / liquid CHG. Both used the
      products the night before and the morning of surgery and received verbal and written
      guidance.
