
    
      The Chongqing intracerebral hemorrhage study will try to address several key issues in ICH.
      The baseline clinical data including demographic, disease severity scores, prior drug history
      and relevant medical history, premorbid mRS scores will be prospectively recorded. The
      admission and follow-up imaging data of all registered patients will be saved as Dicom format
      for future use and review. The functional outcomes will be assessed and recorded by
      experienced neurologists.
