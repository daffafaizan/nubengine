
    
      Inclusion criteria Severe isolated TR for more than 6 months

      Exclusion criteria

        1. Severe mitral and aortic valvular heart disease

        2. Age equals to or more than 75 years

        3. Malignancy or serious comorbidity with life expectancy < 5years

        4. Acute or subacute infective endocarditis requiring antibiotics therapy

        5. Untreated hyperthyroidism

        6. Constrictive pericarditis

        7. Unclosed significant atrial septal defect

        8. Previous tricuspid valve surgery

        9. Significant coronary artery disease

       10. Cardiomyopathy

       11. Pregnancy

       12. Severe resting pulmonary hypertension(TR Vmax >4.0 m/s)
