
    
      This is a randomized clinical trial carried out in primary care. The study's scope of
      activity will include ten urban primary care centers. All selected patients with inclusion
      criteria will receive a geriatric assessment and other required medical treatment. Next, they
      will be allocated either to an intervention group or control group. The intervention group
      will be trained for three months according to the OTAGO exercise program (training phase).
      Followed by a loyalty phase during which they will be monitored quarterly for a year by their
      assessment team. The control group will be receive normal medical treatment. Falls and
      fractures will be monitored quarterly in both groups during 15 months.
