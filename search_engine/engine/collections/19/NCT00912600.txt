
    
      This was a prospective multicenter observational cohort study of elderly individuals
      presenting to one of 15 emergency departments and possibly qualifying for admission to an
      ICU. To be included, subjects had to be aged at least 80 years and be diagnosed by the ED
      physician with one of 74 conditions potentially warranting ICU admission.Outcome studied are
      ICU eligibility as assessed by emergency and ICU physicians, hospital and six-month death and
      changes in functional status in the six month following emergency department visit.
