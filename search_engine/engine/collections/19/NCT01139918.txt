
    
      This study will recruit three cohorts of 40 patients each: moderate psoriatic arthritis and
      moderate psoriasis, mild psoriatic arthritis and moderate psoriasis, moderate psoriatic
      arthritis and mild psoriasis. Eligible patients will fill the following questionnaires:
      Dermatology Life Quality Index (DLQI), Health Assessment Questionnaire (HAQ), EQ-5D,
      Functional Assessment of Chronic Illness Therapy-Fatigue (FACIT-fatigue) and 36-Item Short
      Form Health Survey Questionnaire (SF-36). In addition, each patient will be asked on a 4
      point Likert scale how much their answer to each question is related to the impact of their
      skin disease or their psoriatic arthritis.
