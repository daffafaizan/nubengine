
    
      3-weekly/ 1cycle treatment

        -  Trastuzumab intravenous administration at a loading dose of 8 mg/kg on day 1 followed by
           6 mg/kg every 3 weeks

        -  Capecitabine oral administration at a dose of 1000 mg/m2 twice daily for 14 days every 3
           weeks (from evening on day 1 to morning on day 15)

        -  Cisplatin intravenous administration at a dose of 80 mg/m2 on day 1
