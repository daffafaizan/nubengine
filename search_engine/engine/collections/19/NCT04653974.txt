
    
      Needle-free injection system(Comfort-In) and dental injection method was applied on the teeth
      of children who need dental treatment.70 patients were included in the study. Patients were
      randomized into two grups according to the injection technique.

      Group1: Needle-free injection system(Comofrt-In) 2:Dental injection method. The pain
      intensity was assessed during anesthesia, during treatment and at the end of thetreatment by
      the Wong Baker Faces Pain Scale.

      The datas were analyzed with a three-way variance method in repeated.
