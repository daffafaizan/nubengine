
    
      In the first phase of two weeks, the sensor-augmented pump therapy (SaP) is carried out
      without these interruption PLGM algorithm. In a second phase for 6 weeks, the PLGM function
      is set. Both phases are compared in terms of the rate of hypoglycemia, the time spent and the
      area under the curve (AUC) glucose range (values <70 mg / dl (3.9 mmol / l)).
