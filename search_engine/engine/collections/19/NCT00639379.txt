
    
      Up to 7 sites in the US will participate. Each investigator will have both test and control
      lens types. There will be a maximum of 6 scheduled visits:

      Visit 1: Habitual lens evaluation / Baseline / Trial fitting Visit 2: Dispensing (to be
      combined with Visit 1 where possible), Visit 3: Questionnaire only (no exam) 7 days (±2)
      after dispensing, Visit 4: 2 week follow-up 14 days (±3) after dispensing, and dispense
      second lens type, Visit 5: Questionnaire only (no exam) 7 days (±2) after 2nd lens dispense,
      Visit 6: 2 week follow-up 14 days (±3) after 2nd lens dispense
