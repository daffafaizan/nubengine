
    
      Query of internal clinical database for identification of patients (2009-2017)with end-stage
      renal disease and critical limb ischemia, receiving either open surgical (Group I "OR") or
      endovascular revascularisation (Group II "EVT").

      Furthermore, retrospective comparison as to morphological criteria (lesion length, peripheral
      run-off, plantar arch) and comorbidities.

      Prospective follow-up of identified patients by means of telephone contacts and/or clinical
      examination of evaluation of Long-term outcome measures (Overall survival, Amputation-free
      survival, wound healing)
