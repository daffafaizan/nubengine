
    
      Sixty of aged over 65 years, living independently in the community for elderly people,
      nondiabetic subjects with serum prealbumin of under 30 mg/dL and BMI under 25 kg/m2 were
      recruited. The subjects were followed for a 2-week pre-intervention period, followed by an
      intervention period, which they received 2 cartons of liquid oral nutritional supplementation
      daily for 2 weeks.
