
    
      Clinically relevant cognitive decrements in relation to diabetes mainly occur during two
      crucial periods in life: the period of brain development in childhood, and the period when
      the brain undergoes neurodegenerative changes associated with ageing.Potential leads for
      treatment are to optimise glycaemic control and avoid and treat vascular risk factors.we want
      to detecte early clinical dementia in diabete by MMSE and Olfactory function.
