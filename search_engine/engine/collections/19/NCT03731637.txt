
    
      A prospective NOD Cohort of 10,000 eligible, enrolled subjects will be assembled over the
      next 3 years, with each patient participating for up to 3 years from the date they meet
      biochemical (glycemic) criteria for diabetes. Sites electronic medical record databases or
      other avenues for recruitment, such as physician and self referral, will be utilized to
      identify subjects meeting criteria for NOD.
