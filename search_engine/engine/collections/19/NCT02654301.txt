
    
      In this double-blind crossover designed study, Participants were randomly assigned to consume
      a sucrose drink (Control, sucrose 50g + deionized water 100g) or a sucrose drink additionally
      containing 5 g (Test 1, sucrose : xylose = 10:1), 3.33 g (Test 2, sucrose : xylose = 15:1) or
      2.5 g (Test 3, sucrose : xylose = 20:1) of D-xylose with a week interval.
