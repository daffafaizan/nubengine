
    
      A population PK approach and an age-down staggered approach will be used. Patients will be
      recruited into 3 predefined age groups: 12-17, 7-11 and 2-6 years. The inclusions will start
      with the older group (Adolescents, 12-17 years), followed by Preadolescents (7-11 years) and
      finally Young Children (2-6 years of age).
