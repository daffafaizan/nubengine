
    
      The dose of cefazolin and Ceftazidime co-administered were 20 mg /kg，they were given
      intraperitoneal twice daily in the first bag and the fourth bag for 5 days，and given 1g once
      daily in the fourth bag for 9 days. Total treatment duration was 2 weeks.

      At least 1-mL serum and 5-mL dialysate samples were collected at 1, 2, 4, 6, 10, 14 days
      after complete instillation of the first dialysis bag containing antibiotic into the
      peritoneal cavity. The samples were then centrifuged at 3,000 rpm, all study samples were
      stored frozen (-80℃) within 1 hour after collection.
