
    
      Leiomyoma are the most common female reproductive tract tumors. Uterine artery
      embolization(UAE) is a minimally invasive treatment alternative to hysterectomy and
      myomectomy. However, post-procedural pain control after UAE remains a major problem.
      Therefore, the aim of study is to evaluate the efficacy of dexmedetomidine in pain control
      after UAE.
