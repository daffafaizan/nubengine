
    
      Patients with hereditary retinoblastoma treated during infancy by external beam radiation
      therapy (EBRT) the risk of second primary cancer occurring within radiation fields is high.
      The aim of this study is to depict by MRI secondary tumors as early as possible, before the
      occurrence of clinical symptoms. Affected patients will be further managed in a national
      expert center with dedicated diagnostic and treatment procedures. However, the benefit of
      such management has to be assessed in terms of prognosis.
