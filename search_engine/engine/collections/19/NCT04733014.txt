
    
      Different attitudes and perceptions are encountered concerning contemporary presbyopia
      management. Exploration of ophthalmologists' attitudes on diagnostic and therapeutic
      management of presbyopia is essential for the development of disease management interventions
      by the Hellenic Ministry of Health and Welfare. Within this context, Greek ophthalmologists'
      perceptions on presbyopia will be explored by means of a custom questionnaire (DePrePer31).
