      Anaesthesia at Beni-Suef University Hospital after obtaining approval from local research and
      ethical committee. Written informed consent will be obtained from each patient before
      operation. Aiming to assess of the early cognitive dysfunction after controlled hypotensive
      anesthesia with either dexmedetomidine or esmolol during middle ear surgeries
