
    
      A parallel randomized clinical trial, examiner-blind, was conducted involving 56 patients
      with ages from 15 to 20, all students of CAMP (Centro de Formação e Integração Social), in
      the city of São Bernardo do Campo, state of São Paulo, Brazil. Randomization: the subjects
      were randomly allocated to two different groups (A for the manual toothbrush and B for the
      sonic toothbrush) by removing from an urn a paper with their respective group. Silness & Löe
      Gingival Index, the Turesky Modification of the Quigley Hein Plaque Index and the Community
      Periodontal Index were evaluated at baseline and after 3 and 6 months.
