
    
      Asymptomatic subjects will be recruited to this study. Superficial electromyography of the
      Sternocleidomastoid, Range of Motion (ROM) and Cranio-Cervical Flexion Test (CCFT) will be
      measured before, immediately after change in focus of attention, 2 days after training under
      a particular focus of attention, and 7 days after training the task under the same focus of
      attention.

      Subjects will be randomized into three groups: Internal focus of attention, external focus of
      attention and control group.
