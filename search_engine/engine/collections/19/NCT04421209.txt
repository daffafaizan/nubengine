
    
      This study is a randomized, double-blind, placebo controlled clinical trial.The main purpose
      of this study is to determine if postsurgical opioid use and pain scores are decreased with
      oral Propranolol treatment. The treatment period will last for six days and the observation
      period will last for three months. Effectiveness of treatment will be assessed by means of
      post-operative opioid consumption as primary outcome measure.
