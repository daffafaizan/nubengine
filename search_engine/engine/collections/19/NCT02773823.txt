
    
      Two public primary schools of a similar size were selected. They were allocated either to the
      intervention or control group. Children in Grade 4th and 5th in each school were invited to
      participate in the physical examination (height and weight measures).Obese children based on
      Chinese BMI cut-off points were included in this study.Children in the intervention group
      were encouraged to take healthy diet habits and increased their physical activity.After eight
      months healthy behavior intervention, Children's height, weight, waist, hip, blood pressure,
      cardiorespiratory fitness will be measured. Fasting blood will be taken to measure lipids and
      glucose levels.
