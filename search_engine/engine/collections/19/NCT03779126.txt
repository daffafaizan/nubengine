
    
      Patients in the present study will be submitted to an evaluation protocol that must be
      started after the consent of the responsible medical team, and the patient through the
      Informed Consent Term. On the first day, patients will be evaluated for their personal
      antecedents, life habits, inflammatory and nutritional status, quality of life, Medical
      Research Council (MRC) and biochemical markers. On the second day, anthropometry, body
      composition and functional capacity will be performed. On the third day, the muscular
      function will be assessed by isokinetic evaluation. After completing the evaluations,
      patients will be allocated into four groups: low frequency (LF), high frequency (HF), low and
      high frequency (LHF), and sham.
