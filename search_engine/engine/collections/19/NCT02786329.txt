
    
      Main goals

        1. Comparative evaluation of the incidence of recurrences after TIVA vs sevoflurane
           anesthesia

        2. Evaluation of the effect of lidocaine infusion associated with TIVA/sevoflurane on the
           incidence of chronic pain and of cancer recurrences and survival.

      Secondary objectives

        1. Evaluation of the influence of lidocaine on postoperative outcome in patients with
           colorectal cancer

        2. Evaluation of the influence of lidocaine on postoperative inflammatory response

        3. Evaluation of the influence of lidocaine on the incidence and severity of postoperative
           pain and outcome in patients with colorectal cancer
