
    
      This is a multinational study with sites in the US, India, Singapore, South Korea, Mexico and
      Chile. There will be 210 subjects (male or female) enrolled that are residing in community or
      facilities with a reliable caregiver that have been clinically diagnosed with Down syndrome.
      The assessments performed during this study will be used to evaluate skills. The study
      duration will be 10 weeks of double blind treatment with a total of 6 visits; 4 in office
      visit and 2 phone visits.
