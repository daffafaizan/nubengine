
    
      All nasopharyngeal patients received radical treatment including neo-adjuvant chemotherapy
      and concurrent chemo-radiotherapy. At the beginning, the investigators assign patient to
      experimental and control groups randomly. Patients in experimental group received standard
      nutritional support by adding NUTRISON. Control patients will give conventional nutritional
      guidance. The investigators collected all the nutritional indexed at first visit, before and
      after radiotherapy, 1, 3, 6, 9, 12 months after radiotherapy. All these data will be set into
      statistical software to produce an optimized model for clinical practice.
