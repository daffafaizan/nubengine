
    
      The patients with univentricular congenital heart disease will perform :

        -  a cardiopulmonary exercise testing with measure VE/VCO2 slope,

        -  a measure of the respiratory drive to hypercapnia with occlusion pressure during the
           rebreathing with at rest (P0,1/PetCO2).

        -  A polysomnography with a scoring of central apnea index. Correlation will be evaluate
           between P 0,1/PetCO2 with VE/VCO2 slope and between P0,1/PetCO2 central apnea index.
