
    
      The diagnosis criteria of type 2 diabetes mellitus are in accordance to 1999 World Health
      Organization (WHO) diabetes diagnostic criteria. The diagnosis criteria of dyslipidemia are
      based on the Guidelines for the Prevention and Treatment of Dyslipidemia in Chinese adults
      (2016, revision edition). The outcomes mainly include HbA1c, fasting blood glucose,
      postprandial blood glucose, body weight, waist circumference, blood lipid and TCM symptom
      score.
