
    
      Study sites: Shanghai, Guangzhou, Beijing

      Eligible subjects were enrolled in the study and randomized into one of the 2 study arms:

      Sequence A1-A2: Oral consumption of milk containing both A1 and A2 type beta casein at Visit
      1 and milk containing only A2 type beta casein at Visit 2; Sequence A2-A1: Oral consumption
      of milk containing only A2 type beta casein at Visit 1 and milk containing both A1 and A2
      type beta casein at Visit 2.

      Washout period: 2 weeks between Visit 1 and Visit 2
