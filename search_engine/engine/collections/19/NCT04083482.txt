      failure ，AKI，fliud overload as well as oliguria in ICU .In clinical patients ，continous
      venovenous hemofoiltration （CVVH）is actually the method of chioce for CRRT in critically ill
      and hemodynamic instable patients .CVVH has significant beneficial effects on removing
      inflammatory cytokines ，improving oxygen index，increasing cardiac index and regulating immune
      dysfuntion .
