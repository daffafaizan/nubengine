
    
      Hemifacial spasm patients who received botulinum toxin A injection were divided into two
      groups.Patients of unilateral group were injected to the affected side and the dose of
      botulinum toxin A was 2.5-3.75 units/per site. Patients of bilateral group were injected to
      the affected side with dose of 2.5-3.75 units/per site and the normal side was injected with
      dose of 1.25 units/per site.

      3 scales for measurement were used.
