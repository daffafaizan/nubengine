
    
      A prospective, sequential two phase multicenter, randomized double-blind, safety, &
      feasibility study. The goal of AIRFLOW-1 will be to compare two energy doses and select the
      optimal energy dose to be utilized in AIRFLOW-2. The goal of AIRFLOW-2 is to compare the
      optimal energy dose to a sham control. All subjects will be followed for a minimum of 3
      years.
