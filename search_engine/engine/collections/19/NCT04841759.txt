
    
      Employees of the Medical University of Vienna, Austria and the General Hospital of Vienna,
      Austria who survived a SARS-CoV2 infection will be invited to take part in an eight weeks
      post-COVID-19 rehabilitation program which is part of a workplace health promotion measure.
      This program consists of eight weeks of exercising (twice per week supervised resistance
      training + individual, heart-rate controlled endurance training recommendations) with a
      sports scientist and a sports medicine specialist, complemented by one session of nutrition
      consultation with a nutritionist and two sessions of psychological consultation with a
      clinical psychologist.

      Parallel to this workplace health promotion program, the scientific evaluation of this
      intevention will be undertaken via this study.
