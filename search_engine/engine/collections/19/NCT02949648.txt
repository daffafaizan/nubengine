
    
      Subjects are from a cohort of Youth Quitline callers and smoking youth (N=200) in Hong Kong.
      The longitudinal study will investigate the effects of baseline e-cigarette use on quitting
      (including relapse), nicotine addiction and intention to smoke at 12-month telephone
      follow-up. A qualitative study will be conducted on 10 ex- and 10 current smokers to
      supplement and facilitate interpretation of the quantitative findings.
