
    
      This study is intended to be a randomized controlled trial comparing immediate cord clamping
      which represents the current standard of care at Oklahoma University Medical Center with
      delayed cord clamping among preterm neonates born between 24 weeks and 34 weeks 0 days weeks'
      gestation. Allocation sequence will be generated by simple randomization using random number
      table in the stratum 24-28 weeks 6 days and 29 weeks to 34 weeks 0 days weeks' gestation
      stratum.
