
    
      This study will be carried out on four groups:

      Group (I): twenty children diagnosed to have idiopathic inflammatory myopathies(IIM)

        -  Group (II): twenty adults diagnosed to have IIM

        -  Group (III):20 healthy children matching age and sex as first control group to children
           with IIM .

        -  Group (VI):20 healthy adults matching age and sex as second control group to adults with
           IIM.

      All patients will be evaluated at baseline at & 4-months follow up using Semi-Quantitative
      strain elastography
