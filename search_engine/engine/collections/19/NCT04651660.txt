
    
      Main study procedures and assessments:

           criteria, CT-scan, Tonal audiometry

        -  Visit 3 (D+30+/-14) FOLLOW-UP 1 MONTH: Tonal audiometry, Impedance, Fitting maps

        -  Visit 4 (D+60+/-14) FOLLOW-UP 2 MONTH: Tonal audiometry, Impedance, Fitting maps,
           Phonemes

        -  Visit 5 (D+90+/-14) FOLLOW-UP 3 MONTH: Tonal audiometry, Impedance, Fitting maps,
           Phonemes
