
    
      For some complicated hepatolithiasis, it is difficult to decide whether to perform surgery on
      biliary stone and portal hypertension simultaneously or separately by staged surgery.There's
      a high risk of intraoperative bleeding, postoperative liver failure and variceal bleeding for
      these patients.How to develop a optimized algorism of simultaneous and staged surgery is
      highly needed. In this protocol, we will assign the eligible cases into two group. And then
      perform surgery as simultaneously or separately.By long-term follow-up,to examine the safety
      and advantages of each procedure.
