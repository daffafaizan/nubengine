
    
      To obtain preoperative, operative and postoperative clinical outcomes data for the Zimmer
      Gender Solutions PFJ Knee Prosthesis used in primary partial knee arthroplasty such as knee
      scoring and patient satisfaction such collection using Patient Questionaire and SF-12 forms.
      Kaplan-Meir survivorship analysis of the device will be performed. Radiographic analysis of
      the device will also be performed at 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, and 10 year follow-up. It is planned that
      the resultant data will be pooled and published by the evaluating surgeons.
