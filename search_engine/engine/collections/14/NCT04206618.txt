
    
      Serum samples for the measurement of several myokines will be obtained from: 1) equal groups
      of premenopausal women with normal BMD, postmenopausal women with normal BMD, postmenopausal
      women with osteopenia, and postmenopausal women with osteoporosis; 2) women with hip fracture
      compared with controls (women subjected to surgery due to knee osteoathritis); 3) women with
      osteoporosis treated with teriparatide or denosumab for 12 months
