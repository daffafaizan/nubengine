
    
      Informed consent will be obtained from all individual participants included in the study.
      Blood sample will be obtained the day before laparoscopic surgery during regular clinical
      preoperative procedures. Peritoneal fluid and uterine fluid will be obtained during regular
      laparoscopic procedure indicated for infertility, the exact protocol of sample obtaining will
      be revealed after publication of results. The aim of the study was to identify potential
      diagnostic and predictive biomarkers or drug targets.
