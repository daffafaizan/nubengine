
    
      Two groups of people, one composed of healthy volunteers and the other of patients with
      incident type 2 diabetes mellitus who never received treatment for their condition, fill in
      the food frequency questionnaire and the physical activity questionnaire. DNA microarray
      genotyping is used to process the DNA samples of the participants. The data on the
      composition of the participants' gut microbiota is obtained through sequencing of their stool
      samples.
