
    
      Patients who assigned to the pitavastatin treatment first group will be given the
      pitavastatin 4 mg qd treatment for 12 weeks after randomization, followed by wash-out period
      of 3 weeks and life-style modification treatment for 12 weeks. Similarly, patients who
      assigned to the life-style modification treatment first group, will be treated with
      life-style modification treatment for 12 weeks after randomization, followed by wash-out
      period of 3 weeks and pitavastatin treatment for 12 weeks.
