
    
      Ten patients will be selected randomly from the outpatient clinic, Alexandria University
      seeking for an immediate implant placement. The sample will be selected to match the
      inclusion and exclusion criteria. It will be operated upon in the Oral and Maxillofacial
      Department, Faculty of Dentistry, Alexandria University. One stage surgery will include tooth
      extraction, placement of an immediate implant, bone grafting material and autologous soft
      tissue graft from the buccal pad of fat. Patients will be assessed clinically and
      radiographically to evaluate bone and soft tissue measurements.
