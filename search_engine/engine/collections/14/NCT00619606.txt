
    
      Hypoxia is common during endoscopy. Following sedation induction, oxygen saturation falls
      moderately in most patients, whilst some drop to unacceptable levels. To monitor such changes
      endoscopic practices routinely use oximeters and capnography.

      TwinGuard is an all-in-one device which incorporates a bite block with the functionality of
      nasal cannula as well as monitoring expired air (carbon dioxide)
