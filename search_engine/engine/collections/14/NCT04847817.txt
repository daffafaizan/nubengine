
    
      In this study ,the participants will be enrolled sequentially in the COVID-19 care referral
      centers. The infected hospitalized patient will be identified by the physicians in hospital.
      After the patient has given his/her informed consent the permission will be sought from him
      to approach his household contacts. The household contacts will be screened, and included in
      the study.

      Patients hospitalized and positive for COVID-19 by PCR and their household contacts of all
      ages and gender (40% minimum of female), will be enrolled upon receipt of written informed
      consent.
