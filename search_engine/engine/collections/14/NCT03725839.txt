
    
      A minimum of 40 OSA participants who currently use CPAP will be recruited for the trial.
      Participant will be in the trial for three weeks- first week will be a collection of Positive
      Airway Pressure baseline data on participant's usual interface. The participant will then use
      the trial interface in-home for 2 weeks. There is also an option of a six month extension if
      the participant wish to continue on the trial interface.
