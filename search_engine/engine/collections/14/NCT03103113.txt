
    
      Study Design (Brief Description)

        1. Inclusion criteria Patients had underwent infertility blood test or treatment in Lee's
           women hospital

        2. Exclusion criteria No

        3. Endpoint

      1. Values of blood test (PTT, IDM, ANA, Lupus, protein-s) 2. Embryo quality 3. Results of
      intravenous immunoglobulin (IVIG) treatment (pregnancy or not) 4. Alive birth rate

      4. Withdraw criteria and rescue medication No 5. Sample Size and Study Duration Sample Size:
      total 500 women who underwent IVF treatments and tests from Jan. 2007 to Dec. 2010.

      Study Duration: from date of IRB agreement to Dec. 2012 6. Please specify objectives of study
      when collecting extra specimen from participants No more need to collect extra specimen from
      participants in this study 7. Expected Endpoints of Treatment No 8. Others No
