
    
      Subjects:

      All the PHPT patients attending outdoor patient clinic of Endocrinology and General Surgery
      department of Nehru Hospital, PGIMER, Chandigarh will be enrolled in this study.

      Sample collection:

      A parathyroid tissue sample will be taken from the patients undergoing for parathyroidectomy.
      Samples will be collected immediately after surgery, snap frozen and stored at -80oC until
      use. In addition 10 ml of blood will also be taken from these patients for estimating
      biochemical parameters.

      Controls:

      Normal parathyroid gland will be used as control and it will be obtained from patient
      undergoing thyroidectomy for multinodular goiter as an elective procedure at the institute.
