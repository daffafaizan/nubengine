
    
      The investigators hypothesize that Veterans in the integrated conditions will show greater
      reductions in substance abuse and PTSD symptom severity at the end of treatment and at 6 and
      9 month follow-ups. The investigators further hypothesize that offering Veterans Prolonged
      Exposure (PE) at the onset of treatment in the integrated condition will leader to greater
      retention and satisfaction than in the sequential treatment design.
