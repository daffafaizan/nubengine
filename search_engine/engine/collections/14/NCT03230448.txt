
    
      People hospitalized after a suicide attempt will answer to two questionnaires about the
      suicide intentionality and the acute alcohol consumption.

      There is 2 arms:

      people doing suicide attempt and with positive level of alcohol (up to 0.1g.L) people doing
      suicide attempt and with negative level of alcohol

      110 patients will be included in this study so 55 in each arm.
