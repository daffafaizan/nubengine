
    
      A minimum of 20 OSA participants who currently use a nasal pillows mask will be recruited for
      the trial. Participant will be on the trial for three weeks - first week will be a collection
      of Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) baseline data on the participant's usual mask. The
      participant will then use the trial mask in-home for 2 weeks. There is also an option of a
      two month extension if the participant wish to continue on the trial mask.
