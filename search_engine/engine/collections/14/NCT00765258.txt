
    
      The purpose of the study is to report the use of CT fluoroscopy guidance during the
      performance of percutaneous sacroplasty. In addition, clinical results will be reported with
      the Roland Morris Disability Questionnaire(RMDQ)pre and post procedure, demonstrating the
      utility of the RMDQ in assessing improvement in patients pain, mobility and ability to
      perform activities of daily living.
