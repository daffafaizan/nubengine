
    
      The investigators enrolled participants in international symposium of Korean association for
      laboratory science (laboratory animal researchers) and pet animal exhibition (pet owner and
      pet-related industry workers) after informed consents. Subjects were asked to response to
      questionnaires regarding their age, gender, comorbid allergic disease, family history of
      allergy, pet or laboratory animal, exposure time, allergic symptoms during exposure to
      animals. Subjects underwent skin prick test with animal allergens and other inhalant
      allergens.
