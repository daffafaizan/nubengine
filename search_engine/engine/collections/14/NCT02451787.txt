      supplementation on biomarkers of these 'hidden' changes in apparently healthy young adults.
      The investigators will also explore the effect of improvement in vitamin D status in young
      apparently healthy adults in regard to muscular strength and response time, glucose control,
      a hormone that is linked to vitamin D (parathyroid hormone (PTH) ) and mood.
