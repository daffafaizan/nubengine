
    
      The aim of the study is to study the efficacy of a proprioceptive stimulation device on
      prevention of calf muscle atrophy after a total knee replacement.

      The investigators developped a safe vibration and pressure stimulation device for le lower
      limb. The investigators want to carry out a RCT in order to demonstrate the efficacy of the
      stimulation device, compared to a not stimulated controle group.
