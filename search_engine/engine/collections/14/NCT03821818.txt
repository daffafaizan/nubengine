
    
      Subjects will first be screened for cardiovascular pathologies and then tested for hidden
      food allergies. After the results subjects will eliminate all food that showed leukocyte
      reactions for 90-days. Subjects will then also be invited to use the Willis Exercise protocol
      (Aerobic-surge exercise > 75% max heart rate, for 2-minutes, 5/day. Body composition testing
      and Body Mass Index calculation will be accomplished at enrollment and after completion of
      the 90-day study.
