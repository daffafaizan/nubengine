
    
      Subjects that are undergoing video EEG monitoring in their home for their epilepsy at Seer
      Medical will be consented to participate in this study for a minimum of two days and/or the
      duration of their monitoring period for their clinical care. Subjects will be asked to wear
      up to 4 different commercially available seizure detection devices and complete surveys.

      When the subjects clinical EEG monitoring is completed data scientists will analyze the data
      to identify patterns.
