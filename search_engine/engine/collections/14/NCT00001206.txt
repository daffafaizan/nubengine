
    
      This protocol allows the continuing prospective longitudinal study of ADHD probands for whom
      we have neurobiological measures, particularly quantitative brain MRI. The follow-up consists
      of structured psychiatric interviews, and neuropsychological tests. Repeat MRI scans are
      obtained through protocol 89-M-0006.

      The second purpose of this protocol is a study of the genetics of
      Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD). Subjects who have ADHD, their family
      members, and controls are studied under this protocol.
