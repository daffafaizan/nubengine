
    
      Two boys with clinical signs of velocardiofacial syndrome, at ages of 4.7 and 6,7 years old
      are the subjects of this study.Audiological evaluation, including pure tone audiometry,
      tympanometry, acoustical reflex, and Computerized Tomography of temporal bones, and analyses
      of DNA sample with markers of 22q11 region were performed.
