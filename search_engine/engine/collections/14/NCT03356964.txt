
    
      The study will be performed using the stimulation medication Gonal-F which is a recombinant
      FSH-preparation. Due to application as pen-device, it allows reduction of the stimulation
      dosage in steps of 12.5IU.

      It is important to evaluate the dynamics of progesterone-rise after final oocyte maturation,
      as this dynamic might also have an influence on the receptivity of the endometrium.
