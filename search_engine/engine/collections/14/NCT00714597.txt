
    
      Randomization had to take place just prior to the first study drug injection.

      The total duration of observation per participant was 35-42 days from randomization broken
      down as follows:

        -  10 to 14-day double-blind treatment period;

        -  25 to 32-day follow-up period.

      Mandatory bilateral compression ultrasound [CUS] had to be performed between 10 to 15 days
      after randomization.
