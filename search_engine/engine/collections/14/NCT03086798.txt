
    
      Patients who undergo oxo-maxillofacial surgery need naso-tracheal intubation to present good
      surgical view-field. but, sometimes, the naso-tracheal tube is unable to advance from
      nasopharynx, oropharynx, into trachea without assistance. Using techniques of either Magill
      forceps or cuff inflation is advantageous is not determined.
