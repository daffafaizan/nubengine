
    
      The study will recruit women of 40-65 years old from an established consumer panel in China,
      South Korea, and Taiwan.

      Women who meet the inclusion criteria for Part I will be recruited to sign the informed
      consent form (ICF) and participate in Part I of the survey.

      Based on the information collected in Part I of the survey, peri- and post-menopausal women
      who meet the inclusion criteria for Part II will be directed to Part II of the survey. The
      survey will be terminated for women who do not meet the inclusion criteria for Part II.
