
    
      A prospective randomized study to assess the use of Bulkamid for anal incontinence using
      three different transanal injection techniques .

      Patients with anal incontinence severity Wexner score >7. Standardised injection treatment at
      baseline. Clinical status and subjective symptom evaluation (Wexner score and FIQL) at
      baseline, 2 months, 6 months and 1 year.
