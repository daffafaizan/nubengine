
    
      This study consists of three parts, Part A, Part B and Part C. Part A is a dose escalation.
      Part B was terminated early. The (one) subject who received Macugen is not discussed in this
      website. Part C had subjects receive one of two doses of VEGF Trap (0.15 mg or 4.0 mg).

      This is the first study in which human subjects received intravitreal injections of VEGF Trap
      in a study eye.
