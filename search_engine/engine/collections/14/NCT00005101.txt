
    
      PROTOCOL OUTLINE: Patients undergo physical examination and complete history with emphasis on
      risk factors for osteoporosis and screening for occult secondary causes of osteoporosis
      through blood serum and 24 hour urine collection.

      Patients with more than 6 months since prior bone mineral density measurements or who did not
      receive measurements at participating center, undergo dual energy x-ray absorptiometry over
      30 minutes for bone mineral density measurements.
