
    
      In this study, we will enroll 81 subjects who will be randomized to either the nicotine
      lozenge or tobacco-free snuff to reduce their ST use over 8 weeks with follow-up at 12 weeks.
      Forty subjects will be recruited at Mayo Clinic in Rochester, MN, and 40 subjects will be
      recruited at the Oregon Research Institute in Eugene, Oregon.
