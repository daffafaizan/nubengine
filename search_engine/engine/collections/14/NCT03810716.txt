
    
      Sample Size: Sample size of 176 participants (interventional group=88, control group=88), it
      is possible to detect minimum differences of 5% to 20%.

      Statistical analysis plan: The data collected will be entered into a database in Microsoft
      Excel, to be analyzed by the Epi Info statistical program. The qualitative variables will be
      analyzed by frequencies and percentages, the quantitative variables by relative risk.
