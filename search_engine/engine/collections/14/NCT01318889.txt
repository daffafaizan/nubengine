
    
      Patients will be given a bottle of 5% Dexpanthenol mouthwash and they are asked to rinse 10
      ml of it three times a day. At the onset of mucositis and then for 4 subsequent weeks they
      will be examined to evaluate the condition of their oral mucosa.By the advent of mucositis
      stage 4 ,at any time during the 4 weeks of study,the patient would be out.if any patient
      shows allergic reaction to dexpanthenol,he/she would be out of study too.
