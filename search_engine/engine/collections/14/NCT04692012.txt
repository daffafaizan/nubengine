
    
      This study is to investigate the effect of fresh myopic corneal lenticule implantation as
      allogenic implant(-1.50D) that will be taken from myopic patients to implant on pseudophakic
      patients with residual hypermetropic refraction(+1.0D) using VisuMax Femtosecond laser -
      Smile module surgery with primary objective to assess (increase) visual acuity .
