
    
      Inhalation of nebulized drug is an alternative route of administration that is relatively
      easy to set up, does not require venpuncture, but is associated with high bioavailability of
      the administered drug. Information on the pharmacokinetics of dexmedetomidine in the
      pediatric population is very limited, especially in children. To the best of investigators
      knowledge, there are no published reports on the pharmacokinetics of nebulized
      dexmedetomidine in children.
