
    
      STUDY DESIGN

      Prospective randomized double blind study including patients diagnosed with severe
      symptomatic aortic stenosis and anemia undergoing aortic valve replacement. The patients will
      be identified in the cardiac surgery / aortic stenosis outpatient clinic or in the
      hospitalization department of the Institut Universitaire de Cardiologie et de Pneumologie de
      Quebec.

      SAMPLE SIZE

      100 patients (50 patients per group).
