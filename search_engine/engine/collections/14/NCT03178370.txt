
    
      Patients were evaluated by two methods in each of five areas: inflammatory, autonomic,
      enteric, electrophysiologic, and hormonal.

      In part one, patients had similar GI Symptoms regardless of baseline gastric emptying or
      diabetic/non-diabetic status and all patients demonstrated abnormalities in each of the 5
      areas studied.

      In part two, patients showed early and late effects of electrical stimulation with changes
      noted in multiple areas, categorized by improvement status.
