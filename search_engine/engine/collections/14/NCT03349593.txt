
    
      Preoperative transthoracic echocardiography (TTE) is conducted in 100 individuals.
      Biventricular function (i.e. systolic and diastolic properties), left ventricular filling
      pressures and level of venous return are assessed by TTE.

      Moreover Troponine T and Nt-proBNP, ECG are collected.
