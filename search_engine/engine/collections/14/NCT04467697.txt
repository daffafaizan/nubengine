
    
      This is the six-month treatment extension of Study MRS-TU-2019, which like Study MRS-TU-2019
      (NCT03198728), is an open-label study. The MRS-TU-2019 ABPM Extension Study (MRS-TU-2019EXT;
      NCT04467697), will extend the participation for up to 170 MRS-TU-2019 subjects who chose to
      enroll into the MRS-TU-2019EXT, for a target of 135 evaluable subjects reaching the 4-month
      ABPM assessment
