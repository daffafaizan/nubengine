
    
      The objectives of this study are to evaluate the safety and efficacy of the study device.
      This study will document any device-related, surgical or post-operative complication and
      adverse radiographic observations. Improvement in pain, function, and health economic data
      will be compared with improvements documented with other joint systems. Outcome measures will
      be quantified through statistical analysis of study device , HOOS questionnaire, radiographic
      assessments, Harris Hip Score, and adverse event reports.
