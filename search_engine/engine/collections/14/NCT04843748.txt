
    
      Device: Hanita Lenses Trifocal IOL, SeeLens AFR (Intensity IOL). Indication studied: The
      Hanita Lenses Trifocal IOL is intended for implantation in the capsular bag in the posterior
      chamber in order to replace the crystalline lens of the eye to attain visual correction of
      aphakia in adult patients in whom a cataractous lens has been removed, who desire improved
      uncorrected vision, useful near and intermediate visual functions, and reduced spectacle
      dependence.

      Study description: This study is a prospective, single-arm single-center, open-label study.
