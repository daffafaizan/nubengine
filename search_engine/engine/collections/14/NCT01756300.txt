
    
      The RENABLATE feasibility study CS156 (EC12-02) is a prospective, multi-center,
      non-randomized feasibility study to evaluate the safety and effectiveness of renal artery
      sympathetic denervation using the investigational Celsius® ThermoCool® Renal Denervation (RD)
      Multi-electrode Ablation Catheter and integrated ablation system to treat resistant
      hypertension.
