
    
      This is a simple controlled clinical trial. In which 66 eyes of 33 patients were studied.
      Fifteen patients were assigned to the placebo group and 18 to the Selenium group. We
      randomized into two groups the patients with mild clinical activity according to CAS score.
      Group A took placebo pills twice a day which consisted in 100µg of starch, and Group B took a
      pill of Selenium 100 µg twice a day. All the subjects tool the pills during six months.
      Patients of both groups where examined and evaluated with CAS score before and after the
      first, third and sixth month of treatment.
