
    
      Objective: Pilot evaluation to which extend the implementation of a newly developed combined
      diagnostic and operation room, the multifunctional image guided therapy suite (MIGTS) may
      influence the emergency treatment of multiply injured patients.

      Summary Background: Controlled data on the impact of modern technology integration in
      multiple trauma management and involved care providers are lacking.

      Methods: Quasi-randomized clinical intervention study comparing the treatment of multiple
      trauma patients (injury severity score, ISS >16) in the MIGTS versus controls.
