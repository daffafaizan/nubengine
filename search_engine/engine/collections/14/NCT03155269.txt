
    
      This will be a double blind, single-center, randomized-controlled trial testing the effect of
      fortified beverage on bone turnover markers as compared to placebo control in 25-45 years old
      premenopausal women. The study will consist of two groups: Group 1 (Test) - Protein rich
      beverage powder fortified with MMN and Group 2 (control) - Low protein non-fortified
      iso-caloric beverage powder.
