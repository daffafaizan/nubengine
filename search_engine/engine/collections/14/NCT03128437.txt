
    
      This is a 2-arm randomized control trial to test the effect of an anxiety sensitivity
      intervention on health behaviors among 18-35 year-olds. Participants will be 18-35 year-olds
      with high anxiety sensitivity randomized to 1)Exercise Exposure Intervention or 2)Assessment
      Only Control Condition. Participants in both groups will be followed for 8 weeks.
