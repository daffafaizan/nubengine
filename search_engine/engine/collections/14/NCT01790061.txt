
    
      The present clinical trial aims to re-establish a gut functionality state of intestinal
      microbiota through FMT as a therapy for UC. Investigators established a standard bacteria
      isolation from donated fresh stool in lab. Then the bacteria is transplanted to mid-gut
      through regular gastroscope. Patients in this study will assigned to receive FMT(s) or
      traditional treatments according to associated guidelines and follow-up for long term. All
      data were recorded in China Microbiota Transplantation System.
