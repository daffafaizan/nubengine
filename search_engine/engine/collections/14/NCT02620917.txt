
    
      The aim of the study is to collect clinical and metabolic characteristics of patient treated
      with CSII in Italy.

      Data will be collected through a questionnaire sent by e-mail to CSII experienced Diabetes
      Centers ( both adult and pediatric patients) . The questionnaire assessed: (1) number of
      CSII-treated patients; (2) patients and pump characteristics; (3) structure and organization
      of Diabetes Centers providing CSII therapy; (4) metabolic control.
