
    
      Up to 400 children, ages 2 months through 14 years

        1. To assess measures of injection performance, including estimates of injection
           completeness by collecting residual surface moisture with filter paper that will be
           circled with indelible ink; the volume will be compared to a reference set of moisture
           recordings.

        2. To document completeness of injections; completeness is defined as <10% of 0.1 ml volume
           remaining on the skin surface immediately after injection. Measurement of the bleb size
           following injection will recorded in millimeters.

        3. To ensure device is well received by subjects. Subject's response to the injection will
           be noted by the subject and/or family members after the procedure for up to 48 hours.
