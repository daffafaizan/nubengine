
    
      Chart review conducted on all patients having had Prolift surgery since 2005 by Dr. Lary
      Sirls, Dr. Predeep Nagaraju and Dr. Kenneth Peters at William Beaumont Hospital.
      Preoperative, intraoperative and post-operative data will be collected from the hospital and
      physician medical records. A questionnaire will be mailed to patients whose charts have been
      reviewed to assess urinary and bowel function, satisfaction with treatment and changes in
      sexual function. Completion of the questionnaire is voluntary.
