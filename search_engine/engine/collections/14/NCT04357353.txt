
    
      Eligibility: Men with erectile dysfunction. Ages 50-80. International Index of Erectile
      Function score of mild (17-21/25) or moderate (8-16/25).

      Intervention and Evaluation:Men who consent to join the trial will be randomized to receive
      either a single injection PRP or saline to the penis. All patients will have blood drawn in
      clinic the day of injection. Those randomized to PRP arm will have their own blood prepared
      and separated into the PRP. This solution of their own blood will be injected back into the
      penis.

      Follow Up: Men will be evaluated at 1 , 3, and 6 months after the injections and then asked
      to complete the IIEF by phone or by electronic means
