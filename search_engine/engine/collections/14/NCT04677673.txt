
    
      DRAGON SENILE research, Neoadjuvant Chemotherapy plus Surgery versus surgery first for
      elderly Gastric Cancer patients, is an investigator-initiated; phase II/III, open-label,
      randomized controlled study. The main purpose of this study is to compare the feasibility and
      efficacy of neoadjuvant chemotherapy (modified SOX) for elderly patients with locally
      advanced gastric cancer.
