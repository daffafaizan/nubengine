
    
      The Arrhythmia Management System records rhythm, heart rate, subject-reported symptoms and
      multiple parameters that include respiration rate, activity and body posture. These biometric
      data could provide clinicians better insight into the context of the detected arrhythmias,
      subject-reported symptoms and wellness status. Hence, the purpose of the study to determine
      the associations among biometric data, arrhythmias, symptoms and subject wellness.
