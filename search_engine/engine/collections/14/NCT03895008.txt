
    
      The aim of this study is to compare the stage-for-stage prognosis of patients with cardia and
      non-cardia gastric cancer after operation.In this study, patients with cardiac and
      non-cardiac gastric cancer who undergo radical resection from January 2019 to December 2024
      will be included. We will follow up the patient's progression, recurrence time and survival
      time.
