
    
      Patients underwent minimally invasive esophagectomy in Zhongshan Hospital of Fudan University
      will be enrolled and be assigned to wide or narrow gastric conduit group randomly.
      Intra-operative blood supply and vascular SaO2 will be observed during the operation, and the
      rate of anastomotic leakage, together with its clinical details will be recorded in the two
      groups.
