
    
      In this cross-sectional observational study, we include HIV-infected children (6-12 years) on
      cART for ≥ 6 months, with viral loads ≤ 1000 copies/ml in Kilimanjaro Region, Tanzania.
      Psychopathology and competence will be assessed using the Child Behaviour Checklist.
      Cognitive performance will be assessed using the Raven's Coloured Progressive Matrices and
      the digit span test. Non-adherence is defined as any reported missed doses over the previous
      3 days or <100% adherence since the last clinical visit. Analysis of covariance and logistic
      regression models will be used to assess differences between groups. .
