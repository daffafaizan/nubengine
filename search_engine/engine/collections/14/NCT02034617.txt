
    
      The Design is a randomised controlled study in two groups; intervention group versus standard
      care. Parents who meet the inclusion criteria are given oral and written information about
      the study aim and procedure and are asked for participation. If the parents consent they will
      be randomised to one of two groups. Follow-ups will be conducted at 1 month, 4 months, 10-12
      months corrected age and when the child is 4 years.
