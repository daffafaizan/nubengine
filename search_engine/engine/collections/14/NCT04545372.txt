
    
      This research aims to evaluate the effect of aerobic exercises on the immune system
      anti-inflammatory (IL-10) and pro inflammatory (IL-17) cytokines and its consequences on the
      functional status in the Multiple Sclerosis patients.

      Aerobic exercises are a term to describe planned, structured and repetitive physical activity
      undertaken over a prolonged period to maintain or improve physical fitness and functional
      capacity .
