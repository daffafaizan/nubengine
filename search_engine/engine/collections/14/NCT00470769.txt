
    
      To validate color Doppler ultrasonography of renal arteries for the assessment of renal blood
      flow (RBF), we comparing left and right RBF estimates and their sum (total RBF) by
      echo-Doppler with data obtained by 99mTc-DTPA scintigraphy in 50 patients with hypertension
      and/or moderate renal failure, compared with 30 normal control subjects.
