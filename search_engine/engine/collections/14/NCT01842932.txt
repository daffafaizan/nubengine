
    
      Cimetropium bromide is premedication of colonoscopy. However, it is contraindication for some
      diseases; benign prostatic hyperplasia(BPH) or other obstructive uropathy, intestinal
      obstruction, pyloric stenosis, glaucoma, myasthenia gravis, megacolon, paralytic ileus. In
      this cases, Flospan (phloroglucin) is effective alternative drug. So our research was to
      compare the effects of these two drugs.
