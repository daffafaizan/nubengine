
    
      Retrospective analysis of a prospective database to create a Risk Score. Post-operative
      neurosurgery complications recording (Hyponatremia < 135mmol/l,Insulin administration,
      Nausea-Vomiting, Glasgow Coma Score, Inhalation, Post-operative intra-cranial bleeding
      requiring neurosurgery, Intra-cranial hypertension, Use of osmotherapy, Status epilepticus,
      Brain death, Death).

      Collection of datas as Duration of mechanical ventilation, Length of ICU stay, Length of
      hospital stay.

      Validation of this score on an independent prospective cohort.
