
    
      This is an observational, multicentric, case-control and one-single-visit study of patients
      with severe haemophilia A and inhibitors against FVIII in therapy with FEIBA®.

      Two subject groups will be included: Group 1 (cases) will be composed of patients with severe
      haemophilia A and inhibitors in prophylaxis with FEIBA®; Group 2 (controls) will be composed
      of patients with severe haemophilia A and inhibitors on-demand treatment with FEIBA®.
