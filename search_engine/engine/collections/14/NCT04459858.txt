
    
      A tear of the rotator cuff is one of the most common disorders of the shoulder. It is assumed
      that a large Critical Shoulder Angle (CSA) is associated with the occurrence of rotator cuff
      tears. This retrospective study is to investigate the influence of the Greater Tuberosity
      Angle (GTA) in addition to CSA on patient outcome in patients with rotator cuff tear.
