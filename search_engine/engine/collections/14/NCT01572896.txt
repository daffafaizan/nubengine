
    
      In summary, no rigorous Internet self-management intervention has been undertaken to improve
      HRQL, symptoms, treatment adherence, knowledge, and self-efficacy in youth with JIA. The
      proposed "Managing JIA Online" Trial will rigorously examine the effects of this Internet
      intervention on HRQL and other health outcomes thereby filling a significant gap in our
      knowledge related to promotion of self-management care for adolescents with JIA.
