
    
      This is a prospective, randomized, open-label, monocentric, prospective study of two parallel
      groups of patients in whom a chest tube is to be inserted:

        -  Group 1: Insertion of the drain according to the usual management protocol.

        -  Group 2: Insertion of the drain according to the usual management protocol supplemented
           by the use of a hypnosis software application (HYPNO-VR).
