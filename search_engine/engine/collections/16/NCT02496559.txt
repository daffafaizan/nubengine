
    
      Design: A randomized controlled observational study including children 0-6 year with fever
      and/or respiratory symptoms at 4 different Out-of-hour Services and at 1 Emergency Children
      Department Clinic at a hospital (open clinic). The data consist of clinical data and
      anamnestic information from a nurse at OOH-service collected before the consultation, the
      doctor's journal and a questionnaire to parents before the consultation and 1 week after the
      consultation.
