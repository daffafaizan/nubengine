
    
      In patients who undergo pterygium surgery, eyes will be consecutively assigned to receive
      either DEXTENZA or topical prednisolone acetate 1%.15 eyes will receive a Dextenza insertion
      into both the upper and lower puncta at the time of surgery, followed by at postoperative
      month 1 visit institution of topical prednisolone acetate 1% bid x 2 weeks then qd x 2 weeks
      then discontinued. 15 eyes will receive starting on postoperative day 1 topical prednisolone
      acetate 1% q2 hours x 2 weeks then qid for 2 weeks then bid for 2 weeks then qd for 2 weeks
      then discontinued.
