
    
      Total knee arthroplasty is a well documented and efficient treatment method for degenerative
      knee arthritis. Nevertheless it is reported that 15-20% of patients are not satisfied with
      the result. Some of the complaints are anterior knee pain, mid flexion instability and range
      of motion. Attune Fixed Bearing Posterior Stabilized knee have been developed to address the
      common complaints after knee arthroplasty surgery . The investigators like to use the Attune
      Fixed Bearing Posterior Stabilized knee with patella resurfacing on 65 of Our departments
      eligible patients to evaluate the functional results. Our primary outcome measure is change
      in Knee Osteoarthritis Outcome Score.
