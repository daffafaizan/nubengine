
    
      Patients undergoing laparoscopic cholecystectomy were separated into 3 groups. Group 1:
      Received TAP block Group 2: Received OSTAP Group 3: No Block Standard General Anesthesia was
      induced and maintained in all patients. At the end of the operation patient controlled
      analgesia with morphine was applied to all patients.

      Pain scores were evaluated at 0,2,4,6,12,24 hours postoperatively. Total morphine consumption
      and pain scores were compared.
