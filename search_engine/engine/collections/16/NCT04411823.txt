
    
      Subjects will undergo an upper endoscopy with EndoFLIP before sleeve gastrectomy to measure
      parameters from endoscopy and EndoFLIP device to develop a prediction model for
      post-operative gastroesophageal reflux disease. Subjects will undergo a repeat upper
      endoscopy with EndoFLIP at 6 months after surgery and complete questionnaires during
      follow-ups up to 5 years.
