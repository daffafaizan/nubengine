
    
      Objective: evaluate the efficiency of a nasal hypertonic spray Puressentiel in subjects with
      allergic rhinitis and nasal obstruction.

      Inclusion criteria: subjects with allergic obstructive rhinitis without treatment or
      remaining symptomatic despite treatment.

      Duration of exposure: 2 nasal sprays per day (morning and evening) during 30 months.

      Primary criteria of efficacy: Rhinitis questionnaire.

      Secondary criteria: Inspiratory nasal peak flow
