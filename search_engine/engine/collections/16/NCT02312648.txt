
    
      The hypotheses this study is that patients if conduct the program of early mobilization
      during hospitalization achieve better performance in the walk test (distance) at hospital
      discharge and 60 days after the surgical procedure compared to patients who only carry out
      the program of respiratory exercises. Intervention and control groups will receive breathing
      exercises up to 24 hours postoperatively, and the intervention group will also receive early
      mobilization exercises.
