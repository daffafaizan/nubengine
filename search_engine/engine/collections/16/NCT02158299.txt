
    
      A prospective,randomized analysis of breast-conserving surgery or mastectomy plus axillary
      lymphadenectomy for those patients with sentinel node positive using Microfibrillar Collagen
      Hemostat Flour 、 OK-432 or using nothing in equal probability.Up to 12 months,a total of 180
      will be recruited in plan.During the operation and after the axillary
      lymphadenectomy，according the arms，Investigators put Microfibrillar Collagen Hemostat
      Flour（avitene）、 OK-432（Sapylin） or nothing into patients axillary wounds。And Investigators
      will the statistical significance of these three arms that the postoperative drainage
      magnitude and duration.Also the complications associated with axillary lymphadenectomy will
      be studied.
