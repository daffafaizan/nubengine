
    
      All patients undergoing pancreaticoduodenectomy in Helsinki University Hospital are recruited
      in the study. Patients meeting the inclusion criteria and giving consent to participate the
      study will be randomised to whether epidural analgesia group or wound catheter analgesia
      group. Both groups get additional analgesia with intravenous PCA-oxycodone (patient
      controlled analgesia).
