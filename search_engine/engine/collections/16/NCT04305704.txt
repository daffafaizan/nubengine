
    
      Title: Observational Registry With p64 MW HPC in Unruptured Anterior Circulation Aneurysms
      Under prasugrel

      Purpose: To assess safety and effectiveness of p64 MW HPC or p48 MW HPC under prasugrel in
      consecutive patients with unruptured anterior circulation aneurysms.

      Follow-up intervals: Independent follow-ups (after 3 to 6 months and after 12 months and
      after 24 months) according to site specific standard.
