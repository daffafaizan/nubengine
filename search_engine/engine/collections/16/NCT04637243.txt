
    
      The study will be a retrospective chart review of patients who have been diagnosed with
      benign or malignant liver and bile duct disease under the practices of Rohan Jeyarajah, M.D.,
      Houssam Osman, M.D., F.A.C.S., Edward Cho, M.D., Sc.M at Methodist Health System Hospital in
      Richardson, T.X. We plan to conduct an analysis of patients meeting the inclusion criteria
      form 2005 to present (up to the day before this study receives IRB approval). Charts will
      also be reviewed by the PI. Sub-I, Fellows, Office Staff, Clinical Research Coordinator who
      are delegated to do so by the PI. Data will be obtained from a national database. Data will
      be analyzed primarily by the study conductors.
