
    
      Randomized multicenter controlled clinical trial comparing hydroposturotherapy in short
      thermal cure with standard hyperdiuresis in patients with residual stones, especially lower
      caliciels after urological treatment.

      5 inclusion centres

        -  Central Hospital, Nancy

        -  Nancy Urology Center

        -  Clinique de la Croix du Sud in Toulouse

        -  Edouard Herriot Hospital in Lyon

      The radiological images will be reviewed by the radiology department of the Central Hospital,
      Nancy.
