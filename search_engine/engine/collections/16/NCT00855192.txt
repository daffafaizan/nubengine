
    
      Pregnant women between 15-28 weeks, who speak english are invited to join an 8 week group to
      relieve anxiety and depression.

      We have collected sociodemographic data, as well as social support, life events.

      Outcome measures are a decrease in anxiety and depression-as measured by the Edinburgh
      Postnatal Depression Scale, the Cambridge Worry Scale, and the STAI-Trait Scale.
