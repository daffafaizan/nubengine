
    
      A total of 40 subjects diagnosed with T2DM taking once-daily evening BI, scheduled to undergo
      elective surgery under general anesthesia will be consented and randomized in a 1:1 ratio to
      received either 50% or 25% reduction of their regular evening BI dose on the evening before
      surgery. Blood glucose levels (BGL) will be recorded perioperatively according to
      institutional guidelines.
