
    
      The main purpose: To investigate if Lactobacillus paracasei GMNL-133(LP), Lactobacillus
      fermentum GM-090(LF), Lactobacillus paracasei GMNL-133 (LP) with Lactobacillus fermentum
      GM-090 (LF) combination products could be used for the adjuvant treatment of atopic
      dermatitis and asthma.

      The Secondary Objective: To investigate whether probiotics consumption improve the quality of
      life and allergic symptoms to different people with Lactobacillus paracasei GMNL-133 (LP),
      Lactobacillus fermentum GM-090 (LF) and Lactobacillus paracasei GMNL-133(LP) and
      Lactobacillus fermentum GM-090 (LF) combination products.
