
    
      The purpose of the study was to collect initial safety and efficacy information of use of the
      AlphaCore System by clinicians in an emergency setting as an adjunctive treatment to standard
      of care for the relief of acute bronchoconstriction. After consent was obtained and screening
      completed, subjects were stimulated two times, 30 minutes apart, for 90 seconds each.
      Subjects were assessed prior to and immediately post the first stimulation and at 15, 30, 60,
      and 90 minutes; follow was also conducted at day 7 and day 30.
