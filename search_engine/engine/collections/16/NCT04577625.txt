
    
      The probiotic Lactobacillus reuteri ATCC PTA 6475 has been shown to modulate testosterone
      levels in mice. It is not known if these effects are seen in humans and this trial is
      intended to study this.

      The aim of this study is to investigate if L. reuteri ATCC PTA 6475 will effect blood
      testosterone levels in in healthy male subjects 50-65 years of age.
