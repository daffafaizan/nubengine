
    
      The purpose of this study is to collect data that reports on the clinical outcomes of
      patients treated with the Spineology Rampart DUO device in a standard of care instrumented
      lateral lumbar interbody fusion procedure at one or two contiguous levels. Data is collected
      prospectively at specified time points per protocol and includes both objective and
      subjective measures. Patients will be followed to a maximum of two years.
