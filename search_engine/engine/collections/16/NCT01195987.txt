
    
      Patients will provide one blood sample after qualifying for the study, and signing consent.
      The patients will have a skin test TB, Candida, and Trichophyton placed and will read the
      results themselves and mail back the card with the measurements to be recorded. Labs will
      test for ESR, CBC, CRP, antiCCP, Cryoglobulins, RF, CH50, C3, C4, Flow cytometry, and would
      have already been tested for HIV and Hepatitis C.
