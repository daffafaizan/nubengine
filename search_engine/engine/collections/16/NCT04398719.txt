
    
      This study aims to examine the effect of cannabidiol (CBD), pre-treatment, as Epidiolex, on
      brain microglial activation in healthy human subjects using [11C]PBR28 PET imaging.
      Secondarily, this study aims to examine the effect of cannabidiol (CBD) pre-treatment on
      central pain-sensitization in healthy human subjects. Lastly, this research study will also
      examine the relationship between brain microglial activation and central pain sensitization
      with CBD pre-treatment.
