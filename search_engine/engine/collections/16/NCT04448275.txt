
    
      Haemophilic patients with Femoral Neuropathy will be randomly divided in two equal groups
      control group which will receive conventional selected exercise program and study group will
      receive the same exercise training program in addition to Neurodynamics Nerve flossing three
      times per week for three successive months.

      The Evaluation will done pre and post interventions included:

      femoral nerve motor conduction velocity, muscle test For Iliopsoas and Quadriceps muscles and
      Pain visual analogue scale.
