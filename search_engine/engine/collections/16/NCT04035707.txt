
    
      The greatest risk of hypersensitivity occurs during anesthesia for female patients and it is
      connected with the induction phase using skeletal muscle relaxants . Epidemiological data
      presents that the frequency of hypersensitivity reactions caused by rocuronium have been
      increased. Due to the multiple advantages reports of triggering allergic or hypersensitivity
      reactions caused by rocuronium create doubts about the safety of application .
