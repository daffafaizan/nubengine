
    
      The subjects consisted of 22 male and 26 female subjects , ages between 20 to 30 years (mean
      age, 23.3 years). They were screened for excessive nasal congestion, drug and food taken, and
      neurological disorders prior to participation in the experiment. The experiments were
      approved by the Ethical Committee for Research Involving Human Research Subject, Naresuan
      University. All subjects read and signed an informed consent before participation.
