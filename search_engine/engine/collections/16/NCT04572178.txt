
    
      We would like to include up to 15 pregnant women. Inclusion criteria are: (i) current
      pregnancy, between pregnancy week 15 and 23 and, (ii) current smoking of at least one
      cigarette per day. After an initial survey, the participating women are scheduled twice a
      week for counseling and evaluation of their smoking status.They will receive cash payment of
      25 Euro if her carbon monoxide reading does not exceed the value "3", on two consecutive
      dates per week. A second survey will be conduced in pregnancy week 36 and a third survey,
      eight weeks postpartum.
