
    
      This is a prospective, multi center, phase-IV with blinded analysis and central reading of
      polysomnography trial.

      The study is performed in patients with a standard CRT-D or Implantable Cardioverter
      Defibrillator (ICD) devices indication. A total of 160 subjects will be recruited in France.
      The study duration is 03 months for each subject.
