
    
      The intervention will be provided during a 3-month period with follow-up through 6 months
      after randomization. Body mass index (BMI) and waist circumference measurements will be
      collected at baseline and at 6, 12, 18, and 24 weeks. Participants will also complete
      measures assessing eating behaviors and physical activity at baseline and at 12 and 24 weeks.
