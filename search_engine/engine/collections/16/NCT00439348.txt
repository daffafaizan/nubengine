
    
      Researchers will identify a cohort of gynecologic patients presenting for annual examinations
      and offer participation in the study. Physician researchers will randomize patients to 2
      types of physician directions about OTC medications, then follow compliance over the course
      of the next 7 months. A total of 123 participants in each arm will undergo recruitment, until
      a total of 246 participants are recruited.

      Inclusion criteria include: female, non-pregnant patients presenting for annual gynecologic
      examinations between the ages of 19-50.

      Exclusion criteria include: outside of study age range, patient taking thyroid medication,
      history of kidney stones, history of hypercalcemia, non-English speaking.
