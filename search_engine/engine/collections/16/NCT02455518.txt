
    
      To perform a randomized, double blind 4-arm clinical trial of the comparative efficacy of 4
      oral analgesics in the initial management of acute musculoskeletal extremity pain presenting
      to the ED. The 4 groups are as follows:

        1. Oxycodone/acetaminophen (5/325)

        2. Hydrocodone/acetaminophen (5/325)

        3. Codeine/acetaminophen (30/300)

        4. Ibuprofen/acetaminophen (400/1000)
