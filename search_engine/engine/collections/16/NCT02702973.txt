
    
      Patients with melanomas of the skin whose tumors have been resected completely were
      collected. The detailed ocular examinations including intraocular pressure, slit lamp
      microscope, indirect ophthalmoscope and color fundus photography were done before the
      induction therapy, 2 weeks after the induction therapy, after the whole course of induction
      therapy and each month in the maintenance treatment period. Fundus examinations were carried
      out by experienced ocular fundus doctors and the retinopathy characteristics were recorded.
