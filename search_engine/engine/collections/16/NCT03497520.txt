
    
      -  Design: Prospective study

        -  Setting: hospital rehabilitation department

        -  Intervention: Participants are educated asymmetric spinal stabilization exercise along
           scoliosis curve patterns, including the location of apex and the direction of convexity.
           After performing asymmetric spinal stabilization exercise, participants are compared the
           main outcome measures.

        -  Main outcome measures: Cobb angle, Apical vertebral rotation in Adam's test, surface
           EMG, Scoliosis Research Society-22(SRS-22) quastionnaire
