
    
      During Visit 1, blood samples will be collected to assess iron status. During Visit 2, after
      an overnight fast, participant will be given a standardized meal (rice and potatoes) with a
      portion of cooked beef (200 g). Blood samples will be collected right before and 2 hour after
      the meal for NTBI analysis.

      NTBI concentration before and after meal consumption will be compared.
