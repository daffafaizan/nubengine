
    
      Twenty nine patient with IgA nephropathy with proteinuria (urine protein to creatinine ratio
      > 0.3 mg/mg) were included in the study. All patients had received losartan treatment for 24
      weeks. Changes in blood pressure, proteinuria, renal function, and biochemical parameters
      were prospectively evaluated before and at 4 weeks and 24 weeks.
