
    
      A patient will receive study drug for about 12 weeks. Throughout the study, the study doctor,
      on best medical judgment, may gradually increase or decrease the dose of the study
      medication. The adjustments will dependent on the subject's response and whether the subject
      has side effects. Once the subject has completed treatment under this study, the subject may
      resume standard treatment for his/her obsessive compulsive disorder by their regular doctor.
