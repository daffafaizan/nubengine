
    
      To investigate whether intensive metabolic intervention of PCOS women before pregnancy can
      improve pregnancy outcome.Besides, the investigators aim to investigate the best therapy
      strategy of metabolic intervention before pregnancy.The investigators plan to recruit PCOS
      women with IGR / DM not effective of metformin treatment at childbearing age. By using
      acarbose, GLP-1 analogue, berberin et al. the investigators will intervent the participants'
      metabolic statues for 3 months before pregnancy and to compare outcome in each group.
