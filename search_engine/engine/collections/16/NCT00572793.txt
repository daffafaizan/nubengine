
    
      After obtaining informed consent maternal demographics, vital signs, height, weight, medical
      history, pregnancy dating, dipstick for urine protein, previous lab results, nuchal
      translucency results and uterine artery Doppler results (if done) will be obtained. Blood for
      PP13 will be obtained at 7-13 weeks and optionally at 21-23 weeks. Hospitalization and
      delivery information will be obtained via a chart review for evidence of preeclampsia.
