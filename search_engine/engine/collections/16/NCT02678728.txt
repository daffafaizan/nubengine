
    
      Patients will be divided into 2 groups; dexmedetomidine group and control group.
      Dexmedetomidine group will receive dexmedetomidine during and after thoracic artery surgery.
      And control group will receive the same amount of normal saline instead. Analyzing patients
      lung function and serum factors, which indicates the degree of systemic inflammatory
      responses and oxidative stress, The investigators will investigate the protective effect of
      dexmedetomidine on lung.
