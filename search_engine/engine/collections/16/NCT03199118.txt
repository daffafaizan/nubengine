
    
      The study population will be divided into 2 groups:

      Group 1:

      Patients suffering from Miller class I or II gingival recession will be treated with a
      coronally advanced flap in conjunction with a sub-epithelial connective tissue graft. The
      patients should be non-smoker, medically free and above 18 years

      Group 2:

      Patients suffering from Miller class I or II gingival recession will be treated with a
      coronally advanced flap in conjunction with a sub-epithelial connective tissue graft and
      platelet rich fibrin. the patients should meet the same inclusion criteria mentionned above.
