
    
      Samarium tracer infusion of 1 mCi/kg administered. SPECT images wil be used to determine the
      distribution of dose delivered to the tumor. This information will be used to determine
      target doses of external beam radiotherapy. The treatment infusion of Samarium, 30 mCi/kg,
      will be administered and dosimetry will confirm total dose delivered, and information will be
      used to finalize doses of external beam radiotherapy. Approximately 14 days after treatment
      infusion, autologous stem cells infusion is administered. Radiotherapy will be delivered
      according to the judgement of the treating radiation oncologist.
