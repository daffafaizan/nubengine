
    
      1. Establish a baseline of the condition of haemophilia in Ivory Coast (number of patients,
           demographics, reassessment of diagnosis, joint status...).(year 1)

        2. Develop tools to assess the intervention (eg transcultural validation of QoL
           questionnaires, develop booklets to record bleedings...). (year 1)

        3. Intervention phase that will focus on education of patients and their family, education
           of caregivers, training of the laboratory to the diagnosis of haemophilia and promotion
           of non substitutive strategies to improve haemophilia care in Ivory Coast (DDAVP,
           antifibrinolytics, physiotherapy, dental care,...).

        4. Follow-up phase during 2 years.

        5. Outcome analysis
