
    
      TAP block is being used a method of multimodal balanced analgesia reduces postoperative pain,
      opioid requirement after surgery and adverse effect of opioid such as sedation, nausea
      vomiting and ileus. Dexmedetomidine ,an alfa-2 adrenoreceptor agonist, is being used and
      adjuvant capable of prolonging duration of sensory and motor block on nerve blocks.
