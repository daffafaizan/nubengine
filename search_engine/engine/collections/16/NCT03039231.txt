
    
      The Freespira Breathing System (FBS) developed by Palo Alto Health Sciences, Inc, is a
      portable home device , and has been employed in breathing biofeedback in adults with panic
      disorder (PD). The FBS has received FDA clearance for the treatment of PD adults and is
      currently commercially available.

      This study is a prospective, single arm, un-blinded investigation of the Freespira Breathing
      System in the PTSD population.
