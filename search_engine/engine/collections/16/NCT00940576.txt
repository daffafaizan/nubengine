
    
      Background: Dietetic effects of mare's milk have been reported for a long time and can be
      based on bactericidal and immunological components of mare's milk.

      Objective: Dietetic effects of oral intake of mare's milk in adolescent patients with chronic
      inflammatory bowel diseases were investigated.

      Design: In a randomized, placebo-controlled, double-blind, cross-over intervention study,
      eight Crohn's disease patients and nine ulcerative colitis patients received daily 250 mL
      mare's milk or placebo drink.
