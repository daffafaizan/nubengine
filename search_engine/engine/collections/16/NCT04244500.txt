
    
      Study objectives

      Primary:

      - Measure the proportion of non-traumatic emergency department (ED) visits infected with
      influenza during the peak of the influenza season

      Secondary:

        -  Describe the demographic and medical profile of influenza-related and
           non-influenza-related visits

        -  Identify socio-demographic or other risk factors associated with ED visit outcome

        -  Describe the influenza positivity rate among ED visits for different causes

        -  Measure the proportion of influenza positive patients without classical ILI symptoms
