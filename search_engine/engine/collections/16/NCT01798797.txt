
    
      Patients with systolic heart failure (HF) implanted with Medtronic wireless implantable
      cardiac defibrillator (ICD) or cardiac resynchronization therapy device with defibrillation
      capabilities (CRT-D) devices will be enrolled in the study. The purpose of the study is to
      evaluate the usability of CareLink HFRS to manage a patient's heart failure.
