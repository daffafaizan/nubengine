
    
      This study aims to investigate the experiences of communication for patients with
      depression.Purposive sampling will be used to recruit participants in this study.Eligible
      participants consist of those who attend the outpatient department of psychiatry
      regularly,have emotional stability and clear consciousness,More than 20 years old,can be
      communicated in Mandarin or Taiwanese,and are willing to take the interviews.In-depth
      interviews will be conducted to collect qualitative data,and data will be categorized and
      analyzed according to Tesch(1990).Sampling will be stopped until data saturation.The findings
      may be referenced by the relevant agencies and medical institutions to care the patients with
      depression,as well as provide a basis for nursing care and education.
