
    
      Forty healthy volunteers will receive a combination of CV4 technique, CV4 sham, sacral
      technique (ST) and sacral sham technique (SST).

      Each sessions, split by 4 hours wash out, will start with a 10 min of no treatment (resting
      state), followed by two randomized interventions. Alpha EEG will continuously recorded during
      each intervention and 10 min after, for a total of approximately 50-60 min per session.
