
    
      Subjects are contacted on a daily basis after having a cesarean delivery to determine current
      pain, current pain unpleasantness, worst pain, worst pain unpleasantness, average pain, and
      average pain unpleaseantness. Preoperatively subjects complete a psycho-social/depressive
      screening questionnaire. Pain treatments/satisfaction assessments are captured for the first
      24 hours. Mother/baby outcomes are also captured. Physical activity and pain scores are also
      correlated with the subject's wearing of a Fitbit physical activity bracelet with eligible
      subjects for 60 days post-delivery.
