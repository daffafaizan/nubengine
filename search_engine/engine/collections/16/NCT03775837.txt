
    
      Previous studies have indicated that Panax Ginseng C.A. Mey Extract may have the ability to
      improve liver function. Therefore, the investigators conduct a randomized, double-blind,
      placebo-controlled study to investigate the effects of the Panax Ginseng C.A. Mey Extract on
      liver function in adults; the safety of the compound are also evaluate. The Investigators
      examine AST, ALT, GGT, and other metabolic parameters at baseline, as well as after 4 and 8
      weeks of intervention. Sixty adults were administered either 600 mg of Panax Ginseng C.A. Mey
