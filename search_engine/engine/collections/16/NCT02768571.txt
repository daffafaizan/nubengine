
    
      Randomized double-blinded, placebo-controlled multicenter study with two treatment groups

      Number of Patients: 80 patients (n = 40 per group) 5 study centers will participate in this
      study.

      Group 1(Cerebrolysin): cerebrolysin 30 ml with 100 ml dilution/day * 21 days with
      rehabilitation

      Group 2(Placebo): saline 100 ml/day * 21 days with rehabilitation
