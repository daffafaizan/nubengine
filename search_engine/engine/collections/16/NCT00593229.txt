
    
      Children age, 6 months - 18 years, are eligible if they have one of the following categories
      of thrombotic microangiopathy (TMA):(1) severe D+HUS; (2) non-familial atypical HUS; (3)
      familial atypical HUS; or (4) TTP

      Patients are seen intially and annually thereafter

      Biosamples are collected for storage annually.
