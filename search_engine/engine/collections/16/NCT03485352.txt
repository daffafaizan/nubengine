
    
      The patients will be selected for ten months and will be monitored for 12 months there are
      preoperative period, 3 months, 6 months and 12 months after surgery. Anthropometrical
      evaluation, biochemistry, polysomnography and sleep questionnaire (Epworth and Psqi) and
      midpoint sleep questionnaires will be performed to classify the chronotype for sleep pattern
      evaluation.
