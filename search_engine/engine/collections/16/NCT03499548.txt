
    
      10 hours of an adapted CBT therapy for suicide will be delivered across 2 weeks in 5 2 hour
      sessions to male prisoners. They will initially be assessed on several measures of mood,
      social support and suicidality. They will then undergo therapy. Following this they will be
      asked to re-completed the initial measures and a post-therapy interview to ascertain their
      thoughts on their experience of the therapy.
