
    
      Platelets are the cells initiating the wound healing and also supporting it by secreting
      various growth factors actively. These growth factors released by platelets act the increase
      of connective tissue healing, bone regeneration and repair, fibroblast mitogenesis, wound
      angiogenesis and the activation of macrophages by stimulating cell proliferation signals.

      Titanium-prepared, platelet-rich fibrin (T-PRF), is a new platelet concentrate, is formed in
      titanium tubes may be more efficient to activate platelets in comparison with glass tubes.The
      titanium tubes is utilized to refrain any inverse effects of glass tubes and also silica.
