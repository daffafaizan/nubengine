
    
      METHODOLOGY:

        -  Upper gastrointestinal endoscopy and colonoscopy on patients with obscure bleeding and/
           or iron deficiency anemia.

        -  Video capsule endoscopy on those fulfilling the inclusion criteria

        -  Randomization to Misoprostol 200 micrograms or placebo, 4 times each day given for 8
           weeks to aspirin/ NSAID users with erosive small bowel lesions.

        -  Video capsule endoscopy at 8 weeks to check healing of small bowel lesions.

        -  Full blood count at baseline and monthly intervals (0, 4, and 8 weeks)
