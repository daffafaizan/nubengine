
    
      With a sample size of 36, 3 patches of a single stable vitiligo patient will be prepared
      using dermabrasion,dermaroller system and liquid nitrogen induced blister. The autologous non
      cultured epidermal cell suspension will be prepared using cold trypsinization from the skin
      graft taken from patient.After preparation of the recipient site,the suspension is applied
      and dressing is done.The patient is followed up for 3 months and extent of
      repigmentation,colour match,pattern of repigmentation,patient satisfaction score, vitiligo
      quality of life index before and after surgery and effect on acral vs non acral regions are
      studied for all the 3 methods.
