
    
      From January 2008 to September 2015, 765 patients were prospectively randomized for early
      discharge after laparoscopic colectomy according to E.R.A.S. programs: after obtained
      informed consent, 384 patients were randomly assigned to the "Ultra" E.R.A.S. group and 381
      to Classic E.R.A.S. group.
