
    
      This randomized study investigates whether two treatment conditions:Mind-Body Bridging (MBB)
      and Supportive Education (SED) are effective in improving sleep disturbance and self-reported
      indicators of other co-morbid symptoms in veterans with GWI. The two treatment conditions
      will each comprise 3 sessions of 2 hr. duration and conducted over 3 consecutive weeks (Week
      1-3).
