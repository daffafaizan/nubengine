
    
      Segmental forward maxillary distraction osteogenesis(DO)was performed. Distractor connecting
      buccal molar segments to the anterior maxilla was kept for 7 days. Then distraction was
      performed till overcorrection and consolidated for 3-months. Periodontal clinical parameters
      were recorded at baseline, 3 and 6-months postoperatively. Gingival crevicular fluid was
      collected . Soft tissue healing was evaluated clinically and histologically after
      distraction.
