
    
      AIM 1. To understand the unique experiences of emerging adult college students during the
      Covid-19 pandemic (especially around the themes of the self, relationships with others, and
      the future).

      AIM 2. To evaluate the feasibility of the INI for emerging adult college students during the
      Covid-19 pandemic, both quantitatively and qualitatively.

      AIM 3. To evaluate the preliminary efficacy of the INI on distress, PTG, Quality of Life and
      Meaning-Making.
