
    
      Patients with macular hole with a diameter larger than 400µm have better sucess with ILM flap
      transposition, but there are no data published examining the outcome of macular hole with a
      diameter <400µm. Therefore, patients with macular hole with a diameter <400µm will be
      randomized for ILM flap transposition and ILM peeling for surgical repair of macular holes
