
    
      Induction course are:

      GO 3mg/m2 on days 1, 4,7 + the three dose levels were as follows:

      level 1: DNR: 45 mg/m2 x 3 days + AraC: 100 mg/m2 x 7 days level 2: DNR: 60 mg/m2 x 3 days +
      AraC: 100 mg/m2 x 7 days level 3: DNR 60 mg/m2 x 3 days + AraC: 200 mg/m2 x 7 days. with 20
      mg of methylprednisolone prior to each GO infusion. Consolidation course: patients in CR may
      receive 2 additional courses of consolidation chemotherapy with Amsacrine 90 mg/m2 daily for
      3 days, and Ara-C (1g/m2/12 hours x 3 days) + GO 3 mg/m2 on day 1.

      Treatment with HSCT is offered at the discretion of the physician in charge of the patient. A
      delay between last infusion of GO and HSCT above 3 months is recommended
