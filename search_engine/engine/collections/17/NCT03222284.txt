
    
      The purpose of this study is to evaluate the utility and user experience of a mobile
      application, called Cornerstones4Care Powered by Glooko App (C4C), for persons diagnosed with
      diabetes type 2.Subjects will be asked to use the Cornerstones4Care Powered by Glooko App for
      an approximately 20 minute monitored demonstration session, followed by a 28-day period of
      daily engagement with the application.The Cornerstones4Care Powered by Glooko App lets users
      input nutrition, medication, exercise and other daily information to assist in the management
      of diabetes type 2. It also has the ability to sync with connected devices, such as blood
      glucose monitors. Additionally, the application offers a library of educational content for
      users.
