
    
      The purpose of this study is to investigate the (sublingual) microcirculation in patients
      after an out of hospital cardiac arrest (OHCA). The microcirculation is measured in this
      study by Sublingual Side Stream Darkfield (SDF) Imaging and Near InfraRed Spectroscopy (NIRS)
      at admission and after 12 and 24 hours.
