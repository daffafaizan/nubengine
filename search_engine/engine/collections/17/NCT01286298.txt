
    
      The null hypothesis: diode laser gingivectomy is not effective in gingival enlargement
      related to orthodontic treatment.

      Outcome measures:

      Plaque Index Gingival Index Bleeding on Probing Probing Pocket Depth Gingival Overgrowth
      Index Pain score by VAS

      Inclusion criteria:

        1. between 10-40 year-olds (inclusive).

        2. gingival enlargement on the labial side of anterior teeth.

        3. fit and healthy.

        4. non-smokers.
