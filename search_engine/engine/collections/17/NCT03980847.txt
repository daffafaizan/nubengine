
    
      20 healthy patients referring to periodontics department of Boroujerd Azad university of
      dentistry after obtaining consent to participate in the research project, which has more than
      20 teeth, are over 18 years old and require socket preservation surgery to tooth extraction
      for the periodontal problem, caries or Fractures were selected. Furthermore, divided the
      socket randomly to two groups(A, B)including A: bone graft wif Alendronate and B: bone graft
      alone, after 3 months wif trephine will collect the samples. following the histomorphometric
      analysis.
