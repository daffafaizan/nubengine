
    
      There are many causes of chronic kidney disease. The most common cause is diabetes mellitus.
      Acute renal failure is a rapidly progressive loss of renal function, generally characterized
      by body fluids disturbances and electrolyte derangement.

      It is believed that far infrared radiation of the central nervous system, the endocrine
      system and the kidneys will help in the management of renal failure and a possible cure.
