
    
      Eligible patients whose assisted living centers are randomized to the information only arm
      will receive a letter from their clinician with links to informational video. Eligible
      patients whose assisted living centers are randomized to the information plus clinician
      follow-up arm will receive the letter and a structured follow-up call from their clinician.
      Eligible patients whose assisted living centers are randomized to the usual care arm have
      advance care planning discussions with a clinician at admission, annually, and sometimes with
      a hospitalization or other change in condition.
