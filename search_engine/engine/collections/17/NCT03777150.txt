
    
      A prospective analysis of high perioperative risk patients that are about to be subjected
      into an elective operation will be included into the study. They will be allocated into two
      groups. Group ICU patients are those that are admitted into the ICU for postoperative care
      and those that are admitted into a standard ward consist Group Ward. Demographic data, the
      length of hospital stay, the outcome, the need of mechanical ventilation, the complications
      and the total cost will be recorded.
