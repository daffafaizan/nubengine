
    
      There will be two groups of 50 patients. One with Retinal Vein Occlusion, and the other one,
      with no Retinal Vein Occlusion will be chosen from the consultation.

      The compared group will be paired on different criteria including hypertension, ocular
      hypertension, diabetes, sex, age.

      They will all spend a night at home with a Sleeping Monitor (Nox T3) in order to evaluate if
      they have an Obstructive Sleep Apnea.
