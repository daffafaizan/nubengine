
    
      A prospective, randomized and concurrent controlled, non-inferiority pivotal trial in which
      subjects who receive a DCD donor heart transplant will be compared to subjects who receive a
      standard criteria donor heart transplant (Standard of Care 1 [SOC1] and Standard of Care 2
      [SOC2] - from both randomized and concurrent control groups), adjusting for differences in
      risk factors.
