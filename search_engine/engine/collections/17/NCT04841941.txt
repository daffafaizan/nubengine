
    
      Study Design:

      A clinical performance study without a control group to compare the G4Multi contact lens to
      other approved soft contact lenses for presbyopia

      Study Objectives:

        1. To assess the multifocal vision in the various ranges of the HANITA G4 Multi.

        2. To rate the comfort of the lenses in individuals with presbyopia

      Study population:

      Healthy men and women above the age of 18 diagnosed with presbyopia who have used contact
      lenses in the past

      Recruitment :

      A total of 30 subjects completing at least 1 month of participation.
