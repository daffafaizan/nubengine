
    
      Following angiography, patients with significant diameter stenosis >50% and lesion length (>
      25mm) requiring single or multiple long-stent placement (total stent length>28mm) by visual
      estimation and eligible for LONG-DES IV trial inclusion and exclusion criteria will be
      randomized 1:1 to a) CYPHER and b) ENDEAVOR RESOLUTE stent by the stratified randomization
      method.
