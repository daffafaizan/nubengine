
    
      This study is the First in Human (FIH) study to investigate safety, tolerability and
      pharmacokinetics in healthy Japanese and Caucasian adult male subjects when ONO-2910 is
      administered as single and multiple doses orally. Also, the purpose of this study is to
      evaluate pharmacokinetics of oral single-dose of ONO-2910 in Japanese elderly male and female
      subjects.
