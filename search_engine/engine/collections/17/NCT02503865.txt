
    
      Purpose: To create the complex scientifically proved concept of Metabolic syndrome (МS)
      development based on elaboration of the new methods of clinical diagnostics of МS components
      and highly effective МS clinical management in comparative studying of clinical, laboratory
      and cost effectiveness.

      Participants and methods: This clinical study included 351 adults (184 female) in the
      Scientific research institute of cardiology and internal diseases (Almaty, the Republic of
      Kazakhstan). Statistical analysis was performed using Microsoft Excel-2008 in updating
      Lapach, Chubenko et al (2000) and SPSS for Windows v.17.0.
