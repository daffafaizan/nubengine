
    
      Methods: In this Randomized Controlled Trial, patients undergoing partial hepatectomy will be
      administered a single pre-operative dose of Pregabalin or a placebo. Primary endpoints are as
      follows:

        -  Post-operative analgesic requirements

        -  Post-operative VAS scale (rated 0-10)

        -  Complications

        -  Post-operative antiemetic requirement

        -  Length of stay
