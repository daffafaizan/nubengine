
    
      The patients with spasticity for at least 6 months were randomized for 3 groups. Group I:
      patients receive one session of shock wave per week for 3 weeks; Group II: patients receive
      only one session of shock wave; Group III: patients receive the sham shock wave per week for
      3 weeks. The outcome measurements include Modified Asthow scale, passive range of motion,
      Disability Assessment Scale and Fugl-Meyer Assessment Scale (hand function and wrist
      control).
