
    
      This study will be a prospective, randomized controlled trial to assess the efficacy of a
      caregiver-directed intervention designed to improve anxiety in caregivers of patients with
      malignant gliomas, in comparison with caregivers who receive usual care.

      The aim of this study is to test the efficacy of this intervention in reducing distress in
      caregivers of patients with malignant gliomas, in comparison with usual care. The study will
      be conducted in 120 caregivers.
