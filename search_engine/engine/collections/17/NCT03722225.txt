
    
      This is a descriptive, single arm, non-randomized interventional study. The intervention
      consisted of carbohydrate loading prior to and intermittent high carbohydrate intake during
      physical exercise and a proactive use of Real-Time Continuous Glucose Monitoring (rtCGM) to
      achieve and maintain glucose control.
