
    
      A retrospective study investigating young adults aged <45 years for males and <55 years for
      females admitted at Saud Al Babtain Cardiac Center with diagnosis of Acute Myocardial
      Infarction, determining its risk factors for Coronary Artery Disease (CAD) related to
      cultural characteristics and common positive consanguinity of parents as well as the
      prevalent diabetes and smoking.
