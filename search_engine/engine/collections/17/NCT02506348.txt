
    
      In this study we will compare the ocular tolerability between preservative-free Diclofenac
      and Nepafenac drops. Using a standard comparative scale we will measured discomfort, itching,
      burning and pain in intervals of 1 sec, 5 sec, 1 min and 5 min post-administration.

      The results may aid both doctors and patients in decision regarding the post surgical
      anti-inflammatory treatment.
