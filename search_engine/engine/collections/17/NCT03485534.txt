
    
      Tenofovir Disoproxil and Tenofovir Disoproxil Fumarate is a nucleotide analogue and a potent
      inhibitor of human immunodeficiency virus type 1 reverse transcriptase and hepatitis B virus
      (HBV) polymerase.

      The primary efficacy end point at week 48 of this study was defined as the combination of an
      HBV DNA level of less than 400 copies per milliliter and histologic improvement .
