
    
      Retrospective observational unicentric study in nursing-home residents with COVID-19. Health
      status monitoring data available until May 15, 2020. For all participants, gender, age,
      disability, history and comorbidities, treatments, date of last vitamin D3 supplementation,
      results of last blood test, date of suspicion / diagnosis of COVID-19, COVID-19 OSCI score,
      and eventual hospitalization or death (surveillance data available until May 15, 2020) are
      collected.
