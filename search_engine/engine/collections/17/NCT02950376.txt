
    
      In order to evaluate the addiction severity and the effect of rehabilitation objectively,
      this study develops a virtual environment model based on the virtual reality technique,
      making the subjects in a drug related or neutral scene , at the same time recording the
      physical signs and eye movements index. Reliability and validity of this system will also be
      tested in this study. And we will construct the norm of addiction assessment system in
      Shanghai .The evaluating system will be very beneficial to develop novel and practical
      assessment for synthetic durg related disorder, and provide a reference for other drug
      dependence diagnosis.
