
    
      The specific aims of the project are:

        1. To determine whether Mindfulness- Based Stress Reduction program is associated with a
           reduction in symptoms of depression, anxiety, and psychological distress.

        2. To assess indications of efficacy, assess feasibility of enrollment and compliance among
           this patient population

        3. Provide pilot data that can be used to apply for larger grants to more fully develop
           this approach
