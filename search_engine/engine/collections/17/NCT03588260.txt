
    
      The patients who have agreed to participate in the study from patients diagnosed with
      idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis referred to the center of pulmonary rehabilitation from the
      interstitial lung disease polyclinic will be included in the study. In addition, a healthy
      adult control group will be established from similar age groups. The two groups will be
      compared in terms of outcome measures.The study outcome measurements are maximum
      inspiratory/expiratory muscle strength , international physical activity questionaire,
      modified Medical Research Council dyspnea scale, Short form 36 quality of lide questionaire,
      fatigue severity scale, six minute walking test.
