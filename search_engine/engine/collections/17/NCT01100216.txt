
    
      Treatment will be a 3-period crossover study using Commit Nicotine Lozenge, Stonewall
      Disposable Tobacco Pieces and Camel Snus Frost Pouches. Each period will last 14 days with 11
      days of product use and 1.5 days abstinence of all nicotine-containing products for a total
      of 10 weeks. Participants will begin the study by completing a baseline assessment on their
      own product, Skoal Wintergreen.
