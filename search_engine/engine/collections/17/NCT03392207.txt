
    
      Study Design: This is a prospective cohort study. Population: Target groups for vaccination
      defined by The National Immunization Program of Brazil: healthcare workers and elderly
      (people over 60 years old). Study Size: A total of 400 individuals (150 health care workers
      and 250 elderly), presenting for immunization with IB TIV, and who agree to participate after
      providing Informed Consent.
