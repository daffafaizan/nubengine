
    
      A total of 45 patients (13 males and 32 females) participated in the current study. The
      participants were assigned to one of two groups in a random manner: an experimental group who
      received hippotherapy combined with Schroth Exercise and the control group who received only
      schorth exercise. (FVC, FEV1, FEV1/FVC, MVV, and 6MWT) were measured before and after 10
      weeks intervention
