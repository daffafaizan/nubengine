
    
      Implementation of an ICU diary in a randomized design in 36 ICUs in France The ICU diary
      contains entries from staff and families or relatives

      Quantitative description of psychological well-being of the patients and families or
      relatives are evaluated 3 months after ICU discharge

      Qualitative evaluation of how the patient uses the diary is scheduled by a phone interview 6
      months after ICU discharge.

      An amendment of the ethic committee permitted to increase in 2016/02 both the number of
      eligible patients to 700 and the number of centers.
