
    
      PROTOCOL OUTLINE: The posterior region of 1 orbit is irradiated over 2 weeks with
      supervoltage equipment. The other orbit is treated 6 months later. The sequence of therapy is
      randomly assigned.

      The untreated eye receives a simultaneous sham treatment during each radiotherapy session.

      Patients are followed at 3, 6, 9, 12, and 36 months.
