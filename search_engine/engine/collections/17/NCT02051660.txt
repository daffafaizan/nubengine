
    
      Principal hypothesis: The manualized psychotherapeutic intervention (CALM) will result in a
      significantly greater reduction in depression and psychological distress compared to a
      non-manualized supportive psycho-oncological intervention. Secondary hypothesis: The
      manualized psychotherapeutic intervention (CALM) will further result in a significantly
      improve psychological well-being, quality of life and sense of meaning compared to a
      non-manualized supportive psycho-oncological intervention.
