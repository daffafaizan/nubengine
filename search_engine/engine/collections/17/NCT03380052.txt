
    
      To validated the OLGA and OLGIM staging system with serum pepsinogen to estimate gastric
      cancer risk according to Lauren's histologic classification in South Korea, where the
      incidence rate of GC is high. In addition, the investigators attempted to investigate the
      interaction among the several risk factors including OLGA or OLGIM staging system for the
      risk of GC to help establish surveillance strategy.
