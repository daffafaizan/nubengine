
    
      -  Participants will be asked to come into the clinic for additional visits before they
           begin hormone therapy. The following procedures will be performed: Cold-activated PET/CT
           scan; body composition DXA scan; blood tests, questionnaires and abdominal fat biopsy.

        -  During hormone therapy, the participant will return to the clinical once after 3 months,
           and again after 6 months, to draw blood for laboratory tests.

        -  After 12 months of hormone therapy, the participant will return to teh clinic to repeat
           the following procedures: Cold-activated PET/CT scan; body composition DXA scan; blood
           tests; questionnaires and abdominal fat biopsy.
