
    
      Randomized, split plot, double-blind, placebo controlled trial. Non-diabetic patients with
      full-thickness wounds will be treated in the following manner: in half of the surface insulin
      will be applied in a daily basis, on the other half, saline solution will be used as placebo.
      Biopsy specimens of both sides will be obtained on days 0 and 14. Number of blood vessels
      will be evaluated as main outcome.
