
    
      This study will have three stages. Stage 1: Safety and initial bioavailability of Entera's
      oral EP101 in adult male healthy volunteers. (SA-BA) Stage 2: Assessment of bioavailability
      and safety of Entera's oral EP101 in adult male healthy volunteers. (SA-BA-PK-PD) Stage 3:
      Safety, PK, and PD of final dose of Entera's oral PTH(1 - 34) in adult healthy male and
      female volunteers. (SA-BA-PK-PD)
