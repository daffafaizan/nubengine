
    
      A multicenter, randomized, double-blind, comparator and vehicle-controlled study in subjects
      with acne vulgaris. Approximately 1320 subjects will be enrolled. Subjects will be randomized
      to 1 of 4 parallel study groups in a 1:1:1:1 ratio (clindamycin / benzoyl peroxide
      gel:clindamycin gel:BPO gel:vehicle gel).
