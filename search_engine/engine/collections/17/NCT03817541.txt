
    
      Edmon (trade mark) is a New Device from BBraun, measuring the expiratory concentration of
      propofol in anaesthetized patients.

      The investigators will test the accuracy and stability of such measurements in comparison
      With plasma propofol Levels.

      The investigators will test during target Control infusion (Marsh model) and assumed stable
      plasma Levels.

      Plasma samples of propofol will be taken for comparison With exhaled propofol.
