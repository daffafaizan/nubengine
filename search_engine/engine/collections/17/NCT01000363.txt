
    
      Patients who participate in the diabetes group visits should achieve improvement in Clinical
      outcomes - better A1C levels, reduced blood pressure, reduced cholesterol levels ; adherence
      to evidence-based medicine - keeping appointments and scheduling eye exams, foot-checks,
      and/or taking medication as prescribed; behavioral outcomes- goal setting, achievement of
      goals, problem solving; access - attendance at educational sessions; ability to obtain
      affordable medications and devices; and satisfaction among patients and health professionals.
