
    
      This is a pilot study that aim to enroll 60 subjects with high bleeding risk.

      All patients with coronary artery stenosis suitable for DCB treatment will undergo 1:1
      randomization either to NSE predilation or NC balloon predilation using a randomization
      schedule.

      All subjects will be screened according to the predefined inclusion and exclusion criteria
      per protocol and will further undergo IVUS before DCB treatment. Data and images will be
      collected during the index procedure, and at the predefined 6-month IVUS follow-up visit.
