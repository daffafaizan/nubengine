
    
      Primary Aim: In healthy, cognitively intact elderly subjects, determine the effects of an
      8-week synbiotic supplement intervention on the stool microbiome (pre- and post-intervention)
      as compared to Triphala alone and placebo.

      Secondary Aim: In healthy elderly subjects, examine the effects of an 8-week synbiotic
      supplementation intervention on inflammatory blood markers compared to herb alone or placebo.

      Exploratory Aim 1: In healthy elderly subjects, examine the effects of an 8-week synbiotic
      intervention on gastrointestinal and psychological health via questionnaires compared to herb
      alone or placebo.
