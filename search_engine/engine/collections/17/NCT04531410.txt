
    
      Two group of matched children with CF were randomized to two type of oils given 20 g oil and
      800 mg DHA daily for one year and anthropometry, pulmonary function, biochemistry, resting
      energy expenditure, lipid mediators, inflammatory and intestinal markers were studied at
      start and at 6 months and 1 year. Dietary intake was controlled and life quality recording at
      start and end of study.
