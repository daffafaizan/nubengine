
    
      Diabetic nephropathy is leading cause of ESRF that many therapy recommended for it. In this
      study, we investigate effect of turmeric on diabetic nephropathy, thus we selected diabetic
      nephropathy patients and divided them into 2 groups. One group received turmeric + ACE
      inhibitor + Angiotensin II receptor blocker (ARB) and the second group received only ACE
      inhibitor + ARB.
