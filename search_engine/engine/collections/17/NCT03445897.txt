
    
      Bolivian cutaneous leishmaniasis due to Leishmania braziliensis was treated with the
      combination of miltefosine (150 mg/day for 28 days) plus intralesional pentamidine (120
      ug/mm2 lesion area on days 1, 3, and 5). Efficacy and tolerance parameters were assessed.
      Efficacy was assessed at 1, 3, and 6 months post therapy. Tolerance was assessed on each day
      therapy was administered.
