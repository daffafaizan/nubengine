
    
      Participants will consume a tart cherry or placebo product for 30 days and attend 5 visits in
      the Exercise Physiology Lab at Ohio University over the 30 day period. They will self-collect
      a stool sample at baseline, 14 days and 30 days after supplementation. Blood samples will be
      taken at baseline, 7 days, 14 days, and 30 days after supplementation. Throughout the study
      participates will rate symptoms (stomach, head, etc.), pain and sleep quality using an online
      survey for which the link will be emailed. Participants will maintain normal diet and
      exercise routine and to track these for the duration of the study.
