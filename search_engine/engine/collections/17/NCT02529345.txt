
    
      It is a Prospective, multi-center, physician-sponsored clinical study.

      The objective of this clinical investigation is to evaluate the clinical outcome (up to 12
      months) of treatment by means of stenting with the RoadSaver (Terumo) in subjects at high
      risk for carotid endarterectomy requiring carotid revascularization due to significant
      extra-cranial carotid artery stenosis.
