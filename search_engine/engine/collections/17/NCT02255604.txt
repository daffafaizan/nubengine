
    
      Patients are injected with 3 or 4 doses of alk 225 Phleum Pratense 1000 standard quantity
      units/ml in a lymph node in the groin.

      The investigators monitor clinical response and use of medication during the grass pollen
      season. SMS (Symptom and Medication Score) will be used.

      The investigators count the number of immunoglobulin E grass producing plasma cells in blood
      one week after each injection. The investigators also do basophil activation tests, nasal
      provocation tests and titrated skin prick test to monitor effect.

      Adverse events will be recorded.
