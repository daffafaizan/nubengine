
    
      Instruments used to determine change after 3 and 9 months:

      Osteoporosis knowledge: Osteoporosis Knowledge Assessment Test (Winzenberg et al. BMC
      Musculoskelet Disord 2003) Calcium intake: Food frequency questionnaire (Angus et al. J Am
      Diet Assoc 1989) Calcium and exercise self-efficacy: Osteoporosis Self-Efficacy Scale (Horan
      et al Res Nurs Health 1998) Physical activity: CHAMPS II (Stewart et al. J Gerontol A Biol
      Sci Med Sci 2001)
