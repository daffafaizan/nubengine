
    
      Valvular heart diseases are significant problem in Polish population. Coexistence of coronary
      artery disease in patients with VHD increases the risk of death and affects further
      therapeutic strategy.

      Patients with moderate and severe valvular heart diseases are included into the study. The
      set of variables subject to interpretation consisted of demographic data, medical history,
      result of coronary angiography, and type of applied percutaneous and surgical treatment
