2625. chromosome aberrations in human cells following treatment with imuranfive women with various collagen diseases were studied.  bone marrow aspirateswere obtained from each patient before and 12 to 24 days after start of imuran(azothioprine) therapy.  fifty metaphases from each sample were counted.  inpatients nos. 1 and 2 the increase in cells with structural abnormalitiesduring therapy is significant.  the aberrations found were mostly breaks of thechromatid and chromosomal type.  a few abnormal chromosomes were seen,among which a ring chromosome.  in patient no. 1 chromatid exchanges wereseen in three cells.
