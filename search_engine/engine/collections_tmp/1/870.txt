a comparison of human placental lactogen (hpl) and human growth hormone(hgh) in hypopituitary patients.the conclusions based on the studies reported arethat placental lactogen (hpl) at a dose level of 200 mg./day did not induce a positive nitrogen balance in two hypopituitarysubjects.  in addition, lactogen does not augmentthe nitrogen retaining effect of growth hormonewhen given in a dose of 200 mg./day.
