dipetalonema obtusa (mccoy,1936) comb. n. (filarioidea:onchocercidae) in colombian primates, with a description of the adultmicrofilaria obtusa mccoy, 1936, was identified inthe blood of 29 (31%) of 93 cebuscapucinus and c. albifrons from northern colombia, but was notfound in an additional 42 c. apella and99 saimiri sciurea from other areas in colombia.  the adult maleand female, recovered from the peri-esophageal connective tissue of the infected animals, are describedin detail.  dipetalonema obtusa (mc-coy, 1936) comb. n. resembles tetrapetalonema marmosetae faust,1935, but is smaller and the rightspicule is complex, not simple.
